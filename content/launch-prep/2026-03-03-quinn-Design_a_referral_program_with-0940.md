Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. This design focuses on being engaging, rewarding, and adaptable.

**Program Name:** (Let’s call it) “Share the [Your Brand Name] Love”

**1. Core Concept:**

* **Simple & Easy to Understand:** The program should be incredibly easy for customers to understand and participate in.
* **Multi-Tiered Rewards:** Offering different reward levels based on the referred customer’s engagement helps motivate both the referrer and the new customer.
* **Trackable & Automated:**  Essential for smooth operation and accurate reward delivery.


**2. Referral Structure & Incentives:**

Here’s a breakdown of tiers and rewards, designed to be flexible for your specific business:

* **Tier 1: The Basic Referral (Easy Entry)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  [Small Incentive - e.g., 10% off next purchase, Free Shipping on their next order, Small Gift Card ($5-$10)]
    * **New Customer Reward:** [Small Incentive - e.g., 5% discount on their first purchase, Welcome discount code]
    * **Mechanics:**  Referrer gets a unique referral link they share.  New customer uses that link to sign up or make a purchase.

* **Tier 2: The Enthusiast Referral (Increased Engagement)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** [Medium Incentive - e.g., 20% off their next purchase, $15 Gift Card, Exclusive Early Access to New Products/Features]
    * **New Customer Reward:** [Medium Incentive - e.g., 10% discount on their first purchase, Free premium feature trial]
    * **Mechanics:** Same as Tier 1, but with a slightly more desirable reward.

* **Tier 3: The Advocate Referral (High Engagement - For repeat customers)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** [Large Incentive - e.g., $50 Gift Card, Exclusive Merchandise Item, VIP Status for a month]
    * **New Customer Reward:** [High Value Incentive - e.g., 15% discount on their first purchase, Free consultation/support, Extended trial period]
    * **Mechanics:**  This tier is geared toward loyal customers who already love your product.



**3. Tracking & Automation:**

* **Unique Referral Links:**  Crucial. Use a referral marketing platform (like ReferralCandy, Mention, Ambassador, or even a robust CRM with referral tracking) to generate these links.
* **Automated Email Sequences:**
    * **For Referrers:** Automated email sent when a referral is made: “Someone used your link! Here’s a summary and your reward details.”
    * **For New Customers:**  Welcome email with their discount code and information about the referral program.
* **Dashboard:**  A simple dashboard for both the referrer and the administrator to track referrals, rewards issued, and overall program performance.

**4. Promotion & Communication:**

* **Website Promotion:**  Clearly display the referral program on your website (homepage, product pages, checkout page).
* **Social Media:**  Run engaging social media campaigns promoting the program – contests, testimonials, highlight the rewards.
* **Email Marketing:**  Promote the program in your regular email newsletters.
* **In-App/In-Product Messaging:**  If you have an app or in-product experience, integrate a referral prompt.
* **Referral Cards/Posters (If relevant to your business):** For brick-and-mortar stores.

**5. Terms & Conditions (Important Legal Considerations):**

* **Eligibility:**  Clearly state who is eligible to participate (e.g., existing customers only).
* **Reward Redemption Rules:**  Specify how rewards can be redeemed
