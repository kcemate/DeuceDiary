## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a structured approach to tracking and analyzing your competition, allowing you to understand their strategies, identify opportunities, and ultimately improve your own performance.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Gain a deep understanding of the competitive landscape to inform strategic decision-making and drive business growth.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths/weaknesses.
    * Monitor competitor activities – product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns, etc.
    * Understand competitor customer perception and sentiment.
    * Identify potential opportunities and threats.
    * Benchmark your performance against the competition.

**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Step 1: Identification:**
    * **Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target market. (List 3-5)
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions that satisfy the same customer needs. (List 3-5)
    * **Potential Future Competitors:** Companies that could enter the market in the future. (List 2-3)
* **Step 2: Prioritization:**  Rank competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:**  A larger market share indicates a stronger competitive presence.
    * **Growth Rate:**  Rapidly growing competitors pose a greater threat.
    * **Strategic Relevance:**  Competitors with strategies similar to yours warrant closer attention.
    * **Impact on Your Business:** Which competitors have the most significant influence on your market.
