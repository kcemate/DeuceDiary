**Retention Analysis Plan**

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Understand and improve customer retention rates across key time horizons (D1, D7, D30).
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key drivers of early churn (D1-D7).
    * Determine patterns related to longer-term engagement (D7-D30).
    * Develop actionable insights to reduce churn and increase lifetime value.
    * Prioritize interventions based on identified churn risks.

**2. Data Collection & Tracking:**

* **Data Sources:**
    * **Product/Service Usage Data:** Track key activities within your product or service (e.g., logins, feature usage, transactions, time spent in app, content consumed).
    * **Customer Support Interactions:**  Record all support tickets, chat logs, and phone calls (categorize reasons for contact – bugs, questions, feedback, etc.).
    * **Billing/Subscription Data:** Track subscription status, payment history, upgrades, downgrades, cancellations.
    * **Onboarding Data:** Capture data from the onboarding process – completion rates, tutorial views, first-time user actions.
    * **Customer Surveys (Exit Surveys & Pulse Surveys):** Gather direct feedback from users, particularly those who churn or who are at risk.
* **Tracking Metrics:**
    * **Retention Rate (Cohort-Based):** This is the core metric.  Segment your users into cohorts based on their signup date. Calculate the percentage of users within each cohort who are *still active* after each benchmark.
    * **Churn Rate:**  The inverse of retention – the percentage of users who stopped being active within a given period.  Calculate this for each cohort.
    * **Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU):** Daily Active Users (DAU), Weekly Active Users (WAU), and Monthly Active Users (MAU) to measure overall engagement.
    * **Feature Usage:** Track the usage of key features - identify which features are correlated with higher retention.
    * **Support Ticket Volume/Resolution Time:** Measure the volume of support requests and how quickly they're resolved.  Longer resolution times can indicate dissatisfaction.
    * **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Gauge customer loyalty and willingness to recommend.
    * **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** Measure customer satisfaction with specific interactions or the overall product experience.
