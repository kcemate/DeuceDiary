He’s obsessed with collecting lost buttons, convinced he can communicate with pigeons, and harbors a deep, unsettling fascination with the unsettlingly realistic mannequins in the local department store. When his estranged father suddenly reappears – a charismatic, slightly unsettling antiques dealer with a secret – Deuce’s already strange world becomes even stranger.  Navigating the chaos of first love, family secrets, and the unnerving realization that maybe, just *maybe*, he’s not entirely sane, Deuce chronicles it all in a series of increasingly bizarre diary entries. 

*Deuce Diary* is a witty and poignant exploration of identity, family, and finding your place in a world that doesn't always make sense.
