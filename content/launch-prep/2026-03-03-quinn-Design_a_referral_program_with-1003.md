Okay, let's design a referral program with tiered incentives. This design will be adaptable to different business types, so I’ll focus on creating a framework you can customize.

**Program Name:** (Let's call it) "Spread the [Your Brand Name]"

**Overall Goal:** To organically grow your customer base through trusted recommendations.

**I. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The existing customer who refers a new customer.
* **Referee:** The new customer who signs up/makes a purchase through the referrer’s link.
* **Tracking:** Crucial!  You'll need a system to track referrals – ideally through a referral software or a custom-built solution within your CRM or e-commerce platform. Key data points:
    * Referrer ID
    * Referee ID
    * Date of Referral
    * Amount of Purchase (for tiered rewards)
* **Link Generation:** Simple, trackable links for referrers.  Shortened URLs are great for sharing.  Ideally, these links should lead directly to a signup page or a specific product page (depending on your goals).

**II. Tiers & Incentives (This is the core of the program - customize these!)**

We’ll use a tiered structure to incentivize both the referrer and the referee.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Everyone)**

* **Referrer Reward:** 10% discount on their next purchase. (Easy to implement, immediate value)
* **Referee Reward:** 10% discount on their first purchase. (Low barrier to entry, attractive offer)
* **Trigger:**  Referee completes their first purchase.


**Tier 2: Bronze (Reaching a Threshold)**

* **Eligibility:** Referrer has referred 3 new customers who have made a purchase.
* **Referrer Reward:** 15% discount on their next purchase + Free [Small Gift - e.g., Sample size, sticker, small branded item]. (Increased reward for consistent referrals)
* **Referee Reward:** 15% discount on their first purchase.
* **Trigger:**  Referee completes their first purchase.



**Tier 3: Silver (Top Performers)**

* **Eligibility:** Referrer has referred 5+ new customers who have made a purchase.
* **Referrer Reward:** 20% discount on their next purchase + Free [Medium Gift - e.g.,  Full-size product sample, branded accessory] + Featured on your website/social media. (Significant incentive, recognition)
* **Referee Reward:** 20% discount on their first purchase.
* **Trigger:** Referee completes their first purchase.

**Tier 4: Gold (Top Referrers - Optional, for high-value products/services)**

* **Eligibility:**  Referrer has referred 10+ new customers who have made a purchase. (Requires a higher commitment)
* **Referrer Reward:**  A significant gift (e.g., $50 gift card to your store, premium product bundle) + Exclusive early access to new products/services + Dedicated account manager support. (For loyal, high-value customers)
* **Referee Reward:** 25% discount on their first purchase.
* **Trigger:** Referee completes their first purchase.
