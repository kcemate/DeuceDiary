**1. The “Mutual Benefit” Approach (Best for Broad Outreach)**

* **Subject:** Collaboration Opportunity: [Your Brand] & [Their Brand] – Reaching [Shared Audience]
* **Body:**

Hi [Contact Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I’m the [Your Title] at [Your Brand] – we’re a [brief, compelling description of your brand - e.g., “a wellness brand dedicated to helping busy moms prioritize self-care,” or “a certified yoga instructor offering online classes”].

I've been a long-time admirer of [Their Brand] and particularly love [mention something specific you genuinely like - a product, a campaign, a value].  We share a similar passion for [mention shared value - e.g., “empowering healthy lifestyles,” “promoting mental well-being,” “sustainable wellness”].

At [Your Brand], we’re focused on [briefly state your key offerings - e.g., “providing delicious, plant-based meal plans,” “offering guided meditation sessions”]. We believe a collaboration could be hugely beneficial for both our audiences, particularly those interested in [mention specific audience interests aligned with both brands].

I'd love to explore potential collaboration ideas like:
*   [Suggest 2-3 specific ideas – e.g., “a joint giveaway,” “a co-branded content series,” “an exclusive discount code for each other's customers”]

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute chat to discuss how we could create something amazing together?

You can learn more about us here: [Link to your website]

Thanks for your time and consideration!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]
[Your Website]
[Your Social Media Links]


**2. The “Specific Offer” Approach (Good for Targeted Outreach – Influencers, Complementary Brands)**

* **Subject:** Collaboration Proposal: [Your Brand] + [Their Brand] – [Specific Offer Details]
* **Body:**

Hi [Contact Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Your Brand], and I’m reaching out because I believe your audience would love [Your Product/Service]!

We're [brief description - e.g., "a sustainable activewear brand"] and we've been impressed with your work in [mention specific area – e.g., “your fitness tips on Instagram,” “your focus on healthy eating”]

We'd like to propose a partnership where we [Clearly outline the specific offer - e.g., “you receive a free [Product Name] to review and share with your audience,” “we create a custom discount code for your followers,” “you host a live Q&A session on our Instagram channel about [Relevant Topic]”].

Here’s what we’re offering:
*   [Detailed breakdown of the offer – e.g., "3 Months supply of [Product] + prominent placement on our website."]
*   [Value proposition for them - e.g., “Increased engagement with your audience,” “A valuable product to share,” “A chance to partner with a brand your followers trust.”]

To learn more about our brand and this proposal, you can check out our website: [Link to your website]

Let me know if you're interested in discussing this further!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]
