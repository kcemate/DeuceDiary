Okay, here’s a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week and including suggested content types and goals. This is a template – you'll need to adapt it based on your specific product, target audience, and launch goals.

**Overall Launch Month Goal:**  Build excitement, drive initial adoption, collect early feedback, and establish a community.

**Key Metrics to Track:** Website traffic, social media engagement (likes, shares, comments), sign-ups/trials, downloads, customer reviews, and support requests.

---

**Week 1: The “We’re Live!” & Excitement Phase**

* **Theme:** Announcing the Launch & Highlighting Key Benefits
* **Goal:** Generate immediate awareness and drive initial traffic.
* **Content:**
    * **Monday:** Official Launch Announcement (All Channels – Website, Social Media, Email) -  A short, impactful video or image with a clear call to action (CTA) - “Try it Now!” or “Learn More.”
    * **Tuesday:** Deep Dive - Feature Spotlight (Blog Post & Social Media) - Focus on ONE core feature and why it's valuable.  Use visuals (screenshots, GIFs).
    * **Wednesday:** Customer Testimonial (Social Media - short video or quote graphic) - If you have early beta users, this is crucial.
    * **Thursday:**  "Behind the Scenes" - Quick look at the team, the development process, or what inspired the product. (Instagram Story, Short TikTok Video)
    * **Friday:**  Launch Day Recap & Q&A (Social Media Live Session – Instagram/Facebook) - Address frequently asked questions and show enthusiasm.
* **Channels:** Website, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn (tailor messaging), Email Newsletter


**Week 2: Demonstrating Value & Addressing Concerns**

* **Theme:** Showing How It Works & Building Trust
* **Goal:**  Drive sign-ups/trials, answer common questions, and address potential objections.
* **Content:**
    * **Monday:** Tutorial/How-To Video (YouTube, Website, Embedded in Blog Post) –  A short, digestible video demonstrating the *most* common use case.
    * **Tuesday:** FAQ Post (Website Blog) – Address top 5-10 frequently asked questions about pricing, features, or usage.
    * **Wednesday:** Case Study Snippet (Social Media, Blog Post) -  A brief, compelling example of how someone could benefit (even a hypothetical one if you don’t have a true case yet).
    * **Thursday:** “Myth Busting” – Address a common misconception about your product category or this product specifically. (Social Media)
    * **Friday:**  Contest/Giveaway (Social Media) - “Like & Share” to win a prize – Generates engagement and spreads awareness.
* **Channels:** Website, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Email


**Week 3: Community Building & User-Generated Content**

* **Theme:** Encourage Engagement & Show You're Listening
* **Goal:** Foster a community, gather feedback, and build social proof.
* **Content:**
    * **Monday:**  User Prompt – Ask a question to your audience – “What’s the biggest challenge you’re facing that [Product Name] could help with?” (Social Media)
    * **Tuesday:** Share User-Generated Content (If you have any – repost someone using your product) -  Give credit and thank the user.
    * **Wednesday:**  Blog Post - "Tips & Tricks" –  Provide helpful advice related to your product's use.
    * **Thursday:**  Poll/Quiz (Instagram Story, Twitter) -  Fun, interactive content related
