7. **Malware & Security Threats:**  Anything that could be considered a malicious app – including hidden code, exploits, or attempts to compromise user devices – gets rejected instantly.
8. **Content Restrictions (for certain categories):**  Apps in categories like gambling, health & fitness, or social networking have particularly stringent content requirements.
9. **Lack of Required Information:** Failing to provide all necessary information in the app’s metadata (keywords, description, screenshots, etc.) can lead to rejection.
10. **Violation of Apple’s Human Review Guidelines:** This is a catch-all for issues that don't neatly fit into the above categories, but still violate Apple’s policy.
