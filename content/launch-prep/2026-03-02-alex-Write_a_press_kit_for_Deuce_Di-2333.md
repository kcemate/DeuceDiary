Stuck in Havenwood, a town obsessed with its annual pie-eating contest and harboring a surprising amount of mystery, he's resigned himself to a quiet existence. As Finn and Lila's relationship blossoms, he uncovers family secrets that threaten to unravel everything he thought he knew, forcing him to confront his own identity and embrace the unexpected. *Deuce Diary* is a story about finding your place, accepting your imperfections, and daring to fall for someone, even if they’re a little bit strange.
