Okay, let's design an analytics dashboard for the Deuce Diary launch metrics. This dashboard will be geared towards tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) and providing insights into user engagement, acquisition, and overall success.

**I. Overall Goal of the Dashboard:**

To provide a centralized, visual view of Deuce Diary's launch performance, allowing the team to quickly assess key metrics, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions for optimization and marketing efforts.

**II. Dashboard Sections & Key Metrics:**

We'll break the dashboard into several sections, each focusing on a specific area of performance.

**1. Acquisition & Awareness (Top Row - High Level):**

* **Metric:**  *Installs* (Total) -  The total number of times the app has been downloaded. (Displayed as a large, prominent number)
* **Metric:** *New Users* (Daily/Weekly/Monthly) - Tracks the number of unique users who started using the app during that period. (Line graph - trends over time)
* **Metric:** *Source Breakdown* -  Percentage breakdown of installs by channel (e.g., App Store, Google Play, Paid Ads, Organic Search, Social Media). (Pie chart or stacked bar chart)
* **Metric:** *Cost Per Install (CPI)* - (If running paid campaigns)  Calculated based on ad spend and number of installs. (KPI - Color-coded based on target)
* **Visualization:**  A visually engaging chart showcasing the trend of new users and their source channels.


**2. Engagement & Retention (Middle Row - User Behavior):**

* **Metric:** *Daily Active Users (DAU)* - Number of unique users who opened the app each day. (Line graph - trends over time)
* **Metric:** *Weekly Active Users (WAU)* - Number of unique users who opened the app each week. (Line graph - trends over time)
* **Metric:** *Monthly Active Users (MAU)* - Number of unique users who opened the app each month. (Line graph - trends over time)
* **Metric:** *Session Duration* - Average time spent per session. (KPI -  Tracked daily/weekly/monthly)
* **Metric:** *Number of Entries/Diaries Created* - Total number of diaries started by users. (KPI - Tracked weekly/monthly)
* **Metric:** *Entry Frequency* - Average number of entries per diary (calculated on average per user). (KPI - Tracked weekly/monthly)
* **Metric:** *Most Popular Entry Types* -  Categorization and frequency of entries (e.g., Mood, Activities, Thoughts, Gratitude). (Bar Chart - helps identify popular topics)
* **Visualization:** DAU/WAU/MAU line graphs overlaid to show daily, weekly and monthly trends,  and the Entry Frequency bar chart.



**3. Content & Features (Bottom Row - User Interaction):**

* **Metric:** *Feature Usage* (Tracking specific feature adoption - e.g., Mood Tracking, Gratitude Prompts, Journal Templates) - Percentage of users using each feature. (Stacked Bar Chart - helps identify underutilized features)
* **Metric:** *Most Used Journal Templates* - Which templates are users choosing most frequently. (Bar Chart)
* **Metric:** *Completion Rate of Prompts* - How many users complete mood/gratitude/reflection prompts. (KPI - Tracked daily/weekly/monthly)
* **Metric:** *User Ratings & Reviews* - Number of ratings & overall star rating on app stores. (Text summary - monitor sentiment).
* **Visualization:** A stacked bar chart clearly showing feature usage.
