Okay, let's craft a post-launch scaling plan with defined thresholds. This plan focuses on a SaaS product, but the principles can be adapted for other types of products. It’s broken down into phases and includes key metrics to monitor.

**Product:** (Let’s assume it's a collaborative project management tool – “ProjectZen”)

**Goal:** Scale ProjectZen sustainably, ensuring a great user experience while managing growth effectively.

**I. Phase 1: Initial Traction (0 - 3 Months Post-Launch)**

* **Focus:** Validate core assumptions, establish foundational infrastructure, and gather initial user feedback.
* **Metrics:**
    * **Monthly Active Users (MAU):**  Target: 500 - 1000.  Thresholds:
        * **Below 500:**  Critical - Immediately investigate user churn rate and acquisition source effectiveness.  Prioritize bug fixes based on impact.
        * **500 - 1000:**  Stable - Continue monitoring, focusing on understanding user behavior within the product.
    * **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Target: Under $50. Thresholds:
        * **CAC > $60:**  Urgent - Re-evaluate marketing channels.  Consider A/B testing messaging, landing pages, and targeting.
        * **CAC $50 - $60:**  Watch closely - Optimize campaigns and refine targeting.
    * **Churn Rate (Monthly):** Target: Below 5%.  Thresholds:
        * **Churn > 7%:** Immediate action – Analyze churn reasons (exit surveys, support tickets).  Address immediate pain points.
        * **Churn 5% - 7%:**  Investigate deeper –  Identify patterns in churned users.
        * **Churn < 5%:**  Excellent - Maintain current strategies.
    * **Server Response Time:** Target:  Average 95% of requests under 200ms. Thresholds:
        * **Average > 500ms:**  Investigate infrastructure bottlenecks.  Scale up servers or optimize database queries.
* **Scaling Activities:**
    * Basic infrastructure scaling (AWS, Google Cloud, etc.) - Auto-scaling enabled.
    * Ongoing bug fixes and minor feature improvements based on early feedback.
    * Dedicated user support channels (email, knowledge base).


**II. Phase 2: Growth & Refinement (3 - 6 Months Post-Launch)**

* **Focus:**  Accelerate user growth, refine product based on larger data set, and start exploring new features.
* **Metrics:**
    * **MAU:** Target: 2,000 - 5,000.  Thresholds:
        * **Below 2,000:**  Review acquisition strategy – explore viral loops, referral programs, and expanded content marketing.
        * **2,000 - 5,000:**  Optimizing user onboarding and engagement.
    * **CAC:** Target: Under $40.  Thresholds:
        * **CAC > $50:**  Re-evaluate marketing ROI.  Shift budget to higher-performing channels.
    * **Conversion Rate (Free Trial to Paid):** Target: 15-20%. Thresholds:
        * **Below 10%:**  Improve onboarding flow, offer more compelling trial features, and refine messaging.
    * **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target: DAU/MAU > 0.6 (60% engagement). Thresholds: Monitor for dips and investigate.
* **Scaling Activities:**
    * Introduce advanced analytics dashboards for user behavior tracking.
    * Implement more robust user segmentation.
