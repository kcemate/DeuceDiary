Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service. This calendar focuses on building excitement, addressing early adopter feedback, and gently pushing users to explore the full feature set. **This is a template – you’ll need to tailor it to your specific product, target audience, and launch strategy.**

**Assumptions:**

*   **Launch Date:** Let's assume the launch date is October 26th, 2023.
*   **Product Type:** This calendar works well for SaaS, mobile apps, or digital products.  Adjust the specific content to fit your offering.
*   **Channels:** This assumes you’ll be using a mix of:
    *   Social Media (Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, Facebook - adjust based on where your audience is)
    *   Email Marketing
    *   Blog
    *   Community Forum (if you have one)


**Key Themes for Month 1:**

*   **Celebration & Gratitude:** Thanking early adopters.
*   **Value Demonstration:** Highlighting key benefits.
*   **Onboarding Support:** Helping users get started.
*   **Feedback & Iteration:**  Encouraging and responding to feedback.
*   **Building Community:** Fostering connections.

**Content Calendar - Month 1 (October 26th - November 25th, 2023)**

**Week 1: (Oct 26th - Nov 1st) – The Initial Buzz**

*   **Oct 26th (Launch Day):**
    *   **Social:** Official launch announcement across all channels.  Excitement, link to sign-up/purchase, launch video. (All Channels)
    *   **Email:** Launch email to subscribers – welcome, highlights, special offer (if any).
    *   **Blog:** “We’re Live! Introducing [Product Name]” -  Detailed overview, key features, and a call to action.
    *   **Priority:** Respond to immediate questions and comments on social media.
*   **Oct 27th:**  “Behind the Scenes” -  Short video or post about the product’s creation.  Humanize the brand. (Social Media)
*   **Oct 28th:**  Top 3 Reasons Why You Need [Product Name] – Concise benefits. (Social Media, Blog - short post)
*   **Oct 29th:**  Tutorial – Simple “How To” – Show a key basic function. (Social Media – Short Video, Blog - Link to longer tutorial)
*   **Oct 30th:**  Q&A – Address common questions based on early inquiries. (Social Media – Live Session, Blog - FAQ)


**Week 2: (Nov 2nd - Nov 8th) –  Deepening Engagement & Addressing Questions**

*   **Nov 2nd:**  User Spotlight – Feature a beta tester or early adopter (with their permission).  (Social Media, Blog)
*   **Nov 3rd:**  “Did You Know?” – Highlight a lesser-known feature. (Social Media, Email)
*   **Nov 4th:**  Blog:  "Common Mistakes to Avoid When Starting With [Product Name]" – Proactive support.
*   **Nov 5th:**  Tutorial - More complex function. (Social Media – Video, Blog – Link)
*   **Nov 6th:**  Run a Poll/Quiz: “What’s your biggest challenge with [related problem]?” (Social Media) - Use results for future content ideas.
*   **Nov 7th:**  Feedback Request -  Directly ask users for feedback on their experience
