# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, covering different tiers, rewards, and considerations, along with a sample structure you can adapt:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** "Spread the Word," "Refer & Reward," "Unlock Rewards," (Choose something catchy and relevant to your brand)
* **Branding:** Integrate the program into your overall brand messaging – emphasize the value of community and word-of-mouth.


**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Who are you referring to?** (Existing customers, specific demographics, etc.)
* **What do you want to achieve?** (New customer acquisition, increased brand awareness, customer loyalty, etc.)

**3. Referral Structure – Tiered System (Recommended)**

A tiered system motivates participants at different levels. Here’s a proposed structure:

* **Tier 1: Early Birds (Low Incentive)**
    * **Referrer:** Existing Customer
    * **Referee:** New Customer
    * **Reward for Referrer:** 10% off their next purchase  (or $5 credit)
    * **Reward for Referee:** 10% off their first purchase
    * **Activation:** Easy sharing via unique referral link or code.
* **Tier 2: The Advocates (Mid-Level Incentive)**
    * **Referrer:** Customer who has made 2+ purchases
    * **Referee:** New Customer
    * **Reward for Referrer:** 15% off their next purchase *OR* a free gift (e.g., a small accessory related to your product)
    * **Reward for Referee:** 15% off their first purchase + Free Shipping
    * **Activation:**  Same as Tier 1, plus potentially an email template for the referrer to easily share.
* **Tier 3: The Champions (High Incentive)**
    * **Referrer:** Customer who has made 5+ purchases
    * **Referee:** New Customer
    * **Reward for Referrer:** 20% off their next purchase *OR* a higher-value gift (e.g., a premium product, exclusive access to a sale)
    * **Reward for Referee:** 20% off their first purchase + Free Shipping +  Early access to new products/features
    * **Activation:**  Dedicated support for Champions – perhaps a phone call to discuss their experience.


**4. Incentive Types (Mix & Match)**

* **Discounts:** (Percentage or fixed amount off) – Most common and effective.
* **Credits/Store Credit:** Flexible and allow customers to choose what they want.
* **Free Products/Samples:** Great for driving trial and generating excitement.
* **Free Shipping:**  Always a popular incentive, especially for e-commerce.
* **Exclusive Access:** Early access to new products, sales, or features.
* **VIP Treatment:**  Priority customer support, personalized recommendations, or invitations to exclusive events.
* **Points System:**  Referrals earn points that can be redeemed for rewards (can be combined with other incentives).

**5.  Referral Methods & Tracking**

* **Unique Referral Links:**  The most trackable method. Generate a unique link for each referrer.
* **Unique Referral Codes:**  Good for SMS or email sharing.
* **Social Media Sharing:**  Enable easy sharing on platforms (Facebook, Twitter, etc.) with pre-written messages.
* **Email Campaigns:**  Automated emails to existing customers prompting them to refer.
* **Tracking Software:** Crucial! Use referral marketing software (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Extole) or integrate with your e-commerce platform (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.) to accurately track referrals, conversions, and rewards
