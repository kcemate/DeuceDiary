But the real strangeness begins when Deuce discovers a locked diary belonging to his deceased grandfather – a diary filled with increasingly unsettling entries detailing a life spent observing a peculiar, silent creature that haunts the edges of town. As Deuce delves deeper into the diary’s secrets, he begins to experience unsettling visions and a growing sense of dread, questioning his own reality and the truth behind his family's past. 

*Deuce Diary* is a poignant exploration of isolation, memory, and the unsettling beauty of the overlooked.
