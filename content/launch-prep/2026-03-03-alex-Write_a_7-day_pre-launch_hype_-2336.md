Okay, here’s a 7-day pre-launch hype campaign for "Deuce Diary," designed to build excitement across Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, and Reddit. **Overall Campaign Theme:** “Uncover the Secrets. Before the Diary Opens.”  (Focus on the hidden story and the reader's role in uncovering it.)

**Hashtags:** #DeuceDiary #SecretDiary #MysteryReads #DarkAcademia #HiddenSecrets #BookTok #IndieLit #ComingSoon

**Day 1: The Enigma Begins - Twitter & Reddit**

* **Twitter:** (8:00 AM) - Simple, cryptic image: Close-up of an antique, leather-bound diary with slightly faded gold lettering. Text: "Something is hidden. Something is waiting. #DeuceDiary"
* **Reddit (r/fantasy, r/books, r/indieauthors):** (10:00 AM) - Post a brief, intriguing paragraph teasing the world and asking for initial thoughts. "A small, isolated school. A collection of students. And a diary that holds a dangerous secret.  What kind of mysteries would you expect to find within?  Drop your theories below. #DeuceDiary" – Include a blurred, atmospheric image of the school grounds.
* **Instagram:** (4:00 PM) - Static image of the diary - similar to Twitter's, slightly more polished. Caption: “The story begins… #DeuceDiary”

**Day 2: Character Hints - TikTok & Instagram**

* **TikTok:** (12:00 PM) - Short, atmospheric video (15-30 seconds) using trending audio. Quick cuts showing gothic architecture, flickering candlelight, rain-streaked windows. Text overlay: "They are not what they seem..." – End with a close-up of the diary. Challenge:  Ask users to guess the main character's archetype ("Is this a dark academia rebel? A troubled scholar?"). Use trending sounds related to mystery or suspense. #DeuceDiary #DarkAcademia #BookTok
* **Instagram:** (6:00 PM) - Carousel post. 
    * Slide 1: Image of a character silhouette, partially obscured by shadow.
    * Slide 2: Text overlay: "Meet [Character Name - teased, not revealed]… They guard a secret. #DeuceDiary"
    * Slide 3: Quote from a very short, evocative snippet of the book’s writing style. 

**Day 3: Reveal a Detail - Twitter & Reddit**

* **Twitter:** (9:00 AM) - Reveal a single, specific detail from the book's world - e.g., “The school’s library is rumored to contain books that rewrite themselves. #DeuceDiary” – Post a related, slightly unsettling image (e.g., a library shelf with oddly arranged books).
* **Reddit:** (3:00 PM) -  Post a poll: "If you were a student at [School Name - teased name], what subject would you *avoid*? (Options: Alchemy, Demonology, Ancient Languages, Astronomy)" - This encourages engagement and gives you insight into reader interests.


**Day 4: Influencer Outreach & TikTok Trend - TikTok & Instagram**

* **TikTok:** (1:00 PM) - Launch a simple challenge:  "Recreate your favorite dark academia aesthetic! Use #DeuceDiary and show us your vibe."  (Encourage users to show off books, candles, dark clothes, etc.)
* **Instagram & TikTok:** Reach out to 2-3 micro-influencers
