**Synopsis:**

Leo is adrift, reeling from the recent death of his mother and carrying a heavy burden of resentment towards his absentee grandfather. He’s sent to the Dust Devil Motel in the remote Mojave Desert to sort through his grandfather's belongings and, more importantly, to understand the man he barely knew. What he finds is more than just dusty furniture and faded photographs – he finds echoes of his grandfather’s past, a persistent feeling of being watched, and unsettling glimpses of a woman who may or may not have died decades ago. 

As Leo delves deeper into the motel’s history, he begins to uncover a tragic love story and a dangerous obsession.  He also starts experiencing inexplicable phenomena – objects moving, voices in the darkness, and a growing sense that the desert itself is trying to communicate.  Forced to confront his grief and his family’s secrets, Leo must learn to trust his instincts and discover his own strength before the past consumes him entirely.
