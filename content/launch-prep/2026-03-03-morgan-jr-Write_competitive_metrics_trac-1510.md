## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competition to inform strategic decisions, improve your offerings, and ultimately, gain a competitive advantage.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape and identify opportunities for differentiation and improvement.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors (direct & indirect)
    * Track their pricing, promotions, and product offerings.
    * Monitor their marketing and sales strategies.
    * Identify their strengths and weaknesses.
    * Detect emerging trends and technologies.
    * Inform product development, marketing, and sales strategies.


**II. Competitor Identification & Segmentation:**

* **Step 1: List Competitors:**
    * **Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi)
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions to the same customer need. (e.g., Streaming services vs. traditional cable TV)
    * **Potential Competitors:** Companies that could easily enter your market.
* **Step 2: Segment Competitors:** Group them based on:
    * **Market Share:** High, Medium, Low
    * **Product/Service Offering:**  Similar, Different
    * **Target Audience:**  Overlapping, Distinct
    * **Pricing Strategy:** Premium, Competitive, Discount
    * **Marketing Channels:**  Digital, Traditional, Social Media
* **Example Table:**

|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Company A | 25% | Product X | Millennials | Premium | Social Media, Influencers | Strong Brand Recognition | High Prices |
| Company B | 10% | Product Y | Gen Z | Competitive | YouTube, Paid Ads | Innovative Product | Limited Reach |
