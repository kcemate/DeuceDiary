* **Monitoring & Alerting:** Implement robust server monitoring, app store review monitoring, and social media listening tools *before* launch.

**II. Crisis Scenarios & Response Plans**

**A. Server Crash/Technical Issues**

* **Severity:** High - Immediate Impact on User Experience
* **Trigger:**  Significant drop in app availability, error messages, slow loading times.
* **Phase 1 (0-2 Hours):**
    * **Confirm & Investigate:** Immediately verify the crash with the development team. Identify the root cause (e.g., DDoS, code bug, database issue).
    * **Public Statement (Twitter/Status Page):** "We’re aware of an issue affecting Deuce Diary. Our team is working to resolve it ASAP. We’ll provide updates here: [Link to Status Page]."
    * **Prioritize Fix:** Dev team focused solely on resolving the issue.
* **Phase 2 (2-6 Hours):**
    * **Regular Updates:**  Every 30-60 minutes via Twitter/Status Page, acknowledging progress.  Be realistic – avoid overpromising.
    * **User Support (Discord):**  Dedicated support team addressing user concerns and troubleshooting.
* **Phase 3 (6+ Hours):**
    * **Full Resolution & Rollback:** Announce full resolution, thank users for patience, and outline steps taken to prevent recurrence. Consider a small in-app reward for affected users.

**B. 1-Star Reviews (Massive Volume)**

* **Severity:** Medium - Damage to Reputation
* **Trigger:** Sudden influx of negative 1-star reviews – indicative of a widespread problem or unmet expectations.
* **Phase 1 (0-24 Hours):**
    * **Analyze Reviews:**  Identify common themes in the negative reviews.  Are they related to gameplay, UI, bugs, or monetization?
    * **Prioritize Fixes:** Dev team focuses on addressing the most prevalent issues based on review feedback.
    * **Respond to Key Reviews:**  Select a few *genuine* 1-star reviews and respond constructively, offering support and explaining ongoing improvements.  (Don’t get into arguments).
* **Phase 2 (24-72 Hours):**
    * **Monitor & Iterate:** Continue analyzing reviews and adjusting the development roadmap.
    * **Promote Positive Feedback:**  Highlight positive reviews (when genuinely received) to balance the negative.
