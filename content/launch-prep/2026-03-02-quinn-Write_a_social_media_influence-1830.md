**I. General Principles for Outreach**

* **Do Your Research:**  Seriously. Don't send generic messages.  Understand their content, engagement rate, audience demographics, and values.
* **Be Authentic:**  Micro-influencers value genuine connections.  Don't sound like a corporate bot.
* **Start Small:**  Focus on micro-influencers (1,000 - 10,000 followers) – they tend to have higher engagement rates and are more approachable.
* **Personalize, Personalize, Personalize:**  Mention something specific you noticed about their content.
* **Be Responsive:**  If they respond, engage with their reply!
