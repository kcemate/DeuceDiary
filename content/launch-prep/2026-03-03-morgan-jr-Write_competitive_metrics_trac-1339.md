## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competitors to inform your strategy and identify opportunities for growth. It focuses on key areas and provides actionable steps for tracking, analyzing, and reacting to competitive data.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To proactively understand the competitive landscape, identify emerging threats and opportunities, and inform strategic decision-making to maintain or improve market share.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths & weaknesses.
    * Track competitor pricing, promotions, and product changes.
    * Analyze competitor marketing strategies and messaging.
    *  Identify potential disruptive technologies or trends.
    *  Benchmark your performance against key competitors.


**II. Key Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Identify Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services targeting the same customer base.
* **Identify Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions that address the same customer needs.
* **Prioritization (Based on Relevance):**
    * **High Priority (Top 3):** Competitors with significant market share, strong brand recognition, and direct product overlap. (Requires most in-depth tracking)
    * **Medium Priority (4-6):** Competitors with noticeable presence, evolving product lines, and potential for disruption.
    * **Low Priority (7+):** Niche competitors or emerging brands that require occasional monitoring.
