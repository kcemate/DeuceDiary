**Template 1: Short & Sweet - For Initial Contact (Influencers & Smaller Brands)**

We’re huge fans of your work and especially appreciate your focus on [Mention specific content area - e.g., mindful living, healthy recipes, yoga].

At [Your Brand Name], we [Briefly describe your brand - 1-2 sentences]. We believe our values align, and we’d love to explore a potential collaboration. 

Would you be open to a quick chat to discuss ideas?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Website]

**Why it works:**  Low pressure, immediately shows you've done your research, and includes a clear call to action.
