## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy to build a thriving and engaged community around *Deuce Diary* across Discord and Reddit, fostering a space for fans to connect, discuss, and celebrate the series.  It focuses on consistent content, clear moderation, and targeted engagement tactics, all tracked with measurable growth metrics.

**I. Overall Vision:**

To cultivate a passionate and supportive community around *Deuce Diary*, recognizing it as more than just a fanbase but a collective of individuals sharing a love for the story, characters, and unique art style.  We aim for a community that’s welcoming, respectful, and fosters creative expression.


**II. Platforms & Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):**
    * **Purpose:** Real-time discussion, direct interaction with the creator (potential), deeper engagement, and building closer relationships.
    * **Server Structure:**
        * **#general-chat:** Main channel for casual conversation, sharing memes, and announcements.
        * **#deuce-discussion:** Dedicated to in-depth discussions about the story, characters, lore, and theories.
        * **#art-showcase:**  A space for members to share their own *Deuce Diary* inspired art (fanart, edits, writing, etc.).  Strictly moderated to avoid overwhelming the channel.
        * **#character-specific:** Channels for individual characters (Deuce, Suki, etc.) – allows for deeper fan interpretations.
        * **#requests-and-suggestions:**  A channel for fans to offer constructive feedback or suggestions to the creator.
        * **#off-topic:** A designated space for non-*Deuce Diary* related conversations.
    * **Frequency:** Active moderation, daily activity checks, potentially daily “Ask Deuce” Q&A sessions (depending on the creator’s time).


* **Reddit (Secondary Reach & Long-Form Discussions):**
    * **Purpose:**  Wider reach, promotion of Discord, facilitating longer-form discussions, and archiving of content.
    * **Subreddit Name:**  r/DeuceDiary
    * **Content Strategy:**  Regularly post links to Discord activity, interesting fan theories, highlight fan art, and share news about the series. Encourage discussions in threads.
    * **Reddit Engagement:**  Active moderation, responding to comments, and promoting engaging discussion topics.


**III. Content Strategy:**

* **Creator-Led Content (High Priority):**
    * **Behind-the-Scenes:**  Creator provides insights into the creation process - sketches, concept art, development notes.
    * **Q&A Sessions:**  Live sessions on Discord and/or Reddit, addressing fan questions directly.
    * **Lore Expansion:**  Creator offers hints and expansions on the series’ lore.
    * **Character Spotlights:**  Highlighting characters with in-depth analysis and discussion prompts.
* **Community-Generated Content (Medium Priority - Encouraged):**
    * **Fan Art Contests:**  Regular contests with prizes (e.g., featured spot on Discord, shoutout from creator).
    * **Fan Fiction Challenges:**  Prompts to inspire fan fiction writing.
    * **Theory Discussions:**  Encourage fans to share and debate theories.
    * **Cosplay/Roleplay Content:**  Sharing photos and stories of cosplay/roleplay experiences.


**IV. Moderator Guidelines:**

* **Core Values:** Respect, inclusivity, constructive discussion, and adherence to the series’ tone.
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **Enforcing Rules:**  Swiftly and consistently enforcing rules against harassment, hate speech, spam, and NSFW content.
    * **Community Management:**  Starting discussions, welcoming new members, and highlighting community content.
