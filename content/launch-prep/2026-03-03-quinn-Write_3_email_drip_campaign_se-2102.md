Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for approximately 5-7 emails over 2-3 weeks. I’ll include details on the goal, target audience, content, and timing.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Goal: Establish Connection & Build Trust)**

* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who signed up through a website form, contest, or lead magnet.
* **Goal:** Introduce the brand, build trust, and nurture the subscriber's interest.
* **Timeline:** 7 Days
* **Email Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Immediately after signup): Welcome & Thank You:**  (Day 1)
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:** Warm welcome, reiterate their signup choice, briefly introduce your brand's mission & values. Offer a small, instant reward (e.g., a free checklist, discount code) if applicable.  Link to your ‘About Us’ page.
    * **Email 2 (3 Days Later):  Problem/Solution Introduction:** (Day 4)
        * **Subject:** Struggling with [Pain Point Your Brand Solves]?
        * **Content:**  Identify a common pain point your target audience faces. Briefly acknowledge the problem and subtly introduce how your brand provides a solution.
    * **Email 3 (3 Days Later):  Highlight a Key Benefit:** (Day 7)
        * **Subject:**  Here’s How [Your Product/Service] Can Help You
        * **Content:** Deep dive into ONE key benefit of your product/service.  Use a testimonial, statistic, or short case study to illustrate the value.
* **Metrics to Track:** Open rates, click-through rates (to the website, specific blog posts, or product page).



**Campaign 2: Product Focused - Early Stage Consideration (Goal: Drive Interest & Product Awareness)**

* **Target Audience:** Subscribers who have shown interest in a specific product category. (This would be based on behavior - e.g., downloaded a related guide, visited a product page).
* **Goal:**  Highlight product features and benefits, show them the value and help them move towards consideration.
* **Timeline:** 14 Days
* **Email Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Immediately after Trigger):  "You Might Be Interested In..."** (Day 1)
       * **Subject:**  We Thought You’d Like This: [Product Name]
       * **Content:** Introduce the specific product the subscriber showed interest in. Quickly highlight 2-3 key features.
    * **Email 2 (4 Days Later):  Deep Dive into a Feature:** (Day 5)
       * **Subject:**  Unlock [Specific Feature] with [Product Name]
       * **Content:** Focus on ONE powerful feature, explain *how* it works, and its benefits.  Include a short video demo if possible.
    * **Email 3 (7 Days Later):  Comparison & Differentiation:** (Day 12)
       * **Subject:**  [Product Name] vs. The Competition - Here's What Makes Us Different
       * **Content:** Briefly compare your product to a competitor, focusing on your unique selling proposition (USP).  Don’t bash the competition – highlight your strengths.
    * **Email 4 (14 Days Later): Case Study or Customer Testimonial:** (Day 16)
       * **Subject:**  See How [Customer Name] Achieved [Result] with [Product Name]
       * **Content:** Share a real-life example of a customer successfully using the product.
