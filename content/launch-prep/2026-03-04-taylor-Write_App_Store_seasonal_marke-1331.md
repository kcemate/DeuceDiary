**I. Winter (November - February)**

* **Theme:** Cozy, Holiday, Resolution-Focused, Gift-Giving
* **Key Goals:** Drive downloads, promote gift-card/subscription options (if applicable), highlight holiday-themed features.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **November - Pre-Holiday Push:** (Starting around Black Friday/Cyber Monday)
        * **App Store Search Ads:**  Target keywords like "gift ideas," "holiday games," "winter activities."
        * **Social Media Campaigns:**  Run contests, giveaways, and promote holiday-themed content.  Influencer outreach.
        * **Email Marketing:**  Send out promotional emails to existing users.
    * **December - Peak Holiday Season:**
        * **Limited-Time Offers:**  Offer discounts, free trials, or bundled deals related to holiday needs.
        * **App Store Feature Placement:**  Work with the App Store team to try and get your app featured during the holidays.
        * **Holiday-Themed Creative:**  Use festive imagery and messaging in your app store listings and marketing materials.
    * **January - New Year’s Resolutions:**
        * **“Start Fresh” Messaging:**  Position your app as a tool to help users achieve their New Year’s resolutions. (Fitness apps, productivity apps, habit trackers are especially well-suited).
        * **Promote Premium Features:**  Offer special introductory pricing for premium features.
        * **User Testimonials:**  Showcase stories of users who have benefited from your app.
* **Content Ideas:**
    * Holiday-themed in-app events or challenges.
    * Tutorials on how to use your app to achieve New Year’s goals.
    * Blog posts and social media content about winter activities and wellness.
