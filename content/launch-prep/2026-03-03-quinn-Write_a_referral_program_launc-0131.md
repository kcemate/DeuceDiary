## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, leveraging a tiered system to incentivize both referrals and encourage viral growth. It addresses key aspects including rewards, mechanics, viral loop design, fraud prevention, and tracking.

**1. Program Name & Theme:** “Spread the Decoupling” -  Plays on the core philosophy of Deuce Diary and encourages sharing of mindful decision-making.

**2. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Short-Term (1 Month):**
    * Achieve 1,000 referrals generated.
    * Acquire 500 new paid subscribers.
    * Generate a 10% conversion rate from referred users to paid subscribers.
* **Mid-Term (3 Months):**
    * Reach 5,000 referrals.
    * Acquire 2,000 new paid subscribers.
    * Improve brand awareness and engagement through the referral program.
* **Key Metrics:** Referrals Generated, New Subscribers from Referrals, Conversion Rate (Referral -> Paid), Referral Rate (Overall), Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) through Referrals.


**3. Rewards Structure (Tiered System):**

| Tier | Referrer Achieves | Referee Receives |
|---|---|---|
| **Bronze (0-5 Referrals)** | 1 Month Free Premium Access | 10% off their first month’s premium access |
| **Silver (6-15 Referrals)** | 3 Months Free Premium Access | 20% off their first month’s premium access + free access to a bonus module |
| **Gold (16+ Referrals)** | 6 Months Free Premium Access + Exclusive Content Download | 30% off their first month’s premium access + access to an exclusive workshop/webinar |

* **Payment Method:** Credits towards Deuce Diary Premium subscriptions.
* **Rationale:** Tiered structure motivates increased effort to achieve higher rewards and creates a sense of accomplishment.

**4. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Initial Email Blast:**  Existing subscribers receive a personalized email announcing the program with a compelling invitation to share.
* **Social Media Campaign:**  Launch a coordinated campaign across all social media channels (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn) showcasing the benefits and sharing referral links. Use engaging visuals - short videos demonstrating Deuce Diary’s value.
* **In-App Promotion:** Highlight the referral program within the Deuce Diary app.
* **Referral Link Distribution:**  Provide easy-to-share referral links through:
    * **Dedicated Landing Page:** A central hub for the program with clear instructions and tracking.
    * **Email Signature:**  Encourage users to include their referral link in their email signatures.
    * **Social Media Profiles:**  Direct users to their referral link within their profiles.
    * **Within the App:**  A prominent “Refer a Friend” button.
* **Triggered Emails:**
    * **Referral Sent:**  Automated email to the referrer confirming their referral and providing them with instructions.
    * **Referee Signed Up:** Automated email to the referee welcoming them to Deuce Diary and explaining the benefits they've received.
    * **Reward Redemption Reminder:**  Send reminders to referrers about upcoming reward expiration dates.
