Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. This design will be adaptable to different businesses, so I'll provide options and considerations along the way.

**Program Name:** (Example) "Spread the Word & Get Rewarded!" or "[Your Brand Name] Advocates"

**1. Core Concept & Goals:**

* **Goal:** Drive new customer acquisition, increase brand awareness, build community, and reward loyal customers.
* **Target Audience:** Existing customers who are enthusiastic about your product/service.
* **Program Type:**  We’ll focus on a tiered system with increasing rewards based on referral volume and success.


**2. Referral Structure (Tiered System):**

* **Tier 1: The "Welcome" Tier (Easy Entry)**
    * **Referral:**  New customer refers a friend.
    * **Referral Method:** Simple link/code provided to the referrer. (e.g., “Get 10% off your next order when you share this code: WELCOME10”)
    * **Reward for Referrer:** 10% off their next purchase. (This is a low-risk incentive to encourage initial participation)
    * **Reward for Referred Friend:**  Welcome discount (e.g., 10% off their first order).
* **Tier 2: The "Bronze" Tier (Medium Volume)**
    * **Requirement:**  Referrer has made at least one purchase.
    * **Referral:** New customer refers a friend.
    * **Reward for Referrer:** 15% off their next purchase.
    * **Reward for Referred Friend:** Welcome discount + Free shipping on their first order.
* **Tier 3: The "Silver" Tier (High Volume)**
    * **Requirement:** Referrer has made at least 3 purchases.
    * **Referral:** New customer refers a friend.
    * **Reward for Referrer:** $25 Store Credit
    * **Reward for Referred Friend:** Welcome discount + Free shipping + Small Gift (e.g., a branded sticker or sample)
* **Tier 4: The "Gold" Tier (Top Performers)**
   * **Requirement:** Referrer has made at least 10 purchases
   * **Referral:** New customer refers a friend.
   * **Reward for Referrer:** 20% Off their next purchase + Exclusive access to new product launches.
   * **Reward for Referred Friend:** Welcome discount + Free shipping +  A higher-value gift (e.g., a premium product sample)

**3. Incentive Details & Types:**

* **Discount Codes:** (Most common and versatile) – Percentage discounts, fixed amount discounts.
* **Free Products/Services:**  Samples, mini-versions of your core offering.
* **Free Shipping:**  A consistently attractive incentive, especially for e-commerce.
* **Store Credit:**  Allows customers to redeem rewards in your store.
* **Exclusive Access:** Beta access to new features, early product releases, or VIP events.
* **Gift Cards:**  Can be appealing for a broad audience.
* **Charitable Donation:** (Optional) –  Donate a percentage of the referral fee to a charity – great for socially conscious brands.


**4. Tracking & Technology:**

* **Unique Referral Links/Codes:** Essential for accurately tracking referrals.
* **Referral Software:** Consider using a referral program platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, ReferralStream) – These can automate the process, track referrals, and manage rewards.  If budget is a concern, many e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce) have referral apps.
* **Manual Tracking (Starting Out):**  Spreadsheets can work initially, but it’s prone to errors
