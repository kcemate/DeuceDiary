Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers, incentives, and considerations for making it effective:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** (Examples) “Refer & Reward,” “Spread the Word,” “[Your Brand Name] Connect,” “Inner Circle Referral”
* **Visuals:**  A clean, branded graphic representing sharing and rewards.

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **How it Works:** Customers refer new users to your product/service.
* **Tracking:** Use a referral tracking platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Mention, or integrate with your CRM/Marketing Automation software). These platforms automate the process, track referrals, and handle payouts.
* **Terms & Conditions:**  Clearly state the rules – who can refer, how many referrals, when rewards are issued, etc. (Legal review recommended).


**3. Referral Levels & Incentives (Tiered System - Recommended)**

This is where it gets interesting! Tiered incentives drive more engagement.

* **Level 1: Basic Referral (Easy to Earn)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  [Small Incentive - e.g., 10% off next purchase, Free Shipping, $5 Credit]
    * **New Referral Reward:** [Small Incentive - e.g., 5% off, Free Trial Extension,  Exclusive Content]
    * **Frequency:** Can be earned for each successful referral.
* **Level 2:  Active Referral (Increased Effort)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** [Mid-Tier Incentive - e.g., 20% off, Free Premium Feature for 1 month,  $10 Credit]
    * **New Referral Reward:** [Mid-Tier Incentive - e.g.,  Discount on a bundled product, Early access to new releases, A small gift (branded item)]
    * **Requirements:**  Must have referred at least 2 people in Level 1.
* **Level 3:  Elite Referral (High Engagement)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** [High-Tier Incentive - e.g.,  Significant discount (30-50%), Exclusive VIP access,  Personalized onboarding]
    * **New Referral Reward:** [High-Tier Incentive – e.g.,  Premium product/service for a longer period, Gift card, Featured on social media]
    * **Requirements:** Must have referred at least 5 people in Levels 1 & 2.

**4. Incentive Types - Beyond Discounts**

* **Cash Rewards:**  (If your business model allows –  often more complex legally)
* **Gift Cards:** (Amazon, Starbucks, etc., – broad appeal)
* **Exclusive Content/Access:**  Beta access, early releases, webinars, tutorials.
* **Physical Products:** Branded merchandise, sample products (if relevant).
* **Experiences:** (If relevant - e.g., a VIP event, personalized consultation)
* **Donations to Charity:**  (Partner with a charity and donate a percentage of the referral reward – good for social good brands)
