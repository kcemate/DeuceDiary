**I. Phase 1: Assessment & Understanding (Weeks 1-4)**

* **Goal:** Deeply understand current revenue generation, identify pain points, and define key performance indicators (KPIs).
* **Activities:**
    * **Data Audit:**
        * **Sales Data:** Analyze historical sales data (transaction history, product/service performance, customer segments, channels, seasonality). Look for trends, drop-off rates, and successful patterns.
        * **Marketing Data:** Review marketing campaign performance (channels, ad spend, targeting, messaging, conversion rates).
        * **Customer Data:**  Segment customers by demographics, behavior, purchase history, lifetime value (LTV).  Understand customer acquisition cost (CAC).
        * **Website/App Analytics:** Google Analytics (or similar) – Track user behavior, bounce rates, time on site, conversion paths.
    * **Customer Research:**
        * **Surveys:**  Gather feedback on pricing, value perceptions, and unmet needs.
        * **Customer Interviews:** Conduct 1:1 conversations to understand motivations, frustrations, and desired experiences.
        * **Usability Testing:** (If applicable) Test website/app user flows to identify friction points.
    * **Competitive Analysis:** Identify competitor pricing, promotions, and customer offerings.
* **Key Metrics:**
    * **Overall Revenue:** Baseline for tracking progress.
    * **Average Order Value (AOV):**  How much customers are spending per transaction.
    * **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**  Cost to acquire a new customer.
    * **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**  Predictive value of a customer over their entire relationship.
    * **Conversion Rates:**  Percentage of visitors/leads who complete desired actions (e.g., purchase, sign-up).
    * **Channel Effectiveness:**  Which marketing channels are driving the most revenue?
