**About the Book:**

Deuce – a name he chose himself – is trying to rebuild his life after his best friend, Leo, died in a car accident. He’s living in their shared hometown, working a dead-end job, and attempting to navigate the confusing world of dating, primarily through obsessive journaling. When he meets Maya, a captivating artist who seems to see a different side of him, Deuce finds himself unexpectedly drawn to her. 

However, the shadows of Leo’s death linger, and Deuce struggles to reconcile his desire for connection with the overwhelming grief that threatens to consume him. *Deuce Diary* is a powerful meditation on loss, memory, and the desperate need for understanding, told in the intensely personal and often unsettling format of a young man’s private journal.
