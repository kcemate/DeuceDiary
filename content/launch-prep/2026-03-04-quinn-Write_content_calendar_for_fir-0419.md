Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week, with estimated frequency and content ideas. This is a template – you’ll need to tailor it to your specific product, target audience, and marketing goals. 

**Assumptions:**

* **You've launched!** This calendar assumes the product is live and available for purchase/use.
* **You have basic marketing channels:** Website, Social Media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn – adjust based on your target audience), Email Marketing.
* **Goal:** Drive awareness, encourage trial/adoption, and gather initial feedback.

**Overall Tone:**  Enthusiastic, helpful, and focused on solving the user’s pain point.

---

**Week 1: The “Wow” & Immediate Value**

* **Frequency:** Daily Social Media Posts, 2-3 Email Sends
* **Focus:**  Celebrating launch, highlighting key features, providing immediate value.
* **Content Ideas:**
    * **Monday:**  Launch Announcement – Big, exciting post across all channels. Include a video/GIF demonstrating the core functionality. (Social Media - All Platforms)
    * **Tuesday:**  "Why We Built This" - Share the problem you’re solving and your passion. (Blog Post, Social Media - Facebook/LinkedIn)
    * **Wednesday:** Feature Spotlight – Focus on ONE key feature and how it benefits the user. (Social Media - Instagram Reels/TikTok, Short Video, Twitter/X)
    * **Thursday:**  Tutorial/Quick Start Guide -  A short video or blog post showing users how to do something simple. (YouTube, Website, Social Media - All Platforms)
    * **Friday:**  Customer Testimonial (if available - even a beta user) or Case Study -  Build trust. (Social Media - Facebook, Website)
    * **Saturday:**  Behind-the-Scenes – Show the team working, a glimpse of the process (Humanizes the brand). (Instagram Stories, Social Media - Facebook)
    * **Sunday:**  Reminder – Link to purchase/sign up, reiterate the value proposition. (Email – 1 Send)



**Week 2:  Deep Dive & Community Building**

* **Frequency:** 3-4 Social Media Posts, 1 Email Send
* **Focus:**  Deeper dives into features, starting community engagement.
* **Content Ideas:**
    * **Monday:**  FAQ Post – Address common questions about the product. (Blog Post, Social Media - Facebook)
    * **Tuesday:** Feature Deep Dive - Focus on a slightly less obvious, but still valuable feature. (Social Media - Instagram Carousel, Twitter/X Thread)
    * **Wednesday:**  User Tip -  Share a clever way to use the product. (Social Media - Instagram Stories, TikTok)
    * **Thursday:**  Run a Poll/Question - "What's your biggest challenge related to [your product’s area]?" (Social Media - Instagram/Facebook) – Start gathering insights.
    * **Friday:**  Welcome New Users – Dedicated post welcoming new users and offering support. (Social Media – All Platforms)
    * **Saturday:**  User-Generated Content Prompt -  Encourage users to share how they're using the product with a specific hashtag. (Social Media - All Platforms)
    * **Sunday:**  Email – Highlight a success story from the week or promote a limited-time offer (1 Send)

* **Frequency:** 2-3 Social Media Posts, 1 Email Send
* **Focus:**  Handling objections, providing more in-depth information, establishing expertise.
