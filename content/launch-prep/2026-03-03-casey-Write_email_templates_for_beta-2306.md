**To confirm your participation, please reply to this email.**  We’ll then send you a more detailed guide and instructions.

Thank you for your support!

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```

Let us know if you’re in by replying to this email!

Cheers,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```
