**Assumptions:**

* **You’ve already had a pre-launch phase (teaser content, email list building).** This calendar focuses on solidifying momentum after the launch.
* **You have a clear understanding of your target audience.** This will inform the tone and content you create.
* **You have tracking in place (Google Analytics, social media analytics) to measure success.**


**Overall Theme for Month 1: “Welcome & Get Started”** – Focus on onboarding, key benefits, and building excitement.

**Week 1: Launch Confirmation & Initial Excitement (Focus: Awareness & Acquisition)**

* **Monday:** **Launch Announcement (All Channels):**  A big, bold announcement across all platforms. Include a link to your website and key features.  *Format:* Video, Image, Blog Post, Social Media Posts (Variety of formats - short video, carousel, text).  *Goal:*  Drive initial traffic and shares.
* **Tuesday:** **Behind-the-Scenes:** A “meet the team” post, a quick story about the development process, or a peek at the office. *Format:* Instagram Story, short video, blog post snippet. *Goal:* Humanize the brand.
* **Wednesday:** **Feature Spotlight 1:**  Highlight a *single*, core feature and explain its biggest benefit. *Format:*  Short video demo, infographic, blog post. *Goal:* Educate about value.
* **Thursday:** **User Testimonial (if available):** Share a quote from an early tester or beta user. *Format:* Quote graphic, short video testimonial. *Goal:* Build social proof.
* **Friday:** **Welcome Offer/Promotion:** Launch a limited-time discount, free trial extension, or bonus for new users. *Format:* Social Media Promotion, Email Blast. *Goal:* Drive conversions.

**Week 2: Onboarding & Quick Wins (Focus: Activation & Engagement)**

* **Monday:** **Onboarding Tutorial (Video - All Channels):**  A short, step-by-step video demonstrating how to get started with the product/service. *Format:* YouTube, embedded on website, shared across social media. *Goal:* Reduce friction & improve user experience.
* **Tuesday:** **"Did You Know?" Tip:** Share a quick, helpful tip related to a specific feature. *Format:* Short social media post, GIF, image with text overlay. *Goal:* Increase engagement and show ongoing value.
* **Wednesday:** **Customer Question/FAQ:** Address a common question people might have. *Format:* Blog post, video answering a frequently asked question. *Goal:* Provide support and address concerns.
* **Thursday:** **User-Generated Content Contest (Start):**  Encourage users to share their experiences using a specific hashtag. *Format:* Announcement post, guidelines for contest. *Goal:*  Generate buzz, build community.
* **Friday:** **Behind-the-Scenes (Team Highlight):**  Focus on a different team member or their role. *Format:*  Short interview, Instagram Story take over. *Goal:*  Humanize the brand.
