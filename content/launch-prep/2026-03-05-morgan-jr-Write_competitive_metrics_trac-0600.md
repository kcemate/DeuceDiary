**I. Executive Summary**

This Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan aims to provide a structured approach to understanding and responding to the competitive landscape.  By consistently monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs) against our competitors, we can identify trends, opportunities, and threats, ultimately informing strategic decisions and driving improved performance. This plan emphasizes data-driven insights and actionable recommendations.

**II. Goals & Objectives**

* **Primary Goal:**  Gain a proactive understanding of competitor activities and their impact on our business.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strategies and tactics.
    * Track competitor performance across relevant areas.
    *  Uncover gaps in our own offerings and identify potential areas for innovation.
    *  React quickly to competitor moves and maintain a competitive advantage.
    *  Inform strategic planning, product development, marketing, and sales decisions.

**III. Phase 1: Identification & Prioritization (Weeks 1-4)**

* **1.1. Competitor Identification:**
    * **Tier 1 Competitors:** Direct competitors – those offering similar products/services to the same target market. (List 3-5)
    * **Tier 2 Competitors:** Indirect competitors – those serving similar customer needs but through a different offering. (List 5-10)
    * **Tier 3 Competitors:** Emerging or niche competitors with potential to disrupt the market. (List 3-5)
* **1.2. Prioritization Criteria:**
    * **Market Share:**  Identify the biggest players.
    * **Growth Rate:** Which competitors are expanding rapidly?
    * **Innovation:** Which are introducing new features or technologies?
    * **Customer Perception:**  Analyze reviews and social media sentiment.
    * **Strategic Significance:** Which competitors pose the greatest risk or opportunity to our business?
* **1.3. Data Sources:**
    * **Website Monitoring:**  Regularly check competitor websites for new products, pricing changes, promotions, and blog posts. (Tools: SimilarWeb, SEMrush, Ahrefs)
    * **Social Media Monitoring:** Track competitor activity on platforms relevant to your industry. (Tools: Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Mention)
    * **Industry Reports & Analyst Research:**  Subscribe to relevant industry reports and analyst research.
    * **Customer Feedback:**  Analyze customer reviews, surveys, and support tickets related to competitors.
    * **Public Filings & Press Releases:**  Monitor competitor announcements.
