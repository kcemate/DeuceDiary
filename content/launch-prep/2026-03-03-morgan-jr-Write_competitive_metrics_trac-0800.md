## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to consistently track and analyze your competition to inform strategic decisions and maintain a competitive edge.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To proactively identify opportunities and threats presented by competitors, enabling us to refine our strategies and maintain (or improve) our market position.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns, etc.)
    * Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Benchmark our performance against competitors.
    * Identify potential disruptive innovations.
    * Inform product development, marketing, and sales strategies.


**II. Scope & Competitors:**

* **Define “Competitor”:** Be clear about who constitutes a competitor. This isn't just direct competitors but also adjacent players offering similar solutions or targeting the same customer segment.
* **Tiered Competitor List:** Categorize competitors based on their influence and potential impact:
    * **Tier 1 (Direct):** Companies offering similar products/services and directly competing for the same customers. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi) - *Highest Focus*
    * **Tier 2 (Adjacent):** Companies offering related products/services or targeting the same customer segment but with a slightly different value proposition. (e.g., Spotify vs. Apple Music) - *Moderate Focus*
    * **Tier 3 (Potential):** Newer players or companies with the potential to grow and become a significant threat. (e.g., a new social media platform) - *Low Focus - Monitor Regularly*
* **List of Competitors:** Maintain a dynamic list, updating as needed. (Use a spreadsheet, CRM, or dedicated competitive intelligence tool)
