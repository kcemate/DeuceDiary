## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a structured approach to tracking and analyzing your competitors' activities, allowing you to stay ahead of the curve, identify opportunities, and inform strategic decisions.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Gain a comprehensive understanding of the competitive landscape to improve [Your Company Name]'s strategic positioning and performance.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors (direct and indirect)
    * Track competitor activities across key areas (e.g., product development, marketing, pricing, customer service)
    * Analyze competitor strengths & weaknesses
    * Identify emerging trends and potential threats/opportunities
    * Inform strategic decisions related to product development, marketing campaigns, pricing strategies, and overall market positioning.


**II. Key Competitors Identification & Categorization:**

* **Step 1: Initial Identification:** Brainstorm a list of all potential competitors – those directly offering similar products/services and those serving similar customer needs.
* **Step 2: Categorization:** Group competitors based on:
    * **Direct Competitors:**  Offer similar products/services to the same target audience. (High priority)
    * **Indirect Competitors:**  Offer alternative solutions addressing the same customer need. (Medium priority)
    * **Future Competitors:**  Companies with the potential to enter your market. (Low priority - monitor regularly)
* **Step 3: Documentation:**  Create a spreadsheet or database documenting each competitor with key information:
    * **Company Name & Website**
    * **Products/Services Offered**
    * **Target Market**
    * **Business Model**
    * **Company Size & Revenue (if available)**
