Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health & wellness brands, categorized by approach and with variations to suit different levels of engagement. Remember to **personalize** these heavily based on your research of the potential partner – a generic email won’t cut it!

**1. The Casual & Curious Approach (Good for Smaller Brands, Micro-Influencers)**

**Subject: Collaboration Ideas - [Your Brand] & [Their Brand]**

**Template:**

Hi [Name],

I've been a big fan of your work on [mention something specific you admire - their content, product, mission, etc.]! I especially loved [cite a specific example].

At [Your Brand], we’re focused on [brief, compelling description of your brand - 1-2 sentences].  We think our audiences could really benefit from each other’s offerings.

I'd love to chat briefly about potentially exploring some collaboration opportunities – maybe a shared giveaway, a guest post, or simply connecting to share some resources.

You can learn more about us here: [Link to Your Website]

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]

**Why it works:** Low pressure, shows you've done your research, and offers a range of possibilities.


**2. The Value-Driven Approach (Good for Businesses with a Clear Complementary Offering)**

**Subject: Partnership Opportunity:  Boosting [Their Audience’s Pain Point]**

**Template:**

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out from [Your Brand], a [describe your brand and what you offer - e.g., “plant-based supplement company focused on digestive health”].

We’ve noticed [Their Brand] is incredibly successful in helping [Their Target Audience] with [Specific Pain Point their audience faces – e.g., “managing stress,” “building healthy habits,” “improving sleep”].  

At [Your Brand], we offer [Specifically how your product/service solves a related problem - e.g., “a range of probiotics to support gut health,” “a guided meditation program,” “natural sleep aids”].

I believe a partnership could be mutually beneficial by [Explain the specific benefit - e.g., "providing your audience with a targeted solution to a common challenge," "driving traffic to both our sites"].

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to discuss this further?

[Link to your website]

Thanks for your time!
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]

**Why it works:** Directly addresses the partner’s audience's needs and shows how your brand provides a valuable solution.



**3. The Influencer-Focused Outreach (Essential for Micro & Mid-Tier Influencers)**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity with [Your Brand] - [Relevant Topic]**

**Template:**

Hi [Name],

I’m a huge admirer of your content on [Platform] – your [mention specific content style or niche - e.g., "honest reviews of wellness products," "creative healthy recipes," "inspiring fitness journeys"] really resonates with me and your audience.

At [Your Brand], we’re passionate about [brief brand description] and believe your voice would be a fantastic fit to promote our [specific product/service].

We’d love to explore a collaboration like:

*   **Sponsored Post:** Sharing our [product] in a dedicated post.
*   **Product Review:** Receiving a sample to share with your audience.
*   **Giveaway:** Hosting a giveaway featuring our products.

You can see some of our brand's vibe here: [Link to Your Website/Instagram]

Let me know if this is something you'd be interested in!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
