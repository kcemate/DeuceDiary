Okay, let's design a comprehensive analytics dashboard for the Deuce Diary launch. This design prioritizes understanding user behavior across the entire funnel, identifying valuable cohorts, and tracking the viral growth potential.

**Dashboard Name:** Deuce Diary - Launch Performance & Growth

**Overall Goal:** Provide real-time insights into user acquisition, engagement, retention, and viral spread to optimize marketing, product, and growth strategies.

**I. Dashboard Structure (Multi-Tab Design)**

We'll use a multi-tab structure to break down the information logically.

* **Tab 1: Executive Summary (Overview)** - High-level KPIs at a glance
* **Tab 2: Funnel Analysis** - Deep dive into the user journey
* **Tab 3: Cohort Analysis** - Understanding user groups and retention trends
* **Tab 4: Viral Coefficient Analysis** - Measuring and optimizing sharing behavior
* **Tab 5: Segmented Performance** - Drill-down by key user segments


**II. Tab Breakdown & Metrics**

**Tab 1: Executive Summary**

* **KPIs (Large, Clear Numbers):**
    * **Total Installs:**  Raw number of downloads/installs. (Time Period: Last 7 days, 30 days)
    * **New Signups:**  Number of users who completed the signup process. (Time Period: Last 7 days, 30 days)
    * **First Log Rate:** Percentage of signups who actually opened and used the app at least once. (Time Period: Last 7 days, 30 days)
    * **D7 Retention:** Percentage of users who opened the app 7 days after signup. (Time Period: Last 7 days, 30 days)
    * **D30 Retention:** Percentage of users who opened the app 30 days after signup. (Time Period: Last 7 days, 30 days)
    * **Premium Conversion Rate:** Percentage of users who upgraded to premium. (Time Period: Last 7 days, 30 days)
    * **Total Premium Users:**  Number of users subscribed to premium.
* **Trend Charts:**
    * Line chart showing the trend of each KPI over the last 30 days.
* **Sparklines:** Small, individual trend lines next to each KPI for visual monitoring.


**Tab 2: Funnel Analysis**

* **Visual:** Funnel chart depicting the conversion rates between each stage:
    * **Stage 1: Install** (Number of installs)
    * **Stage 2: Signup** (Number of signups)
    * **Stage 3: First Log** (Number of users who logged in at least once)
    * **Stage 4: D7 Log** (Users logged in at least once 7 days after signup)
    * **Stage 5: D30 Log** (Users logged in at least once 30 days after signup)
    * **Stage 6: Premium Conversion** (Users who converted to Premium)
* **Metrics (Associated with the Funnel Chart):**
    * **Conversion Rate (Each Stage):** Percentage of users who move from one stage to the next.
    * **Drop-off Rate (Each Stage):** Percentage of users who leave a stage.
    * **Time to First Log:** Average time it takes for a user to log in after signup.
    * **Time to Premium Conversion:**  Average time it takes for a user to upgrade to premium.
* **Segmentation (Filters):** Ability to slice the funnel by:
    * **Marketing Channel:** (e.g., Facebook Ads, Organic Search, App Store Featured)
    * **Device Type:** (iOS, Android)
