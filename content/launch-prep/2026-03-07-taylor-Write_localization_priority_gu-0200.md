**3. Germany (High)**
* **Rationale:** Largest economy in Europe, highly educated population, strong industrial base, and a culture of quality and reliability.  High purchasing power.

* **Key Considerations:**  Legal compliance (GDPR), culturally-specific communication styles (directness), high attention to detail.



**4. United Kingdom (Medium-High)**

* **Focus:**  English (British English is crucial – standardized spellings and idioms).
