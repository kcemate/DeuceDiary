**1. Casual Trackers (The “Just Trying to Do Better”)**

* **Motivation:** Primarily seeking self-improvement, feeling a bit overwhelmed and wanting simple tools to manage their day. They aren't necessarily intensely competitive or obsessed with streaks.

* **Behavioral Triggers:**

* **Content Focus:** Simple tips for getting started with habit tracking, highlighting the benefits of consistency, short journaling prompts focusing on gratitude/reflection.
* **Tone:** Friendly, supportive, encouraging, non-judgmental.

* **Tone:** Energetic, competitive, motivating, celebratory.
