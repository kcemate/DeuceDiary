1. **Content Policy Violations:** This is the *biggest* culprit.  This covers a massive range, including:

5. **User Interface/User Experience (UI/UX) Issues:** A confusing, poorly designed, or frustrating user interface is a major turnoff for reviewers and will likely be rejected.

6. **Privacy Issues:**  Not being transparent about data collection practices, failing to provide a clear and accessible privacy policy, or violating user privacy settings.

7. **Misleading or Deceptive Practices:**  App advertising or in-app behavior that tricks users into spending money or performing actions they didn't intend.

8. **Payment Issues:**  Problems with in-app purchases, subscriptions, or payment processing.

9. **App Not Working as Described:** The app doesn’t actually do what it claims to do in the description or screenshots.

10. **Inconsistent App Behavior:**  App features that work on one device but not another, or inconsistent behavior within the app itself.
