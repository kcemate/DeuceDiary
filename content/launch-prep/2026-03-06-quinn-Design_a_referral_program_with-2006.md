Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, covering different tiers and considerations, aiming for something attractive and effective.

**Program Name:** (Choose something catchy – e.g., “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “Level Up with [Your Brand]”)

**1. Core Structure:**

* **Referrer:** The customer who refers a new customer.
* **Referee:** The new customer referred by the referrer.
* **Reward for Referrer:** The incentive given to the referrer upon successful referral.
* **Reward for Referee:** The incentive given to the new customer upon signing up/making their first purchase.

**2. Tiered Incentive Structure (Example - Adapt to your business!)**

This is crucial for motivating different levels of participation.

| Tier       | Referral Goal   | Referrer Reward                                   | Referee Reward                                    |
|------------|-----------------|----------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| **Bronze**   | 1 Referral      | $10 Store Credit / Discount Code                  | 10% Off First Purchase                              |
| **Silver**   | 3 Referrals     | $25 Store Credit / Discount Code                  | 15% Off First Purchase                              |
| **Gold**     | 5 Referrals     | $50 Store Credit / Discount Code + Free Shipping   | 20% Off First Purchase + Free Gift (Small Item)      |
| **Platinum** | 10+ Referrals   | $100 Store Credit / Discount Code + Free Shipping + Exclusive Access | 25% Off First Purchase + Premium Gift (Higher Value)|


**3. Incentive Types (Beyond Credits/Discounts):**

* **Store Credit:** Flexible, allows for choice.
* **Discount Codes:**  Simple, encourages immediate action.
* **Free Shipping:**  Highly desirable, especially for e-commerce.
* **Free Gift:**  Creates excitement and adds perceived value (consider products related to your brand).
* **Early Access:** Give Gold/Platinum tier members early access to new products or sales.
* **VIP Status:** Elevate the referrer's status within your brand.
* **Points System:**  Allow referrers to accumulate points for each referral that converts and redeem those points for rewards.

**4. Referral Methods & Tracking:**

* **Unique Referral Links:**  *Absolutely essential*. Generate a unique link for each referrer.  This is how you track the source of new customers.  Use a referral program platform (see below) or build your own tracking (more complex).
* **Social Sharing Buttons:** Make it easy for people to share the link directly on social media platforms.  Provide pre-written social copy.
* **Email Referral Campaign:** Send emails to existing customers prompting them to refer.
* **In-App Referral (If Applicable):**  Integrate the referral program into your app.
* **QR Codes:**  For businesses with physical locations.

**5.  Technology & Platforms:**

* **Referral Program Software:** These platforms automate everything - tracking, reward distribution, email marketing, reporting.  *Highly Recommended*
    * **ReferralCandy:** Popular, easy to use.
    * **Friendbuy:** Strong focus on e-commerce.
    * **Viral Loops:**  Great for product-led growth.
    * **WordUp:**  Another robust option.
* **Your Website/E-commerce Platform:** Many e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc.) have built-in referral app integrations.
