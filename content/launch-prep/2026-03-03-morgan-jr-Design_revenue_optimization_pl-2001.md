**I. Understanding the Current State (Assessment - 20% of Time)**

* **1. Revenue Audit:**
    * **What:**  A deep dive into all revenue streams.  Don't just look at overall sales; break it down.
    * **Metrics:**
        * Total Revenue
        * Revenue per Customer
        * Average Order Value (AOV)
        * Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV)
        * Revenue by Product/Service
        * Revenue by Channel (e.g., online, retail, wholesale)
        * Revenue by Segment (e.g., demographics, customer type)
    * **Tools:** CRM, Sales Reporting, Google Analytics, POS data.
* **2. Customer Analysis:**
    * **Who:** Who is buying from you?
    * **Segments:** Identify distinct customer segments based on behavior, needs, and value.  (e.g., high-value, casual, new, loyal).
    * **Customer Journey Mapping:** Understand the steps customers take from awareness to purchase and beyond.  Identify pain points and opportunities.
* **3. Competitive Analysis:**
    * **Who:** Who are your key competitors?
    * **Pricing:** What are they charging?
    * **Offers:** What promotions are they running?
    * **Strengths & Weaknesses:**  Where do you excel, and where do they?

        * **Seasonal Promotions:** Capitalize on holidays and seasonal trends.
