## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Optimization

**Goal:** Optimize key App Store elements to improve install rates and overall app performance.

**Timeframe:** 4-6 Weeks (Iterate and adjust based on initial results)

**Metrics:**
* **Install Rate (IR):** Percentage of users who click on your app's listing and then install it. (Primary Metric)
* **Conversion Rate (CR):** Percentage of users who view your app's listing and then click the "Install" button.
* **Keyword Rankings:** Position of your app for target keywords.
* **App Store Page Views (ASPV):** Number of times your app's listing is viewed.
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR) - Title & Subtitle:** Percentage of users who see your title/subtitle and then click on your app listing.
* **CTR - Keyword Tab:** Percentage of users who see your keyword list and then click on your app listing.
* **CTR - Screenshots Tab:** Percentage of users who see your screenshot gallery and then click on your app listing.

**Testing Methodology:**

1. **Define KPIs & Goals:**  Clearly state the target IR increase (e.g., 5-10%) and other goals for each test.
2. **Keyword Selection:** Identify 2-3 high-volume, relevant keywords for each test.  Use tools like Sensor Tower, App Annie, or Mobile Action for keyword research.
3. **Test Creation:**  Create separate test variations within your App Store Connect account. This allows you to run concurrent tests.
4. **Split Traffic:** App Store automatically splits traffic 50/50 between the Control and Variation versions.  You can adjust this percentage based on your desired test size.
5. **Run the Test:**  Let the test run for a sufficient period (typically 7-14 days) to gather enough data.
