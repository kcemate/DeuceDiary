**I. Keyword Research & Density (Score: 6/10)**

* **Recommended Keywords (Tiered):**
    * **Tier 1 (High Priority - Focus):** “Mood Tracker,” “Daily Journal,” “Meditation App,” “Mental Wellness,” “Community Support,” “Healthy Habits”
    * **Tier 2 (Medium Priority - Expand):** “Group Fitness,” “Exercise Tracker,” “Stress Relief,” “Anxiety Support,” “Positive Affirmations”
    * **Tier 3 (Low Priority - Explore):** “Self-Care,” “Wellness Challenge,” “Habit Tracker”
* **Keyword Density Recommendations:**
    * **Title:**  Prioritize primary keyword(s) - e.g., “WellConnect: Mood Tracker & Wellness Community”
    * **Short Description:**  Naturally weave 2-3 high-priority keywords into the first 30 characters (attention-grabbing).
    * **Long Description:** Integrate a wider range of keywords throughout, focusing on benefits and features. Aim for a keyword density of 5-8% (a guideline - prioritize natural language).
