## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to effectively track and analyze your competition, providing insights for strategic decision-making.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary Goal:** To understand the competitive landscape, identify key strengths and weaknesses of competitors, and leverage that knowledge to inform our strategy (e.g., product development, marketing, pricing).
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify top 3-5 direct competitors.
    * Understand competitor pricing strategies.
    * Analyze competitor marketing campaigns & messaging.
    * Monitor competitor product releases and updates.
    * Track competitor customer reviews and sentiment.
    * Identify potential opportunities to differentiate ourselves.


**II. Data Sources & Tracking Methods:**

| **Data Source** | **Tracking Method** | **Frequency** | **Responsibility** | **Tools** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Competitor Websites** | Website Audits (Content, Product Pages, Pricing, Blog, Legal) | Weekly/Bi-weekly | Marketing Analyst / Product Manager |  - Google Analytics (for website traffic) - SEMrush / Ahrefs (for keyword analysis & backlinks) - BuiltWith (for technology stack) |
| **Social Media** |  Monitor channels (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube) – Brand Mentions, Engagement, Content Quality | Daily/Weekly | Social Media Manager / Marketing Analyst | - Social Listening Tools (Mention, Brand24, Hootsuite Insights) - Native Social Media Analytics |
| **Review Sites** | Track reviews on sites like Trustpilot, G2, Capterra, Yelp, Google Reviews | Weekly | Customer Support / Marketing Analyst | - Review Monitoring Tools (Reputation.com, BrightLocal) - Manual Review |
| **Industry Reports & Analyst Data** | Subscribe to industry reports from Gartner, Forrester, IDC, etc.  | Quarterly | Market Research Analyst / Strategy Team | - Subscription-based Industry Reports - Paid Analyst Access |
| **Trade Shows & Conferences** | Attend trade shows & conferences to observe competitors' presentations and demos | As Applicable | Sales Team / Product Manager | -  Observation - Note Taking |
| **Product Monitoring Tools** | Utilize tools to track competitor product updates & new features | Weekly/Monthly | Product Manager | - Product Hunt - G2 - Capterra - AppFolio (for SaaS competitors) |
| **Google Alerts** | Set up alerts for competitor names, product names, and key industry terms. | Daily | Marketing Analyst / Brand Manager | - Google Alerts |
| **Job Boards** | Track competitor hiring trends to understand their growth and priorities | Monthly | HR / Strategy Team | - LinkedIn, Indeed |


**III. Key Metrics to Track:**

This is categorized for clarity.  Prioritize based on your industry & business.

* **Product/Service Metrics:**
    * **New Features:** Frequency & Innovation Level (tracked through product monitoring tools)
    * **Product Reviews & Ratings:** Overall score & trending sentiment (tracked through review sites)
    * **Product Roadmap:**  Monitor their upcoming releases & planned features
* **Marketing Metrics:**
    * **Website Traffic:** Total traffic, traffic sources, bounce rate (Google Analytics)
    * **Keyword Rankings:**  Their positions for relevant keywords (SEMrush/Ahrefs)
    * **Social Media Engagement:** Likes, shares, comments, follower growth

    * **Campaign Types & Messaging:** Analyze their promotional campaigns – What are they emphasizing?
