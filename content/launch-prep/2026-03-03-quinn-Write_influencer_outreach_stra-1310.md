## Influencer Outreach Strategy: Bathroom Humor Creators

**I. Understanding Your Target Audience & Defining Your Brand**

* **Niche Down:** Within bathroom humor, there's a spectrum. Are you going for:
    * **Dark & Dry:** Think Bill Burr, Jim Norton.
    * **Observational & Relatable:** Think Jerry Seinfeld (early days), Mitch Hedberg.
    * **Absurdist & Surreal:** Think Bo Burnham, Tim Heidecker.
* **Brand Voice:** Define your brand's tone. Is it cynical, self-deprecating, supportive, or something else? This will help you identify influencers whose style aligns.
* **Content Pillars:** What kind of content are you creating? (TikTok sketches, YouTube videos, podcasts, Reddit threads, etc.) This impacts which influencers will be most receptive.


**II. Identifying Potential Influencers**

* **Platforms to Explore:**
    * **TikTok:** Huge potential - search hashtags like #toiletjokes, #bathroomcomedy, #awkwardmoments, #funnytoilet, #relatabletoilet. Look for creators with high engagement and a comedic style.
    * **YouTube:** Similar hashtags, but focus on longer-form content and creators with established channels.
    * **Instagram:**  Consider accounts that share memes, relatable comics, or short, humorous stories.
    * **Reddit:** Subreddits like r/funny, r/Showerthoughts, r/awkward, r/jokes – these can be goldmines for identifying engaged audiences and potential influencers.
    * **Podcast Platforms:** Look for podcasts focused on comedy, observational humor, or even just generally relatable life stories.
* **Key Metrics to Consider:**
    * **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments) / Followers – This is *crucial*.  High follower counts are useless if engagement is low. Aim for 2-5% or higher.
    * **Audience Demographics:** Ensure their audience aligns with your target demographic.
    * **Content Quality:** Assess the quality of their content – is it genuinely funny, well-produced, and consistently engaging?
    * **Authenticity:**  Do they seem genuine and relatable?  Are they actively engaging with their audience?
