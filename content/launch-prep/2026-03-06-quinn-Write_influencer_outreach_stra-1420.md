**I. Understanding Your Target Influencers (Phase 1 - Research - 1-2 Weeks)**

* **Identify Platforms:** Where does your humor thrive? Primarily:
    * **TikTok:** Huge for short-form, viral bathroom humor.
    * **Instagram:** Reels, Stories, and feed content work well.
    * **YouTube:** Longer-form skits, challenges, and compilations.
    * **Reddit:** Subreddits like r/funny, r/awkward, r/showerthoughts are key.
* **Define Your Ideal Influencer:** Don't just look for high follower counts. Focus on these qualities:
    * **Relatability:** Do their content and humor style align with yours? (e.g., dry wit, cringe comedy, observational humor)
    * **Engagement Rate:**  A high engagement rate (likes, comments, shares) is *far* more valuable than a large follower count. Aim for 2-5% minimum, ideally higher.
    * **Niche Audience:**  Look for creators already engaging with bathroom humor or similar awkward/relatable content.
    * **Consistency:** Are they consistently posting new content? 
* **Research Tools:**
    * **TikTok Discover:** See trending sounds and hashtags related to bathroom humor.
    * **Instagram Search:** Use hashtags like #bathroomhumor, #awkward, #cringecomedy, #showerthoughts, #funny
    * **YouTube Search:** Same as above.
    * **Manual Search:**  Explore subreddits and search for related content.
    * **Influencer Marketing Platforms (Optional):** Tools like Upfluence or HypeAuditor can help with larger-scale research, but for this niche, manual research is likely more effective.

**II. Influencer Segmentation & Prioritization (Phase 2 - 3-5 Days)**

* **Tiered Approach:**
    * **Tier 1: Micro-Influencers (1k-10k followers):** These have the highest engagement rates and are more likely to be genuinely passionate about their niche. Start here.
    * **Tier 2: Mid-Tier Influencers (10k-50k followers):**  Potentially wider reach, but engagement might be slightly lower.
    * **Tier 3: Macro-Influencers (50k+ followers):**  Consider for larger campaigns, but be *extremely* cautious about authenticity.
* **Create a Spreadsheet:** Track influencers, their platforms, follower count, engagement rate, and a note on their vibe/style.

**III. Outreach Strategy (Phase 3 - Ongoing)**

* **Personalized Messaging is KEY:**  *Never* send generic emails or DMs.
    * **Reference Their Content:**  Show you've actually *watched* their videos/posts. “Loved your take on [Specific Video/Post] – really relatable!”
    * **Explain the Connection:** Clearly state why you're reaching out. “We’re developing [Your Product/Brand] specifically for moments like these, and we think your audience would appreciate it.”
    * **Highlight Mutual Values:** “We both understand the importance of a good laugh after a tough day, which is why we’re excited about…”
* **Outreach Channels:**
    * **Instagram DM:** Quick, direct, and effective.
    * **TikTok DM:** Good for short, engaging messages.
    * **Email (If available):**  More formal, but can be used for larger collaborations.
