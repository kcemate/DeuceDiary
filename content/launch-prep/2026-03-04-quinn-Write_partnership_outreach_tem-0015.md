Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health/wellness brands, tailored for different scenarios and levels of formality.  I've included variations to suit different partnership types (influencers, blogs, complementary brands, etc.).

**Template 1: Short & Sweet - Ideal for Micro-Influencers & Smaller Brands**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand Name] & [Their Brand Name]**

Hi [Name],

I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand Name], a [brief, impactful description - e.g., "brand creating mindful meal prep recipes," or "company offering natural stress relief solutions"]. 

I've been a big fan of your work on [mention specific content/platform - e.g., "your Instagram account," "your blog posts about yoga"].  I especially loved [mention something specific you liked].

We think our brands would be a great fit for each other’s audiences. I’d love to explore a potential collaboration – maybe a giveaway, a product review, or a shared content piece.

You can learn more about us here: [Link to your website]

Would you be open to a quick chat?

Best,
[Your Name]

**Key Elements:**

*   **Personalized:** Always mention *why* you're reaching out to *this* person.
*   **Concise:** Get to the point quickly.
*   **Clear Call to Action:**  "Quick chat?" is easier to respond to than a complex request.


**Template 2: More Detailed – For Influencers & Blogs (Medium-Range Reach)**

**Subject: Partnership Proposal: [Your Brand Name] + [Their Brand Name] – Aligning Our Wellness Communities**

Dear [Name],

My name is [Your Name], and I'm the [Your Title] at [Your Brand Name]. We’re a [Detailed brand description - mission, values, product/service].  We focus on [Specific niche - e.g., "sustainable yoga and mindful living," "functional nutrition for busy professionals"].

I’ve been consistently impressed by your content on [Platform - e.g., "YouTube," "The [Blog Name]"], particularly [Specific example of their content – video, post, etc.]. Your focus on [Mention their key themes/values] resonates deeply with our brand.

**Here’s a potential collaboration idea:** [Clearly outline 1-2 specific ideas – e.g., “A sponsored post reviewing our [Product Name]”, “A collaborative recipe using your [Their Product] and our [Your Product]”, “A series of Instagram stories discussing [Relevant Topic]”]

**We're open to discussing other opportunities that align with your audience.** We're happy to offer [Mention what you can offer - e.g., “product samples, affiliate commission, paid sponsorship.”].

You can find more details about [Your Brand Name] here: [Link to your website]

I’d love to schedule a brief call to discuss this further.  Please let me know what time works best for you.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand Name]
[Contact Information]

**Key Elements:**

*   **Detailed Brand Intro:**  Clearly communicate what you do and why it matters.
*   **Specific Examples:**  Show you’ve actually engaged with their content.
*   **Concrete Proposal:**  Offers immediate ideas.
*   **Value Proposition:**  What’s in it for *them*?
