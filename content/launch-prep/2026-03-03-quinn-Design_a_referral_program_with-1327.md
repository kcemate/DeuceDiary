Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, incorporating different levels and potential rewards to make it engaging and effective:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** "Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love" or "[Your Brand Name] Rewards" (Keep it friendly and inviting)
* **Visuals:**  Use a consistent visual theme across all referral materials – colors, logo, imagery.

**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Before designing, define:** Who are you trying to reach with this referral program? (Existing customers, social media followers, etc.)
* **Key Goals:**  What do you want to achieve? (Increase new customer acquisition, boost brand awareness, grow your email list, etc.)


**3. Referral Program Structure - Tiered Levels:**

This is where we build excitement and reward consistent referrals.

* **Level 1: The "Welcome" Referral**
    * **Trigger:** New customer refers a friend.
    * **Referrer Reward:**  Small instant reward for the referrer - **10% off their next purchase** or a **$5 credit** to their account.
    * **Referee Reward:**  New customer gets a discount or special offer - **15% off their first purchase**
    * **Rationale:** Immediate gratification for both parties and gets the ball rolling.
* **Level 2: The "Bronze" Referral (After 1 Referral)**
    * **Trigger:** Referrer has successfully referred at least one friend who has made a purchase.
    * **Referrer Reward:**  **$10 store credit**
    * **Referee Reward:** Same as Level 1 - 15% off their first purchase.
    * **Rationale:**  Rewards consistent action.
* **Level 3: The "Silver" Referral (After 3 Referrals)**
    * **Trigger:**  Referrer has successfully referred at least three friends who have made a purchase.
    * **Referrer Reward:** **$25 store credit** + A free small branded item (e.g., sticker, keychain) 
    * **Referee Reward:** 10% off their first purchase
    * **Rationale:**  Increased commitment, a bigger reward feels more valuable.
* **Level 4: The "Gold" Referral (After 5 Referrals)**
    * **Trigger:**  Referrer has successfully referred at least five friends who have made a purchase.
    * **Referrer Reward:**  **$50 store credit** + Exclusive access to a new product launch or sale.
    * **Referee Reward:** 20% off their first purchase
    * **Rationale:**  Highest tier - recognizes significant loyalty, access to premium opportunities.



**4.  Incentives Breakdown & Value:**

* **Store Credit:**  Flexible, easy to understand, and allows customers to choose what they want.
* **Discounts:**  Popular and effective, especially for first-time purchases.
* **Free Products/Items:**  Adding a small, branded item creates a sense of value and reinforces brand loyalty. (Consider items directly related to your brand/product)
* **Exclusive Access:** (For Gold Tier) – This is *powerful*.  Early access to new products, VIP sales, or even a personalized consultation can create a strong sense of belonging.
