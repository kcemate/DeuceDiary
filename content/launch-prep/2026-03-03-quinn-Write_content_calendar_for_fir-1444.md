**Assumptions:**

* **Product/Service:** Let's assume a SaaS product – "Streamline," a project management tool for small teams.
* **Target Audience:** Small business owners, team leads, project managers.
* **Goals:** Drive awareness, generate leads, encourage trial sign-ups, and build initial user engagement.
* **Channels:**  This calendar focuses on a mix of social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, potentially Facebook/Instagram), email marketing, and a blog.


**Key:**

* **LB:** LinkedIn
* **TW:** Twitter
* **Blog:**  Your company blog

* **Demo:**  Live or recorded product demo
