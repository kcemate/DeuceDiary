**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. January - New Year, New App (Theme: Resolutions & Fresh Starts)**

* **Week 1-4:**
    * **Theme:** "Start Strong" - Focus on apps that help users achieve new year resolutions (fitness, productivity, learning, habit tracking).
    * **Actions:**
        * **Launch Campaign:** Announce a “New Year, New You” campaign.
        * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Update keywords based on trending New Year's resolutions.
        * **Social Media:** Content focused on goal-setting, motivation, and how your app helps. Run contests with prizes related to the app’s benefits.
        * **Paid Advertising:**  Run targeted ads on Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads focusing on resolution-related keywords.
        * **PR:**  Reach out to bloggers/media who cover New Year’s resolutions.
* **Key Dates:** January 1st (New Year's Day), January 15th (Martin Luther King Jr. Day - opportunity for reflection & planning)


**II. February - Love & Connection (Theme: Relationships & Self-Care)**

* **Week 1-4:**
    * **Theme:** "Connect & Care” -  Highlight apps related to relationships, dating, self-care, mental wellness, and creativity.
    * **Actions:**
        * **ASO:**  Update keywords relating to Valentine's Day & love.
        * **Social Media:** Content promoting connection, self-love, and celebrating relationships (romantic and platonic). Run Valentine's Day themed contests.
        * **Email Marketing:** Send emails to your subscriber list with offers related to relationship-focused apps.
        * **Influencer Marketing:**  Partner with influencers in the dating, wellness, or relationship space.
* **Key Dates:** February 14th (Valentine's Day)


**III. March - Spring Forward (Theme: Renewal & Activity)**

* **Week 1-4:**
    * **Theme:** "Bloom & Grow" -  Shift focus to apps related to fitness, outdoor activities, gardening, personal development, and travel planning.
    * **Actions:**
        * **ASO:** Update keywords related to spring, outdoor activities, and travel.
        * **Social Media:**  Content showcasing spring themes: new beginnings, exploration, healthy living.
        * **Promotions:**  Offer discounts or trial periods for fitness or travel apps.
        * **Content Marketing:** Blog posts or articles related to spring activities and how your app supports them.
* **Key Dates:** March 1st (Daylight Saving Time), St. Patrick's Day (March 17th)


**IV. April - Earth Month & Productivity (Theme: Sustainability & Efficiency)**

* **Week 1-4:**
    * **Theme:** “Live Green, Work Smart” -  Focus on apps related to sustainability, productivity, organization, and time management. (Earth Day is a major driver)
    * **Actions:**
        * **ASO:**  Update keywords related to Earth Day and sustainability.
        * **Social Media:**  Content promoting sustainable practices and productivity tips.
        * **Partnerships:**  Collaborate with environmental organizations or sustainable brands.
        * **Charitable Donation:**  Donate a portion of sales to an environmental charity
