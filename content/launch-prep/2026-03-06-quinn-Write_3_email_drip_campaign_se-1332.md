Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each with a different focus and goal, designed to be adaptable to your specific business and audience. I’ve included suggested content for each email, frequency, and overall goal.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Focus: Building Rapport & Awareness - 7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and encourage engagement with your core offering.
* **Frequency:** Every 24-48 hours
* **Audience:** New subscribers who have signed up through a lead magnet (e.g., free ebook, checklist, webinar signup).

**Email Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 1): Welcome & Thank You**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
    * **Content:**  Warmly welcome the subscriber. Express gratitude for joining. Briefly reiterate the value they received for signing up.  Introduce your brand’s mission and a *very* brief overview of what you offer. Include a friendly photo or brand image.
    * **Call to Action (CTA):** “Explore Our [Most Popular Resource]” (link to a relevant piece of content)
* **Email 2 (Day 3):  Problem/Solution (Highlight Pain Point)**
    * **Subject:** Struggling with [Relevant Problem]?
    * **Content:**  Acknowledge a common pain point your target audience experiences. Briefly explain why it's a challenge.  Hint at how your brand/product/service can help (without being overly salesy).
    * **CTA:** “Read Our Blog Post on [Related Topic]” (link to a helpful blog post)
* **Email 3 (Day 5):  Introduce a Key Benefit**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock [Desired Outcome] with [Your Brand]
    * **Content:**  Focus on one specific benefit of your product/service. Use a relatable example or case study (short one) to illustrate it.
    * **CTA:** “See How It Works” (link to a product page or demo video)
* **Email 4 (Day 7):  Social Proof & Testimonial**
    * **Subject:** What People Are Saying About [Your Brand]
    * **Content:** Share a brief, impactful testimonial or social proof (e.g., positive review, Instagram mention).  This builds trust and credibility.
    * **CTA:** “Read More Testimonials” (link to a testimonials page)


**Campaign 2:  Lead Nurturing - Product Focused (Focus: Driving Consideration & Education - 14 Days)**

* **Goal:** Guide leads through the stages of awareness and consideration, leading them closer to a purchase. This assumes the subscriber has shown interest in a specific product.
* **Frequency:** Every 2-3 Days
* **Audience:** Subscribers who have shown interest in a particular product (e.g., visited a product page, added to cart but didn't purchase).

**Email Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 1):  Reminder & Benefit Reinforcement**
    * **Subject:** Still Thinking About [Product Name]?
    * **Content:** Briefly restate the value proposition of the product.  Remind them of the key features and benefits.
    * **CTA:** “Learn More About [Product Name]” (link to the product page)
* **Email 2 (Day 4):  Deep Dive Feature 1**
    * **Subject:**  Discover the Power of [Feature 1] in [Product Name]
