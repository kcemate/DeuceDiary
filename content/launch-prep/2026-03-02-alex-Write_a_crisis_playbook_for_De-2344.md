**I. Core Principles - Regardless of the Crisis**

* **Transparency is Key:** Be honest and upfront with your audience. Don't try to hide problems or sugarcoat them.
* **Rapid Response:** Time is critical. Speed in acknowledging and addressing issues significantly impacts perception.
* **Centralized Communication:** Designate a small, core team (lead developer, marketing lead, community manager) to coordinate all communications.  Avoid conflicting messages.
* **Document Everything:** Keep meticulous records of all actions taken, communications sent, and conversations held. This is crucial for accountability and future reference.
* **Empathy:** Remember your users are invested in your game. Show genuine concern for their frustration.

**Scenario 1: Server Crash - The Immediate Downtime**

* **Severity:** High – This is the biggest immediate threat to user experience.
* **Immediate Actions (0-30 mins):**
    * **Acknowledge:** Immediately post on Twitter, Discord, Reddit (if active), and in-app notifications. "We're aware of a server issue affecting Deuce Diary. Our team is working to resolve it ASAP. We'll provide updates."
    * **Diagnostics:** Developer team immediately investigates the cause. Prioritize identifying the root problem.
    * **Status Page:** Create a simple status page (e.g., on a website or a basic Google Doc) to provide updates in real-time. Link prominently.
    * **ETA:** Give a realistic (even if slightly conservative) Estimated Time of Arrival for resolution.  Update the ETA as you learn more.
* **Short-Term Actions (30 mins - 4 hours):**
    * **Rollback (if possible):** If the issue stems from a recent update, consider rolling back to a stable version.
    * **Scale Resources:**  Immediately increase server capacity.  Automated scaling is ideal if you have it.
    * **Continuous Updates:** Keep users informed with regular (at least hourly) updates on the progress of the fix.
* **Long-Term Actions:**  Invest in robust server infrastructure, monitoring, and automated failover systems.

* **Severity:** Medium – Indicates a significant problem with gameplay, performance, or user experience.

    * **Monitor Reviews:** Actively monitor app store reviews (Apple App Store, Google Play Store).

    * **Identify Patterns:** The development team needs to immediately investigate if there are recurring themes in the negative reviews.
* **Short-Term Actions (30 mins - 4 hours):**
    * **Bug Fixes:** Prioritize addressing the issues raised in the reviews.  Release a patch if necessary.
    * **Developer Response:**  A short, public statement addressing the most common complaints.  “We’re actively working on [specific issue] and will have an update soon.”
* **Long-Term Actions:**  Establish a system for continuously monitoring user feedback, prioritizing bug fixes, and implementing improvements based on player suggestions.
