## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Optimization

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for key App Store elements to optimize app install rates and ultimately, user acquisition.

**1. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Install Rate (AIR) – Specifically focusing on installs from the App Store.
* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Improve App Store Page Conversion Rate (CVR) – Percentage of users who click the install button.
    * Increase Average Session Length (within app) –  Shows engagement after install.
    * Improve Keyword Rankings – Higher rankings can drive organic discovery.
* **Key Metrics to Track:**
    * **Install Rate (AIR):** Total Installs / Total App Store Impressions
    * **Conversion Rate (CVR):**  Install Button Clicks / Total App Store Impressions
    * **Keyword Rankings:** Track position for target keywords in relevant categories
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):**  Impressions / Clicks
    * **Session Length (within App):** Track average time users spend actively using the app after install.


**2. A/B Testing Elements & Hypotheses:**

| **Element**          | **Variant A (Control)** | **Variant B (Test)** | **Hypothesis**                               | **Rationale**                                    |
|-----------------------|--------------------------|-----------------------|---------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------|
| **App Title**         | “My Awesome App”           | “My Awesome App - [Unique Feature]” | Variant B will have a higher CTR due to highlighting a key benefit. | Clearer titles with a relevant feature draw attention. |
| **App Subtitle**      | “The best app for…”         | “The best app for [Specific Need]”      | Variant B will have a higher CVR due to targeting a more specific audience. | Targeting audience improves relevance and CTR.      |
| **Keywords (Focus: High-Volume)** | “app, games, fun”          | “puzzle, games, brain training”       | Variant B will increase installs due to better keyword match. |  Changing keywords can significantly impact rankings. |
| **Keyword Descriptions (Short)** | “Play amazing puzzle games!” | “Enjoy challenging brain puzzles - improve your mind!” | Variant B will have a higher CTR due to more compelling description. | Better descriptions can improve relevance and drive clicks.|
| **App Icon**         | Current Icon             | Slightly Modified Icon (color change, subtle detail change) | Variant B will have a higher CVR due to visual appeal.  |  Icon changes can subtly impact recognition and click rate. |
| **Screenshots & Videos**| Standard Screenshots       | 3D Animated Screenshot demonstrating core feature | Variant B will have a higher CVR due to showcasing a key feature visually. | Engaging visuals are critical for conversion.       |
| **Call to Action (CTA) Button Text** | “Download Now”            | “Play Now”                               | Variant B will have a higher CVR due to more urgent phrasing. |  Stronger CTAs can increase conversion.          |
| **App Previews (Videos)** | 30-Second Explainer Video  | 60-Second Longer, In-App Gameplay Video     | Variant B will have a higher CVR due to showcasing gameplay. |  Longer videos provide a more immersive experience. |


**3. Testing Methodology:**

* **Platform:** App Store Connect (Apple)
* **Traffic Allocation:** 50/50 split – Equally distribute traffic between Variants A & B.
* **Duration:** Minimum 2-4 weeks – Allow sufficient time for data collection and statistical significance.
* **Sample Size:** Utilize App Store Connect’s attribution features to track installs and impressions
