**Template 1: Short & Sweet - Ideal for Micro-Influencers & Small Businesses**

* **Platform:** Email & LinkedIn
* **Tone:** Friendly, enthusiastic, focused on mutual benefit
* **Subject Line (Email):**  Collaboration Opportunity: [Your Brand] + [Their Brand]
* **Body:**

Hi [Name],

We absolutely love what you’re doing at [Their Brand] – especially [mention a specific post, product, or value they offer - shows you’ve done your research]!

At [Your Brand], we’re passionate about [briefly state your brand's mission – 1-2 sentences]. We think our audiences would really appreciate each other's content.

We’d love to explore a potential collaboration – maybe a giveaway, content swap, or shared promotion.  Would you be open to a quick chat to see if there’s a fit?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Brand]
[Link to your website]

**Template 2: More Detailed - For Medium-Sized Influencers & Brands with Clear Offers**

* **Platform:** Email (Preferred - allows for more context) & LinkedIn
* **Tone:** Professional, informative, highlighting value proposition
* **Subject Line (Email):** Partnership Proposal: [Your Brand] & [Their Brand] – Boosting Wellness Together
* **Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], the [Your Title] at [Your Brand]. We create [briefly describe your product/service and its key benefit – e.g., “plant-based protein powders designed to fuel active lifestyles”].

We've been following your work at [Their Brand] for a while now and are truly impressed with your commitment to [mention their values and what you admire - e.g., “sustainable wellness practices” or “inspiring healthy habits”].

**Here’s what we’re envisioning:**

*   **[Specific Collaboration Idea 1]:** [Detailed explanation - e.g., “A 4-week challenge on your Instagram featuring our [product]”]
*   **[Specific Collaboration Idea 2]:** [Detailed explanation - e.g., “Guest post on your blog about [relevant topic]” ]
*   **[Incentive/Offer for their audience]:** [e.g., “15% off code for your followers,” “Exclusive discount,” “Free sample”]

We believe this partnership would be mutually beneficial – reaching a new audience while supporting your mission. 

You can learn more about us here: [Link to your website]

Would you be open to a 15-minute call to discuss this further?

Thanks for your time!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Brand]
[Contact Information]
