**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar - 2024 (Example)**

**I. January - New Year, New Habits**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Wellness
* **Dates:** January 1st - January 31st
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Launch/Promote Wellness Apps:** Focus on apps related to fitness, meditation, sleep tracking, healthy eating.
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Keyword research focused on “new year resolutions,” “goal setting,” “healthy habits.”
    * **Social Media Campaign:** Share inspirational quotes, tips for building good habits, and highlight app features that support these goals. Run contests/giveaways for free subscriptions.
    * **Email Marketing:** Welcome emails to new users, offering tips for getting the most out of the app.
    * **Paid Advertising (Apple Search Ads):** Target keywords related to resolutions.
* **Messaging:** “Start the year right! [App Name] helps you achieve your goals and build a healthier you.”
