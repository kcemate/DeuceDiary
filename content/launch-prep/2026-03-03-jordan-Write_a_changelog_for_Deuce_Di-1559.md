**Overview:** This is the initial release of Deuce Diary, a simple diary app designed for documenting your thoughts and experiences as a young Jedi learning the ways of the Force.

**New Features:**

* **Diary Entry Creation:**  You can now create new diary entries with a title, date, and a free-text field for recording your thoughts, feelings, and observations.
* **Date Picker:** A user-friendly date picker makes it easy to select the date for your diary entries.

* **Theme Support:**  A basic light theme is included, offering a cleaner reading experience.

**Known Issues & Limitations:**

* **Limited Formatting:** Formatting options are currently very basic (bold, italics). More advanced formatting will be considered for future updates.
* **No Cloud Sync:**  Diary entries are stored locally on your device. There is no automatic synchronization with a cloud service.  (Manual backups are recommended!).
* **Mobile Device Compatibility:**  Testing primarily conducted on desktop platforms (Windows).  Further testing on mobile devices is required.
* **Potential for Data Loss:**  Due to the lack of cloud sync, there is a risk of data loss if your device is damaged or lost.  Regular backups are strongly recommended.
