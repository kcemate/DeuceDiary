Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencers, with variations to suit different levels of formality and goals.

**1. Beta Testers Email Template**

**Subject: Invitation to Test [Product Name] - Be a Part of the Early Days!**

**Option 1: Formal & Detailed**

```email
Dear [Beta Tester Name],

We’re thrilled to invite you to participate in the beta testing phase of [Product Name], our new [Product Category - e.g., mobile app, software, game]!  We're incredibly excited about this product and believe your feedback will be invaluable as we refine it before its official launch.

[Product Name] is a [Brief, 1-2 sentence description of the product].  We're particularly interested in your thoughts on [Specific areas you want feedback on - e.g., user interface, performance, specific features].

**Here’s what you’ll be doing:**

*   Using [Product Name] and providing detailed feedback through [Method of Feedback - e.g., a dedicated forum, a Google Form, weekly video calls].
*   Testing specific scenarios we'll provide to ensure we're covering all the key use cases.
*   Reporting any bugs or issues you encounter clearly and concisely.

You can access the beta version here: [Link to Beta Access]

The beta period will run from [Start Date] to [End Date].  We estimate it will take you approximately [Estimated Time Commitment - e.g., 2-4 hours] per week to participate.

As a thank you for your time and valuable input, you’ll receive [Reward - e.g., early access to the full version, a discount, a gift card].

If you’re excited to get started, please confirm your participation by replying to this email. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us at [Support Email Address].

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team
```

**Option 2:  More Casual & Enthusiastic**

```email
Hey [Beta Tester Name],

We're so stoked to be inviting you to test out [Product Name]! We’re building a [Product Category] and really want your honest opinion to make it the best it can be.

[Product Name] is all about [Brief, exciting description - e.g., making productivity easier, creating awesome art, solving a specific problem].

We’d love for you to spend some time with it and let us know what you think.  We’ll be using [Method of Feedback - e.g., a short survey] to get your feedback, and we'll provide you with some specific tasks to try out.

The beta runs from [Start Date] to [End Date].

As a thank you, we'll be [Reward - e.g., giving you a shout-out on our social media, offering a small discount].

Let us know if you're in by replying to this email!

Cheers,

The [Your Company Name] Team
```
