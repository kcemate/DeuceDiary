**2. Goals:** (What are you trying to achieve?)
* Increase new customer acquisition.
* Boost brand awareness.
* Build customer loyalty.
* Grow your customer base organically.


**3. Structure & Tiers:**

We’ll use a tiered system to incentivize different levels of participation.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Everyone)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 10% off their next purchase. (Easy to implement, low cost)
    * **Referee Reward:** 5% off their first purchase. (Encourages initial conversion)
    * **Mechanics:**  Simple code generation for the referrer to share.
* **Tier 2: Silver Referral (Refer 2+ People)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 20% off their next purchase. (Increased incentive)
    * **Referee Reward:** 10% off their first purchase.
    * **Mechanics:** Same as Tier 1, plus potentially a small badge on the referrer’s profile to signal their status.
* **Tier 3: Gold Referral (Refer 5+ People)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** $25 Gift Card to your store. (Significant reward)
    * **Referee Reward:** 15% off their first purchase.
    * **Mechanics:** Could include an exclusive email newsletter for Gold Referrers.
