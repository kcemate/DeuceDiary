**I. DM Template Kit - Tailored for Micro-Influencers**

**General Principles:**

* **Personalized is Key:** Never send a generic blast. Mention something specific about their content you genuinely enjoy.
* **Keep it Short & Sweet:** Micro-influencers are busy. Get to the point.
* **Focus on Value:** Highlight how a collaboration benefits *them*.
* **Be Authentic:** Don’t sound like a corporate robot.

**A. Health Micro-Influencers (1k - 10k Followers)**

* **Template 1 (Initial Connection - Casual):**
   “Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Just came across your feed and *loved* your posts on [Specific Topic - e.g., mindful mornings, healthy recipes]. Your approach to [Mention something specific you liked] is really inspiring!  I’m [Your Brand] and we’re passionate about [Your Brand’s Mission - briefly].  Would love to chat & see if there's a potential fit! ✨”

* **Template 2 (If They've Engaged with Your Content):**
   “Hi [Influencer Name]! Spotted your comment on my recent [Post Type - e.g., Reel, Story] about [Topic] – great point!  We’re [Your Brand] and we’re working on [Brief Description of What You Do].  Thinking about a collab opportunity that could benefit your audience with [Benefit - e.g., quick healthy tips].  Interested in a quick chat?”

* **Template 3 (Follow-Up - Gentle Reminder):**
   “Hey [Influencer Name], just wanted to check in! Still interested in hearing about a potential collaboration?  We’d love to brainstorm ideas that align with your awesome health content. 😊”
