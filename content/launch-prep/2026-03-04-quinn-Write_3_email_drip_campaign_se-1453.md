# Write 3 email drip campaign sequences.

Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different goal. I’ve included subject lines, email content, and timing for each sequence.  Remember to tailor these to *your* specific brand, audience, and product/service!

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introductory (Focus: Building Trust & Brand Awareness - 5 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, offer a valuable resource, and nurture a new subscriber.
* **Target:** New Email Subscribers
* **Timing:**  5 days – 1 day on signup, 1 day rest, then 3 days spaced out.

**Email 1: (Day 0 – Immediately After Signup)**
* **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
* **Content:**
   * Warm greeting and thank you for subscribing.
   * Briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values. (Keep it concise - 2-3 sentences).
   * Example: "We're so excited to have you join the [Your Brand Name] community!  We're passionate about [Your Brand's Focus] and helping you achieve [Benefit for Customer]."
   * Link to your website homepage.


**Email 2: (Day 1 – 24 Hours Later)**
* **Subject:** Your Free [Resource Name] is Waiting! 🎁
* **Content:**
   * Remind them of the resource you promised (e.g., ebook, checklist, template, mini-course).
   * Briefly highlight the key benefits of downloading it.
   * Example: "As promised, here's your free guide to [Resource Topic]! This will help you [Benefit 1] and [Benefit 2]."
   * Clear call to action: "Download Now!" (Button)


**Email 3: (Day 3 – 72 Hours Later)**
* **Subject:**  A Little Insight into [Your Niche] 💡
* **Content:**
   * Share a valuable tip, statistic, or short article related to your brand’s niche.  This demonstrates expertise.
   * Keep it light and engaging, not overtly sales-y.
   * Example: "Did you know that [Interesting Fact]? We believe it's important for [Reason]..."
   * Link to a relevant blog post on your website.



**Campaign 2: Product/Service Specific (Focus:  Driving Sales - 7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce a specific product or service and move subscribers closer to a purchase.
* **Target:** Subscribers who have shown interest in a particular product category (e.g., after browsing a specific page).
* **Timing:** 7 Days – 1 Day on trigger, 3 days rest, then 3 days spaced out.

**Email 1: (Day 0 – Triggered by Page View/Interest)**
* **Subject:**  You Might Be Interested in [Product Name] 🔎
* **Content:**
    * Acknowledge they’ve been looking at something related. “We noticed you were checking out our [Product Category]…"
    * Briefly highlight the *primary* benefit of the specific product they viewed.
    * Example: "Our [Product Name] helps you [Solve a specific problem] easily and efficiently."
    *  Eye-catching image of the product.


**Email 2: (Day 3 – 72 Hours Later)**
* **Subject:**  Don't Miss Out: [Product Name] is Selling Fast! ⏳
* **Content:**
   * Create a sense of urgency. (Only use if true - don't fabricate scarcity).
   * Recap key benefits – focus on 2-3 strongest features.
   * Include a testimonial or social
