Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers and strategies to maximize engagement and growth.

**Program Name:** (Example) "Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love" or "[Your Brand Name] Rewards"

**I. Core Structure & Tiers**

We'll use a tiered system to reward increasing levels of referrals. This encourages continued effort.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referrer (New Referrals)** - Everyone who refers someone gets a small initial reward.
* **Tier 2: Silver Referral (Multiple Referrals)** - Achieved after a certain number of successful referrals.
* **Tier 3: Gold Referral (Significant Volume)** - For those consistently driving strong results.



**II. Incentives - What You Offer**

This is the most important part! Offer a mix of incentives that appeal to your target audience.

| Tier         | Incentive 1         | Incentive 2            | Incentive 3 (Optional - for Gold Tier) |
|--------------|----------------------|-------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| **Basic**     | **10% Discount Code** |  Free Shipping on Next Order | N/A                                    |
| **Silver**    | **20% Discount Code** |  $25 Store Credit         |  Exclusive Early Access to New Products |
| **Gold**      | **40% Discount Code** |  $75 Store Credit         |  Personalized Consultation / 1-on-1 Support + Exclusive Product |



**III. Referral Mechanics & Tracking**

* **Unique Referral Links/Codes:** Every user gets a unique link or code that they can share. This is *essential* for accurate tracking.  Use a referral marketing platform (see "Tools" below) to make this simple.
* **Tracking:**  A robust tracking system is crucial.  This needs to:
    *  Track each referral.
    *  Identify which referrer was responsible for a new customer.
    *  Calculate payouts automatically.
* **Simple Sharing Options:**  Make it ridiculously easy for people to share.
    *  **Social Media Buttons:** Pre-populated links for Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc.
    *  **Email Templates:**  Provide branded email templates for users to easily copy and paste.
    *  **Text Message Links:** (If appropriate for your business)
    *  **QR Codes:** For print marketing materials.

**IV. Program Rules & Terms**

* **Eligibility:**  Who can participate? (Existing customers only? Specific user segments?)
* **Redemption Rules:**  How do people claim their rewards? (Minimum purchase requirements? Expiration dates?)
* **Fraud Prevention:**  Outline measures to prevent abuse (e.g., limiting referrals from one person to themselves).
* **Program Changes:** State that you reserve the right to modify or terminate the program.

**V. Timeline & Reward Distribution**

* **New Customer Acquisition:**  The new customer gets the reward.
* **Referral Reward:** The referrer receives the reward *after* the new customer completes a purchase or specific action (e.g., signs up for a newsletter, activates an account).
* **Timing:**  Establish clear timelines for reward delivery (e.g., "You'll receive your discount code within 24-48 hours of the new customer's purchase.").
