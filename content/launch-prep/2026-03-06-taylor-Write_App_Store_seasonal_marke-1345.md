**Q1: January - March – New Year, New Goals & Resolution Apps**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Wellness, Productivity
* **January (New Year’s Focus):**
    * **Campaign:** “Start Strong” - Focus on apps related to productivity, habit tracking, fitness, and personal finance.
    * **Activities:**
        *  Promote trial periods or discounted subscriptions.
        *  Run targeted ads to users interested in New Year’s resolutions.
        *  Create content showcasing the app’s ability to help users achieve their goals. (Blog posts, short videos, infographics)
        *  Influencer outreach to relevant wellness and productivity niches.
* **February (Love & Relationships/Self-Care):**
    * **Campaign:** “Invest in Yourself & Connections” -  Target apps related to dating, mindfulness, meditation, and self-improvement.
    * **Activities:**  Run Valentine’s Day themed promotions (e.g., discounted couples apps, self-care bundles).  Content could focus on connection, self-love, and well-being.
* **March (Spring Equinox - Mindful Reset):**
    * **Campaign:** “Spring into Action” - Transition from resolution focus to broader wellness & productivity.
    * **Activities:** Promote seasonal fitness apps, meditation apps, and apps focused on decluttering and organization.  Consider a "reset" promotion – free trial or discount.


**Q2: April - June –  Outdoor Activities & Summer Prep**

* **Theme:**  Summer, Travel, Outdoor Adventures,  Skills Acquisition
* **April (Earth Day & Outdoor Focus):**
    * **Campaign:** "Get Outdoors" -  Promote fitness apps (running, hiking, cycling), navigation apps, camping apps, and outdoor gear apps.
    * **Activities:** Partner with outdoor brands, run contests related to outdoor activities.
* **May (Mother’s Day & Family Apps):**
    * **Campaign:** "Celebrate Family" - Apps for family activities, educational games, and family communication.
    * **Activities:** Mother’s Day discounts, family-focused content.
* **June (Summer Starts – Travel & Learning):**
    * **Campaign:** “Explore & Learn” – Highlight travel apps, language learning apps, educational games for kids, and apps for skill development.
    * **Activities:**  Summer travel deals promotion, contests related to travel experiences. Consider seasonal challenges within the app.



**Q3: July - September – Summer Fun & Back-to-School**

* **Theme:**  Summer, Travel, Games, Back-to-School
* **July (Summer Games & Entertainment):**
