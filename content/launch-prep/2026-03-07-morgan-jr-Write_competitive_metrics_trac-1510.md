## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to effectively track and analyze competitive data to inform strategic decisions and maintain a competitive advantage.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape, identify key threats and opportunities, and ultimately, improve our performance relative to competitors.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Monitor competitor activities across key areas (product, pricing, marketing, innovation, etc.).
    * Identify emerging trends and technologies affecting our industry.
    *  Benchmark our performance against competitors across relevant metrics.
    *  Inform strategic decisions related to product development, pricing, marketing campaigns, and market positioning.


**2. Scope & Competitive Set:**

* **Define “Competitor”:**  Be specific about who qualifies. This could include:
    * **Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target market.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions to the same customer needs.
    * **Potential Competitors:**  New entrants or companies expanding into our market.
* **Identify Competitive Set:**  Based on your market and product, create a ranked list of your key competitors. (e.g., Top 3, Top 5, Top 10).
* **Segmentation:**  Categorize competitors based on size, target market, and strategic approach.  This will help you focus your efforts.
