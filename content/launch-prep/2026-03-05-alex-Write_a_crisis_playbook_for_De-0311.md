## Deuce Diary Launch: Crisis Playbook

**Goal:** Minimize negative PR, maintain audience trust, and swiftly address any issues impacting the launch of *Deuce Diary*.

**I. Pre-Launch (Weeks Before - Phase 1)**

* **Team Roles & Responsibilities:**
    * **PR Lead (Sarah):** Oversees all external communications, media outreach, and crisis management.
    * **Social Media Manager (Liam):** Monitors social media sentiment, responds to comments, and executes planned social media responses.
    * **Community Manager (Chloe):**  Engages with the community, answers questions, and identifies potential issues within the pre-launch community.
    * **CEO/Creator (Mark):**  Approves all major messaging, provides final oversight, and authorizes escalation.
* **Monitoring:**
    * **Social Listening Tools:** Utilize tools like Hootsuite Insights, Brandwatch, or Mention to track mentions of *Deuce Diary*, the author, and relevant keywords.
    * **Reddit & Forums:** Active monitoring of subreddits like r/books, r/writing, and any relevant genre-specific communities.
    * **Review Sites:** Monitor Goodreads, Amazon reviews, and any potential review sites.
* **Scenario Mapping:** Brainstorm potential crisis scenarios and draft preliminary responses. (See Section III for examples)


**II. Launch Day - Phase 2 (First 24-48 Hours)**

**Priority 1: Immediate Response (Within 30 Minutes of a Potential Issue)**

* **Initial Alert:** Liam immediately identifies a potential crisis through social listening or direct feedback.
* **Triage:** Sarah quickly assesses the severity of the issue – is it a minor misinterpretation, a technical glitch, or a serious criticism?
* **Escalation:** If deemed a crisis (severity Level 2 or 3 - see below), Mark is immediately notified.

**Severity Levels:**
* **Level 1 (Minor):**  Negative comments, typos, minor bugs, low volume of complaints. – Liam handles initial response, Sarah monitors.
* **Level 2 (Moderate):**  Increased negative sentiment, criticism of specific aspects, 10-20 negative reviews. – Sarah & Liam coordinate response, Mark approves messaging.
* **Level 3 (Major):**  Widespread negative publicity, significant technical issues, serious allegations, legal concerns. –  Mark, PR Lead, Legal Counsel (if needed) coordinate a full response.


**Communication Channels:**
* **Social Media:** Liam posts timely responses addressing concerns, debunking misinformation, and directing users to official channels.
* **Email:** Chloe manages a dedicated email inbox for customer support and complaint handling.
* **Website:** Mark updates the website with relevant announcements and FAQs.
