**II. Data Collection & Sources:**

* **First-Party Data (Most Valuable):** This is data *you* collect directly from your users.
    * **Website Analytics:** Google Analytics (behavioral, demographics, interests, acquisition source)
    * **CRM Data:** Customer Relationship Management system (purchase history, demographics, interactions, support tickets, loyalty program status)
    * **Email Marketing Data:** Open rates, click-through rates, subscriptions, purchase links clicked.
    * **App Usage Data:** (if applicable) Frequency of use, features used, in-app purchases.
    * **Surveys & Feedback:**  Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Satisfaction (CSAT), Product Feedback, User Interviews.
    * **Loyalty Programs:** Detailed customer data, purchase frequency, engagement level.
* **Second-Party Data:** Data shared by another organization with your consent (e.g., a partner).
* **Third-Party Data:** Data purchased from external providers (demographics, interests, lifestyle data - use cautiously and ensure compliance).  Generally, prioritize first-party.
