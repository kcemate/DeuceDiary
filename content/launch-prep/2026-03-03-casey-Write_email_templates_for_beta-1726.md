Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencers, designed to be adaptable to your specific needs.  I've included variations to suit different levels of formality and relationship.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template**

**Subject: Invitation to Test [Product Name] - Help Us Shape the Future!**

**Option 1: Formal & Detailed**

```email
Dear [Beta Tester Name],

We're thrilled to invite you to be a beta tester for [Product Name], our new [brief product description - e.g., mobile app, software platform, game]! We’re building this with a strong focus on [mention key features/goals] and your feedback will be invaluable in ensuring a polished and successful launch.

As a beta tester, you’ll be asked to:

*   [List 2-3 key tasks - e.g., Use the product for X hours, complete specific tasks within the app, provide feedback on a particular feature]
*   Report any bugs or issues you encounter.
*   Share your overall impressions and suggestions.

We’ll be providing you with [Link to Beta Access/Instructions] and a dedicated channel through [Slack/Discord/Forum – specify where to get support].  We'll be checking in regularly to gather your thoughts.

Your participation is crucial to making [Product Name] the best it can be. We genuinely appreciate your time and expertise!

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team

[Link to your website]
```

**Option 2:  More Casual & Enthusiastic**

```email
Hey [Beta Tester Name],

Super excited to have you on board as a beta tester for [Product Name]! We’re building this awesome [brief product description] and really want your input to help us make it amazing.

We’d love for you to [List 1-2 key tasks - e.g., test out the core features, try out the new UI] and let us know what you think.  It’s super important that you report any bugs you find - the more feedback we get, the better!

You can find everything you need here: [Link to Beta Access/Instructions] and we'll be hanging out in [Slack/Discord/Forum – specify where to get support].

Thanks so much for helping us out!

Best,

[Your Name] & The [Your Company Name] Team
```

**Important Notes for Beta Testers:**

*   **Segment your beta testers:**  Consider having different levels of access or expectations based on their expertise.
*   **Provide Clear Instructions:** Make the process as easy as possible for them.
*   **Respond Quickly:**  Acknowledge their feedback promptly and show you're listening.
