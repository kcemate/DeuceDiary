Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week, with suggested content types and estimated frequency. This is a template – you'll need to tailor it specifically to *your* product, target audience, and launch goals.

**Important Assumptions:**

* **Launch Date:** We're assuming the launch is happening at the start of Month 1.
* **Product Type:** This calendar is flexible but geared toward a SaaS product, app, or service. Adjust based on your specifics.
* **Goals:** This calendar focuses on driving awareness, engagement, and initial user adoption.
* **Platforms:**  I’ll suggest platforms, but prioritize based on where your audience spends their time (e.g., Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Blog).

**Overall Month 1 Focus:**  Joy, Excitement, Education, and Initial Support.


---

**Week 1: The "Wow" & Initial Buzz (Focus: Awareness & Excitement)**

* **Monday:** **Launch Announcement (All Platforms)** - Big, celebratory post!  Video demo, core features highlight, link to website/landing page. (High Priority)
* **Tuesday:** **Behind-the-Scenes Story (Instagram/Facebook/LinkedIn)** –  A short, personal story about the creation of the product - the team, the challenges, the vision. Humanize it. (Medium Priority)
* **Wednesday:** **Feature Spotlight 1 (Blog/Social Media)** - Deep dive into *one* key feature. Explain *why* it’s valuable. (Medium Priority)
* **Thursday:** **User Question/Poll (Twitter/Facebook)** -  Ask your audience what they're most excited about. Spark a conversation. (Low Priority - Engagement Driver)
* **Friday:** **Welcome & Onboarding Tip (Email/Website)** – A short, friendly email welcoming new users and a quick tip on how to get started. (High Priority)
* **Weekend:** **Social Media Recap/Highlight Reel (All Platforms)** - Short clips of launch excitement, key features showcased.


**Week 2:  Demonstration & Value (Focus: Education & Value Proposition)**

* **Monday:** **Tutorial Video - Basic Use Case (YouTube/Website/Social Media)** – Short, digestible video (2-3 mins max) showing how to do one simple, core task. (High Priority)
* **Tuesday:** **Customer Problem/Solution (Blog/LinkedIn)** – Clearly define a problem your product solves, then illustrate how it provides the solution. (Medium Priority)
* **Wednesday:** **Feature Spotlight 2 (Social Media - focused on specific target audience)** - Similar to Week 1, but focusing on a different key feature relevant to a specific segment. (Medium Priority)
* **Thursday:** **Q&A Session (Live Stream/Social Media)** – Host a short live Q&A session addressing common questions. (Medium Priority - Engagement)
* **Friday:** **Case Study Snippet (Blog/Social Media)** - Share a short excerpt from a case study (if available – even a beta case study is valuable) highlighting a success. (Low Priority - Build Credibility)
* **Weekend:** **User Tip of the Week (Social Media)** -  A clever tip or trick to help users get more out of the product. (Low Priority - Engagement)



**Week 3:  Community Building & Feedback (Focus: Engagement & Iteration)**

* **Monday:** **User Spotlight (Social Media)** – Feature a beta user or early adopter. Get their quote and testimonial. (High Priority)
* **Tuesday:** **Blog Post - "5 Things You Didn’t Know About [Product Name]"** –  A fun, lighthearted post highlighting some lesser-known features or benefits
