* **Entry Detail View:**  Clicking on an entry in the list opens it in a detailed view, allowing you to read, edit, and delete it.
* **Save Functionality:** Entries are automatically saved to local storage.
* **Dark Mode:**  Implemented a dark mode for reduced eye strain.
