Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health & wellness brands, categorized by approach and with varying levels of formality.  I've included options for different partnership types (influencers, complementary brands, events, etc.) and suggestions for personalization.

**Important Note Before You Start:** *Always* research the potential partner thoroughly. Tailor these templates to their specific audience, values, and content style. Generic outreach is rarely effective.

**1. Short & Sweet – For Micro-Influencers & Small Complementary Brands (Email/DM)**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand] & [Their Brand]**

Hi [Name],

I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand], a [brief description - e.g., natural skincare brand, mindful meditation app]. I’ve been really impressed with your [mention specific content - e.g., morning routine videos, healthy recipes] and think our audiences would really connect!

We're always looking for exciting collaborations.  I'd love to explore a potential partnership – maybe a giveaway, a product swap, or a shared post.

You can learn more about us here: [Link to Your Website]

Would you be open to a quick chat?

Best,
[Your Name]

**Why it works:** Direct, friendly, and focuses on mutual benefit.

**2. More Detailed – For Mid-Tier Influencers & Brands (Email)**

**Subject: Partnership Proposal: Aligning [Your Brand] & [Their Brand] to Support [Relevant Wellness Theme]**

Dear [Name],

My name is [Your Name], and I’m the [Your Title] at [Your Brand], a [detailed description - e.g., certified organic supplement company focused on stress reduction].  We're passionate about [your brand's mission - e.g., empowering individuals to live their healthiest, most balanced lives].

I’ve been consistently impressed by your work on [mention specific content – e.g., your honest reviews of fitness trackers, your insightful guides on gut health].  Your audience aligns perfectly with our focus on [key target audience – e.g., mindful wellness for busy professionals].

**Here’s what we’re envisioning:**

*   **Potential Collaboration Ideas:** [List 2-3 specific ideas - e.g., Sponsored Post featuring our [product], Co-created content series on [topic], Giveaway for your followers]
*   **Our Offer:** [Outline what you're offering – e.g., Product samples, Affiliate commission,  Paid sponsorship fee (if applicable - be transparent)]
*   **Metrics:** [Briefly mention what you'd track - e.g., Engagement rates, Reach, Sales conversions]

You can find more about [Your Brand] here: [Link to Your Website]

I’d love to schedule a 15-minute call to discuss how we can work together.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Contact Information]


**Why it works:** Demonstrates you've done your research, clearly outlines the proposal, and proposes concrete ideas.
