## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the strategy for building and nurturing a thriving community around *Deuce Diary* focusing on Discord and Reddit. **I. Platform Strategy**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** Discord will be the central hub for real-time discussions, collaborative activities, and deeper engagement.
    * **Server Structure:**
        * **#general:**  Announcements, casual conversation, sharing fan art.
        * **#deuce-discussion:** Deep dives into lore, theories, character analysis, meta discussions.
        * **#creative-corner:** Dedicated space for fan fiction, art, music, cosplay, and other creative projects.
        * **#roleplay-zone:**  Structured and unstructured roleplay opportunities (with clear guidelines).
        * **#suggestions & Feedback:**  For community input on content, game development, etc.

* **Reddit (Amplification & Long-Form Discussion):** Reddit will serve as an amplification platform, driving traffic to Discord and providing space for more in-depth conversations and curated content.
    * **Subreddit: r/DeuceDiary** (Ideally, the official subreddit)
    * **Focus:** Long-form discussions, sharing of high-quality fan content, AMAs with the developers (if possible), and community-driven content calendars.
