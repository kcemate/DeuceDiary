## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Element Optimization

**Goal:** To improve app install rates and key metrics (e.g., Conversion Rate, Average Session Length) by systematically testing and optimizing App Store elements.

**Timeline:** 4-6 Weeks (Can be adjusted based on your specific goals and resources)

**Metrics We’ll Track:**

* **Install Rate:** (Installs / Impressions) – Primary metric
* **Conversion Rate:** (Installs / Impressions) -  More refined measure of impression effectiveness
* **App Store Page Views:**  Indicates visibility and interest
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Screenshots & Videos:** Measures engagement with visual assets
* **Keyword Ranking (Top of Page - TOTP):** Monitor keyword performance post-test
* **Average Session Length:**  (Time Spent in App / Number of Sessions) -  Post-install behavior metric (requires app analytics)

**Tools:**

* **Sensor Tower/App Annie/Mobile Action:**  Keyword research, competitor analysis, rank tracking, and A/B testing.
* **App Store Connect Analytics:**  Native reporting for iOS app performance.
* **Google Analytics for Firebase/Mixpanel/Amplitude:**  Tracking in-app behavior and user engagement.

**1. App Title & Subtitle (High Impact - Short Duration Tests)**

* **Objective:**  Improve keyword targeting & initial impression.
* **Variants:**

* **Duration:** 14 Days
