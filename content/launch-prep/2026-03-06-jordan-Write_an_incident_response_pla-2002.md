**1. Purpose:**

This plan outlines the procedures for identifying, containing, eradicating, and recovering from security incidents impacting [Your Organization Name]’s information systems, data, and operations. It aims to minimize damage, restore services quickly, and prevent future occurrences.

**2. Definitions:**

* **Incident:** Any event that violates, or threatens to violate, the organization's security policies, acceptable use policies, or legal requirements.  This includes malware infections, unauthorized access, data breaches, denial-of-service attacks, and suspicious activity.
* **Severity Levels:**
    * **P0 - Critical:**  Imminent and widespread disruption; significant data loss; critical business operations severely impacted. Requires immediate, full-scale response. (e.g., Ransomware attack encrypting core systems, large-scale data breach involving sensitive customer information).
    * **P1 - High:**  Significant disruption; potential for widespread impact; noticeable impact on business operations; potential for data compromise. Requires rapid, coordinated response. (e.g., Successful malware infection on a critical server, unauthorized access to sensitive data, DDoS attack affecting website availability).
    * **P2 - Medium:**  Limited disruption; potential for impact on a specific system or process; minimal impact on business operations; potential for minor data exposure. Requires prompt investigation and resolution. (e.g., Suspicious login attempts, phishing email detected, minor malware detected on a non-critical system).
