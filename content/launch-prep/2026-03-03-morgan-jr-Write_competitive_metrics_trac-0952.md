## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to consistently track and analyze your competitors' activities, informing your own strategy and enabling you to stay ahead. It focuses on a structured, repeatable process and leverages a variety of data sources.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Understand competitor strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and market positioning to inform our strategic decisions and ultimately drive growth.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify emerging trends in the industry.
    * Understand competitor pricing strategies and promotions.
    * Monitor competitor product development and feature releases.
    * Assess competitor marketing campaigns and messaging.
    * Identify potential threats and opportunities.


**II. Key Metrics & Categories:**

We’ll track metrics across several categories, segmented by competitor tiers (High, Medium, Low) based on their market share/influence.

| Category        | Metric Examples                               | Tier Focus (High/Medium/Low) | Frequency    | Data Source                               | Notes                               |
|-----------------|---------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|-------------|-------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------|
| **Product/Service** | Feature Set, Innovation Rate, Pricing, Reviews | High/Medium                    | Weekly/Monthly| Product Pages, Review Sites (Trustpilot, Capterra), Social Media, Industry Reports | Track new releases, competitor’s roadmap |
| **Marketing**    | Website Traffic, Social Media Engagement, Ad Spend (if visible), Content Volume & Topics, Email Marketing Activity | All Tiers                      | Weekly/Monthly| SimilarWeb, Social Media Analytics, SEMrush, Ahrefs, Email Tracking Tools |  Analyze channel effectiveness     |
| **Sales & Pricing**| Pricing Changes, Promotions, Bundles, Discounts, Sales Volume (estimated) | High/Medium                    | Monthly/Quarterly| Public Announcements,  Industry Reports, Customer Surveys (optional) | Understand price sensitivity     |
| **Customer Experience** | Customer Satisfaction (CSAT), Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Reviews (Sentiment Analysis) | All Tiers                      | Quarterly      | Review Sites, Social Media Monitoring, CRM Data (if overlaps)   | Gauge customer perception         |
| **Online Presence** | Domain Authority, Backlinks, SEO Rankings, Mobile Friendliness, Site Speed | All Tiers                      | Monthly       | SEO Tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs), Website Graders (Google PageSpeed Insights) | Assess digital visibility          |
| **Partnerships & Alliances**| New Partnerships, Co-Marketing Campaigns  | High/Medium                    | Monthly/Quarterly| Press Releases, Industry Events, Social Media| Understand their ecosystem          |



**III. Data Sources & Tools:**

* **Free/Low-Cost:**
    * **Google Alerts:** Set up alerts for competitor names, products, and keywords.
    * **Social Media Monitoring Tools (Free tiers):**  Mention, Hootsuite (limited)
    * **Website Graders:** Google PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix
    * **SimilarWeb:**  Provides website traffic estimates and competitor analysis. (Limited free tier)
* **Paid Tools (Recommended):**
    * **SEMrush/Ahrefs:** Comprehensive SEO and competitive analysis tools.
    * **SimilarWeb:**  Detailed website traffic and competitor intelligence.
    * **BuzzSumo:**  Content analysis and influencer identification.
    * **Trustpilot/Capterra:**  Monitor customer reviews and ratings.
    * **Google Analytics:** Track website traffic and user behavior (ours and potentially competitors').

**IV. Tracking Process & Reporting:**

1. **Competitor Selection:** Identify key competitors (High, Medium, Low) based on market share, product overlap, and strategic significance.
2. **Data Collection:**  Regularly gather data from identified sources. Utilize scheduled reports
