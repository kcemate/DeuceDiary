## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, leveraging a compelling viral loop to drive user acquisition, increase engagement, and build a thriving community. We’ll focus on simplicity, attractive rewards, robust tracking, and proactive fraud prevention.

**1. Program Name & Theme:** “Spread the Diary Love” – Emphasizes sharing and community.

**2. Mechanics:**

* **Referrer Rewards:**
    * **Tiered System (Based on Referrals):**
        * **Level 1 (First 3 Referrals):** 1 Month Premium Subscription (Full access to all features)
        * **Level 2 (Next 5 Referrals):** $25 Gift Card (Amazon, Starbucks, etc.) - allows for broader appeal.
        * **Level 3 (10+ Referrals):** Exclusive "Deuce Diary Master" Badge displayed on their profile, recognition within the community.
    * **Instant Reward:**  Referrers receive a small bonus (e.g., 50 Deuce Points - our in-app currency) upon successful referral.
* **Referee Rewards:**
    * **Free 1-Week Premium Trial:** New users receive a full week of premium features after being referred.
    * **Welcome Bonus:** Upon signup, new users receive a small welcome gift (e.g., 100 Deuce Points).
* **Viral Loop Design:**
    * **Shareable Link:**  Generate a unique referral link for each user.
    * **Social Sharing Buttons:** Integrate direct share buttons on the app and website – easy sharing to Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and even messaging apps.
    * **In-App Notification:**  When a referral is successful, the referrer receives a celebratory notification and a prompt to share their link.
    * **Community Spotlight:** Feature top referrers on the Deuce Diary social media channels and potentially within the app (with their permission).


**3. Fraud Prevention:**

* **Unique Referral Links:**  Every user gets a unique, trackable referral link.
* **Manual Verification:** Initially, a small team will manually review high-volume referral activity to identify suspicious patterns.
* **IP Address Monitoring:** Track referrals from the same IP address to identify potential bulk referrals.
* **Email Address Verification:** Ensure referees are using valid email addresses.
* **Account Activity Monitoring:** Flag accounts with unusually high referral rates or suspicious sharing behaviors.
* **Thresholds:**  Establish thresholds for referral rates (e.g., flag accounts referring more than X per day) for manual review.
* **Terms of Service:** Clearly outline prohibited behaviors (e.g., creating fake accounts, using automated tools) in the Terms of Service.

**4. Tracking Implementation:**

* **Dedicated Referral Software:** Invest in a robust referral marketing platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Viral Loops, Friendbuy) – crucial for scaling and sophisticated tracking. Key features:
    * **Unique Link Generation:** Automated creation of unique referral links.
    * **Real-Time Analytics:** Track referrals, conversions, and ROI.
    * **Segmentation:** Analyze referral performance by source, segment, and referrer tier.
    * **Automated Reward Distribution:**  Handles reward fulfillment automatically.
* **Internal Tracking:** Integrate referral data into Deuce Diary’s existing analytics dashboards for a holistic view.
* **Key Metrics:**
    * **Referral Rate:** Number of referrals per active user.
    * **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of referred users who become paying subscribers.
    * **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) via Referral:** Cost of acquiring a new user through the referral program.
    * **Average Referral Depth:** Number of referrals generated by a single user.
