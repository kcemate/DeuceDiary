**Retention Analysis Plan**

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall:** To understand user behavior after initial engagement to identify areas for improvement in onboarding, product value delivery, and user support.
* **Specific:**
    * Identify key drop-off points during the initial days and weeks.
    * Understand the reasons behind user churn.
    *  Quantify the impact of specific interventions (e.g., onboarding improvements, new feature releases) on retention.
    *  Establish baseline retention rates for accurate future comparison.


**II. Definitions & Metrics:**

* **Retention Rate:** Percentage of users who remain active after a specific period.  (Calculated as: (Number of Users at Day X - Number of New Users at Day X) / Number of Users at Day 1 * 100)
* **Active User:**  *Crucially, define "Active."* This will vary depending on your product. Examples:
    * **App:**  Logged in and used key features at least once.
    * **SaaS:**  Used the core product functionality at least once.
    * **Web Product:** Completed a key action (e.g., created a project, uploaded a file).
* **Cohort:** Group of users who started using the product during the same period (e.g., all users who signed up in January).  This is critical for comparing retention across different user groups.
* **Churn Rate:**  Percentage of users who stop being active. (100% - Retention Rate)
