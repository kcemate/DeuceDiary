2. **China (Mainland China):**

   * **Focus:**  Tone & voice are paramount; character encoding/font support, Mobile-first approach, Mandarin simplification & traditional characters, Brand reputation sensitivity, understanding of the *guanxi* (relationships) factor.

   * **Metrics:**  Mobile app downloads/usage, e-commerce sales in localized versions, brand sentiment analysis.

3. **Germany:**

   * **Timeline:** Medium-High priority, ongoing investment.
   * **Metrics:**  Mobile app usage, e-commerce sales, brand perception in Japan.
