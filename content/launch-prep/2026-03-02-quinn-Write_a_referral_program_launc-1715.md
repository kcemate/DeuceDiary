## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive organic growth through word-of-mouth marketing. We’ll focus on a generous, incentivized system, a strategically designed viral loop, robust fraud prevention, and detailed tracking to measure success and optimize the program.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Increase User Acquisition:**  Drive 20% of new Deuce Diary users through referrals within the first 6 months.
* **Boost Engagement:**  Encourage existing users to actively participate in the program and recommend the app.
* **Build Community:** Foster a sense of community around the Deuce Diary brand.


**II. Referral Program Mechanics:**

* **Referrer Rewards:**
    * **Tiered System (Initial Launch - Simple):**
        * **Level 1 (1 Referral):**  1 Month Premium Subscription (Deuce Diary Pro)
        * **Level 2 (3 Referrals):** 3 Month Premium Subscription (Deuce Diary Pro)
        * **Level 3+ (5+ Referrals):** $50 Amazon Gift Card 
    * **Reward Trigger:**  Referee completes their first full 7-day diary entry. This ensures commitment from the referee.
* **Referee Rewards:**
    * **Welcome Offer:** 1 Week Free Premium Subscription upon referring a new user.
    * **Early Access (Future - Phase 2):**  Offer early access to new features for referees who consistently refer and engage.
* **Referral Link Generation:**
    *  Unique, trackable referral links will be generated for each user through the app (built-in within the profile settings).
    *  Option to share via social media with pre-written captions.


**III. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Phase 1 (Launch - Awareness & Initial Drives):**
    * **Email Campaign:** Introduce the referral program to existing users with a compelling email highlighting the benefits for both referrer and referee.  Use engaging visuals and a clear call-to-action.
    * **In-App Notification:** Prompt users to share with a friendly notification highlighting the rewards.
* **Phase 2 (Growth - Leverage Referrals):**
    * **Social Media Contests:** Run occasional contests (e.g., "Refer 3 friends & win a $100 Deuce Diary Gift Card") to incentivize participation.
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relevant wellness & self-care influencers to promote the program.
    * **“Refer a Friend” Button:** Prominently display the referral link on the app's onboarding screens and within the diary interface.
* **Phase 3 (Retention - Ongoing Engagement):**
    * **Gamification:**  Introduce badges or points for referring multiple users or referring users who also complete their diary entries.
    * **Regular Updates:**  Highlight successful referrals within the Deuce Diary community forum or social media channels.



**IV. Fraud Prevention:**

* **IP Address Monitoring:** Track referral traffic originating from suspicious IP addresses.  Set thresholds for volume and unusual patterns.
* **Account Verification:** Implement a basic account verification process (e.g., email confirmation) to reduce the risk of fake accounts.
* **Manual Review:**  Establish a process for the team to manually review suspicious referral links and accounts.
* **Referral Link Limits:** Implement limits on the number of referrals a user can generate per day/week.
* **Monitor for Bot Activity:** Utilize tools to identify and block referral traffic originating from bots.

**V. Tracking Implementation:**

* **Dedicated Referral Tracking System:** Integrate a dedicated referral tracking platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Ambassador, ReferralDog) to manage the program and collect data.
