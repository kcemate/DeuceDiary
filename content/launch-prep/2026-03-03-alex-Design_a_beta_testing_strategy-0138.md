**1. Overall Philosophy:**

* **Phased Rollout:** We'll avoid a massive, sudden release. This minimizes risk and allows us to adapt based on early feedback.
* **Focused Feedback:** We’re not just looking for “it works.” We need actionable insights into usability, content quality, and areas for improvement.
* **Community Building:** Beta testers become early advocates, and their input helps shape the product's direction.
