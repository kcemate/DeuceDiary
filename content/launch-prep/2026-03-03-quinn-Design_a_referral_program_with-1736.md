Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a detailed plan, broken down into sections, with different tiers and variations to suit various business models.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name Ideas:** "Spread the Word," "Refer & Reward," "Team Boost," "[Your Brand Name] Circle"
* **Visuals:**  Use a consistent visual theme for the program – a badge, graphic, or icon – to make it recognizable.

**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Who are you trying to attract with referrals?** (Existing customers, specific demographics, new users?)
* **What are your goals?** (Increase user base, drive sales, build brand awareness, acquire new leads?)

**3. Referral Tiers & Incentives:**

This is the core of the program.  Offer a tiered system to motivate different levels of participation.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy to Achieve)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** 10% off their next purchase. (This is a low-risk, attractive incentive)
    * **Requirement:**  Referral must make a purchase of a minimum value (e.g., $25).
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Reward:** 5% discount on their first purchase. 
* **Ease of Sharing:**  Simple shareable links, pre-written social media posts, email templates.


**Tier 2:  Mid-Level Referral (Requires More Engagement)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** $25 Store Credit
    * **Requirement:**  Referral must make a purchase of a minimum value ($75) AND be an active user for 30 days.
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Reward:** 10% discount on their first purchase.
* **Increased Sharing Options:**  Referral codes, referral links that track conversions.

**Tier 3:  VIP Referral (Highest Engagement -  For Top Users)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** $50 Store Credit OR  A free premium product/service upgrade (if applicable).
    * **Requirement:** Referral must make a purchase of a minimum value ($150) AND be an active user for 90 days.
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Reward:** 15% discount on their first purchase + a small welcome gift (e.g., branded item).
* **Exclusive Features:**  Priority support, early access to new products, personalized recommendations.

**4. Incentive Types (Beyond Discounts):**

* **Cash Rewards:** (Especially effective for services or SaaS)
* **Gift Cards:** (Popular and versatile)
* **Store Credit:** (Encourages continued spending)
* **Free Products/Services:** (High perceived value)
* **Experiences:** (Tickets to events, subscriptions – if relevant to your brand)
* **Loyalty Points:**  Stackable points that can be redeemed for various rewards.


**5. Mechanics & Technology:**

* **Referral Link Generation:** Your system *must* easily generate unique referral links or codes.
* **Tracking:** Robust tracking is essential to measure program performance. (Google Analytics, referral software, CRM integration)
* **Automated Email Flows:**
    * **Welcome Email (for new referrals):**  Introduce the program and guide them on how to earn rewards.
    * **Notification Email (for the referrer):**  Inform them when a referral makes a purchase or meets a requirement.
    * **Thank You Email (for both parties):**  Reinforce the positive experience.
