**Product Launch Retrospective - Week 1**

**Date:** [Date of Retrospective]
**Product:** [Product Name]
**Launch Date:** [Launch Date]
**Attendees:** [List of Attendees - Include Roles]
**Facilitator:** [Name of Facilitator]

**I. Goal of this Retrospective:**

To quickly assess the initial launch performance, identify key successes and challenges, and establish a prioritized action plan for the next week.  This isn’t about blame – it’s about learning and improving.

**II. Methodology:**

We'll use a combination of:
* **Round Robin:** Each team member briefly shares their top 1-2 observations.
* **Dot Voting:**  Prioritize issues and ideas based on impact.
* **Action Item Assignment:**  Assign owners and deadlines for follow-up actions.


**III. Metrics & Discussion - Week 1 Focus**

**(A) Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) – Tracked Daily (or at least every 6-8 hours)**

* **1. Acquisition:**
    * **Downloads/Installs (Platform-Specific):** [Number] -  *Where did the majority of installs come from? (App Store, Google Play, Website, etc.)*
    * **Website Traffic:** [Number] - *Overall traffic, traffic to key landing pages.*
    * **New User Sign-Ups:** [Number] - *Track different sign-up methods.*
    * **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** [Dollar Amount - estimate] - *How much are we spending to acquire each new user?*

* **2. Activation & Engagement:**
    * **First Session Rate:** [%] - *Percentage of users who open the app/product for the first time.*
    * **Key Feature Usage (Top 3):** [Percentage of Users Using Each] - *Which features are users immediately interacting with? (e.g., Onboarding flow, primary CTA).*
    * **Session Length (Average):** [Minutes] - *How long are users spending with the product in a single session?*
    * **Number of Sessions Per User (Average):** [Number] – *How frequently are users returning?*

* **3. Retention:**
    * **Day 1 Retention:** [%] - *Percentage of users who return the day after signing up.* (This is *crucial* to watch)
    * **Day 7 Retention:** [%] - *Percentage of users who return 7 days after signing up.*

* **4. Technical (Briefly):**
    * **Crash Rate:** [%] - *Number of crashes per install/user.* (Significant spikes need immediate investigation)
    * **Error Rate (Most Frequent):**  [Brief description] – *What are users encountering most frequently?*

**(B)  Discussion Prompts – Group into Categories**

* **1. What Went Really Well? (Dot Voting – Prioritize)**
    *  What surprised us positively?
    *  Which marketing channels performed best?
    *  Was the onboarding process smooth and effective?
    *  Were there any unexpected positive user behaviors?

* **2. What Went Wrong? (Dot Voting – Prioritize)**
    *  What technical issues did users report?
    *  Were there any usability problems identified?
    *  Were there any gaps in the user experience?
    *  Did any marketing campaigns underperform?
    *  Were there any issues with customer support?
