**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (as of late 2023/early 2024):**

3. **Privacy Issues:**  Not disclosing data collection practices adequately, using tracking technologies without consent, or violating privacy laws (like GDPR or CCPA).
4. **Intellectual Property Infringement:** Using copyrighted material (music, video, artwork, etc.) without licenses.
5. **Malware/Security Risks:** Apps containing viruses, trojans, or otherwise posing a security threat to users’ devices.
6. **Functionality Issues:**  App crashes, bugs, or broken functionality that significantly impacts the user experience.
7. **Violations of Content Restrictions:**  This covers everything from mature content, hate speech, violence, and illegal activities.
8. **Apple Trademark/Brand Misuse:** Using Apple trademarks in a way that suggests affiliation or endorsement without permission.
9. **Fake Reviews/Ratings:**  Using bots or fraudulent means to artificially inflate positive reviews.
10. **Unnecessary Permissions:** Asking for permissions that aren’t directly related to the app's core functionality.
