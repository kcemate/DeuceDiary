**Important Note:** This calendar assumes you’re aiming for a consistent level of engagement, not just big bursts.  Regular, smaller updates and promotions are more effective than infrequent, massive campaigns.

**Q1: January - March – Focus: New Year's Resolutions & Goal Setting**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Achievement, Self-Improvement
* **January (New Year, New You):**
    * **Tactics:**
        * Launch a “New Year, New You” promotion highlighting how your app helps users achieve their goals. (e.g., fitness tracker, habit builder, productivity app)
        * Offer a limited-time discount or free trial.
        * Create a blog post or social media content series focused on setting realistic goals.
        * App Store Optimization (ASO):  Update keywords to reflect “new year,” “goals,” “self-improvement.”
    * **KPIs:** App Downloads, New User Registrations, Free Trial Conversions, Website Traffic
* **February (Love & Connection):** (Adapt if your app is relationship-focused)
    * **Tactics:**  Run a contest or giveaway related to connection or self-care.  Highlight features that support relationships (dating apps, communication apps, mental wellness apps)
    * **KPIs:** App Downloads, Engagement within the app (e.g., message frequency), Social Media Engagement
* **March (Spring Prep):**
    * **Tactics:** Transition to a theme of preparation for Spring. (Fitness apps – ‘Get ready for running’, Health apps – ‘Spring Detox’)
    * **KPIs:**  Conversion Rates, Active Users



**Q2: April - June – Focus: Seasonal Shifts & Community Building**

* **Theme:** Spring Renewal, Summer Excitement, Community Engagement
* **April (Easter/Spring):**
    * **Tactics:**  If relevant to your app, run a themed campaign. (e.g., family activity apps, recipe apps). Promote features that support outdoor activities (hiking, camping apps).
    * **KPIs:** App Downloads, In-App Purchases (if applicable), User Engagement
* **May (Mental Health Awareness Month):**
    * **Tactics:**  Highlight mental wellness features within your app. Partner with mental health influencers or organizations.  Run a social media campaign promoting self-care.
    * **KPIs:** App Downloads, Usage of Mental Wellness Features, Social Media Reach & Engagement
* **June (Summer Starts):**
    * **Tactics:** Focus on summer-related activities. (Travel apps, entertainment apps, summer learning apps).  Run contests and giveaways.
    * **KPIs:** App Downloads, In-App Purchases (if applicable), User Retention
