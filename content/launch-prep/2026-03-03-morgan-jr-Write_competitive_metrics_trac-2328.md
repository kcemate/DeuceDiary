## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a structured approach to tracking and analyzing your competition, providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making. It’s designed to be adaptable and scalable based on your business size and resources.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deep understanding of the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and inform strategic decisions related to [Your Business Area - e.g., Product Development, Marketing, Pricing].
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths/weaknesses.
    * Monitor competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns).
    * Benchmark your performance against the competition.
    * Identify emerging trends and disruptive technologies.
    * Understand customer perception of your competitors.


**II. Key Competitors & Categories:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):** Businesses offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi) – Focus on deep, continuous monitoring.
* **Tier 2 (Adjacent Competitors):** Businesses offering related products/services or targeting a slightly different segment of the market. (e.g., Netflix vs. Hulu) – Monitor regularly for significant changes.
* **Tier 3 (Potential Disruptors):**  Newer companies or technologies posing a potential threat. (e.g., Tesla vs. Traditional Auto Manufacturers) – Keep a watchful eye for innovation and shifting trends.
