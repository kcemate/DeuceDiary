**II. Phase 1: Initial Response (First 24-48 Hours - High Priority)**

* **Trigger Events & Response Actions:**
    * **Website/App Crash:**
        * **Immediate:** Technical Lead confirms issue, initiate immediate troubleshooting.
        * **Communication:**  Automated notification on website/app acknowledging issue and estimated resolution time.  Social media announcement: “We’re aware of a temporary issue and our team is working hard to resolve it.  We appreciate your patience!”

        * **Assessment:** Communications Lead analyzes sentiment – is it isolated, widespread, and what’s the core concern?

        * **Assessment:**  Communications Lead reviews the review - Is it factually inaccurate? Is it purely opinion?

* **Escalation:**  If the situation is beyond the team’s capacity, immediately escalate to the Lead.

* **Technical Troubleshooting:**  Prioritize resolving technical issues. Provide regular updates to the public.
* **Proactive Communication:**  Announcements on social media addressing key concerns and providing updates on resolutions.
