Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here’s a breakdown, covering different levels of complexity and potential rewards, along with considerations for implementation.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Example:** “Share the [Your Brand Name] Love” or “[Your Brand Name] Rewards” -  Choose a name that’s catchy and reflects the program's purpose.

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **How it Works:**  Simple and easy for both the referrer and the referred.
    * **Referrer:**  Gets a unique referral link or code.
    * **Referred:**  Clicks the link/uses the code to sign up or make a purchase.
* **Tracking:**  Crucially important! Use a referral marketing platform (like ReferralCandy, Smile, Ambassador, or even build your own with integrations) to accurately track referrals and reward payouts.

**3. Incentive Levels (Tiered Structure is Highly Recommended):**

Here’s a tiered system, with increasing rewards as the referrer refers more people:

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy to Achieve)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  10% off their next purchase.
    * **Referred Reward:**  10% off their first purchase.
    * **Trigger:** First referral.
    * **Goal:**  Drive initial awareness and encourage first-time users to try the product/service.

* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level Referral (Moderate Effort)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** $10 Credit / Store Credit
    * **Referred Reward:** 15% off their first purchase.
    * **Trigger:**  2nd referral.
    * **Goal:**  Encourage deeper engagement and repeat purchases.

* **Tier 3:  Top-Tier Referral (Significant Effort - for High Performers)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** $25 Credit / Store Credit or a Free Premium Feature (if applicable)
    * **Referred Reward:** 20% off their first purchase + Free Shipping (if possible)
    * **Trigger:** 5th referral.
    * **Goal:**  Reward loyal advocates and maximize lifetime value.


**4.  Types of Rewards Beyond Monetary:**

* **Exclusive Access:**  Early access to new products, beta testing, or exclusive content.
* **VIP Status:**  Elevated customer service, personalized support, or faster response times.
* **Points System:**  Allow users to earn points for referrals that can be redeemed for rewards.
* **Contests & Giveaways:**  Referral programs can be tied to contests with larger prizes.  "Refer 3 friends and you'll be entered to win a [Grand Prize]!"



**5.  Communication & Promotion:**

* **Landing Page:**  Create a dedicated landing page explaining the program in detail.
* **Email Marketing:**  Automated emails to:
    * **New Users:** Introduce them to the referral program upon signup.
    * **Existing Users:**  Remind them about the program and highlight successful referrals.
    * **Referrers:** Notify them of successful referrals and available rewards.
* **Social Media:**  Promote the program on all your social media channels. Use visually appealing graphics and videos.  Run referral contests.
* **Website Integration:**  Place referral links prominently on your website (footer, product pages, checkout page).
* **In-App Notifications (if applicable):** Encourage referrals within your mobile app.
