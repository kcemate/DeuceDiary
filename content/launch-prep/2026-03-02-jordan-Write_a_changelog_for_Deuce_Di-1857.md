## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 Changelog - Launch!

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Welcome to Deuce Diary!** This launch marks the initial release of our productivity journaling app, designed to help you track your habits, set goals, and reflect on your day. This version includes core features and represents a significant step towards our vision of a powerful and insightful personal journal.

**I. Social Features:**

* **Public Diary Access (Limited Beta):**  Enable the option to make your diary publicly accessible to other Deuce users (controlled via user settings).  *Note: This is a limited beta feature and may experience occasional performance hiccups.*
* **Follow & Connect:**  Users can now follow other users and view their publicly shared diary entries.
* **Commenting (Beta):**  Users can leave comments on public diary entries.  *Note: Moderation is not yet implemented. Use responsibly.*
* **Friend Requests:**  Initiate connection requests with other Deuce users.
* **Searchable Public Diaries:** Ability to search for public diaries based on user name or keywords within entries.


**II. Tracking Features:**

* **Habit Tracking:** Core functionality to add and track daily habits with customizable reminders.
* **Daily Journal Entry:** A dedicated space to record your thoughts, feelings, and experiences of the day.
* **Goal Setting:** Define short-term and long-term goals directly within your diary entries and link them to habits.
* **Habit Streak Tracking:**  Automatically tracks and displays your current streak for each habit.
* **Customizable Entry Types:** Option to define custom entry types beyond standard daily journaling (e.g., “Meditation,” “Workout,” “Reading”).
* **Tagging:** Ability to tag diary entries with relevant keywords for easy searching and analysis.


**III. Gamification Features:**

* **Points System:** Earn points for completing habits, achieving goals, and writing daily entries.
* **Leveling System:**  Progress through levels based on accumulated points, unlocking cosmetic rewards.
* **Leaderboards (Beta):**  A leaderboard displaying top users based on points earned. *Note: This leaderboard is currently limited in size and may experience performance issues.*
* **Milestone Achievements:** Earn achievements for specific milestones, such as reaching a streak goal or completing a certain number of entries.
* **Daily Challenges (Coming Soon - Planned for v1.1):**  Introducing optional daily challenges to encourage consistent habit tracking.


**IV. UI/UX Improvements:**

* **Clean & Minimalist Design:**  A focus on a distraction-free journaling experience.
* **Intuitive Navigation:** Streamlined navigation for easy access to all features.
* **Dark Mode Support:**  Added a Dark Mode option for comfortable use in low-light conditions.
* **Improved Entry Editor:**  Enhanced text editor with formatting options (bold, italics, lists) for more expressive journaling.
* **Mobile-First Design:** Optimized for both iOS and Android devices.


**V. Infrastructure & Technical:**

* **Cloud-Based Storage:** All diary entries are stored securely in the cloud, ensuring data backup and synchronization across devices.
* **Cross-Platform Synchronization:**  Seamlessly synchronize your diary data between iOS, Android, and Web apps.
* **Initial Server Infrastructure Setup:**  Successfully launched the initial server infrastructure to support user accounts and data storage.
* **Beta Testing Feedback Incorporated:**  Significant improvements and bug fixes based on initial beta testing feedback.
* **Basic Analytics Tracking:**  Enabled basic analytics tracking to monitor app usage and identify areas for improvement.
