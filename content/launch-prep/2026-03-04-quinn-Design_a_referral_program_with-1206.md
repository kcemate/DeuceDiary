# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a detailed plan, broken down into sections, with varying levels of complexity to suit different business types and budgets.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Catchy Name:** “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “[Your Brand] Community,” “[Your Brand] Perks”
* **Visual Identity:**  A simple logo or graphic that clearly represents the referral program.


**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **How it Works:**
    * **Referrer:** A customer who refers a new customer.
    * **New Customer:** The person who signs up or makes a purchase through the referrer's link/code.
* **Tracking:** Crucial!  You'll need a system to track referrals. This could be:
    * **Dedicated Referral Software:** (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Ambassador) - Offers robust features, analytics, and management. (Cost: $50-$500/month)
    * **Custom Tracking with Website/App Integration:**  (More technical, requires development) – Track using unique referral links or codes. (Cost: Variable - depends on development effort)
    * **Simple Spreadsheet (for very small businesses):**  Manual tracking can work initially, but quickly becomes unmanageable.


**3. Incentive Structure – Tiered Rewards:**

This is where you’ll really attract participation. Offer a tiered system to reward both the referrer and the new customer.  Here's a breakdown of potential incentives:

**Tier 1: Basic (For Every Successful Referral)**

* **Referrer Reward:**
    * **Small Discount:** 10-15% off their next purchase.
    * **Store Credit:** $5 - $10 credit to use on the website.
    * **Loyalty Points:**  100-200 loyalty points (if you have a loyalty program).
* **New Customer Reward:**
    * **Welcome Discount:** 10-15% off their first purchase.
    * **Free Shipping:** (If applicable to your business)

**Tier 2:  Mid-Level (For Referrals Leading to a Purchase)**

* **Referrer Reward:**
    * **Increased Discount:** 20-25% off their next purchase.
    * **Larger Store Credit:** $15 - $25
    * **Exclusive Product/Access:**  Early access to new products, a limited-edition item, or a VIP experience.
* **New Customer Reward:**
    * **Bonus Discount:** 15-20% off their first purchase *and* Free Shipping.
    * **Double Loyalty Points:** (If applicable)


**Tier 3:  High-Level (For Multiple Referrals/Significant Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:**
    * **Significant Discount:** 30-40% off their next purchase.
    * **Premium Gift:**  A higher-value gift or product.
    * **Gift Card:**  $50 - $100 gift card to your store or a related business.
    * **VIP Status:**  Exclusive benefits like priority support, personalized recommendations, etc.
* **New Customer Reward:**
    * **Enhanced Welcome Offer:**  Larger discount, bundle offer, or a free upgrade.



**4. Promotion & Communication:**

* **Website Banner:** Prominent placement on your website.
* **Email Marketing:**
    * **Welcome Email:** Introduce the program to new customers.
    * **Referral Campaign Emails:**  Highlight the benefits and make it easy to share.
    * **Referral Reminders:**  Send occasional reminders to customers about the program.
