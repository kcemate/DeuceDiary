Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different purpose and with varying lengths. I've included estimated timings and suggested content. Remember to tailor these to your specific brand voice and target audience.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build initial excitement, and get the subscriber to engage.
* **Timing:** Day 1 – Day 7
* **Audience:** New Subscribers (Anyone who signs up through a landing page or form)

**Sequence:**

* **Day 1: Welcome Email (“We’re Thrilled You’re Here!”)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:**  A warm welcome, express gratitude for subscribing, briefly explain what they can expect from you (e.g., "Get exclusive deals, helpful tips, and be the first to know about new products."), and include a friendly photo/logo. Offer a small welcome gift (e.g., 10% off first order, free ebook).
    * **Call to Action:** “Explore our website” (link to your homepage) or “Claim your welcome discount!”

* **Day 3: Brand Story/Values Email (“Meet the Team”)**
    * **Subject:** The Story Behind [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:** Share the story of your brand – why you started it, your mission, and your core values.  Humanize your brand by briefly introducing the team.  Use a compelling image or video.
    * **Call to Action:** “Learn More About Us” (link to your “About Us” page)

* **Day 5: Highlight a Top Product/Service (“Problem Solver”)**
    * **Subject:** Solve [Pain Point] with [Product Name]
    * **Content:** Focus on one of your most popular products or services. Clearly state the problem it solves for the customer and highlight its key benefits.  Include a compelling image or short video demo.
    * **Call to Action:** “Shop Now” (link to the product page) or “Learn More”

* **Day 7 (Optional):  Social Proof Email (“What Others Are Saying”)**
    * **Subject:** Don’t Just Take Our Word For It…
    * **Content:** Include a short customer testimonial or review.  You can also link to your social media pages.
    * **Call to Action:** “Read More Reviews” or “Follow Us on [Social Media Platform]”



**Campaign 2: Nurturing Lead (7-14 Days - Triggered by Website Behavior)**

* **Goal:**  Build trust, provide value, and move leads closer to a purchase.  *This sequence is best triggered when someone visits specific pages on your website (e.g., product pages, blog posts).*
* **Timing:** Day 7 - Day 14 (after a specific website interaction)
* **Audience:** Users who have visited key pages on your website (e.g., product pages, resource library)

**Sequence:**

* **Day 7:  Content Pillar Email (“Helpful Resource”)**
    * **Subject:**  [Relevant Topic] - Your Guide to [Benefit]
    * **Content:**  Send a link to a valuable piece of content (blog post, ebook, infographic) that directly addresses the topic the user was browsing on your site.  Provide a brief introduction to the content.
    * **Call to Action:** “Read the Full Guide” (link to your blog post/resource).

* **Day 10:  Product Feature Spotlight (“Digging Deeper”)**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock [Benefit] with [Product Feature]
