## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a strategy for consistently tracking and analyzing your competition to inform strategic decisions, identify opportunities, and proactively respond to threats.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  To gain a competitive advantage by understanding your competitors’ strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and market positioning.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor activities and trends.
    * Understand competitor product/service offerings and pricing.
    * Analyze competitor marketing and sales strategies.
    * Monitor competitor performance and identify areas for improvement in your own business.
    * Proactively anticipate competitor reactions to your actions.

**2. Scope & Competitors:**

* **Define Your Core Competitors:**  Don't just list names. Categorize them based on:
    * **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering similar products/services to the same target audience.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering different solutions that address the same customer need.
    * **Potential Competitors:**  Companies entering your market or developing similar offerings.
* **Prioritize Competitors:** Rank your competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:**  Percentage of the total market they control.
    * **Growth Rate:**  How quickly they are expanding.
    * **Innovation:**  Their ability to introduce new products/services.
    * **Brand Strength:**  Customer perception and loyalty.
* **Competitor Matrix:** Create a matrix to visualize your competitors across key metrics (see Section 4).
