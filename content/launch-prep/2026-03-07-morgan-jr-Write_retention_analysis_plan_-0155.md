**I. Executive Summary:**

This plan outlines a structured approach to measuring and improving customer retention. The analysis will be data-driven and informed by customer feedback.

**II. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary Goal:** Reduce overall customer churn rate.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify the primary drivers of churn within the first 7 days.
    * Understand the reasons for churn in the 30-day window, focusing on longer-term issues.
    * Establish baseline retention rates for key segments.
    * Develop and implement targeted interventions based on identified churn drivers.

**III. Data Collection & Sources:**

* **Customer Surveys:** (Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Satisfaction (CSAT), Exit Surveys - triggered upon churn) – Direct feedback on customer experience.
* **Marketing Automation Data:** (Email open/click rates, website behavior) –  Engagement with marketing communications.
