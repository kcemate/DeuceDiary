## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently tracking and analyzing your competition, driving strategic decisions and informing your own business growth.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deep understanding of your competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and ultimately, improve your own business performance.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * **Identify Key Competitors:** Define who your primary competitors are (direct, indirect, and potential).
    * **Understand Their Strategies:** Analyze their product offerings, pricing, marketing, customer service, and overall business model.
    * **Monitor Their Performance:** Track their market share, growth rates, and customer satisfaction.
    * **Identify Gaps & Opportunities:**  Uncover areas where you can differentiate yourself and outperform them.
    * **Inform Strategic Decisions:** Use competitive insights to shape your product roadmap, marketing campaigns, and overall business strategy.


**II.  Key Metrics & Data Sources:**

This section details the specific metrics you’ll track, categorized for clarity:

**A. Market Share & Growth:**

* **Metric:** Market Share (by segment) - % of total market captured
* **Data Source:** Industry reports (Gartner, Forrester, Statista), company annual reports (publicly available), market research firms, internal sales data
* **Frequency:** Quarterly/Annually

* **Metric:** Revenue Growth Rate – % change in revenue compared to the previous period
* **Data Source:** Company annual reports, financial news outlets, industry databases
* **Frequency:** Quarterly/Annually

* **Metric:** Customer Acquisition Rate - Number of new customers acquired per period
* **Data Source:** Internal Sales Data, Marketing Analytics
* **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly


**B. Product & Offering:**

* **Metric:** New Product Launches – Number & frequency of new product releases
* **Data Source:** Company websites, press releases, product listings, social media, competitor blogs
* **Frequency:** Real-time monitoring, Weekly/Monthly

* **Metric:** Feature Updates – Tracking new features and improvements
* **Data Source:** Same as New Product Launches
* **Frequency:** Weekly/Monthly

* **Metric:** Product Reviews & Ratings - Average rating across major platforms (e.g., Capterra, G2, Trustpilot)
* **Data Source:** Review platforms, social media mentions
* **Frequency:** Weekly/Monthly


**C. Pricing & Promotions:**

* **Metric:** Average Selling Price (ASP) – Average price of your products/services
* **Data Source:** Internal Sales Data, Ecommerce platforms
* **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly

* **Metric:** Discount & Promotion Frequency - Number and type of promotions offered
* **Data Source:** Competitor websites, marketing emails, social media
* **Frequency:** Weekly/Monthly

* **Metric:** Bundled Offers – Analysis of product bundles and their price points
* **Data Source:** Competitor websites, promotional materials
* **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly


**D. Marketing & Sales:**

* **Metric:** Website Traffic & Engagement – Visitors, bounce rate, time on site
* **Data Source:** Google Analytics, SimilarWeb
* **Frequency:** Weekly/Monthly

* **Metric:** Social Media Presence – Followers, engagement rate, content reach
* **Data Source:** Social Media Analytics (Facebook Insights, Twitter Analytics, LinkedIn Analytics)
* **Frequency:** Weekly/Monthly

* **Metric:** Advertising Spend & Performance – Spend on various channels (Google Ads, Social Media Ads) and conversion rates
* **Data Source:** Advertising Platform Reports, Marketing Automation Platform
* **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly

* **Metric:** Lead Generation - Number of leads generated from different sources
* **Data Source:** CRM System, Marketing Automation Platform
* **Frequency:** Monthly/Quarterly
