Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencers, with variations to suit different levels of engagement and your brand's voice.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template (Focus: Feedback & Partnership)**

**Subject: Exclusive Access: [Product Name] Beta Program - Help Us Shape the Future!**

**Option 1 (Formal & Detailed):**

```email
Hi [Beta Tester Name],

We're thrilled to invite you to join our exclusive beta program for [Product Name]! We’re incredibly excited about this project and believe your feedback is crucial to ensuring it’s the best possible product.

[Product Name] is a [brief, compelling description – 1-2 sentences].  We're looking for feedback on [mention key areas you’re focusing on – e.g., usability, features, performance].

**Here’s what you’ll get:**

* **Early access** to [Product Name]
* **Dedicated support** from our team
* **The opportunity to shape the product’s direction** through your honest feedback.

**To get started:** [Link to onboarding materials/instructions]

**We’d really appreciate it if you could:** [Specific request - e.g., Use the product for X days,  Focus on Feature A and Feature B,  Share your thoughts in our dedicated forum here: [Link to Forum]]

Thank you for your time and commitment to helping us build something amazing!

Best regards,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```

**Option 2 (More Casual & Enthusiastic):**

```email
Hey [Beta Tester Name],

Guess what?! You're one of the first to get access to our new [Product Name]! We're so excited and we need your awesome feedback to make it even better.

[Product Name] is a [brief, exciting description]. We’re really keen to hear how you're finding it and what you think could be improved.

We'd love it if you could [Specific request – e.g.,  Try out the main features and tell us what you think,  Let us know if anything is confusing or doesn't work as expected].

You can find all the info you need here: [Link to onboarding materials/instructions]

Thanks a bunch!

Cheers,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```

**Key Considerations for Beta Testers:**

* **Clearly define expectations:**  Be specific about what you want them to do and the level of commitment required.
* **Provide support:**  Let them know you’re there to answer questions.
* **Express gratitude:**  Acknowledge their valuable contribution.
