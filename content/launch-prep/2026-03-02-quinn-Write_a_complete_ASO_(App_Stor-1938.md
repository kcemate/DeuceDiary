**III. Visual Assets Audit**

* **Problem:**  Weak Visuals – blurry screenshots, generic icon, lack of a compelling video.
* **Recommendations:**
    * **High-Quality Screenshots:**  Showcase key features in action: user interface, social feed, challenge creation, group participation, progress tracking.  Use attractive, vibrant graphics.
    * **App Preview Video (15-30 seconds):**  A dynamic video demonstrating the app's core value proposition - users connecting, participating in challenges, and feeling supported.  Use upbeat music and clear visuals.

**IV. Conversion Funnel Analysis & Optimization**

* **Current Funnel:**  Download -> Onboarding (Tutorials) -> Account Creation -> Feature Exploration -> Ongoing Engagement
* **Potential Bottlenecks:**
    * **Onboarding:** Too much information, overwhelming tutorials.
    * **Account Creation:**  High friction - requires too many fields.
* **Recommendations:**
    * **Simplified Onboarding:**  Implement a quick, interactive tutorial focusing on the most important features. Offer a progress bar and clear calls to action.
    * **Reduced Account Fields:**  Only collect essential information (email, password). Offer social login options.
