Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product/service, broken down by week and with suggested content types. This assumes a typical launch with some initial buzz. **Important:** This is a template – you'll need to adapt it heavily based on your specific product, target audience, launch goals, and the actual results you're seeing.

**Overall Launch Goals (Example - Adapt to Yours):**

*   **Week 1:** Drive Awareness – Get the product in front of as many people as possible.
*   **Week 2:** Build Excitement & Early Adoption – Encourage trial and initial purchases.
*   **Week 3:**  Showcase Value & Address FAQs – Demonstrate the product’s benefits and answer common questions.
*   **Week 4:**  Gather Feedback & Community Building – Focus on user experience and fostering a community.


---

**Week 1: Ignition - Awareness & Initial Buzz**

*   **Monday:** **Launch Announcement (All Platforms)** -  Big, bold announcement! Video, blog post, social media campaign.  Link to landing page.  *Content Type: Video, Blog Post, Social Media (Image/Video)*
*   **Tuesday:** **Behind-the-Scenes Post (Social Media - Instagram, Facebook)** -  Humanize the launch – show the team, the process, the inspiration. *Content Type: Photo/Short Video*
*   **Wednesday:** **Influencer Outreach (Social Media - Twitter, Instagram)** -  Send product to key influencers for early reviews/mentions. *Content Type:  Reach Out - Tracking responses & influencer posts*
*   **Thursday:** **"What is [Product Name]?" Explainer Post (Blog, LinkedIn)** – A clear, concise definition of your product and its core value proposition. *Content Type: Blog Post, LinkedIn Article*
*   **Friday:** **Launch Day Recap & Giveaway (Social Media)** – Celebrate the launch, thank people for their support, run a simple giveaway to drive engagement. *Content Type: Social Media (Image/Video, Contest)*

**Week 2:  Get Them Trying It**

*   **Monday:** **Demo Video (YouTube, Website, Social Media)** - Short, engaging demo highlighting key features. *Content Type: Video*
*   **Tuesday:** **"3 Reasons Why You Need [Product Name]" (Social Media, Blog)** – Focus on benefits, not just features. *Content Type: Social Media (Carousel), Blog Post*
*   **Wednesday:** **Early Adopter Spotlight (Social Media - Case Study Snippet)** – Share a positive testimonial or early feedback from a user. *Content Type: Quote Graphic, Short Video*
*   **Thursday:** **Limited-Time Offer Announcement (Email, Social Media)** – Create urgency with a launch discount or special offer. *Content Type: Email, Social Media (Image/Video)*
*   **Friday:** **Q&A Session (Live on Social Media - Instagram/Facebook Live)** - Answer user questions about the product in real-time. *Content Type: Live Video*



**Week 3:  Show the Value**

*   **Monday:** **Deep Dive into a Key Feature (Blog Post, YouTube Tutorial)** - Focus on one specific feature and how it solves a problem. *Content Type: Blog Post, Tutorial Video*
*   **Tuesday:** **FAQ Post (Website, Social Media)** – Address frequently asked questions about pricing, usage, compatibility, etc. *Content Type: Listicle, Social Media (Image with Text)*
*   **Wednesday:** **Customer Problem/Solution Story (Blog, Social Media)** - Showcase how your product helps a customer overcome a specific challenge. *Content Type: Blog Post, Case Study Snippet*
