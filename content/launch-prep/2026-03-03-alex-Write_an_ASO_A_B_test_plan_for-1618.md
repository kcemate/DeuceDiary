**Metrics:**

* **Conversion Rate (CVR):** Percentage of users seeing the app page who ultimately install.
* **Retention Rates (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30):**  Helps understand if optimized elements are improving user engagement.
* **Keyword Ranking:** Monitor changes in rankings for key target keywords.
* **App Page Views:** Track overall visibility.
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on App Icon:** Measures visual appeal.
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on App Preview Video:** Assesses video effectiveness.

**Tools:**

* **Sensor Tower/App Annie/Mobile Action:**  For keyword research, tracking, competitor analysis, and A/B testing.
