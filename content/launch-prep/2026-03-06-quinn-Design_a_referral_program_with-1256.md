# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with compelling incentives. Here's a detailed breakdown, broken down into sections with options for different business types and budgets:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Catchy Name:** “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “Level Up with [Your Brand],” “[Your Brand] Army”
* **Visuals:** Incorporate a clear visual element - a badge, icon, or graphic that represents referrals.

**2. Core Mechanics - How it Works**

* **Simple Referral Process:** Make it *extremely* easy for a referred customer to participate.
    * **Unique Referral Link/Code:**  Each referrer gets a unique link or code to share. This is crucial for tracking.
    * **Easy Sharing Options:** Provide buttons for sharing on social media (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram), email, and messaging apps (WhatsApp, Slack).  Pre-written copy for social media is *highly* recommended.
    * **Clear Instructions:** Outline the steps for both the referrer and the referred customer.
* **Tracking:**  Use a referral tracking system (many CRM and marketing automation platforms have built-in features, or you can use a dedicated referral program platform like ReferralCandy, Mention Me, or Ambassador).

**3. Incentives – The Heart of the Program**

This is where you’ll need to tailor the incentives to your business and target audience. Here’s a tiered approach with cost considerations:

**Tier 1: Basic (Lower Cost, Quick Wins)**

* **For the Referrer:** 
    * **Discount Code:** 10-20% off their next purchase. (Cost: Relatively low - percentage of sales)
    * **Small Gift Card:** $5-$10 gift card to your store/service. (Cost: Moderate - depends on the gift card value)
    * **Loyalty Points:**  Award 50-100 points for each successful referral. (Cost: Low – depends on your loyalty program structure)
* **For the Referred Customer:**
    * **Welcome Discount:** 10-15% off their first purchase. (Cost: Low – discount based on average order value)


**Tier 2: Mid-Level (Increased Engagement)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Higher Discount:** 20-30% off their next purchase.
    * **Bonus Loyalty Points:** 100-200 points per referral.
    * **Exclusive Early Access:**  Give referrers early access to new products/services or sales.
    * **Small Gift Basket (Optional):**  For high referral volume, a small curated gift basket related to your brand. (Cost: Moderate – depending on contents)
* **For the Referred Customer:**
    * **Increased Welcome Discount:** 15-20% off their first purchase.
    * **Free Shipping:** (If applicable to your business). (Cost: Moderate – depends on shipping costs)


**Tier 3: Premium (Higher Investment, High Impact - Best for Established Brands)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Significant Cash Reward:** $25-$100 per referral. (Cost: Higher – requires careful budgeting)
    * **Tiered Rewards:** Based on the number of referrals, unlock higher rewards (e.g., $50 for 3 referrals, $100 for 5, etc.).
    * **VIP Experiences:** Offer exclusive experiences related to your brand (e.g., a private consultation, a product demo, a special event invitation).
* **For the Referred Customer:**
    * **Largest Discount/Offer:** 30-50% off, or a bundled offer.
