**Important Note:** This calendar is focused on *App Store Optimization (ASO)*, App Store advertising (Apple Search Ads), and strategic content/promotion. It doesn't include social media marketing, which should be a separate, ongoing effort.

---

**Overall Philosophy:** Each season should have a core theme that aligns with the season’s spirit and the app's benefits.

**Key Metrics to Track:**

*   **Keyword Rankings:** Monitor your app’s visibility in the App Store search results.
*   **Install Volume:**  Track daily and weekly installs.
*   **Conversion Rates:**  Percentage of people who view your app page and then install it.
*   **App Store Page Views:** How many people are looking at your app page.
*   **Cost Per Install (CPI) – for Apple Search Ads:**  Crucial for budget allocation.
