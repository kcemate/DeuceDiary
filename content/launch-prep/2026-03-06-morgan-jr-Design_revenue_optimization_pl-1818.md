**I. Understanding the Current State (Assessment - Weeks 1-4)**

* **1.1 Define Revenue Goals:**
    * **Specific:**  Increase overall revenue by X% within Y timeframe. (e.g., "Increase overall revenue by 15% within 12 months.")
    * **Measurable:**  Set quantifiable metrics (e.g., average order value, customer lifetime value, conversion rates).
    * **Achievable:**  Be realistic based on market conditions and company capabilities.
    * **Relevant:**  Align revenue goals with overall business objectives.
    * **Time-Bound:**  Establish clear deadlines for each goal.
* **1.2 Analyze Current Revenue Streams:**

* **1.3 Data Collection & Analysis Tools:**

    * **Marketing Automation:** Marketo, Pardot – streamline marketing efforts and track campaign performance.
    * **Sales Analytics:**  Salesforce reports, Excel – analyze sales data, identify trends, and forecast revenue.
    * **Customer Feedback:** Surveys, reviews, social media monitoring - understand customer pain points and desires.
