**Scenario 1: Server Crash/Technical Issues**

* **Severity:** High - Immediate user frustration, potential loss of users, reputational damage.
* **Trigger:** Slow loading times, app crashing, inability to login, server errors.
* **Response:**
    * **TL - Immediate Action (0-30 mins):**
        * Diagnose the root cause - Is it high traffic, code error, infrastructure issue?
        * Initiate immediate server scaling/optimization.
        * Communicate internally – everyone aware of the situation.
    * **PRL - Internal Communication (0-15 mins):** Draft a concise, honest statement acknowledging the issue and stating steps being taken. Focus on "We're on it."
    * **PRL & CM - External Communication (15-60 mins):**
        * **Social Media:** Post updates - "We're aware of an issue and working to resolve it ASAP. Thank you for your patience!" Include a link to a status page (if possible).
        * **Discord/Forum (if applicable):**  Active monitoring & immediate responses to user concerns.
        * **Email Subscribers:**  Send a personalized email explaining the situation and estimated resolution time.
    * **TL - Resolution & Monitoring (60+ mins):** Track progress, communicate updates, and prevent recurrence.


**Scenario 2: 1-Star Reviews/Negative Feedback**

* **Severity:** Medium – Represents user dissatisfaction, potential for snowball effect.
* **Trigger:** Significant volume of 1-star reviews, widespread complaints about features, usability, or bugs.
* **Response:**
    * **CM - Monitoring & Triage (Ongoing):**  Closely monitor app store reviews, social media mentions, and any feedback channels. Categorize issues (bug reports, feature requests, negative opinions).
    * **TL & CM – Prioritize & Respond (Within 24-48 hrs):** Acknowledge negative reviews publicly (where appropriate - Reddit, Twitter).  Thank users for their feedback.  Assign bugs/feature requests to the development team.  Prioritize addressing critical issues.
    * **PRL - Sentiment Analysis (Ongoing):** Monitor overall sentiment and adjust messaging as needed.
