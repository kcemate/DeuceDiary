## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for tracking and analyzing your competitive landscape, allowing you to proactively adapt your strategy and maintain a competitive edge.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Understand the competitive landscape to identify opportunities for growth, mitigate risks, and inform strategic decision-making.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths/weaknesses.
    * Monitor competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns, etc.).
    *  Understand competitor customer perception and satisfaction.
    *  Detect potential threats and opportunities.
    *  Inform product development, marketing, sales, and customer service strategies.


**II. Key Metrics & Categories:**

We’ll categorize metrics to ensure a holistic view. Here's a breakdown with examples; prioritize based on your industry and business model:

**A. Product Metrics:**
* **New Product Launches:** Number of products launched, launch timing, initial customer response.
* **Feature Adoption:**  Tracking the usage of competitor features (requires research & possibly tools).
* **Product Reviews & Ratings:** Monitor reviews on major platforms (Amazon, G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, etc.).  Analyze sentiment.
* **Innovation Rate:**  How frequently are they releasing updates and new features?
* **Product Roadmap (Publicly Available):**  Analyze their announced plans – it’s a strong signal.

**B. Pricing & Promotion Metrics:**
* **Pricing Changes:** Track changes in pricing, discounts, and bundles.
* **Promotional Offers:** Monitor the frequency, type, and effectiveness of competitor promotions.
* **Subscription Model Changes (if applicable):**  Analyze changes in tiers, pricing, and benefits.

**C. Marketing & Brand Metrics:**
* **Website Traffic:** Monitor competitor website traffic (using tools like SimilarWeb, SEMrush) – overall and key landing pages.
* **Social Media Engagement:** Track follower count, likes, shares, comments, and reach across relevant platforms.
* **Search Engine Rankings:**  Monitor competitor rankings for key industry keywords.
* **Advertising Spend & Channels:**  Estimate competitor ad spend and channels used (based on SEMrush, iSpot.tv data).
* **Content Marketing:** Analyze competitor blog posts, webinars, ebooks, and videos.

**D. Sales & Customer Metrics:**
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** (Requires your own data) Compare your CAC to estimated competitor CAC.
* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):** (Requires your own data) Compare your CLTV to estimated competitor CLTV.
* **Churn Rate:** (Requires your own data)  Compare your churn rate to estimated competitor churn rates.
* **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) & Net Promoter Score (NPS):**  Monitor competitor scores through review sites and surveys.



**III. Data Sources & Tools:**

* **Manual Research:** Regular website visits, social media monitoring, reviewing industry publications.
* **Web Analytics Tools:** Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics (for your website)
* **SEO Tools:** SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz (for competitor keyword rankings & website analysis)
* **Social Media Monitoring Tools:** Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Brand24
* **Competitive Intelligence Platforms:** SimilarWeb, SpyFu, Crayon (Provide in-depth competitor data)
* **Review Monitoring Tools:**  G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, Yelp
* **Industry Reports & Market Research:** Gartner, Forrester, Statista
