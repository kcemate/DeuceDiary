## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to effectively track and analyze your competitors to inform strategic decisions and improve your own performance. It focuses on gathering relevant data, establishing key metrics, and implementing a robust tracking system.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Gain a deep understanding of your competitors’ strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and market positioning to inform your own business decisions.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their market share.
    * Monitor competitor pricing and promotional strategies.
    * Analyze competitor product offerings and innovation.
    * Track competitor online presence and brand perception.
    *  Identify potential opportunities for differentiation and competitive advantage.


**II. Competitor Identification & Segmentation:**

* **Step 1: Identify Direct Competitors:** List companies offering similar products/services to your target market.
* **Step 2: Identify Indirect Competitors:** List companies offering alternative solutions to your target market needs.
* **Step 3: Categorize Competitors:** Segment competitors based on their:
    * **Size:** Large, Medium, Small
    * **Market Segment:** (e.g., Premium, Budget, Niche)
    * **Strategy:** (e.g., Disruptive, Traditional, Value-focused)
    * **Strength:** (e.g., Strong Brand, Technological Innovation, Extensive Distribution)

**III. Key Metrics & Data Sources:**

| Category             | Metric                               | Data Source                                      | Frequency      | Notes                                                     |
|----------------------|---------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Market Share**      | Market Share (%)                     | Industry Reports (e.g., Gartner, Forrester), Statista | Quarterly/Annual | Crucial for understanding competitive landscape.            |
| **Pricing**          | Average Selling Price (ASP)             | Competitor Websites, Price Comparison Tools,  Retail Reports | Weekly/Monthly  | Track changes, identify price promotions.                |
| **Promotions**       | Promotion Frequency, Discount %          | Competitor Websites, Social Media, Email Marketing   | Weekly/Monthly  | Assess promotional effectiveness and frequency.            |
| **Product/Service**   | Number of Product Lines, New Product Launches | Competitor Websites, Press Releases, Industry News  | Monthly/Quarterly | Track innovation and expansion.                          |
| **Features/Benefits**| Top Features (based on reviews)       | Product Reviews (e.g., Amazon, Capterra), Social Media, Blogs | Monthly/Quarterly | Understand what customers value.                         |
| **Customer Reviews**| Average Rating, Sentiment Analysis      | Review Sites (e.g., Trustpilot, Yelp), Social Media   | Monthly/Quarterly | Gauge customer satisfaction and identify areas for improvement.|
| **Digital Presence**  | Website Traffic, Social Media Followers | SimilarWeb, SEMrush, Social Media Analytics       | Weekly/Monthly  | Track brand awareness and engagement.                     |
| **SEO Performance**  | Keyword Ranking, Domain Authority         | SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz                                | Monthly        | Assess SEO effectiveness.                                  |
| **Content Marketing**| Blog Post Frequency, Content Topics       | Competitor Websites, Social Media                | Monthly/Quarterly | Understand content strategy and identify gaps.            |



**IV. Tracking System & Tools:**

* **Spreadsheet (Google Sheets, Excel):**  Simple, cost-effective for initial tracking and reporting.
* **CRM Integration:**  (Salesforce, HubSpot) –  Link competitor data to customer interactions.
* **Competitive Intelligence Tools:**
    * **SEMrush:** SEO, Keyword Research, Competitor Analysis
    * **Ahrefs:** Keyword Research, Backlink Analysis, Competitor Analysis
