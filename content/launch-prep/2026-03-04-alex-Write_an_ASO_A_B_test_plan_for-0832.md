## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements, focusing on improving install rates and engagement.  We'll break down the elements we'll test, the metrics we'll track, and the process for running and analyzing the tests.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs & Daily Active Users (DAU)
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve Key Metric Engagement (e.g., Session Length, Retention, Feature Usage)
* **Overall Approach:** Data-driven, iterative testing focusing on small, impactful changes.


**II. Elements to Test (Prioritized):**

Here’s a breakdown of elements we'll test, grouped by priority:

**High Priority (Immediate Impact - Short Testing Cycles)**

* **App Icon:** (Most impactful - small changes can drive huge lifts)
    * **Variation A:** Current Icon
    * **Variation B:** Slight color adjustment, subtle change to a key element (e.g., animal in a game icon).
* **App Title:** (Good for keyword relevance and clarity)
    * **Variation A:** Current Title
    * **Variation B:** Adding a relevant keyword (e.g., "Puzzle Games," "Running Tracker") while maintaining readability.
* **Short Description (Subtitle):** (Captures attention in the search results)
    * **Variation A:** Current Subtitle
    * **Variation B:** Rephrasing the subtitle to emphasize a key benefit or feature.


**Medium Priority (Requires Longer Testing - Medium Impact)**

* **Long Description:** (Detailed information - needs careful A/B)
    * **Variation A:** Current Long Description
    * **Variation B:** Adding a customer testimonial/social proof; Restructuring for better readability;  Highlighting a key differentiator.
* **Screenshots & App Previews (Video):** (Visuals are crucial)
    * **Variation A:** Current Screenshots & Video
    * **Variation B:** New screenshots highlighting a specific feature; Short, engaging video showcasing the app's core functionality.
* **Keywords (App Store Search):** (Targeted search terms)
    * (This is less of an A/B test and more of a continuous optimization – focusing on adding/removing terms based on data.)


**Low Priority (Long-Term Experimentation - Smaller Potential Impact)**

* **App Preview Carousel:** (More complex to change; larger potential impact if done correctly)
    * (Testing different visuals and messaging within the carousel)
* **Call to Action Button Text:** (e.g., “Download Now” vs. “Get Started”)


**III. Metrics to Track:**

* **Install Rate (IR):**  The percentage of users who tap on your app's icon and then install the app. (Primary Metric)
* **Conversion Rate (CVR):** The percentage of users who view your app's page and then install. (Important for understanding overall effectiveness)
* **Keyword Rankings:** Track keyword rankings within the App Store Search.
* **App Page Views:** Number of users visiting your app’s page.
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Screenshots/Video:** Percentage of users who view the screenshots and/or video.
* **Average Session Length:**  How long users spend in the app.
* **Retention Rate (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30):**  Percentage of users who return to the app after a certain period.
* **Feature Usage (if applicable):** Track adoption rates of key features.
