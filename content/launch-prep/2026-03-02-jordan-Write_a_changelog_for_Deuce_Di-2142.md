## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 Changelog - Launch!

**Date:** October 26, 2023

**Summary:** We’re thrilled to announce the official launch of Deuce Diary v1.0.0! This release brings a robust set of features designed to help you track your habits, build accountability, and achieve your goals. This is a significant step forward and we’re incredibly excited to see you utilize it.

---

**I. Social Features:**

* **Friend Connections:**
    * **Added:** Ability to connect with friends and followers within the Deuce Diary community.
    * **Functionality:**  Users can search for friends by username and send/accept friend requests.
    * **Note:** Currently limited to 500 connections.
* **Activity Sharing (Optional):**
    * **Added:**  Option to share completed activities and milestones with connected friends.
    * **Functionality:**  Sharing is private by default, but users can choose to make it public or visible only to connections.
    * **Note:** Sharing is designed to be motivational, not competitive. 
* **Activity Comments:**
    * **Added:**  Ability to comment on friend's shared activities.
    * **Functionality:**  Simple text-based commenting system to offer encouragement and support.


**II. Tracking Features:**

* **Habit Creation & Management:**
    * **Added:**  Comprehensive habit creation interface.
    * **Functionality:** Users can define habits with customizable names, descriptions, frequency (daily, weekly, custom), and reminder times.
    * **Supported Habit Types:**  Binary (Yes/No), Numerical (Track quantity - e.g., water intake, steps), and Interval (Track time - e.g., meditation sessions).
* **Activity Logging:**
    * **Added:**  Simple “Mark Complete” button for each tracked habit.
    * **Functionality:**  Easily log completion of a habit for the current day.
* **Streak Tracking:**
    * **Added:**  Automatic calculation and display of streak length for each habit.
    * **Functionality:**  Motivates continued consistency by visualizing current streak.
* **Customizable Tracking Periods:**
    * **Added:** Option to set tracking periods for habits (e.g., 7 days, 30 days, indefinitely).
    * **Functionality:** Tailor tracking based on individual goals and commitment levels.


**III. Gamification Features:**

* **Points System:**
    * **Added:**  Points awarded for completing habits.
    * **Functionality:**  Points contribute to overall progress and provide a reward for consistency. (1 point per completed habit).
* **Badges:**
    * **Added:**  A collection of badges awarded for specific achievements (e.g., “7-Day Streak”, “100 Points Earned”, “First Habit Completed”).
    * **Functionality:**  Provides visual recognition of progress and encourages further engagement.
* **Leaderboard (Limited):**
    * **Added:**  A leaderboard ranking users based on accumulated points (Top 100). 
    * **Functionality:**  Provides a competitive element, but focused on overall progress, not direct comparisons.


**IV. UI/UX Improvements:**

* **Clean & Intuitive Dashboard:**
    * **Redesign:**  Overhauled the main dashboard for improved clarity and ease of navigation.
* **Improved Habit List View:**
    * **Changes:**  Habits are now displayed in a clear, organized list with key information (streak, points, next reminder).
* **Enhanced Onboarding Experience:**
    * **Changes:**  Streamlined the initial setup process with a guided tutorial.
