**Dimensions:** 1200 x 630 pixels (App Store Standard)

**1. Colors:**

* **Dominant:** Deep Charcoal Grey (#333333) - Represents the darkness of the story, motorcycle leather, and a sense of mystery.
* **Accent 1:** Crimson Red (#DC143C) -  Represents passion, danger, and the relationship between Jake and Skye. Used sparingly but powerfully.

* **Background:** Slightly textured charcoal grey - to avoid a flat, sterile look.  A subtle, almost imperceptible, graphic of a tire track could be layered very subtly.


**2. Layout & Composition:**

* **Central Focus:** A close-up, slightly blurred, but evocative image of Jake and Skye riding on a motorcycle.  Their faces should be partially obscured, adding to the mystery.  Ideally, their expressions would be subtly intense - a shared glance, a slight smirk.  They should be wearing their signature leather jackets.
* **Rule of Thirds:** The motorcycle and riders are positioned roughly along the upper third and lower third lines of the graphic, with a negative space in the center to draw the eye.
* **Layered Elements:** The background will have subtle grunge texture layers to add to the 'lived-in' feel of the show.

**3. Device Mockup Placement:**

* **iPhone 15 Pro/Max (Ideal):**  The mockup will be centered, taking up approximately 60% of the vertical space. This reflects the target audience and shows the app's compatibility.
* **Smaller Mockups (Supporting):**  Smaller mockups of an iPad (to show broader compatibility) will be placed in the corners – either top left or top right – subtly demonstrating the app’s usability on different devices.

**4. Text Overlay:**

* **Headline (Large, Chrome Font):** “Deuce Diary” - Bold, impactful, and using a slightly distressed font to match the show’s aesthetic. Positioned prominently across the top, utilizing the metallic silver.
* **Subheadline (Smaller, Chrome Font):** “Love. Betrayal. Motorcycles.” - Positioned directly beneath the headline.  A slightly condensed font.

**5. Brand Elements:**

* **Show Logo:**  The iconic “Deuce Diary” logo (the two dice) should be subtly placed near the motorcycle in the image - perhaps as a reflection on the chrome of the bike or partially visible in the rider's jacket. It shouldn't dominate the graphic.
* **Show Title Font:** Utilize the font used in the show's title cards - a slightly stylized, gritty font, complementing the other typography.
