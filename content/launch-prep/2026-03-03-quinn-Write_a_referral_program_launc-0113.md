## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan - “Spread the Sweetness”

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive user growth through word-of-mouth marketing. The program, titled "Spread the Sweetness," will incentivize both referrers and referees with compelling rewards, incorporating a viral loop and robust fraud prevention measures.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary:** Increase user base by 20% within 3 months of launch.
* **Secondary:** Improve user engagement & retention by 10% through increased content interaction and community participation.
* **Measurable KPIs:**
    * Number of Referrals Generated
    * Conversion Rate (Referrer -> Referee)
    * Referee Acquisition Cost (RAC)
    * Referral Program Engagement Rate (percentage of users actively participating)
    * Viral Coefficient (measure of the program's virality)


**2. Mechanics & Rewards:**

* **Referrer Rewards:**
    * **Tiered System:**
        * **Level 1 (First 5 Referrals):** 1 Month Premium Subscription (automatic upgrade) + 100 “Sweet Treats” (in-app currency – redeemable for exclusive content/badges).
        * **Level 2 (6th+ Referrals):** 50 “Sweet Treats” per referral.
    * **Sustainability:** Tier rewards incentivize ongoing referrals.
* **Referee Rewards:**
    * **Welcome Offer:** 1 Week Premium Subscription upon signing up through a referral link.
    * **Sweet Treat Bonus:**  Initially, receive 50 “Sweet Treats” upon completing their first diary entry and sharing it to their preferred social media platform.
* **Viral Loop Design:**
    * **Share to Social:** Referee earns the Sweet Treats bonus upon sharing their diary entry.  This encourages sharing and extends the reach of Deuce Diary.
    * **Referral Prompt:** Within the app, users will be prompted regularly (especially after a diary entry) to share their referral link.
    * **Referral Link Shortener:** All referral links will be shortened using a service like Bitly for tracking and clean aesthetics.



**3. Technology & Tracking Implementation:**

* **Tracking Platform:**  **AppsFlyer** (or similar robust attribution platform) - Provides granular tracking of referral sources, campaign performance, and user behavior.
* **Referral Software Integration:** **ReferralCandy** or **Setl** - Automates the referral program process, handles reward distribution, and provides reporting. We will carefully evaluate based on pricing and features.
* **Unique Referral Codes:** Each referrer receives a unique code for accurate tracking.  Generated within the referral software.
* **Tracking Metrics:**
    * **Referral Link Clicks:** Track the number of clicks on each referral link.
    * **Conversion Tracking:** Measure how many clicks lead to sign-ups.
    * **User Behavior:** Monitor referral users’ activity (diary entries, engagement, etc.) to assess the impact of the program.
    * **Cohort Analysis:** Track referral cohorts to understand long-term success.

**4. Fraud Prevention:**

* **Manual Review:** Implement a system for manual review of suspicious referral activity (e.g., multiple accounts created with the same referral link).
* **IP Address Restrictions:** Consider implementing IP address restrictions based on geographic location (though this can impact legitimate users).
* **Account Verification:**  Stronger account verification (e.g., phone number verification) for users signing up through referral links.
* **Thresholds & Limits:** Set limits on the number of referrals a user can make within a specific timeframe (to prevent automated bot activity).  
* **Blacklist Monitoring:** Continuously monitor for and block suspicious referral links.
