**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Focus: New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:**  Introduce your brand, build excitement, and encourage engagement.
* **Target Audience:**  New subscribers to your email list.
* **Timing:**  Immediate (as soon as signup) - 30 days

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1: (Immediately - Welcome Email)**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
    * **Content:**  Warm welcome, thank you for joining, brief brand story (1-2 sentences), and a compelling reason to stay (e.g., exclusive discounts, valuable content).  Include a friendly image.
    * **Call to Action:** “Explore our [Top Product/Service]” (Link)
* **Email 2: (3 Days Later - Value Proposition)**
    * **Subject:**  Discover [Benefit 1] and [Benefit 2] with [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:** Highlight 2-3 key benefits your product/service offers.  Use short, benefit-focused copy.  Include a customer testimonial if available.
    * **Call to Action:** “Learn More About Our Benefits” (Link to your ‘About’ page or a benefits landing page).
* **Email 3: (7 Days Later - Content Teaser)**
    * **Subject:**  Your Guide to [Relevant Topic] is Here!
    * **Content:**  Introduce a valuable piece of content (blog post, ebook, video).  Provide a short excerpt to entice the reader.
    * **Call to Action:** “Read the Full Guide” (Link)
* **Email 4: (14 Days Later –  Social Proof)**
    * **Subject:**  See What Others Are Saying About [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:** Showcase positive reviews, social media mentions, or user-generated content.
    * **Call to Action:** “Read More Reviews” (Link to your reviews page)
* **Email 5: (30 Days Later - Offer)**
    * **Subject:**  As a Thank You, Here’s [Discount Code/Freebie]
    * **Content:** Offer a small incentive to encourage a first purchase (e.g., 10% off, free shipping, a small freebie).
    * **Call to Action:** “Shop Now & Save!” (Link to your store)
