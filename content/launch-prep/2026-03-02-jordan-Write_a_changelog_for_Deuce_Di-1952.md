**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 – Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Summary:** Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is the initial public launch of our habit tracking application, designed to help you build better habits and achieve your goals. This version introduces core functionality for logging, tracking, and motivating your progress.

**I. Social Features**

* **Friend Connections:**
    * **Added:** Users can now connect with friends within the Deuce Diary community.
    * **Functionality:**  Search for friends by username and send/accept friend requests.
    * **Note:**  Friend connections are currently limited to 50 per user.
* **Shared Habit Challenges (Beta):**
    * **Added:** Introduce beta testing for shared habit challenges.
    * **Functionality:** Create or join challenges with friends, competing to consistently complete specific habits. (Limited to 3 active challenges per user)
* **Activity Feed:**
    * **Added:**  A central feed displaying activity from friends, including completed habits, challenge progress, and new challenges.


**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Creation:**
    * **Added:** Users can create new habits with a custom name, description, and icon.
    * **Functionality:**  Set desired frequency (daily, weekly, specific days), reminders, and target completion time.
* **Habit Logging:**
    * **Added:** Users can easily log completion of their habits directly within the app.
    * **Functionality:**  Simple tap-to-log functionality for each habit.
* **Streak Tracking:**
    * **Added:** Tracks your consecutive days of habit completion – your current streak!
* **Habit History:**
    * **Added:**  A detailed log of all habit completions, displayed chronologically.
    * **Functionality:** View past completions, identify patterns, and analyze your progress.
* **Habit Editing:**
    * **Added:** Users can modify existing habits to adjust frequency, reminders, and goals.


**III. Gamification Features**

* **Points System:**
    * **Added:** Earn points for completing habits.
    * **Functionality:** Points are awarded based on completion frequency and difficulty (as determined by user-set goals).
* **Badges:**
    * **Added:** Unlock badges for reaching milestones (e.g., 7-day streak, 30-day streak, completing a specific habit a certain number of times).
* **Leaderboards (Beta):**
    * **Added:** Introduce beta testing for leaderboards, ranking users based on points earned. (Limited to 100 users to ensure performance)



**IV. UI/UX Improvements**

* **Onboarding Tutorial:**
    * **Added:** A new interactive tutorial guiding new users through the core features of Deuce Diary.
* **Dashboard Redesign:**
    * **Updated:** The main dashboard has been redesigned for a cleaner and more intuitive user experience.
* **Improved Habit List View:**
    * **Updated:** Optimized the way habits are displayed in the list, with clearer icons and habit names.
* **Dark Mode Support:**
    * **Added:** Toggleable Dark Mode for comfortable use in low-light environments.
* **Responsive Design:** Improved responsiveness across different screen sizes (phones and tablets).


**V. Infrastructure & Technical**

* **Database Migration:** Migrated to a new PostgreSQL database for improved performance and scalability.
* **API Updates:** Initial API endpoints implemented for basic habit creation and retrieval.
