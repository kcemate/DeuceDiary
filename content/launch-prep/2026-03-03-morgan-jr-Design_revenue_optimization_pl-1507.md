    * **Competitive Analysis:**  Understand competitor pricing, promotions, and offerings.
    * **Cost Analysis:**  Accurately track costs associated with each sales channel and product/service.
* **2. Customer Segmentation:**
    * Identify distinct customer segments based on behavior, needs, and value. (e.g., High-Value Customers, New Customers, Price-Sensitive Customers, etc.)
* **3. Opportunity Mapping:**  Identify key areas for improvement:
    * **Pricing Inefficiencies:** Are you overpricing some products/services? Underpricing others?
    * **Sales Process Bottlenecks:** Where are sales stalling?
    * **Marketing Channel Underperformance:** Which channels aren’t delivering returns?
    * **Product/Service Gaps:**  Are there unmet customer needs we could address?
