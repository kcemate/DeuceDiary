* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Drive consistent user engagement and time spent within the app.

    * Reduce customer acquisition cost (CAC) through word-of-mouth.

**2. Program Mechanics:**

* **Referrer Reward:**

* **Referral Link Generation:** Users will generate unique referral links via their Deuce Diary profile settings. 
* **Tracking:**  Referrals will be tracked using unique referral codes assigned to each user.

**3. Viral Loop Design (Key to Success):**

* **Social Sharing:** Integrate prominent “Share to [Social Media Platform]” buttons within the app and on the website.
