**Deuce Diary - Version 1.0.0 - Release Date: October 26, 2023**

**What's New in This Release?**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is the first official release of the game! This version includes the core gameplay loop, allowing you to chronicle the life of Deuce Bigalow, Defender of Earth.  We've focused on delivering a stable and enjoyable experience based on the initial prototype.

**Key Features:**

* **Core Gameplay:**  Experience the core gameplay loop of identifying and responding to threats, resolving conflicts, and maintaining the status quo as Deuce Bigalow.
* **Diary Entry Creation:**  Create and save diary entries detailing your actions, thoughts, and encounters.  Entries are the heart of the experience.
* **Threat System:**  Encounter a variety of escalating threats, from petty criminals to shadowy organizations, each requiring a unique approach.
* **Simple Combat:** Engage in basic, turn-based combat with a selection of weapons and tactics.
* **Base Management (Limited):**  Manage a small portion of Deuce's base - primarily for storing equipment and receiving orders.
* **Save/Load Functionality:**  Save your progress and load it later to continue your adventures.
* **User Interface:**  A basic, functional UI designed for quick navigation and diary entry management.


**Bug Fixes & Improvements:**

* **Fixed:**  A critical bug that prevented diary entries from being saved correctly.
* **Fixed:** Resolved a visual glitch in the combat UI that caused incorrect health bar display.
* **Fixed:**  Corrected an issue where the game would occasionally freeze when attempting to load a saved game.
* **Improved:**  Optimized loading times for diary entries.
* **Improved:**  Enhanced the responsiveness of the UI, making navigation smoother.
* **Fixed:**  Minor text alignment issues within the diary entry editor.
* **Added:**  Basic tutorial prompt on first launch to guide the player.

**Known Issues & Limitations:**

* **Limited Combat Depth:** Combat is currently a basic system. We plan to expand upon this in future updates.
* **Base Management is Minimal:** The base management system is extremely simplified.
* **UI Polish:** The user interface is functional but lacks visual polish.  We will be focusing on improvements in future releases.
* **Potential for Save Corruption:** While we've addressed the primary save issues, we recommend regularly saving your game to minimize the risk of data loss. (Due to the new save system).
