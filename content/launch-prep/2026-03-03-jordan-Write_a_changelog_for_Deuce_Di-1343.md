**Deuce Diary - Version 1.0.0 - Release Notes**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**What's New:**

This is the initial release of Deuce Diary, offering a core experience of investigation, diary entries, and a touch of atmospheric horror.  We’re incredibly excited to share this first iteration with you!

**Key Features:**

* **Core Gameplay Loop:**  Explore the Blackwood Estate, uncovering clues through examining objects and reading diary entries. Your goal is to unravel the mystery surrounding the estate’s former owner, Elias Blackwood.
* **Diary System:**  Collect and read Elias Blackwood’s diary entries. These entries provide crucial context, revealing fragments of his life and the events leading up to his disappearance.
* **Interactive Environment:**  Interact with a variety of objects throughout the estate.  Close doors, examine furniture, and search for hidden items that trigger diary entries or reveal new areas.
* **Atmospheric Soundtrack & Visuals:**  Experience a suitably unsettling atmosphere through a curated soundtrack and stylized visuals designed to evoke a sense of dread and isolation.


**Bug Fixes & Improvements:**

* **Fixed:**  Occasional stuttering issues on lower-end systems have been addressed.
* **Fixed:**  A bug causing the game to crash when attempting to examine the grand piano has been resolved.
* **Fixed:**  Incorrect dialogue text in the first diary entry has been corrected.
* **Fixed:**  Minor visual glitches in the hallway leading to the library have been addressed.
* **Improved:**  Navigation and camera controls have been slightly refined for smoother movement.
* **Improved:**  UI elements have been adjusted for better readability.
