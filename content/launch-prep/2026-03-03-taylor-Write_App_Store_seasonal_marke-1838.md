**Seasonal Marketing Calendar – App Store Focus**

**I. January - New Year, New You (Post-Holiday Slowdown)**

* **Theme:**  Motivation, Goal Setting, Fresh Starts
* **Activities:**
    * **Promote Relevant Features:** Highlight features that support user goals (e.g., fitness apps – “New Year, New Fitness!”; productivity apps – “Start Strong in 2024”).
    * **Limited-Time Offers:**  Offer a free trial extension, discounted premium features, or a one-time discount.
    * **Content Marketing:** Blog posts/social media content related to new year resolutions and how your app can help.
    * **ASO Focus:**  Refresh keywords, update app description to reflect the new year theme.
* **Timing:** January 1st - January 31st


**II. February - Love & Connections**

* **Theme:** Romance, Relationships, Connection
* **Activities:**
    * **Valentine’s Day Promotions:**  Special offers for dating apps, relationship apps, or gift-related apps.  (e.g., "Share the Love," "Find Your Perfect Match").
    * **User-Generated Content Campaign:** Encourage users to share how they're using the app to connect with others.
    * **App Feature Spotlight:** Highlight features that promote connection (e.g., group chats, profile matching).
    * **ASO:** Target Valentine's Day related keywords.
* **Timing:** February 1st - February 14th (Valentine’s Day) & February 15th - 28th (Post-Valentine's)

**III. March – Spring Awakening & New Beginnings**

* **Theme:** Renewal, Energy, Growth
* **Activities:**
    * **Spring Cleaning/Decluttering Apps:** Promote apps that help with organization, productivity, and streamlining life. ("Get Organized for Spring!")
    * **Seasonal Content:** Content related to Spring – gardening, outdoor activities, etc. (if relevant to your app).
    * **Introduce New Features:** Launch a new feature timed to coincide with Spring – e.g., a new outdoor activity tracker.
    * **ASO:** Update keywords to include "Spring" and related terms.
* **Timing:** March 1st – March 31st


**IV. April – Earth Month & Community**

* **Theme:** Sustainability, Community, Helping Others
* **Activities:**
    * **Partner with Environmental Organizations:**  If relevant, align your app with an environmental cause (e.g., donation app, sustainable living app).
    * **Community Engagement:** Run contests or challenges that encourage user interaction within the app.
    * **Promote Social Impact Features:** Highlight any features that allow users to make a positive impact.
    * **ASO:** Focus on keywords related to sustainability and social good.
* **Timing:** April
