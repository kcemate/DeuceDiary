**II. Identifying Potential Influencers – The Key to Success**

* **Criteria for Selection:**
    * **Engagement Rate:** Don’t just look at follower count. Calculate engagement rate (likes + comments / follower count) – a higher rate is better.

    * **Authenticity:** Do they seem genuine and relatable?
