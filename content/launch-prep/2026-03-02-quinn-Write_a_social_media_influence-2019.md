Okay, here's a social media influencer outreach template kit designed specifically for micro-influencers (health, humor, and lifestyle niches), including DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief. This is a starting point – tailor it to your brand and product!

**I. DM Templates (Segmented by Niche)**

**A. Health Influencers:**

* **Initial Contact (Warm-Up - 3-5 Days After Follow):**
   * "Hey @[Influencer Handle]! 👋 Huge fan of your content & your focus on [mention specific health topic - e.g., mindful eating, functional fitness, mental wellness].  We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re passionate about [briefly state your brand’s mission related to health].  Just wanted to say hello and see if you'd ever be interested in exploring a potential collab!"
* **Follow-Up (If No Response - 7-10 Days After Initial):**
   * "Hi @[Influencer Handle]! Just checking in – we noticed you're a big advocate for [mention a value they share].  We believe our [product/service] aligns really well with that!  Would love to chat briefly about possibilities. 😊"
* **Short & Sweet (If They Respond to Warm-Up):**
   * “Love what you’re creating!  We're [Brand Name], offering [brief product benefit].  Would you be open to discussing a sponsored post/review?  Link to our website: [Link]”



**B. Humor Influencers:**

* **Initial Contact (Playful Tone):**
   * "OMG @[Influencer Handle]! Your [specific video/post/joke] made my day 😂 We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re kinda obsessed with [your brand's product/service] too.  Thinking a laugh-worthy collab could be epic!  Let’s chat?"
* **Follow-Up (If No Response):**
   * “Seriously though, you’re hilarious.  We're [Brand Name] and we’re trying to spread joy one [product/service] at a time.  Would you be up for a fun partnership? 😜 [Link to your Instagram/TikTok]"
* **Short & Sweet:**
   * “Need a good laugh AND a great [product/service]?  We're [Brand Name].  Think you'd be down to make some content together?  [Link]”



**C. Lifestyle Influencers:**

* **Initial Contact (Genuine & Engaging):**
   * "Hey @[Influencer Handle]! Your aesthetic is *everything*! ✨ We love how you showcase [mention something specific you admire - e.g., travel, home decor, self-care].  [Your Brand Name] is all about [briefly state brand mission].  Would love to connect!"
* **Follow-Up (If No Response):**
   * “Beautiful content! We’re [Brand Name], and we believe in [mention brand value - e.g., quality, experiences, sustainable living].  Could your audience benefit from [product/service]?  Happy to discuss!”
* **Short & Sweet:**
    * "Obsessed with your style!  We're [Brand Name] and would love to collaborate on a post/story to share our [product/service] with your amazing followers. 💫 [Link]"
