Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service. This calendar is built around a "build excitement, drive adoption, gather feedback" strategy. I've included suggestions for various channels and estimated frequency. **Remember to tailor this to *your* specific product/service, target audience, and resources.**

**Assumptions:**

*   You've already launched your product/service.
*   You have a basic social media presence (even if it's just starting out).
*   You have a website or landing page with key information.

**Key Themes for Month 1:**

*   **Awareness:** Let people know it exists!
*   **Education:** Explain what it does and why it's valuable.
*   **Trial/Experience:** Encourage people to try it out.
*   **Community Building:** Start engaging with your users.
*   **Feedback Loop:**  Actively solicit and respond to feedback.
