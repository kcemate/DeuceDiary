Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service. This calendar is designed to build excitement, drive initial adoption, and gather early feedback. It’s a template, so you'll need to adapt it to your specific product, target audience, and launch goals.

**Assumptions:**

* **You've launched!** This calendar starts a week *after* launch.
* **You have a basic social media presence:** We'll focus on key platforms like Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, and potentially LinkedIn (depending on your product).
* **Goal:** Drive awareness, encourage sign-ups/trials, and gather initial feedback.

**Key Themes for Month 1:**

* **Week 1: Celebrate & Introduce (Excitement & Basics)** – Focus on the launch itself, highlighting key features, and answering common questions.
* **Week 2: How It Works & Benefits (Value Proposition)** –  Demonstrate the product's utility and explain the core benefits in an engaging way.
* **Week 3: User Stories & Social Proof (Trust & Validation)** –  Feature early user feedback, testimonials, or case studies.
* **Week 4: Deep Dive & Community Building (Engagement & Iteration)** – Address more advanced features, host Q&A sessions, and encourage community interaction.
