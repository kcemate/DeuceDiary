Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for varying lengths. I’ve included subject lines, email content, and estimated timing. Remember to customize these heavily to fit *your* specific brand, product/service, and audience!

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introductory (New Subscriber - Goal: Build Initial Interest)**

* **Goal:**  Introduce your brand, offer a valuable resource, and gently encourage engagement.
* **Timing:** 3 Emails over 7-10 Days
* **Segment:** New Subscribers (Anyone who signs up through your website, lead magnet, or other signup form)

**Email 1: Immediately After Signup (Day 1)**
* **Subject Line:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
* **Content:**
    * **Greeting:** “Hi [Subscriber Name],”
    * **Thank You:** “Thank you so much for joining the [Your Brand Name] community! We’re thrilled to have you.”
    * **Brief Intro:** “We’re [briefly describe what your brand does – one sentence]. We’re passionate about [your brand’s core values/mission].”
    * **Resource Delivery:** “As a welcome gift, we've just sent you [Name of Lead Magnet - ebook, checklist, template, etc.].  It’s designed to help you [briefly state the benefit of the resource].”
    * **Call to Action:** “Download your [Lead Magnet Name] here: [Link]”
    * **Image:** Brand logo or a relevant image relating to the lead magnet.



**Email 2:  Deeper Dive & Social Proof (Day 4-5)**
* **Subject Line:**  Discover How [Problem Your Product Solves]…
* **Content:**
    * **Recap:** “Just a quick reminder about the [Lead Magnet Name] you downloaded – we hope you're finding it helpful!”
    * **Problem/Solution:**  “Many people struggle with [pain point your product addresses]. That’s why we created [Your Product/Service].”
    * **Social Proof:** “Here’s what some of our customers are saying: [Include a short, impactful testimonial or review - screenshot or quote].”
    * **Call to Action:** "Learn more about how [Your Product/Service] can help you: [Link to Product Page]"


**Email 3:  Limited-Time Offer & Reminder (Day 7-10)**
* **Subject Line:** ⏳ Don’t Miss Out! Special Offer Inside…
* **Content:**
    * **Urgency:** “For a limited time, we’re offering [Discount/Bonus/Free Shipping] on [Your Product/Service].”
    * **Benefit Reminder:** “This is your chance to [reiterate the biggest benefit].”
    * **Call to Action:** “Claim your offer now: [Link to Product Page with Offer]”
    * **Image:** Eye-catching image of your product/service.
