# Write 3 email drip campaign sequences.

Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each with a different focus and designed for different stages of the customer journey. I've included estimated length for each sequence and notes on key elements.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introductory (New Subscriber - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and encourage initial engagement.
* **Target:** New subscribers who signed up through a lead magnet (e.g., ebook, checklist, free trial) or website signup form.
* **Sequence Length:** 5-7 Emails
* **Email 1 (Immediately): Welcome & Thank You**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand]!
    * **Content:**  Warm welcome, express gratitude for joining, reiterate the benefit of their signup (the lead magnet), briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values. Include a clear call to action – “Download your [Lead Magnet] now!”  Link to download.
* **Email 2 (2 Days Later):  Problem/Pain Point**
    * **Subject:**  Struggling with [Relevant Problem]?
    * **Content:**  Address a common pain point your target audience experiences that your product/service solves.  Don't directly pitch; focus on empathy and understanding.  Briefly mention how you can help.
* **Email 3 (3 Days Later):  Introduce a Key Benefit**
    * **Subject:**  [Benefit] Made Easy with [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Focus on *one* key benefit of your product/service. Use a relatable scenario or story to illustrate how it solves the pain point from Email 2.
* **Email 4 (5 Days Later):  Social Proof (Optional)**
    * **Subject:** What Others Are Saying About [Your Brand]
    * **Content:** Include a short testimonial or positive review.  A link to your social media profiles can also be included.
* **Email 5 (7 Days Later):  Offer a Small Incentive (Optional)**
    * **Subject:**  Exclusive Offer Just For You!
    * **Content:**  Offer a small discount or bonus for first-time buyers – something low-commitment to encourage a purchase. (e.g. 10% off, free shipping).



**Campaign 2:  Nurturing & Education (Mid-Stage - 7-14 Days)**

* **Goal:**  Educate subscribers about your product/service, build authority, and move them closer to a sale.
* **Target:** Subscribers who have opened emails from Campaign 1, but haven’t yet purchased.
* **Sequence Length:** 7-14 Emails
* **Email 1 (7 Days After Email 5 from Campaign 1):  Deep Dive into a Feature**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock the Power of [Specific Feature]
    * **Content:**  Focus on a key feature of your product/service.  Explain its functionality and *how* it solves a specific problem. Use visuals (screenshots, GIFs).
* **Email 2 (10 Days Later): Case Study or Success Story**
    * **Subject:** How [Client Name] Achieved [Result] with [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Share a case study or customer success story that demonstrates the value of your product/service.
* **Email 3 (14 Days Later):  Comparison (Gentle - avoid direct criticism)**
    * **Subject:**  [Your Product/Service] vs. [Competitor - if appropriate, and tastefully]
    * **Content:**  Highlight *differences* and advantages, focusing on benefits. Frame it as helping subscribers make an informed decision. Avoid directly bashing competitors.
