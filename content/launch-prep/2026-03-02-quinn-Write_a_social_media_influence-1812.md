Okay, here's a social media influencer outreach template kit tailored for micro-influencers (health, humor, lifestyle), including DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief. This is designed to be adaptable - tweak it to fit your brand’s voice and specific campaign goals.

**I. DM Templates (Tailored by Category)**

**A. Health Influencers:**

* **Template 1 (Initial Reach - Warm Up):** “Hey @[InfluencerName]! 👋 Just came across your amazing content on [Specific Post/Topic]. Your insights on [Specific Area] are seriously valuable! ❤️”
* **Template 2 (Direct Offer - Less Formal):** “Hi @[InfluencerName]! We’re [Your Brand Name], and we're huge fans of your healthy living vibes!  We're running a campaign focused on [Campaign Goal - e.g., mindful eating, workout recovery] and thought you’d be a great fit. Would you be open to a quick chat about a potential collaboration?”
* **Template 3 (Value-Driven):** “Hey @[InfluencerName]!  We noticed you're passionate about [Specific Health Topic].  We've developed [Product/Resource - e.g., a new smoothie recipe, a training guide] that aligns perfectly with your audience’s interests.  Happy to send you a sample for review!”


**B. Humor Influencers:**

* **Template 1 (Funny Intro - Engage First):** “Okay, @[InfluencerName], we need to talk. Your sense of humor is *legendary*. 😂 We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re looking for someone to bring the laughs to our [Campaign Goal - e.g., product launch, social media challenge].”
* **Template 2 (Direct Offer - Playful):** “Hey @[InfluencerName]! We're building a campaign that needs a serious dose of your brilliance.  We'd love to send you [Product/Gift] in exchange for a fun, relatable post. 😉  Thoughts?”
* **Template 3 (Reference a Recent Post):** "We saw your recent [Post Topic] and we were laughing!  We're doing a campaign around [Similar Topic] and we think you'd be amazing at it.  Want to chat?"


**C. Lifestyle Influencers:**

* **Template 1 (Genuine Compliment):** “Hi @[InfluencerName]!  Your content always brings a little joy to my feed – especially your [Specific Style/Content Type - e.g., cozy home decor, travel adventures]. We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re working on a campaign about [Campaign Goal - e.g., self-care, mindful living].”
* **Template 2 (Collaborative Question):** "Hey @[InfluencerName]!  We're curious - what does a perfect [Day/Situation - e.g., weekend morning, evening in]?  We're [Your Brand Name] and we'd love to explore how we can help people create those moments.  Would you be interested in a collaboration?"
* **Template 3 (Highlight Brand Values):** “Love your focus on [Influencer's Values - e.g., sustainability, community].  We're [Your Brand Name], and we share those values too.  We'd love to collaborate on a campaign that showcases [Product/Service] and celebrates [Shared Value].”

**Important Notes for All DM Templates:**

* **Personalization is Key:** Always replace bracketed information with specifics.
* **Start with a Genuine Compliment:** People respond better to authentic appreciation.
* **Keep it Concise:** Micro-influencers are busy!
