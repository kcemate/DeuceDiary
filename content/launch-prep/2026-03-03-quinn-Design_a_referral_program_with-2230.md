Okay, let's design a referral program with a focus on engagement and attractive incentives. Here's a breakdown, covering different tiers and options, along with considerations for implementation:

**1. Program Name:** (Choose something relevant to your brand - examples: "Share the Love," "Spread the Word," "Refer & Reward")

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **Simple Referral Link:** Every user gets a unique referral link. This is crucial for tracking.
* **Tracking:** Utilize a referral tracking platform or integrate referral tracking into your existing system (e.g., using a service like ReferralCandy, Mention, or building your own simple tracking with UTM parameters).
* **Clear Terms & Conditions:**  Make sure your terms are easy to understand and cover everything: eligible users, rewards, limitations, and how you'll handle disputes.

**3. Referral Tiers & Incentives (This is the most important part!)**

We'll use a tiered system to reward increasing levels of referral activity. This encourages users to refer multiple people.

**Tier 1:  Initial Referral (Low Barrier)**

* **Referrer Incentive:** 10% discount on their next purchase. (Easy to achieve and encourages immediate action)
* **Referee Incentive:** 5% discount on their first purchase. (Provides an incentive for the new customer)
* **Trigger:** First successful referral.


**Tier 2:  Multiple Referrals (Mid-Level)**

* **Referrer Incentive:** $25 Store Credit
* **Referee Incentive:** 10% discount on their first purchase.
* **Requirements:**  Referrer must refer at least 2 new users.
* **Timing:**  Activated after the referrer has successfully referred 2 new users.


**Tier 3:  Top Tier – “Referral Champion” (High Barrier)**

* **Referrer Incentive:** $100 Store Credit / Gift Card
* **Referee Incentive:** 15% discount on their first purchase.
* **Requirements:** Referrer must refer at least 5 new users.
* **Timing:** Activated after the referrer has successfully referred 5 new users.

**4. Incentive Types - Beyond Discounts:**

* **Experiences:** (If relevant to your business)  e.g.,  "Refer a friend and get a private consultation,"  "Refer a friend and be entered to win a VIP experience.”
* **Early Access:** Give top referrers early access to new products, features, or sales.
* **Exclusive Content:** Offer premium content, guides, or webinars to top referrers.
* **Bundled Products:** Create a special “Welcome Bundle” that top referrers receive for their new referrals.
* **Charitable Donation:**  “For every referral, we’ll donate $X to [Charity Name].” – this adds a social good element.


**5. Communication & Promotion:**

* **Onboarding:** Immediately after signup, introduce the referral program.  Make it prominent.
* **Email Marketing:**
    * **Welcome Email:** Introduce the program.
    * **Referral Reminder:**  Send reminders to users who haven't referred anyone.
    * **Achievement Email:** When a user earns a reward, send a celebratory email.
    * **New Referral Notification:** Notify the referrer when a new user signs up through their link.
* **Website Banner:** Prominently display the referral program on your website.
* **Social Media:** Regularly promote the program on your social channels.  Run contests related to referrals.
* **In-App Notifications (if applicable):**  For mobile apps, use push notifications to remind users and announce rewards.
