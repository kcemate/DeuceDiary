    * **Cohort Analysis:** Grouping users based on acquisition date to track retention across different segments.
* **User Surveys (NPS, CSAT, Exit Surveys):** Qualitative data to understand *why* users are leaving.

    * **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):** Measures satisfaction with specific features or aspects of the app.
    * **Exit Surveys:** Collected when users uninstall or close the app, asking for reasons for leaving.
* **App Store Reviews & Social Media Monitoring:**  Identify pain points and sentiment expressed by users.
