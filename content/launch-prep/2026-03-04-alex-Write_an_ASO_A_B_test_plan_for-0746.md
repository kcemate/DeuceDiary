## ASO A/B Test Plan: Optimizing App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements, focusing on improving install rates and overall app engagement.

**1. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve App Store Page Conversion Rate (Impression to Install), Increase Average Session Length, Improve App Rating & Reviews
* **Key Metrics:**
    * **Install Rate (IR):** Percentage of impressions that result in an install. (Most Important)
    * **Conversion Rate (CR):** Percentage of users who view the app page and then install.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Keyword Matches:** How many users click on the app when searching for your target keywords.
    * **Average Session Length:** Tracks how long users spend in the app after installation.
    * **App Rating & Reviews (Average Star Rating, Number of Reviews):** Gauges user satisfaction.
    * **Description View Rate:** Percentage of users who view the entire app description.

**3. A/B Test Methodology:**

* **Testing Framework:** Google Optimize, Apptimize, Adjust, or similar A/B testing platforms.
* **Split Ratio:** 50/50 initially for variants A and B.
* **Test Duration:** Minimum 2-4 weeks for sufficient data to collect, allowing for multiple user sessions and varying seasonality.
* **Segmentation:**
    * **Device Type:** iOS & Android separately to identify platform-specific performance.
    * **User Cohorts:** Based on install source (e.g., paid ads, organic search, referrals) – Helps isolate the impact of different marketing channels.
    * **Geo-Location:**  If your app caters to a specific region, testing within those regions will be more relevant.
* **Statistical Significance:**  Determine statistical significance using a 95% confidence interval. This helps confirm that the observed difference isn't just due to random chance.  Tools will typically calculate this for you.
