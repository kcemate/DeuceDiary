**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. January - New Year, New Habits**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Wellness
* **Marketing Focus:** Promote features that support user’s resolutions (e.g., fitness trackers, habit trackers, productivity apps, meditation apps).
* **Activities:**
    * **Launch Campaign:** "Start 2024 Right" - Discounted premium features, limited-time tutorials.
    * **Social Media:**  Content focused on New Year’s resolutions, healthy habits, motivation.  Run contests – "Share your 2024 goal and win [app feature]."
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Keywords related to New Year's resolutions, self-improvement, and goals.
    * **Email Marketing:** Welcome sequence for new users, offering tips and encouragement.


**II. February - Love & Connection**

* **Theme:** Romance, Relationships, Self-Care
* **Marketing Focus:**  Highlight features that foster connection and well-being (dating apps, relationship trackers, games for couples, self-care apps).
* **Activities:**
    * **Valentine's Day Promotion:**  "Share the Love" – Offer a discount on premium features for couples or gift subscriptions.
    * **Social Media:**  Content about love, relationships, self-care, and romantic gestures.  Run polls – “What’s your love language?”
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relationship or lifestyle influencers.
    * **ASO:** Keywords related to Valentine’s Day, dating, love, and relationships.

**III. March - Spring Awakening & Productivity**

* **Theme:** Renewal, New Beginnings, Productivity
* **Marketing Focus:**  Focus on apps that help users organize their lives, learn new skills, or embrace the changing season (task managers, learning apps, gardening apps, home organization apps).
* **Activities:**
    * **Spring Launch:**  "Get Organized for Spring" - Offer a free trial or discounted introductory price.
    * **Social Media:**  Content related to spring cleaning, gardening, productivity tips, and new beginnings.
    * **Blog Posts:** Create content about productivity strategies, spring trends, and how your app can help.
    * **ASO:** Keywords: Spring, organization, productivity, learning, renewal.



**IV. April – Easter & Family Fun**

* **Theme:** Family, Celebration, Games
* **Marketing Focus:**  Highlight games, family-friendly apps, and tools that facilitate connection and entertainment.
* **Activities:**
    * **Easter Promotion:**  Run a contest with prizes related to your app.  Create Easter-themed in-app content (if applicable).
    * **Social Media:**  Family-friendly content, Easter-themed games, and user-generated content.
    * **ASO:** Keywords: Easter, family, games, kids, entertainment.


**V. May – Memorial Day & Outdoor Activities**

* **Theme:** Outdoor Adventure, Relaxation
