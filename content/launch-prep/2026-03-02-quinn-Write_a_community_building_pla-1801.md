## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy for building a thriving community around *Deuce Diary*, focusing on fostering discussion, encouraging creative expression, and celebrating the unique world and characters. We’ll utilize Discord and Reddit as primary platforms, with a clear content strategy and robust moderator guidelines to ensure a positive and engaging environment.

**I. Platform Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** Discord will be the core of the *Deuce Diary* community, offering real-time interaction, deeper discussions, and exclusive content opportunities.
    * **Server Structure:**
        * **#general-chat:**  For casual conversation, announcements, and general community updates.
        * **#deuce-diary-discussion:** Dedicated to discussing the story, characters, lore, and theories.
        * **#art-and-fanworks:** Showcase and feedback on fan art, writing, and other creative contributions.
        * **#roleplay-zone:** Designated space for organized roleplay activities.
        * **#off-topic:**  A space for members to connect outside of *Deuce Diary* (carefully monitored).
        * **#moderator-talk:**  Private channel for moderators to communicate and coordinate.
* **Reddit (Discoverability & Long-Form Discussion):** Reddit will be used for broader reach, sparking discussions, and curating longer-form content like analysis and theories.
    * **Subreddit: r/DeuceDiary** – Main hub for Reddit discussions, sharing of fan content, and linking to Discord.


**II. Content Strategy:**

* **Consistent Posting Schedule:**
    * **Discord:** Daily engagement prompts, “Deuce of the Day” spotlights, discussion questions, and shared creative challenges.
    * **Reddit:** 2-3 posts per week – including curated fan art, thought-provoking questions, and links to significant Discord discussions.
* **Content Pillars:**
    * **Lore Deep Dives:**  Dedicated posts and discussions exploring the history, magic system, and world-building of *Deuce Diary*.
    * **Character Analysis:**  Analyzing the motivations, relationships, and development of the characters.
    * **Theory Crafting:**  Encouraging and facilitating the development of theories about the story and future developments.
    * **Fan Creations Showcase:**  Regularly highlighting and celebrating fan art, writing, music, and other creative endeavors.
    * **Interactive Content:** Polls, quizzes, and short writing prompts to get members involved.
* **Content Formats:**
    * **Discord:** Text-based discussions, polls, voice channels for impromptu discussions.
    * **Reddit:** Text-based posts, image/video uploads (where relevant), links to Discord discussions.
