Okay, let's design a referral program with tiered incentives – aiming to be engaging, rewarding, and sustainable. Here’s a breakdown, incorporating different elements and levels of reward:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name Options:** "Spread the [Your Brand] Love," “Refer & Reward,” “[Your Brand] Circle,” “Bring a Friend”
* **Branding:** Use your brand colors, logo, and a consistent tone of voice throughout the program materials.

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referral Method:** (Choose one or a combination)
    * **Unique Referral Link:** Each user gets a unique, trackable link. This is the most common and easiest to implement.
    * **Unique Code:** Users share a unique code.
    * **Social Sharing:** Direct sharing buttons on your website and within your product/service.
* **Tracking:** Utilize referral tracking software (e.g., ReferralCandy, ReferralHero, Ambassador) or build your own system to accurately monitor referrals.
* **Clear Communication:** Explain the program clearly on your website, in your app, and in any onboarding materials.


**3. Referral Tiers & Incentives (This is the heart of the program)**

We’ll use a tiered system to encourage multiple referrals.

**Tier 1: The Initial Spark (Easy to Reach)**

* **Referral Action:**  New customer refers a friend.
* **Referrer Incentive:** **10% Discount on their next purchase** (or $5 credit, depending on your average order value). *This is the basic incentive, making it easy to participate.*
* **New Customer Incentive:** **10% Discount on their first purchase** *This is the incentive for the friend.*

**Tier 2: The Enthusiast (Moderate Effort)**

* **Referral Action:** Referrer refers 2-3 friends who make a purchase.
* **Referrer Incentive:** **20% Discount on their next purchase** *Increased reward for dedication.*
* **New Customer Incentive:** **15% Discount on their first purchase** *Still attractive to new customers.*

**Tier 3: The Ambassador (High Effort – For Top Referrers)**

* **Referral Action:** Referrer refers 5+ friends who make a purchase.
* **Referrer Incentive:** **$50 Gift Card to your store/service.** *This is a significant reward for top advocates.*
* **New Customer Incentive:** **20% Discount on their first purchase** *Reward to keep the momentum going.*

**4. Additional Incentives & Gamification (To Boost Engagement)**

* **Tier Leaderboard:** (If applicable – particularly for SaaS or community-based brands) Display a leaderboard showing the top referrers, creating friendly competition.
* **Early Access:** Top referrers receive early access to new products, features, or sales.
* **Exclusive Content/Experiences:** Offer exclusive webinars, tutorials, or behind-the-scenes content only to referrers.
* **Monthly Prize Draw:**  Randomly select a referrer each month to win a larger prize (e.g., gift basket, premium subscription).
* **Social Media Recognition:** Feature top referrers on your social media channels (with their permission!).

**5.  Terms & Conditions (Important!)**

* **Eligibility:**  Clearly state who can participate (e.g., existing customers only).
* **Restrictions:**  Specify any limitations (e.g., only one discount per customer, cannot be combined with other offers).
* **Duration:**  Set a timeframe for the program (e.g., 6 months, 1 year).
* **Fraud Prevention:**  Include clauses to prevent fraudulent activity (e.g., verification of referrals).
