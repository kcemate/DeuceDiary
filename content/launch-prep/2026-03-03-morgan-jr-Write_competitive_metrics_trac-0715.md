## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a strategy for consistently monitoring and analyzing our competition, allowing us to identify opportunities, threats, and ultimately, make informed decisions to improve our strategy and performance.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Strategic Insight:** Understand our competitors' strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and market positioning.
* **Opportunity Identification:** Spot gaps in the market or areas where we can differentiate ourselves.
* **Threat Detection:**  Proactively identify emerging threats (e.g., new competitors, disruptive technologies)
* **Performance Benchmarking:**  Compare our key metrics against those of our competitors to identify areas for improvement.
* **Strategic Adjustments:**  Use competitive insights to refine our marketing, product development, pricing, and overall business strategy.

**II. Key Competitors & Segmentation:**

* **Identify Core Competitors:** List 3-5 direct competitors – those offering similar products/services to the same target audience.
* **Identify Adjacent Competitors:** List 2-3 competitors offering slightly different products/services targeting a similar segment.
* **Segmentation:**  Categorize competitors based on:
    * **Size:** (Enterprise, SME, Startup)
    * **Market Share:** (Dominant, Niche, Emerging)
    * **Strategy:** (Cost Leadership, Differentiation, Innovation)
    * **Product/Service Focus:** (e.g., Features, Quality, Price)

**III. Metrics to Track - Categorized:**

|                    | Lead Generation (Cost/Volume)         | CRM, Marketing Automation Platforms | Weekly       | Lead quality & cost efficiency            |
