**Launch Retrospective: [Product Name] - Week 1**

**Date:** [Date of Retrospective]
**Attendees:** [List of Team Members - Product, Engineering, Marketing, Support, etc.]
**Goal:** To quickly assess the launch of [Product Name], identify immediate successes & challenges, and prioritize actions for the next week.

**I. Introduction (5 mins)**

*   **Brief Recap:** (Facilitator) - Quickly remind everyone of the launch goals and objectives.
*   **Ground Rules:** (Facilitator) - Reinforce a safe and open environment for honest feedback (no blame, focus on learning).


**II. Key Metrics & Performance (15-20 mins)**

*   **Overall Launch Status:** (Scale: Red/Yellow/Green - Overall health of the launch) – Why? (Brief explanation)
*   **Quantitative Metrics - *Track these daily for a snapshot*:**
    *   **Downloads/Installs (if applicable):** [Actual Number] vs. Projected [Projected Number] – *Why the difference?* (e.g., marketing campaign effectiveness, app store visibility)
    *   **New Users:** [Actual Number] vs. Projected [Projected Number] – *Segment by acquisition channel (if possible)* – *Why the difference?*
    *   **Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU):** [Daily/Weekly/Monthly Active Users] – *Trend analysis – is it increasing, stable, or decreasing?* – *Why?* (e.g., engagement, onboarding issues)
    *   **Retention Rate (Day 1, Day 7):** [Percentage] – *Which cohort is showing lower retention?* - *Why?* (e.g., feature usability, onboarding problems)
    *   **Conversion Rate (if applicable - e.g., Free to Paid):** [Percentage] - *What’s driving the conversion rate?* - *Is it lower than anticipated?* - *Why?* (e.g., pricing, trial limitations)
    *   **Error Rate / Crash Rate:** [Percentage of Crashes/Errors] - *What types of errors are most common?* - *Where are they occurring?* - *Why?* (e.g., bug fixes, platform incompatibility)
    *   **Feature Usage:** (Top 3-5 most used features) -  *Are these features aligned with our core value proposition?* – *Why are they being used so much?*
    *   **Time to First Value (TTFV):** [Average Time] - *Are users quickly realizing the core value?* - *Is it taking too long?* - *Why?* (e.g., onboarding friction)
