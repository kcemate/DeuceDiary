**IV.  Phased Retention Analysis & Strategies**

**A. D1 – Immediate Post-Acquisition (24-48 Hours)**

* **Focus:** Identifying *Why* Users Didn’t Return
* **Metrics:** D1 Churn Rate, Activation Rate (Did they complete the initial setup?)
* **Data Analysis:**
    * **Review:** Check if there were any known issues at the time of signup (website errors, confusing onboarding).
    * **Analyze:**  Look at drop-off points in the onboarding flow.
    * **Segmentation:** If possible, segment churned users based on signup method (e.g., paid ad vs. organic search) or demographic data.
* **Strategies (Based on D1 Findings):**
    * **Immediate Win-Back Email:** Send a personalized email offering help or a quick tutorial if the onboarding was confusing.
    * **Technical Support Outreach:**  For users who encountered technical issues, prioritize immediate support
