**Program Name:** (Example: “Spread the Word Rewards”)

**1. Core Mechanics & Structure:**

* **Referral Link/Code:** Every participant (both referrer and referee) receives a unique referral link or code. This is crucial for tracking.
* **Simple Sign-Up:** Easy registration for both the referrer and the referee.
* **Clear Instructions:**  Provide simple, step-by-step instructions on how to refer (e.g., "Share your link," "Send this code to a friend").
* **Tracking & Reporting:** Implement a system to track referrals, conversions, and reward fulfillment.
* **Terms & Conditions:** Have clear terms outlining eligibility, limitations, and potential disputes.

**2. Tiers of Rewards (This is where you tailor to your business):**

We’ll use a tiered system to motivate different levels of participation.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Entry Level)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 10% discount on their next purchase.
    * **Referee Reward:** 10% discount on their first purchase.
    * **Trigger:** Referee makes their first purchase.
* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level - "Referral Champion"**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 20% discount on their next purchase *plus* Free shipping on their next order.
    * **Referee Reward:** 15% discount on their first purchase *plus* a small free gift (e.g., sample product, small accessory).
    * **Trigger:** Referee makes their second purchase.
* **Tier 3: Top-Level - “Loyal Promoter”**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  $50 Store Credit.
    * **Referee Reward:** 20% discount on their first purchase *plus* a premium gift (e.g., full-size product, exclusive merchandise).
    * **Trigger:** Referee makes their third purchase.

**3. Incentive Types - Beyond Discounts:**

* **Cash Rewards:** (Can be more expensive, but highly motivating – best for certain industries)  e.g., $10-$25 per referral.
* **Gift Cards:** (Popular & versatile – allows flexibility) – Amazon, Starbucks, or even gift cards specific to your business.
* **Exclusive Products/Experiences:** (Great for creating excitement) - Limited edition items, early access to new products, VIP experiences.
* **Points System:**  Earn points for referrals, which can be redeemed for discounts or rewards.
* **Tiered Status/Badges:**  Recognize top referrers with badges or status levels that unlock further benefits.
* **Charitable Donations:**  For every referral, donate a small amount to a cause your audience cares about. (Good for socially conscious businesses).

**4.  Promotional Channels:**

* **Website:** Prominently display the referral program on your website (homepage, product pages, checkout).
* **Email Marketing:**  Send emails to existing customers announcing the program and reminding them to refer.  Segment your email list to target people most likely to refer.
* **Social Media:** Run contests, share referral links, and engage with your audience.  Use visually appealing graphics and videos.
* **In-App Referral (if applicable):** Integrate the program directly into your mobile app.
* **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with influencers to promote the referral program to their followers.
