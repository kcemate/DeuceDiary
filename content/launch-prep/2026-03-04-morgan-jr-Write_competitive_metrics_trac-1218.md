## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competitors' activities, ultimately informing strategic decisions and driving growth.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deeper understanding of the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and inform strategic decisions related to [Your Company's Focus – e.g., Marketing, Product Development, Pricing].
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Monitor competitor strategies (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns).
    * Identify emerging trends and technologies in the market.
    * Understand customer perception of competitors.
    * Detect potential threats to your market share.
    *  Inform decisions related to [Specific areas, e.g., R&D priorities, marketing budget allocation, product roadmaps].


**II. Competitor Selection:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors - Critical Focus):** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target market.  (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi) - *Dedicate most resources here.*
* **Tier 2 (Adjacent Competitors - Important Observation):** Companies offering products/services that could potentially fulfill similar needs or target a slightly different segment. (e.g., Starbucks vs. Dunkin’ Donuts) - *Regular monitoring and trend analysis.*
* **Tier 3 (Potential Disruptors - Monitor for Innovation):** Smaller companies or startups with innovative approaches that could challenge the market. (e.g., Tesla vs. Established Automakers) - *Focus on overall trends and disruptive technologies.*
