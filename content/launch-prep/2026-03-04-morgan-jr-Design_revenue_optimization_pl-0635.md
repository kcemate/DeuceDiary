* **1. Data Audit:**

* **2. Competitive Analysis:**

* **3. Customer Segmentation:**
    * Divide customers into distinct groups based on behavior, needs, and profitability. (e.g., High-Value, Medium-Value, Low-Value, New Customers).
* **4.  Identify Quick Wins:** Look for low-hanging fruit - areas with immediate potential for improvement (e.g., abandoned cart recovery, streamlining the checkout process).
