* **Influencer Types:**

    * **Mid-Tier Influencers (10k-50k followers):** Worth considering if your budget allows and they genuinely fit your brand.
    * **Mega-Influencers (50k+ followers):** Generally not worth the cost for this niche; their reach is broad but engagement is often lower.
