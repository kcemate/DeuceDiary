**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is the culmination of significant development and user feedback, bringing a robust and engaging experience for tracking and reflecting on your daily habits. This release introduces core features designed to help you build positive routines and gain deeper insights into your behaviors.

**I. Social Features**

* **Community Groups:**
    * **Added:** Users can now join or create public and private Community Groups based on shared goals (e.g., "Productivity," "Mindfulness," "Fitness").
    * **Functionality:** Groups allow for sharing progress, challenges, and motivational support within a defined community.
    * **Moderation:** Basic moderation tools (reporting inappropriate content) have been implemented.  Further moderation features are planned for future releases.
* **Friend Connections (Limited Beta):**
    * **Added:** Ability to connect with other Deuce Diary users via a direct connection system.
    * **Functionality:**  Users can view each other's daily entries (publicly shared), send encouragement, and participate in shared challenges. *Note: This feature is currently in a limited beta and may experience stability issues.*
* **Public Profile (Basic):**
    * **Added:** Users can now create a public profile displaying their goals, current habits, and a brief bio.
    * **Privacy:** Profile visibility is controlled by the user.

**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Tracking:**
    * **Added:** Core habit tracking functionality. Users can add habits with custom names, frequencies (daily, weekly, specific days), and target times.
    * **Smart Reminders:** Customizable smart reminders based on habit frequency and user-defined times.
* **Daily Entry Journal:**
    * **Added:**  A dedicated space for users to record their daily thoughts, feelings, and reflections related to their tracked habits.
    * **Rich Text Support:**  Basic rich text editing (bold, italics) within journal entries.
* **Habit Linking:**
     * **Added:** Users can link habits to specific days of the week for more targeted tracking and analysis.
* **Multiple Habit Tracking:**
    * **Added:**  Users can track multiple habits concurrently, giving a holistic view of their routine.


**III. Gamification Features**

* **Streaks:**
    * **Implemented:**  A streak system that tracks consecutive days of habit completion, encouraging consistency.
    * **Streak Visualization:**  Visual representation of streak length on the dashboard.
* **Achievements & Badges:**
    * **Added:** A system of achievements and badges awarded for reaching milestones (e.g., 7-day streak, completing a habit for 30 days).
    * **Badge Categories:** Badges categorized by accomplishment type (e.g., "Consistency," "Challenge," "Progress").
* **Progress Bars & Charts:**
    * **Added:** Dynamic progress bars for each habit, visually representing completion percentage.
    * **Basic Charts:**  Simple line charts showing habit completion trends over the past week and month.

**IV. UI/UX Improvements**

* **Dashboard Redesign:**
    * **Implemented:** A streamlined dashboard providing an overview of current habits, streaks, and achievements.
* **Intuitive Habit Creation Flow:**  Simplified steps for adding new habits.
* **Improved Navigation:**  Revised menu structure for easier access to all features.
* **Dark Mode:**  Added a Dark Mode option for comfortable viewing in low-light conditions.
