**Template 2:  Mid-Test Check-in (Gathering Specific Feedback)**

Subject:  How's Your Experience with [Product Name]? (Quick Feedback Request)

Hi [Tester Name],

Thanks again for joining our beta program! We're really interested to hear about your experience so far with [Product Name].

We’re currently focusing on [mention specific feature or area you’re tracking] and would love to get your thoughts.  Could you please take a few minutes to answer a couple of questions in this short form: [Link to Google Form/Survey]

Or, if you prefer, you can share your feedback directly here: [Link to Slack Channel/Email].

Your insights are invaluable to us!

Best regards,
The [Your Company Name] Team


**Key Considerations for Beta Testers:**

*   **Clearly Define Expectations:**  Be upfront about the time commitment required and the types of feedback you're seeking.
*   **Provide Dedicated Support:**  Have a channel for testers to easily ask questions and get help.
*   **Show Appreciation:**  Acknowledge their contributions and make them feel valued.  Consider small thank-you gifts or rewards for particularly helpful feedback.
