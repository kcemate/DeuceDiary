Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different purpose and with varying lengths. I’ve included suggested triggers, content examples, and estimated timing. Remember to tailor these to your specific brand, audience, and product/service.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introduction - (New Subscriber - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and set expectations.
* **Trigger:** Subscription to email list.
* **Sequence Length:** 5-7 Emails
* **Email 1 (Immediately - Welcome Email):**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
    * **Content:**  Warm welcome, express gratitude for subscribing, briefly introduce your brand’s mission/values, and highlight a key benefit of being a subscriber (e.g., exclusive discounts, early access). Include a friendly image or GIF.
* **Email 2 (Day 2):**
    * **Subject:**  The Problem We Solve - [Briefly State Your Core Offering]
    * **Content:**  Focus on the pain point your product/service addresses.  Tell a short customer story (testimonial or example).  Link to a relevant blog post or resource that expands on this problem.
* **Email 3 (Day 4):**
    * **Subject:** Meet the [Team/Founder] -  Get to Know Us
    * **Content:**  Humanize your brand.  Introduce the team behind the company or the founder's story. A photo is essential. Build a connection.
* **Email 4 (Day 6):**
    * **Subject:**  Popular [Product Category] - Check Out Our Best Sellers
    * **Content:** Showcase a selection of your most popular products or services with images and short descriptions.  Include clear calls to action (CTAs) to browse.
* **Email 5 (Day 7 - Optional):**
    * **Subject:**  Last Chance: Get [Small Incentive]
    * **Content:**  A gentle reminder of the value of subscribing and a small, low-pressure incentive (e.g., a discount code for their first order, free shipping).

* **Sequence Length:** 7-14 Emails
* **Email 1 (Day 1 - Triggered):**
    * **Subject:**  You’re Interested in [Product Name] – Here’s What You Need to Know

    * **Subject:**  [Product Name]:  Common Questions Answered
