**To Get Started:**

*   [Link to Beta Test Signup/Instructions]

[Your Website Link]


**Notes for Beta Testers:**

*   **Personalize:** Add a sentence acknowledging something you know about the tester (e.g., “We know you’re a big fan of [related product/category]”).
*   **Clearly Defined Goals:** The more specific you are about what you want them to test, the better the feedback will be.
*   **Incentives (Optional):**  If you’re offering incentives (early access, discounts, etc.), mention them prominently.
