**Deuce Diary - Version 1.0.0 - Changelog**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is the initial release of Deuce Diary, a digital journal designed for thoughtful reflection and personal development, inspired by the classic novel *The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy*. This version introduces the core features for recording entries, setting reminders, and exploring your thoughts.
