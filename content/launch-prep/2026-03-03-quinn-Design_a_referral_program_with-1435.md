Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, covering different aspects and offering a few variations to suit different business models:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Option 1 (Friendly):** “Spread the Word & Earn!”
* **Option 2 (Direct):** “Refer & Reward”
* **Option 3 (Unique to Your Brand):**  (e.g., “Level Up Your [Your Brand Name] with Friends”) - *Tailor this to your brand’s voice.*


**2. Core Mechanics – How it Works:**

* **Referral Link/Code:** Each referrer gets a unique link or code they can share. This is *crucial* for tracking.
* **Two Types of Participants:**
    * **Referrer:** The person who shares the link/code.
    * **New Customer:** The person who signs up or makes a purchase through the referrer’s link/code.
* **Tracking:** A robust system to track referrals and conversions is essential. Most referral platforms or marketing automation tools can handle this.

**3. Incentive Structure – What People Get (Tiered is Recommended)**

This is where you'll attract the most participants. Here’s a breakdown of incentives, categorized by level & cost:

**Tier 1: Immediate Rewards (Low Cost, High Engagement)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Discount on Next Purchase:** 10-20% off their next order. (Cost: Relatively low - $1-3)
    * **Small Store Credit:** $5 - $10 credit to use on their account. (Cost: $5-10)
    * **Bonus Points (if applicable):**  Increase their points balance by a significant amount. (Cost: Minimal)
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Welcome Discount:** 10-15% off their first purchase. (Cost: Low)
    * **Free Shipping:** On their first order. (Cost: Variable - depends on shipping costs)



**Tier 2: Mid-Level Rewards (Medium Cost, More Valuable)**

* **For the Referrer (After a successful referral):**
    * **Larger Discount:** 25-50% off. (Cost: $5-10)
    * **Gift Card:** $25 - $50 gift card to your brand or a popular retailer. (Cost: $25-50)
    * **Exclusive Product/Access:** Early access to new products, limited-edition items, or VIP-level features. (Cost: Moderate - dependent on the value of the item)
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Bundled Discount:** Offer a discount on a specific product bundle. (Cost: Dependent on bundle price)



**Tier 3: High-Level Rewards (Higher Cost, Top Performers)**

* **For the Referrer (After multiple successful referrals - e.g., 3 or more):**
    * **Significant Gift Card:** $100+ gift card. (Cost: $100+)
    * **Premium Membership:**  Upgrade to a higher-tier membership with exclusive benefits. (Cost: Variable)
    * **Personalized Thank You:** (More valuable for smaller businesses) A handwritten note or small, thoughtful gift. (Cost: Low – $5-10)
* **For the New Customer:**
     * **Free Product:** Offer a free sample or a smaller version of a popular product. (Cost: Depends on product cost)
