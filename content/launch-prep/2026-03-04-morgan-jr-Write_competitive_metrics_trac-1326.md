## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to consistently track and analyze your competition, informing strategic decision-making and ultimately boosting your business performance.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deep understanding of the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and ultimately improve our strategic positioning.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strategies.
    * Monitor competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns, etc.).
    * Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Identify gaps in the market that we can exploit.
    * Track competitor performance and adjust our strategy accordingly.

**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **List of Competitors:** Create a comprehensive list – include direct competitors (selling similar products/services) and indirect competitors (offering alternative solutions).
    * **Tier 1 (High Priority):** Competitors most directly impacting our market share, revenue, or brand perception. (e.g., Market leaders, closest rivals) – Target for detailed tracking.
    * **Tier 2 (Medium Priority):** Competitors with notable influence, potentially disruptive, or operating in adjacent markets. – Regular monitoring required.
    * **Tier 3 (Low Priority):** Smaller players, niche markets, or those with limited potential impact. – Occasional observation.
* **Categorization:** Group competitors by their strategic approach (e.g., Cost Leader, Differentiation, Niche Player).
