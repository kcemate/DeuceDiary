Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different purpose and with varying lengths. I've included details on triggers, content, and estimated length.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscriber)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build a relationship, and encourage initial engagement.
* **Trigger:** Immediately after someone subscribes to your email list (via website form, contest, etc.).
* **Length:** 3-5 Emails (Over 7-10 days)
* **Email 1: Immediate (Within 5 minutes)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:**
        * Warm welcome and thank you for subscribing.
        * Briefly state your brand's mission and what you offer.
        * Include a visually appealing hero image.
        * Link to your most popular resource (e.g., blog post, free guide, product page).
        * Quick call to action: “Check out our [resource] to get started!”
* **Email 2: Day 3**
    * **Subject:**  [Problem Your Brand Solves] – We Get It.
    * **Content:**
        * Briefly elaborate on the problem your brand helps solve.  Focus on the *pain points* of your target audience.
        * Share a relatable story or statistic related to the problem.
        *  Link to a relevant blog post or case study demonstrating your expertise.
* **Email 3: Day 7**
    * **Subject:**  Meet the Team (Behind the Scenes)
    * **Content:**  (Optional – If you have a personable brand)
        * Introduce a key team member or the story behind the brand.  Humanize your company.
        *  Quick highlight of a recent accomplishment or positive customer review.
* **Email 4 (Optional): Day 10**
    * **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out! [Limited-Time Offer]
    * **Content:**  (Introduce a small discount or exclusive offer to encourage a first purchase – *only* if appropriate for your business and audience).



**Campaign 2: Product/Service Promotion (Lead Nurturing)**

* **Goal:** Drive sales or interest in a specific product/service.  This assumes you've already gathered some information about the subscriber's interests.
* **Trigger:** After a user has engaged with content on your website or interacted with a specific product page. (e.g. adds to cart, downloads a resource)
* **Length:** 5-7 Emails (Over 14-21 days)
* **Email 1: Day 1**
    * **Subject:** You looked at [Product Name] - Here’s Why You’ll Love It
    * **Content:** Reiterate interest in the product they viewed.  Highlight 2-3 key benefits.  Use a compelling image of the product.
* **Email 2: Day 3**
    * **Subject:**  Customer Spotlight: How [Product Name] Helped [Similar Customer]
    * **Content:**  Share a case study or testimonial from a satisfied customer using the product. Focus on the *results* they achieved.
* **Email 3: Day 7**
    * **Subject:**  Frequently Asked Questions About [Product Name]
    * **Content:** Address common concerns or questions about the product.  Provide detailed answers and maybe a short video.
* **Email 4: Day 10**
    * **Subject:** Limited Time Offer - Get [Product Name] at [Discount]
    * **Content:**  Introduce a limited-time discount or promotion to incentivize a purchase.
