Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week with suggested content types and estimated frequency. This is a template – you'll need to adapt it heavily to your specific product, target audience, and marketing goals.

**Assumptions:**

* **Post-Launch:** This calendar covers the period immediately following the launch (Weeks 1-4).
* **Goal:** This calendar leans towards driving awareness, encouraging initial adoption, and gathering feedback.
* **Channels:** We'll assume a mix of social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn – adjust based on where your audience is), email marketing, and potentially a blog.


**Overall Monthly Theme:** "Welcome! Get Started & See What You Can Do"

---

**Week 1: The Big Reveal & Initial Excitement (Focus: Awareness & First Impressions)**

* **Monday:** Launch Announcement - BIG post across all channels. High-quality image/video showcasing the product/service.  (Social: All, Email: Launch Announcement Email)
* **Tuesday:** "What is [Product Name]?" - Short explainer video (1-2 mins) on YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels. (Social: TikTok, Instagram, YouTube)
* **Wednesday:** Feature Spotlight #1 - Deep dive into a single core feature with a screenshot or GIF. (Social: Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn – tailored to audience)
* **Thursday:**  Behind-the-Scenes - Humanize the brand!  A photo or short video of the team involved, or a glimpse at the creation process. (Social: Instagram, Facebook)
* **Friday:**  Welcome & Offer – Limited-time introductory offer or discount. (Social: All, Email: Special Offer Email)
* **Weekend:**  Community Poll -  "What are you most excited to use [Product Name] for?" (Social: Instagram Stories, Twitter/X)


**Week 2:  How-To & Value Proposition (Focus: Education & Showing Value)**

* **Monday:**  Tutorial 1 - Quick “How To” guide for a common, simple use case. (Social: YouTube, Blog Post, Instagram Reels)
* **Tuesday:**  Testimonial Highlight - Share a positive early review or quote (with permission!). (Social: All, potentially a dedicated testimonial page on your website)
* **Wednesday:**  Feature Spotlight #2 - Focus on another key feature. (Social: LinkedIn, Twitter/X - focus on professional benefits)
* **Thursday:**  FAQ - Address 3-5 frequently asked questions. (Social: Twitter/X, Blog Post)
* **Friday:**  Case Study Snippet - A brief example of how someone is using the product to achieve a specific result. (Social: LinkedIn, Blog Post - link to full case study)
* **Weekend:**  User Generated Content Request - Encourage users to share their experiences with a specific hashtag. (Social: All – actively monitor & share)


**Week 3:  Community Building & Engagement (Focus:  Loyalty & Feedback)**

* **Monday:**  "Meet the Team" - Introduce a team member with a short bio and photo. (Social: Instagram, LinkedIn)
* **Tuesday:**  Challenge/Contest - Small, easy-to-participate-in contest related to the product/service. (Social: All – drive entries to your website or social media)
* **Wednesday:**  Community Spotlight -  Share user-generated content (UGC) and thank users. (Social: All)
* **Thursday:**  Q&A Session (Live or Pre-recorded) – Host a live Q&A on Instagram/Facebook or record a video answering common questions. (Social: Live Instagram/Facebook)
