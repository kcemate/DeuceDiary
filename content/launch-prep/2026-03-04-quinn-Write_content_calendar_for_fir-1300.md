Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service. This calendar is designed to build momentum, gather feedback, and drive initial adoption. It’s a template – you'll need to customize it heavily based on your specific product, target audience, and launch goals.

**Assumptions:**

* **Launch Date:** We're starting this calendar *one week* after the launch date. This gives a little breathing room for initial excitement to settle and allows for thoughtful content creation.
* **Product/Service:** Let’s assume it’s a SaaS product (e.g., a project management tool) for this example, but you can easily adapt it.
* **Channels:** This calendar incorporates content for: Website/Blog, Social Media (Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn – adjust to your audience), Email Marketing, and potentially a short Video Series.
