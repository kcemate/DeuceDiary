7. **Intellectual Property Infringement:** Using copyrighted material (music, images, text, etc.) without permission is a frequent cause of rejection.  Also, apps that mimic competitor apps too closely can be flagged.
8. **App Category Misrepresentation:**  Submitting an app that doesn't reasonably fit within its claimed category.
9. **Inappropriate Content:** Content that is sexually suggestive, violent, hateful, discriminatory, or otherwise offensive is automatically rejected.
10. **Technical Issues/Poor User Experience:** Including issues like confusing UI, broken features, and a generally poor user experience (UX) can lead to rejection, especially if it reflects poorly on the App Store.
