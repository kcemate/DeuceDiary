**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (as of Late 2023/Early 2024 - These can shift slightly)**

1. **Policy Violations (General):** This is the *biggest* category. Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines are incredibly detailed and broad.  Minor violations can lead to rejection.
2. **Intellectual Property Infringement:**  Using copyrighted material (music, images, characters, code) without proper licenses is a common and severe reason for rejection.
3. **Misleading or Deceptive Practices:** Apps that falsely claim to do something they don't, make exaggerated promises, or trick users into spending money are rejected.
4. **Privacy Violations:**  Collecting and handling user data without proper consent, clear disclosures, or adhering to privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA) is a major problem.
5. **Malware/Security Issues:**  Apps containing viruses, spyware, or other malicious code are immediately rejected.
6. **Functionality Issues:**  Apps that don't function as described, crash frequently, or have significant bugs are rejected.
7. **Payment Issues:**  Incorrect payment processing, fraudulent payment schemes, or not complying with Apple's App Store payments policies.
8. **App Store Guidelines Violations (Specific):**  This covers everything from misleading descriptions, inappropriate content, violating age restrictions, etc.
9. **Lack of Transparency:** Failure to provide accurate information about the app, its permissions, and data collection practices.
10. **App Not Meeting Apple's Technical Standards:** Issues with code quality, UI/UX design that doesn’t adhere to Apple’s standards, or lack of accessibility features.
