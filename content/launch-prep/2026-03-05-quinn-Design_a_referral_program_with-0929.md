Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers and motivations, with estimated costs and considerations. We'll call this program "Boost & Bloom" (feel free to change the name!).

**1. Program Name:** Boost & Bloom

**2. Core Concept:** Encourage existing customers to refer new customers by rewarding them and the new customer they bring on board.

**3. Tiers & Incentives (Detailed):**

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy Entry)**
    * **Referrer Incentive:** 10% discount on their next purchase (e.g., $10 off a $100 purchase).
    * **New Customer Incentive:** 5% discount on their first purchase.
    * **Mechanics:**  Referrer shares a unique referral link.  New customer uses that link to sign up or make a purchase.
    * **Cost:**  $5 - $10 per referral (depending on discount size)
    * **Goal:**  Low barrier to entry, generates initial momentum.

* **Tier 2: Silver Bloom (Increased Reward)**
    * **Referrer Incentive:** $25 Gift Card to your store/platform.
    * **New Customer Incentive:** 10% discount on their first purchase.
    * **Mechanics:** Same as Tier 1, but with a higher value reward.
    * **Cost:** $30 - $50 per referral
    * **Goal:**  Attract more motivated referrals and reward those who have been loyal.

* **Tier 3: Gold Bloom (Top Tier - Highest Reward)**
    * **Referrer Incentive:** $75 Gift Card to your store/platform + Free Shipping on their next order for 6 months.
    * **New Customer Incentive:** 15% discount on their first purchase + Free Shipping on their first order.
    * **Mechanics:** Same as Tier 1 & 2, with premium incentives.
    * **Cost:** $80 - $120 per referral
    * **Goal:** Reward top advocates, driving significant new customer acquisition.


**4. Referral Link Generation:**

* **Unique Links:** Absolutely crucial.  Use a referral marketing platform (see below) to automatically generate and track unique referral links for each customer.
* **Social Sharing Buttons:**  Include prominent "Refer a Friend" buttons on your website, in emails, and within your app (if you have one).
* **Email Templates:**  Create pre-written email templates for referrers to easily share.  Include a personal touch.
* **QR Codes:**  Generate QR codes that link directly to the referral program page, great for print materials or social media.

**5. Tracking & Analytics:**

* **Referral Marketing Platform:** Invest in a platform like:
    * **ReferralCandy:**  Popular, easy to use, supports various integrations. (Pricing starts around $49/month)
    * **Friendbuy:** Focuses on e-commerce, strong referral tracking. (Pricing starts around $99/month)
    * **Upward:** Great for smaller businesses with simpler needs. (Pricing starts around $39/month)
* **Google Analytics:** Track referral traffic, conversion rates, and overall program performance.
* **Custom Reporting:**  Build a dashboard to monitor key metrics – number of referrals, new customers acquired, total revenue generated by referrals.


**6. Communication & Promotion:**

* **Launch Announcement:**  Clearly announce the program to your existing customer base via email, social media, and website banners.
* **Regular Reminders:**  Send automated email reminders to referrers (e.g., "Don't forget to share and earn!").
