**What is Deuce Diary?**

Deuce Diary is a darkly comedic, coming-of-age graphic novel series by debut creator, Elias Vance. Following the story of 16-year-old Deuce "Dice" Harding, a sharp-tongued, fiercely independent teenager navigating the bleak and hilarious realities of suburban life in the decaying Rust Belt town of Havenwood.  Dice’s world is a mess of dead-end jobs, crumbling family secrets, and a desperate yearning for something – *anything* – to change.  With a distinct visual style that blends gritty realism with surrealist flourishes, Deuce Diary offers a raw and unflinching look at adolescent angst, family dysfunction, and the quiet desperation of a forgotten America.

**About the Creator - Elias Vance**

Elias Vance is a self-taught artist and writer from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He holds a degree in Fine Arts and has spent years honing his skills in illustration and sequential storytelling.  Deuce Diary is his first published graphic novel series, and he’s driven by a desire to tell stories that are both challenging and rewarding, exploring the complexities of the human experience with honesty and a healthy dose of dark humor.

**Synopsis (Volume 1 - "The Fallout")**

"The Fallout" introduces readers to Dice and the strange, unsettling reality of Havenwood. Dice is stuck working a dead-end job at the local auto repair shop, constantly dodging his domineering father and grappling with the disappearance of his mother years ago.  When a bizarre series of events, including cryptic messages from an anonymous source and increasingly unsettling visions, force him to confront the town’s dark history and his own troubled past, Dice must decide if he’s willing to risk everything to uncover the truth - even if it means facing the darkest corners of himself.
