## Deuce Diary Launch: Crisis Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Purpose:** To provide a standardized response plan for handling potential crises during the launch of *Deuce Diary*. This playbook outlines roles, communication protocols, and escalation procedures.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be upfront and honest with the public, even if the news isn't ideal.
* **Speed:** Respond quickly and decisively. Delays erode trust.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge the concerns and frustrations of readers and fans.
* **Accuracy:** Verify information before disseminating it.
* **Consistency:** Maintain a unified message across all channels.


**II. Potential Crisis Categories & Response Protocols:**

**1. Technical Issues (Website Downtime, Payment Problems):**

* **Severity:** High - Immediate impact on user experience and revenue.
* **Initial Response (Within 5 Minutes):**
    * **Team Lead (CTO/Tech Lead):** Activate Incident Response Team. Begin troubleshooting and identifying the root cause.
    * **Social Media Manager:** Post a brief, reassuring update: “We’re aware of an issue and our team is working hard to resolve it. We'll provide updates as they become available.” - Include a link to a dedicated status page.
* **Follow-up (Within 15-30 Minutes):**
    * **Team Lead:** Provide regular updates on progress – estimated resolution time, steps being taken.
    * **Customer Support:**  Be prepared for a surge in inquiries. Implement a knowledge base with FAQs.
* **Long-Term:** Root cause analysis & preventative measures documented.

**2. Negative Reviews & Criticism (Poor Writing, Unfulfilled Expectations):**

* **Severity:** Medium - Can damage reputation and deter new readers.
* **Initial Response (Within 30 Minutes):**
    * **Social Media Manager:** Monitor social media channels for negative comments.  Acknowledge concerns publicly – "We appreciate your feedback and are taking it seriously. We’re dedicated to delivering the best possible story and value to our readers."
    * **Community Manager:**  Identify key influencers and thought leaders to engage with privately.
* **Follow-up (Within 1-2 Hours):**
    * **Content Lead/Author:** Respond directly to individual negative reviews, offering explanation, apology (if appropriate), and reassurance.
    * **Analyze Sentiment:** Track overall sentiment to understand the extent of the issue.
* **Long-Term:**  Review feedback, potentially iterate on the content based on constructive criticism.


**3. Controversy/Offensive Content (Allegations of Misrepresentation, Harmful Themes):**

* **Severity:** Critical - Highest potential for significant damage to brand and reputation.
* **Initial Response (Within 15 Minutes):**
    * **PR Manager:** Assemble a crisis communication team. Immediately cease any promotional activity.
    * **Legal Counsel:**  Consult with legal to assess potential legal ramifications.
    * **Social Media Manager:** Acknowledge the situation with a statement of concern: "We take these allegations extremely seriously and are immediately investigating.” - Avoid making definitive statements until facts are confirmed.
* **Follow-up (Within 1-2 Hours):**
    * **Author:** Cooperate fully with any internal or external investigation.
    * **PR Manager:** Prepare a detailed public statement, outlining steps being taken, and emphasizing commitment to responsible storytelling.
* **Long-Term:**  Full investigation, potential content revisions, and damage control.  This could require significant PR efforts and potentially collaboration with experts.

**4. Security Breach (Data Leak, Hacking Attempt):**

* **Severity:** Critical - Immediate threat to user data
