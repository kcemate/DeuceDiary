**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (As of Late 2023/Early 2024)**

2. **Misleading or Deceptive Behavior:**  Apps that don't accurately represent their functionality, charge unexpectedly, or trick users are almost guaranteed to be rejected.
3. **Privacy Violations:** This is a HUGE area of focus for Apple. Apps must be transparent about data collection, storage, and sharing.  Failure to comply with App Tracking Transparency (ATT) is a critical factor.
4. **Intellectual Property Infringement:**  Using copyrighted images, music, trademarks, or software without permission is a major red flag.
5. **Malware or Security Issues:** Apps that contain malicious code, attempt to steal data, or compromise user security are immediately rejected.
6. **Performance Issues:**  Apps that crash frequently, drain excessive battery, consume excessive data, or have poor performance are considered unusable and rejected.
7. **Inappropriate Content:**  Content that is sexually explicit, violent, discriminatory, or otherwise offensive is not permitted.
8. **Functionality Issues:**  Apps that don’t work as described, have bugs that severely impact usability, or are simply broken are rejected.
9. **Unclear or Misleading Permissions Requests:** Asking for unnecessary permissions or not explaining why permissions are needed is a common rejection reason.
10. **Lack of App Metadata Compliance:**  Missing or inaccurate information like app name, description, keywords, screenshots, and videos can lead to rejection.
