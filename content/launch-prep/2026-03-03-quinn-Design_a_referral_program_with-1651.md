Okay, let's design a referral program with tiered incentives. This design focuses on motivating both the referrer and the referred, with varying levels of rewards to encourage participation.

**Program Name:** “[Your Brand Name] Connects” (Replace with your brand name)

**Overall Goal:** To increase brand awareness, acquire new customers, and build a strong community through word-of-mouth marketing.

**I. Core Structure & Mechanics**

* **Referral Link/Code:** Each referrer receives a unique referral link or code. This allows tracking of referrals accurately. (Ideally, using a system like ReferralCandy, Ambassador, or building your own with API integration).
* **Tracking:** Implement robust tracking to see how many referrals are generated, which channels are most effective, and which incentives are driving the most sign-ups.
* **Terms & Conditions:**  Clearly define the rules, including:
    * Eligibility (e.g., existing customers only)
    * Valid duration of the program
    * What constitutes a successful referral (e.g., new customer making a purchase)
    * Limitations (e.g., maximum referrals per person)


**II. Tiers & Incentives (This is the most important part!)**

We’ll use a tiered system to reward escalating levels of referrals.  Here's a suggested structure:

**Tier 1: “Welcome to the Network” (First Referral)**

* **Referrer Reward:** 10% off their next purchase.
* **Referred Reward:** 10% off their first purchase.
* **Goal:**  Simple entry point, incentivizes initial sharing.
* **Trigger:**  New customer makes a purchase after being referred.

**Tier 2: “Community Builder” (3 Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** $25 Gift Card to [Your Brand]
* **Referred Reward:** 15% off their first purchase.
* **Goal:** Reward deeper engagement & commitment.
* **Trigger:**  Referrer has successfully referred 3 new customers.

**Tier 3: “Brand Advocate” (7 Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** $75 Gift Card to [Your Brand] + Exclusive Access to [e.g., early product releases, VIP events, behind-the-scenes content]
* **Referred Reward:** 20% off their first purchase +  Free shipping on their first order.
* **Goal:**  Recognize top referrers, build loyalty, and create brand advocates.
* **Trigger:**  Referrer has successfully referred 7 new customers.


**III.  Incentives Breakdown & Considerations**

* **Gift Cards:**  Popular and easily redeemable. Set a reasonable value based on your average order value.
* **Discounts:**  Ensure discount percentages are attractive.  Test different discount levels to see what drives the best conversion rates.
* **Free Shipping:** A powerful incentive, especially for e-commerce.
* **Exclusive Access:**  Leverage brand loyalty – early access, VIP events, content, etc. – to make advocates feel valued.
* **Points System (Alternative):** You could use a points system where referrals earn points that can be redeemed for rewards. This provides more flexibility for your rewards.


**IV.  Promotion & Communication**

* **Website Banner:**  Prominently display the referral program on your website.
* **Email Marketing:**
    * **Welcome Email:** Introduce the program to new customers.
    * **Referral Reminder Emails:** Send regular emails to existing customers encouraging them to refer.
    * **Reward Claim Instructions:**  Provide clear instructions on how to claim rewards.
* **Social Media:**  Run targeted social media campaigns showcasing the referral program. Use engaging visuals and hashtags.
