## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a strategy for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competition, providing valuable insights for strategic decision-making and competitive advantage.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deep understanding of your competitors' strategies, strengths, weaknesses, and market positioning, ultimately informing our own strategy and driving growth.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their target audiences.
    * Track competitor pricing, promotions, and product offerings.
    * Monitor competitor marketing activities (advertising, social media, content).
    * Analyze competitor strengths and weaknesses across key areas (product, service, customer experience, etc.).
    * Identify emerging trends and potential threats.
    *  Quantify the impact of competitor activities on our own performance.


**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):**  Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi)
* **Tier 2 (Indirect Competitors):** Companies offering different products/services that could satisfy the same customer need. (e.g., Coffee shops vs. Energy Drinks)
* **Tier 3 (Potential Future Competitors):** New entrants or companies expanding into your market.
* **Prioritization:** Focus on the top 3-5 Tier 1 competitors based on market share, relevance to your business, and potential threat level.
