**Deuce Diary: A Retro-Futuristic Mystery – Synopsis**

“Deuce Diary” is a gripping, atmospheric neo-noir mystery set in the chrome-plated, neon-drenched metropolis of Veridium City in 1957. Following the unexplained disappearance of famed inventor, Elias Thorne, young journalist Lila Sterling is tasked with uncovering the truth. Armed with her trusty notebook (and a surprisingly advanced micro-recorder), Lila delves into Veridium’s underbelly – a world of automated street sweepers, sentient robots, and shadowy corporations – piecing together a conspiracy that reaches the highest levels of city government.
