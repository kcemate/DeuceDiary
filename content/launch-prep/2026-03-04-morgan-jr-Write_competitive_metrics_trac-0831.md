## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently tracking and analyzing your competitive landscape, allowing you to identify opportunities, threats, and ultimately, make better strategic decisions.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Gain a deeper understanding of your competitors' strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and customer perceptions to inform our business decisions and maintain a competitive advantage.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their market positioning.
    * Monitor competitor activities, including product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns, and partnerships.
    * Understand competitor customer satisfaction levels and identify their strengths and weaknesses in serving customers.
    * Detect emerging trends and technologies that could impact our business.
    * Proactively identify and address potential threats.


**II. Key Competitors:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):** These are companies offering similar products or services to the same target market.  (e.g., Netflix vs. Disney+, Apple vs. Samsung) – **Focus: Deepest Analysis**
* **Tier 2 (Indirect Competitors):** These companies offer alternative solutions or address the same customer needs in a different way. (e.g., Spotify vs. Apple Music, Google Docs vs. Microsoft Office) – **Focus: Broad Monitoring**
* **Tier 3 (Emerging Threats):** New entrants, disruptive technologies, or smaller companies with innovative approaches. – **Focus: Initial Surveillance & Trend Watching**
