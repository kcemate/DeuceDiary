**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (For New Subscribers - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build a connection, and encourage engagement.
* **Audience:** New subscribers who signed up through a website form, landing page, or contest.
* **Timing:** 1 email immediately upon signup, followed by 4-6 emails spaced out over 5-7 days.
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome & Thank You:**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:**  Warmly welcome the subscriber.  Thank them for joining. Briefly introduce your brand's mission and values (1-2 sentences).  Include a friendly tone. Offer a small incentive (e.g., 10% off first order, free guide download) – but don't make it the *only* thing.
        * **Call to Action (CTA):** “Learn More About Us” (links to your ‘About Us’ page)
    * **Email 2 (Day 3): Problem/Solution:**
        * **Subject:** Struggling with [Pain Point Your Product Solves]?
        * **Content:** Identify a common pain point your product addresses. Briefly explain how your brand helps solve this problem.  Focus on benefits, not just features.
        * **CTA:** “Discover How We Can Help” (links to a relevant blog post, case study, or product page).
    * **Email 3 (Day 5):  Behind the Scenes/Story:**
        * **Subject:** The Story Behind [Your Brand Name]
        * **Content:** Share a brief, engaging story about your brand’s origins, values, or team.  Humanize your brand.
        * **CTA:** “Meet the Team” (links to your team page or a short video).
    * **Email 4 (Day 7 - Optional):  Top 3 Products/Resources:**
        * **Subject:** Our Most Popular [Product Category]
        * **Content:** Showcase 3 of your most popular products or resources. Briefly explain why they’re valuable.
        * **CTA:** “Shop Now” (links directly to the product pages).


* **Key Metrics:** Open Rate, Click-Through Rate (CTR), Unsubscribe Rate, Conversion Rate (if applicable – e.g., discount code redemption).
