## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements to improve install rates, user engagement, and ultimately, app success.

**I. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs
* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Improve Click-Through Rate (CTR) from App Store Search Results & Featured Cells
    * Increase Conversion Rate (CVR) from Click to Install
    * Enhance User Engagement (e.g., Daily/Monthly Active Users - DAU/MAU)
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Rate (IR):** Percentage of users who install the app after viewing the app page. (Most important)
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of users who click on the app page from search or featured results.
    * **Conversion Rate (CVR):** Percentage of users who click and then install.
    * **Average Session Length:** Duration of user sessions within the app.
    * **Retention Rate (D1, D7, D30):** Percentage of users returning to the app after 1, 7, and 30 days. (Indirectly affected by ASO)
