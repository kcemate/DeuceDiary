* **Psychographic Data:** (This is often more challenging to collect but very valuable)
    * **Lifestyle:** (Active, Home-focused, Social, etc.) – *Example: Outdoor gear companies target active individuals.*

    * **Interests:** (Hobbies, Passions) – *Example: Music streaming services segment based on genre preferences.*

* **Behavioral Data:** (This is often the most actionable)

    * **Website Activity:** (Pages visited, time spent, searches) – *Example:  A travel site can see if users are browsing specific destinations.*
    * **App Usage:** (Features used, frequency, duration) – *Example: A fitness app can identify users who only use the workout tracker.*
