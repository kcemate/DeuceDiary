Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, aiming for a balance between motivating participants and maintaining a healthy program cost, broken down into sections with options and considerations:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** “Share the [Your Brand Name] Love,” “Bring a Friend, Get Rewarded,” or something catchy and relevant to your brand.
* **Visuals:**  Create a simple logo or visual element for the program to easily identify it.

**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Who are you trying to reach?** (Existing customers, specific demographics, etc.)
* **What’s your primary goal?** (New customer acquisition, increased brand awareness, user growth, etc.)  This will heavily influence your incentive structure.


**3. Referral Mechanism – How it Works:**

* **Simple Tracking:** This is KEY. You need a reliable way to track referrals.
    * **Unique Referral Links:**  Each participant gets a unique link (using a tool like referralcandy.com, Loopin, or building your own simple tracking).
    * **Unique Codes:**  Offer a unique code for participants to share.
    * **Social Sharing Buttons:**  Make it incredibly easy for people to share directly on social media platforms.
* **Process:**
    1. **Referrer Action:**  The person making the referral clicks their link or uses their code.
    2. **New Customer Action:** The referred customer makes a purchase (or completes a specific action, depending on your business).
    3. **Tracking & Reward:**  The system automatically recognizes the referral and triggers the reward for the referrer.

**4. Incentive Structure – The Core of the Program**

This is where you need to be strategic.  Here's a tiered system with options, from basic to more complex:

* **Tier 1: Basic (Everyone)**
    * **For the Referrer:**  A small discount (e.g., 10% off their next purchase) – *Cost: Low* – Good for initial engagement.
    * **For the Referred Friend:** A small discount (e.g., 5% off their first purchase) – *Cost: Low* - Encourages adoption.
* **Tier 2: Mid-Level (After a Referral is Made)**
    * **For the Referrer (After Successful Referral):**  A Gift Card (e.g., $10 - $25 to your store/platform) – *Cost: Medium* -  Demonstrates appreciation and encourages further referrals.
    * **For the Referred Friend (After Successful Referral):**  Free Shipping on their First Order – *Cost: Medium* –  A significant motivator for online purchases.
* **Tier 3: High-Level (For Top Referrers)**
    * **For the Referrer (Based on Number of Successful Referrals):** Tiered rewards – e.g.,
        * 3-5 Referrals:  $50 Gift Card
        * 6-10 Referrals: $100 Gift Card
        * 11+ Referrals:  $250 Gift Card or Premium Product/Service
        * *Cost: High* –  Incentivizes highly engaged users to actively promote your brand.

**5.  Reward Redemption & Tracking:**

* **Easy Redemption:**  Make it simple for people to redeem their rewards.  Clear instructions and an easy-to-use interface are crucial.
* **Tracking System:**  You *must* have a system to track rewards issued, redeemed, and remaining.
