**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Understand user behavior post-acquisition to identify key churn points and areas for product/marketing optimization.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * **Day 1:**  Determine initial activation rate and identify barriers to immediate value realization.
    * **Day 7:**  Assess product engagement and identify why users are dropping off after the initial onboarding.
    * **Day 30:**  Evaluate long-term value perception and identify factors driving sustained usage or contributing to churn.

**II. Metrics & Definitions:**

* **Retention Rate:**  Percentage of users who remain active after a specific time period.

* **Active User:**  Defined by the core action users take within the product/service. (Example: Daily Active Users - DAU; Weekly Active Users - WAU) – *Clearly define your active user definition*.
* **Churn Rate:**  Percentage of users who stop being active during a specific time period. (Calculated as 1 - Retention Rate)
* **Conversion Rate (Optional):** If applicable, track conversion rates through specific features or towards paid subscriptions.
* **Feature Usage:** Track usage of key features – identifies engagement levels and potential areas for improvement.
* **Time to Value (TTV):** The time it takes a user to experience the core benefit of the product. Shorter TTV often correlates with higher retention.



**III. Data Collection & Tracking:**

* **Analytics Platform:** Utilize a robust analytics platform (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude) to track user behavior.

* **Data Frequency:**  Daily data collection for tracking immediate activation, weekly data for the 7-day benchmark, and monthly data for the 30-day benchmark.
