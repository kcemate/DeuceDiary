## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive user acquisition through organic growth and viral loops. It focuses on clear incentives, robust tracking, and proactive fraud prevention to maximize impact.

**1. Program Name & Branding:** "Spread the Deuce" – Playful, memorable, and directly tied to the brand.

**2. Mechanics & Incentives:**

* **Referrer Rewards:**
    * **Tiered System:**
        * **Level 1 (Refer 1-5 Users):** 25% off next month’s subscription
        * **Level 2 (Refer 6-10 Users):** 50% off next month’s subscription + Exclusive Deuce Diary sticker pack
        * **Level 3 (Refer 11+ Users):** 75% off next month’s subscription + Featured profile on DeuceDiary.com + Personalized shoutout on social media
    * **Bonus Referral:** Every referral converts to a new paying subscriber, the referrer receives an additional 10% off their subscription for that month.
* **Referee Rewards:**
    * **Discounted Subscription:** New subscribers receive 20% off their first month’s subscription.
    * **Welcome Gift (Optional - Phase 2):**  Consider a small digital goodie bag (e.g., a curated playlist, a printable habit tracker) for the first 100 referrals.
* **Referral Link/Code:** Unique, trackable links/codes for each referrer.  Can be generated via a custom referral dashboard or integrated within the app/website.


**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Social Sharing Buttons:** Prominent, easy-to-use social sharing buttons within the app/website, directly linked to the referral link/code.  Focus on visually appealing graphics.
* **Email Templates:**
    * **Referral Invite:** Triggered when a user signs up. Includes a personalized message and the referral link/code.
    * **Reminder Email:** Sent weekly to active referrers, highlighting their progress and offering a gentle nudge.
    * **Success Email:** Sent to the referrer upon a successful referral (referee signs up and subscribes).
* **In-App Notifications:**  "Your friend has joined Deuce Diary! Check them out and earn rewards!" -  Triggered when a referral is made.
* **Community Integration (Long-Term Goal):**  Integrate referral rewards into existing community events and challenges within the app/website.

**4. Fraud Prevention:**

* **IP Address Monitoring:** Track referral activity by IP address to identify suspicious patterns of multiple referrals from the same location.
* **Account Validation:** Implement a two-factor authentication system, especially for higher-tier rewards.
* **Conversion Tracking:** Closely monitor referral conversions.  If a referral link consistently leads to abandoned subscriptions, investigate further.
* **Manual Review:**  Designate a team member to periodically review high-volume referrals and investigate any irregularities.
* **Limit Referrer Tier Progression:**  Implement a minimum referral time to unlock higher tiers –  e.g., 30 days of active use before progressing.
* **Bot Detection:**  Utilize tools that detect and block bot traffic generating fraudulent referral links.

**5. Tracking Implementation:**

* **Dedicated Referral Dashboard:**  A central hub for referrers to:
    * Track their referrals.
    * See their earned rewards.
    * Generate their referral link/code.
