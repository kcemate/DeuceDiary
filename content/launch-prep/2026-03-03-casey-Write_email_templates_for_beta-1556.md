Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencers, with variations to suit different levels of formality and engagement.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template (Focus: Gathering Feedback & Building Relationship)**

**Subject: Invitation to Test [Product Name] - Help Shape the Future!**

**Option 1 (Formal):**

Dear [Beta Tester Name],

We’re thrilled to invite you to join our beta program for [Product Name], our new [product category – e.g., productivity app, mobile game, software tool]. We’re incredibly excited about this project and believe your expertise in [mention their area of expertise if you know it - e.g., user experience, mobile gaming, data analysis] would be invaluable in helping us refine it.

As a beta tester, you'll have exclusive access to [Product Name] and will be asked to provide detailed feedback on [mention key areas you're focusing on - e.g., usability, features, performance]. Your insights will directly contribute to the final product and help us ensure a seamless experience for all users.

[Link to Beta Program Details/Registration Page]

We appreciate your time and dedication. We’ll be in touch to schedule a brief kickoff call and provide you with your login details.

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team

**Option 2 (Less Formal):**

Hey [Beta Tester Name],

We're super excited to be launching [Product Name] and we'd love your help! We’re looking for beta testers like you to give us the lowdown on the latest version.

We need your honest feedback on [mention key areas - e.g., how easy it is to use, if the features are working as expected].  We'll be sending you access to [Product Name] and asking you to share your thoughts.

[Link to Beta Program Details/Registration Page]

Thanks so much for your help – we really value your opinion!

Best,

[Your Name] & The [Your Company Name] Team


**Key Considerations for Beta Testers:**

*   **Clear Expectations:** Be very specific about what you need them to do and what kind of feedback you’re looking for.
*   **Communication Channels:**  Specify how you’ll communicate (e.g., Slack, email, forum).
*   **Incentives:**  Consider offering a small incentive (early access, discount, gift card) for their participation.
