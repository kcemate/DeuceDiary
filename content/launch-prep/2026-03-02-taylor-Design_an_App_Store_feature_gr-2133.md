He should have a cold, detached expression.  Think subtle shading and a slight “off” angle.  It *shouldn’t* be a cute or friendly depiction.
* **Layering:** The portrait is the focus, but the background isn't empty.  We'll layer elements slightly out of focus to create depth and a sense of disorientation.
* **Rule of Thirds:**  Use the rule of thirds to place the portrait off-center, adding to the slightly unsettling feel.
