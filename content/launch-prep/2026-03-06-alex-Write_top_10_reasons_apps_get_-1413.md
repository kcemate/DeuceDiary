**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (as of Late 2023/Early 2024 - Apple’s Guidelines Evolve!)**

3. **Privacy Violations:**  Failing to comply with Apple's Privacy Policy, collecting excessive data, or not clearly disclosing data collection practices.
4. **Intellectual Property Infringement:**  Using copyrighted material (music, images, code, trademarks) without permission.

8. **Technical Issues:** Problems with the app's code, design, or user interface.  This can include issues with localization.
9. **Using Third-Party SDKs:**  Some SDKs can trigger rejections if they’re deemed to violate Apple’s rules or aren't properly integrated.
10. **Not Meeting App Store Requirements:**  Specifically regarding app size, supported devices, or minimum operating system version.
