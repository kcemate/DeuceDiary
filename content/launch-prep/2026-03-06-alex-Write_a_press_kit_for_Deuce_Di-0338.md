**1. ABOUT DEUCE DIARY**

“Deuce Diary” is a powerful and brutally honest coming-of-age story following the tumultuous journey of 16-year-old Deuce, navigating the complexities of identity, addiction, family dysfunction, and the struggle for self-acceptance. Told through a series of intimate diary entries, the story exposes the raw realities of life on the fringes, offering a refreshing and unflinching perspective on teenage struggle. 

This project isn't about glorifying bad behavior; it’s about understanding the *why* behind it – the desperation, the pain, and the yearning for connection that can lead a young person down a dangerous path.  “Deuce Diary” aims to spark conversation about addiction, mental health, and the importance of empathy and support.
