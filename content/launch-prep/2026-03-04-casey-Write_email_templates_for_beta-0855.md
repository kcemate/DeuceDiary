**1. Beta Tester Email Template**

*   Providing detailed feedback on your experience – what you liked, what you found confusing, and any suggestions for improvement.
*   Completing a short survey at the end of your testing period.

**Timeline:**

*   Beta testing will run from [Start Date] to [End Date].
