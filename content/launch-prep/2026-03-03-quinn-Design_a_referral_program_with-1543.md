**3. Referral Tiers & Mechanics:**

We’ll structure this with a tiered system to reward escalating levels of referrals.

*   **Tier 1:  Basic Referral (Easy to Achieve)**
    *   **Referral Action:**  New customer refers a friend via unique referral link or code.
    *   **Reward for the Referrer:**  **10% discount** on their next purchase. (This is a low-risk, attractive incentive).
    *   **Reward for the Referred Friend:** 10% off their first purchase.
    *   **Ease of Use:** Simple sharing via email, social media copy, or text.

*   **Tier 2:  Silver Referral (Mid-Level Achievement)**
    *   **Referral Action:**  New customer refers 2-3 friends.
    *   **Reward for the Referrer:** **20% discount** on their next purchase.
    *   **Reward for the Referred Friend:** 15% off their first purchase.
    *   **Bonus:**  Early access to new products or features. (Adds exclusivity).

*   **Tier 3:  Gold Referral (High-Level Achievement – Best for Loyal Customers)**
    *   **Referral Action:** New customer refers 5+ friends.
    *   **Reward for the Referrer:** **$50 Gift Card** to your store / platform. (Significant incentive).
    *   **Reward for the Referred Friend:** 20% off their first purchase + a small welcome gift (e.g., a branded sticker or small product sample).
    *   **Bonus:**  Priority customer support.
