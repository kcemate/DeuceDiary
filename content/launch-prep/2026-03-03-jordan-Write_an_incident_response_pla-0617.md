**1. Introduction**

This Incident Response Plan (IRP) outlines the procedures for identifying, containing, eradicating, recovering from, and learning from security incidents affecting [Organization Name]’s systems, data, and operations.  The plan is designed to minimize disruption, protect assets, and ensure business continuity.

**2. Goals**

*   Rapid identification and classification of security incidents.
*   Effective containment and mitigation of incidents.
*   Minimization of damage to systems and data.
*   Compliance with relevant regulations and legal requirements.
*   Continuous improvement of our security posture.

**3. Definitions**

*   **Incident:** Any event that has the potential to violate security policies, compromise the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information systems or data, or disrupt business operations.
*   **Severity Levels:**  (See Section 4)
*   **Incident Response Team (IRT):** The designated group responsible for executing this plan.
*   **Stakeholders:** Individuals or groups who may be affected by or have an interest in an incident.
