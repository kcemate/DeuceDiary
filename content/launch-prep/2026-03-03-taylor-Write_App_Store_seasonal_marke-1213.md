**Seasonal Marketing Calendar – Fitness/Wellness App (Example)**

**I. January: New Year, New You (Resolution Season)**

* **Theme:** Motivation, Fresh Start, Goal Setting, Health Reboot
* **Content Pillars:**  Healthy Habits, Motivation, Goal Setting Tips,  Quick Workouts, Healthy Recipes
* **Campaigns:**
    * **ASA Campaign:** Targeting keywords like “new year fitness,” “weight loss app,” “healthy habits.”
    * **App Store Featured:** Highlight features that support goal setting and tracking (e.g., custom goal creation, progress tracking).
    * **Content:** Blog posts, social media content, and email sequences focused on resolutions. “30-Day Challenge” promotions within the app.
* **Key Metrics:** App downloads, sign-ups, active users, goal completion rate within the app.
* **Timeline:** Begins Dec 26th - ongoing through January 31st


**II. February:  Heart Health & Self-Care**

* **Theme:**  Love Your Body, Mental Wellbeing, Self-Care, Heart Health
* **Content Pillars:**  Stress Management, Mindfulness, Heart Health Awareness, Healthy Recipes for Valentine’s Day
* **Campaigns:**
    * **ASA Campaign:**  Targeting “stress relief app”, “meditation app”, “heart health tips”.
    * **App Store Featured:** Promote features focused on stress reduction, mindfulness exercises, and heart health metrics.
    * **Content:**  Valentine's Day-themed content (e.g., “Treat Yourself” challenges), articles on self-care.
* **Key Metrics:**  Engagement with mindfulness/meditation features,  app session length, new user sign-ups.

**III. March:  Spring Reset & Movement**

* **Key Metrics:**  Usage of outdoor workout plans, downloads of recipe content, conversion rates.
