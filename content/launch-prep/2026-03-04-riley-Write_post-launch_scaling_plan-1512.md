Okay, let's craft a post-launch scaling plan with clear thresholds. This plan will focus on a SaaS product, but the principles can be adapted for other types of launches. We’ll break it down into phases and use a metric-driven approach with defined thresholds for each.

**Product:** Let's assume we're launching a project management SaaS called "FlowTask."

**Overall Goal:** Sustainable growth and a positive user experience as FlowTask gains traction.

**Phase 1: Initial Traction (0-30 Days Post-Launch)**

* **Focus:** Validation, Early Adopter Feedback, Critical Bug Fixes
* **Metrics:**
    * **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target: 50 - 100. Threshold: **Below 50 – Immediate investigation.** If consistently below, re-evaluate marketing efforts, onboarding, and core functionality. **Above 100 – Continue monitoring closely.**
    * **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** Target: 150 – 300. Threshold: **Below 150 – Significant concern.** Requires immediate action – increased marketing, perhaps a simplified feature set. **Above 300 –  Maintain momentum, focus on feature refinement.**
    * **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Target: $50 - $75. Threshold: **Above $75 – Critical review of marketing channels.**  Switch focus to more efficient channels (e.g., content marketing, targeted ads). **Below $50 - Analyze for opportunity to scale marketing spend.**
    * **Churn Rate (Trial to Paid):** Target: <10%. Threshold: **Above 10% – Immediate investigation of onboarding and value proposition.**  Simplify trial, offer better support, highlight key benefits.
    * **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** Target: +30. Threshold: **Below +20 - Serious issues impacting user satisfaction.**  Prioritize addressing negative feedback and usability problems. **Above +40 - Great signal, maintain and leverage positive reviews.**

* **Scaling Actions:**
    * **Support:** Dedicated support channel (email, chat) – Response time target: <1 hour.
    * **Onboarding:** Streamlined onboarding flow, tutorials, and knowledge base.
    * **Bug Fixes:** Prioritize critical bugs based on impact.


**Phase 2: Growth Acceleration (31-90 Days Post-Launch)**

* **Focus:** Expanding User Base, Feature Iteration based on Feedback
* **Metrics:**
    * **DAU:** Target: 200 – 500. Threshold: **Below 200 -  Review marketing performance, consider A/B testing marketing copy.** **Above 500 – Invest in scaling infrastructure (servers, database).**
    * **MAU:** Target: 600 – 1200. Threshold: **Below 600 – Increase investment in marketing channels with highest ROI.** **Above 1200 – Explore partnerships or referral programs.**
    * **CAC:** Target: $40 - $60.  Threshold: **Above $60 – Adjust marketing strategies, explore new acquisition channels.** **Below $40 - Optimize existing campaigns.**
    * **Churn Rate (Trial to Paid):** Target: <7%. Threshold: **Above 7% – Improve trial experience, offer incentives for conversion.**
    * **Feature Usage:** Monitor usage of key features to identify areas for optimization or new features.

* **Scaling Actions:**
    * **Increased Support Staff:**  Based on support ticket volume.
    * **Feature Prioritization:**  Based on user feedback and data.
    * **Automation:** Implement automation for tasks like onboarding and customer support.
