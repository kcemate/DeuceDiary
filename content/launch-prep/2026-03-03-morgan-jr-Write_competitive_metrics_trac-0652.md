This Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan outlines a systematic approach to regularly monitor and analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) against our primary competitors. This isn't a one-time project; it’s an ongoing process built around regular reporting and strategic reviews.

**II. Goals & Objectives**

* **Primary Goal:** To gain a deep understanding of our competitive landscape and inform strategic decisions.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify emerging trends in competitor activity.
