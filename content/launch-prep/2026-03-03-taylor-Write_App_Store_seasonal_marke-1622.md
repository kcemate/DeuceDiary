**Seasonal Marketing Calendar for an App**

**I.  January: New Year, New Habits**

*   **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Self-Improvement
*   **Focus:**  Highlight app's ability to help users achieve new year's resolutions.
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **ASO:** Keyword research focusing on "new year's resolutions," "goal setting," "habit tracker."
    *   **Paid Ads:**  Target users searching for "new year's goals" or similar terms.  Promote onboarding tutorials.
    *   **Email:** Welcome email series offering tips for setting achievable goals within the app.
    *   **Social Media:** Content related to motivation, productivity, and small wins.  Run a "New Year's Challenge" within the app.
    *   **Blog:**  "5 Ways [App Name] Can Help You Achieve Your New Year’s Goals."
