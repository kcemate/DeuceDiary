Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each tailored to a different goal and with varying lengths. I've included suggested timing, content ideas, and a focus on building a relationship with the recipient.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (7-14 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and set expectations.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who’ve signed up through a website form, landing page, or lead magnet.
* **Timing:**
    * **Day 1: Welcome Email** - Immediately after signup.
    * **Day 3: Value Offer Reminder** – Remind them of the lead magnet they downloaded.
    * **Day 5: Brand Story (Short)** -  A brief, engaging story about *why* you started your business.
    * **Day 8: Problem/Solution Introduction** – Highlight the pain points your product/service addresses.
    * **Day 11: Customer Testimonial/Social Proof** –  A short quote or image from a satisfied customer.
    * **Day 14: Offer a Small Incentive** -  Discount code or free resource.


**Example Email Content:**

**Email 1 (Day 1):**

* **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
* **Body:** “Hi [Subscriber Name], Thanks so much for joining the [Your Brand Name] community! We’re thrilled to have you. As a thank you, here’s a link to your [Lead Magnet Name] again: [Link].  We’re passionate about [Your Brand Mission/Value] and we’re excited to share [what you offer] with you.  Stay tuned for more helpful tips and updates!”

**Email 2 (Day 3):**

* **Subject:** Still Loving Your [Lead Magnet Name]? 🤩
* **Body:** “Just a friendly reminder that you downloaded our [Lead Magnet Name]!  We think it's packed with valuable information that can help you [benefit of lead magnet].  Don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions!”

**Email 3 (Day 5):**

* **Subject:**  The Story Behind [Your Brand Name] 🌟
* **Body:** “[Your Brand Name] started because [brief, personal story - 2-3 sentences].  We believe in [core values] and want to help you [achieve a goal related to your offering].”

**Campaign 2:  Nurturing Leads - Product Feature Focus (14-21 Days)**

* **Goal:**  Move prospects closer to a purchase by highlighting key features and benefits of your product.
* **Target Audience:** Subscribers who have engaged with your content (e.g., visited your website, opened previous emails) - perhaps those who clicked on a specific blog post.
* **Timing:**
    * **Day 7: Feature Highlight #1** - Focus on one key benefit.
    * **Day 14: Feature Highlight #2** - Expand on a different benefit.
    * **Day 21:  Case Study/Use Case** – Show how someone else is benefiting.
