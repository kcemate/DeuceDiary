## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a comprehensive approach to tracking and analyzing your competition, providing insights for strategic decision-making and driving business growth.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape to identify opportunities, mitigate threats, and inform our strategy.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths/weaknesses.
    * Monitor competitor activities (pricing, marketing, product changes).
    * Benchmark our performance against the competition.
    * Identify emerging trends and threats in the market.
    *  Inform strategic decisions related to product development, marketing, pricing, and sales.


**2. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Tier 1 Competitors (Critical):** These are direct competitors offering similar products/services and posing the most significant threat. (e.g., Directly competing brands)
* **Tier 2 Competitors (Significant):**  Offer similar solutions but may target a different segment or have a unique value proposition. (e.g., Adjacent brands, alternative solutions)
* **Tier 3 Competitors (Monitor):** Smaller players, niche brands, or emerging trends.  Monitoring is primarily for awareness.
* **Tracking Process:**
    * **Market Research:** Utilize tools like Google Trends, industry reports, and analyst reports.
    * **Customer Feedback:**  Analyze customer reviews on sites like Yelp, Trustpilot, Capterra, G2, etc.
    * **Social Listening:** Monitor social media channels (Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram) for mentions of competitors and their activities.
    * **Website Analysis:** Regularly check competitor websites for updates, pricing, and promotional offers.
