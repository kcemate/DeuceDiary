# Write 3 email drip campaign sequences.

Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for varying lengths. I’ve included suggested timing and content for each email, aiming for a total length of 5-7 emails per sequence.

**Sequence 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Ideal for New Subscribers - 5-7 Emails)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and encourage initial engagement.
* **Timing:** Immediately after signup, then spread out over 5-7 days.
* **Email 1: Immediate Welcome (Within 5 mins of Signup)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:**  Thank you for subscribing! Briefly reiterate what they signed up for (e.g., “Get exclusive tips on healthy cooking”). Include a friendly welcome message and a link to your website homepage.
* **Email 2: Brand Story (24-48 hours)**
    * **Subject:** Our Story: Why We Do What We Do 
    * **Content:**  A short, engaging story about your brand's origins, mission, and values.  Humanize your brand – focus on *why* you started it. Include a compelling image.
* **Email 3: Top 3 Benefits (72 hours)**
    * **Subject:**  3 Ways [Your Product/Service] Can Help You
    * **Content:** Highlight 3 key benefits of what you offer. Use bullet points for clarity. Include a relevant image or GIF. Link to a product page or a blog post that expands on one of the benefits.
* **Email 4: Customer Testimonial (3-5 days)**
    * **Subject:** Don't Just Take Our Word For It…
    * **Content:**  Share a positive customer testimonial (with the customer's permission, of course!).  This builds social proof.
* **Email 5: Quick Tip/Resource (5-7 days)**
    * **Subject:**  [Relevant Tip Related to Your Niche]
    * **Content:** Provide a valuable, actionable tip or resource (e.g., a short checklist, infographic, or link to a helpful article).  Position yourself as an expert.


**Sequence 2:  Lead Nurturing - Focusing on a Specific Product (7-10 Emails)**

* **Goal:** Move leads closer to a purchase for a particular product/service. This sequence assumes they've shown some initial interest (e.g., downloaded a resource).
* **Timing:**  Spread out over 7-10 days, with increasing urgency.
* **Email 1: Reminder & Value Recap (24 hours after lead action - Download, etc.)**
    * **Subject:**  Just a Reminder: Your [Resource Name]
    * **Content:** Thank them for downloading the resource. Briefly recap the key benefits they’ll get from it.
* **Email 2: Problem/Solution (3 days)**
    * **Subject:**  Are You Struggling With [Pain Point]?
    * **Content:** Identify a common pain point your product solves.  Empathize with the reader’s challenge.
* **Email 3: Product Introduction (4-5 days)**
    * **Subject:** Introducing [Product Name]: The Solution You Need
    * **Content:**  Introduce your product and *specifically* how it addresses the pain point identified in Email 2.  Include a short video demo or compelling image.
* **Email 4: Feature Spotlight (6-7 days)**
    * **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out On This Key Feature of [Product Name]
    * **Content:**  Focus on one specific, valuable feature of the product. Explain *why*
