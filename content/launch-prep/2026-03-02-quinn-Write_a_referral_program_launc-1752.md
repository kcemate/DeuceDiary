## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive user acquisition, increase engagement, and build a strong community. The program focuses on a tiered reward structure, a viral loop leveraging social sharing, robust fraud prevention, and detailed tracking for optimization.

**1. Program Name & Branding:** “Spread the Deuce” – playful and memorable, highlighting the core product.

**2. Mechanics & Rewards (Tiered System)**

* **Referrer (Existing User):**
    * **Level 1 - First Referral:** 100 Deuce Points (redeemable for premium features, merchandise, or discounts)
    * **Level 2 - Every Subsequent Referral (within 30 days):** 50 Deuce Points
    * **Level 3 - Referral Converts to Paid Subscription (monthly or annual):** 300 Deuce Points (Significant incentive)
* **Referee (New User):**
    * **Immediate Reward:** 25 Deuce Points upon signup & first login.
    * **Conversion Reward (after 7 days of activity):** 50 Deuce Points - Encourages initial engagement.
* **Bonus Promotion (Limited Time):**  “Double Deuce Day” - First 1000 referrers receive a bonus 150 Deuce Points.

**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Social Sharing Buttons:** Prominently placed buttons on the Deuce Diary app & website for sharing to:
    * **Instagram:**  Designed template with a visually appealing image of the diary & a compelling caption ("Start your daily journey with Deuce Diary! Share & get rewarded!").
    * **Facebook:** Similar template, focused on community and benefits.
    * **Twitter:** Short, engaging tweet with a trackable link.
* **Unique Referral Link:** Each user gets a personalized referral link (e.g., `deuce.diary/referral/[user_id]`) to track referrals.
* **"Invite a Friend" Feature:** Within the app itself, a simple button to copy and paste the referral link to friends.
* **Gamified Sharing:**  Introduce a challenge – "Refer 3 friends & unlock a special diary sticker!” - to motivate further sharing.


**4. Fraud Prevention:**

* **Manual Review:** Initially, all referred users will be manually reviewed for genuine sign-ups.
* **IP Address Monitoring:** Flag accounts with multiple sign-ups from the same IP address – potential bot activity.
* **Device ID Tracking:** Monitor unique device IDs to identify suspicious patterns.
* **Verification Email:**  Require new users to verify their email address – reduces fake accounts.
* **Activity-Based Thresholds:** Suspicious activity (rapid sign-ups, no app usage) will trigger manual review.
* **Terms & Conditions:** Clearly define program rules and consequences for fraudulent behavior.  Include a reporting mechanism.

**5. Tracking Implementation:**

* **Referral Software:** Utilize a dedicated referral marketing platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, MentionMe, Friendfluence) for robust tracking and management.
    * **Key Metrics:**
        * **Number of Referrals:** Total number of referrals generated.
        * **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of referrals who sign up & become active users.
        * **Average Referral Value:** Average revenue generated by referred users (if applicable - premium subscriptions).
        * **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Cost of acquiring a new user through the referral program.
        * **Churn Rate:** Track if referral users are more likely to churn.
