Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for approximately 7-10 emails over 2-4 weeks. I've included subject lines, email content snippets, and suggested timing.  Remember to tailor this to *your* specific brand, product, and audience.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Goal: Build Rapport & Establish Value)**

* **Target Audience:** New Subscribers (those who signed up through a lead magnet, website form, etc.)
* **Length:** 7 Emails
* **Overall Tone:** Friendly, informative, and focusing on the benefits of being part of your community.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Immediately): Welcome & Lead Magnet Delivery**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! Here's Your [Lead Magnet Name]
    * **Content:**  A warm welcome, reiterates why they signed up, and delivers the promised lead magnet (e.g., ebook, checklist, template).  Include a quick call to action - "Explore the [Lead Magnet]"
* **Email 2 (24 hours later):  Introduce Your Brand Story**
    * **Subject:**  The Story Behind [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:** Briefly share your brand's mission, values, and history.  Focus on *why* you do what you do – connect with your audience on an emotional level.
* **Email 3 (3 days later):  Top 3 Benefits of [Your Product/Service]**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock [Benefit 1], [Benefit 2], & [Benefit 3]
    * **Content:** Highlight 3 key benefits of your product/service, tying them to problems your audience faces. Include a compelling image.
* **Email 4 (5 days later):  Customer Story/Testimonial**
    * **Subject:**  “[Customer Name] Achieved [Result] With [Your Product/Service]”
    * **Content:**  Share a short customer success story or testimonial.  Social proof is powerful!
* **Email 5 (7 days later):  Quick Tip/Value Add (Related to their Lead Magnet)**
    * **Subject:**  Level Up Your [Topic from Lead Magnet]!
    * **Content:**  Offer a quick, actionable tip related to the content of their lead magnet – demonstrating your expertise and providing ongoing value.
* **Email 6 (10 days later):  Introduce a Related Product/Service (Soft Sell)**
    * **Subject:**  Want to Take It Further?
    * **Content:**  Introduce a slightly related product or service that complements their initial interest.  Don't push a hard sell - focus on solving a slightly more complex problem.
* **Email 7 (14 days later):  Reminder & Special Offer (Limited Time)**
    * **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out! [Discount/Offer] Ends Soon
    * **Content:**  Gentle reminder of the value they’ve received and a limited-time offer to encourage purchase –  incentivize action.
