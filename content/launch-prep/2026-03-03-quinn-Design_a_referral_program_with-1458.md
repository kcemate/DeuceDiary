Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives, focusing on being attractive to both the referrer and the referred. Here's a detailed breakdown, covering different tiers and considerations:

**1. Program Name & Theme:**

* **Option 1 (Modern):** "Spread the [Brand Name] Love"
* **Option 2 (Benefit-Focused):** "Get Rewarded for Sharing"
* **Option 3 (Playful):** "Bring a Friend & Get [Cool Perk]"

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **Simple Signup:** Easy for both referrer and referred to understand and participate.
* **Unique Referral Links/Codes:** Generate unique links or codes for each user. This is crucial for tracking.
* **Tracking & Reporting:** Use a referral program platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Loopin, or build your own integration – depending on your tech capabilities).  This allows you to see who's referring whom, and how many successful referrals are occurring.

**3. Incentive Structure – Tiered Rewards (Most Effective)**

We'll implement a tiered system to incentivize more referrals.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (First Referral)**

* **For the Referrer:**  [Small Incentive] -  Example:
    * **Discount Code:** 10% off their next purchase
    * **Small Gift Card:** $5-$10
    * **Free Product Sample:** (Relevant to your product - e.g., a skincare sample, a small tool)
* **For the Referred:** [Moderate Incentive] – Example:
    * **Discount Code:** 15% off their first purchase
    * **Free Shipping:** On their first order

**Tier 2: Silver Tier (After 2 Referrals)**

* **For the Referrer:** [Medium Incentive] - Example:
    * **Increased Discount Code:** 15% off their next purchase (or a larger amount)
    * **Bonus Credit:** $25 to their account
    * **Exclusive Early Access:** To new products or features.
* **For the Referred:** [Moderate Increase] - Example:
    * **Discount Code:** 20% off their next purchase
    * **Free Gift:** (Small, branded item - e.g., a sticker, keychain)

**Tier 3: Gold Tier (After 5 Referrals)**

* **For the Referrer:** [Significant Incentive] - Example:
    * **High-Value Discount Code:** 20% off their next purchase
    * **Cash Reward:** $50 - $100 (depending on your budget)
    * **VIP Status:** Access to a dedicated customer support line or exclusive events.
* **For the Referred:** [Significant Reward] - Example:
    * **Larger Discount Code:** 25% off their next purchase
    * **Premium Gift:** (Higher-value item - e.g., a larger product, upgraded version)


**4.  Bonus/Milestone Rewards (Optional but Engaging)**

* **Referral Leaderboard:**  Publicly recognize top referrers (with their permission) to create a competitive element.
* **Double Reward Days:**  Occasionally offer double rewards for referrals on specific days.
* **Referral Milestone Rewards:**  Reward users when they reach specific referral numbers (e.g., 10, 20, 50 referrals).

**5. Communication & Promotion**

* **Clear Landing Page:** A dedicated page on your website explaining the program.
* **Email Marketing:** Automated emails to:
    * Welcome new users and explain the program.
    * Remind existing users to share.
    * Notify users when someone they referred makes a purchase.
