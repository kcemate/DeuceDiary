**I. Understanding Your Target Influencers (Research Phase - 1-2 Weeks)**

* **Identify the Sub-Genres:** Bathroom humor isn’t monolithic. Segment your target influencers by style:
    * **Dark Humor:** Think offensive, uncomfortable, pushing boundaries. (e.g., @TheRealMattSmith - if appropriate for your brand)
    * **Dry/Deadpan Humor:**  Delivered with incredibly serious expressions.
    * **Relatable/Observational:** Focusing on common bathroom experiences (long waits, awkward encounters, etc.).
    * **Animation/Memes:** Leveraging GIFs, short animations, and popular meme formats.
    * **Voiceover/Sound Design:** Using audio for comedic effect.
* **Platform Focus:** Where is your audience *actually* hanging out? Primarily TikTok? Instagram Reels? YouTube Shorts? Twitch (surprisingly, some bathroom humor creators stream). Don't spread yourself too thin.
* **Audience Size & Engagement:** Don't *just* look at follower count. Focus on:
    * **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments) / Followers – a higher rate indicates a more active and responsive audience. Aim for 2-5% or higher.
    * **Comment Quality:** Are people genuinely engaging in conversation, or just spamming?
    * **Audience Demographics:** (If available)  Ensure they align with your target demographic.
* **Tools for Research:**
    * **TikTok Creator Marketplace:** Excellent for finding creators by niche.
    * **Instagram Search:** Use hashtags like #bathroomhumor, #toiletcomedy, #funnytoilet, #shitposting, #peepeehumor (be careful with the last one – gauge your brand's tolerance!)
    * **Google Trends:** See what bathroom humor trends are currently popular.
    * **Social Blade:**  Analyze influencer stats.

**II. Building Your Outreach List (1 Week)**

* **Create a Spreadsheet:** Columns for: Creator Name, Platform, Follower Count, Engagement Rate, Notes (e.g., “Likes dark humor,” “Strong comment section”), Outreach Method, Status (e.g., “Contacted,” “Responding,” “Collaborated”).
* **Tiered Approach:** Categorize influencers by potential impact:
    * **Tier 1 (High Impact):** 10k+ followers, high engagement, strong brand alignment.  Focus your most targeted efforts here.
    * **Tier 2 (Mid Impact):** 5k-10k followers, good engagement. Good for wider reach.
    * **Tier 3 (Micro-Influencers):**  1k-5k followers, very high engagement.  Often more willing to collaborate for smaller compensation.

**III. Crafting Your Outreach Message (Before Sending - 1-2 Days)**

* **Personalization is KEY:** *Never* send generic copy/paste messages.  Reference specific content they’ve created.
* **Start with a Genuine Observation:** “I just saw your video about [specific bathroom humor topic] and it absolutely killed me!”
* **Clearly State Your Value Proposition:** What's in it for *them*? Examples:
    * **Collaboration Opportunities:** "I'd love to collaborate on a series of short videos around [relevant theme]."
    * **Product/Service Mention (if relevant):** "We’re a company creating [product] that would be perfect for [bathroom humor context].  I'd love to send you a sample for review/usage." (Be careful – must be genuinely relevant!)
