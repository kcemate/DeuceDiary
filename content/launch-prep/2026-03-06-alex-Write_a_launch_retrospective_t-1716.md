**I. Reflection & Discussion (15-30 mins)**

* **What went well?** (Focus on positive outcomes, successes, and things that aligned with expectations)

* **What went wrong?** (Be honest and specific. Focus on issues that impacted the goal)
    * *Prompt:* "What roadblocks did we encounter? Where did we fall short of expectations?"
* **What were the biggest challenges?** (Root cause analysis – Why did these things happen?)
    * *Prompt:* "If we could change one thing immediately, what would it be?  What contributed to that challenge?"
* **How effective was our communication?** (Internal and external)
    * *Prompt:* “Did we communicate effectively with the user? Did we communicate effectively with the team?”
* **Did we meet our initial assumptions?** (Were we wrong about user behavior, technical performance, marketing reach?)
    * *Prompt:* “What surprised us about how users interacted with the launch?”
