**II. Key Metrics & Visualizations:**

Here’s a breakdown of the key metrics, categorized with suggested visualizations:

**A. Acquisition & Growth:**

* **New Users (Daily/Weekly/Monthly):** Line chart – Tracks the flow of new users over time. Highlight spikes related to marketing campaigns.
* **Total Users (Daily/Weekly/Monthly):** Line chart – Overall user growth.

* **Source of Acquisition:** Pie chart or Bar chart – Shows where new users are coming from (e.g., App Store, Social Media, Referrals).  Segment by platform (iOS, Android).

**B. Engagement & Activity:**

* **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Line Chart – Most crucial daily metric.
* **Weekly Active Users (WAU):** Line Chart – Tracks weekly trends.
* **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** Line Chart –  Shows overall reach.
* **Diary Entries Started:** Number – Total number of new diary entries created. (Number / Gauge)
* **Diary Entries Completed:** Number – Total diary entries completed. (Number / Gauge) -  This is a key engagement indicator.
* **Average Diary Entry Length:** Average – Average words/characters per diary entry. (Number/Gauge) -  Can indicate depth of reflection.
* **Time Spent in App (Daily/Weekly/Monthly):** Line Chart - Total time users spend in the app.
* **Session Duration:** Average – Average length of a single user session. (Number/Gauge)

**C. Content Performance:**

* **Most Popular Diary Prompts:** Bar Chart – Shows which prompts are being used most frequently.
* **Most Viewed Diary Entries (Overall):** Bar Chart –  Shows which diary entries have the most views. (Helps understand popular topics/styles).
* **Most Liked/Reacted Diary Entries:** Bar Chart - Measures reaction to specific entries.
* **Content Category Breakdown (Diary Entries):** Pie chart or Tree Map -  Break down diary entries by category (e.g., Gratitude, Challenges, Goals, Reflections, Ideas). Useful for understanding user interests.
* **Trending Topics (Keywords within Diary Entries):** Word Cloud or Bar Chart – Analyze the most frequently used words in diary entries (requires text analysis).


**D. Retention:**

* **Retention Rate (Day 1, Day 7, Day 30):** Line Chart -  Percentage of users who return after specific time periods.  Critical for understanding long-term viability.
* **Churn Rate:** Calculated based on retention - Percentage of users who stop using the app within a given period. (Number/Gauge)
