Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health/wellness brands, categorized by approach and with varying levels of formality. Choose the template that best suits your brand's voice and the potential partner's style.

**1. Short & Sweet - Perfect for Instagram & Quick Outreach**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand] + [Their Brand]**

Hi [Partner Name],

We love what you’re doing at [Their Brand]! We're [Your Brand], a [brief, impactful description - e.g., "brand focused on mindful movement" or "company creating healthy meal plans"]. 

We think our audiences would really connect. We'd love to explore a potential collaboration – maybe a giveaway, a shared post, or a product feature.

Check us out: [Link to Your Website/Instagram]

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]

**Why it works:** Direct, friendly, and easy to read. Good for initial contact.

**2. Slightly More Detailed - Ideal for Blogs, Podcasts, & More Established Brands**

**Subject: Partnership Proposal - Aligning Our Wellness Visions**

Dear [Partner Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I'm the [Your Title] at [Your Brand]. We're a [Detailed Brand Description – 2-3 sentences highlighting your mission, values, and what you offer]. 

We've been consistently impressed with [Their Brand]'s work in [Mention Specific Content/Initiatives You Admire – be genuine!]. We believe there’s a strong synergy between our brands and audiences – both of us are passionate about [Shared Value – e.g., “promoting healthy lifestyles,” “supporting mental wellbeing,” “sustainable wellness”].

**Potential Collaboration Ideas:**

*   [Idea 1 - e.g., Guest Post on Their Blog]
*   [Idea 2 - e.g., Joint Instagram Giveaway]
*   [Idea 3 - e.g., Feature Their Product in Our Newsletter]

We’re open to discussing other creative opportunities as well.  You can learn more about us here: [Link to Your Website]

Would you be open to a brief chat to explore how we could collaborate?

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]
[Link to Your Social Media]


**Why it works:** Demonstrates you've done your research, outlines clear collaboration ideas, and invites a conversation.

**3.  Focusing on Value Exchange – Great for Offering Something Specific**

**Subject:  Exclusive Offer for [Their Audience] – [Your Brand] Partnership**

Hi [Partner Name],

We’re reaching out because we believe our [Your Product/Service] would be a fantastic addition to your audience’s wellness journey.

At [Your Brand], we're dedicated to [Your Brand’s Core Benefit - e.g., "helping busy individuals prioritize self-care," "providing effective tools for stress reduction"]. 

**Here's what we’d like to offer your audience:** [Specifically state the offer – e.g., “A 20% discount code,” “A free trial of our app,” “Exclusive access to our upcoming webinar”].

We’d love to collaborate to promote this offer to your engaged community.  Let’s discuss how we can mutually benefit our audiences.

Check us out: [Link to Your Website/Offer Page]

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]


**Why it works:**  Directly highlights the benefit to the partner's audience and makes it clear what you’re offering.  This is good if you have a tangible incentive.
