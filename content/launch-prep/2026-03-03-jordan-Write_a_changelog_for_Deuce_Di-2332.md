**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Summary:** This is the initial release of Deuce Diary, a diary app designed to capture your thoughts, feelings, and experiences as if you were Deuce Bigalow!

**New Features:**

* **Diary Entry Creation:**  You can now create new diary entries with a title, date, and a rich text field to record your thoughts and feelings.
* **Date Selection:**  Choose a specific date for your diary entry using our easy-to-use date picker.
* **Rich Text Formatting:**  Basic formatting options are available within the diary entry text field:

    * **Italic Text:**  Emphasize specific words or phrases.
* **Entry List View:**  A list view displays all your diary entries, sorted chronologically.
* **Basic Search:**  Find specific diary entries by title.
* **Save Functionality:**  Entries are automatically saved as you type.
* **Initial UI Design:** A functional and visually fitting UI based on the Deuce Bigalow theme.

* **Fixed:**  Corrected an issue where the date picker wasn't correctly displaying dates.
* **Fixed:**  Resolved a minor UI glitch where the date display was slightly misaligned.
