**Retention Analysis Plan**

**1. Goal:**

* To understand and improve user retention by identifying key drop-off points and implementing strategies to keep users engaged over time.

**2. Timeframes & Benchmarks:**

**3. Data Collection & Tracking:**

* **Analytics Platform:** (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase) - This is your core data source.
* **Event Tracking:**  Set up robust event tracking to capture user actions:
    * **Onboarding Events:**  Sign-ups, account creation, tutorial completion, key feature usage.
    * **Engagement Events:** Feature usage, content consumption, social interactions, etc. (Define these based on your product)
    * **Churn Events:** Account deletion, inactivity for a sustained period.
* **User Segmentation:**  Segment users based on:
    * **Acquisition Channel:** (e.g., paid advertising, organic search, referral) - To understand which channels are driving the best retention.
    * **Demographics:** (e.g., age, location) – To identify potential differences in retention patterns.
    * **User Behavior:** (e.g., usage frequency, feature adoption) -  To identify high-value users and those at risk.
    * **Plan Type:** (Free, Premium, etc.) - To analyze the retention of different tiers.

**4. Analysis & Reporting - By Timeframe:**

**A. D1 Analysis:**

* **Focus:**  First-time user experience.
* **Key Questions:**
    * What percentage of users complete the onboarding flow?
    * Are there any points in the onboarding flow where users are dropping off?  (Heatmaps, funnel analysis)
    * What’s the user's initial impression of the product?  (Based on initial actions and potentially in-app surveys).
* **Deliverables:** D1 Retention Report –  detailed breakdown of completion rates, drop-off points, and initial user behavior.

**B. D7 Analysis:**

* **Focus:**  Early engagement and the ‘stickiness’ of the product.
* **Key Questions:**
    * What percentage of users are still active on Day 7?
    * Are users experiencing "activation gaps" - are they initially engaging but then becoming inactive?
    * What features are users primarily engaging with on Day 7?
