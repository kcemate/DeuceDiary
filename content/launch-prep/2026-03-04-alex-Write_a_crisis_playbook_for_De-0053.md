## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Purpose:** This playbook outlines the steps to take in the event of a crisis during the launch of *Deuce Diary*, aiming to minimize damage, protect brand reputation, and swiftly address issues.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be honest and open with your audience.
* **Speed:** Respond quickly and decisively.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge and validate customer concerns.
* **Collaboration:** Work together as a team to resolve the issue.
* **Documentation:** Record everything – timelines, actions taken, and communications.


**II. Potential Crisis Scenarios & Response Levels:**

We’ll categorize potential crises by severity and outline response levels:

**Level 1: Minor Issues (Low Impact - Immediate Attention)** - Examples:
* Technical glitches on the website (e.g., payment errors)
* Minor typos in marketing materials
* Negative comments on social media (isolated, low volume)

**Response:**
* **Ownership:** Social Media Manager, Community Manager, Tech Support
* **Action:** Immediately acknowledge the issue on relevant channels.  Fix the problem. Publicly apologize if necessary. Monitor for escalation.
* **Timeline:** 1-4 hours


**Level 2: Moderate Issues (Medium Impact - Dedicated Team)** - Examples:
* Significant website downtime
* Negative reviews accumulating rapidly
* Social media backlash over a specific comment/misinterpretation
* Shipping delays 

**Response:**
* **Ownership:** PR/Communications Lead, Social Media Manager, Customer Support Lead, Operations Lead
* **Action:**  Public statement addressing the issue, detailed explanation, timeline for resolution. Increased monitoring of all channels. Offer direct support to affected customers (e.g., refunds, discounts).  Escalate to leadership.
* **Timeline:** 4-24 hours


**Level 3: Severe Issues (High Impact - Crisis Management Team)** - Examples:
* Major security breach impacting user data
* Widespread negative publicity in major media outlets
* Product defect causing serious harm/safety concerns
* Significant legal challenges

**Response:**
* **Ownership:** CEO, PR/Communications Lead, Legal Counsel, Operations Lead, Customer Support Lead
* **Action:** Activate Crisis Management Team.  Immediate legal consultation.  Issue a formal statement acknowledging the severity and outlining immediate steps being taken.  Contact key stakeholders (influencers, partners).  Full transparency with authorities if required.  Suspend impacted features/products if necessary.
* **Timeline:** 24-72 hours (This will be highly dependent on the nature of the crisis)



**III. Key Roles & Responsibilities:**

* **Crisis Management Team (CMT):** CEO, PR/Communications Lead, Legal Counsel, Operations Lead, Customer Support Lead.  This team makes critical decisions and coordinates all responses.
* **Social Media Manager:** Monitors social media channels, responds to comments, escalates issues to the CMT.
* **Community Manager:** Engages with the online community, builds relationships, and provides support.
* **Customer Support Lead:** Manages customer inquiries and complaints, oversees support channels.
* **Tech Support:** Addresses technical issues and downtime.
* **PR/Communications Lead:** Drafts and disseminates public statements, manages media inquiries.

**IV. Communication Channels & Protocols:**

* **Internal Communication:** Slack channel dedicated to crisis communication – #deuce-crisis
* **Social Media:**
    * **Twitter:** Immediate updates, acknowledging issues, directing users to support channels.
    * **Instagram:** Visually appealing announcements, addressing concerns, directing users to support channels.
    * **Facebook:** Community updates, Q&A sessions, directing users to support channels.
