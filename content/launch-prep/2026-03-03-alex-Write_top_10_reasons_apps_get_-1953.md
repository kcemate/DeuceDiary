**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (as of late 2023/early 2024)**

1. **Policy Violations (General):** This is the *biggest* reason. Apple's App Store Review Guidelines are incredibly detailed, and almost any deviation can lead to rejection. This includes things like misleading content, deceptive behavior, and unauthorized use of Apple trademarks.

2. **Intellectual Property Infringement:** Using copyrighted images, music, software code, or ideas without permission is a massive red flag.  Even unintentional similarities can lead to rejection.

3. **Misleading or Deceptive Behavior:** Apps that trick users into buying things, subscribing without clear disclosures, or manipulating data in a misleading way are automatically rejected.

4. **Privacy Violations:**  This has become *extremely* stringent.  Apps must clearly explain how they collect, use, and share user data. Failing to do so, or collecting data without consent, will be denied.  Specifically, Apple's ATT (App Tracking Transparency) guidelines are central to this.

5. **Performance Issues:** Crashes, excessive battery drain, slow loading times, and generally poor performance are significant problems. Apple’s automated testing is quite effective.

6. **App Store Guidelines Violations – Specific Categories:** This can encompass various areas, including:
   * **Health & Fitness:** Strict rules around health claims, data accuracy, and user privacy.
   * **Gaming:** Rules around in-app purchases, advertising, and game mechanics.
   * **Finance:** Heavy regulation and oversight due to sensitive financial information.
   * **Education:** Strict guidelines for educational content and data collection.

7. **Lack of Required Information:**  Not providing all the necessary information in the app's metadata (title, description, keywords, screenshots, etc.) can lead to rejection.

8. **Inaccurate or Misleading Screenshots/Videos:**  Screenshots must accurately represent the app's functionality.  Misleading or deceptive visuals are a no-go.

9. **Malware or Security Issues:**  Any vulnerability that could compromise user security will be instantly rejected.

10. **Duplicate Apps:**  Submitting an app that is substantially similar to an existing app can be rejected due to potential confusion and competition concerns.
