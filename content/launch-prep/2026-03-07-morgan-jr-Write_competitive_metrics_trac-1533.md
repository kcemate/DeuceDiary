## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competitors' activities, driving strategic insights and informing your business decisions. 

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape to identify opportunities for growth, mitigate threats, and refine your strategy.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * **Identify Key Competitors:** Define your primary competitors (direct and indirect).
    * **Monitor Changes:** Track shifts in competitor strategies, product offerings, pricing, and marketing campaigns.
    * **Benchmark Performance:** Measure your performance against competitors across key metrics.
    * **Identify Trends:**  Uncover emerging trends and technologies impacting your industry.
    * **Inform Strategic Decisions:** Use competitive insights to inform product development, marketing, sales, and pricing strategies.


**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Step 1: Identify Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi)
* **Step 2: Identify Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions that address the same customer needs. (e.g., Starbucks vs. Dunkin' Donuts - both coffee, but different experiences)
* **Step 3: Prioritization Matrix:** Rate competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:** (High, Medium, Low) -  A larger share often indicates more significant influence.
    * **Revenue:** (High, Medium, Low) -  Indicates financial strength and resources.
    * **Growth Rate:** (High, Medium, Low) - Signals potential threat or opportunity.
    * **Strategic Alignment:** (High, Medium, Low) – How closely aligned are their values and approaches to yours?
    * **Relevance to Your Business:** (High, Medium, Low) - How directly does their activity impact your core business?
