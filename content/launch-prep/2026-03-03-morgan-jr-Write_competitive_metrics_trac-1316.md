**I. Goals & Objectives**

* **Overall Goal:**  To gain a competitive advantage by understanding our competitors' strengths and weaknesses, and proactively adapting our strategy to outperform them.
* **Specific Objectives (Examples):**
    * Identify key competitive differentiators.
    * Track competitor pricing strategies.
    * Monitor competitor marketing campaign performance.
    * Understand competitor product/service updates.
    * Identify emerging threats and opportunities in the market.

**II. Identifying Your Key Competitors**

* **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering the same or very similar products/services to the same target audience. (Be specific – name them!)
* **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering alternative solutions that meet the same customer needs. (Consider substitutes, adjacent products, etc.)
* **Emerging Competitors:**  New companies entering the market or startups with disruptive potential.
