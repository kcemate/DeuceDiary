Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, combining different strategies and tiers to maximize engagement.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** “Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love” or "[Your Brand Name] Rewards"
* **Visuals:** Use a clear, appealing logo or graphic that represents the referral program.

**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Define Your Ideal Referral:** Who are you trying to reach? (e.g., existing customers who are happy, specific demographics)
* **Goals:**  What are you hoping to achieve? (e.g., acquire new customers, increase brand awareness, boost sales) – Set realistic targets (e.g., 10% growth in new customer acquisition)

**3. Referral Program Structure – Tiers & Incentives:**

We’ll use a tiered system to reward increasing levels of participation.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Everyone)**

* **Referrer Incentive:** 
    * **Option 1 (Discount):** 10% off their next purchase.
    * **Option 2 (Small Credit):** $5 credit towards their account.
    * **Trigger:**  New customer signs up through the referral link.
* **Referee Incentive:** 10% off their first purchase (This incentivizes the new customer to make a purchase).
* **Ease of Use:**  Simple referral link, easy to share on social media, email, and messaging apps.


**Tier 2: Silver Referral (Referrers who refer 3+ people)**

* **Referrer Incentive:**  Upgrade to 15% off their next purchase *plus* a small gift (e.g., branded sticker, small sample)
* **Referee Incentive:** 15% off their first purchase
* **Tracking:**  Clear tracking of referrals for the referrer to see their progress.

**Tier 3: Gold Referral (Referrers who refer 5+ people)**

* **Referrer Incentive:** 20% off their next purchase *plus* a higher-value gift (e.g., premium product, exclusive access to a sale).  Could also include a monthly subscription discount for a limited time.
* **Referee Incentive:** 20% off their first purchase
* **Recognition:**  Featured on your website/social media (with their permission).


**4. Incentive Details & Value:**

* **Discount Percentages:** Adjust based on your margins. 10-20% is a good starting point.
* **Gift Values:**  Keep gifts relevant to your brand and affordable to produce. (Stickers, samples, small merchandise items, early access).
* **Exclusive Access:** Consider offering early access to sales, new product launches, or beta programs to top-tier referrers.
