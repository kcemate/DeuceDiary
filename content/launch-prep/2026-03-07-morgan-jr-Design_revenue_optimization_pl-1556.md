**I. Understanding the Current State (Assessment - 2-4 Weeks)**

* **1. Define "Revenue":** Be specific. Are we focusing on:
    * **Gross Revenue:** Total sales before any deductions.
    * **Net Revenue:** Gross revenue minus returns, discounts, and allowances.
    * **Recurring Revenue:** (If applicable) Revenue from subscriptions, contracts, etc.
* **2. Analyze Existing Revenue Streams:**
    * **Product/Service Breakdown:** What products/services generate the most revenue, and which are underperforming?
    * **Customer Segmentation:**  Who are our best customers? (Demographics, behavior, purchase history). Identify segments with high lifetime value (LTV).
    * **Channel Analysis:** Which sales channels are most effective (e.g., direct sales, e-commerce, retail partners)?
    * **Pricing Analysis:** What are our current pricing strategies?  Are they competitive? Do they reflect the value we offer?
    * **Sales Process Review:** Map out the entire customer journey from initial awareness to purchase and ongoing engagement.  Identify bottlenecks and drop-off points.
* **3. Data Collection:**
    * **CRM Data:** Leverage your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.) for sales data, customer information, and marketing campaign performance.
    * **Website Analytics:** Google Analytics to track website traffic, user behavior, and conversion rates.
    * **Marketing Automation Data:** Track email open rates, click-through rates, and lead generation effectiveness.
    * **Customer Feedback:** Surveys, reviews, social media monitoring, and customer service interactions.
* **4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) – Establish Baselines:**
   * **Revenue Growth Rate:** Overall percentage increase.
   * **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**  Cost to acquire a new customer.
   * **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** Predicted revenue a customer will generate during their relationship with us.
   * **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of leads that become customers.
   * **Average Order Value (AOV):** Average amount spent per transaction.
   * **Churn Rate:** Percentage of customers who stop using our product/service.
