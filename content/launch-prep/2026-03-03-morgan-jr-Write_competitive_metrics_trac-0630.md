## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competitors to inform strategic decisions and drive growth. It focuses on key areas, defines specific metrics, and outlines a process for tracking, analyzing, and acting on the data.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To understand the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and ultimately improve our strategic decision-making and performance.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strategies.
    * Track competitor pricing, promotions, and product offerings.
    * Monitor competitor marketing activities and brand messaging.
    * Identify emerging trends and potential disruptions.
    * Benchmark our performance against the competition.


**II. Competitive Identification & Prioritization:**

* **List Competitors:**  Create a comprehensive list of all direct and indirect competitors.
    * **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering similar products/services to the same target audience.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering alternative solutions to the same customer problem.
* **Prioritization Matrix:** Rank competitors based on impact & proximity. Consider:
    * **Market Share:**  Percentage of the market controlled.
    * **Growth Rate:**  Rate of expansion.
    * **Relevance to Target Audience:**  How closely aligned are they with your customers' needs?
    * **Strategic Alignment:**  How closely do their strategies align with or challenge ours?
    * **Resources:** Size of company, funding, team.

   **(Example Matrix - Simplified)**

   | Competitor | Market Share | Growth Rate | Relevance | Strategic Alignment | Priority (High/Medium/Low) |
   |---|---|---|---|---|---|
   | Competitor A | 30% | 15% | High | Low | High |
   | Competitor B | 15% | 25% | Medium | Medium | Medium |
   | Competitor C | 5% | 10% | Low | High | Low |



**III. Key Metrics Tracking:**

This section categorizes metrics by area of focus.  **Focus on the 3-5 most impactful metrics for each competitor, based on your prioritization.**

* **A. Product/Service:**
    * **New Product Launches:** Frequency, features, pricing.
    * **Product Reviews & Ratings:** Track sentiment and identify areas for improvement. (Tools: Trustpilot, G2, Capterra)
    * **Product Features:**  Document new features and their impact.
    * **Innovation Rate:** How quickly do they release updates/new features?
* **B. Pricing & Promotions:**
    * **Pricing Changes:**  Track changes in their pricing structure.
    * **Promotional Offers:**  Analyze discounts, coupons, bundles, and special offers. (Tools: Price Comparison Websites, Social Media Monitoring)
    * **Value Propositions:**  What are they emphasizing in terms of value?
* **C. Marketing & Sales:**
    * **Website Traffic:** Analyze visitor numbers, bounce rate, and source of traffic. (Tools: Google Analytics)
    * **Social Media Activity:** Track follower count, engagement (likes, shares, comments), content themes, and advertising spend. (Tools: Social Media Monitoring Tools – Hootsuite, Sprout Social)
    * **Advertising Campaigns:** Monitor their paid advertising (Google Ads, Social Media Ads) – keyword targeting, ad copy, landing pages.
    * **Content Marketing:** Analyze their blog posts, ebooks, webinars, and other content formats.
    * **SEO Ranking:** Monitor keywords, domain authority, and backlink profiles. (Tools: SEMrush, Ahrefs)
* **D. Customer Experience:**
    * **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) Scores:** Track their publicly available scores
