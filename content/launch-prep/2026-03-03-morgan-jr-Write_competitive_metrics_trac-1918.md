## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to track and analyze your competitors to gain insights for strategic decision-making. It focuses on identifying key metrics, establishing a tracking process, and interpreting the data to inform your business.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Understand the Competitive Landscape:** Identify direct and indirect competitors and their strengths & weaknesses.
* **Identify Opportunities:**  Uncover areas where you can differentiate yourself and gain a competitive advantage.
* **Benchmark Performance:**  Understand how your performance stacks up against the competition.
* **React Strategically:**  Respond effectively to competitor moves and market trends.

**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi)
* **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions that fulfill similar customer needs. (e.g., Starbucks vs. Coffee Shops)
* **Tiered Prioritization:** Rank competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:**  Larger market share warrants more attention.
    * **Growth Rate:**  Rapidly growing competitors require immediate analysis.
    * **Threat Level:** Competitors with aggressive pricing or innovative strategies pose a higher threat.
    * **Relevance to Your Business:**  Focus your efforts on competitors most relevant to your current goals.
