**I. Overall Goals of the Dashboard:**

* **Track Key Launch Success Metrics:**  We need to know if we're acquiring users, engaging them, and driving sales.
* **Identify Immediate Issues:** Quickly spot any problems (low conversion, high churn, etc.) that need immediate attention.
* **Inform Optimization Decisions:** Use data to understand what’s working, what’s not, and adjust marketing and product strategies accordingly.
* **Monitor Trends:** Track the evolution of key metrics over time to understand the long-term health of the launch.

We’ll structure the dashboard into several sections, with a mix of high-level and more granular data.

**A. Top-Level Overview (KPIs - Big Numbers):**

* **Total Downloads/Installs:** (Line Chart - Daily/Weekly) - Shows overall growth.
* **Daily Active Users (DAU):** (Large Number) - Represents the core engaged user base.
* **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** (Large Number) - Gives a broader view of reach.

* **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** (Large Number) -  A measure of monetization success.
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** (Large Number) -  How much are we spending to get a new user? (Calculate based on marketing spend)

    * Referral Programs
    * PR/Media Mentions
