**Synopsis:**

"Deuce Diary" follows the increasingly chaotic life of Arthur Penhaligon, a quiet, unassuming librarian struggling with existential dread and a crippling fear of social interaction. His life takes a spectacularly bizarre turn when he inexplicably develops a second head - and a highly opinionated, endlessly verbose inner voice housed within it, which he refers to as “Deuce.”

Deuce is a cynical, world-weary donkey with an insatiable appetite for gossip, existential debate, and the perfect cup of Earl Grey tea. He offers Arthur brutally honest (and often absurd) commentary on everything from Arthur’s disastrous dating life to the mundane anxieties of everyday existence.  As Arthur tries to navigate his newfound duality, he grapples with questions of identity, purpose, and the overwhelming urge to argue with a talking donkey about the philosophical implications of toast.
