**II. Defining "Competitive"**

* **Identify Key Competitors:**  This isn't just about direct rivals. Consider:
    * **Direct Competitors:** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions that could satisfy the same customer need.
    * **Potential Competitors:**  Companies that *could* enter your market (e.g., adjacent industries, larger corporations with expansion plans).
* **Prioritize Competitors:** Not all competitors are equally important. Rank them based on:
    * **Market Share:** Who holds the biggest slice of the pie?
    * **Growth Rate:** Which competitors are expanding fastest?
    * **Strategic Importance:**  Who poses the biggest threat or offers the most valuable learning opportunities? (Focus on 3-5 key competitors initially)
