**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar - 2024 (Example)**

**I. January - New Year, New App (Focus: Resolution & Self-Improvement)**

* **Theme:** “Start Strong” – Focus on apps that help users achieve New Year’s resolutions (fitness, productivity, learning, mindfulness).
* **Activities:**
    * **Launch Campaign:**  Promote apps with new year’s themed bundles or offers.
    * **Influencer Outreach:** Partner with fitness, productivity, and wellness influencers to promote relevant apps.
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):**  Use relevant keywords like "New Year's Resolutions," "Goal Setting," "Productivity," etc.
    * **Social Media:** Content around goal setting, motivation, and app tutorials.  Run contests encouraging users to share their resolutions.
* **Key Metrics:** App Downloads, Conversion Rate, User Retention (especially early days), App Store Ranking
* **Offer:** Limited-time discounts or free trial periods.


**II. February - Love & Connection (Focus: Relationships & Social)**

* **Theme:** “Connect & Grow” – Highlight apps related to dating, social networking, communication, and personal growth within relationships.
* **Activities:**
    * **Valentine's Day Promotion:**  Offer discounts or themed content for dating apps and relationship-focused apps.
    * **User-Generated Content Campaign:** Encourage users to share how your app helps them connect with others.
    * **ASO:**  Target keywords like “dating app”, “relationship advice”, “social network”.
    * **Social Media:**  Romantic content, tips for connecting, and showcasing app features for building relationships.
* **Key Metrics:** User Engagement (time spent in app),  New User Acquisition, Social Media Reach, Referral Rate
* **Offer:**  Free premium features for a limited time.


**III. March - Spring into Action (Focus: Productivity & Motivation)**

* **Theme:** “Get Organized” –  Focus on apps that help users plan, manage their time, and improve productivity.
* **Activities:**
    * **Spring Cleaning Campaign:**  Promote apps for decluttering, scheduling, and task management.
    * **ASO:** Focus on keywords like “productivity app”, “time management”, “to-do list”.
    * **Content Marketing:** Blog posts and guides on productivity tips and how your app can help.
    * **Social Media:**  Visual content showcasing organized workspaces and productive habits.
* **Key Metrics:**  Trial Sign-Ups,  Feature Usage,  User Growth
* **Offer:** Discounted premium subscriptions.
