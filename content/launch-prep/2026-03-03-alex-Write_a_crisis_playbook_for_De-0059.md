## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

This playbook outlines immediate responses to potential crises during the launch of Deuce Diary, categorized by severity and outlining immediate steps, escalation paths, and long-term mitigation strategies.

**I. Immediate Response (Within 1-4 Hours)**

**A. Server Crash:**

* **Severity:** High - Impacts immediate user access, potentially damaging reputation.
* **Immediate Actions:**
    1. **Confirm & Communicate:** Immediately acknowledge the issue on all platforms – Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Discord, within the app itself (if possible). “We’re aware of a server issue and our team is working hard to resolve it ASAP. We appreciate your patience.”
    2. **Technical Team Activation:** Activate Incident Response Team – Lead Engineer, DevOps, Customer Support Lead.
    3. **Identify Root Cause:** Rapidly diagnose the cause (traffic spike, code issue, infrastructure problem).
    4. **Rollback (if possible):**  If a recent code deployment is suspected, rollback immediately.
    5. **Monitor & Update:** Provide continuous updates – estimated resolution time, progress updates, and reasons for delays. Transparency is key.
    6. **External Communication (if needed):**  Prepare a brief statement for media outlets if the problem is severe and impacting a large user base.


**B. 1-Star Reviews (Significant Volume):**

* **Severity:** Medium - Indicates significant user dissatisfaction.
* **Immediate Actions:**
    1. **Monitor Review Platforms:** Track reviews on App Store, Google Play, Reddit, etc.
    2. **Customer Support Prioritization:** Flag negative reviews for immediate attention.
    3. **Address Specific Concerns:** Respond to individual reviews (even negative ones) with empathy and offer assistance. Acknowledge frustrations.
    4. **Identify Patterns:** Analyze reviews for common themes (e.g., confusing UI, broken features, slow performance).
    5. **Internal Investigation:**  Team leads need to investigate the reported issues.


**C. Apple Rejection:**

* **Severity:** High - Potentially halts app distribution, severely impacting launch momentum.
* **Immediate Actions:**
    1. **Review Apple’s Guidelines:** Immediately understand the specific reason for rejection.
    2. **Contact Apple Developer Support:**  Open a support ticket and provide all requested documentation promptly.
    3. **Correct the Issue:**  Make the necessary changes to the app based on Apple's feedback.
    4. **Resubmit:**  Submit the corrected app for review, including a clear explanation of the changes made.


**II. Short-Term Response (4-24 Hours)**

**A. Competitor Copies:**

* **Severity:** Medium - Can dilute market position and perceived uniqueness.
* **Actions:**
    1. **Verify the Copy:** Determine the extent of the copying (features, marketing, UI).
    2. **Legal Consultation:**  Immediately consult with legal counsel regarding potential IP infringement. Document evidence of copying.
    3. **Highlight Differentiation:**  Reinforce Deuce Diary's unique value proposition in marketing and messaging. Focus on what makes it special.
    4. **Monitor Competitor Activity:** Track their actions closely.


**B. Unexpected Viral Trend (Positive or Negative):**

* **Severity:** Variable - Potentially hugely beneficial or damaging.
* **Actions:**
    1. **Monitor Sentiment:** Track mentions on social media, news outlets, and forums.
    2. **Craft a Response:** Tailor the response to the nature of the trend.
       * **Positive Viral:** Amplify the trend, engage with users, and consider influencer collaborations.
       * **Negative Viral:** Address the issue head-on, take responsibility (if warranted), and strive to shift the narrative.
