## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy focused on optimizing key App Store elements – primarily the title, subtitle, keywords, description, screenshots, and preview video – to improve app installs and conversions.

**1. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs & Conversions (Users clicking through to install)
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve App Store Ranking, Enhance User Engagement (Time in App, Session Length)
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Conversion Rate (ICR):**  Percentage of users who view the app page and then install it. (This is our primary metric)
    * **Keyword Ranking:** Track keyword ranking for relevant terms.
    * **Impressions:** Number of times the app page is shown in search results.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Screenshots & Video:** Percentage of users who view the screenshots and video.
    * **Average Session Length:**  Average duration of user sessions within the app.
    * **Retention Rate (D1, D7, D30):**  Percentage of users who return to the app after 1, 7, and 30 days. (Longer-term impact)

* **Segmentation:**
