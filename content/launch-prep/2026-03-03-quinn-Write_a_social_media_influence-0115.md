**I. DM Templates (Segmented by Niche)**

**A. Health Influencer Template:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up - 3-5 days after finding their profile):** “Hey @[InfluencerName]! 👋 Loving your content on [Specific topic they focus on - e.g., mindful eating, morning routines, yoga]. Really resonates with [mention something specific you liked – e.g., your approach to stress management].”
* **Follow-Up DM (1-2 days after initial):** “Just wanted to say again – your content is fantastic!  We’re [Brand Name], and we’re passionate about [Brand Mission - e.g., supporting sustainable wellness].  Would love to chat briefly about potential collaborations.”
* **Offer DM (after they’ve responded positively):** “Hi @[InfluencerName]! We'd love to propose a collaboration where you could try our [Product Name] and share your honest experience with your audience. We’re open to creative content ideas – would you be interested in discussing a potential partnership?”

**B. Humor Influencer Template:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up):** “Okay, @[InfluencerName], you’re hilarious! 😂 Seriously, your [mention a specific funny post/video] had me in stitches.  Just wanted to say - you're a legend.”
* **Follow-Up DM:** “Just wanted to check in and say we're still laughing at your content! We’re [Brand Name], and we're all about spreading good vibes and a good chuckle. We think your humor would be a great fit for our brand.”
* **Offer DM:** “We're thinking a fun challenge or sponsored post featuring our [Product Name - something lighthearted] would be a blast!  We'd love to collaborate on something that aligns with your brand of comedy.  Interested in chatting?”

**C. Lifestyle Influencer Template:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up):** “Hey @[InfluencerName]!  Your lifestyle posts are seriously inspiring - love the vibe you've created around [mention a specific aspect - e.g., travel, home decor, self-care].”
* **Follow-Up DM:** “Just wanted to connect! We're [Brand Name], and we’re creating [describe what you create - e.g., curated home goods, sustainable fashion, wellness products].  We believe your audience aligns perfectly with our brand values."
* **Offer DM:** “We'd love to send you our [Product Name] to feature in your next post/story. We’re open to ideas - a styling session, a product review, or even showcasing it in your everyday life.”
