**III. Tab Breakdown with Metrics & Visualizations:**

**1. Overview/Summary (High-Level Snapshot)**

*   **Headline Metrics (Large, Prominent Numbers):**
    *   **Total Users:** Total number of accounts created.
    *   **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Crucial for understanding daily engagement.
    *   **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** Provides a broader view of the user base.
    *   **Retention Rate (7-Day, 30-Day):**  Key indicator of user stickiness.  (Percentage of users who return after 7 or 30 days).
*   **Trend Charts:**
    *   DAU & MAU over Time (Line chart – last 30-60 days) - Helps identify growth patterns, spikes, and dips.
    *   Retention Rate Over Time (Line chart) – See how retention improves or declines.
*   **Quick Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    *   New Users (Daily/Weekly) –  Number of new accounts created.
    *   Average Diary Entries Per User – Shows engagement with the core feature.

**3. Engagement**

*   **Daily/Weekly/Monthly Diary Entry Volume:** (Line Chart) –  Tracks the frequency of users writing in their diaries.  This is *the* core engagement metric.
