Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different purpose, with estimated timing and content. I've aimed for a balance between engagement and gentle persuasion.  Remember to adapt these to your specific brand voice, target audience, and product/service.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscribers - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Build a relationship, introduce your brand, and start establishing trust.
* **Target:** New subscribers who opted in via your website, social media, or lead magnet.
* **Timing:**  Automated, triggered immediately upon signup.
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 1):  The Warm Welcome**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:**  Thank you for subscribing! Briefly reiterate their reason for signing up (e.g., "You’re getting access to our exclusive guide...").  Introduce your brand's mission and values in a friendly, approachable way. Include a visually appealing brand image. Link to your "About Us" page.
    * **Email 2 (Day 3):  Highlighting Top 3 Benefits**
        * **Subject:**  Here's What You'll Get With [Your Brand Name]
        * **Content:** Focus on 3 key benefits your product/service provides. Use bullet points and keep it concise.  Include a short testimonial or case study snippet (if available).  Link to a relevant page on your website (e.g., a product page or blog post).
    * **Email 3 (Day 5 or 7):  A Quick Question & Resource**
        * **Subject:**  Tell Us – What Are You Hoping to Achieve?
        * **Content:**  A simple question to encourage engagement (e.g., "What are your biggest challenges with [related topic]?"). Offer a valuable free resource – a checklist, infographic, short ebook – that addresses their potential pain points.  Link to download.


**Campaign 2:  Product/Service Feature Spotlight (Existing Subscribers – 14-21 Days After Initial Contact)**

* **Goal:** Drive awareness of a specific product feature or benefit, and encourage a purchase.
* **Target:** Subscribers who have engaged with previous emails (clicked links, opened emails) or have been on your website.
* **Timing:** Triggered by website activity, email opens, or clicks.
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 14-21): Introducing the Feature**
        * **Subject:**  Unlock [Benefit] with [Product Name]'s [Feature]
        * **Content:** Introduce a specific feature of your product/service.  Explain *what* it does and, more importantly, *how* it benefits the user.  Use an image or short video demo.  Link directly to the relevant product page.
    * **Email 2 (Day 17-21):  Real-World Example**
        * **Subject:**  See How [Customer Name/Type] Uses [Feature]
        * **Content:**  Provide a case study, customer testimonial, or example of how someone else is successfully using the feature.  Focus on the *results* they achieved.  Link to the full case study.
    * **Email 3 (Day 21):  Limited-Time Offer**
        * **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out! [Discount/Bonus] for [Product Name]
        * **Content:**  Create a sense of urgency with a limited-time offer related to the featured product. Clearly state the offer and the deadline.  Make it easy to purchase – link directly to the product page.
