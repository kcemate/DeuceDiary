## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a strategy for tracking and analyzing your competition to inform your business strategy and improve your performance. It focuses on identifying key metrics, establishing a consistent tracking process, and leveraging the insights for action.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand your competitive landscape to identify opportunities for growth, differentiation, and strategic advantage.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Track changes in competitor offerings, pricing, and marketing activities.
    * Benchmark your performance against the competition.
    * Uncover emerging trends in the industry.
    * Inform product development, marketing strategy, and pricing decisions.

**2. Identifying Key Competitors:**

* **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering similar products or services to the same target audience.
* **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering alternative solutions that address the same customer need.
* **Potential Competitors:** New entrants or businesses expanding into your market.
* **List Creation:** Create a detailed spreadsheet listing your top 5-10 competitors, categorized by direct, indirect, and potential.  Include their website URLs.

**4. Data Collection & Tracking Process:**

* **Dedicated Team/Person:** Assign responsibility for tracking competitor activities – ideally a marketing analyst or research specialist.
* **Tools:**
    * **Website Tracking:** SimilarWeb, SEMrush, Ahrefs (for website traffic, SEO, and marketing data).
    * **Social Media Monitoring:** Hootsuite, Sprout Social (for tracking mentions, engagement, and sentiment).
