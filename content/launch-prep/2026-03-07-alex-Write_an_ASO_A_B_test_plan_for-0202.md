## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for key App Store elements, aiming to optimize for installs, engagement, and ultimately, revenue.

**1. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase app installs by X% within Y months.
* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Increase app store page conversion rate (CVR) by Z%.
    * Improve average session length by W%.
    * Boost app rating by V stars.
* **Key Metrics to Track:**
    * **Impressions:** How many times the app is shown.
    * **Clicks:** Number of clicks on the app store page.
    * **Installs:** Number of successful installations.
    * **Conversion Rate (CVR):**  Clicks/Impressions.
    * **Retention Rate:** Daily/Weekly/Monthly Retention.
    * **Session Length:** Average time users spend in the app.
    * **App Rating:** Average star rating.

**2. Elements to Test (Prioritized):**

We’ll focus on high-impact, relatively easy-to-test elements first:

* **1. App Title (High Priority - 30-40% of Tests)**: Testing different title lengths and keyword combinations.
* **2. Subtitle (High Priority - 20-30% of Tests)**: Variations focusing on key benefits and unique selling points.
* **3. Keywords (Medium Priority - 10-20% of Tests)**: Experimenting with different combinations targeting relevant search terms.
* **4. Icon (Medium Priority - 10-20% of Tests)**: Slight variations in color, shape, or visual elements.
* **5. Screenshots/Video (Low Priority - 5-10% of Tests)**: Testing different layouts, highlights, and calls to action.



**3. A/B Test Structure:**

* **Test Duration:** 2-4 weeks per test, depending on traffic and expected impact.  Shorter tests (1 week) are suitable for quick checks.
* **Test Size (Sample Size):**  We’ll use a sample size calculator to determine the required number of installs for statistically significant results. A minimum of 50-100 installs per variation is generally recommended, but this will depend on your app's download volume.  
* **Variation Strategy:**
    * **Control (A):** Our current App Store listing (baseline).
    * **Variation (B):**  Changes to one element at a time (e.g., a new title, subtitle, or keyword combination).
* **Randomization:** The app store algorithm will randomly show the variations to users.
* **Segmentation:**  Consider segmenting tests based on:
    * **Device Type:** iOS vs. Android
    * **User Demographics:** (If available and ethically appropriate) - Age, location, etc. (Be mindful of privacy regulations).
    * **App Store Region:** Different languages and user behaviors in different regions.
