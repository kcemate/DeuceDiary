## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Vision:** To cultivate a thriving, passionate, and supportive community around the *Deuce Diary* universe, fostering deep engagement with the lore, characters, and overall creative vision of the project.

**Overall Tone:** Respectful, Creative, Enthusiastic, Focused on Appreciation & Discussion – leaning into the diary’s introspective and potentially melancholic feel.

**I. Platforms & Focus:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** This will be the core of the community. It provides real-time interaction, voice channels, and dedicated spaces for different discussions.
    * **Channels:**
        * #general: General Deuce & related discussions.
        * #lore-discussion: Deep dives into the world, timelines, and story elements.
        * #character-analysis: Focused conversations on specific characters and their motivations.
        * #art-and-fanworks: Dedicated space for sharing and discussing fan art, writing, and other creative works. (With clear tagging system)
        * #voice-chat: Regular voice chats for events, brainstorming, and informal conversation.
        * #suggestions-and-feedback: A dedicated channel for constructive feedback and ideas for the project itself.
        * #moderation-chat: Private channel for moderators to communicate and address issues.
* **Reddit (Secondary - Amplification & Discovery):**  Utilized for broader reach, sharing highlights, and promoting Discord.
    * **Subreddit:** /r/DeuceDiary – A hub for longer-form discussions, image sharing, and news about the project.
    * **Focus:**  Reddit should drive traffic to Discord and act as a place for showcasing the best content from the community.


**II. Content Strategy:**

* **Creator-Led (Priority):**  The creator(s) will regularly participate in both Discord and Reddit, fostering conversations, answering questions, and sharing insights.
* **Community-Generated Content:** Encourage and highlight fan content.
    * **Weekly Themes:** Introduce weekly themes (“Character Week,” “Lore Dive,” “Art Challenge”) to inspire content creation and engagement.
    * **"Diary Entry of the Week":**  Feature a particularly insightful or engaging fan interpretation of a diary entry.
    * **Fan Fiction Challenges:**  Present prompts for short fan fiction pieces.
    * **Art Challenges:**  Weekly or monthly art prompts related to the diary.
* **Curated Content:**
    * **Lore Deep Dives:** Regularly share curated information and analysis from the creator(s) on key lore elements.
    * **Behind-the-Scenes Updates:** (When available) Share snippets of development progress, inspirations, or creative decisions.


**III. Moderator Guidelines:**

* **Role:** Moderators are responsible for maintaining a positive and productive community environment, enforcing rules, and assisting members.
* **Training:** Mandatory training on community guidelines, conflict resolution, and Discord moderation tools.
* **Key Guidelines:**
    * **Respectful Communication:** No personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
    * **Rule Enforcement:** Consistent and fair application of community guidelines.
    * **Proactive Engagement:**  Moderators actively participate in conversations, welcome new members, and encourage positive interactions.
    * **Conflict Resolution:**  Mediate disputes calmly and constructively, guiding members toward solutions.
    * **Transparency:**  Clearly communicate moderation decisions and rationale.
* **Team Size:** Start with a small, dedicated team and expand as needed.

**IV. Engagement Tactics:**

* **Welcome System:**  Automated welcome message for new Discord members, directing them to key channels and resources. Manual welcome messages from moderators.
* **Icebreaker Events:** Regular “introduction” channels or dedicated voice chats to help members get to know each other.
