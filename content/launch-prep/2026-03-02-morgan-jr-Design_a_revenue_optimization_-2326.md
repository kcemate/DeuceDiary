**II. Paywall Strategy – When & How**

    * **High Engagement Users:** (Spending > X minutes/week, accessing > Y number of features, consuming > Z content pieces):  Immediately present a paywall offer (e.g., "Unlock Premium for Deeper Insights").
    * **Content "Starters":** (Users interacting with specific high-value content – top articles, courses, templates): Offer a limited-time trial or a discounted introductory rate.
    * **Exit Intent:**  When a user is about to leave the site (detected by mouse movement, form abandonment), trigger a reminder of the value they’re missing and a limited-time offer.
    * **Segmented Paywalls:**  Different paywall messaging and offers based on user segment (e.g., students vs. professionals).
* **Paywall Types:**
    * **Persistent Paywall:**  Subtle, always-on reminder of premium features. (Use sparingly - can be intrusive).
    * **Contextual Paywall:**  Displayed when a user tries to access content restricted to subscribers (e.g., full-length courses).
    * **Pop-Up Paywall:** Triggered by specific actions or based on time spent on the site. (Use very cautiously - can be annoying).
