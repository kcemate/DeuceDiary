Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, designed to build momentum, gather feedback, and engage your audience. This calendar is a template - you’ll need to tailor it specifically to your product/service, target audience, and marketing goals.

**Assumptions:**

*   **You’ve already launched:** This calendar starts *after* the launch date.
*   **You have a basic social media presence:**  We'll focus on content that expands on what you've already established.
*   **You’re aiming for engagement & feedback:**  This month is about nurturing relationships.


**Key Themes for Month 1:**

*   **Celebration & Thank You:** Acknowledge the launch and thank your early adopters.
*   **Education & Value:**  Showcase the key benefits and how to use your product/service.
*   **Community Building:** Encourage interaction and start a conversation.
*   **Gathering Feedback:** Actively solicit opinions and suggestions.

**Content Calendar – Month 1 Post-Launch**

**(Note: This is a weekly breakdown. Adjust frequencies based on your platform and analytics.)**

**Week 1:  Initial Excitement & Welcome**

*   **Monday:** (Social Media - All Platforms) - **Launch Announcement Recap:** "We did it! 🎉 [Product/Service Name] is officially here!  Thank you to everyone who’s already checking it out. Here's a quick reminder of what it does…" (Include a short video or GIF). #launchday #newproduct #[yourindustry]
*   **Tuesday:** (Blog/Website) - **"Welcome to the [Product/Service Name] Family!"** - A warm welcome post explaining the vision behind the product, your team, and what you’re hoping to achieve.
*   **Wednesday:** (Social Media - Instagram/TikTok) - **"How to Get Started" - Short Tutorial:**  A super-quick, visually appealing video (15-60 seconds) demonstrating the very first step.  "30 Seconds to Success!" #tutorial #getstarted #howto
*   **Thursday:** (Social Media - Twitter/LinkedIn) - **Poll:** "What's the biggest challenge you were facing before using [Product/Service Name]?" (Options related to your product's core problem).
*   **Friday:** (Social Media - All Platforms) – **User Spotlight (if available)** – Share a positive comment or early testimonial from a user.  "We love hearing from our early users!  [Quote & User Handle]" #testimonial #customersuccess
*   **Weekend:** (Social Media - Organic) -  Relaxed content - Behind-the-scenes image/video, asking users how they're using the product.



**Week 2: Deep Dive & Feature Highlights**

*   **Monday:** (Blog/Website) – **Feature Spotlight 1:**  A detailed post focusing on *one* key feature and its benefits.  Include screenshots/GIFs.
*   **Tuesday:** (Social Media - LinkedIn) –  **Article Link:** Share a relevant industry article and connect it to how your product addresses that trend.
*   **Wednesday:** (Social Media - Instagram/Facebook) – **"Feature Friday"**:  Highlight a different feature with a short video demo. Use Instagram Reels for engaging demos.
*   **Thursday:** (Social Media – Twitter) - **Q&A Session:** Announce a short Q&A session (live or scheduled) where you answer user questions.
*   **Friday:** (Email Marketing) - **Newsletter:** Send an email to your subscriber list highlighting the week's content and offering a special discount or bonus for early adopters.
