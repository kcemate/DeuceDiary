## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to effectively track and analyze competitive performance, providing insights for strategic decision-making and competitive advantage.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To continuously monitor and understand the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and inform our strategic decisions to maintain or improve our market position.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths & weaknesses.
    * Track competitor activity (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns, etc.).
    * Analyze competitor performance across key metrics (e.g., market share, customer satisfaction, brand perception).
    *  Identify emerging trends and disruptive technologies.
    *  Validate our own strategies and identify areas for improvement.
