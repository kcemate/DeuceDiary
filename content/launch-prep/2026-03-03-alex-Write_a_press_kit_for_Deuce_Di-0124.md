**A Note on Deuce Diary:** Deuce Diary is a rapidly growing subscription box service dedicated to fostering creativity and mindfulness for young adults (18-25) through curated art supplies, prompts, and journaling exercises. We're building a community of makers and thinkers, one diary at a time.

**1. Executive Summary:**

Deuce Diary offers a monthly subscription box designed to combat creative block and boost mental wellbeing. Each box contains high-quality art supplies, themed journaling prompts, and inspirational content, providing a dedicated space for self-expression and reflection.  We’re tapping into the growing demand for mindful activities and creative outlets among young adults, focusing on authenticity, community, and a beautiful, tactile experience.


**2. Founder Bio - Elias Vance**

Elias Vance is the founder and creative director of Deuce Diary.  Driven by a lifelong passion for art and a deep interest in mental wellness, Elias developed Deuce Diary out of a personal need to reconnect with his creativity and manage stress.  Prior to launching Deuce Diary, Elias worked as a freelance graphic designer and spent several years exploring mindfulness practices.  He’s committed to building a brand that not only provides beautiful tools but also fosters a supportive community.  Elias holds a Bachelor’s Degree in Fine Arts from the University of California, Berkeley.
