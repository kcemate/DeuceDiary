Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different goal and with varying lengths. I’ve included subject lines, email content, and estimated timing.  Remember to tailor these to *your* specific brand, audience, and product/service.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscriber - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and set expectations.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who signed up via a website form, landing page, or social media.
* **Tone:** Friendly, informative, and enthusiastic.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 1): Welcome & Thank You!**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! Let’s Get Started.
    * **Content:**
        * Warm greeting and thank you for subscribing.
        * Briefly introduce your brand’s mission and what you offer. (1-2 sentences)
        * Link to your website's "About Us" page for more details.
        * Include a friendly call to action – "Explore our website!"
* **Email 2 (Day 3):  Problem/Solution - Briefly Highlight Pain Point**
    * **Subject:**  Struggling with [Relevant Pain Point]?
    * **Content:**  Identify a common problem your target audience faces. Briefly describe how your product/service addresses it. (Keep it concise – 2-3 sentences). Offer a helpful resource like a blog post or free guide related to the pain point.
* **Email 3 (Day 5 or 7):  Introduce a Key Feature/Benefit**
    * **Subject:** Unlock [Benefit] with [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Focus on a single, compelling feature or benefit of your offering.  Use an image or short video if possible.  Include a clear call to action – “Learn More” or “See it in Action”.


**Campaign 2:  Lead Nurturing - Product Demo/Case Study (5-10 Days)**

* **Goal:**  Move leads further down the funnel by providing more compelling reasons to purchase.
* **Target Audience:** Subscribers who have engaged with your content (e.g., downloaded a lead magnet, visited specific product pages).
* **Tone:** More detailed, persuasive, and focused on value.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 3):  Follow-Up on Lead Magnet/Interaction**
    * **Subject:**  Loved the [Lead Magnet Name]? Here’s What’s Next.
    * **Content:**  Refer back to the lead magnet they downloaded. Offer a deeper dive into a related topic or a relevant question.  “Did you know [Insight]?”
* **Email 2 (Day 6): Case Study/Testimonial**
    * **Subject:**  How [Client Name] Achieved [Result] with [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Present a short case study or testimonial highlighting a successful customer story.  Quantify the results whenever possible. (Use a screenshot or short video)
