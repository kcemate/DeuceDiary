**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. January - New Year, New Goals (Focus: Resolution Apps & New Year’s Themes)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Health & Wellness
* **Key Dates:** January 1st, Martin Luther King Jr. Day (US), Super Bowl
* **Campaign Activities:**
    * **Resolution App Push:**  Promote apps related to fitness, habit tracking, mindfulness, and financial planning.
    * **“New Year, New You” Promotions:** Highlight the app’s ability to help users achieve goals. Offer introductory discounts.
    * **Social Media:**  Content focused on motivation, goal-setting tips, and user success stories. Run contests related to New Year’s resolutions.
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with wellness and productivity influencers.
    * **ASO:** Target keywords related to “new year,” “goals,” “habits,” etc.


**II. February - Love & Connection (Focus: Relationship & Social Apps)**

* **Theme:** Romance, Connection, Socializing
* **Key Dates:** Valentine’s Day
* **Campaign Activities:**
    * **Relationship Apps Promotion:**  Promote dating apps, relationship advice apps, and communication apps.
    * **Valentine’s Day Specials:**  Offer themed discounts, in-app gifts, or bundles.
    * **Social Media:**  Content centered around love, friendship, and connection. Run contests or polls.
    * **Email Marketing:** Send personalized Valentine’s Day messages to users (if you have their consent).
    * **ASO:** Target keywords like “Valentine’s Day,” “dating,” “relationships,” “love.”


**III. March – Spring into Action (Focus: Productivity & Utility Apps)**

* **Theme:** Renewal, Productivity, Planning
* **Key Dates:** International Women's Day, St. Patrick’s Day, March Madness
* **Campaign Activities:**
    * **Productivity App Spotlight:**  Promote apps for task management, calendar scheduling, note-taking, and time management.
    * **March Madness Promotions:** (If relevant) Leverage the excitement of March Madness with contests or discounts.
    * **Social Media:**  Focus on productivity tips, organizational strategies, and springtime inspiration.
    * **ASO:** Keywords around "productivity," "organization," "task management," "planning," "time management."
