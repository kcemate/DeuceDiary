**Overall Goal:**  Grab attention, communicate the core themes (vampires, romance, dark humor), and entice users to learn more.

**1. Colors:**

* **Dominant:** Deep Crimson (#8B0000) – This is the absolute key color. It evokes blood, danger, and passion – essential for a vampire story.
* **Secondary:** Charcoal Gray (#36454F) – Provides contrast, adds a touch of gothic elegance, and balances the intensity of the red.
* **Accent:** Pale Gold (#D4AF37) – Sparingly used for highlights and brand elements. Represents wealth, immortality, and maybe a touch of irony. 
* **Background:** Mostly Dark Gray (#222222) – Keeps the graphic from feeling too cluttered and allows the red and gold to pop.


**2. Layout:**

* **Central Image:**  A strikingly alluring but subtly unsettling image of a young woman (around 25-30) with dark hair and captivating eyes.  She's *not* traditionally beautiful; her expression is a mix of vulnerability and awareness, a hint of danger. The image is slightly out of focus/blurry to create an ethereal effect.  She’s dressed in a flowing, dark velvet gown with a subtle gold trim. The image is roughly 60% of the graphic space.
* **Layered Text & Elements:**  The text and brand elements are strategically layered *behind* the central image, partially obscured. This creates a sense of mystery and intrigue, as if the reader is just glimpsing a dangerous secret.
* **Composition:** We'll use a slight diagonal composition, guiding the eye down and across the graphic.
