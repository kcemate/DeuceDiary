**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. January – New Year, New Goals (Focus: Motivation, Productivity, Wellness)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Resolutions, Setting Goals
* **Activities:**
    * **Launch Promotions:** Introduce a “New Year, New You” bundle or in-app offer.
    * **Content:** Blog posts/videos about setting achievable goals, productivity tips, and starting a healthy habit.
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):**  Keywords related to goal setting, productivity, and new year resolutions.
    * **Paid Advertising:** Target users interested in self-improvement, habit tracking, and productivity tools. 
* **Example App Categories:** Productivity, Habit Trackers, Fitness, Meditation, Language Learning

**II. February – Love & Connection (Focus: Relationship Building, Romance)**

* **Theme:** Valentine's Day, Connection, Self-Love
* **Activities:**
    * **Promotional Offers:**  “Share the Love” discount, free premium trial for a limited time. 
    * **Content:**  Content focused on relationships (dating tips, communication strategies), self-care, and expressing love.
    * **App Store Screenshots/Videos:** Highlight features that support connection –  e.g., video calls, message features, social sharing.
* **Example App Categories:** Dating, Relationship Advice, Social Networking, Communication Apps


**III. March – Spring into Action (Focus: Fitness, Health, New Experiences)**

* **Theme:** Spring Renewal, Health & Wellness, Outdoor Activities
* **Activities:**
    * **Launch Promotions:**  “Spring Reset” offer (discounted subscriptions, new features)
    * **Content:**  Blog posts/videos about healthy recipes, workout routines, outdoor activities, and seasonal wellness tips.
    * **ASO:** Keywords related to fitness, health, running, outdoor activities.
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with fitness influencers or health bloggers.
* **Example App Categories:** Fitness, Health & Wellness, Running, Nutrition, Outdoor Activity Apps


**IV. April – Earth Month & Easter (Focus: Sustainability, Family, Fun)**

* **Theme:** Earth Day, Family, Easter Celebrations
* **Activities:**
    * **Earth Day Promotion:**  Highlight app features that support sustainability (e.g., eco-friendly tracking, donations, resourcefulness). 
    * **Easter Promotion:**  Consider a family-friendly promotion.
    * **Content:** Content related to environmental awareness, family activities, and Easter traditions.
* **Example App Categories:** Environmental Apps, Family Apps, Games, Education Apps


**V. May – Mental Health Awareness Month (Focus: Wellbeing, Mindfulness)**

* **Theme:** Mental Wellness, Stress Reduction, Mindfulness
* **Activities:**
    * **Promotional Offers:**  Offer free trials of premium features or access to guided meditations.
    * **Content:**  Focus on mental health, mindfulness, stress
