## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements, aiming to improve install rates, engagement, and ultimately, app revenue.

**1. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs
* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Improve App Store Conversion Rate (Impressions to Installs)
    * Increase Session Length
    * Improve Retention Rates (7-day, 30-day)
    * Drive Revenue Growth (where applicable)
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Rate:** Percentage of users who install the app after viewing the app page. (This is our core metric)
    * **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of impressions that lead to an install.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Screenshots/Video:** Percentage of users who click on screenshots or the app's video.
    * **Average Session Length:** Duration of time users spend within the app.
    * **Retention Rate (7-day & 30-day):** Percentage of users who return to the app after 7 and 30 days.
    * **App Store Rating & Reviews:** Monitor changes in overall rating and sentiment analysis.

We’ll focus on testing elements that directly impact user perception and intent.
