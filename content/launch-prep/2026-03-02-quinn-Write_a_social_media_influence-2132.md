**I. DM Templates (Tailored by Category)**

**General Opening (Use for all categories - adapt to their tone)**

**A. Health Influencers**

* **After General Opening:** "We're a [Your Brand - brief description - e.g., healthy snack brand, fitness apparel company] focused on [mention your brand’s core value – e.g., fueling your workouts, mindful eating]. We think your audience would really appreciate [mention specific product/benefit]."

* **Example DM (Short & Sweet):** “Hey [Influencer Name], loved your tips on healthy breakfast! We’re a snack brand focused on energy for workouts. Would you be interested in a quick chat?”

**B. Humor Influencers**

* **After General Opening:** “Your sense of humor is *amazing*! Seriously, your [mention a specific funny post] had us in stitches. 😂”
* **Connect to Brand:** “We're a [Your Brand - description] and we're always looking for a bit of laughter. We think your audience would appreciate our [mention specific product/benefit - e.g., quirky stickers, funny merchandise].”
* **Call to Action:** “Would you be up for a virtual laugh and a potential collab? 🤣”
* **Example DM (Playful):** “Hey [Influencer Name], your memes are LEGENDARY. We make funny stickers that are guaranteed to make people smile. Wanna chat?”

**C. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **After General Opening:** “Your lifestyle content is so inspiring!  I especially loved your post about [mention a specific post - e.g., weekend adventure, home decor tip, self-care routine].”
* **Highlight Shared Values:** “We’re [Your Brand] and we're passionate about [mention your brand's core value - e.g., sustainable living, creating beautiful homes, mindful travel] – which I know is important to you too.”
* **Call to Action:** “Would you be open to discussing a partnership that aligns with your brand?”
* **Example DM (Authentic):** “Hey [Influencer Name], your travel pics are gorgeous! We create eco-friendly travel accessories. Would you be interested in partnering to promote responsible travel?”
