Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different goal and with varying lengths and levels of personalization. I've included estimated send times and content ideas. Remember to tailor these to your specific brand voice, audience, and product/service!

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscriber - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Build a relationship, introduce your brand, and set expectations.
* **Audience:** New email subscribers who signed up through a website form, lead magnet download, or contest entry.
* **Sequence Length:** 5 Emails
* **Tone:** Friendly, informative, welcoming

**Email 1: Immediate – “Welcome to [Your Brand]!” (Day 0)**
* **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to the [Your Brand] Family!
* **Content:** Warm welcome, thank them for subscribing, briefly state what they can expect from your emails (e.g., tips, exclusive offers, updates), and include a captivating image related to your brand.
* **Call to Action:** Click to learn more about us [link to your “About Us” page].

**Email 2:  Value-Driven – “[Problem Your Brand Solves] – You’re Not Alone!” (Day 3)**
* **Subject:** Struggling with [Specific Pain Point]?
* **Content:**  Address a key problem your target audience faces.  Share a short, relatable story or statistic. Position your brand as a solution.
* **Call to Action:** Read our top blog post about [related topic] [link to blog post].

**Email 3: Product/Service Highlight (Day 5)**
* **Subject:** Introducing [Your Best-Selling Product/Service]
* **Content:** Showcase one of your core offerings with a compelling image and brief description.  Focus on the *benefits*, not just the features.
* **Call to Action:** Check out [product/service] here [link to product/service page].

**Email 4: Social Proof (Day 6)**
* **Subject:** What Our Customers Are Saying…
* **Content:**  Include a short testimonial or review from a satisfied customer.  Ideally, include a customer photo.
* **Call to Action:** See more testimonials [link to your testimonials page].

**Email 5:  Offer & Reminder (Day 7)**
* **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out!  [Small Discount/Freebie]
* **Content:**  Reinforce the value of your brand and offer a small incentive to encourage engagement (e.g., 10% off, free guide download).
* **Call to Action:** Claim your [offer] now [link to offer page].
