**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. January - New Year, New Resolutions (Focus: Habit Building, Productivity)**

* **Theme:** New Year, New You - Focus on how your app helps users achieve goals, improve routines, or boost productivity.
* **Dates:** January 1st – January 31st
* **Activities:**
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Update keywords based on New Year’s resolution trends (e.g., "fitness," "meditation," "productivity").
    * **Social Media Campaigns:**  Run contests encouraging users to share their goals and how your app helps them. “#NewYearNewApp”
    * **Blog Posts/Articles:** "5 Ways [App Name] Can Help You Stick to Your Resolutions," “Productivity Tips for a Fresh Start.”
    * **Paid Campaigns:** Targeted ads focusing on productivity and self-improvement apps.

**II. February – Love & Connection (Focus: Relationship Apps, Wellness)**

* **Theme:** Love & Connection - Highlight apps related to relationships, dating, wellness, and self-care.
* **Dates:** February 1st – February 28th
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO:** Adjust keywords to include Valentine’s Day related terms.
    * **Social Media:**  Run promotions targeting couples and individuals.  "Share a sweet moment with [App Name]."
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relationship or wellness influencers.
    * **Email Marketing:**  Target existing users with content related to connection & wellbeing.
    * **Paid Campaigns:**  Promote apps focused on dating, connection, and self-care.


**III. March – Spring into Action (Focus: Fitness, Travel, Productive Living)**

* **Theme:** Spring Renewal – Emphasize fresh starts, outdoor activities, and planning for the warmer months.
* **Dates:** March 1st – March 31st
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO:** Update keywords to reflect spring-related terms (e.g., “hiking,” “travel,” “gardening”).
    * **Social Media:**  Run contests around outdoor activities and travel. #SpringIntoAction
    * **Content Marketing:** Create guides on spring-related topics relevant to your app (e.g., "Best Hiking Trails Near You" - for a hiking app).
    * **Paid Campaigns:** Promote apps related to travel, fitness, and outdoor activities.

**IV. April – Earth Month (Focus: Sustainability, Wellness, Social Impact)**

* **Theme:**  Sustainable Living & Wellness –  Connect your app to themes of environmental responsibility and wellbeing.
* **Dates:** April 1st – April 30th
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO:**  Include keywords related to sustainability, eco-friendly practices, and wellness.
    * **Social Media:** Partner with environmental organizations or influencers.  Highlight how your app promotes sustainable habits.
    * **App Updates:** Release updates that align with Earth Month themes (e.g., carbon footprint tracking, sustainable product recommendations).
