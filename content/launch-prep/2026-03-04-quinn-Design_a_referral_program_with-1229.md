Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here’s a detailed plan, broken down into sections to cover all the important aspects:

**1. Program Name & Theme:**

* **Name:** "Spread the [Your Brand] Love" or "[Your Brand] Rewards" or "[Your Brand] Connect" – Keep it friendly and positive.
* **Theme:**  (This helps with marketing and messaging) – Consider a theme that aligns with your brand.  Examples: "Unlock Exclusive Perks," "Grow Together," "Share the Success."


**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Define Your Ideal Referral:**  Who are you trying to attract with this program? (e.g., new customers, loyal customers, specific demographics).
* **Program Goals:** What do you want to achieve? (e.g., increase new customer acquisition, boost brand awareness, grow your customer base by X% within Y months).

**3. Referral Structure & Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The person who refers a new customer.
* **New Customer:** The person who signs up/purchases after being referred.
* **Tiered System (Recommended - adds complexity and excitement):**
    * **Tier 1 (Basic):** Simple referral - Referrer gets a small reward.
    * **Tier 2 (Advanced):**  Requires the referred customer to make a purchase or meet a minimum spending threshold.
    * **Tier 3 (Elite - Optional):** For high-performing referrers (e.g., referring multiple customers).

**4. Incentives - What's in it for the Referrer & New Customer?**

This is the heart of the program. Offer a mix of incentives that appeal to your target audience:

**For the Referrer:**

* **Tier 1 (Basic):**
    * **Discount Code:** 10-20% off their next purchase. (Most common and cost-effective)
    * **Small Store Credit:** $5 - $10 credit towards future purchases.
    * **Points Reward:** Earn points for each successful referral. Points can be redeemed for discounts or other rewards.
* **Tier 2 (Advanced):**
    * **Higher Percentage Discount:** 20-30% off.
    * **Gift Card:**  $25 - $50 gift card to your brand or a partner retailer.
    * **Exclusive Product Access:** Early access to new products or limited-edition items.
* **Tier 3 (Elite - Optional):**
    * **Significant Gift Card:** $100+
    * **VIP Status:**  Priority customer support, exclusive events, or extended warranty.
    * **Commission (If applicable and feasible):**  A small percentage of the new customer's purchases.


**For the New Customer (Referral):**

* **Discount Code:**  10-20% off their first order. (Very popular and encourages immediate purchase)
* **Welcome Gift:** Small freebie or sample related to your brand.
* **Free Shipping:** For a certain period after signing up.
* **Loyalty Points Bonus:** Immediately adds loyalty points to their account.
