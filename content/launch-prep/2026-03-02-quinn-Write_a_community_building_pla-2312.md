## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy to build a thriving and engaged community around Deuce Diary, leveraging Discord and Reddit as primary platforms. It focuses on fostering a welcoming, creative, and supportive environment centered around the book’s themes of identity, self-discovery, and artistic expression.

**I. Core Values & Brand Identity (Foundation)**

* **Core Values:**
    * **Creativity:** Celebrating artistic expression in all forms.
    * **Vulnerability & Support:** Fostering a safe space for sharing personal experiences and seeking support.
    * **Respect & Kindness:** Maintaining a positive and inclusive environment for all members.
    * **Exploration & Discovery:**  Encouraging exploration of self and ideas related to the book’s themes.
* **Brand Tone:**  Reflective, introspective, hopeful, creative, and genuine. Avoid overly promotional or forceful messaging.


**II. Platform Strategy**

* **Discord (Primary Hub)**
    * **Purpose:** Real-time discussions, creative prompts, deep-dive conversations, and a closer-knit community.
    * **Channels:**
        * **#welcome:** New member onboarding & announcements.
        * **#general:** Casual discussions about the book, art, and life.
        * **#deuce-discussions:** Dedicated to analyzing specific scenes, characters, and themes.
        * **#creative-corner:** Sharing fan art, writing, music, poetry - anything inspired by the diary. (Categorized further by medium)
        * **#prompt-palooza:** Daily/weekly creative prompts.
        * **#community-resources:** Links to fan theories, character wikis, external resources.
        * **#off-topic:**  Lighthearted conversation and connection building.
    * **Role Management:**  Clearly defined roles for Moderators and Key Contributors.
* **Reddit (Content Discovery & Broader Reach)**
    * **Purpose:**  Driving traffic to Discord, hosting Q&As, sharing longer-form content, and engaging with a wider audience.
    * **Subreddit:** r/DeuceDiary (Ideally, establish a dedicated subreddit)
    * **Content Strategy:**
        * **Daily/Weekly Discussions:**  Similar to Discord – focusing on specific chapters, characters, and themes.
        * **Image/Video Posts:** Fan art, character analyses, mood boards, etc.
        * **Creative Prompts:**  Drive user-generated content.
        * **AMA (Ask Me Anything):**  Potentially involve the author for Q&A sessions.


**III. Content Strategy**

* **Initial Content (First 3 Months):**
    * **Author Involvement:** The author should consistently participate on both platforms – responding to comments, running Q&As, and seeding discussions.
    * **Prompt-Based Content:** A heavy focus on creative prompts to kickstart the community.
    * **Character Analysis Posts:**  Deep dives into each character’s motivations and development.
    * **Thematic Exploration:**  Discussing the overarching themes of identity, family, and artistic expression.
    * **Visual Content:** Share stills from the book (with permissions), mood boards, and encourage fan art.
* **Long-Term Content:**
    * **Community-Generated Content Spotlight:** Regularly feature the best fan creations.
    * **Themed Weeks/Months:**  Focus on specific aspects of the book (e.g., “Character of the Week,” “Artistic Inspiration Month”).
    * **Creative Challenges:**  Regular challenges (writing prompts, art contests, etc.) with rewards.
    * **Behind-the-Scenes Content (Author):**  Share insights into the book’s creation process.
