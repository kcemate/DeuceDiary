Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, covering various aspects and offering customizable options.

**1. Program Name & Theme:**

* **Example:** “Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love” or “Refer & Reward” - Keep it catchy and aligned with your brand.


**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Define:** Who are you trying to reach with this referral program? (Existing customers, specific demographics, etc.)
* **Goals:** What do you want to achieve? (Increase new customer acquisition, boost brand awareness, grow your user base, etc.)  Quantify this if possible (e.g., “Increase new customer acquisition by 15%”).

**3. Referral Mechanics & Structure:**

* **Types of Referrals:**
    * **Direct Referral (Most Common):**  A current user refers a friend who signs up.
    * **Indirect Referral (Less Common):** A user shares a link that leads to a discount code, which a new user then applies. (Good for lower-cost items or for driving trial.)
* **Tracking:**  You *need* a robust system to track referrals. This could be:
    * **Unique Referral Links:**  Generate a unique link for each user. (Best for tracking)
    * **Unique Codes:**  Provide each referrer with a unique code to share.
    * **Social Media Integration:**  Using referral-specific hashtags or tracking within social platforms (if possible).
* **Process:**  Clearly outline the steps:
    1. **Referrer Receives Link/Code:**  User gets their unique link or code.
    2. **New User Signs Up:**  New user clicks the link/uses the code during signup.
    3. **Verification:**  You verify the referral (usually automatic based on the link/code).
    4. **Rewards Triggered:**  The referrer and/or new user receive their reward.

**4. Incentives – Tiered System is Recommended**

This is the most crucial part!  Offer a layered approach to incentivize participation.  Here’s a breakdown of potential incentives, categorized by tiers:

* **Tier 1: Basic (For Everyone Referred)** - Low Barrier to Entry
    * **New User Discount:** 10-20% off their first purchase. (Highly effective)
    * **Welcome Gift:** A small freebie (e.g., free shipping on their first order, a small sample product)
* **Tier 2: Mid-Level (For the Referrer)** - Motivating for Consistent Sharing
    * **Discount on Next Purchase:** 15-25% off their next order.
    * **Points/Rewards Currency:**  Earn points for each successful referral, which can be redeemed for discounts, products, or other perks.
    * **Early Access to New Products/Features:** Give referrers a sneak peek.
* **Tier 3: Top-Tier (For High Performers)** - For Super-Referrers
    * **Larger Discount:** 30-50% off.
    * **Gift Card:**  To your store or another relevant retailer.
    * **Exclusive VIP Experience:**  Priority customer support, invitations to events, etc.

**5. Rewards Structure Details (Example):**

| Referral Type | Reward for New User | Reward for Referrer |
|---|---|---|
| 1 Referral = $10 Discount | 10% off First Purchase | 15% off Next Purchase |
| 3 Referrals = $30 Gift Card | 20% off First Purchase | 25% off Next Purchase |
| 5+ Referrals = VIP Status + $100 Gift
