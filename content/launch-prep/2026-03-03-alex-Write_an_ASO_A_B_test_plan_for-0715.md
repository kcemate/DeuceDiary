## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Element Optimization

**Objective:** To systematically improve app store performance (App Store page views, conversion rates, and ultimately installs) through data-driven optimization of key ASO elements.

**Duration:** 6-8 Weeks (Adjust based on initial data and potential variation)

**Metrics:**
* **Impressions:** Number of times the app store page is displayed.
* **App Store Page Views:** Number of times the app store page is viewed.
* **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of impressions that result in a click.
* **Install Rate (Conversion Rate):** Percentage of page views that result in an install.
* **Keyword Ranking:** Position of the app in relevant keyword searches. (Track daily/weekly)
* **Category Ranking:** Position of the app within its chosen category. (Track daily/weekly)

**Tools:**
* **App Store Connect Analytics:** Native analytics dashboard within Apple Developer.
* **Sensor Tower, App Annie, Mobile Action:** (Optional - For more advanced data, competitor analysis, and deeper insights)
* **Google Sheets/Excel:** For data tracking, analysis, and reporting.
