**I. DM Templates (Segmented by Category)**

**A. General Introduction (All Categories - Use this first)**

* **Template 1 (Warm & Casual):** "Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Huge fan of your content – seriously, [mention a specific recent post/video you genuinely liked]! Love the vibe you’re creating. 😊"
* **Template 2 (Slightly More Formal):** "Hi [Influencer Name], I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand]. We’re really impressed with your work in the [category - e.g., health & wellness] space and your engaged audience.”
* **Template 3 (Short & Sweet):** “Hey [Influencer Name], Love your content!  Just wanted to say a quick hello from [Your Brand].”


**B. Health Influencers**

* **Template 4 (Focus on Alignment):** "Hi [Influencer Name]! We're a brand focused on [briefly describe your product/service - e.g., sustainable wellness products] and admire your commitment to [mention their specific health focus - e.g., mindful living, plant-based nutrition]. Would love to chat about a potential collab!"
* **Template 5 (Specific Benefit):** “Hi [Influencer Name], We've noticed your audience is passionate about [specific health topic – e.g., gut health]. We have [product/service] that could be a great fit for them, and we'd love to explore how we could work together!"

**C. Humor Influencers**

* **Template 6 (Playful Tone):** "Hey [Influencer Name]! Your [mention a funny video/post] had us in stitches! 😂 We're [Your Brand], and we appreciate a good laugh.  We think our [product/service] could be a hilarious addition to your content."
* **Template 7 (Relatable):** "Hi [Influencer Name],  Totally get the struggle!  We’re [Your Brand] and we're all about bringing a little joy to everyday life.  Would love to see how we can make your audience smile more!"



**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **Template 8 (Connect to Lifestyle):** “Hey [Influencer Name]! Your style/lifestyle is so inspiring – we love your focus on [mention a specific aspect - e.g., minimalist living, travel]. We’re [Your Brand] and our products/services align with that vibe.”
* **Template 9 (Problem/Solution):** “Hi [Influencer Name],  We know your audience is interested in [mention a common lifestyle need – e.g., organization, productivity].  We offer [product/service] that can help with that, and we’d love to explore a collaboration!”
