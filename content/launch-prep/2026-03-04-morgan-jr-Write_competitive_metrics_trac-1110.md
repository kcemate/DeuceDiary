## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for continuously monitoring and analyzing your competitors to inform your strategy, identify opportunities, and stay ahead of the curve. 

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deep understanding of our competitive landscape, identify threats and opportunities, and ultimately improve our [Product/Service/Brand] performance.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors (direct & indirect).
    * Track their marketing efforts (advertising, content, social media).
    * Analyze their pricing and promotions.
    * Monitor their product development and innovation.
    * Understand their customer perception and sentiment.
    * Identify gaps in our offering that competitors are exploiting.


**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **List Competitors:**
    * **Direct Competitors:** Companies offering a similar product or service to the same target audience. (List 3-5)
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions to the same customer need. (List 5-10)
    * **Potential Competitors:** Emerging startups or larger companies that could potentially enter our market. (List 2-3 - focus on tracking their progress)
* **Prioritization Matrix:**  Rank competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:** % of the market they control.
    * **Growth Rate:** How quickly they’re expanding.
    * **Strategic Significance:**  How much their actions impact our business.
    * **Innovation Level:**  How frequently they release new features/products.
    * **Score:** Assign a score (1-5) for each criterion, weighting them based on importance.  This creates a priority list for deeper analysis.
