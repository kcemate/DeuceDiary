Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencers, with variations to suit different levels of formality and goals.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template (Focus: Feedback & Engagement)**

**Subject: [App/Product Name] - Help Us Shape the Future! (Beta Test Invite)**

**Body:**

Hi [Beta Tester Name],

We’re thrilled to invite you to join our beta test for [App/Product Name]! We’re working on building [brief, compelling description of your product - 1-2 sentences] and your feedback is *absolutely critical* to making it the best it can be.

**What you’ll be doing:**

*   Using [App/Product Name] and exploring its features.
*   Providing detailed feedback on your experience - what worked well, what could be improved, and any bugs you encounter.
*   (Optional: Participating in short, scheduled video calls for deeper discussions)

**To get started:**

*   [Link to Beta Access/Instructions]
*   [Link to a Feedback Form - Google Forms, Typeform, etc.]
*   [Link to a Dedicated Slack Channel or Forum – if you have one]

We’ll be closely monitoring the feedback channel and appreciate your enthusiasm!  We'll be reaching out periodically to check in on your progress.

Thanks in advance for your help!

Best regards,

The [Your Company Name] Team

[Your Website Link]

---

**Variations for Beta Testers:**

*   **More Formal:** Remove “Hi” and use “Dear [Beta Tester Name].”
*   **More Enthusiastic:** Add a sentence like, “We're so excited to have you onboard and can't wait to hear your thoughts!”


**2. Friend Email Template (Focus: Excitement & Early Access)**

**Subject: You’re One of the First to Try This!**

**Body:**

Hey [Friend’s Name],

Guess what?! I've been working on something really cool, and I wanted you to be one of the first to check it out. It’s a [brief description of the project - 1-2 sentences, highlighting what makes it fun/interesting].

[Link to product/demo – even if it’s a landing page]

I’m really excited about it and would love to hear your thoughts.  Let me know what you think!

Cheers,

[Your Name]

---

**Variations for Friends:**

*   **Personalized:** Add a specific memory or inside joke to make it more relatable.
*   **Simple:** If it's a very informal project, you can just say, "Check this out – I think you'll like it!"
