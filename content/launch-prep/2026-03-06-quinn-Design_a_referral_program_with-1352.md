Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers, reward types, and operational considerations:

**1. Program Name & Theme:**

* **Example:** “Share the Spark” – This suggests spreading something positive (your product/service) and highlights the benefit to the referrer.
* **Alternative Themes:** "Team Up & Grow," "Spread the Love," “Level Up Together” – Choose a name that aligns with your brand.


**2. Target Audience & Referral Goal:**

* **Before you design anything, determine:**
    * **Who are you trying to reach?** (Existing customers, specific demographics, etc.)
    * **What's a realistic referral goal?** (e.g., 100 new users, 50 sales within a month) – This will heavily influence the reward structure.

**3. Referral Tiers & Incentive Structure:**

This is the core. We’ll use a tiered system to reward increasing levels of referral activity.

| Tier Level | Referred by | New User Condition | Incentive for Referrer | Incentive for New User |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 1: Spark Starter** | Existing Customer |  First referral | **10% Discount** on next purchase | 10% discount on their first purchase |
| **Level 2: Flame Builder** | Existing Customer (with 2+ referrals) |  Second/Third referral | **$25 Gift Card** (to your store/platform) |  Free trial extension (if applicable) |
| **Level 3: Blaze Master** | Existing Customer (with 5+ referrals) |  Fourth/Fifth+ referral | **$50 Gift Card + Exclusive Product/Service** | Premium feature access for 3 months |



**4. Incentive Details & Values:**

* **Discount Codes:** Percentage or fixed amount (consider psychology – 10% often feels more appealing than $1).
* **Gift Cards:** Choose gift cards relevant to your audience (Amazon, Starbucks, Target, etc.).
* **Free Products/Services:** This is powerful if you have options.  Offer a complimentary item or service that aligns with your business.
* **Extended Trials/Subscriptions:**  Excellent if you sell SaaS or subscriptions.
* **Exclusive Content/Access:**  VIP access to webinars, early product releases, etc.
* **Points/Loyalty System:**  Reward referrals with points that can be redeemed for various benefits.
* **Charitable Donation:** (Optional - aligns with social causes) - “For every referral, we’ll donate $X to [Charity].”



**5.  How the Referral Works (Mechanics):**

* **Simple Referral Link:** Each customer gets a unique referral link.  This is *crucial* for tracking.  Use a referral program platform (see options below).
* **Sharing Options:**  Make it *easy* for people to share:
    * **Direct Link:** Shareable URL
    * **Social Media Buttons:** Pre-populated tweets/posts.
    * **Email Template:** Provide a pre-written email they can copy and paste.
    * **QR Code:** For easy sharing via mobile.
* **Tracking:**  Implement a system to accurately track referrals:
    * **Referral Program Platform:** (See list below) – This is *highly recommended*.
    * **Unique Codes:**  Consider using referral codes (especially for smaller businesses).
