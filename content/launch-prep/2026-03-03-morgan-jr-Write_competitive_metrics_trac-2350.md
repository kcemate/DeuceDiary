## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to consistently track and analyze your competition to inform strategic decisions and gain a competitive advantage. It focuses on a structured approach, key metrics, reporting cadence, and action items.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses to identify opportunities for improvement and differentiation within our own business.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strategies.
    * Monitor competitor performance and market share.
    * Detect emerging trends and potential threats.
    * Inform pricing, marketing, product development, and customer service decisions.


**2. Competitor Identification & Segmentation:**

* **Identify Key Competitors:**
    * **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering similar products/services to the same target market.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering alternative solutions that address the same customer needs.
    * **Potential Competitors:** Newer entrants or established businesses expanding into our market.
* **Segment Competitors:**  Group them based on strategy, size, and target market to tailor your analysis (e.g., “Market Leader - Price Focused,” “Niche Player - Innovation Driven,” “Small Disruptor - Social Media”).
* **Tools for Identification:**
    * Google Search & Keyword Research
    * Social Media Monitoring (Brand24, Hootsuite Insights, Mention)
    * Industry Reports & Market Research
    * Customer Feedback (Reviews, Surveys)
    * Sales Team Feedback
