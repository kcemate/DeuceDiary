**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 – Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 marks the official launch of our habit tracking application. This release focuses on delivering a solid, user-friendly foundation for building positive routines. We’ve listened to initial feedback and built a core set of features designed to help you establish and maintain your habits.

**I. Social Features**

*   **Community Groups:**  Users can now join and participate in public Community Groups based on shared habit goals (e.g., "Healthy Eating," "Productivity," "Fitness"). (Beta – Limited functionality, focused on discussion and encouragement)
    *   *Notes:* Group creation is currently restricted to administrators. Content moderation is being monitored.
*   **Friend Connections (Limited):** Users can now connect with up to 3 friends within the app.
    *   *Features:*  Send encouragement messages, view friend's public habit streaks (visibility controlled by the friend).
    *   *Notes:*  Direct messaging is *not* supported in this initial release.
*   **Activity Sharing (Public):** Users can choose to share their daily habit completion status publicly within their joined Community Groups. (Configurable privacy settings available).

**II. Tracking Features**

*   **Habit Creation:**
    *   Users can create new habits with customizable names, descriptions, icons, and reminder frequencies.
    *   Habit types:  Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Custom (defined frequency).
*   **Habit Logging:**
    *   Simple, one-tap logging to mark habits as completed for the day.
    *   Option to log partial completion (e.g., 50% on a workout) with numeric input.
*   **Streak Tracking:**  Automatic tracking of consecutive days/weeks/months completing a habit, displayed prominently.
*   **Habit History:**  Detailed log of habit completion data, viewable by day, week, or month.
*   **Habit Editing & Deletion:**  Users can modify habit details or remove habits from their tracking list.
*   **Goal Setting Integration:** Ability to link habits to specific, measurable goals.
