Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers, reward types, and tracking mechanisms. This design is adaptable to most businesses, but you'll need to tailor the specifics to your industry, product/service, and budget.

**Program Name:** (Choose something memorable - e.g., “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “Level Up”)

**I. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The customer who refers a new customer.
* **Referee:** The new customer who signs up or makes a purchase through the referrer’s link/code.
* **Tracking:** Crucial! Use a referral program software (e.g., ReferralCandy, Ambassador, ReferralHero, Smile.io, or a simpler spreadsheet if you’re starting small). These tools automate tracking, reward distribution, and reporting.
* **Terms & Conditions:**  A clear, concise document outlining the rules – eligibility, limits, expiration dates, and any potential disputes. (Have a lawyer review this!)

**II. Incentive Structure – Tiered Rewards (Most Recommended)**

This creates a sense of excitement and encourages multiple referrals.

**Tier 1:  Basic Referral (First Referral)**

* **Referrer Reward:** [Small Incentive - $5 - $10 Credit, 10% Discount, Small Gift] -  This is your ‘entry-level’ reward.
* **Referee Reward:** [Small Incentive - 5% Discount on First Order, Free Shipping, Small Welcome Gift] -  Something enticing to get them to try the product/service.

**Tier 2:  Silver Referral (2nd+ Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** [Medium Incentive - $15 - $25 Credit, 15% Discount, Exclusive Product/Service, Early Access] -  Increased value for loyal referrers.
* **Referee Reward:** [Medium Incentive - 10% Discount, Free Gift with Purchase, Extended Trial Period] -  Maintain the incentive for the new customer.

**Tier 3:  Gold Referral (5+ Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** [Larger Incentive - $50 - $100 Credit, Premium Product/Service, VIP Access, Gift Card] – For top-tier referrers.
* **Referee Reward:** [High-Value Incentive - 20% Discount, Exclusive Bundle, Personalized Gift] –  Showcase the value of the referral.

**III. Reward Types – Diversify to Appeal to Different Customers**

* **Credits:**  Easy to implement, allow flexibility.
* **Discounts:**  Always popular.
* **Free Products/Services:**  Great for showcasing your offerings.
* **Gift Cards:**  To popular retailers (gives the referrer more choice).
* **Exclusive Access:** Early access to new products, beta programs, or sales.
* **VIP Treatment:** Priority customer support, exclusive content, or events.
* **Merchandise:** Branded swag (t-shirts, mugs, etc.) can build brand loyalty.

**IV.  Referral Link/Code Generation**

* **Unique Links:**  Generate unique referral links for each referrer.  This is essential for tracking.
* **Discount Codes:** Offer a specific code for each referrer to share.
* **Social Sharing Buttons:**  Make it incredibly easy for people to share on social media.

**V.  Promotion & Communication**

* **Website Banner/Pop-up:**  Prominently display the referral program on your website.
* **Email Marketing:** Announce the program to existing customers and highlight successful referrals.
* **Social Media:** Run contests and promotions tied to the referral program.
* **In-App Notifications:** (If you have an app)
