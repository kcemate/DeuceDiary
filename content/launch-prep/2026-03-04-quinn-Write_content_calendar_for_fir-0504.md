Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service, designed to build excitement, gather feedback, and drive initial adoption. This calendar is a template – you'll need to adjust it based on your specific product, target audience, and launch goals.

**Assumptions:**

* **You've already had a launch event/announcement.** This calendar assumes the initial fanfare is over and you're moving into sustained engagement.
* **You have a decent social media presence (at least 3 platforms).**  We'll focus on a mix of content types.
* **You're tracking key metrics:** Website traffic, sign-ups, downloads, user engagement, social media reach.


**Content Calendar - Month 1 Post-Launch**

**Theme for Month 1: "Welcome & Discovery" - Focus on introducing the product/service and highlighting its core benefits.**

**Week 1: (Days 1-7) - The "Wow" & Initial Buzz**

* **Monday (Day 1):** **Launch Follow-Up -  "We Did It!" Post** – Celebrate the launch!  Share a heartfelt thank you, a key stat from the launch (e.g., website traffic, initial sign-ups), and reiterate the core value proposition. (All Platforms - Image/Video)
* **Tuesday (Day 2):** **Feature Highlight 1 - "Problem Solver"** – Focus on one key feature that directly addresses a common pain point for your target audience.  Short video demo or GIF. (Social Media - Primarily Instagram, Twitter, Facebook)
* **Wednesday (Day 3):** **Behind-the-Scenes - "Meet the Team"** –  Humanize your brand.  Short video or photo of the team involved in the product’s creation. (Blog Post + Social Media)
* **Thursday (Day 4):** **User-Generated Content Prompt – "Share Your [Product/Service] Moment"** - Encourage early adopters to share their experiences. Run a contest or offer a small reward for participation. (Social Media – Instagram, TikTok, Facebook)
* **Friday (Day 5):** **FAQ - "Quick Start Guide"** – Answer frequently asked questions in a digestible format (checklist or short video).  Link to your detailed help center. (Blog Post + Social Media)
* **Saturday (Day 6):** **Testimonial Snippet – “Real People, Real Results”** – Share a short, impactful quote from an early user. (Social Media -  Instagram Story, Twitter)
* **Sunday (Day 7):** **Rest & Reflect –  "Top 3 Highlights of the Week"** – Roundup of the best content from the week. (Social Media -  Instagram Story, Twitter Thread)



**Week 2: (Days 8-14) -  Deeper Dive & Use Cases**

* **Monday (Day 8):** **Feature Highlight 2 - "Efficiency Boost"** – Highlight another key feature and how it improves efficiency. (Social Media - Video Demo)
* **Tuesday (Day 9):** **Blog Post - "5 Ways to [Achieve a Desired Outcome] with [Your Product]"** –  Demonstrate how the product helps users achieve specific goals. (Blog + Social Media)
* **Wednesday (Day 10):** **Tutorial - "Quick Tip"** – Simple tutorial on how to use a specific feature. (Short Video - YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels)
* **Thursday (Day 11):** **Interactive Content - “Quiz: Which [Product Feature] is Right for You?”** – Engaging way to guide users to relevant features. (Social Media – Instagram Story, Facebook)
