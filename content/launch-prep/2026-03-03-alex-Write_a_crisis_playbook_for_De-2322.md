## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

**Purpose:** To outline procedures for responding to potential crises during the launch of *Deuce Diary*, ensuring minimal damage to reputation, sales, and user trust.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be honest and open with your audience. Acknowledging issues quickly builds trust.
* **Speed:** Rapid response is crucial. Delaying responses can amplify negative sentiment.
* **Empathy:** Demonstrate understanding and concern for affected users.
* **Control the Narrative:** Proactively provide accurate information and address misinformation.
* **Teamwork:** Clearly defined roles and responsibilities ensure coordinated responses.

**III. Detailed Response Procedures (Following Initial Actions)**

**A. Technical Issues (High Priority - Ongoing)**

* **Severity Levels:** Establish clear severity levels (Critical, High, Medium, Low) based on impact.
* **Escalation Path:** Dev Team -> Head of Dev/Engineering -> CEO/CTO
* **Communication:** Regular status updates on Status Page, Twitter, Discord.


**B. Negative Reviews/Social Media Buzz (High Priority - Ongoing)**

* **Sentiment Analysis:** Utilize tools like Brand24, Mention, or Hootsuite Insights to track sentiment.
* **Response Strategy:**
    * **Positive Comments:**  Thank users for positive feedback.
    * **Neutral Comments:** Acknowledge concerns, offer support, and direct to relevant resources.
    * **Negative Comments:**  Acknowledge frustration, offer a direct contact for further assistance, and address specific concerns. *Avoid direct arguments.*
* **Content Moderation:** Actively monitor and remove abusive or inappropriate comments.


**C. Content Controversy (High Priority - Immediate)**

* **Investigation:** Immediate internal review of the content, including user feedback.
* **Legal Consultation:**  Rapid legal review to assess potential legal ramifications.
* **Public Statement:** Draft a carefully worded public statement acknowledging the issue, expressing concern, and outlining steps taken to address it.
* **Content Removal (If Necessary):**  Based on legal and ethical considerations.
