# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here’s a breakdown, aiming for a balance between attracting referrals and keeping your rewards sustainable.

**1. Program Name:** (Something catchy & relevant to your brand)
* Example: “Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love” or “Refer & Reward”

**2. Target Audience & Goals:**
* **Before designing the program, clarify:**  Who are you trying to attract? What do you want to achieve (e.g., new customers, increased brand awareness, boost sales)? This will heavily influence the reward tiers.


**3. Referral Mechanics:**

* **How Referrals Work:**
    * **Simple Code/Link:** Each referrer gets a unique referral code or link.
    * **Tracking:** Use a referral tracking tool (many CRM and marketing automation platforms have built-in options - ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, ReferralSage, or even a Google UTM tracking link). This is *crucial* for accurate tracking and reward distribution.
* **Two Types of Referrals:**
    * **Direct Referral:**  A current customer refers a new customer directly.
    * **Indirect Referral (Optional):**  A customer shares a link that leads to a new customer signing up through the share, but they aren't actively involved in the introduction. (This can be trickier to manage with incentives).



**4. Incentive Tiers & Rewards (This is the core – design these strategically!)**

Here’s a tiered structure – adjust the amounts based on your business and budget.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy to Achieve)**

* **Referrer Reward:** 10% off their next purchase (or equivalent value)
* **New Customer Reward:** 5% discount on their first purchase
* **Trigger:**  New customer makes their first purchase.
* **Ease of Achievement:** High - Every referral results in a reward.

**Tier 2:  Mid-Level Referral (Requires a Bit More Effort)**

* **Referrer Reward:** $10 Gift Card to your Store/Service
* **New Customer Reward:** 10% off their first purchase
* **Trigger:** New customer makes their first purchase
* **Volume Needed:**  Requires 3-5 referrals for the referrer to unlock this.
* **Rationale:**  Motivates consistent referrals.

**Tier 3:  Top-Tier Referral (High Engagement)**

* **Referrer Reward:** $50 Gift Card to your Store/Service
* **New Customer Reward:** 15% off their first purchase
* **Trigger:** New customer makes their first purchase *and* the referrer has made at least 5 referrals.
* **Rationale:**  Rewards highly active and valuable customers.  This is for your best ambassadors.



**5. Reward Fulfillment & Timing:**

* **Automated:** The ideal scenario is automated. Your tracking system should trigger reward issuance as soon as a new customer meets the criteria.
* **Manual (Initially):** If you’re starting small, you might manually issue rewards to ensure accuracy.
* **Clear Communication:**  Let both the referrer and the new customer know when they’ve earned a reward and how to redeem it.  Use email and potentially SMS notifications.

**6. Promotion & Communication:**

* **Website Banner/Pop-up:**  Prominently display the referral program on your website.
* **Email Marketing:**  Introduce the program to your existing customer base. Segment your email lists for targeted messaging.
* **Social Media:**  Run contests, highlight successful referrals, and use referral-specific hashtags.
* **In-App/In-Product Messaging (if applicable):**  If you have a mobile app, integrate the referral program directly into the user experience.
