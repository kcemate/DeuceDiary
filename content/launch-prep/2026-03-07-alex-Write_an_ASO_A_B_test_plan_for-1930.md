## ASO A/B Test Plan: Optimizing App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements to drive installs and improve user engagement.  It focuses on measurable metrics and a phased approach.

**I. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs & Revenue
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Conversion Rate (ICR):** Percentage of users who view your app’s page and subsequently install it. (Most important)
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on App Icon:** Percentage of users who see your icon and click on it.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on App Preview Video:** Percentage of users who see your video and click on it.
    * **Keyword Conversion Rate:** Percentage of users who search for a specific keyword and then install your app.
    * **Average Session Length:** Time users spend actively engaging with the app after installation.
    * **App Page Views:** Total number of visits to your app's page.
    * **App Rating & Reviews (Indirectly):**  While harder to directly influence with A/B testing, changes in keywords can impact review volume.


**II. Testing Elements & Variables:**

We'll focus on A/B testing the following App Store elements, prioritizing those with the highest potential impact:

* **A. App Icon:** (High Impact)
    * **Variant A:** Existing App Icon
    * **Variant B:** Modified App Icon – Experiment with color changes, subtle shape adjustments, and icon elements.
* **B. App Preview Video:** (High Impact)
    * **Variant A:** Existing App Preview Video
    * **Variant B:** Revised App Preview Video –  Try different lengths (e.g., 15s vs. 30s), focus on key features, include different music, or showcase a different user experience.
* **C. App Title:** (Medium Impact - Careful Testing Needed)
    * **Variant A:** Existing App Title
    * **Variant B:** Slightly Modified App Title -  If relevant, experiment with keywords, clarity, and emotional appeal (e.g., “Productivity Tracker” vs. “Get Things Done”). *Caution: Don't drastically change the title.*
* **D. Subtitle (Short Description):** (Medium Impact)
    * **Variant A:** Existing Subtitle
    * **Variant B:** Revised Subtitle –  Refine the value proposition, highlight a key benefit, or use stronger verbs.
* **E. Keyword Targeting (Short & Long Tail):** (Low-Medium Impact - Requires Ongoing Refinement)
    * We won't directly A/B different keyword sets in this initial plan, but we'll be analyzing which keywords are driving installs and then optimizing the keyword list based on those findings.
