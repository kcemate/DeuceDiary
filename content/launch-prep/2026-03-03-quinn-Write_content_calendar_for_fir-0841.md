Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week with suggested content types and goals. This is a template – you'll need to tailor it to your specific product, target audience, and launch strategy.

**Assumptions:**

*   **Post-Launch:** We're assuming you’ve already launched and users are starting to adopt the product/service.
*   **Goal:** The primary goals for this month are to drive user adoption, gather initial feedback, and build excitement.
*   **Channels:** This calendar assumes you'll be using a mix of:
    *   Social Media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn – adjust based on your audience)
    *   Email Marketing
    *   Blog Posts
    *   In-App Messaging (if applicable)


**Key:**

*   **Social Media:** SM
*   **Email:** EM
*   **Blog:** B
*   **In-App:** IA
*   **Goal:** (A) Awareness, (D) Drive Adoption, (F) Feedback

**Week 1:  "Welcome & Celebrate!" (Focus: Initial Excitement & Basic Usage)**

*   **Monday:** (SM) – Launch Announcement Recap! Celebrate the launch with a visually appealing graphic or short video. “We’re thrilled to officially launch [Product Name]! 🎉” #launchday #newproduct
    *   (A) – Awareness
*   **Tuesday:** (B) – "What is [Product Name]? A Quick Guide" – A simple blog post explaining the product's core value proposition and key features.
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption – Introduce value
*   **Wednesday:** (SM) – User-Generated Content (UGC) Prompt:  "Share your first experience with [Product Name] using #ProductName!"  Reshare the best ones.
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption – Encourage initial usage
*   **Thursday:** (EM) – Welcome Email Series - Triggered email sequence for new sign-ups, welcoming them, highlighting key benefits, and offering a quick tutorial link.
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption
*   **Friday:** (IA) – Welcome message in app -  Friendly greeting to new users, quick tip for getting started. 
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption
*   **Weekend:** (SM) – Poll: “What’s the one feature you’re most excited to use in [Product Name]?” – Drive engagement.


**Week 2: “Let’s Get Practical” (Focus:  Tutorials & Common Use Cases)**

*   **Monday:** (B) – “Top 3 Ways to [Achieve a Key Benefit] with [Product Name]” - Blog post with specific use cases.
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption – Show immediate value.
*   **Tuesday:** (SM) – Short Video Tutorial:  "How to [Simple Task in Product Name]" - TikTok/Reels/YouTube Shorts.
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption
*   **Wednesday:** (EM) – Case Study Preview: "Early Success Stories" – Teaser email about a customer who’s already seeing positive results.
    *   (D) - Drive Adoption
*   **Thursday:** (IA) – In-app tip of the day - Small reminder of key functionality.
    *   (D) – Drive Adoption
*   **Friday:** (SM) – Q&A Session (Live or Recorded): Answer frequently asked questions about the product.
    *   (F) – Feedback – Address common concerns.
*   **Weekend:** (SM) – Highlight User Testimonials.
