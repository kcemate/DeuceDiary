**About *Deuce Diary***

*Deuce Diary* follows the story of Deuce, a 14-year-old boy navigating the awkward, chaotic landscape of middle school. Deuce isn't a hero; he’s messy, insecure, and often makes spectacularly bad decisions. Through a blend of internal monologue, sketchbook entries, and glimpses into his everyday life, readers are invited into Deuce's world – a world filled with crushes, failed experiments, frustrating family dynamics, and the constant pressure to figure out who he is.  The book’s unique, hand-drawn aesthetic, reminiscent of a genuine teenager's sketchbook, adds to its immersive and captivating feel.
