* **Dominant:** Deep Teal (#008080) – This color establishes a cool, slightly unsettling tone, reminiscent of vintage wallpaper and water. It's the base color.
* **Secondary:** Dusty Rose (#D8BFD8) – Used for key text and smaller graphic elements, providing a touch of femininity and a slightly faded, retro feel.
* **Accent:** Antique Gold (#B8860B) – Sparingly used for the diary itself and possibly a subtle highlight, adding a sense of age and importance.
* **Neutral:** Off-White (#F5F5DC) – For the background of the portrait and to provide contrast, avoiding a stark white.


**2. Layout:**

* **Central Focus: The Portrait:**  A slightly unsettling, highly stylized portrait of a teenage girl in the style of a vintage magazine cover. Think slightly wide-eyed, a subtle smirk, and a somewhat awkward pose. The style should be reminiscent of 60s/70s art -  slightly stylized, almost illustrated, with a focus on expression over realism.  Don’t make it *too* creepy, but enough to pique interest.
* **Diary Placement:** The Deuce Diary itself (the actual physical object) sits partially overlapping the girl’s right hand, as if she's holding it.  The diary should be rendered in the Antique Gold color.  Its design will be understated, classic, and slightly worn - not a flashy, modern design.
* **Background:** The Deep Teal color will subtly bleed into the background, creating a layered effect.  Consider a faint, almost imperceptible pattern in the teal background – perhaps stylized floral or geometric shapes – to add visual interest without distracting from the portrait.

* **iOS iPhone 15 Pro Max (Recommended):**  The graphic will be sized optimally for the iPhone 15 Pro Max. This is the most current and popular device.
