Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service, broken down by week and focusing on a mix of engagement, education, and sales. This is a template - **you'll need to tailor it heavily to your specific product/service, target audience, and launch strategy.**

**Assumptions:**

*   **You've already done pre-launch buzz.** This calendar assumes a foundation of awareness built through email lists, social media teasers, etc.
*   **You have a central hub (website/landing page).** This is where most links and information will direct users.
*   **You have defined key metrics** (website traffic, sign-ups, trial activations, social engagement) to track your success.

**Key Content Pillars:**

*   **Welcome & Onboarding:** Helping users get started.
*   **Value Proposition:**  Reinforcing *why* people should use your product.
*   **Tutorials & How-Tos:** Showing users how to get the most out of it.
*   **Community & Engagement:** Building excitement and a loyal user base.


---

**Week 1: The Excitement & "Get Started" Phase**

*   **Theme:**  Celebration & Initial Guidance
*   **Monday:**  **Launch Announcement Recap (All Platforms)** -  High-energy post celebrating the launch. Include a compelling image/video.  Link to your landing page. (Social, Email)
*   **Tuesday:** **Welcome Email Series - Part 1: “You Did It!”** -  A personalized welcome email thanking users for joining and highlighting the core benefit. (Email)
*   **Wednesday:** **Short Tutorial - "First Steps"** -  A 60-90 second video demonstrating the absolute *easiest* way to get started. (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, Website)
*   **Thursday:** **Q&A - Ask Us Anything (Social Media)** -  Hold a live Q&A session on Instagram/Facebook/Twitter to address immediate questions.  Save the recording. (Social)
*   **Friday:** **User Story Spotlight** – Feature a beta user or early adopter (with their permission) sharing their initial experience. (Blog, Social)


**Week 2:  Deeper Dive & Addressing FAQs**

*   **Theme:**  Value & Use Cases
*   **Monday:** **Blog Post: “5 Ways [Product/Service] Can Solve [Problem]”** –  Focus on the key pain points your product addresses. (Blog)
*   **Tuesday:** **Tutorial - “Feature Spotlight: [Specific Feature]”** -  A more detailed explanation of one key feature.  (YouTube, Website)
*   **Wednesday:** **Customer FAQ - "Common Questions Answered"** -  Compile a list of frequently asked questions and answer them clearly. (Website, Social Media)
*   **Thursday:** **Behind-the-Scenes Content** - Show a bit of your team/process - humanizes your brand. (Instagram Story, TikTok)
*   **Friday:** **Case Study Snippet** -  A short excerpt from a case study (if available) highlighting a success. (Social, Website)
