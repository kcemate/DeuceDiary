## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive user acquisition, engagement, and brand awareness. The program leverages a tiered rewards system, a viral loop, and robust fraud prevention measures.

**1. Program Name & Theme:** “Spread the Spread” -  Plays on the book's theme and encourages sharing.

**2. Mechanics & Rewards (Tiered System)**

* **Referrer (Existing User):**
    * **Tier 1 (First 5 Referrals):** 1,000 Deuce Coins (in-app currency) & Exclusive "Spread the Spread" Badge on their profile.
    * **Tier 2 (Subsequent Referrals - Every 5 after 5):** 500 Deuce Coins & Exclusive "Spread the Spread" Badge.
    * **Tier 3 (Every 10 after 5):** 250 Deuce Coins
* **Referee (New User):**
    * **Immediate Reward:** 2,000 Deuce Coins upon registration and completing their first diary entry.
    * **Bonus (After 7 days):** 500 Deuce Coins for completing their first 3 diary entries. (Incentivizes engagement)
* **Viral Loop:**  Referrers receive a unique referral link. Referees use this link to sign up. Once the referee completes a diary entry, they receive the initial 2,000 coins - encouraging continued use.



**3. Viral Loop Design - Driving User-Generated Content & Sharing**

* **Social Sharing Buttons:**  Seamlessly integrated within the diary creation flow. Allow users to directly share their diary entry (with a customizable message) to platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter.
* **“Share Your Spread” Campaign:**  Periodically run contests on social media encouraging users to share their diaries using the hashtag #DeuceDiary and tagging Deuce Diary. Feature winning entries within the app.
* **Leaderboard (Optional - Later Phase):** Introduce a leaderboard for top referrers (with high-value rewards) to foster competition. (Monitor carefully for unintended behavior).

**4. Fraud Prevention**

* **Email Verification:** Mandatory email verification for both referrers and referees to minimize fake accounts.
* **Unique Referral Links:**  Each referrer receives a unique, trackable link.  We'll use a system that auto-generates and manages these links.
* **Activity Monitoring:**
    * **Suspicious Activity Thresholds:** Flag accounts exhibiting unusual referral patterns – e.g., generating a disproportionate number of referrals relative to their own activity.
    * **IP Address Tracking:** Monitor IP addresses for potential bot activity.
    * **Device Fingerprinting:**  Track device fingerprints to detect multiple accounts originating from the same device.
* **Manual Review:**  Have a dedicated team or automation tools to periodically review flagged accounts.
* **Terms & Conditions:** Clearly outline prohibited behaviors (e.g., using multiple accounts, creating fake referrals) in the program's terms and conditions.

**5. Tracking Implementation**

* **Dedicated Referral Tracking System:**  Implement a robust system – likely using a third-party referral marketing platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy) or custom development – to track:
    * **Referral Link Clicks:**  Counts the number of people clicking on a referrer’s link.
    * **Referral Conversions:** Tracks which referrals result in new user sign-ups and diary entries.
    * **Redemption Rates:** Monitors the success of reward redemption.
    * **ROI Calculation:**  Determine the cost of the program versus the value generated (new users acquired, increased engagement).
