Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week and including various content types. This is a template - you'll need to tailor it to your specific product/service, target audience, and launch goals.

**Assumptions:**

*   **Launch Date:** Week 1 of the calendar starts on Launch Day.
*   **Goal:** Drive awareness, initial adoption, and gather early feedback.
*   **Channels:** This calendar assumes you’ll be utilizing channels like: Blog, Social Media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn - adapt to your target audience), Email Marketing, and potentially a Product Hunt/Similar platform.


---

**Month 1 Post-Launch Content Calendar**

**Week 1: The Buzz - Celebrate & Introduce**

*   **Monday (Launch Day):**
    *   **Content:** Official Launch Announcement (All Channels) - Press release, blog post, social media blitz.  Include a high-quality video explaining the product/service.
    *   **Goal:** Maximize initial visibility.
*   **Tuesday:**
    *   **Content:**  “Behind the Scenes” - Short video/story on social media showing the team, the development process, or the inspiration behind the product.
    *   **Goal:** Humanize the brand.
*   **Wednesday:**
    *   **Content:** Tutorial – Quick “How To” video or guide on the core functionality. (YouTube, Blog, Social Media)
    *   **Goal:**  Demonstrate value & ease of use.
*   **Thursday:**
    *   **Content:** Customer Testimonial (Quote/Image) - If you have early users, share a positive experience. (Social Media, Blog)
    *   **Goal:** Social Proof
*   **Friday:**
    *   **Content:** Q&A Session - Live Q&A on Instagram/Facebook/Twitter to answer questions and address common concerns.  Promote in advance.
    *   **Goal:** Engagement, address doubts.


**Week 2: Deep Dive & Value**

*   **Monday:**
    *   **Content:** Blog Post -  “Top 5 Ways [Product/Service] Can Solve [Problem]” - Detailed explanation of benefits.
    *   **Goal:** Educate potential users.
*   **Tuesday:**
    *   **Content:**  Social Media -  Infographic – Visually explain a key feature or benefit.
    *   **Goal:**  Easy-to-digest information.
*   **Wednesday:**
    *   **Content:** Case Study Snippet (if available) - Short excerpt from a case study showing how the product/service helped a customer. (Blog, Social Media)
    *   **Goal:** Build credibility.
*   **Thursday:**
    *   **Content:**  User Tip - "Pro Tip" - Share a small, valuable tip for using the product/service effectively. (Social Media)
    *   **Goal:** Encourage usage, showcase advanced features.
*   **Friday:**
    *   **Content:**  Poll/Question - Engage your audience on social media. "What's the biggest challenge you're facing with [problem your product solves]?"  (Social Media)
    *   **Goal:** Gather insights, drive engagement.


**Week 3: Community Building & Feedback**

*   **Monday:**
    *   **Content:**  Blog Post – “Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)” – Address common questions. (Blog)
    *   **Goal:** Reduce support requests, provide helpful information.
*   **Tuesday:**
    *   **Content:**  Social Media Contest - Run a small contest to generate excitement
