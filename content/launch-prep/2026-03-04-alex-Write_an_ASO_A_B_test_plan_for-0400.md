## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Optimization

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for key App Store elements, aiming to improve install rates and engagement. It focuses on a phased approach, starting with simpler changes and progressing to more complex tests.

**I. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs & Engagement
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Rate (IR):**  Percentage of users who see your app in search results and then install it. (Most important)
    * **Conversion Rate (CR):** Percentage of users who click on your app’s icon and then install it.
    * **Keyword Ranking:**  Track position of target keywords within search results.
    * **App Page Views:**  Number of users viewing your app page.
    * **Category Ranking:**  Track your app’s ranking within its chosen category.
    * **User Retention (First 7 days, 30 days):**  Understanding how the changes affect long-term user behavior.


**II. Test Categories & Elements:**

We'll prioritize testing these elements, focusing on their relative impact and ease of implementation:

| **Category**         | **Element**              | **Rationale**                        | **Testing Approach** |
|-----------------------|--------------------------|-------------------------------------|-----------------------|
| **Title & Subtitle**   | App Title & Subtitle       | Key factors driving initial impressions | Iterative – Small changes, frequent tests |
| **Keywords**          | Primary & Secondary Keywords | Optimize for relevant search queries  | Keyword Rotation & Variation Tests |
| **Screenshots**       | Image Order & Captions      | Showcase key features & benefits        | Order Variations, Caption A/B Testing |
| **App Preview Video** | Video Content & Length     | Captivate users and demonstrate value | Content Variation, Length Tests |
| **Description (Short)**| Key Feature Bullets        | Quickly communicate core benefits    | Bullet Point Variations |
| **Description (Long)**| Call to Action & Tone      | Guide user flow & encourage download | CTA Variations, Tone Testing |



**III. A/B Testing Methodology (Phased Approach):**

**Phase 1: Quick Wins (1-2 weeks)**

* **Test:** App Title & Subtitle variations (focusing on clarity & keyword inclusion)
* **Variation 1 (Control):** Existing Title & Subtitle
* **Variation 2 (Variant):**  Experiment with different phrasing, incorporating high-volume keywords.
* **Metrics:** IR, CR, App Page Views
* **Tools:** App Radar, Sensor Tower, App Annie (for tracking rankings & traffic)

**Phase 2: Keyword Optimization (2-4 weeks)**

* **Test:** Primary & Secondary Keyword Rotation (Using Keyword Variation Tools)
* **Variation 1 (Control):** Existing Keyword List
* **Variation 2 (Variant):** Rotate through different keyword sets targeting broader and niche queries.
* **Metrics:** IR, CR, Keyword Ranking (daily/weekly)
* **Tools:** App Radar, Sensor Tower, Google Search Console (for keyword trends)

**Phase 3: Visual Appeal (3-6 weeks)**

* **Test 1: Screenshots - Order Variations**
    * **Variation 1 (Control):** Existing screenshot order
    * **Variation 2 (Variant):** Reorder screenshots focusing on most compelling visuals first.
* **Test 2: Screenshots - Captions**
