## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements, focusing on driving installs and engagement. It emphasizes a data-driven approach and iterative improvements.

**I. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs & Daily Active Users (DAU)
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve App Store Conversion Rate (CVR), Reduce Bounce Rate, Increase Retention
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Rate:** Percentage of users who see the app in the App Store and click to install.
    * **Conversion Rate (CVR):** Percentage of users who view the app’s detail page and then install.
    * **Bounce Rate:** Percentage of users who land on the app’s detail page and leave without installing.
    * **Average Session Length (iOS):** Measures user engagement within the app.
    * **Retention Rate (1-Day, 7-Day, 30-Day):** Tracks user retention over time.
    * **Keyword Ranking:** Monitor changes in ranking for target keywords.
