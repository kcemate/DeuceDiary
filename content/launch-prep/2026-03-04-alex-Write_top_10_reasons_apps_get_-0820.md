**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (as of Late 2023/Early 2024 - Apple’s Guidelines are constantly updated, so this is a general overview):**

3. **Privacy Violations:**  Collecting user data without proper consent, disclosing sensitive data unnecessarily, or violating Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) policies.
4. **Malware/Security Issues:**  Apps that contain viruses, spyware, or other malicious code are immediately rejected.
5. **Functionality Issues:**  Apps that don't work as advertised, crash frequently, or have significant bugs.
6. **Violations of Apple's Human Interface Guidelines (HIG):** This is a broad category covering UI/UX issues – poor navigation, confusing design, inconsistent use of iOS elements, etc.
7. **Intellectual Property Violations (Beyond Copyright):** This includes trademarks (using a name or logo too similar to another brand) and patents.
8. **Content Restrictions:** Apps that contain inappropriate content (violence, hate speech, pornography, etc.) are prohibited.
9. **App Store Policy Violations:**  A catch-all for anything not specifically covered above but still violating Apple's terms.
10. **Lack of Required Information:** Failing to provide accurate app metadata (title, description, keywords, screenshots, etc.) or required app information.
