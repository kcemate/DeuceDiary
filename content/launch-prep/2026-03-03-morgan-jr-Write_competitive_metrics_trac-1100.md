## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing our competition, allowing us to identify opportunities, threats, and ultimately, make more informed strategic decisions.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape to improve our strategy, product, and marketing efforts.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Track competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns).
    * Identify emerging trends and potential disruption in the market.
    * Benchmark our performance against competitors.
    * Inform strategic decisions related to product development, marketing, and sales.


**2. Key Competitors Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., If you sell SaaS CRM, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM are Tier 1) - *Highest Priority Tracking*
* **Tier 2 (Adjacent Competitors):** Companies offering slightly different products/services or targeting a similar segment. (e.g., A marketing automation tool targeting small businesses could be Tier 2 to a CRM) - *Medium Priority Tracking*
* **Tier 3 (Potential Disruptors):** Companies with innovative technologies or business models that could threaten our market share. (e.g., A blockchain-based solution for our industry) - *Lowest Priority Tracking, Monitoring for Early Signals*
