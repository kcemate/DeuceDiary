Okay, here's a social media influencer outreach template kit designed for micro-influencers (health, humor, lifestyle), including DM templates, collaboration offers, and a content brief.  I've included variations to tailor to each niche and offer flexibility.

**I. DM Templates (Tailored by Niche)**

**A. General Opening (Use for all)**

* **Option 1 (Friendly & Casual):** “Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Loving your content – seriously, [mention a specific post you genuinely liked] was hilarious/inspiring/fantastic! 😊”
* **Option 2 (Slightly More Formal):** “Hi [Influencer Name], I’m [Your Name/Brand Name] and I'm a big fan of your work, especially your focus on [mention specific area – e.g., mindful living, relatable humor, sustainable style].”


**B. Health Influencers**

* **DM 1 (Focus on Shared Values):** “We're passionate about [mention brand’s health focus - e.g., holistic wellness, healthy recipes, mental wellbeing] just like you!  We'd love to explore a potential collab to share [mention specific product/service] with your amazing audience.”
* **DM 2 (Specific Ask):** “Would you be open to reviewing our [product name]? We think it aligns perfectly with your content about [mention their content focus - e.g., healthy routines, nutrition tips].  No pressure – just curious!”
* **DM 3 (If they've engaged with you before):** "Loved your recent post about [specific topic]! We were thinking about how our [product/service] could complement that message. Would you be interested in chatting?"



**C. Humor Influencers**

* **DM 1 (Highlighting Fun):** “Dude, your [hashtag/type of content - e.g., relatable memes, skits] is straight fire 🔥 Seriously, we need more laughter in the world!”
* **DM 2 (Playful Collaboration Idea):** “We've got a few silly ideas for a collab that we think your audience would *love*. Think [brief, funny concept – e.g., a challenge, a prank, a funny review].”
* **DM 3 (Acknowledging Their Style):** "Your brand of humor is *exactly* what we're going for! We’d love to send you [product/gift] and let you create some hilarious content with it.”



**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **DM 1 (Focus on Lifestyle Alignment):** “Obsessed with your [mention specific aesthetic/lifestyle element - e.g., cozy aesthetic, travel style, minimalist life]! It’s so inspiring.”
* **DM 2 (Offer a Relevant Product):** "We're launching [product name] which is designed for [mention relevant lifestyle - e.g., busy moms, travel enthusiasts, home organizers] and we think it would be a great fit for your audience.”
* **DM 3 (Open-ended question):** "What’s one thing you’re loving in your life right now? We're always looking for inspiration and would love to hear it! 💫"
