Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each tailored to a different goal and with estimated timings. I've included suggested content, triggers, and potential variations.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build excitement, and encourage initial engagement.
* **Target:** New subscribers who have signed up through a website form, landing page, or lead magnet.
* **Trigger:** Immediately after signup.

**Sequence:**

* **Day 1: Welcome Email – “Thank You & A Little About Us”**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:** Warm welcome, reiterate their signup choice (e.g., “Thanks for downloading our free guide!”), brief brand story (1-2 sentences), highlight your mission/values, and a friendly tone.  Include a visually appealing brand image.
    * **Call to Action:**  “Learn More” (link to your website’s ‘About Us’ page)
* **Day 3:  Problem/Pain Point – “Are You Struggling With [Related Issue]?”**
    * **Subject:**  Is [Problem your product/service solves] holding you back?
    * **Content:**  Address a common problem your target audience faces.  Relate it to their signup choice.  Emphasize the frustration this problem causes.  Keep it brief and empathetic.
    * **Call to Action:** "Take our quiz to see if you’re experiencing this" (link to a short quiz) or “Read our blog post on this topic” (link to relevant blog post).
* **Day 5: Product/Service Highlight – “Meet [Key Product/Service]”**
    * **Subject:**  Introducing [Product/Service Name] – Your Solution!
    * **Content:** Briefly explain one key product or service and its biggest benefit. Focus on *what it does for the customer*, not just features. Use an image or short video if possible.
    * **Call to Action:** "See it in Action" (link to a product demo or landing page) or “Shop Now” (if applicable).
* **Day 7: Offer/Discount – "Exclusive Welcome Offer!"**
    * **Subject:**  Just for Joining – [Discount Percentage or Offer]!
    * **Content:**  Provide a small incentive for their first purchase or engagement. This could be a discount code, free shipping, or a bonus gift.  Create a sense of urgency ("Limited Time Offer!").
    * **Call to Action:** "Claim Your Discount" (link to your online store or offer page).
