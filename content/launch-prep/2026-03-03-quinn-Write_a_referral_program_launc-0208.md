**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Phase 1 (Launch - 2 Weeks):** Focus on direct sharing - Encourage users to share their referral link via social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter) with a compelling visual (branded graphic).
* **Phase 2 (Growth - 4 Weeks):** Introduce “Refer a Friend” messaging within the app itself.  Highlight the benefits for both parties.  Consider a simple in-app sharing button.
* **Phase 3 (Sustainability - Ongoing):**

**4. Fraud Prevention:**

* **Manual Review of High-Volume Referrals:** Initially, flag referrals with unusually high numbers or from suspicious IPs.
* **IP Address Monitoring:** Track IP addresses and identify potential bot activity or bulk referral attempts.
* **Account Verification:** Implement basic account verification steps (e.g., email confirmation, social login) to reduce fake accounts.
* **Referral Link Validation:**  Verify the legitimacy of referral links to ensure they’re being generated through the app/website.  Blacklist suspicious links.
* **Duplicate Account Detection:** Identify and prevent the creation of multiple accounts by the same user.
* **Rate Limiting:**  Limit the number of referrals a single user can make within a given timeframe.
