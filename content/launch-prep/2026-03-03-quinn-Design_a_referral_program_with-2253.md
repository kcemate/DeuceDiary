Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating different levels of complexity and aiming for something engaging and effective.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** (Example) "Share the Spark," "Level Up Together," "Refer & Reward" – Choose something that aligns with your brand and feels exciting.
* **Logo/Visuals:**  A simple icon or graphic representing sharing/growth would be good.

**2. Target Audience & Goal:**

* **Clearly define who you're targeting with the referral program.** (e.g., existing customers, specific demographics, users with high engagement)
* **What’s the primary goal?** (e.g., acquire new users, increase brand awareness, grow a specific product/service segment)

**3. Referral Structure & Mechanics:**

* **Referral Method:**
    * **Unique Referral Links:** Each referred user gets a unique link they can share. *This is crucial for tracking.*
    * **Discount Code:**  The referrer receives a code they can use for a discount.
    * **Social Sharing:**  Make it easy to share directly on social media (with pre-written captions).
    * **Email Invitation:**  Send a personalized email invitation to existing users to refer.
* **Tracking:** Implement a system to track referrals – ideally within your CRM or marketing automation platform.

**4. Incentives – Tiered Levels (Crucial for Engagement!)**

Here's a tiered system. Adapt the rewards to fit your budget and business.

* **Level 1:  The “Welcome” Reward (For both referrer & referee)**
    * **Referee Sign-Up Bonus:** 10% off their first purchase/subscription. (This encourages initial adoption)
    * **Referrer Reward:**  A small thank you - maybe a $5 credit, a free digital download, or a small exclusive discount.
* **Level 2: The "Growth" Reward (Referrer - Achieved after 2 successful referrals)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 20% off their next purchase/subscription.
    * **Referee Reward:**  Same as Level 1 (10% off).
* **Level 3: The "Advocate" Reward (Referrer - Achieved after 5 successful referrals)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** $25 credit/store credit.
    * **Referee Reward:** 15% off their next purchase/subscription.
* **Level 4: The "VIP" Reward (Top Referrers - Quarterly or Annual Recognition)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  Exclusive access to new products/features, personalized consultation, a premium gift box, or a higher percentage discount (e.g., 30%).  This is for truly enthusiastic advocates.


**5.  Terms & Conditions (Important for legal protection):**

* **Eligibility:**  Specify who can participate (e.g., existing customers only).
* **Validity:**  Set a timeframe for the referral program (e.g., 6 months, 1 year).
* **Redemption Rules:**  Clearly outline how rewards are claimed and used.
* **Fraud Prevention:**  Include a clause stating that fraudulent activity will result in termination of participation.
* **Changes to Terms:** Reserve the right to modify the program terms.
