**Q1: January - February (New Year, New Resolutions)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Productivity, Health & Wellness.
* **Key Events:** New Year’s Day, Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Valentine's Day, Super Bowl.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Launch/Promotion:** Ideal time to promote apps focused on productivity, habit tracking, fitness, meal planning, and self-improvement.
    * **Content Focus:**
        * **Blog Posts:** “Top 5 Productivity Apps for 2024,” “How to Start a New Year with Healthy Habits,” “Apps to Help You Achieve Your Goals.”
        * **Social Media:**  Motivational quotes, tips related to your app’s core benefit, user success stories. Run contests related to setting goals.
        * **Paid Advertising:**  Target users interested in self-improvement, productivity, and health.
        * **App Store Updates:**  Highlight new features that support goal setting or healthy routines.
    * **Special Offers:**  Limited-time discounts, free trial extensions for relevant apps.
* **Metrics to Track:** Downloads, Active Users, Conversion Rates, App Store Ranking.


**Q2: March - April (Spring, Productivity Boost)**

* **Theme:** Spring Cleaning, Renewal, Productivity, Mental Wellness.
* **Key Events:** St. Patrick's Day, Earth Day, April Fool's Day.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Launch/Promotion:**  Apps focused on organization, cleaning (home or digital), learning, and mental health benefit from this period.
    * **Content Focus:**
        * **Blog Posts:** “Spring Cleaning Your Digital Life,” “Boost Your Productivity with These Apps,” “Stress Reduction Apps for Spring.”
        * **Social Media:** Visual content featuring spring themes, before-and-after cleaning examples, mental wellness tips.
        * **User-Generated Content:** Encourage users to share how your app helps them "spring clean" their lives.
    * **Paid Advertising:**  Increase bids during St. Patrick’s Day for relevant keywords.
    * **App Store Updates:**  Introduce features that improve organization or streamline workflows.
* **Metrics to Track:**  Downloads, Engagement,  User Retention, Average Session Length.
