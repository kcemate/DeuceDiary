Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here’s a comprehensive plan, broken down into sections, with options for customization depending on your business and budget.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Catchy Name:** “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “[Your Brand] Buddies,” “Level Up with [Your Brand]” – something memorable and relevant.
* **Visuals:**  Create a logo or graphic that’s consistent with your brand and clearly represents the referral program.

**2. Core Mechanics – How It Works**

* **Easy Sign-Up:** The referral process should be ridiculously simple.
* **Two Types of Referrals:**
    * **Direct Referrals:**  Existing customers directly invite new customers.
    * **Indirect Referrals:**  A referral happens through a shared link or code (e.g., a friend shares a discount code). This is crucial for scaling.
* **Tracking:**  You *must* have a system to track referrals. This could be:
    * **Unique Referral Links:** Generate a unique link for each customer.
    * **Unique Referral Codes:**  Assign a short code to each customer.
    * **Software Integration:** Use referral marketing platforms (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Mention Me) – these automate everything and are well worth the investment.

**3. Incentives – What’s in it for Everyone?**

This is the most important part! Here's a tiered system with options (consider your profit margins and marketing budget):

* **For the *Referrer* (The Person Making the Referral):**
    * **Tier 1 (Basic - Easy to Implement):**
        * **Discount on Next Purchase:** 10-20% off their next order. (Low Cost)
        * **Small Gift Card:** $5-$10 gift card to your store or a popular retailer. (Moderate Cost)
        * **Points in Loyalty Program:** If you have a loyalty program, award bonus points.
    * **Tier 2 (Mid-Level - More Attractive):**
        * **Increased Discount:** 20-30% off their next purchase.
        * **Exclusive Product/Service:**  Offer a special product or service only available to referrers.
        * **Free Shipping:**  For a set period.
    * **Tier 3 (High-Level – For Top Performers):**
        * **Larger Gift Card:** $25-$50.
        * **VIP Access:**  Early access to new products, exclusive events.
        * **Monthly Subscription Credit:**  If you offer subscriptions, credit their account.


* **For the *New Customer* (The Person Being Referred):**
    * **Discount on First Purchase:** 10-20% off (Essential – encourages trial)
    * **Welcome Gift:** Small, relevant freebie (sample, sticker, etc.) - can enhance perceived value.
    * **Free Shipping:** On their first order.
    * **Bonus Points:** In your loyalty program.



* **For the *Referee* (The Person Receiving the Referral):**  Often overlooked, but rewarding the recipient can drive even more referrals.
    * **Tier 1:** Same discount as the referrer (simple).
    * **Tier 2:**  A slightly larger discount or bonus for referring a friend.
