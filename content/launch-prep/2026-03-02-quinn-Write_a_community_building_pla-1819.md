## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the strategy for building a thriving and engaged community around *Deuce Diary* on Discord and Reddit, centered around fostering discussion, celebrating the lore, and creating a welcoming space for fans.

**I. Overall Vision:**

To cultivate a passionate and dedicated community around *Deuce Diary* that feels like a vibrant extension of the story itself. This community will be a hub for discussion, speculation, fan art, and shared appreciation for the world and characters. 

**II. Platforms & Strategy:**

* **Discord - The Heart of the Community:**
    * **Purpose:** Real-time discussion, exclusive content announcements, close-knit interaction, and building a core community.
    * **Channels:**
        * **#general:**  Welcome, announcements, casual conversations.
        * **#lore-discussion:** Deep dives into the lore, theories, worldbuilding, and history.
        * **#character-chats:** Dedicated channels for specific characters (e.g., #Deuce, #Lukan, #Eris).
        * **#art-showcase:**  Showcase and discussion of fan art, cosplay, and creative works.
        * **#theory-crafting:** Dedicated to developing and discussing theories surrounding the story.
        * **#off-topic:**  A space for members to connect outside of *Deuce Diary*. (Moderated to maintain focus)
        * **#suggestions:** A channel for community suggestions for content, events, or improvements.
    * **Frequency:** Active channel management, daily moderation, and occasional interactive events.
* **Reddit - Broader Reach & Discovery:**
    * **Purpose:**  Reach a wider audience, encourage longer-form discussions, and drive traffic back to Discord.
    * **Subreddit:** r/DeuceDiary 
    * **Content Focus:** Longer-form discussions, image/video sharing, AMAs (Ask Me Anything) with the creator, sharing of fan content, and announcements.
    * **Frequency:**  Consistent posting schedule (at least 3-5 times per week), active moderation, and engagement with other relevant subreddits.


**III. Content Strategy:**

* **Creator-Led Content:** (Most Crucial)
    * **Regular Lore Drops:**  Small, digestible pieces of lore revealed through Discord & Reddit.
    * **Character Spotlights:** In-depth looks at individual characters - their motivations, backstory, relationships.
    * **Behind-the-Scenes:**  Insights into the creative process, worldbuilding choices, and inspirations.
    * **Interactive Q&A Sessions:** Regularly scheduled sessions on both platforms where the creator answers community questions.
* **Community-Generated Content (Encouraged & Highlighted):**
    * **Fan Art Contests:** Regularly held contests with prizes (digital art packs, mentions, etc.).
    * **Fan Theories & Discussions:**  Encourage members to share and debate theories within appropriate channels.
    * **Cosplay Showcase:** Celebrate fan cosplay creations.
    * **Writing Prompts & Collaborative Storytelling:**  Opportunities for community members to contribute to the story’s narrative (with creator’s input/approval).



**IV. Moderator Guidelines:**

* **Core Values:**  Respect, Inclusivity, Positivity, Knowledge Sharing.
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **Enforce Rules:**  Maintain a safe and welcoming environment by enforcing community guidelines.
    * **Channel Management:**  Keep channels organized and active.
    * **Engagement:**  Start conversations, answer questions, and promote community interaction.
    * **Conflict Resolution:**  Mediate disputes fairly and calmly.
    * **Content Moderation:** Review user-generated content to ensure it aligns with guidelines.
