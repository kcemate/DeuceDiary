**I. DM Templates (Tailored by Niche)**

**General Opening (All Niches - Send this first)**

* **Template 1 (Friendly & Curious):** "Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Just stumbled across your feed and I'm *loving* your content – seriously, [mention a specific post you genuinely liked - e.g., your recent workout post, your hilarious take on something, your gorgeous travel photo]. You seem to have such a great community! 😊"
* **Template 2 (Direct & Value-Focused):** "Hi [Influencer Name], I'm [Your Brand Name], and we're a [brief, one-sentence description of your brand/product - e.g., ‘sustainable skincare brand,’ ‘comedy app,’ ‘brand of comfy loungewear’].  I think your audience would really appreciate [mention a key benefit of your product].”

**Health Influencers**

* **Template 3 (Specific to Health Goal):** "Hey [Influencer Name], I noticed you often share content about [mention their focus - e.g., healthy recipes, mindful movement, fitness tips]. We're launching a new [product - e.g., protein powder, yoga mat, wellness course] that would be a fantastic addition to your audience’s healthy lifestyle."
* **Template 4 (Personalized & Trust-Building):** “Hi [Influencer Name],  I really admire your approach to [mention their specific philosophy - e.g., intuitive eating, holistic wellness]. We're a brand committed to [align with their values - e.g., clean ingredients, ethical sourcing, supporting mental wellbeing] and would love to explore a potential collab."


**Humor Influencers**

* **Template 5 (Playful & Referencing Content):** "Hey [Influencer Name], your [mention a specific funny post or video - e.g., ‘that relatable meme,’ ‘your sketch comedy bit’] had me in stitches! 😂  We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re all about bringing a little laughter to people’s days.  Would love to chat about collaborating!"
* **Template 6 (Light & Engaging):** “Hi [Influencer Name], You're killing it with the laughs! We’ve got [describe your brand in a funny way - e.g., ‘the most awkward t-shirts,’ ‘a seriously silly app’] and think we’d be a hilarious match.”

**Lifestyle Influencers**

* **Template 7 (Relatable & Aspirational):** "Hey [Influencer Name],  Your content always looks so [describe what you admire - e.g., 'stylish,' 'calm,' 'adventurous']! We're [Your Brand Name], and we create [what you offer - e.g., ‘beautiful home decor,’ ‘eco-friendly accessories,’ ‘curated travel guides’] that fit into a beautiful lifestyle.”
* **Template 8 (Community Focused):** “Hi [Influencer Name], Your community seems so engaged and passionate - something we value a lot at [Your Brand Name]! We're looking for authentic voices to share our [product/service] with.”



**Follow-Up (After Initial DM - Send 2-3 days later if no response)**
