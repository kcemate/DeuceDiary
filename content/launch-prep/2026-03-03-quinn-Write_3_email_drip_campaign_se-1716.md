Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for varying lengths. I’ve included suggested timing, content, and calls to action.  Remember to tailor these to your specific brand, audience, and product/service!

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (7 Days - Ideal for New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and set expectations.
* **Target Audience:** New email subscribers
* **Timing:**  Immediately after signup, then 1 day, 3 days, 7 days after.
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 0 – Immediately):**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:** Warm welcome message. Briefly explain what your brand is about (1-2 sentences). Express excitement about having them join the community.  Include a visually appealing brand image.
        * **Call to Action:**  “Explore our website: [Link to your website]”
    * **Email 2 (Day 1):**
        * **Subject:**  Discover Why [Your Brand] is Different
        * **Content:**  Highlight 2-3 key differentiators - what makes you unique? Focus on the *benefits* for the customer (e.g., “We deliver the freshest ingredients,” “Our personalized service helps you achieve your goals”).
        * **Call to Action:** “Learn More About Our Story: [Link to ‘About Us’ page]”
    * **Email 3 (Day 3):**
        * **Subject:**  [Relatable Problem] - We Can Help!
        * **Content:**  Address a common pain point your target audience experiences. Offer a brief, empathetic statement.
        * **Call to Action:** “Read our Blog Post on [Relevant Topic]: [Link to a helpful blog post]”
    * **Email 4 (Day 7):**
        * **Subject:**  Exclusive Offer for New Subscribers! 🎁
        * **Content:**  Introduce a small, attractive offer (e.g., 10% off their first purchase, free shipping, a valuable resource).  Create urgency ("Limited time offer!").
        * **Call to Action:** “Claim Your Discount: [Link to your product/service page with the discount applied]”

**Campaign 2: Product/Service Feature Spotlight (14 Days - Great for Driving Sales)**

* **Goal:**  Educate potential customers about a specific product or service feature and drive conversions.
* **Target Audience:**  Subscribers who have shown interest in a particular category (e.g., purchased a related product in the past, visited a specific page on your site).
* **Timing:**  Days 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 14
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 1):**
        * **Subject:** Introducing [Product/Service Name] - [Key Benefit]

        * **Call to Action:** “See [Product/Service Name] in Action: [Link to product/service page with demo or video]”
