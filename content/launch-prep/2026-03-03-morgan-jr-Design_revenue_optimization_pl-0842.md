**I. Executive Summary**

* **A. Revenue Breakdown:**

    * **Churn Rate:**  Percentage of customers who stop doing business with you.
    * **Net Promoter Score (NPS):**  Measure customer loyalty and willingness to recommend your business.
* **C. Pricing Analysis:**
    * **Current Pricing Strategy:**  What is your current pricing model (e.g., cost-plus, value-based, competitive)?

    * **Competitive Pricing:** Analyze competitor pricing for similar products/services.
* **D. Marketing & Sales Performance:**
    * **Marketing ROI:**  Evaluate the return on investment for your marketing campaigns.
    * **Sales Conversion Rates:** Track the percentage of leads that convert into customers.
    * **Sales Cycle Length:** Analyze how long it takes to close a sale.
