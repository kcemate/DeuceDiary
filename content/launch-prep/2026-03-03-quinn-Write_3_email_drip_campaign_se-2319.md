Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different purpose and with varying lengths. I've included subject lines, key content for each email, and suggested timing between emails.  These are templates – you'll need to tailor them to your specific brand, product/service, and audience.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding (New Subscriber - Immediate to 7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and guide new subscribers towards a first purchase or action.
* **Target Audience:** New email subscribers who signed up via your website, landing page, or social media.
* **Timing:** Immediate (as soon as signup), Day 1, Day 3, Day 5, Day 7

| Email # | Subject Line                  | Content                                                                                                                            | Timing     |
|---------|-------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| 1       | Welcome to [Your Brand]!       | Thank you for subscribing! Briefly introduce your brand and what subscribers can expect (e.g., exclusive deals, helpful content). Include a friendly tone.  Link to your website.  | Immediately |
| 2       |  [Benefit 1] - Get Started!   | Focus on ONE key benefit of your product/service.  Share a quick tip or how-to related to that benefit. Include a relevant image.   | Day 1      |
| 3       |  We're Here to Help!           | Address common questions or FAQs.  Include links to your support resources (FAQ page, help center, contact form). Humanize your brand. | Day 3      |
| 4       |  Exclusive Offer Just for You! | Introduce a limited-time discount or special offer.  Create a sense of urgency.  Clearly state the offer and how to redeem it. | Day 5      |
| 5       |  [Popular Product/Service] Spotlight | Showcase one of your most popular offerings with a brief description and positive customer testimonial.  Include a clear call to action (CTA).| Day 7      |



**Campaign 2: Lead Nurturing - Interest-Based (7-14 Days After Initial Engagement)**

* **Goal:**  Move leads further down the funnel by providing valuable content related to their interests.  This assumes they’ve interacted with your content (e.g., downloaded an ebook, watched a video).
* **Target Audience:**  Subscribers who have engaged with specific content (tracked through website behavior, form submissions, etc.).
* **Timing:** Day 7, Day 10, Day 14

| Email # | Subject Line                      | Content                                                                                                                                                                        | Timing     |
|---------|----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| 1       |  Loved the [Ebook/Video Title]?   | Recap the key takeaways from the content they engaged with.  Offer a deeper dive or related resources.  Include a visual reminder of the content.                               | Day 7      |
| 2       |  [Industry Trend] - What You Need to Know | Provide insights and commentary on a relevant industry trend that aligns with your brand’s expertise.  Position yourself as a thought leader.  | Day 10     |
| 3       |  Case Study: [Relevant Customer]  | Share a case study showcasing how a customer achieved a positive result using your product/service.  Use specific data and results. | Day 14     |



**Campaign 3: Abandoned Cart Recovery (1-3 Days After Abandonment)**

* **Goal:** Recover lost sales by reminding customers about items left in their carts and addressing potential concerns.
