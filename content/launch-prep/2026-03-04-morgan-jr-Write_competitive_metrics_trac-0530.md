**II. Defining the Competition**

* **Identify Competitors:** Create a tiered list of competitors:
    * **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering similar products/services to the same target market. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi)
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering alternative solutions that address the same customer need. (e.g., Coffee shops vs. Tea shops)
    * **Potential Competitors:** Emerging companies or those entering new markets that could pose a future threat.
* **Competitor Profiling:** For *each* identified competitor, gather the following data:
    * **Products/Services:** Detailed analysis – features, pricing, quality, innovation.
    * **Marketing Strategies:** Channels used (Social Media, Paid Ads, Content Marketing, Email, PR), messaging, branding, campaigns.
    * **Sales Strategies:** Sales process, distribution channels, pricing models, incentives.
    * **Customer Experience:** Customer service, online reviews, user reviews, NPS scores.
    * **Financial Performance:** (Publicly available data only) Revenue, market share estimates, profitability.
    * **Technology & Innovation:** Patents, R&D investments, new product launches.
