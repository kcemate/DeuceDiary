**Phase 1: Assessment & Diagnosis (Weeks 1-4)**

* **1.1 Define "Revenue":** Be specific. Is it gross revenue, net revenue, recurring revenue, or a combination?  Understanding your primary revenue metric is crucial.
* **1.2 Current Revenue Analysis:**
    * **Data Collection:** Gather *all* relevant revenue data. This includes:
        * Sales Data (volume, pricing, discounts, promotions)
        * Marketing Data (channel performance, campaign ROI, lead sources)
        * Customer Data (segmentation, lifetime value, churn rate, acquisition cost)
        * Product/Service Data (cost of goods sold, profitability by product/service)
    * **Segmentation:** Segment your customer base (e.g., by demographics, behavior, purchase history) – this will inform targeted strategies.
    * **Channel Analysis:**  Map out all your revenue channels – online, retail, wholesale, partnerships, etc.
    * **Competitive Analysis:** Understand competitor pricing, offerings, and market share.
* **1.3 Identify Revenue Leaks:** Pinpoint areas where revenue is being lost or unrealized:
    * **Lost Sales:**  Why are deals falling through? (Price, features, competition, sales process)
    * **Low Average Order Value (AOV):**  Can you upsell, cross-sell, or bundle products/services?
    * **High Churn Rate:**  Are customers leaving for cheaper alternatives or better experiences?
    * **Inefficient Pricing:**  Are you undercharging or overcharging based on perceived value?
    * **Marketing Inefficiency:** Are you spending money on channels with low ROI?

**Phase 2: Strategic Initiatives (Weeks 5-12)**

This phase focuses on implementing changes based on your diagnosis.  Prioritize these based on potential impact and feasibility.

* **2.1 Pricing Optimization:**
    * **Value-Based Pricing:**  Price based on the *perceived* value to the customer, not just cost-plus.
    * **Dynamic Pricing:** (If applicable) Adjust prices in real-time based on demand, inventory, and competitor pricing.
    * **Tiered Pricing:**  Offer different pricing tiers with varying features or levels of service.
    * **Promotional Pricing:** Strategic use of discounts, bundles, and limited-time offers (but avoid eroding margins).
* **2.2 Sales Process Optimization:**
    * **Sales Training:** Equip your sales team with the skills to close more deals (e.g., consultative selling, negotiation techniques).
    * **Sales Automation:**  Streamline tasks like lead nurturing, follow-ups, and reporting.
    * **Lead Qualification:**  Implement a robust lead scoring system to focus on the most promising prospects.
    * **CRM Optimization:** Ensure your CRM is effectively managing the sales process and providing valuable insights.
* **2.3 Marketing Optimization:**
    * **Channel Optimization:** Invest in the channels that deliver the highest ROI.  Experiment with new channels.
    * **Content Marketing:**  Create valuable content that attracts and engages potential customers.
    * **SEO & SEM:**  Optimize your website and run targeted search engine marketing campaigns.
    * **Retargeting:**  Re-engage website visitors who didn't convert.
