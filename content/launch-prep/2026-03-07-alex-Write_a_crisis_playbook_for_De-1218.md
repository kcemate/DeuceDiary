## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Purpose:** To provide a structured approach to managing potential crises during the launch of “Deuce Diary,” ensuring a smooth rollout and minimal damage to brand reputation.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Honest and timely communication is paramount.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge user concerns and frustrations.
* **Speed:** Rapid response times are critical, especially in the initial hours.
* **Collaboration:** A cross-functional team (Marketing, PR, Customer Support, Engineering, Legal) will manage the response.
* **Documentation:** Keep a detailed log of all communications, issues, and actions taken.


**II. Potential Crisis Scenarios & Response Protocols:**

**A. Technical Issues (Most Likely - 60%)**

* **Scenario 1: Server Overload/Slow Performance:** Users report significant lag, crashes, or inability to access the game.
    * **Priority:** Critical
    * **Immediate Actions:**
        * **Engineering Team:** Prioritize monitoring, identify root cause (CPU, RAM, database), and implement immediate scaling solutions (if possible).
        * **Communication (PR/Marketing):**  Acknowledge the issue on all channels (Twitter, Facebook, Discord, Website).  “We’re aware of performance issues and our team is working urgently to resolve them. We appreciate your patience.” – Keep updates consistent.
        * **Customer Support:** Prepare FAQs addressing common issues, monitor support channels for influx of reports.
    * **Timeline:** 15-60 minutes (Initial response), 1-4 hours (Root cause identified and initial fixes deployed)
* **Scenario 2: Bug/Glitch Introduced:** A new bug is discovered impacting gameplay, progression, or UI.
    * **Priority:** Critical
    * **Immediate Actions:**
        * **Engineering Team:** Immediately isolate and confirm the bug, develop a hotfix.
        * **Communication (PR/Marketing):**  Announce the bug, apologize for the inconvenience, and provide an estimated timeframe for the fix.  “We’ve identified a bug that’s affecting [specific area].  We’re working on a fix and will update you as soon as possible.”
        * **Customer Support:**  Prepare detailed instructions on how to reproduce the bug (if possible) and workaround solutions.
    * **Timeline:** 30-90 minutes (Initial response), 1-4 hours (Hotfix deployed)

**B. Content-Related Issues (20%)**

* **Scenario 1: Controversial Content/Offensive Material (Unlikely, but needs planning):**  A piece of content within the game is deemed offensive, triggering backlash.
    * **Priority:** Critical
    * **Immediate Actions:**
        * **Legal Team:**  Assess the legal implications, advise on response.
        * **PR/Marketing:** Craft a statement acknowledging the concern, expressing disappointment, and outlining the review process. “We take these concerns extremely seriously.  We are immediately reviewing this content and committed to taking appropriate action.” – Delay definitive action until legal counsel advises.
        * **Engineering Team:** Prepare to swiftly remove the content if legal approves.
    * **Timeline:** 1-4 hours (Initial response, dependent on legal review)
* **Scenario 2: Community Backlash – Misinterpretation of Lore:**  The community misinterprets lore or a narrative element, leading to significant debate and criticism.
    * **Priority:** High
    * **Immediate Actions:**
        * **Marketing/Community Managers:**  Engage directly with community members, clarify the intended meaning of the content, and address concerns thoughtfully.
