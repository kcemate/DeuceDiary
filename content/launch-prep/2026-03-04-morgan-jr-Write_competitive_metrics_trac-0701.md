## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a structured approach to tracking and analyzing your competitors, providing insights for strategic decision-making and competitive advantage.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and inform strategic initiatives to improve our performance.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors across various tiers (direct, indirect, emerging).
    * Monitor competitor strategies, offerings, and pricing.
    * Analyze competitor marketing efforts and brand perception.
    * Benchmark our performance against competitors in key metrics.
    * Identify potential areas for innovation and differentiation.


**II. Competitor Identification & Categorization:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., If you sell CRM, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM are Tier 1)
* **Tier 2 (Indirect Competitors):** Companies offering alternative solutions addressing the same customer need. (e.g., For CRM - spreadsheets, manual processes)
* **Tier 3 (Emerging Competitors):**  New players or startups disrupting the market. (Monitor industry news and trends for potential newcomers)
* **Documentation:** Create a spreadsheet or database to document each competitor, including:
    * **Company Name & Website**
    * **Industry Segment**
    * **Target Audience**
    * **Product/Service Offering**
    * **Business Model**
    * **Estimated Market Share (if available)**
    * **Key Personnel (CEO, Marketing Lead, etc.)**


**III. Metrics to Track - Categorized:**

| Category          | Metric Examples                               | Data Source(s)                           | Frequency      | Responsible Team    |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|----------------|----------------------|
| **Financial**       | Revenue, Profit Margin, Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Lifetime Value (LTV) | Annual Reports (Public), Industry Reports | Quarterly/Annually| Finance/Strategy     |
| **Marketing**       | Website Traffic, Lead Generation, Conversion Rates, Social Media Engagement, Brand Sentiment, Advertising Spend, SEO Ranking | Google Analytics, Social Media Analytics, SEMrush, Brand Monitoring Tools | Weekly/Monthly    | Marketing             |
| **Product/Service**| Features, Functionality, Pricing, User Reviews, Product Roadmap, Innovation Rate | Competitor Websites, Product Demos, Online Reviews (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot), Industry News | Monthly/Quarterly | Product/R&D          |
| **Sales**           | Sales Cycle Length, Win Rate, Average Deal Size, Sales Team Size | Sales Data, CRM, Sales Reports            | Monthly/Quarterly | Sales                |
| **Customer**        | Customer Satisfaction (CSAT), Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Retention Rate, Churn Rate | Customer Surveys, Feedback Forms, Support Tickets | Quarterly/Annually| Customer Success/Support|
| **Operational**     | Employee Growth, Technology Adoption, Scalability  | Publicly available information, industry reports | Annually        | Operations/HR         |



**IV. Data Collection & Tools:**

* **Google Analytics:** Website traffic, user behavior.
* **Social Media Analytics (Facebook Insights, Twitter Analytics, LinkedIn Analytics):** Engagement, reach.
* **SEMrush/Ahrefs:** Keyword research, competitor SEO analysis.
* **Mention/Brand24:** Brand monitoring, social listening.
* **G2/Capterra/Trustpilot:** Product reviews and ratings.
* **Industry Reports:** Gartner, Forrester, IDC – Market size, trends, and competitive analysis.
* **Financial Databases:** Bloomberg, Reuters – Financial data.
* **CRM System:** Tracking sales performance and customer
