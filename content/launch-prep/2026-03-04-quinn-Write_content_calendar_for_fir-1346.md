**Important Assumptions:**

* **You’ve already built an audience:** This calendar assumes you have some initial followers through pre-launch marketing, email lists, or social media.
* **You have defined your core messaging:** You know *why* people should use your product/service.
* **You're aiming for a mix of content:**  This calendar combines educational, engaging, and promotional content.
* **Platform Focus:** This assumes a focus on a few key platforms - likely social media (Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, X/Twitter) and a blog. Adapt to your primary channels.

**Month 1 Post-Launch Content Calendar**

**Week 1:  Excitement & Onboarding (Focus: Introducing & Getting Started)**

* **Monday:** **Launch Announcement Recap (Social & Blog)** -  Re-share the official launch announcement. Highlight key features and benefits with a visually appealing image or short video. (Type: Announcement, Visual)
* **Tuesday:** **"How To" - Quick Start Guide (Blog & Short Video - TikTok/Instagram Reels)** -  A super simple video (60-90 seconds) demonstrating the *very* first steps of using your product/service. "X Steps to [Desired Outcome]" (Type: Tutorial, Video)
* **Wednesday:** **Behind-the-Scenes (Instagram Story/X/Twitter)** -  Show a glimpse of the team behind the product, or a quick update on something exciting happening. Humanizes the brand. (Type: Behind-the-Scenes, Story)
* **Thursday:** **FAQ - Top 3 Questions (Blog & Social Media)** – Address the most common questions people are likely to have.  (Type: FAQ, Text-Based)
* **Friday:** **User Testimonial Snippet (Social Media)** - If you have early adopters, share a short, positive quote or screenshot of their feedback. (Type: Social Proof)


**Week 2: Deep Dive & Value Proposition (Focus: Benefits & Problem Solving)**

* **Monday:** **Blog Post - "Why [Your Product] Solves [Problem]"** –  A longer-form blog post that dives deeper into the problem your product solves and how it’s different. (Type: Blog Post, Informative)
* **Tuesday:** **Case Study Teaser (Social Media - Carousel Post)** – Start a mini-case study.  Present the initial problem a customer faced and hint at the solution.  "Sarah struggled with… but then she discovered…" (Type: Case Study, Visual)
* **Wednesday:** **Interactive Poll/Quiz (Instagram Story/X/Twitter)** –  "What's your biggest challenge with [relevant topic]?" – Use the responses to generate content ideas. (Type: Engagement, Interactive)
* **Thursday:** **"Did You Know?" Feature Highlight (Social Media)** –  Focus on a less-obvious feature and explain its value. (Type: Feature Spotlight, Visual)
* **Friday:** **Community Question - Ask for Feedback (Social Media)** –  "What’s one thing you’d love to see us add to [product]?" – Encourage interaction. (Type: Community Building, Open-Ended)
