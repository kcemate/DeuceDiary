Okay, here's a draft App Store seasonal marketing calendar, broken down by season and with suggested tactics. This is a template – you'll need to adapt it to your specific app, target audience, and budget. I've included estimated timeframes and prioritized key events.

**Important Note:** This calendar assumes you're primarily focusing on the App Store itself (ASO, featured placements, paid campaigns) and can be integrated with broader marketing efforts (social media, email, etc.).

---

**I. Winter (November - February)**

* **Theme:** Cozy, Festive, Holiday Cheer, New Year's Resolutions
* **Focus:**  Drive downloads leading into holiday gifting, promote apps that help with winter wellness, and capitalize on New Year’s resolutions.
* **Timeline:**
    * **November (Pre-Holiday Push):**
        * **November 1-15:** ASO Refresh - Optimize keywords related to holiday gifting, "best gifts," "unique presents."
        * **November 15-30:** Launch limited-time holiday-themed in-app offers (discounts, bundles).  Start running Apple Search Ads targeting holiday gift searches.  Create holiday-themed app screenshots/videos.
        * **November 30:** Black Friday/Cyber Monday promotions - Consider a significant discount or special offer.
    * **December (Peak Holiday Season):**
        * **December 1-24:** Heavy focus on Apple Search Ads and App Store Spotlight. Leverage holiday keywords aggressively. Run contests/giveaways within the app to boost engagement.
        * **December 15-31:**  Run retargeting campaigns to users who visited your app store page but didn’t download.
    * **January (Post-Holiday & New Year’s):**
        * **January 1-15:**  Shift messaging to New Year's Resolutions – highlight how your app helps users achieve their goals.  ASO updates based on trending resolutions.
        * **January 15-31:** Analyze Black Friday/Cyber Monday performance and adjust ASO/AS campaigns. Start planning for February.

**II. Spring (March - May)**

* **Theme:** Renewal, Wellness, Fresh Starts, Outdoors
* **Focus:** Capitalize on spring cleaning, health & fitness trends, and outdoor activities.
* **Timeline:**
    * **March (Transition & Wellness):**
        * **March 1-15:** ASO updates for “spring cleaning,” “reset,” “self-care.” Promote health & wellness features.
        * **March 15-31:** Launch a spring-themed in-app event or challenge (e.g., 30-day fitness challenge).
    * **April (Spring & Outdoor Activities):**
        * **April 1-15:** Focus on apps related to outdoor activities – hiking, biking, gardening, etc. Run Apple Search Ads targeting these activities.
        * **April 15-30:** Partner with relevant influencers to promote your app to their audience.
    * **May (Mother’s Day & Graduation):**
        * **May 1-15:** Mother’s Day promotion – offer a gift card or special bundle.
        * **May 15-31:** Target graduation-themed apps (study tools, career apps).

**III. Summer (June - August)**

* **Theme:** Travel, Fun, Experiences, Relaxation
* **Focus:**  Appeal to summer leisure activities and travel plans.
* **Timeline:**
    * **June (Travel & Entertainment):**
        * **June 1-15:** ASO updates for “summer travel,” “vacation planning,” “entertainment.” Promote apps relevant to these themes.
