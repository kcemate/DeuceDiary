**About the Book:**

Deuce “D” Davenport is just trying to survive eighth grade. Between the relentless social hierarchy of Middle School North, the strange and intensely loyal group of friends he’s made, and the increasingly bizarre happenings in his small town, D is keeping a diary – a *very* detailed diary. But D’s observations are... different. They’re filled with unsettling images, cryptic encounters, and a growing sense that something isn’t quite right. Is he losing his mind? Is someone watching him? Or is there something truly strange lurking beneath the surface of his seemingly ordinary life?

*Deuce Diary* is a visually stunning and emotionally resonant exploration of adolescence, anxiety, and the blurry line between reality and imagination. Walker’s distinctive art style, combined with D’s raw and unflinching voice, creates a truly unforgettable reading experience.
