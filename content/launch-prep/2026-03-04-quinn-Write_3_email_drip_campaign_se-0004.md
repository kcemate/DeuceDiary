# Write 3 email drip campaign sequences.

Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different purpose and with varying lengths and content. I’ve included suggested triggers and timing. Remember to adapt these to your specific brand, audience, and goals.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding (New Subscriber - 5 Emails - 3 Days to 7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and gently guide the subscriber to a key offer.
* **Trigger:** Immediately after a user subscribes to your email list (through a form on your website, contest entry, etc.)
* **Tone:** Friendly, enthusiastic, helpful.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome & Thank You**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
    * **Content:**  Thank you for joining! Briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values.  Reiterate what they signed up for (e.g., "You're now part of our community of [relevant interest]!").  Include a visually appealing brand image.
    * **Call to Action (CTA):** “Learn More About Us” (links to your "About" page)
* **Email 2 (Day 3):  Problem/Solution - Start Addressing Their Needs**
    * **Subject:** Struggling with [Pain Point Your Product Solves]?
    * **Content:** Identify a common problem your product or service solves. Briefly explain the problem and hint at how your brand offers a solution.  Use relatable language.
    * **CTA:** “Discover How We Can Help” (links to a blog post or relevant page)
* **Email 3 (Day 5):  Highlight a Key Benefit**
    * **Subject:**  [Benefit] Made Easy with [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:** Focus on one core benefit of your product or service.  Explain *why* this benefit is valuable.  Use a testimonial or social proof snippet.
    * **CTA:** “See It In Action” (links to a product demo video, case study, or customer testimonial)
* **Email 4 (Day 7): Limited-Time Offer - Gentle Nudge**
    * **Subject:** Don't Miss Out: [Discount/Special Offer]
    * **Content:** Introduce a limited-time offer (e.g., 10% off, free shipping). Create a sense of urgency. *Don’t* make this the primary goal; it’s a gentle reminder.
    * **CTA:** “Claim Your Discount Now” (links to your product page with the discount applied)
* **Email 5 (Day 7):  Last Chance & Value Reminder**
    * **Subject:**  ⏰ Last Chance: [Offer Details]
    * **Content:**  Remind them of the expiring offer. Briefly reiterate the value they’ll receive.
    * **CTA:** “Shop Now Before It’s Gone” (links to your product page)



**Campaign 2: Lead Nurturing – Product Interest (7 Emails – 7 Days to 21 Days)**

* **Goal:** Educate leads about your product/service, build trust, and move them closer to a purchase.
* **Trigger:** User engages with a specific piece of content (e.g., downloads a guide, views a product page multiple times, signs up for a webinar).
* **Tone:**  Informative, helpful, and subtly persuasive.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 7):  Content Delivery - Follow-Up to Engagement**
    * **Subject:**  You Downloaded [Guide Name] - Here’s What’s Next
    * **Content:**  Deliver the content they downloaded.
