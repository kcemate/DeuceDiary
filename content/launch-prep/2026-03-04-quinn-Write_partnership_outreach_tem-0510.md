Hi [Name],

Just wanted to say I’m a huge fan of [Brand Name] and what you're doing – especially [mention something specific you genuinely admire – e.g., your commitment to sustainable yoga mats, your approachable approach to gut health, your vibrant workout content].

I’d love to chat briefly about potentially collaborating on [Suggest a low-commitment idea – e.g., a shared social media post, a giveaway, a short blog exchange]. 

You can check us out here: [Link to your website/social media]

Best,
[Your Name]

**Why it works:** Friendly, shows genuine interest, suggests a manageable starting point.

I’m reaching out from [Your Brand Name], a [describe your brand - e.g., holistic wellness platform, certified organic supplement brand, mindfulness app]. We help [Target Audience – e.g., busy moms, young professionals, athletes] to [Key Benefit – e.g., reduce stress, improve their energy levels, achieve their fitness goals].

I've been consistently impressed with your work at [Brand Name] focusing on [Mention their core area - e.g., mindful movement, healthy recipes, mental wellbeing].  I believe there’s a strong synergy between our brands, particularly in reaching [mention shared target audience].

I’d like to explore opportunities for a partnership that could benefit both our audiences, such as [Suggest 2-3 specific ideas - e.g., a co-branded content series, a product bundle, a cross-promotion campaign].

You can learn more about us here: [Link to your website]

Thanks for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

**Why it works:** Clearly outlines the value proposition for both brands, identifies a shared target audience, and suggests concrete ideas.
