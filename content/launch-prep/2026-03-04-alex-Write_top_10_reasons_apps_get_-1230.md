6. **Performance Issues:** App crashes, slow loading times, excessive battery drain, or poor user experience can trigger rejection.
7. **Technical Issues:** Bugs, glitches, and software instability are common rejection causes.
8. **Malware or Security Risks:** Apps that contain malware, attempt to steal user data, or violate security best practices are automatically rejected.
9. **Content Issues:**  This encompasses a wide range of issues like sexually suggestive content, inappropriate language, or content that violates local laws.
10. **Lack of Transparency / Missing Information:** Not providing accurate app descriptions, missing metadata, or failing to meet Apple’s requirements for app submission.
