## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Optimization

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for key App Store elements, aiming to improve app visibility, install rates, and ultimately, user acquisition.

**1. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve App Ranking, Increase Click-Through Rate (CTR) on App Store page
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Conversion Rate (ICR):** Percentage of users who visit your app page and subsequently install the app. (Target: Increase by X%)
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of users who see your app listing and click on it. (Target: Increase by Y%)
    * **App Ranking (Keyword Ranking):** Track position for target keywords within App Store search results. (Target: Improve ranking for Z keywords)
    * **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Rate:**  Percentage of users who land on your app page and complete a desired action (e.g., trial signup, purchase). (Target:  Increase by W%)
