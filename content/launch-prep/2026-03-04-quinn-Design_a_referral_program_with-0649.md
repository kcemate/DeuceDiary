Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. This design focuses on driving engagement and rewarding both the referrer and the new customer.

**Program Name:** (Example: "Share the [Your Brand] Love")

**I. Core Structure & Goals:**

* **Goal:** Increase customer acquisition, build brand awareness, and foster a loyal community.
* **Duration:** (Example: Ongoing, with potential seasonal promotions) - Let's start with a 6-month rolling program.
* **Target Audience:**  Clearly define who you're trying to attract (e.g., new users, specific demographics).  Tailor incentives accordingly.

**II. Referral Levels & Rewards (Tiered System - Encourages Multiple Referrals)**

This is where we create a compelling reward structure.  We'll offer different tiers based on the number of successful referrals.

| **Tier** | **Number of Referrals** | **Referrer Reward** | **New Customer Reward** | **Reward Type** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 1: “Early Adopter”** | 1-2 |  $10 Store Credit | 10% Discount on First Purchase | Digital Credit & Discount |
| **Level 2: “Brand Ambassador”** | 3-5 | $25 Store Credit  | Free Shipping on First Order | Higher Value Credit & Shipping Benefit |
| **Level 3: “Top Referrer”** | 6+ | $50 Store Credit + Exclusive Gift (e.g., branded item) |  Exclusive VIP Access (early product previews, etc.) | Premium Reward & VIP Access |



**III. Mechanics & How it Works**

1. **Referral Link/Code Generation:**
   * **Unique Link:** Each existing customer receives a unique referral link (using a tool like ReferralCandy, Viral Loops, or building your own).
   * **Unique Code:** Alternatively, offer a unique referral code they can share.

2. **How the Referrer Earns:**
   * The referrer earns their reward when a *new* customer signs up *and makes a purchase* using the referrer’s link/code. (This ensures genuine engagement)
   * **Tracking:** Implement robust tracking to accurately record referrals and purchases.

3. **How the New Customer Earns:**
   * New customers receive the benefit of the reward upon completing their first purchase.

4. **Communication:**
   * **Email Notifications:**  Automated emails for both the referrer and the new customer are *crucial*.
       * **Referrer Email:**  “You've been invited to share the love!  Earn [Reward Amount] when someone you refer makes a purchase.”
       * **New Customer Email:** “Welcome to [Your Brand]! Thanks to [Referrer Name] for the welcome discount/free shipping.”
   * **In-App/Website Notifications:** If you have a mobile app or website, use notifications to remind users about the program.
   * **Social Media:**  Promote the referral program on your social channels.  Run contests and highlight top referrers.


**IV.  Incentive Details & Considerations**

* **Reward Value:**  The value of the rewards needs to be attractive enough to motivate people to participate. Test different reward amounts to see what drives the most referrals.
* **Reward Types:**  Offer a mix of reward types to appeal to different preferences.  Consider:
    * **Store Credit:** Versatile, encourages ongoing purchases.
    * **Discounts:** Immediate savings.
    * **Free Shipping:**  A major motivator.
    * **Exclusive Access:**  Creates a sense of community and value.
