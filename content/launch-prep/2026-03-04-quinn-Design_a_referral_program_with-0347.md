Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating various elements to maximize participation and effectiveness.

**Program Name:** (Let's call it) "Spread the Word Rewards"

**1. Core Concept:**

The program rewards both the referrer (the person sending the referral) and the referred customer with incentives. This dual reward system encourages participation from both sides.

**2. Referral Structure & Tiers:**

We'll use a tiered system to increase engagement and motivation.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 10% discount on their next purchase.
    * **Referred Reward:** 5% discount on their first purchase.
* **Tier 2: Enthusiast (Medium)** – *Unlocked after the referrer refers 3 successful referrals*
    * **Referrer Reward:** 15% discount on their next purchase + Free Shipping on their next order.
    * **Referred Reward:** 10% discount on their first purchase.
* **Tier 3: Ambassador (High)** – *Unlocked after the referrer refers 5 successful referrals*
    * **Referrer Reward:** 20% discount on their next purchase + Exclusive access to new product launches + A small gift (e.g., branded merchandise - t-shirt, sticker pack)
    * **Referred Reward:** 15% discount on their first purchase.

**4. Referral Tracking & Mechanics:**

* **Unique Referral Links:** Each referrer receives a unique referral link (and a referral code) to share.  This is *crucial* for tracking.
* **Tracking System:** Implement a system to track referrals. This can be:
    * **Dedicated Referral Software:** (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, ReferralPilot) - Simplest and most robust.
    * **Custom Tracking:** Use UTM parameters in referral links, or integrate with your e-commerce platform's referral features (if available).
* **Automatic Rewards:** The system automatically applies discounts or credits to the referrer and referred customer's accounts when the referral converts to a sale.
* **Clear Communication:** Send automated emails to both referrer and referred customer upon successful referral and reward activation.

**5. Promotion & Communication:**

* **Website Banner:** Prominently display the referral program on your website.
* **Email Marketing:**  Announce the program to your existing customer base.
* **Social Media:**  Run campaigns on social media, highlighting the benefits of the program.
* **In-App Notifications (if applicable):**  For mobile apps, use push notifications to remind users about the program.
* **Referral Cards/Flyers:**  For brick-and-mortar businesses, consider physical referral cards.
