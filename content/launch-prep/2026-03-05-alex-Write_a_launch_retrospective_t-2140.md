Okay, here's a launch retrospective template specifically designed for the first week of a product launch, along with suggested metrics to track. This template is designed to be flexible and adaptable to your specific product and launch goals.

**Product Launch Retrospective - Week 1**

**Product:** [Product Name]
**Launch Date:** [Date]
**Team Members Present:** [List Team Members]
**Overall Launch Goal (Briefly Restated):** [e.g., Achieve 1000 active users, Secure 50 paying subscribers, Drive initial awareness]

**I.  Icebreaker (5-10 mins)** – *Brief, quick activity to get everyone energized and open to sharing.*
   * Example: "What was one thing you learned this week that surprised you?"


**II.  Review – What Happened? (20-30 mins)**

*   **A. High-Level Recap:**  (Facilitator leads – 5 mins)
    *   Briefly summarize the launch timeline and key events.
    *   Highlight any major successes or unexpected challenges.

*   **B.  Deep Dive – Key Areas of Focus:** (Break into smaller groups or discuss as a whole – 15-20 mins)
    *   **1. User Acquisition:**  How did users find us? (Channels used - Paid Ads, Organic Search, Social Media, Referral, etc.)
    *   **2. Onboarding Experience:** How easy was it for new users to get started? (First-time user flow, tutorial completion, support requests)
    *   **3. Product Usage:** What features are users engaging with the most?  Which are being ignored? (Core Feature Usage, Time Spent in App, Session Length)
    *   **4. Technical Performance:**  Were there any bugs, outages, or performance issues? (Error Rates, Load Times, System Stability)
    *   **5.  Customer Support:** How was the volume and quality of support requests? (Support Ticket Volume, Average Resolution Time, Customer Satisfaction – if collected)
