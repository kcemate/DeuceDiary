Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers and options to make it engaging and effective.

**Program Name:** (Choose something relevant to your brand – e.g., “Spread the [Brand Name] Love,” “Refer & Reward,” “The [Brand Name] Circle”)

**I. Core Structure & Mechanics**

* **How it Works:** Customers earn rewards for referring new customers.
* **Tracking:** Utilize a referral tracking link or code. This is *crucial* for accurate reporting and reward distribution.  Tools like ReferralCandy, Smile.io, or even a simple Google URL builder can do this.
* **Eligibility:** Define who can participate – current customers, registered users, etc.
* **Terms & Conditions:** Clearly state the rules:
    *  Referral link/code must be used to sign up.
    *  New customer must complete a specific action (e.g., make a purchase, sign up for a free trial).
    *  Both the referrer and the referred customer must meet certain criteria (e.g., be active users).
    *  Expiry dates for referrals and rewards.

**II. Incentive Tiers – A Multi-Layered Approach**

This is where you make it *really* compelling.  Offer a mix of incentives to cater to different customer motivations.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Everyone)**

* **For the Referee (New Customer):**  Discount Code - 10% off their first purchase. (This is a standard, easy-to-understand incentive.)
* **For the Referrer:**  Small Recognition -  A thank-you email, a badge on their profile within your platform, or a public shout-out on social media (with their permission).

**Tier 2: Bronze Referral (Refer 3-5 People)**

* **For the Referee:**  Discount Code - 15% off their first purchase.
* **For the Referrer:**  Gift Card –  $10 Amazon/Gift Card/Store Credit (Allows them to actually *use* the reward.)
* **Recognition:**  Featured in a "Referral Hero" section on your website/blog.


**Tier 3: Silver Referral (Refer 5-10 People)**

* **For the Referee:**  Free Shipping on their next order.
* **For the Referrer:**  Higher Value Gift Card – $25 - $50. 
* **Recognition:**  Personalized thank you from the company’s CEO/founder (If feasible - adds a personal touch)

**Tier 4: Gold Referral (Refer 10+ People)**

* **For the Referee:** Extended Trial Period (if applicable), or Premium Feature Access (if applicable)
* **For the Referrer:**  Significant Gift Card – $100 or more.  Consider a higher-tier reward – a premium product, a VIP experience, or a subscription upgrade.
* **Recognition:**  Exclusive invitation to a VIP event (online or in-person) and recognition on your ‘Wall of Fame’ – high-visibility on your website.



**III. Reward Types – Beyond Discounts**

* **Gift Cards:**  Versatile and popular (Amazon, Starbucks, etc.)
* **Store Credit:**  Reduces friction and encourages immediate spending.
* **Free Products/Services:**  Great for product-based businesses.
* **Premium Features:** Unlock advanced features for loyal users.
* **Extended Trial Periods:**  Ideal for SaaS products.
* **VIP Experiences:**  (e.g., online masterclass, exclusive consultation) - Adds significant value.

**IV.  Promotion & Communication**

* **Website Banner:** Prominently display the referral program on your homepage
