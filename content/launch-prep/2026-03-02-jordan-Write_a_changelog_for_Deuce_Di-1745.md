**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is thrilled to be launching! This release represents the culmination of our initial development efforts, bringing you a foundational platform for tracking and reflecting on your habits, goals, and overall well-being.  We’ve focused on a core set of features designed to be intuitive and effective. Thank you for your patience and feedback during development – we’re excited for you to start your journey with Deuce Diary!

**I. Social Features**

* **Community Feed:** (New) Users can now view public posts from other Deuce Diary users.  This allows for inspiration, shared struggles, and encouragement. *Note:* Content moderation is currently manual and may not be immediate.
* **Following:** (New)  You can now follow other users to see their posts and activity in your own feed.
* **Direct Messaging (Limited):** (New)  Implement basic direct messaging functionality allowing users to privately communicate with each other. Currently limited to a maximum of 5 messages per conversation and requires both users to be online simultaneously.
* **Profile Sharing:** (New) Users can choose to make their profile public, private, or visible only to followers.
* **Reporting:** (New) Users can report inappropriate content or behavior within the Community Feed to our moderation team.



**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Tracking:** (Core)  Add new habit tracking functionality to log and monitor the completion of daily habits.  You can set reminders and track progress visually.
* **Goal Setting:** (Core) Define specific goals (short-term and long-term) within Deuce Diary. Break down large goals into smaller, manageable steps.
* **Journaling Prompts:** (New) Receive daily journaling prompts designed to spark reflection and self-awareness. You can customize the frequency of these prompts.
* **Custom Tracking Categories:** (New)  Users can create custom tracking categories beyond just “habits” and “goals” - for example, “Nutrition”, “Fitness”, “Reading”, etc.
* **Recurring Tasks:** (New)  Ability to set up recurring tasks to be automatically logged on specific days.
* **Detailed Logging:** (Core) Each habit, goal, and task can be logged with customizable details: date, time, notes, and associated tags.



**III. Gamification Features**

* **Streaks:** (Core) Maintain consecutive days of habit completion to build streaks and earn recognition.
* **Points System:** (New) Users earn points for completing habits, achieving goals, and participating in the community.
* **Badges:** (New)  Unlock badges for various achievements – streaks, goal completion, community contributions, and more.
* **Leaderboards (Beta):** (New - Limited) A basic leaderboard is available showing users with the highest point totals. *Note: This feature is in beta and may be subject to change.*
