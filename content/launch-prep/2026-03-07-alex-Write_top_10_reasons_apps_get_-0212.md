**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (2024)**

Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines are notoriously strict and can be complex. Here’s a list of the most common reasons for rejection, based on current trends:

1. **Violation of Guidelines (General):** This is the broadest category and covers almost everything else. Apple’s guidelines are constantly being updated, and even minor infractions can lead to rejection.
2. **Misleading or Deceptive Practices:** Apps that falsely advertise their features, collect data without consent, or mislead users about what they’re getting.
3. **Privacy Violations:**  A major area of focus for Apple.  This includes:
    * **Unnecessary Data Collection:** Collecting more data than necessary for the app’s core functionality.
    * **Lack of Transparency:** Not clearly explaining data collection practices in the privacy policy.
    * **Tracking Without User Consent:**  Using tracking technologies (like IDFA) without explicit permission.
4. **Intellectual Property Issues:**  Copyright infringement (music, images, code), trademark violations, or using content without proper licenses.
5. **Malware & Security Risks:** Apps that contain viruses, spyware, or other malicious code. This includes vulnerabilities that could be exploited.
6. **Functionality Issues:** Apps that don't perform as described, have bugs that significantly impact the user experience, or don’t meet Apple’s technical standards (e.g., crashes, performance issues).
7. **Content Restrictions:**  Apps containing inappropriate content (violence, nudity, hate speech, etc.).  This is strictly enforced.
8. **Payment Issues:** Problems with in-app purchases, misleading pricing, or failing to comply with Apple’s payment policies.
9. **App Store Guidelines Violations - Aggregated:** Apple can reject an app if multiple minor violations are detected across different areas of the guidelines.
10. **Lack of Technical Compliance:** Apps not meeting Apple’s technical specifications regarding iOS versions, screen sizes, asset sizes, etc.
