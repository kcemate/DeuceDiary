5. **Intellectual Property Infringement:** Using copyrighted material (music, images, trademarks, etc.) without permission is a frequent rejection reason.

7. **Functionality Issues:** Apps that don't work as advertised, crash frequently, have poor user experience, or have unclear functionality get rejected.
8. **Content Restrictions:** Content that violates Apple’s content restrictions (e.g., explicit sexual content, hate speech, illegal activities) is rejected.
9. **App Metadata Issues:**  Inaccurate descriptions, misleading keywords, or inappropriate screenshots can result in rejection.
10. **Technical Issues:** Problems with the app’s architecture, stability, or lack of proper error handling can raise concerns.
