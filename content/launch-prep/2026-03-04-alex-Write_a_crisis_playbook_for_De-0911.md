* **Tracking:** Monitor social media for mentions of the issue.


**Level 2 - Moderate:**

1. **Acknowledge & Validate:** Post a sincere statement acknowledging the concern, expressing empathy, and thanking the commenter for their feedback.  Don't get defensive.

3. **Address Directly:**  Consider a direct response to the initial commenter, offering a further explanation or solution.
4. **Monitor Intensively:** Increase monitoring of social media and review platforms.
