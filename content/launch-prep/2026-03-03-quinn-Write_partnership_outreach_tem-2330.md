Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health & wellness brands, categorized by approach and with variations to suit different partnership types.  Remember to **personalize** these heavily - generic emails get ignored.

**1. The “Warm Introduction” - Best for Building Relationships (Ideal for Smaller Brands/Influencers)**

* **Subject:** Exciting Collaboration Opportunity: [Your Brand] + [Their Brand]
* **Body:**

Hi [Name],

I’ve been a long-time admirer of your work on [mention something specific – a post, a product, a philosophy] – I particularly loved [cite a specific example].  I’m [Your Name], the founder of [Your Brand], a [brief, compelling description - e.g., brand dedicated to mindful movement & natural wellness].

We’re always looking for like-minded brands to connect with who share our passion for [shared value - e.g., holistic health, sustainable living, empowering women].  I’d love to chat briefly about potential synergies and explore how we could collaborate.

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call sometime next week?

Best regards,

[Your Name]

[Your Website]
[Link to your relevant social media]



**2. The "Specific Collaboration Idea" - Best for Targeted Offers (Good for Influencers & Mid-Sized Brands)**

* **Subject:** Partnership Idea: [Your Brand] & [Their Brand] – [Brief, Exciting Benefit - e.g.,  A Guided Meditation Series]
* **Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out because I truly believe [Their Brand] and [Your Brand] would create a fantastic partnership.  At [Your Brand], we [briefly describe what you do and your target audience - e.g., create delicious, plant-based protein snacks targeting busy professionals].

I had an idea for [Specific Collaboration - e.g., a 4-week guided meditation series focused on stress reduction] that I think would resonate really well with your audience.  We could [explain the proposed collaboration in more detail – e.g.,  you provide the expert guidance, we provide the samples to your subscribers].

I've attached a brief outline of the concept [if you have one] and would love to discuss it further.  Are you open to a quick call to explore this?

Thanks so much for your time!

Best,

[Your Name]

[Your Website]
[Link to relevant content on your website]



**3. The "Value Exchange" - Best for Larger Brands (Offer something of value upfront)**

* **Subject:** Partnership Opportunity: Boost Your [Their Brand's] Community with [Your Brand’s] [Product/Service]
* **Body:**

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], the founder of [Your Brand], and we’re passionate about [Your Brand’s Mission - e.g., promoting overall wellness through convenient, healthy meal solutions]. We’ve been impressed with your work building a thriving community around [Their Brand’s Focus - e.g., yoga and mindful living].

To help you further engage your audience, we’d like to offer you [Specific Offer - e.g., a 30-day supply of our meal kits for you to review, a discount code for your followers, a featured guest spot on your podcast].  This would allow your audience to experience the benefits of [Your Brand's product/service] firsthand.

We're open to exploring other partnership opportunities as well, such as [mention 1-2 other ideas - e.g., a giveaway, a joint content series].  

Would you be available for a brief call to discuss how this could benefit your brand?

Sincerely,

[Your Name
