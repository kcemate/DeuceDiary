## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements, aiming to improve app visibility, install rate, and overall conversion.

**I. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve App Ranking, Enhance User Engagement, Boost Conversion Rate (Install Intent -> Install)
* **Key Metrics to Track:**
    * **Install Rate (IR):**  Percentage of users who see your app on the App Store and then install it. (This is our primary metric)
    * **Conversion Rate (CR) – Install Intent:** Percentage of users who view your app’s page and explicitly intend to install (e.g., tap the "Install" button).
    * **Conversion Rate (CR) – Viewed to Install:** Percentage of users who view your app's page and ultimately install it.
    * **Keyword Ranking (for targeted keywords):** Monitor the app's position in the App Store search results.
    * **App Page Views:** Total number of times the app's page is viewed. (Used for contextual analysis)
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) – From Search:** Percentage of users who click on your app's listing in App Store search results.
    * **Retention Rate:**  (Longer-term metric, track changes after testing)
