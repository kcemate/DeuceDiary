    * **Explain (Briefly):** Provide context if needed – avoid making excuses.
    * **Commit to Action:** Indicate steps being taken (e.g., review, improvement, further communication).
    * **Don’t Speculate:** Avoid guessing or making promises you can’t keep.
* **LC Review:** Legal review of the statement before dissemination.

**Phase 3: Public Statement & Ongoing Engagement (12+ Hours)**

* **CL & SMM Action:**  Release the prepared statement across all relevant platforms.
* **CM Action:** Continued monitoring and engagement, focusing on building a constructive dialogue.
