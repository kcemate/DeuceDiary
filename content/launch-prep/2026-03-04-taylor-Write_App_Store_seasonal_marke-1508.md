**I. January - New Year, New Habits**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Resolutions
* **Campaign Focus:**  Highlight features that help users achieve their New Year's resolutions.
* **Key Activities:**
    * **Launch Promotion:** Offer a limited-time discount, premium features, or content pack for new users.
    * **Content Marketing:** Blog posts, social media content, and email newsletters related to goal setting, productivity, and self-improvement. Link this back to your app.
    * **ASO:** Focus on keywords related to "new year," "goals," "productivity," “habit tracker.”
    * **Example App Types:** Productivity apps, fitness apps, meditation apps, language learning apps.


**II. February – Love & Connection**

* **Theme:** Romance, Self-Care, Connection
* **Campaign Focus:**  Promote features that help users connect with others or take care of themselves (or their loved ones).
* **Key Activities:**
    * **Valentine’s Day Promotion:** Offer a bundled subscription or discount related to dating, relationships, or self-care.
    * **Social Media Contests:** Run contests and giveaways to increase engagement. (e.g., "Share your love story with our app")
    * **User-Generated Content:** Encourage users to share their experiences with the app using a specific hashtag.
    * **ASO:** Keywords: “Valentine’s Day,” “gifts,” “romance,” “self-care,” “dating app.”
    * **Example App Types:** Dating apps, relationship apps, mindfulness apps, self-care apps.


**III. March – Spring into Action**

* **Theme:**  Renewal, Motivation, Exploration
* **Campaign Focus:** Encourage users to try new things and get outside.
* **Key Activities:**
    * **Spring Launch Promotion:**  Introduce new features or content aligned with the season.
    * **Partnerships:** Collaborate with relevant brands or influencers to promote your app.
    * **User Testimonials:** Share success stories from users who’ve benefited from your app.
    * **ASO:** Keywords: “spring,” “renewal,” “motivation,” “outdoor,” “travel”
    * **Example App Types:** Fitness apps, travel apps, outdoor apps, language learning apps.
