**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Changelog**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is the initial release of Deuce Diary, a journaling application designed to help you track your thoughts, feelings, and experiences. This version introduces the core functionality for creating, editing, and viewing diary entries.

**I. New Features:**

* **Diary Entry Creation:** Successfully added the ability to create new diary entries with a title, date, and a rich text editor for recording your thoughts.

* **Rich Text Editor:**  Implemented a basic rich text editor allowing you to format your entries with bold, italic, and underline. (Limitations apply - see Known Issues).
