**2. Program Mechanics & Rewards:**

* **Referrer Rewards:**
    * **Tiered Rewards (Based on Referrals):**
        * **Level 1 (1-5 Referrals):** 1 Month Premium Subscription (Deuce Diary’s highest tier) - Automatic, immediate delivery upon referee signup.
        * **Level 2 (6-15 Referrals):** $25 Amazon Gift Card - Payable via PayPal or other popular payment methods.

* **Referee Rewards:**
    * **Free 14-Day Premium Trial:** New users who sign up through a referral link receive a full 14-day trial of Deuce Diary Premium.
    * **Welcome Discount:**  Upon completion of the trial, they receive a 20% discount on their first month of Premium subscription. (This encourages conversion)
* **Referral Link:**  Unique, trackable URLs will be generated for each user.


**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Social Sharing Integration:** Encourage sharing through:
    * **Pre-populated Tweets/Facebook Posts:**  Provide users with customizable templates for easy sharing with branded hashtags (#DeuceDiary, #SpreadTheDeuce).

* **Gamification:** Introduce elements like:
    * **Referral Leaderboards (Optional):**  Display top referrers (with their consent) to foster competition and motivation. (Careful consideration needed – can discourage some users)

* **Regular Promotions:**  Announce new referral rewards and highlight success stories within the Deuce Diary community.

* **Limited Referrals Per User:** Cap the number of referrals a single user can generate to prevent abuse.
* **Anomaly Detection:** Use analytics to identify unusual referral patterns that deviate from typical user behavior.
