Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating different levels of complexity and focusing on driving engagement and growth.

**Program Name:** (Let's call it) **"Share the [Your Brand Name] Love!"**

**1. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The person who refers a new customer.
* **New Customer:** The person referred by the referrer and who makes a purchase.
* **Reward Structure:**  This is the heart of the program – tiered rewards based on the referral's success.

**2. Incentive Levels & Rewards (Example - Adapt to Your Business!)**

We’ll use a tiered system to encourage more referrals. Here's a breakdown, with suggested rewards – adjust the value based on your profit margins and target audience:

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy to Achieve)**

* **Trigger:** New Customer makes a purchase of $50 or more.
* **Referrer Reward:**  10% discount on their next purchase. (Value: $5)
* **Goal:** Simple, achievable, gets people comfortable with the referral.

**Tier 2:  Silver Referral (Moderate Effort)**

* **Trigger:** New Customer makes a purchase of $100 or more.
* **Referrer Reward:**  $25 Store Credit + 15% discount on their next purchase. (Value: $37.50)
* **Goal:** Encourage more substantial purchases and deeper engagement.

**Tier 3:  Gold Referral (Highest Effort - Top Performers)**

* **Trigger:** New Customer makes a purchase of $250 or more *and* remains an active customer for 3 months (defined as making at least one purchase per month).
* **Referrer Reward:** $100 Store Credit + Exclusive Access to a Limited-Edition Product/Service/Event. (Value: $100 + Exclusive Benefit)
* **Goal:**  Reward loyal advocates who drive significant revenue and brand awareness.


**3. Referral Methods & Tracking:**

* **Unique Referral Links:** Each referrer receives a unique, trackable link (using a tool like Bitly, ReferralCandy, or built-in tracking within your e-commerce platform).
* **Social Sharing:**  Make it easy for referrers to share – provide pre-written social media posts with branded images and links.
* **Email Referral:**  Send personalized email templates to existing customers with their referral link.
* **In-App Referral (if applicable):** Integrate the referral program directly into your mobile app.
* **Tracking:**  Crucially, you *must* track referrals effectively. This will show you what’s working and where to optimize.



**4. New Customer Incentives (To Encourage Purchases)**

* **New Customer Discount:** 10% off their first order when referred. (This is in addition to the referrer's reward).
* **Welcome Gift:** Consider a small welcome gift (e.g., a sample, discount code) for new customers who come through a referral.

**5.  Terms & Conditions (Important!)**

* **Eligibility:** Clearly define who can participate (e.g., existing customers, new customers).
* **Purchase Requirements:**  Specify the minimum purchase amount for the referrer to receive a reward.
* **Time Limits:**  Set a timeframe for the referral to be valid (e.g., 30 days, 60 days).
* **Account Restrictions:**  State any restrictions (e.g., one referral per customer).
* **Fraud Prevention:**  Include clauses to prevent abuse (e.g., invalid referrals).

**6. Promotion & Communication:**

* **Website Banner:** Prominently display the referral program on your website
