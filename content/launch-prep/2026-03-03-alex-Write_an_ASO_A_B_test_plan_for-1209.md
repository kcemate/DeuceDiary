    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** % of visitors who click on the app icon.

**Testing Framework:**

* **Variable:** We'll test one element at a time, with variations of that element.
* **Control Group:** The existing, current version of the App Store element.
* **Variant Group(s):**  Multiple variations of the element, each with a slightly different approach.
* **Traffic Allocation:**  Start with 50/50 split (Control/Variant) and adjust based on performance.
* **Statistical Significance:**  We’ll use A/B testing tools (like App Annie, Mobile Action, Sensor Tower) to determine statistical significance of the results (usually aiming for 95% confidence).
