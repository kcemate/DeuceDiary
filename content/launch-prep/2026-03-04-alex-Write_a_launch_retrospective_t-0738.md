**I. Reflection & Key Observations (Open Discussion - 15-20 mins)**

* **What Went Well?** (Specifically - don’t just say “the marketing worked” - detail *why* it worked)
    *  *Examples:* “Our onboarding flow was exceptionally clear and led users to the core value quickly.” “The social media campaign drove targeted traffic.” “We were incredibly responsive to early user questions.”
* **What Didn't Go Well?** (Be honest and blame-free - focus on the *situation*, not individuals)
    * *Examples:* "The server struggled under initial load, causing some intermittent delays." “We anticipated a higher conversion rate from the landing page.” “Support tickets were higher than expected regarding X issue.”
* **Unexpected Outcomes?** (Anything that surprised us, either positively or negatively)
    * *Examples:* “We saw a huge spike in usage of Feature Y, which we hadn't planned for.” “Users were consistently abandoning the checkout process at step 3.”
* **Biggest Lessons Learned?** (What will we do differently in the future?)
    * *Examples:* “We need to proactively monitor server capacity for future launches.” “We should refine our landing page copy to better reflect user expectations.”
