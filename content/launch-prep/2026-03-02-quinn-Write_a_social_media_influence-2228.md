Okay, here’s a social media influencer outreach template kit tailored for micro-influencers (health, humor, lifestyle), including DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief. This is designed to be adaptable – *always* personalize it!

**I. DM Templates (Segmented by Niche)**

**A. Health Influencers:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up - 3-5k Followers):** “Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Love your content and how you’re promoting [Specific aspect of their health content - e.g., mindful eating, daily movement]. I’m a [Your Brand] and we’re passionate about [Your Brand’s Mission related to health].  Just wanted to say a quick hello!”
* **Follow-Up DM (After 2-3 days, if no response):** “Hi again [Influencer Name]!  Still a big fan of your posts on [Specific topic].  We're working on [Briefly mention a new product/initiative] and think it could resonate with your audience.  Would you be open to a quick chat?”
* **Offer DM:** “Amazing! We'd love to explore a potential collaboration. We’d love to send you [Product/Service] to try & share your honest feedback/experience with your followers. We're open to ideas for content - perhaps a [Suggested Content Idea - e.g., recipe, workout, self-care tip]?”


**B. Humor Influencers:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up - 3-5k Followers):** “OMG, [Influencer Name]! Your [Specific type of humor - e.g., relatable memes, witty observations] is seriously the best.  I’m [Your Brand] and we're all about bringing a little joy to [Your Brand’s Area – e.g., everyday routines, social media].  Just wanted to send you some good vibes!”
* **Follow-Up DM (After 2-3 days, if no response):** “Hey! Still laughing at your [Specific post/video]. We're launching a new [Product/Campaign] and think it would be hilarious with your style.  Interested in a quick brainstorm?”
* **Offer DM:** “Let's make some magic happen! We'd love to send you [Product/Funny Item] to test out & create some funny content. We’re looking for something that aligns with your brand - maybe a [Suggested Content Idea - e.g., unboxing video, silly challenge, meme creation]?”



**C. Lifestyle Influencers:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up - 5k-15k Followers):** “Hi [Influencer Name]!  Your feed is seriously inspiring - I love how you showcase [Specific aspect of their lifestyle - e.g., travel, home decor, personal style]. I'm [Your Brand] – we’re focused on [Your Brand’s Mission related to lifestyle]”
* **Follow-Up DM (After 2-3 days, if no response):** “Just wanted to say your style is amazing! We're launching [New product/collection] and would love for you to share it with your audience.”
* **Offer DM:** “We’d love to send you [Product/Sample] to try & share your authentic experience with your followers.  We’re open to ideas – a [Suggested Content Idea – e.g., lifestyle photo shoot, product review, story takeover] would be amazing!”

**Important Notes for ALL DM Templates:**

* **Personalize:** Replace the bracketed information with specifics.  *Always* mention something specific you genuinely like about their content.
* **Keep it Short:** Micro-influencers are busy
