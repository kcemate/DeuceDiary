## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for tracking competitive metrics, allowing you to understand your rivals, identify opportunities, and ultimately inform strategic decisions. It’s designed to be adaptable and should be tailored to your specific industry, business, and goals.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To gain a deeper understanding of the competitive landscape and identify actionable insights to improve our performance and achieve [Specific Business Objective - e.g., increase market share, drive revenue growth, improve customer acquisition].
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Regularly monitor competitor activities.
    * Identify emerging trends and threats.
    * Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Inform pricing strategies, marketing campaigns, and product development.
    *  Quantify the impact of competitive actions on our business.


**II. Key Metrics Categories & Specific Metrics:**

We'll track metrics across several categories, each with specific measurements.

**A. Product & Offering:**

* **Feature Tracking:** Track new features launched by competitors (e.g., via press releases, announcements, website updates, app stores).
* **Pricing Changes:** Monitor changes in competitor pricing (e.g., discounts, bundles, subscription tiers).
* **Product Reviews & Ratings:**  Track average ratings and sentiment on review sites (e.g., Trustpilot, Capterra, G2 Crowd, Amazon, Google Play Store).
* **New Product Launches:** Document all new product releases, focusing on key features and target audience.
* **Product Roadmap (Publicly Available):**  Monitor competitor roadmap updates for future product direction.


**B. Marketing & Sales:**

* **Website Traffic & Engagement:**
    *  **Unique Visitors:** Monitor changes in website traffic.
    * **Bounce Rate:** Indicates website relevance and user experience.
    * **Time on Site:** Reflects user engagement.
    * **Conversion Rates:** Track how many visitors become leads or customers.
* **Social Media Activity:**
    * **Follower Growth:** Track changes in follower counts across key platforms.
    * **Engagement Rates (Likes, Shares, Comments):**  Measure the effectiveness of content.
    * **Brand Mentions:**  Monitor how often competitors are talked about (using social listening tools).
    * **Sentiment Analysis:** Analyze the tone of mentions – positive, negative, neutral.
* **Advertising Spend & Campaigns:** (If publicly available through industry reports or media)
    * **Estimated Ad Spend:** Track estimated spending on advertising (e.g., Google Ads, Social Media Ads).
    * **Campaign Types:** Identify the types of campaigns competitors are running.
* **Content Marketing:**  Track the quality and quantity of their blog posts, ebooks, webinars, and other content.


**C. Customer Experience & Brand:**

* **Net Promoter Score (NPS):**  Track changes in customer loyalty and satisfaction.
* **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) Scores:** Measure customer satisfaction with specific interactions.
* **Customer Retention Rate:**  Track the percentage of customers who continue to use your product/service.
* **Churn Rate:** Track the percentage of customers who stop using your product/service.
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Track the cost of acquiring a new customer.
* **Brand Sentiment:**  Ongoing monitoring of brand perception across online channels.


**D. Strategic & Operational:**

* **Mergers & Acquisitions:**  Track any acquisitions or partnerships that could impact the competitive landscape.
* **New Partnerships:** Monitor new partnerships that might shift market dynamics.
* **Funding Rounds:** Track competitor funding rounds – indicates growth potential.
* **Employee Headcount (Publicly Available):**  Can indicate investment in growth and R&D.
