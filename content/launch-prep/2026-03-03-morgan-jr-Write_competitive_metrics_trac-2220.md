**III. Metrics Tracking Categories & Specific Metrics**

We’ll categorize metrics to provide a holistic view.  Here’s a breakdown, broken down by area:

**A. Product/Service Metrics (Most Critical - Weekly/Bi-Weekly Tracking)**

* **Feature Adoption Rate:**  Percentage of users actively using new features (compared to competitors).
* **Net Promoter Score (NPS):**  Measure of customer loyalty and willingness to recommend.
* **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT):** Measure of satisfaction with specific interactions/products.
* **Product Usage Frequency:** How often users engage with core product features.
* **Bug Reporting/Resolution Time:**  How quickly we address user-reported issues (compared to competitors).
* **Product Roadmap Updates:**  Track competitor’s product releases and features – note the strategic implications.

**B. Marketing & Sales Metrics (Monthly/Quarterly Tracking)**

* **Market Share:**  Our percentage of the overall market. (Source: Industry reports, analyst data)
* **Website Traffic:**  Overall traffic, traffic sources, bounce rate (compared to competitors).
* **Lead Generation Cost (CPL):** Cost to acquire a single lead.
* **Conversion Rates:**  Lead-to-opportunity, opportunity-to-customer.
* **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**  Total cost to acquire a new customer.
* **Brand Mentions:** Tracking mentions of our brand and competitor brands across social media, review sites, and industry publications. (Sentiment analysis)
* **Advertising Spend & ROI:** Monitoring competitor’s ad spend and the effectiveness of their campaigns.
* **Search Engine Ranking (SERP):** Monitor rankings for key keywords.


**C. Customer Experience (CX) Metrics (Monthly/Quarterly Tracking)**

* **Customer Churn Rate:**  Percentage of customers who cancel their subscriptions/contracts.
* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):**  Predicted revenue a customer will generate during their relationship with us.
* **Support Ticket Volume & Resolution Time:**  Number of support tickets and the time taken to resolve them.
* **Online Reviews (Trustpilot, G2, etc.):** Track and analyze sentiment.
* **Social Media Engagement:** Likes, shares, comments – gauge brand perception.
