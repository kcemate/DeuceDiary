## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 Changelog - Launch!

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is officially live! This release represents the culmination of our initial development efforts, introducing core features designed to help you track your habits, connect with other users, and motivate yourself on your journey to self-improvement. We're incredibly excited to see you use Deuce Diary and provide feedback as we continue to evolve.

---

**I. Social Features:**

* **Community Feed:** (New) Users can now browse a public feed showcasing diary entries and activity from other Deuce Diary users. This allows for inspiration, shared struggles, and potential support.
    *  Filtering options available based on shared goals.
    *  Ability to 'Like' entries to show support and engagement.
* **Friend Requests & Following:** (New)  Connect with other users! Send friend requests and follow users to see their activity and entries in your personalized feed.
    *  Simple "Follow/Unfollow" functionality.
    *  Notifications for new friend requests and follows.
* **Direct Messaging (Basic):** (New) Start basic conversations with your friends within the app.  (Note:  This is a basic implementation and features will expand in future releases.)
    *  Send text messages.


**II. Tracking Features:**

* **Habit Tracking:** (New) The core functionality - easily add, edit, and remove habits you’re trying to build.
    *  Customizable Habit Names & Descriptions.
    *  Daily Check-in Mechanism: Mark habits as complete for the day.
    *  Flexible Scheduling: Set daily, weekly, or custom frequency for habits.
* **Journaling:** (New) Record your thoughts, feelings, and reflections related to your habits in a dedicated journal.
    *  Rich Text Editor: Format your entries with basic text formatting (bold, italics, lists).
    *  Tagging System:  Add tags to your journal entries to categorize your reflections (e.g., #Motivation, #Challenge, #Progress).
* **Streak Tracking:** (New)  Automatic tracking of your consecutive days completing habits.  Streaks are visualized to motivate continued progress.
* **Progress Visualization:** (New) See your progress at a glance through visual charts tracking habit completion over time.
    *  Simple Line Charts for tracking daily completion.


**III. Gamification Features:**

* **Badges:** (New) Earn badges for achieving milestones, streaks, and consistent habit tracking. These badges provide a visual reward for your efforts.
    *  "First Habit Completed" Badge
    *  "7-Day Streak" Badge
    *  "100 Entries" Badge (and more to come!)
* **Points System:** (New)  Gain points for completing habits and contributing to the community (liking entries). Points contribute to a basic leaderboard (currently displaying top 10 users based on points).
* **Leaderboard:** (New)  See how you rank against other users based on points earned. (Currently only shows top 10 - will be expanded)


**IV. UI/UX Improvements:**

* **Modernized Design:** (New)  A complete redesign of the user interface for a cleaner, more intuitive experience.
* **Dark Mode:** (New) Added a Dark Mode option for comfortable viewing in low-light environments.
* **Improved Navigation:** (New) Streamlined navigation with a bottom tab bar for easy access to key sections.
* **Responsive Design:**  (Improved) The app is designed to adapt to different screen sizes and resolutions for optimal viewing on various devices.
