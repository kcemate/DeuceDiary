**I. DM Templates (Segmented by Niche)**

**A. Health Micro-Influencers**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up - 3-5 days after finding their profile):**
   "Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Just came across your amazing content on [Platform] – seriously, your [specific post/video you liked] really resonated with me. Huge fan of your focus on [mention something specific they do - e.g., mindful eating, fitness routines, mental wellness]."

* **Offer DM (2-3 days after initial DM - if they respond positively):**
   "We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re passionate about [briefly state your brand's mission - e.g., empowering healthy lifestyles]. We’d love to explore a potential collaboration! We think your audience would genuinely love our [product/service]. Would you be open to chatting for 15-20 mins about a possible partnership?"

* **Follow-Up DM (If no response after 3 days):**
    "Just wanted to check in – no pressure at all!  We completely understand if you're busy. Still, we really admire your work and think we could be a good fit. 😊"


**B. Humor Micro-Influencers**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up):**
   "OMG, [Influencer Name]! 😂 Your [specific funny post/video] had me dying! You’ve got a seriously great sense of humor.  Love the vibe you’re creating."

* **Offer DM:**
   "We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re building a brand that’s as funny as you are! We're creating [briefly describe what you do - e.g., quirky gadgets, fun accessories, relatable merch] and think your audience would kill for a laugh.  Would you be interested in a fun collaboration?"

* **Follow-Up DM:**
    "Just sending a little reminder – we’re genuinely hoping to bring some smiles to your audience's faces! 😄"


**C. Lifestyle Micro-Influencers**

* **Initial DM (Warm-Up):**
   “Hi [Influencer Name]! Your feed is seriously dreamy – I love your style and the way you share [mention a specific aspect - e.g., travel adventures, home decor, morning routines].  It’s so inspiring!”

* **Offer DM:**
   "We're [Your Brand Name], and we create [briefly describe your product/service - e.g., sustainable products, curated lifestyle boxes, digital courses] that align with that beautiful aesthetic. We believe your followers would appreciate our [product/service]!"

* **Follow-Up DM:**
    “Just wanted to say we're really impressed with your brand – hope you’re having a great week!”
