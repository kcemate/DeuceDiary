**2. Definitions**

* **Incident:** Any event that deviates from the normal operation of an IT system or process, potentially compromising confidentiality, integrity, or availability.
* **Severity Levels:**
    * **P0 (Critical):**  Imminent, widespread disruption with significant business impact.  Requires immediate, full-scale response.
    * **P1 (High):** Significant disruption or potential for significant disruption, impacting critical systems or sensitive data. Requires urgent, coordinated response.
    * **P2 (Medium):** Limited disruption, potential impact on non-critical systems or data, or requiring investigation for potential escalation. Requires timely response and monitoring.
