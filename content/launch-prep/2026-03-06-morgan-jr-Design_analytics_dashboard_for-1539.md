Okay, let's design an analytics dashboard for the Deuce Diary launch metrics. This design focuses on key performance indicators (KPIs) to track success, identify areas for improvement, and inform future marketing and content strategies.

**I. Overall Dashboard Goal:**

To provide a real-time and historical overview of Deuce Diary’s launch performance, allowing the team to quickly understand user adoption, engagement, and revenue potential.

**II. Dashboard Sections & KPIs:**

The dashboard will be divided into several sections, each focusing on a specific aspect of the launch:

**1. Acquisition & Awareness:** (Top Left - High Level)
   * **KPIs:**
      * **Website Visits:** Total number of visits over time (daily, weekly, monthly) – *Track source (organic search, paid ads, social media) for insights.*
      * **New User Sign-Ups:** Total number of new users registering.
      * **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):**  (Total Marketing Spend / Number of New Users) - *Crucial for evaluating campaign efficiency.*
      * **Channel Breakdown:** Percentage of traffic/sign-ups from each marketing channel (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Google Ads, Influencer Marketing, PR).
      * **Impressions & Reach:**  (For paid ads and social media) - *Measures exposure.*
   * **Visualization:** Line chart (Website Visits), Pie Chart (Channel Breakdown),  Number widgets for other KPIs.

**2. Early Engagement & Activity:** (Center - User Behavior)
   * **KPIs:**
      * **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Number of unique users who interact with the diary daily.
      * **Weekly Active Users (WAU):** Number of unique users who interact with the diary weekly.
      * **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** Number of unique users who interact with the diary monthly.
      * **Session Duration:** Average time spent in the diary per session.
      * **Entries Created:** Total number of diary entries created.
      * **Entry Length (Average):**  Average word count per entry. *Provides insight into depth of reflection.*
      * **Feature Usage:**  Tracking usage of key features: (e.g., Mood tracking, Journal Prompts, Photo Integration, Sharing). *Identify which features are driving engagement.*
   * **Visualization:** Line charts (DAU, WAU, MAU over time), Bar charts (Feature Usage), Heatmap (Session Duration vs. Entry Length) –  *Allows for correlations.*

**3. Retention & Churn:** (Bottom Left - User Loyalty)
   * **KPIs:**
      * **Retention Rate:** Percentage of users who return to the diary after a specific period (e.g., 7-day, 30-day, 90-day).  *Critical for long-term success.*
      * **Churn Rate:**  (1 - Retention Rate) -  *Indicates how many users are leaving.*
      * **Time Since Last Activity:**  Average time elapsed since a user’s last diary entry. *Predictive indicator of churn.*
   * **Visualization:** Line charts (Retention Rate over time), Cohort Analysis (Retention by signup month) – *Reveals patterns in user stickiness.*
