Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, incorporating various tiers and considerations to make it engaging and effective:

**1. Program Name:** (Choose something memorable and relevant to your brand)

*   **Example:** “Spread the [Your Brand] Love,” “Refer & Reward,” “The [Your Brand] Circle”


**2. Core Structure & Tiers:**

We’ll use a tiered system to motivate increasing levels of referrals.

*   **Tier 1: New Referrer (Level 1)**
    *   **Reward:**  10% Discount on their next purchase *or* $10 Store Credit.
    *   **Trigger:** First successful referral.
    *   **Easy to Achieve:** Focuses on initial engagement.
*   **Tier 2: Active Referrer (Level 2 - 3 Referrals)**
    *   **Reward:** 15% Discount on their next purchase *or* $25 Store Credit *plus* a small, exclusive gift (e.g., a branded sticker, small accessory related to your product).
    *   **Trigger:**  Referrer successfully refers 3 people who make a purchase.
    *   **Increased Value:**  Recognizes commitment and active participation.
*   **Tier 3:  Loyal Advocate (Level 5+ Referrals)**
    *   **Reward:** 20% Discount on their next purchase *or* $50 Store Credit *plus* an exclusive, higher-value item (e.g., limited-edition product, early access to new releases, VIP experience - if applicable).
    *   **Trigger:** Referrer successfully refers 5+ people who make a purchase.
    *   **Highest Commitment:**  Rewards brand advocates and drives significant growth.



**3. Incentive Breakdown & Value:**

| Tier            | Reward             | Value (Approximate) |

*Note:* The specific values will need to be tailored to your profit margins, product costs, and perceived value to customers.  Consider using a combination of discounts and gift value.

**4. Referral Process (How it Works):**

*   **Easy Sharing:** Provide pre-written referral links and social media copy. Make it *extremely* easy for users to share.
    *   **Website Banner:**  A prominent “Refer a Friend” banner.
    *   **Email Template:**  Automated email to existing customers with a personalized referral link.
    *   **Social Media Posts:**  Provide suggested posts for users to share on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter. (Include branded images/GIFs)
    *   **Unique Referral Links:**  Each user should get a unique link.  This is crucial for tracking.
*   **Tracking:** Use a referral program platform (see options below) or integrate with your e-commerce system.
*   **Notification System:**  Automatically notify both the referrer and the new customer when a referral is made.
*   **Redemption Process:**  Simple instructions on how to redeem the referral discount or credit.
