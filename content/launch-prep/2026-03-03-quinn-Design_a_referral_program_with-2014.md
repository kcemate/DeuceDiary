**1. Core Mechanics & Structure**

* **Referrer:** The existing customer who refers a new customer.
* **Referee:** The new customer who joins through the referrer’s link.
* **Tracking:**  Crucially, you *need* a robust system to track referrals. This could be:
    * **Dedicated Referral Software:** (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Mention) - These offer the most features and automation.
    * **Custom Tracking URL:** (Shorten URLs like Bitly and track clicks) - This is a good, cost-effective starting point.
    * **Spreadsheet (Initially):** For very small businesses, a simple spreadsheet can work.

**2. Incentive Levels (Tiered System - Highly Recommended)**

This is key to motivation.  Offer escalating rewards.

* **Level 1:  The "Thank You" (For the Referrer)** – *Instant Reward*
    * **Reward:** Small discount (e.g., 5-10% off their next purchase, $5 credit).  This is immediate and shows appreciation.
* **Level 2: The "Welcome Bonus" (For the Referee)** – *Awarded Upon Sign-Up*
    * **Reward:**  More significant discount (e.g., 15-20% off their first order), free shipping, or a small free product/service. This incentivizes the new customer to actually use the referral.
* **Level 3: The "Double Win" (For Both)** – *Awarded After a Specific Action*
    * **Reward:**  Both referrer and referee receive a larger reward (e.g., 20-30% off, a higher-value product, extended trial period) *after* the referee completes a specific action – like making their first purchase, using the service for a certain period, or referring someone else. This encourages continued engagement.

**3. Incentive Types (Variety is Good!)**

* **Discounts:** The most common, but can be effective.
* **Credit/Store Credit:**  Flexible, allowing customers to choose what they want.
* **Free Products/Services:**  Great for businesses with a natural upsell opportunity. (e.g., a skincare brand giving a free travel-sized product).
* **Extended Trials:**  Ideal for SaaS or subscription-based businesses.
* **Early Access:**  Give referrers priority access to new products or features.
* **Exclusive Content/Experiences:**  For brands with valuable content or events, offering access can be highly desirable. (e.g., VIP access to a webinar, exclusive behind-the-scenes content)
* **Charitable Donation:**  Option for users to donate a portion of the reward to a chosen charity (appeals to socially conscious customers).



**4. Referral Link/Code Generation**

* **Unique Links:**  Each referrer needs a unique link or code.
* **Easy Sharing:**  Make it *extremely* easy for referrers to share:
    * **Direct Link:**  Shortened, trackable link.
    * **Social Media Sharing Buttons:**  Pre-populated messages and images for easy sharing on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram.
    * **Email Signature:**  Include a referral link in their email signature.
    * **QR Code:**  For physical locations.
