**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introductory (Focus: Building Trust & Brand Awareness - Ideal for New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, establish credibility, and gently guide new subscribers towards your key offerings.
* **Frequency:** 3-5 emails over 7-14 days.
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Immediately after signup):**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand]! 🎉
        * **Content:**  Thank you for subscribing! Briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values.  Offer a small immediate reward (e.g., a downloadable guide, discount code, free shipping on their first order).  Include a friendly welcome message.
        * **Call to Action (CTA):** “Learn More About Us” (links to your “About Us” page) & “Redeem Your Discount”
    * **Email 2 (3-4 days later):**
        * **Subject:**  [Problem Your Product Solves] – We've Got You Covered!
        * **Content:**  Address a common pain point your product or service solves.  Share a relevant statistic or brief story. Position your brand as the solution.
        * **CTA:** “Discover How We Can Help” (links to a relevant blog post or product category)
    * **Email 3 (7-10 days later):**
        * **Subject:**  A Quick Tip for [Related Topic]
        * **Content:**  Provide a valuable, short tip related to your industry or product.  This demonstrates your expertise and builds trust.
        * **CTA:**  "Read Our Blog Post" (links to a helpful blog post)
    * **Email 4 (Optional - 14 days later):**
        * **Subject:**  Still Thinking About It?  Here's a Reminder...
        * **Content:**  Gentle reminder of the initial offer.  Highlight a key benefit or testimonial.
        * **CTA:** "Claim Your Discount Now!"
