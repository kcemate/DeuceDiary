**Q1: January - February (New Year, Resolutions)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Motivation
* **Campaign Types:**
    * **January - “New Year, New You” Push:** Focus on motivation, self-improvement, and achieving goals.  Limited-time offers for premium features or subscriptions. Run a contest with a relevant prize. (High-Impact, Short-Term)
    * **February - “Love Your [App Category]” Campaign:**  (e.g., “Love Your Fitness,” “Love Your Learning”). Highlight benefits related to relationships, self-care, or shared experiences facilitated by your app. Feature user testimonials. (Mid-Impact, Mid-Term)
* **Activities:**
    * Launch New Year’s themed App Store preview video.
    * Create blog posts and social media content around relevant topics (e.g., “Top 5 Fitness Resolutions,” “How to Learn a New Language”).
    * Run targeted Facebook/Instagram ads based on interests like health, fitness, self-improvement, learning.


**Q2: March - May (Spring, Productivity, Easter)**

* **Theme:** Renewal, Growth, Community
* **Campaign Types:**
    * **March - “Spring Into It” Launch/Promotion:** Focus on seasonal relevance (e.g., outdoor activities, gardening apps).  Promote features that align with the new season. (Mid-Impact, Mid-Term)
    * **April – Easter Campaign:**  (If relevant) –  Highlight family-friendly features or create themed content.  Consider a special Easter-themed offer. (Low-Impact, Short-Term)
    * **May – “Productivity Power Up”:**  Capitalize on back-to-school/work trends.  Promote features that help users organize, manage time, and achieve their goals. (Mid-Impact, Mid-Term)
* **Activities:**
    * Updated screenshots showcasing spring-themed content.
    * Run contests and giveaways encouraging user-generated content.
    * Blog posts and social media content about productivity tips.



**Q3: June - August (Summer, Travel, Vacation)**

* **Theme:** Adventure, Relaxation, Experiences
* **Campaign Types:**
    * **June – “Summer Vibes” Launch:**  Highlight features that support summer activities – travel apps, outdoor fitness apps, recipe apps, etc. (High-Impact, Mid-Term)
    * **July - “Travel Smart” Campaign:** (Especially relevant for travel apps). Run promotions linked to travel discounts or partnerships.  (Mid-Impact, Mid-Term)
