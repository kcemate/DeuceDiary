**I. Spring (March - May): Focus - Renewal & Fresh Start**

* **Overall Theme:** Renewal, Re-Engagement, New Beginnings, Seasonal Activities
* **March - Early Spring (March 1st - March 31st): "Spring into Action"**
    * **Activities:**
        * **Re-Engagement Campaigns:**  Send emails and push notifications to inactive users offering special deals or reminding them of the app’s value. (Low - Email/Push)
        * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Refresh app screenshots and descriptions to highlight spring-related features. (Low - ASO)
        * **Promotional Offer:** 10-20% discount on in-app purchases. (Medium - Paid Social/Apple Search Ads)
    * **App-Specific Examples:**
        * **Fitness Apps:** Promote outdoor running/walking challenges.
        * **Productivity Apps:**  “Spring Cleaning” –  focus on decluttering tasks and goals.
        * **Travel Apps:**  Highlight spring destinations and travel deals.
* **April - Mid-Spring (April 1st - April 30th): "Easter & Outdoor Adventures"**
    * **Activities:**
        * **Easter Promotion:** Run a special offer (e.g., extra eggs, bonus features) around Easter. (Medium - Paid Social)
        * **ASO:**  Leverage Easter-related keywords.
    * **App-Specific Examples:**
        * **Gaming Apps:** Seasonal in-game events/challenges.
        * **Photography Apps:**  “Spring Colors” – themes & filters.
* **May - Late Spring (May 1st - May 31st): “May Flowers & New Opportunities”**
    * **Activities:**
        * **User-Generated Content (UGC) Campaign:** Encourage users to share their experiences using the app with a specific hashtag. (Low - Social Media/Contests)
        * **ASO:** Focus on “May” keywords, user reviews, and ratings. (Low – ASO)
    * **App-Specific Examples:**
        * **Learning Apps:** New courses/lessons aligned with spring themes.


**II. Summer (June - August): Focus - Fun & Experiences**

* **Overall Theme:**  Summer Fun, Vacation, Outdoor Activities, Relaxation
* **June - Early Summer (June 1st - June 30th): “Summer Kick-Off”**
    * **Activities:**
        * **Launch Seasonal Content:** Introduce summer-themed features or challenges (new levels, items, etc.). (Medium - App Updates/Marketing)
        * **Paid Social:** Run targeted ads to people interested in summer activities. (Medium - Paid Social)
* **July - Mid-Summer (July 1st - July 31st): "Summer Adventures"**
