**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introductory (Focus: Building Trust & Brand Awareness - 5-7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, provide value, and nurture a new subscriber.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who’ve signed up via your website, landing page, or social media.
* **Sequence Length:** 5-7 Emails
* **Email 1: (Immediately - Day 0)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:** Thank you for subscribing! Briefly introduce your brand – your mission, what you offer, and why you’re excited to have them on board. Include a warm welcome message and a link to your most popular resource (e.g., blog post, free guide, introductory video).
    * **Call to Action:**  "Explore our latest blog post: [Link]"
* **Email 2: (Day 2-3)**
    * **Subject:**  [Problem Your Product Solves] – We’ve Got You Covered
    * **Content:**  Address a common problem your target audience faces.  Show you understand their pain points.  Briefly introduce how your product/service offers a solution (without heavy sales pitch).
    * **Call to Action:** “Read more about [Problem] here: [Link to relevant blog post/resource]”
* **Email 3: (Day 4-5)**
    * **Subject:** Meet the Team (Behind the Scenes)
    * **Content:** Humanize your brand! Share a short story about your team, the values you hold, or a glimpse into your company culture.  Builds trust and connection.
    * **Call to Action:** “Learn more about our mission: [Link to About Us page]”
* **Email 4 (Optional - Day 6-7):**
    * **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out! [Limited-Time Offer – if applicable]
    * **Content:** Introduce a small, low-pressure offer (e.g., discount code, free shipping) to incentivize engagement. (Only use if you have a relevant offer.)
    * **Call to Action:** “Claim your [Discount/Offer] now: [Link to product page]”
