**Program Name:** (Choose something relevant to your brand – e.g., "Boost the Buzz," "Refer & Reward," "Share the Love") – Let’s call it “Refer & Reward” for this example.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Increase User Acquisition:** Primary goal is to bring in new customers.
* **Boost Brand Awareness:** Referral programs organically spread word-of-mouth.
* **Improve Customer Retention:**  Existing customers are more likely to stick around if they’re incentivized to refer.
* **Reduce Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Referrals are often cheaper than traditional marketing.

**2. Referral Structure:**

* **Referrer (The Person Referring):**  Someone who recommends your product/service to a new customer.
* **New Customer (The Referred Person):** Someone who signs up and makes a purchase as a result of the referral.
* **Tracking:** Crucially, you *must* have a robust system to track referrals. This likely involves:
    * **Unique Referral Links:** Each referrer gets a unique link they can share.  These links are essential for tracking. (Use a service like ReferralCandy, Ambassador, or build your own with a tracking URL shortener).
    * **Promo Codes:**  Generate unique promo codes for each referrer.
    * **Database Integration:** Connect your referral system with your CRM/database to track progress.


**3. Incentives – The Core of the Program (Tiered Approach)**

This is where you differentiate your program and drive engagement.  Consider these tiers:

* **Tier 1:  Basic Referral (For Both Referrer & New Customer)** – *Automatic Rewards*
    * **New Customer:** 10% discount on their first purchase. (This is a standard and attractive welcome offer).
    * **Referrer:**  Receive a small thank you gift (e.g., a $5 credit to their account) upon the new customer's first purchase.
* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level (Triggered by New Customer Purchase)** – *Bonus Rewards*
    * **Referrer:**  Receive a 15% discount on their *next* purchase after the new customer's first purchase.  (This encourages repeat business).
    * **New Customer:**  The initial 10% discount.
* **Tier 3:  High-Level (For Significant Referrals or High-Value Referrals)** – *Premium Rewards* (Consider limiting these – they’re the most expensive)
    * **Referrer:**  After 3 successful referrals, receive a gift card (e.g., $50 Amazon gift card), or a higher-tier product upgrade, or a premium service level for a month.
    * **New Customer:** Initial 10% discount.

**4. Incentive Values - Example Ranges (Adjust based on your business)**

* **Small Credits/Discounts:** $5 - $10
* **Gift Cards:** $25 - $50
* **Premium Product/Service:** (e.g., upgraded subscription level) – Value dependent on your offering.
