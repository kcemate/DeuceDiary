**1. Program Name & Theme:** "Spread the Deuce" - Playful, memorable, and encourages sharing.

**2. Mechanics & Rewards:**

* **Referrer (Existing User):**
    * **Tiered Reward Structure:**
        * **Level 1 (1 Referral):**  Receive 1 month of Premium access.
        * **Level 2 (3 Referrals):** Receive 3 months of Premium access.
        * **Level 3 (5 Referrals):** Receive 6 months of Premium access.
    * **Bonus Reward (Monthly - All Referrers):** Top 10 referrers (based on number of successful referrals) receive a small one-time gift (e.g., $5 Deuce Diary merchandise, digital art pack).
* **Referee (New User):**
    * **Automatic 1 Month Free Premium Trial:**  New users who sign up through a referral link receive an automatic one-month free trial of Premium access.
* **Referral Link Generation:** Unique, trackable links generated within the Deuce Diary app/website.

**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Shareable Content:**  Encourage users to share content they create in Deuce Diary (photos, journals, etc.) directly via social media with a customized share link (generated by the app).  This naturally incorporates the referral link.
* **In-App Prompt:**  At specific points (e.g., completing a journal entry, reaching a milestone), users are prompted with: "Loved your diary? Share Deuce Diary with a friend and get rewarded!"
* **Email & Push Notifications:**  Automated emails & push notifications will remind referrers about their referral progress and highlight the rewards.
* **Social Media Campaign (Launch Week):**  Run targeted ads promoting the referral program on platforms where Deuce Diary’s audience spends time (Instagram, Pinterest, TikTok).

* **IP Address Tracking:** Monitor referrals originating from the same IP address.  Set a threshold (e.g., 3 referrals from the same IP) and flag for manual review.
