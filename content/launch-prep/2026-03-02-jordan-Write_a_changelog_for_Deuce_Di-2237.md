**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 – Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Overview:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 marks the official launch of our productivity journal app, designed to help you track habits, reflect on your day, and build a more intentional life. This release includes a wide range of features to support your journey to self-improvement.

**I. Social Features**

*   **Community Sharing (Beta):**  Users can now optionally share their daily entries (text and journal image) with the Deuce Diary community.  *Note:* This is a beta feature and moderation will be ongoing.  Sharing is disabled by default for all new users. (Settings -> Community Sharing - Toggle On/Off)
*   **Friend Connections:**  Ability to connect with friends within the Deuce Diary ecosystem.  Send encouragement, view their shared entries (with their permission), and participate in group challenges (coming soon!).
*   **Private Messaging (Placeholder):**  A placeholder feature for future private messaging capabilities. Currently, this area is functional but doesn't connect to other users. (Placeholder UI only)

**II. Tracking Features**

*   **Habit Tracking:** Core functionality for tracking habits.
    *   **Custom Habits:** Add new habits with customizable names, icons, and tracking frequency (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Specific Days).
    *   **Habit Categories:** Group habits into categories for better organization (e.g., Health, Productivity, Personal Growth).
    *   **Streak Tracking:**  Automatic calculation and display of current streak for each habit.
    *   **Habit Completion Logging:** Simple tap-based logging of habit completion.
*   **Mood Tracking:**  Log your daily mood with a visual mood selector (Emoji Scale).
*   **Journal Prompts:**  A library of daily prompts to stimulate reflection and deeper journaling. Users can also add their own prompts.
*   **Time Tracking:**  Integrated timer functionality for tracking how long you spend on tasks.  Save this data alongside your journal entries.
*   **Entry Notes:** Freeform text field within each daily entry for detailed note-taking.


**III. Gamification Features**

*   **Daily Streaks:**  Reward users for maintaining consistent habit tracking with streak badges and visual progress.
*   **Milestone Badges:** Awarded for achieving specific milestones (e.g., 7-day streak, 30-day journal habit).
*   **Challenge System (Placeholder):**  A placeholder for future group challenges and competitions focused on habit completion.  The UI exists, but no actual challenges are currently available.
*   **Points System:** Earn points for completing habits and journaling. Points contribute to unlocking visual rewards and badges.

**IV. UI/UX Improvements**

*   **Redesigned Dashboard:**  A cleaner, more intuitive dashboard displaying key metrics (current streak, today’s mood, upcoming habits).
*   **Improved Navigation:** Streamlined navigation with a bottom navigation bar for easy access to core features.
*   **Dark Mode:** Introduced a Dark Mode option for comfortable viewing in low-light conditions (Settings -> Appearance).
*   **Entry Preview:**  A quick preview of the current daily entry before saving.
*   **Improved Typography:**  Enhanced readability with a consistent and modern font selection.
*   **Accessibility Improvements:**  Increased contrast ratios and improved support for screen readers.
