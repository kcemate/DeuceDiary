## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to systematically track and analyze your competitors' activities, enabling you to make informed decisions and maintain a competitive advantage.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To proactively understand competitor strategies, identify opportunities and threats, and inform our own strategic decision-making.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Monitor competitor pricing and promotions.
    * Track new product launches and feature updates.
    * Analyze competitor marketing campaigns and messaging.
    * Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Identify potential market shifts and emerging trends.


**II. Competitor Selection:**

* **Define "Competitive":** Clearly outline what constitutes a competitor for your business. This will vary depending on your industry and product/service.
* **Tiered Approach:** Segment competitors into tiers based on their relevance:
    * **Tier 1 (Direct):** Companies offering similar products/services targeting the same customer segment. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi)
    * **Tier 2 (Indirect):** Companies offering alternative solutions that fulfill similar customer needs. (e.g., Netflix vs. Hulu)
    * **Tier 3 (Emerging):**  Newer companies or disruptive entrants with potential to impact the market.
* **Initial List:** Start with a list of 5-10 competitors, then refine it as you gather more data.

**III. Data Collection Methods & Metrics:**

| **Category**          | **Data Source**           | **Metrics to Track**                                  | **Frequency**     | **Tools**                        |
|-----------------------|--------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|--------------------|---------------------------------|
| **Website & Online Presence** | Competitor Website, Social Media, Google Search | Website Traffic, Bounce Rate, Conversion Rates, Top Keywords, Social Media Engagement,  Reviews,  Blog Posts, Content Quality, Landing Page Design | Weekly/Monthly     | SEMrush, Ahrefs, Google Analytics, Social Media Analytics, Mention.com,  SimilarWeb |
| **Pricing & Promotions** | Competitor Website, Retailers, Coupons Sites | Prices, Discounts, Coupons, Bundled Offers, Payment Options      | Weekly/Monthly     | Price Tracker Tools (e.g., Honey, CamelCamelCamel),  Retailer Websites |

| **Marketing & Sales**  | Competitor Website, Advertising, Events | Advertising Spend, Channels Used, Messaging, Campaign Themes, Event Attendance,  Sales Tactics,  Lead Generation  | Monthly/Quarterly  | AdBeat, SpyFu, Eventbrite, LinkedIn Sales Navigator |
| **Customer Sentiment** | Social Media, Review Sites, Forums | Brand Mentions, Sentiment Analysis (Positive/Negative), Customer Feedback,  Support Tickets | Weekly/Monthly     | Brand Monitoring Tools (Mention.com, Sprout Social),  Review Analysis Tools |



**IV.  Tracking & Reporting:**

* **Centralized Dashboard:**  Create a central repository for all collected data – a spreadsheet, database, or dedicated competitive intelligence platform.
* **Automated Reporting:**  Utilize tools to automate data collection and reporting wherever possible (e.g., Google Alerts, social media monitoring tools).
* **Regular Reporting Schedule:**
    * **Weekly:** Website Traffic, Social Media Engagement, Key Price Changes
    * **Monthly:** Detailed competitor analysis, marketing campaign summaries, new product launches
    * **Quarterly:**  Strategic review -  Summarize key findings, identify trends, and adjust tracking priorities.
