Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencers, with variations to suit different levels of formality and goals.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template**

**Subject: Invitation to Test [Product Name] - Help Us Shape the Future!**

**Option 1: Formal & Detailed**

```email
Dear [Beta Tester Name],

We’re thrilled to invite you to join our beta program for [Product Name], a [brief description of product and its purpose - e.g., new productivity app, innovative fitness tracker, upcoming mobile game].  We're incredibly excited about this project and value your feedback immensely.

As a beta tester, you’ll have exclusive access to [Product Name] and the opportunity to help us identify and fix any bugs, improve the user experience, and ensure it meets our vision.

**Here’s what you’ll be doing:**

*   Using [Product Name] for [duration - e.g., 2 weeks, a month].
*   Completing a short daily/weekly questionnaire to document your experience. (Link: [Link to Questionnaire])
*   Reporting any bugs or issues you encounter through [Bug Reporting System - e.g., Jira, Google Form, dedicated email address].
*   Providing honest and constructive feedback.

**To get started:** [Link to access product/instructions].

We’ll be in touch to schedule a brief welcome call to answer any questions you may have.

Thank you for your time and support!

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```

**Option 2:  More Casual & Enthusiastic**

```email
Hey [Beta Tester Name],

We're so excited to get you involved in the beta testing of [Product Name]!  We're building [brief, exciting description of product] and we need your help to make it amazing.

We'd love for you to try it out and let us know what you think - any feedback, big or small, is super valuable.

**Here's the deal:** You'll get early access to [Product Name] and we'll be asking you to:

*   Check it out and use it for [duration].
*   Let us know if anything doesn't work, or if you have any ideas for improvement.
*   (Link to Questionnaire/Bug Reporting)

Let us know if you're in! [Link to access product/instructions]

Cheers,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```
