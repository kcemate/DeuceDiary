Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each tailored for a different goal and with varying lengths. I’ve included details on triggers, content, and estimated length for each.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (7 Days - Ideal for New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:** Build initial brand awareness, establish trust, and encourage engagement.
* **Trigger:** Immediately upon signup (name, email).
* **Segments:** All new subscribers.

**Sequence:**

* **Day 1: Welcome Email (“We’re So Glad You’re Here!”)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:**  Warm welcome, thank you for subscribing, brief brand story (1-2 sentences), highlight the value they’ll get from your content (e.g., exclusive tips, discounts, early access). Include a visually appealing image or GIF.  Link to your website homepage.
    * **CTA:**  “Explore Our Website”
* **Day 3:  Problem/Solution Email (“Are You Struggling With…?”)**
    * **Subject:**  Solving [Pain Point Related to Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Identify a common problem your target audience faces. Briefly explain the problem and position your brand as the solution.  Use relatable language.
    * **CTA:** “Learn How We Can Help” (links to a relevant blog post, case study, or product page)
* **Day 5:  Social Proof Email (“Don’t Just Take Our Word For It!”)**
    * **Subject:**  What Others Are Saying About [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:**  Share a short testimonial, positive review snippet, or highlight a success story.  Include a link to your social media profiles.
    * **CTA:** "Follow Us on [Social Media]"
* **Day 7:  Special Offer Email (“Exclusive Offer for New Subscribers”)**
    * **Subject:**  Your Welcome Gift: [Discount Code or Freebie]
    * **Content:**  Offer a small incentive to encourage a first purchase or further engagement (e.g., 10% off, free shipping, a small ebook).  Make it time-sensitive.
    * **CTA:** “Claim Your Discount”


**Campaign 2:  Lead Nurturing – Product Focused (14 Days - Ideal for Prospects Interested in a Specific Product)**

* **Goal:** Educate prospects about a specific product, address objections, and move them closer to a sale.
* **Trigger:** Subscriber joins a list specifically targeted to a particular product or category.
* **Segments:**  Subscribers interested in [Specific Product Name].

**Sequence:**

* **Days 1-3: Feature Highlight Emails (“Discover the Power of [Product Feature]”)**
    * **Subject:**  Introducing [Key Feature] – You Won’t Want to Miss This!
    * **Content:** Deep dive into a single key feature of your product. Explain its benefits and how it solves a problem. Use screenshots or short video snippets.
* **Days 4-7:  Use Case/Benefit Emails (“How [Product] Can Help You Achieve…”)**
    * **Subject:**  [Scenario Related to Product Use] – Solve It with [Product]
    * **Content:**  Present a realistic scenario where your product would be valuable. Walk through how it’s used and the resulting benefits.
* **Days 8-11:  Addressing Objections Email (“We Know What You’re Thinking…”)**
    * **Subject:**  Common Questions About [Product] – Answered
    * **Content:**  Address common concerns or objections potential customers might have (e.g., price, complexity, integration).
