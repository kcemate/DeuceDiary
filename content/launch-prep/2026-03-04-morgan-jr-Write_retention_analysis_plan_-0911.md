**2. Data Collection & Tracking:**

* **Key Metrics:**

    * **Churn Reasons (Collected Through Surveys & Support):**  Categorize reasons for users leaving (e.g., confusing UI, lack of value, technical issues).
    * **Cohort Analysis:** Segment users based on signup date (cohort) to track retention trends over time.
* **Data Sources:**
    * **Product Analytics Platforms:** (e.g., Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase Analytics) – Track user behavior.
    * **CRM System:** (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot) –  Store user information and interaction history.
