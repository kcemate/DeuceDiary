## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Purpose:** To outline procedures and responsibilities for managing potential crises during the launch of "Deuce Diary," ensuring swift, coordinated, and effective response to mitigate damage and maintain a positive brand image.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be upfront and honest with your audience. Attempting to hide or downplay issues will backfire.
* **Speed:** A rapid response demonstrates you’re taking the situation seriously.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge the concerns of your audience and demonstrate you understand their frustration.
* **Accountability:** Take ownership of the issue, even if it wasn’t entirely your fault.
* **Communication:** Consistent, clear, and documented communication is crucial.


**II. Potential Crisis Scenarios & Response Protocols:**

**Scenario 1: Technical Issues (Website Downtime, Payment Problems, App Glitches)**

* **Severity:** High - Directly impacts user experience and potential sales.
* **Timeline:** Immediate (Within 5 minutes)
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **IT Lead (Liam):**  Identify root cause, initiate immediate fixes, provide status updates every 15 minutes.
    * **Community Manager (Chloe):**  Acknowledge issues on all social media channels - “We’re aware of the issues and our team is working hard to resolve them. We appreciate your patience.” - Monitor sentiment and respond to user concerns.
    * **Marketing Lead (Sam):**  Prepare a brief explanation for broader communication – “We’re experiencing technical difficulties that are impacting [specific issue]. We're committed to resolving this as quickly as possible and will provide further updates.”
* **Communication Channels:**
    * **Twitter:**  Frequent, real-time updates.
    * **Facebook:**  Announcements, updates, and response to comments.
    * **Discord (if applicable):** Dedicated channel for rapid communication and support.
    * **Website Banner:**  Clear message indicating the issue and estimated resolution time.

**Scenario 2: Negative User Feedback (Content Concerns, Controversial Themes)**

* **Severity:** Medium - Can damage brand reputation and lead to significant backlash.
* **Timeline:** Immediate (Within 15 minutes)
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **Content Lead (Maya):**  Analyze the feedback, assess the validity of concerns, and prepare a thoughtful response.
    * **Community Manager (Chloe):**  Moderate online conversations, escalate serious concerns to Maya and the Marketing Lead.
    * **Marketing Lead (Sam):**  Draft a public statement addressing the concerns with sensitivity and reinforcing the core values of “Deuce Diary.”  (Focus on creative intent, artistic expression, etc.)
* **Communication Channels:**
    * **Targeted Response on Platform:**  Directly address the specific criticism, providing context and explaining the creative rationale.
    * **Blog Post (Optional):**  More detailed explanation if the issue warrants.
    * **Community Moderation:**  Remove abusive or hateful comments.


**Scenario 3: PR Backlash (Media Criticism, Influencer Disputes)**

* **Severity:** High - Can damage brand reputation and generate widespread negative attention.
* **Timeline:** Immediate (Within 30 minutes)
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **Marketing Lead (Sam):**  Identify the source of the criticism, assess the potential damage, and develop a strategic response.
    * **PR Agency (If Applicable):** Coordinate messaging and prepare statements for media outlets.

    * **Press Release (If Necessary):**  Controlled
