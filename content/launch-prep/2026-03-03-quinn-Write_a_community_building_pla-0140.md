## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy to build a thriving and engaged community around *Deuce Diary* leveraging Discord and Reddit, underpinned by clear moderation guidelines and consistent, engaging content. The goal is to foster a space for fans to discuss, analyze, theorize, and connect over the story's complex themes and characters.

**I. Platform Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** Discord will be the central hub for real-time discussions, AMAs, organized events, and building close-knit relationships.
    * **Server Structure:**
        * **#general-chat:** Open discussion about the story, fan theories, and general community.
        * **#theory-central:** Dedicated space for detailed, well-reasoned theories with structured threads.
        * **#character-discussion:**  Focused discussion around specific characters, their motivations, and arcs.
        * **#art-and-media:**  Platform for sharing fan art, edits, music playlists, and other creative interpretations.
        * **#events-and-games:** Hosting regular events – quizzes, character polls, collaborative writing prompts, etc.
        * **#moderation-and-support:** Channel for reporting issues and receiving support from moderators.
* **Reddit (Amplifier & Discovery):** Reddit will serve to expand reach, spark broader discussions, and capture content from Discord.
    * **Subreddit: r/DeuceDiary:** Focus on longer-form discussion, in-depth analysis, and sharing high-quality content.
    * **Content Pillars:** Reddit will be a place for curated posts from Discord, pinned threads from the most popular theories, and links to external resources (e.g., fan analyses on sites like Tumblr).



**II. Content Strategy:**

* **Core Content (All Platforms):**
    * **Weekly Discussion Prompts:** Start conversations centered around key moments, character interactions, or plot threads. (Example: "What do you think motivated Silas to...?”, "If [Character A] and [Character B] had a conversation, what would they say?")
    * **Character Spotlight Posts:** Weekly dives into a specific character’s motivations, development, and key relationships.
    * **Thematic Analysis:** Exploring the underlying themes of the story (e.g., morality, family, identity) and encouraging community interpretations.
    * **“Deuce’s Perspective” - Short Reflections:**  Occasionally share short excerpts that *feel* like Deuce's voice, prompting discussion about her thought processes.
* **Discord Specific Content:**
    * **AMA (Ask Me Anything) Sessions:**  Regular AMAs with the author (if possible), or experienced community members with well-developed theories.
    * **Live Character Polls:**  Quick polls to gauge community opinions on character actions and choices.
    * **Collaborative Story Prompts:** Encourage community members to contribute ideas for potential story expansions or alternate timelines.
* **Reddit Specific Content:**
    * **Long-Form Threaded Discussions:** Encourage detailed analysis of specific events or chapters.
    * **High-Quality Fan Art/Edits Showcases:**  Feature outstanding submissions.
    * **Links to External Analysis:**  Share links to insightful fan analyses, critiques, and discussions from outside the immediate community.

* **Core Values:** Respect, Constructive Criticism, Evidence-Based Discussion, and a Focus on the Story.
