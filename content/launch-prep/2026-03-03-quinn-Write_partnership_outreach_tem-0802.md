Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health/wellness brands, categorized by approach and with variations to suit different partnership types. Remember to **personalize** these templates for each specific brand and influencer/organization you're contacting – generic emails rarely work!

**1. The "Warm Introduction" - Best for Established Influencers & Organizations**

* **Subject:** Collaboration Opportunity: [Your Brand] & [Their Brand] - Supporting [Shared Value - e.g., Healthy Living]

* **Body:**

> Hi [Name],
>
> I’ve been a long-time admirer of the work you’re doing at [Their Brand] - particularly [mention a specific piece of content, campaign, or initiative you genuinely appreciate]. We at [Your Brand] are passionate about [Your Brand's Mission – be concise and impactful], and we believe there's a fantastic synergy between our brands.
>
> We’re looking to partner with individuals/organizations who share our commitment to [Key Value - e.g., wellness, mindfulness, fitness]. We envision a collaboration where [briefly outline a potential collaboration - e.g., you could feature our product in your upcoming blog post, we could co-host a live Q&A session, etc.].
>
> I’ve attached a brief overview of [Your Brand] and some potential partnership ideas. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute chat to explore how we could create something valuable for your audience?
>
> Best regards,
> [Your Name]
> [Your Title]
> [Your Brand]
> [Link to Your Website]


**2. The "Benefit-Focused" - Good for Influencers Focused on Specific Products/Content**

* **Subject:** Collaboration Proposal: [Your Product] + [Their Niche] – Helping Your Audience [Desired Outcome]

* **Body:**

> Hi [Name],
>
> We're reaching out because we know your audience is interested in [Their Niche - e.g., healthy breakfast ideas, yoga for beginners, stress management].  At [Your Brand], we've developed [Your Product - e.g., our new protein smoothie, our mindfulness meditation app] specifically to help people [Key Benefit - e.g., fuel their workouts, find inner peace, improve sleep].
>
> We believe [Your Product] would be a great fit for your content because [Explain WHY – e.g., it aligns perfectly with your focus on sustainable living, it's a practical tool for your followers].
>
> We’d love to explore a collaboration that could include:
>   *   [Specific Idea 1 - e.g.,  A sponsored post reviewing the product]
>   *   [Specific Idea 2 - e.g.,  A giveaway for your audience]
>   *   [Specific Idea 3 - e.g.,  Affiliate link promotion]
>
> You can learn more about [Your Product] here: [Link to Product Page]
>
> Let me know if you’re interested in discussing this further!
>
> Thanks,
> [Your Name]
> [Your Brand]
