**Retention Analysis Plan**

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  To understand why users are leaving (churning) and identify key levers to improve user retention rates.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify the primary reasons for churn at D1, D7, and D30.
    * Determine which user segments are most vulnerable to churn.
    *  Measure the impact of implemented retention strategies.
    * Establish a baseline retention rate and track progress over time.


**2. Data Collection & Sources:**

* **User Accounts:**  The core dataset containing user information (email, signup date, registration source, etc.).
* **Product Usage Data:**  This is *critical*.  Capture the following:
    * **Key Actions:**  What actions are users taking within your product? (e.g., creating content, inviting users, completing specific tasks, using key features).
    * **Frequency of Use:** How often are users engaging with the product? (daily, weekly, monthly).
    * **Session Length:** How long are users spending in the product per session?
    * **Feature Adoption:** Which features are users utilizing? Which are they ignoring?
    * **Error Rates & Support Interactions:** Are users encountering issues or needing support?
* **Marketing & Onboarding Data:**  Track which channels users came from (e.g., paid ads, social media, email), and which onboarding flows they followed.
* **User Feedback:**  Gathered through:
    * **In-App Surveys:**  Short, targeted questions triggered by specific events or user behaviors.
    * **Email Surveys:**  Periodic surveys sent to users based on their activity.
    * **Customer Support Tickets:** Analyze common issues and complaints.
    * **User Interviews:** (Optional, but valuable) Qualitative data to understand motivations and pain points.
