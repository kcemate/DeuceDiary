**Campaign 1: Welcome & New Subscriber - Building a Foundation (Goal: Establish Relationship & Provide Value)**

* **Overall Strategy:** This campaign focuses on warmly welcoming new subscribers, introducing your brand, and immediately offering valuable content that aligns with their interests. The goal is to build trust and encourage them to open more emails.
* **Timing:** 3-5 emails over 7-14 days.
* **Email 1: Immediately After Signup (Day 1)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:**
        * Warm welcome message expressing gratitude for subscribing.
        * Briefly introduce your brand’s mission/values. (1-2 sentences)
        * Offer a small, immediate freebie – a checklist, mini-guide, or discount code.  “As a thank you, here’s our [Freebie Name] to help you [Benefit].”
        * Link to your website and social media.
* **Email 2:  Highlighting Key Content (Day 3-5)**
    * **Subject:**  [Relevant Topic] - Your Quick Start Guide
    * **Content:** Share a popular blog post, video, or resource related to your brand’s niche. Explain why it’s valuable. “We’ve noticed many of you are interested in [Topic], so we thought you'd love this…”
* **Email 3:  Introduce a Key Product/Service (Day 7-9)**
    * **Subject:** Solve [Problem] with [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:** Briefly introduce one of your core products/services and how it solves a common pain point for your audience. Focus on benefits, not just features. (Include a compelling image.)
* **Email 4 (Optional):  Testimonial or Social Proof (Day 10-14)**
    * **Subject:**  See What Others Are Saying About [Your Brand]
    * **Content:**  Share a short, positive testimonial or a highlight of social media engagement.  Build social proof.
