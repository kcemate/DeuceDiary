Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for approximately 7-10 emails over 2-4 weeks. I'll include subject lines, email content ideas, and suggested timing.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Goal: Build Initial Connection & Establish Value)**

* **Target Audience:** New Subscribers - People who’ve just signed up for your newsletter or email list.
* **Overall Tone:** Friendly, welcoming, helpful, and informative.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Immediately): Welcome & Thank You**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
    * **Content:** Thank you for subscribing! Briefly introduce your brand's mission and what readers can expect. Offer a small welcome gift (e.g., a discount code, free resource download).  Include a friendly image.
* **Email 2 (2 Days Later):  The Problem We Solve**
    * **Subject:**  Struggling with [Pain Point]? We Can Help.
    * **Content:**  Focus on the core problem your product/service solves.  Empathize with the subscriber's potential challenges. Briefly highlight one key benefit.
* **Email 3 (4 Days Later):  Our Story**
    * **Subject:**  How [Your Brand Name] Started…
    * **Content:**  Share a brief story about your brand's origins – why you started, your values, and what makes you different.  Humanize your brand.
* **Email 4 (6 Days Later): Top 3 Resources**
    * **Subject:**  3 Resources to Help You [Achieve Goal]
    * **Content:**  Provide 3 valuable resources – blog posts, guides, checklists – related to your niche.  Link directly to these resources.
* **Email 5 (8 Days Later): Customer Testimonial (Short)**
    * **Subject:**  “[Customer Name] Loves [Your Product/Service]!”
    * **Content:**  Include a short, compelling customer testimonial (quote and/or image).
* **Email 6 (10 Days Later):  Special Offer (Limited Time)**
    * **Subject:**  Exclusive Offer Just For You!
    * **Content:**  Introduce a special offer – discount, free shipping, bonus – to encourage a first purchase.  Create a sense of urgency.


**Campaign 2:  Lead Nurturing - Product/Service Deep Dive (Goal:  Move Leads Closer to a Purchase)**

* **Target Audience:**  Subscribers who have shown some interest (e.g., downloaded a resource, visited a product page).
* **Overall Tone:**  Informative, persuasive, and benefit-focused.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (3 Days After Interest Trigger):  Re-engage & Recap**
    * **Subject:**  Still thinking about [Product/Service]?
    * **Content:**  Remind them of the resource they downloaded or page they viewed. Briefly reiterate the key benefits.
* **Email 2 (5 Days Later): Feature Spotlight #1**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock [Specific Feature] - Here’s How
    * **Content:**  Dive deeper into one key feature of your product/service and explain how it solves a specific problem. Use visuals (screenshots, GIFs).
* **Email 3 (7 Days Later): Feature Spotlight #2**
     * **Subject:** Don't Miss Out - [Feature] is Key
     * **Content:**  Second deep dive into a different key feature.
* **Email 4 (9 Days Later): Common Questions Answered**
    * **Subject:**  Got Questions About [Product/Service]? We Have
