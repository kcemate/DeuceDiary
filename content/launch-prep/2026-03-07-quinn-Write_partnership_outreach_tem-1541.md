Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health & wellness brands, tailored for different levels of engagement and target partners. I've included variations to suit different budgets and goals.

**Template 1: Short & Sweet - Initial Introduction (Ideal for Smaller Brands/Influencers)**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand Name] & [Partner Brand Name]**

Hi [Contact Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I’m the [Your Title] at [Your Brand Name], a [brief, engaging description of your brand – e.g., "small brand creating mindful wellness boxes," "online platform for beginner yoga," "local studio offering restorative pilates"].

We're huge fans of your work at [Partner Brand Name] and particularly appreciate [mention something specific you admire – e.g., "your commitment to sustainable wellness," "your engaging content on Instagram," "your focus on mental health"].

We think there could be a great synergy between our brands. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute chat to explore potential collaboration ideas?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Website]
[Link to your Instagram/Social Media]


**Why it works:**  Direct, friendly, shows you’ve done your research, and asks for a low-commitment conversation.

**Template 2: More Detailed - Focused on Specific Collaboration Ideas (Good for Mid-Tier Brands/Influencers)**

**Subject: Partnership Proposal: [Your Brand Name] + [Partner Brand Name] - [Specific Collaboration Idea - e.g., "Wellness Giveaway," "Content Collaboration"]**

Hi [Contact Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I’m the [Your Title] at [Your Brand Name]. We’re a [detailed description – e.g., “brand creating organic, plant-based supplements for stress reduction”] and our mission is [your brand's mission].

We’ve been following your work at [Partner Brand Name] for a while and are really impressed with [mention a specific achievement/campaign/piece of content].

We believe a collaboration could be really beneficial for both our audiences. We were envisioning [clearly outline 1-2 specific collaboration ideas – e.g., "a joint giveaway featuring our products and your [product/service],” “a series of Instagram Reels showcasing complementary wellness practices,” “a blog post co-created focusing on [relevant topic]”]. 

We've attached a brief outline of potential ideas [optional – include a short PDF with 2-3 concepts]. 

Would you be interested in exploring this further?  Let’s schedule a 30-minute call to discuss.

Thanks for your time and consideration.

Best Regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Website]
[Link to your Website]
[Link to your Social Media]



**Why it works:**  Demonstrates a clear idea, showcases value for both parties, and provides a clear call to action.
