Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a detailed breakdown, covering different tiers, rewards, and how to manage it effectively.

**Program Name:** (Example) "Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love" or "[Your Brand Name] Rewards"

**1. Core Structure & Tiers**

We'll use a tiered system to motivate different levels of engagement. This provides a sense of progression and rewards higher referral volume.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referrer** (Everyone who makes a referral)
    * **Reward:** 10% off their next purchase.
    * **Trigger:**  First referral made.
* **Tier 2: Enthusiastic Promoter** (Referrals up to 3)
    * **Requirement:** 3 successful referrals
    * **Reward:** 15% off their next purchase + Free shipping on their next order.
* **Tier 3: Brand Advocate** (Referrals up to 5)
    * **Requirement:** 5 successful referrals
    * **Reward:** 20% off their next purchase + Free shipping + A small exclusive gift (e.g., branded sticker, small accessory related to your product).
* **Tier 4: VIP Ambassador** (Referrals up to 10+)
    * **Requirement:** 10+ successful referrals
    * **Reward:** 25% off their next purchase + Free shipping + Exclusive access to new product previews + Personalized thank you gift.


**2. Incentives - The Details**

Let's break down the incentives further:

* **Discount Codes:**  Easy to track and redeem.  Make them unique for each referrer.
* **Free Shipping:** A highly valued incentive, especially for e-commerce.
* **Exclusive Gifts:**  Adds a personal touch and increases perceived value. Think:
    * **Small Branded Merchandise:** Stickers, magnets, pens, keychains.
    * **Early Access:**  Let them be the first to know about new products or sales.
    * **Gift Cards:** (Smaller value – e.g., $10) - Good for retaining engaged referrals.
* **Points System (Optional):** Instead of direct discounts, you could award points for referrals that can be redeemed later. This adds complexity but can be great for loyalty.


**3. Referral Process & Tools**

* **Unique Referral Links:**  **Crucially important!**  Each referrer needs a unique link to track their referrals.  Use a referral program platform (see below) to generate these.
* **Easy Sharing:** Provide pre-written social media posts, email templates, and a direct link that people can easily copy and paste.  Make it as frictionless as possible.
* **Tracking & Reporting:** You *must* track referrals.
    * **Referral Program Platform:**  This is the backbone. Options include:
        * **ReferralCandy:** (Popular, versatile) - [https://www.referralcandy.com/](https://www.referralcandy.com/)
        * **Friendbuy:** (Excellent for e-commerce) - [https://friendbuy.com/](https://friendbuy.com/)
        * **ReferralHero:** [https://www.referralhero.com/](https://www.referralhero.com/)
        * **Many others:** Research based on your business size and needs.
* **Customer Portal (Optional):** A place where referrers can track their referrals, view their rewards, and manage their account.

**4. Communication & Promotion**

* **Launch Announcement:** Announce the program prominently on your website, social media, and email list.
* **Email Marketing:** Send regular emails to existing customers reminding them of the program
