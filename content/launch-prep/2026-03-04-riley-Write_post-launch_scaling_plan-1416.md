* **Phased Approach:** We’ll incrementally increase resources and capacity to minimize risk and allow for iterative improvements.
* **Monitoring & Alerting:** Robust monitoring and alerting systems will be in place to identify potential issues before they impact users.
* **Prioritization:** Focus on scaling the areas with the highest impact and return on investment.

**Phase 1: Stabilization & Initial Growth (0-3 Months Post-Launch)**

* **Goal:** Confirm basic system stability, address critical bugs, and acquire initial users.
* **Metrics:**

* **Trigger for Next Phase:**  All metrics consistently meet or exceed thresholds for 2 consecutive weeks.
