Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here’s a detailed plan, broken down into sections, considering different budget levels and program goals.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** “Spread the [Your Brand] Love” or “Refer & Reward” – Something catchy and reflects the purpose.
* **Visuals:** Use consistent branding - colors, logo, imagery that aligns with your brand.

**2. Goals & Target Audience:**

* **What do you want to achieve?** (e.g., Increase new customer acquisition, boost brand awareness, grow your user base, promote a specific product/service)
* **Who are you trying to reach?** (Knowing your target audience will heavily influence the incentives you offer).  Example: "Existing customers aged 25-45 who are tech-savvy and appreciate convenience."


**3. Referral Mechanics – How it Works**

* **Simple Referral Link/Code:**  Every customer gets a unique referral link or code.  This is crucial for tracking.
* **Tracking:**  Use a referral program platform (like ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, Loopin, or even build a simple one yourself using a CRM) to automatically track referrals, conversions, and rewards.  Don't rely solely on manual tracking – it's error-prone.
* **Clear Communication:**  Make the referral process incredibly easy to understand.  Provide clear instructions on your website, in email campaigns, and within your product/service.

**4. Incentive Structure – Tiered Rewards**

This is the most important part!  We’ll offer different levels of rewards to motivate referrals at different stages.

* **Level 1:  Referrer Reward (Basic)**
    * **Reward:**  A small discount (e.g., 10% off their next purchase) or a small credit to their account.
    * **Trigger:**  New customer signs up through the referral link *and* makes a purchase.
    * **Cost:** Relatively Low – $5 - $15 per referral.
* **Level 2:  Mid-Tier Reward (Motivates Repeat Referrals)**
    * **Reward:**  A larger discount (e.g., 20% off), a free product/service trial, or a gift card (e.g., $25).
    * **Trigger:**  New customer signs up, makes a purchase, and *the referred customer also makes a purchase* (or completes a certain action, like onboarding).
    * **Cost:** Medium – $20 - $50 per referral.
* **Level 3:  High-Tier Reward (For Top Performers/Loyalty)**
    * **Reward:**  Exclusive access to a new product/feature, a premium subscription, a higher-value gift card ($100+), or even a donation to a charity in their name.
    * **Trigger:**  Multiple successful referrals, or a significant milestone reached (e.g., referred 5 customers).
    * **Cost:** High – $75+ per referral.  This is reserved for top performers.

**5. Incentives for the Referred Customer (Crucial!)**

* **Don’t just reward the referrer!** The referred customer needs to be incentivized too.
* **Example:**  A small discount on their first purchase, a welcome bonus, or a free gift with their first order.
* **Cost:**  Similar to Level 1 – $5 - $15 per referral.

**6. Program Duration & Promotion**

* **Duration:**  Consider a rolling program (e.g., ongoing) or a limited-time promotion (e.g., 6-12 weeks) to create urgency.
