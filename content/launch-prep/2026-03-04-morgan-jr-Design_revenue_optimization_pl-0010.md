**II. Phase 1: Assessment & Data Gathering (Weeks 1-4)**

* **Goal:** Understand the current state of revenue and identify key areas for improvement.
* **Activities:**
    * **Revenue Audit:** Analyze historical sales data – broken down by product/service, customer segment, channel, and time period. Identify trends, seasonality, and top/bottom performers.
    * **Customer Segmentation:**  Deep dive into your customer base. Go beyond basic demographics. Create detailed segments based on:
        * **Purchase Behavior:** Frequency, value, product preferences, etc.
        * **Engagement:** Website activity, email interactions, social media engagement.
        * **Needs & Pain Points:** What problems are they solving with your product/service?
    * **Pricing Analysis:**  Review current pricing strategies. Is it competitive? Are margins healthy? Are we leaving money on the table? (Consider competitor pricing, cost-plus analysis, value-based pricing)
    * **Channel Analysis:** Assess the performance of each sales channel (e.g., website, retail, wholesale, partnerships). Identify which channels are most profitable and efficient.
    * **Website & Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Audit:** Analyze website traffic, bounce rates, conversion rates, and user flow.
    * **Competitor Analysis:** Detailed analysis of competitor pricing, marketing, and customer reviews.
* **Key Metrics:** Revenue by category, Average Order Value (AOV), Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV), Conversion Rates, Website Traffic.
* **Tools:** CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho), Google Analytics, Market Research Tools, Competitive Intelligence Platforms.


**III. Phase 2: Optimization – Immediate Wins (Weeks 5-12)**

* **Goal:** Implement quick wins to immediately boost revenue.
* **Activities:**
    * **Pricing Adjustments:** Based on initial analysis, implement minor pricing changes (e.g., strategic promotions, tiered pricing). A/B test different price points.
    * **CRO Tweaks:** Implement 1-2 high-impact CRO changes based on website analytics (e.g., improved call-to-actions, simplified checkout process, optimized landing pages).
    * **Sales Process Optimization:**  Review sales scripts, training materials, and sales team performance.  Focus on improving lead qualification and closing rates.
    * **Abandoned Cart Recovery:** Implement automated email sequences to recover abandoned shopping carts (especially for e-commerce).
    * **Upselling & Cross-selling:** Train sales and customer support teams to identify opportunities for upselling and cross-selling relevant products/services.
* **Key Metrics:**  AOV increase, Conversion Rate increase, Sales Cycle Length Reduction,  Abandoned Cart Recovery Rate.
