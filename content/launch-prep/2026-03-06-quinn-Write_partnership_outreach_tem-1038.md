Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health/wellness brands, categorized by approach and with varying levels of formality. Remember to **personalize these heavily** – research the potential partner thoroughly and tailor the message to their brand and audience.

**1. The Casual & Enthusiastic Approach (Good for Smaller Brands, Instagram Focused)**

**Subject: Collaboration Idea - [Your Brand] + [Their Brand]**

Hey [Contact Name],

Huge fan of what you’re building over at [Their Brand]! I especially love [mention a specific post, product, or value they highlight].

At [Your Brand], we’re passionate about [briefly state your brand’s mission - 1-2 sentences].  We think our audiences could really vibe together – people who care about [shared interest, e.g., mindful living, healthy eating, fitness].

I had a couple of ideas bouncing around for a collaboration, like [mention a *specific* simple idea - e.g., an Instagram giveaway, a shared workout routine, a recipe swap].

Would love to chat briefly and explore if this could be a good fit!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Brand Website]

**Why it works:** Friendly, approachable, immediately shows you’ve done your research.  It focuses on a tangible collaboration idea.

**2. The Value-Driven Approach (Good for Brands with Strong Content or Products)**

**Subject: Partnership Opportunity:  Boosting [Their Audience’s] [Desired Outcome]**

Dear [Contact Name],

I’m reaching out from [Your Brand], a [describe your brand - e.g., wellness coaching platform, organic supplement company] focused on helping people [state your key benefit - e.g., reduce stress, improve their energy levels, build healthy habits].

We’ve noticed [Their Brand] is doing incredible work in [mention their niche/audience] and believe our offerings could provide real value to your followers. Specifically, [mention a *specific* benefit your product/service offers that aligns with their audience’s needs – e.g., “our guided meditations could complement your focus on mental wellbeing,” “our protein powders could support your audience’s fitness goals.”].

We envision a partnership that could involve [Suggest 2-3 ideas - e.g., a sponsored content series, a product review, a limited-edition bundle].  We're happy to discuss tailored options to best meet your brand’s goals.

You can learn more about [Your Brand] here: [Your Website]

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]

**Why it works:** Highlights the benefit for *their* audience. Demonstrates you understand their brand and target market.
