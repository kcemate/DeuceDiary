Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service, broken down by week and including content type suggestions. This is a template – you’ll need to customize it heavily based on your specific product, target audience, launch goals, and platform strategy.

**Key Assumptions:**

* **You've already done initial buzz building:** This calendar assumes you've had pre-launch marketing, a website/landing page, and maybe some early reviews.
* **You're focused on driving awareness, trial, and collecting feedback.**
* **You're using a mix of social media, email marketing, and potentially blog content.**

**Platform Focus:** Let’s assume this calendar focuses on:
* **Instagram:** Visual, engaging, community building.
* **Facebook:** Wider reach, community, driving traffic.
* **Email:** Nurturing leads, announcing updates, offering exclusive content.
* **Blog (Optional):** In-depth content, SEO, thought leadership.


---

**Week 1:  The "Wow" & Immediate Gratification**

* **Theme:** Celebrate the Launch!  Excitement, Relief, Initial Use Cases
* **Monday:**
    * **Social (All):** Launch Announcement! (Video demo, captivating image, enthusiastic copy) – *Goal:  Initial Reaction, Shares*
    * **Email:**  Launch Email - Welcome, Thank You, Quick Link to Demo/Tutorial.
* **Tuesday:**
    * **Social (Instagram/TikTok):**  "Day One" Story - Showcasing how *you* use the product/service. (Short video, relatable struggles) - *Goal: Relatability, Engagement*
    * **Blog (If Applicable):**  "The Story Behind [Product Name]" - Briefly explain the motivation/creation.
* **Wednesday:**
    * **Social (Facebook):**  Run a simple poll: "What's the biggest challenge you were facing before [Product Name]?" - *Goal: Audience Insights*
    * **Email:**  "Quick Start Guide" -  A simplified version of your onboarding process.
* **Thursday:**
    * **Social (Instagram):**  User-Generated Content (UGC) Request –  "Share your first experience with [Product Name] using #YourHashtag!" - *Goal: Community, Validation*
* **Friday:**
    * **Social (All):**  Highlight a Key Feature - Short video or graphic explaining a core benefit. - *Goal: Education, Feature Focus*
    * **Email:**  "Friday Feature Focus" -  Deep dive on a specific element.



**Week 2:  Demonstration & Addressing Questions**

* **Theme:** Show, Don't Just Tell –  How It Works, Addressing Common Concerns
* **Monday:**
    * **Social (Instagram/Facebook):** Tutorial -  "How to [Achieve a Specific Result] with [Product Name]" - *Goal: Value, Practicality*
    * **Blog (If Applicable):** "Top 5 Frequently Asked Questions About [Product Name]"
* **Tuesday:**
    * **Social (Instagram Stories):**  Q&A Session -  Take questions from followers in real-time. - *Goal:  Engagement, Addressing Concerns*
* **Wednesday:**
    * **Social (Facebook):**  Run a contest or giveaway to build engagement.
    * **Email:**  "FAQ Roundup" -  Link to your blog post or a summary of common questions.
* **Thursday:**
    * **Social (Instagram/TikTok):**  Behind-the-Scenes -  A glimpse into your team or process. (Humanize the brand) - *Goal: Connection*
