# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here’s a breakdown, covering various aspects and offering options for different business types and budgets.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Catchy Name:** “Share the [Your Brand Name] Love,” “Bring a Friend, Get Rewarded,” “Spread the Word & Save” - something memorable and engaging.
* **Visuals:**  Create a dedicated graphic for the program – a simple logo or illustration that people can easily recognize.

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer Reward:**  What does the person who *refers* get? (See Incentive Options Below)
* **Referee Reward:** What does the new customer get for joining? (See Incentive Options Below)
* **Tracking:** This is *crucial*. Use a reliable referral tracking system.  Options include:
    * **Dedicated Referral Software:** (e.g., ReferralCandy, Mention, UpPromote) – These are usually paid, but offer robust features.
    * **Integrated Referral Links:** Many platforms (Shopify, Wix, etc.) have built-in referral tools.
    * **Unique Referral Codes:**  Generate unique codes for each user and track them.
    * **Social Sharing Buttons:**  Make it easy for people to share via social media.
* **Terms & Conditions:** Clearly outline the rules:
    * Eligibility (e.g., must be a paying customer)
    * Exclusions (e.g., employee referrals may be limited)
    * Duration (e.g., the program runs for a set period)
    * How rewards are earned and redeemed

**3. Incentive Options – Tiered Rewards (Highly Recommended)**

This creates urgency and encourages participation at different levels.

* **Tier 1: Basic (Entry-Level)**
    * **Referrer:** 10% discount on their next purchase.
    * **Referee:** 10% discount on their first purchase.
* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level (After a certain number of referrals)**
    * **Referrer:** $25 Gift Card to your store/service.
    * **Referee:** 15% discount on their first purchase + Free Shipping.
* **Tier 3: Premium (For Top Performers)**
    * **Referrer:** $100 Gift Card, Exclusive Merchandise, or VIP Access.
    * **Referee:** 20% discount on their first purchase + a small, valuable freebie (e.g., a premium guide, extra product sample).



**4. Incentive Types (Beyond Discounts)**

* **Cash Rewards:** (Potentially more expensive, but highly motivating)
* **Gift Cards:** (Choose relevant gift cards – Amazon, Starbucks, etc.)
* **Free Products/Services:** (Especially good for businesses with a range of offerings)
* **Loyalty Points:** (Convert referrals into loyalty points)
* **Early Access:**  Give referrers and their friends early access to new products or features.
* **Experiences:** (If relevant – tickets to events, exclusive workshops)



**5. Communication & Promotion:**

* **Website Promotion:**  Prominently display the referral program on your website's homepage, product pages, and checkout page.
* **Email Marketing:**  Send targeted emails to existing customers announcing the program and encouraging referrals.
* **Social Media:**  Run contests, share success stories, and use relevant hashtags.  Consider paid social media ads to promote the program.
* **In-App/In-Product Notifications:** (If you have a mobile app or software)
* **Customer Service:** Train your customer service team to promote the referral program.
* **Post-Purchase Emails:**  Include a referral link in thank-you
