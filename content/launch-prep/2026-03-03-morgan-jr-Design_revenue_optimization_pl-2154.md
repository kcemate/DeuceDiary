**2. Average Order Value (AOV) Growth:**  Getting customers to spend *more* per purchase.

* **Tactics:**
    * **Upselling:** Suggesting higher-priced versions of products/services. (e.g., “Would you like to upgrade to the premium version?”)
    * **Cross-selling:**  Suggesting complementary products/services. (e.g., “Customers who bought this also bought…”).
    * **Bundling:**  Offering related products/services at a discounted price. (e.g., "Buy a camera and lens bundle")
    * **Minimum Order Thresholds:** Offer discounts for orders above a certain value.
    * **Free Shipping Thresholds:** “Free shipping on orders over $50”
* **KPIs:** AOV, Conversion Rate (of upsell/cross-sell offers)
