## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to effectively track and analyze your competition to inform strategic decisions, identify opportunities, and ultimately, improve your business performance.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Gain a deep understanding of the competitive landscape to proactively identify threats and opportunities and develop strategies for sustainable growth.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths & weaknesses.
    * Track competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns).
    * Understand competitor customer acquisition strategies.
    * Monitor competitor performance (sales, market share, brand perception).
    * Identify emerging trends and technologies in the market.


**II. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **List All Competitors:** Start with a broad list including:
    * **Direct Competitors:**  Companies offering similar products/services to the same target market.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Companies offering alternative solutions to the same customer need.
    * **Potential Competitors:** New entrants or companies expanding into your market.
* **Prioritize Competitors:** Rank competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:** % of the market controlled by each company.
    * **Revenue:** Annual revenue of each competitor.
    * **Growth Rate:**  Rate at which each competitor's revenue is growing.
    * **Strategic Importance:**  How significantly their actions could impact your business.
    * **Intensity of Competition:**  How fiercely they compete on price, features, and marketing.
