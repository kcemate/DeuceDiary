## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently tracking and analyzing competitor performance to inform strategic decisions and maintain a competitive edge. It focuses on a data-driven approach, prioritizing actionable insights over vanity metrics.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand competitor strategies, identify opportunities for differentiation, and proactively adjust our own strategy to maintain or improve market position.
* **Specific Objectives (Examples - Tailor to your business):**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Track competitor pricing and promotional activities.
    * Monitor competitor customer reviews and social media sentiment.
    * Assess competitor innovation and product roadmap.
    * Identify emerging trends in the competitive landscape.


**2. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):** Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. - *Focus: Deep Dive*
* **Tier 2 (Adjacent Competitors):** Companies offering related products/services that could potentially capture our customers. - *Focus: Monitoring & Trend Analysis*
* **Tier 3 (Potential Disruptors):** Companies with disruptive technologies or business models that could threaten our market share. - *Focus: Vigilant Monitoring & Scenario Planning*
