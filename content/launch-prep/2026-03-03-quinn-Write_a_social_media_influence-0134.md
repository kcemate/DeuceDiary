Okay, here’s a comprehensive social media influencer outreach template kit, tailored for micro-influencers (health, humor, lifestyle) with DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief. This is designed to be adaptable – *always* personalize it!

**I. DM Template Kit (Segmented by Category)**

**A. General Introduction (Use for all influencers)**

* **Template 1 (Warm & Friendly):** "Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋  Love your vibe & the content you’re creating - especially [mention a specific post/video you genuinely liked]. We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re all about [brief brand mission - 1 sentence]. ✨"
* **Template 2 (Direct & Curious):** “Hi [Influencer Name]!  Just came across your account and was really impressed with your take on [topic they cover]. We’re always looking for authentic voices to partner with. 😊”
* **Template 3 (Quick & Engaging):** “Hey [Influencer Name]!  Big fan of your sense of humor/style/health insights.  How’s your week going? 🚀”


**B. Health Influencers**

* **Template 4 (Health-Focused):** “Hi [Influencer Name]!  We’re huge admirers of your focus on [specific health niche - e.g., mindful eating, workout routines, mental wellness]. We're developing [product/service] that aligns with these values. Would love to chat about a potential collab!”
* **Template 5 (Benefit-Driven):** “Hey [Influencer Name], we noticed you're passionate about [health area]. We’re offering [briefly describe product benefit, e.g., a new protein bar that fuels energy]. Would you be open to trying it and sharing your honest thoughts?”


**C. Humor Influencers**

* **Template 6 (Relatable & Funny):** “OMG [Influencer Name]! You’re hilarious! 😂 We’re [Brand Name], and we're creating content that’s just as goofy. We think you'd be perfect for a fun collab – are you open to a chat?”
* **Template 7 (Challenge-Based):** “Hey [Influencer Name]! Love your humor and how you tackle [a specific type of humor they use - e.g., relatable memes, observational comedy]. We're launching a challenge on [platform] and think your audience would love it. Wanna join?”


**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **Template 8 (Lifestyle Alignment):** “Hi [Influencer Name]!  Your style/vibe is *amazing* - it really fits with our brand's aesthetic.  We're [Brand Name], and we're all about [describe lifestyle - e.g., sustainable living, travel, cozy nights in]. 💖”
* **Template 9 (Experience-Based):** “Hey [Influencer Name], we’re excited to share our new [product/service] designed for [describe the lifestyle experience - e.g., weekend getaways, creating a calm home environment].  Would you be interested in seeing how it fits into your world?”



**Important Notes for DMs:**

* **ALWAYS** personalize! Mentioning a specific post/video shows you’ve actually looked at their content.
* **Keep it short & sweet.**  Micro-influencers are busy.
* **Ask a question** to encourage a response (e.g., "What’s your favorite way to relax?").
* **Include a link to your website** - but don’t bombard them with it.
