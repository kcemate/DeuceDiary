## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a strategy for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competitors to inform strategic decisions, identify opportunities, and mitigate threats. It focuses on a structured approach, leveraging data and analysis to gain a competitive edge.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:**  Understand the competitive landscape and use that knowledge to improve our strategy, products, and marketing efforts.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Track competitor activities (product launches, pricing changes, marketing campaigns).
    * Benchmark our performance against competitors.
    * Identify emerging trends and opportunities in the market.
    * Support strategic decision-making (e.g., new product development, pricing adjustments, marketing investments).

**2. Competitor Identification & Prioritization:**

* **List Competitors:** Create a comprehensive list of all direct, indirect, and potential competitors.  Categorize them based on their market segment, target audience, and offerings.
* **Prioritization Matrix:** Rank competitors based on:
    * **Market Share:**  The relative size of each competitor.
    * **Growth Rate:** How quickly they are expanding.
    * **Relevance to Our Business:** How closely their offerings align with ours.
    * **Threat Level:**  How likely they are to pose a direct threat to our business.
    * **Example Matrix:**

| Competitor Name | Market Share (%) | Growth Rate (%) | Relevance Score (1-5) | Threat Level (1-5) | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Competitor A | 30% | 15% | 4 | 3 | High |
| Competitor B | 15% | 8% | 3 | 2 | Medium |
| Competitor C | 5% | 20% | 2 | 1 | Low |

**3. Data Collection - Key Metrics & Sources:**

This section outlines the specific metrics to track and where to find the data.

| Metric Category | Specific Metrics | Data Source | Frequency of Tracking |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Product & Features** | Product Roadmap, New Feature Launches, Beta Programs, Patent Activity | Company Website, Press Releases, Industry News, Patent Databases | Weekly/Monthly |
| **Pricing & Promotions** | Pricing Changes, Discounts, Bundles, Coupons | Competitor Websites, Email Newsletters, Social Media, Monitoring Tools | Weekly/Monthly |
| **Marketing & Sales** | Advertising Spend, Channel Strategy (SEO, SEM, Social Media), Content Marketing, Email Campaigns, Sales Promotions | Competitor Websites, Social Media, Advertising Platforms (Google Ads, Facebook Ads),  Industry Reports,  Social Listening Tools | Weekly/Monthly |
| **Customer Experience** | Customer Reviews (e.g., Trustpilot, G2), Social Media Sentiment, Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Support Tickets | Review Sites, Social Media Platforms, Customer Support Systems | Monthly/Quarterly |
| **Market Share & Growth** | Estimated Market Share, Revenue Data (if public), Number of Customers | Industry Reports (e.g., Gartner, Forrester), Financial Reports (if public), Market Research | Quarterly |
| **Website & App Analytics** | Traffic, Bounce Rate, Conversion Rates, User Behavior | Google Analytics, SimilarWeb, Competitor Website Analysis | Weekly/Monthly |


**4. Tracking Tools & Technology:**

* **Social Listening Tools:** Mention, Brand24, Hootsuite Insights - Track brand mentions, competitor activity, and sentiment across social media.
* **Website Monitoring Tools:** SimilarWeb, Ahrefs, SEMrush - Analyze competitor website traffic, backlinks, and keyword strategies.
* **Pricing Monitoring Tools:** Price2Spy, Wiser, Prisync - Automatically
