## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Vision:** To cultivate a vibrant and supportive community around Deuce Diary, fostering discussion, appreciation, and creative exploration of the series' themes of identity, growth, and artistic expression.

**I. Platforms & Strategy**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):**
    * **Purpose:** Real-time conversations, focused discussions, member introductions, event coordination, and a central point for all community activity.
    * **Channels:**
        * **#general:** Casual conversations, sharing artwork, general feedback.
        * **#deuce-diary-discussion:** Deep dives into specific episodes, character analysis, thematic interpretations.
        * **#fan-art-submissions:** Dedicated space for showcasing fan art with clear guidelines.
        * **#writing-prompts & #creative-challenges:** Encourage creative responses inspired by Deuce Diary.
        * **#voice-chat:** Scheduled voice chats for events, Q&As, or just casual hangouts.
        * **#moderator-chat:** Secure channel for moderators to communicate efficiently.
    * **Frequency:** Constant activity - moderated events, polls, open discussion.
* **Reddit (Content & Discovery):**
    * **Subreddit (r/DeuceDiary):** Central hub for longer-form discussions, meta-analysis, and sharing content.
    * **Purpose:** Drive traffic to Discord, encourage thoughtful discussion, and provide a space for sharing external content (fanfiction, analysis, artwork).
    * **Strategy:** Consistent posting of engaging content – links to interesting discussions, questions, polls, and curated fan content.  Actively participate in discussions and promote community engagement.


**II. Content Strategy**

* **Core Content Pillars:**
    * **Episode Discussions:** Weekly threads/discussions triggered by new episode releases.
    * **Character Deep Dives:** Monthly explorations of specific characters, their motivations, and development.
    * **Thematic Analysis:**  Regular discussion of overarching themes (identity, trauma, family, mental health) within the series.
    * **Fan Creation Showcase:** Regularly highlight and celebrate fan-created content – art, writing, music, videos – across all platforms.
    * **Interactive Content:**  Polls, quizzes, writing prompts, and creative challenges to encourage participation.
* **Content Calendar:**  A monthly calendar outlining content themes and planned activities will maintain consistency.
* **User-Generated Content (UGC) Focus:** Prioritize and amplify content created *by* the community.  This is the lifeblood of the community.


**III. Moderator Guidelines**

* **Role:** Moderation is about *facilitating* community health and engagement, not stifling discussion.
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **Enforcement of Rules:** Consistently and fairly enforce community rules (detailed below).
    * **Conflict Resolution:**  Mediate disputes and maintain a respectful environment.
    * **Content Review:**  Identify and flag inappropriate content.
    * **Community Engagement:** Actively participate in discussions, welcome new members, and highlight community content.
    * **Channel Management:** Ensure channels remain organized and relevant.
* **Key Rules (Example - Adjust as needed):**
    * **Respectful Communication:** No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks.
    * **Spoiler Policy:** Clearly define spoiler rules (e.g., spoiler tags required).
    * **Content Appropriateness:**  No NSFW content, hate speech, or illegal material.
    * **No Gatekeeping:** Encourage inclusivity and avoid discouraging new members.
    * **Respect Intellectual Property:**  Proper credit for fan works – always ask permission before sharing significant works.
