* **Budget:** This assumes a moderate marketing budget. Adjust campaign investments based on your resources.

**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. January – New Year, New Goals (Post-Holiday Slowdown)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Resolutions.
* **Focus:**  Promote apps that help users achieve goals, improve productivity, or build healthy habits.
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO Refresh:** Update keywords based on new year search trends.
    * **Social Media:** Motivational content, tips related to your app’s function, "New Year, New You" campaigns.
    * **Email Marketing:** Welcome series for new users, highlighting app features to achieve goals.
    * **Paid Ads:** Targeted ads around productivity, fitness, and personal development.
* **Metrics:**  Initial install volume, user onboarding completion rate.


**II. February – Love & Connection (Valentine’s Day)**

* **Theme:**  Romance, Relationships, Connection.
* **Focus:**  Apps related to dating, communication, gift ideas, or shared activities.
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO:** Incorporate Valentine’s Day related keywords (e.g., “gifts for her,” “date ideas”).
    * **Social Media:** Content centered around love, relationships, and creating special moments.  Run contests/giveaways.
    * **Paid Ads:** Target couples and singles, focusing on dating, gift recommendations, and relationship-building apps.
* **Metrics:**  Installs, in-app purchase rates (if applicable), user engagement within the app.



**III. March – Spring & Renewal (St. Patrick's Day, International Women’s Day)**

* **Theme:**  New beginnings, vibrancy, celebration.
* **Focus:**  Apps that promote outdoor activities, wellness, or lifestyle improvements.
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO:**  Keywords related to spring activities, outdoor gear, or wellness.
    * **Social Media:** Content about springtime, seasonal activities, and celebrating diversity (International Women’s Day).
    * **Paid Ads:** Targeting spring activities and outdoor hobbies.
* **Metrics:**  Installs, app usage frequency, user feedback.



**IV. April – Earth Month & Taxes (April Fools’ Day)**

* **Theme:** Sustainability,  Financial Management.
* **Focus:**  Apps related to environmentalism, finance, or organization.
* **Activities:**
    * **ASO:** Incorporate Earth Month and tax-related keywords (if relevant to your app).
    * **Social Media:** Promote sustainable practices, offer tips for budget management.
    * **Paid Ads:**  Run campaigns targeting users interested in sustainability or financial planning.
* **Metrics:**  User engagement with features related to the theme.



**V. May – Mental Health Awareness Month & Memorial Day**

* **Theme:** Wellness, Remembrance.
* **Focus:**  Apps supporting mental wellbeing, meditation, mindfulness, or relaxation.
