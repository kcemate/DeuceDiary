**Template 1: Short & Sweet - Initial Contact (Ideal for Micro-Influencers & Small Businesses)**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand Name] & [Partner Brand Name]**

Hi [Partner Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Your Brand Name], a [brief description of your brand - e.g., online yoga studio, organic skincare brand]. We’re huge fans of your work at [Partner Brand Name] – especially [mention something specific you admire - e.g., your commitment to sustainable practices, your awesome community].

We believe our audiences align perfectly, and we’d love to explore a potential collaboration – maybe a giveaway, a joint content piece, or a simple share.

Would you be open to a quick chat to see if this could be a good fit?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Link to Your Website]

**Why it works:**  Fast, friendly, shows you've done your research, and presents a low-pressure ask.

*   [Proposed Collaboration Idea 1 - e.g.,  Creating a guest post for your blog about [relevant topic]]
*   [Proposed Collaboration Idea 2 - e.g.,  Featuring your brand in a podcast episode discussing [relevant topic]]
*   [Proposed Collaboration Idea 3 - e.g.,  Running a joint giveaway to reach a wider audience]

We're open to discussing other ideas as well! You can learn more about us here: [Link to Your Website]

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Contact Information]



**Why it works:** Demonstrates a deeper understanding of the partner’s brand and content, outlines clear collaboration ideas, and proposes a specific next step.
