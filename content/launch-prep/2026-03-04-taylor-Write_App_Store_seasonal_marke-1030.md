**I. Spring (March - May): Re-Engagement & New User Acquisition**

* **Theme:** Renewal, Fresh Start, Growth, Brightness, Re-discovery

    * **Focus:** Re-engagement with lapsed users (email, push notifications - "Spring into Action with [App Name]!")
    * **Content:**  Highlight new features, seasonal-themed tutorials (e.g., “Plan Your Spring Garden” if relevant), contest/giveaway.
    * **Advertising:**  Re-engagement ads, broad interest-based campaigns.
* **April (Easter/Passover):**
    * **Focus:** Leverage Easter/Passover themes (if relevant to your app - games, family activities, charitable apps).
    * **Content:**  Easter-themed in-app promotions, social media contests.
    * **Advertising:** Target families and holiday shoppers.
* **May (Mother’s Day/Memorial Day):**
    * **Focus:** Mother’s Day (gift-related apps), Memorial Day (outdoor activities apps).
    * **Content:**  Gift guides featuring your app, promotional discounts, user-generated content (showcasing how people are using your app for these occasions).
    * **Advertising:**  Targeting gift-givers, outdoor enthusiasts.
