**I. Overall Goals of Beta Testing**

* **Identify Bugs & Technical Issues:** The primary goal is to uncover any glitches, crashes, or performance problems before the public release.
* **Usability Testing:**  Determine if the app is intuitive, easy to navigate, and satisfying to use.
* **Content Feedback:**  Gather input on the diary entry format, prompts, and overall experience of recording thoughts and feelings.
* **Feature Validation:** Confirm that key features are functioning as intended and that users find them valuable.
* **Gauge Interest & Retention:**  Start building an initial user base and understand user engagement patterns.


**II. TestFlight Rollout Plan – Phased Approach**

We'll utilize a phased rollout within TestFlight to minimize risk and gather targeted feedback.

**Phase 1: Core Team & Close Friends/Family (1 Week)**

* **Who to Invite:** 3-5 people - This includes the development team (lead developer, QA tester), the lead designer, and 1-2 close friends/family members who are willing to provide honest feedback.
* **Feedback to Collect:**
    * **Initial Impressions:** Overall first experience, initial usability.
    * **Core Functionality:**  Diary creation, entry saving/loading, search functionality, notification settings.
    * **Basic Bugs:** Any crashes, freezes, or UI glitches.
    * **Content Suggestion:** Initial thoughts on prompts, diary layout, writing style - is it prompting effectively?
* **Tracking:** Dedicated TestFlight feedback channel, daily brief check-ins (15-30 mins) with the core team.  Utilize TestFlight's built-in reporting features.

**Phase 2:  Targeted Beta Group (2-3 Weeks)**

* **Who to Invite:** 15-20 users recruited based on criteria:
    * **Mental Wellness Interest:** Users interested in journaling, self-reflection, and mental well-being. (Targeted through relevant online communities - Reddit, Facebook groups, mental health forums).
    * **Tech Savvy:** Users comfortable with digital tools and providing detailed feedback.
    * **Diverse Demographics:** Ideally, a mix of ages, backgrounds, and writing styles to expose potential biases.
* **Feedback to Collect:**
    * **All from Phase 1**
    * **Longer Entry Usage:** Assess how users are actually *using* the diary (how often, what prompts they use, what they write about).
    * **Content Quality:** Ratings of the helpfulness and quality of diary prompts.  Suggestions for new prompts or categories.
    * **UI/UX Depth:** Detailed usability feedback – "This button is confusing," "The font is too small," "This feature could be improved."
    * **Performance:** Specific questions about loading times, responsiveness, and battery usage.
* **Tracking:**  Dedicated Slack channel for communication, weekly 1-hour feedback sessions with the core team.  Use TestFlight’s analytics (usage, crash reports) alongside a dedicated user survey (Google Forms, Typeform) sent out weekly.

**Phase 3: Expanded Beta (3-4 Weeks)**

* **Who to Invite:** 50-100 users -  Grow the user base further, continuing with the same criteria as Phase 2.
* **Feedback to Collect:**
    * **All from Phase 2**
    * **Community Engagement:** Track how users are interacting with the app and providing feedback within the TestFlight environment.
    * **Feature Request Prioritization:** Begin gathering user requests for new features or improvements.
