**About *Deuce Diary***

*Deuce Diary* follows the painfully honest (and often hilarious) entries of Deuce "Deux" Morgan, a fifteen-year-old obsessed with the enigmatic musician, Silas Blackwood.  Deux isn't just a fan; she's desperately trying to decipher his coded messages, track down his whereabouts, and, let’s be honest, figure out if there’s any chance he notices *her*.  What starts as a harmless obsession quickly spirals into a series of disastrous attempts to get Silas’s attention, involving a chaotic school talent show, a questionable online persona, and a surprising connection with her best friend, Leo.
