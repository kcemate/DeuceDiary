8. **User Interface/User Experience (UI/UX) Problems:** While subjective, Apple has standards for UI/UX design. Apps that are confusing, poorly designed, or have accessibility issues may be rejected.
9. **Malware/Security Risks:** Apps containing malware, vulnerabilities, or any potential security threats are automatically rejected.
10. **Violations of App Store Guidelines (Specific):**  There are numerous sub-categories within the main guidelines covering things like app categorization, age ratings, promotional practices, and usage of 3rd party content.
