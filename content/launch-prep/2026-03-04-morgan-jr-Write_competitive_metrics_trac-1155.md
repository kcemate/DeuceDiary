**I. Executive Summary**

This plan outlines a system for continuously monitoring and analyzing key competitive metrics. The goal is to identify shifts in the market, understand competitor strategies, and ultimately, optimize our own performance. We'll focus on a prioritized set of metrics, regularly review data, and translate insights into strategic recommendations.

**II. Goals & Objectives**

* **Primary Goal:** Gain a proactive understanding of our competitive landscape to drive strategic decision-making.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Track changes in competitor strategies (pricing, promotions, product development).
    *  Measure our relative performance against competitors.
    *  Identify emerging trends and potential threats/opportunities.
    *  Inform resource allocation and strategic planning.

**III. Metric Categories & Specific Metrics**

We’ll categorize metrics for clarity and ensure we're covering all relevant areas. This list is a starting point – tailor it based on your industry and business.

**A. Market Share & Sales Performance:**

* **Market Share:** (Percentage of total market sales) – Tracked quarterly. (Source: Industry Reports, Company Reports, Third-Party Data Providers)
* **Revenue Growth Rate:** (Comparing sales over time) – Tracked monthly/quarterly. (Source: Internal Sales Data)
* **Sales Volume:** (Units sold) – Tracked monthly/quarterly. (Source: Internal Sales Data)
* **New Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** (Cost to acquire a new customer) – Tracked monthly. (Source: Marketing & Sales Data)
* **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV):** (Predicted revenue a customer will generate) – Tracked quarterly. (Source: CRM Data, Sales Data)

**B. Product & Innovation:**

* **New Product Launches:** (Number, Timing, Features) – Tracked continuously. (Source: Competitor Announcements, Product Websites, Press Releases)
* **Product Reviews & Ratings:** (Aggregate scores on key platforms – Amazon, Trustpilot, Capterra, etc.) – Tracked weekly/monthly. (Source: Online Review Sites)
* **Feature Tracking:** (Identifying new features introduced by competitors) - Tracked continuously. (Source: Competitor Product Updates, Blog Posts, Social Media)
* **Innovation Rate:** (Number of patents filed, R&D spending – relative to competitor) – Tracked annually. (Source: Public Patent Databases, Company Financial Reports)

**C. Pricing & Promotions:**

* **Average Selling Price (ASP):** (Average price of your products) – Tracked monthly. (Source: Internal Sales Data)
* **Discount Rates:** (Percentage of discounts offered) – Tracked monthly. (Source: Competitor Websites, Promotions)
* **Promotional Campaign Analysis:** (Effectiveness of competitor promotions – reach, engagement, conversion) – Tracked during and after campaigns. (Source: Social Media Analytics, Website Analytics, Advertising Platforms)

**D. Marketing & Brand:**

* **Website Traffic:** (Overall, by source) – Tracked monthly. (Source: Google Analytics, Other Web Analytics Tools)
* **Social Media Engagement:** (Likes, shares, comments, followers) – Tracked daily/weekly. (Source: Social Media Analytics – Facebook Insights, Twitter Analytics, LinkedIn Analytics, etc.)
* **Brand Mentions:** (Tracking mentions of your brand and competitors online) – Tracked continuously. (Source: Social Listening Tools – Mention, Brand24, Google Alerts)
* **SEO Rankings:** (Keyword rankings) - Tracked weekly/monthly. (Source: SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz)
