**I. Overall Goals & Target Audience**

* **Goal:** Monitor the health and performance of the Deuce Diary launch, identifying key engagement drivers and potential issues.
* **Target Audience:** Marketing Team, Product Team, and potentially Executive Stakeholders.

**II. Dashboard Structure & Key Sections**

We’ll organize the dashboard into several core sections for clarity:

1. **High-Level Overview (Top Row)**
2. **User Acquisition & Growth**
3. **Engagement & Activity**
4. **Content Performance**
5. **Technical Performance & Stability**

**III. Detailed Dashboard Layout & Metrics (with Visualization Suggestions)**

Here's a breakdown of each section, including suggested metrics and visualization types:

**1. High-Level Overview (Top Row - Key Performance Indicators - KPIs)**

* **Metric:** Total Users (Active) –  Number of unique users who have interacted with the diary.
    * **Visualization:** Large, prominent Number Widget (Clearly shows the current number)
* **Metric:** Daily/Weekly/Monthly Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU) –  Trend of user engagement over time.
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart –  Visualize trends over a selectable date range.
* **Metric:**  Conversion Rate (Free to Paid) –  Percentage of free users who upgrade to a paid tier (if applicable).
    * **Visualization:** Gauge Chart or Progress Bar –  Quickly shows progress against a conversion goal.
* **Metric:** Total Revenue (if applicable)
    * **Visualization:** Large Number Widget

**2. User Acquisition & Growth**

* **Metric:** New Users – Number of users signing up during a specific period.
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart –  Track daily/weekly new user trends.
* **Metric:** Acquisition Channel Breakdown –  Where users are coming from (e.g., Social Media, Paid Ads, App Store Search, Referral).
    * **Visualization:** Pie Chart or Bar Chart –  Shows the proportion of users from each channel.
* **Metric:** Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) –  Cost to acquire a new user through each channel.
    * **Visualization:** Bar Chart –  Compare CPA across different channels.
* **Metric:** Organic vs. Paid Acquisition – Ratio of users acquired organically versus through paid advertising.
     * **Visualization:** Donut Chart

**3. Engagement & Activity**

* **Metric:** Diary Entries Created – Number of new diary entries created by users.
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart – Track daily/weekly entry creation trends.
* **Metric:** Average Entries Per User –  Average number of entries created by each user.
    * **Visualization:** Number Widget
* **Metric:** Time Spent in App/Diary –  Average session duration.
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart – Track trends over time
* **Metric:** Daily/Weekly/Monthly Frequency – How often users are logging into their diaries.
    * **Visualization:** Heatmap –  Visualize frequency across days of the week.
* **Metric:**  Features Used –  Track usage of key diary features (e.g., Mood Tracking, Journal Prompts, Photo Uploads, etc.) – could use a stacked bar chart.

    * **Visualization:** Bar Chart - Show the most popular prompts.
