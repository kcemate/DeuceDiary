**I. Assessment & Diagnostics (Weeks 1-4)**

* **Goal:** Understand the current state of revenue generation.
* **Activities:**
    * **Revenue Audit:** Analyze historical revenue data – broken down by product/service, channel, customer segment, time period. Identify trends, seasonality, and high-performing areas.
    * **Customer Analysis:**
        * **Segmentation:** Define customer segments based on behavior, needs, demographics, etc. (e.g., high-value customers, new customers, churn risk).
        * **Persona Development:** Create detailed customer personas for each key segment.  Understand their motivations, pain points, and buying journey.
        * **Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) Calculation:**  Estimate the revenue generated by a customer over their entire relationship with your business.  This helps prioritize efforts.
    * **Channel Analysis:** Evaluate the performance of all revenue channels – website, social media, email, sales team, retail, etc.  Determine ROI for each.
    * **Pricing Analysis:** Review current pricing strategies – is it competitive? Are you maximizing profit margins?  Are you offering tiered pricing effectively?
    * **Competitive Analysis:** Understand your competitors’ pricing, offerings, and marketing strategies.
    * **Website & Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Audit:**  Use tools like Google Analytics, Hotjar, or similar to identify areas of friction on your website that might be preventing conversions.
    * **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Definition:** Identify the core metrics you’ll track to measure success (e.g., conversion rate, average order value, customer acquisition cost, churn rate, revenue per employee).
