**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store (According to Apple’s Review Guidelines)**

3. **Security Vulnerabilities:** Apps that have poor security practices, data collection issues, or pose a risk to user privacy.
4. **Privacy Violations:** Collecting excessive user data, not clearly explaining data usage in the privacy policy, or failing to comply with privacy regulations (like GDPR or CCPA).
5. **Performance Issues:** Apps that crash frequently, drain excessive battery, use excessive data, or are generally slow and unresponsive.
6. **App Store Guidelines Violations:** This is a broad category encompassing many of the other issues. Specific guidelines cover areas like in-app purchases, advertising, user reviews, etc.
7. **Malware/Suspicious Behavior:** Apps that contain malware, have suspicious code, or engage in deceptive behavior.
8. **Inappropriate Content:** Apps containing offensive, violent, or sexually explicit material.
9. **Functionality Issues:** Apps that don't work as advertised, have bugs, or have broken features.
10. **Lack of Clear Functionality:** Apps with unclear purpose or confusing user interface. Users need to understand what the app *does*.
