**1. About *Deuce Diary***

*Deuce Diary* is a poignant and hilarious coming-of-age story told through the diary entries of 16-year-old Leo “Deuce” Davenport, who is navigating the agonizing aftermath of his father’s sudden death.  More than just a grief narrative, it’s a story about the messy, awkward, and surprisingly supportive relationships that help him – and his fiercely loyal best friend, Sam – rebuild their lives.  Deuce’s brutally honest, often self-deprecating voice, combined with sharp observations about teenage life, creates a story that’s both heartbreaking and laugh-out-loud funny.
