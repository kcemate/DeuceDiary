Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each with a different goal and designed for varying lengths. I’ve included subject lines, email content outlines, and estimated timing. Remember to personalize these with your brand voice and tailor them specifically to your audience and offer.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Introductory Offer (Goal: Immediate Engagement & Lead Nurturing)**

* **Goal:**  Welcome new subscribers, introduce your brand, and drive a small initial purchase.
* **Timing:** 3 Emails - Sent over 7-10 days.
* **Audience:** New subscribers who signed up through a landing page or form.

**Email 1: Immediately After Signup (Day 1)**
* **Subject Line:** Welcome to [Your Brand]! 🎉
* **Content:**
    * Warm welcome and thank you for subscribing.
    * Briefly introduce your brand - what you do and your mission (keep it concise).
    * Quick highlight of your core values or what makes you unique.
    * **Offer:**  A small welcome discount (e.g., 10% off their first order).
    * Call to Action (CTA): “Claim Your Discount Now!” – Link to your shop or relevant product page.


**Email 2:  Problem/Solution Focus (Day 4)**
* **Subject Line:** Struggling with [Pain Point Related to Your Product]? 
* **Content:**
    * Identify a common problem your target audience faces – directly related to the problem your product solves.  (e.g., “Tired of messy paperwork?” if you sell organizational tools)
    * Briefly explain why this problem is frustrating.
    * Introduce your product as the solution – highlighting *one* key benefit.
    * Visual element (image or short GIF) showcasing the product in action.
    * CTA: “See How [Your Product] Can Help” – Link to a product page with a detailed description.

**Email 3: Last Chance & Social Proof (Day 7)**
* **Subject Line:** Don't Miss Out: Your Discount Expires Soon! ⏳
* **Content:**
    * Reminder of the welcome discount expiring soon.
    * Short testimonial or positive review from a happy customer (if available).
    * Reiterate the key benefit of your product.
    * CTA: "Claim Your 10% Off Before It's Gone!" – Link to your shop.
