**Seasonal Marketing Calendar - App Store**

**I. January – “New Year, New App” (Post-Holiday Calm)**

*   **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Resolution Apps, Productivity.
*   **Focus:** Drive downloads of apps that support New Year’s resolutions – fitness, habit tracking, productivity, meditation, language learning.
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **Launch Ads:** Run targeted ad campaigns focusing on New Year’s keywords.
    *   **App Store Updates:** Highlight app features that help users achieve their goals.
    *   **Blog/Social Content:** "Top 5 Apps for [Resolution]", “How to [Achieve Goal] with Our App”.
    *   **Influencer Outreach:** Partner with wellness/productivity influencers.
*   **Timeline:** Begins immediately after the holiday rush.
*   **Metrics:**  Downloads, New User Retention, App Store Keywords Ranking

**II. February – “Love & Connection” (Valentine’s Week)**

*   **Theme:** Romance, Relationships, Self-Love, Social Connection
*   **Focus:**  Dating apps, relationship advice apps, self-care apps, social apps (if relevant).
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **Valentine’s Day Specific Ads:** Run ads tailored to romantic interests.
    *   **App Store Featuring:** If your app relates to connection, highlight features for couples or individuals.
    *   **Social Media Campaigns:**  Run contests, giveaways, or themed content.
    *   **Email Marketing:** Send Valentine's Day themed email to subscribers.
*   **Timeline:**  Week leading up to Valentine's Day.
*   **Metrics:**  Downloads, Conversions, In-App Purchases (if applicable)

**III. March – “Spring into Action” (March Madness & Transition)**

*   **Theme:** Spring Cleaning, New Habits, Fitness, Outdoor Activities, Educational Apps.
*   **Focus:**  Fitness trackers, meal planning apps, outdoor activity apps, language learning.
*   **Tactics:**
    *   **Launch Ads:**  Focus on spring-related keywords.
    *   **App Store Updates:** Highlight features related to seasonal activities.
    *   **Content Marketing:** “Start Your Spring Fitness Routine,” “Spring Cleaning Apps to Organize Your Life.”
    *   **Promote Seasonal Content:** Highlight new features related to the season.
*   **Timeline:** Starts March 1st and runs through Spring Equinox (around March 20th)
*   **Metrics:**  Downloads, User Engagement, Active Users
