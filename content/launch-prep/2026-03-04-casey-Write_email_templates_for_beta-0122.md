**Option 2 (More Casual & Enthusiastic):**

```email
Hey [Beta Tester Name],

We're so excited to be launching [Your Product Name] and we’d love your help to make it amazing! We’re looking for beta testers like you to give us your honest feedback.

Basically, you'll get early access to [Your Product Name] and tell us what you think.  We're especially interested in hearing about what's working, what’s confusing, and any cool ideas you have.

**Here’s the deal:**
* Try out [Your Product Name] and let us know what you think.
*  Report any bugs or issues you find.
*  Share your thoughts and suggestions.

[Link to Beta Tester Portal/Instructions]

Thanks so much for your help!

Best,

The [Your Company Name] Team
```

**Key Considerations for Beta Testers:**

*   **Clear Expectations:**  Outline exactly what you expect of them.
*   **Detailed Instructions:** Provide easy-to-follow instructions.
*   **Reporting System:**  Make it simple for them to report issues (e.g., a dedicated form, email address).
*   **Incentives (Optional):** Consider offering a small reward (discount, early access, etc.).
