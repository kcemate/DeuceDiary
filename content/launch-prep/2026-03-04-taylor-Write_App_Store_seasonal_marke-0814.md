**Important Notes Before We Start:**

* **App Category Matters:**  Marketing strategies for a gaming app will be vastly different from a productivity app. This calendar assumes a moderately broad app focus.
* **ASO (App Store Optimization) is Ongoing:**  This calendar highlights *promotional* activities, but don’t forget that consistent ASO - keyword research, description updates, screenshots, video - is crucial throughout the year.
* **Metrics are Key:**  Track everything – installs, retention, engagement, etc. – and adjust your strategy based on the data.
* **Platform Specifics:** iOS and Android have different strengths and user behaviors. Consider tailoring your campaigns accordingly.
* **Budget Allocation:** This calendar doesn't specify budgets.  Your budget will determine the scale of your campaigns.

**I. January – New Year, New Habits**

* **Theme:**  New Year's Resolutions, Self-Improvement, Goal Setting
* **Focus:**  Highlight apps that support healthy habits, productivity, learning, or personal development.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Paid Social (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok):** Target users interested in New Year’s resolutions, productivity, mindfulness, and learning.  Offer introductory discounts or free trials. (High Priority)
    * **ASO Updates:**  Incorporate trending New Year's resolution keywords.
    * **Blog Posts & Content:**  “5 Apps to Help You Stick to Your New Year’s Goals,” “How to Use [Your App] to Achieve Your Resolutions.” (Medium Priority)
    * **Email Marketing:**  Welcome emails with special offers for new users. (Low Priority)

**II. February – Love & Connection**

* **Theme:** Valentine's Day, Relationships, Self-Care, Dating
* **Focus:** Apps related to dating, relationships, gift ideas, or self-care.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Paid Social:** Target users interested in dating, relationships, gifts, and romance. (High Priority)
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relationship or lifestyle influencers. (Medium Priority - if budget allows)
    * **ASO Updates:** Add Valentine’s Day related keywords.
    * **App Feature Showcase:**  Highlight features that support connection or self-care. (Medium Priority)

    * **Paid Social:** Shift focus to productivity and organization. (Medium Priority)
    * **Promotional Offers:**  “Spring Cleaning Bundle” – offer a discount on multiple app purchases. (Low Priority)
