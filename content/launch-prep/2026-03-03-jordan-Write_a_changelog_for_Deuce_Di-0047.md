**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 – Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Overview:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 represents the initial public launch of Deuce Diary, a habit tracking and reflection journal designed to help you build positive routines and understand your thoughts and feelings. This release introduces core features for habit tracking, journaling, and community interaction.

**1. Social Features**

* **Community Feed:**  A public feed where users can share their daily reflections, habit progress, and motivational updates. (Moderation features are under development and will be implemented in a subsequent release.)
* **Follower System:**  Users can now follow other users within the Deuce Diary community.
* **Direct Messaging (Beta):**  A basic direct messaging system allowing users to connect privately with other Diary users. (Limited to 100 messages per user, to be expanded in later updates).
* **Reactions:** Users can react to posts and diary entries with a set of pre-defined emojis (like, heart, comment).


**2. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Creation:**  Easily add new habits with customizable names, icons, frequency (daily, weekly, specific days), and reminders.
* **Habit Tracking:**  Mark habits as complete for each day with a simple toggle.
* **Streak Calculation:**  The app automatically calculates and displays your current habit streak.
* **Habit Categories:** Organize habits into categories (e.g., Health, Productivity, Personal Growth) for better management.
* **Flexible Scheduling:**  Set habits to occur at specific times of day, or trigger them based on other habits (conditional scheduling – initially limited to simple "if/then" scenarios).
* **Detailed Habit Statistics:** View daily, weekly, and monthly charts to visualize your progress for each habit.



**3. Gamification Features**

* **Achievements:** Unlock achievements for reaching milestones (e.g., 7-day streak, 30-day streak, completion of 5 habits).
* **Points System:** Earn points for completing habits and achieving milestones. Points contribute towards unlocking badges and visual rewards.
* **Leaderboards (Beta):**  Participate in friendly competition on the global leaderboards. (Leaderboards are weighted based on activity level; further refinement planned.)
* **Customizable Profile:**  Personalize your profile with a profile picture and a short bio.


**4. UI/UX Improvements**

* **Modern Design:** A fresh, visually appealing user interface designed for ease of use and intuitive navigation.
* **Dark Mode:**  A dark mode option for reduced eye strain and improved aesthetics.
* **Improved Search:** Enhanced search functionality to quickly find habits, diary entries, and users.
* **Responsive Design:**  Optimized for a variety of screen sizes (phones and tablets).
* **Entry Formatting:**  Rich text editing within diary entries (bold, italics, bullet points).
* **Simplified Onboarding:** A streamlined onboarding process to guide new users through the app's key features.


**5. Infrastructure & Technical Improvements**

* **Cross-Platform Compatibility:**  Deuce Diary is now available on iOS and Android.
* **Cloud Sync:** All data is automatically synced across devices via our secure cloud infrastructure.
* **Initial Database Optimization:**  Improvements to the database structure for faster performance and scalability.
* **Testing Framework Integration:** Improved test coverage across the codebase, increasing confidence in the stability of the app.
