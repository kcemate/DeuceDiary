## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competition to inform strategic decisions and drive business growth. It focuses on key metrics, data sources, reporting, and actions.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To understand the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and proactively adjust our strategy to maintain or improve our market position.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    * Track competitor pricing and promotional strategies.
    * Monitor competitor product development and innovation.
    * Understand competitor customer satisfaction and engagement.
    * Identify potential market share shifts due to competitor actions.


**II. Key Competitors & Segmentation:**

* **Identify:** List all direct and indirect competitors (3-5 key direct, 5-10 indirect).
* **Segment:** Categorize competitors based on:
    * **Size:** Enterprise, SME, Startup
    * **Strategy:** Disruptive, Traditional, Niche
    * **Product Offering:** Overlapping, Complementary, Substitutes
* **Prioritize:** Rank competitors based on their relevance to your business (e.g., based on market share, growth potential, and potential impact).
