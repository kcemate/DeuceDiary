**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Summary:** This is the initial release of Deuce Diary, a diary application designed to help you track your thoughts and experiences as a young, somewhat troubled, future Emperor of the Galactic Empire.

**New Features:**

* **Diary Entry Creation:** The core functionality is now live! You can create new diary entries, adding text to record your thoughts, feelings, and daily events.
* **Date & Time Stamping:** Each diary entry is automatically stamped with the current date and time, ensuring accurate record-keeping.
* **Simple Text Editor:** A basic text editor is included for formatting your entries. You can bold, italicize, and change font sizes (though don't overdo it – a simple diary isn’t about vanity).
* **Save Functionality:** Entries are automatically saved as you type, preventing data loss.
* **Basic UI:** A functional, though admittedly somewhat spartan, user interface is present, including entry list display and navigation.

**Bug Fixes:**

* **Fixed:** Initial crash when attempting to save an empty entry.
* **Fixed:** Corrected a minor miscalculation in the date display.
* **Fixed:** Resolved a display issue where the entry list wasn’t correctly populating.
* **Fixed:** Removed a completely unnecessary warning message that appeared on startup.

**Known Issues & Limitations:**

* **Limited Formatting:** The text editor is extremely basic. Don’t expect fancy features like tables or images.  Your prose is all that matters.
* **No Search Functionality:**  Currently, there’s no way to search your diary entries. You’ll have to rely on your memory (or perhaps your loyal advisors).
* **No Backup/Export:** There is no mechanism to save your diary to a file or backup your data.  Keep a copy on a secure datapad! (Seriously, do this.)
* **Dated Aesthetics:**  The UI is intentionally designed to evoke the aesthetic of a young man’s personal journal. It's not polished, but it's functional.
* **Potential for Incoherent Ramblings:** The diary is currently populated with introductory entries. Be aware that some entries may be… confusing.
