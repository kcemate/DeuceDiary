**II. Categories of Competitive Metrics & Tracking Methods**

We'll divide our tracking into key categories, each with specific metrics and methods for collection:

**1. Market Share & Sales Performance:**

* **Metric:**
    * **Market Share (%):**  Estimate based on available data (industry reports, third-party research, internal sales data).
    * **Sales Revenue ($):**  Track competitor revenue (where possible, often through industry reports).
    * **Sales Volume (Units):**  Similar to revenue, track volume if data is available.
    * **New Customer Acquisition Rate:** (Customers gained per period) - Compare to our own.
* **Tracking Method:**
    * **Industry Reports:** Gartner, Forrester, Statista, etc.
    * **Public Financial Statements:** If publicly traded.
    * **CRM Data:** Analyze sales data and customer acquisition patterns.
    * **Market Research:** Commission reports for deeper insights.



**2. Pricing & Promotions:**

* **Metric:**
    * **Average Selling Price (ASP):**  Monitor competitor pricing across their channels.
    * **Discount Rates (%):** Frequency and magnitude of promotions.
    * **Bundle Pricing:** What offers are competitors offering in combination?
    * **Coupon Usage:** If available, track coupon redemption rates.
* **Tracking Method:**
    * **Website Scraping:** Automated tools can track pricing changes (be mindful of terms of service).
    * **Social Media Monitoring:**  Monitor announcements of promotions and discounts.
    * **Competitor Newsletters/Emails:** Subscribe to stay informed.
    * **Manual Price Checks:** Regular manual reviews of competitor websites.


**3. Product & Feature Analysis:**

* **Metric:**
    * **New Product Launches:**  Track the timing and features of competitor launches.
    * **Feature Additions/Updates:** Monitor updates to existing products - especially those relevant to your core offering.
    * **Product Reviews & Ratings:**  Analyze sentiment and identify key strengths & weaknesses. (e.g., Trustpilot, Capterra, G2)
    * **Technical Specifications:**  Assess differences in features, performance, and specifications.
* **Tracking Method:**
    * **Product Release Announcements:**  Monitor competitor blogs, news releases, and social media.
    * **Review Sites:**  Regularly monitor and analyze reviews.
    * **Product Demos/Trials:**  Where available, conduct your own testing to compare features.
    * **Feature Comparison Matrices:** Create a table comparing your product to competitors' on key features.

    * **Website Traffic:** (Unique Visitors, Page Views) - Use tools like SimilarWeb.

    * **Search Engine Rankings:** Track keyword rankings for relevant terms.
