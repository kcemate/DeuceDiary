**Executive Summary:** WellConnect has potential within the Health & Fitness category, offering a community-focused approach to wellness. This audit identifies key areas for improvement, focusing on keyword targeting, visual assets, conversion funnel optimization, and competitive analysis.

**Notes:**

* **Keyword Research Tool:** Utilized App Radar & Sensor Tower for data.
* **Search Volume:**  High volume keywords like “Mental Health” & “Community Support” have significant competition.
* **Long-Tail Keywords:**  Identify and incorporate long-tail keywords (e.g., "Mental health support for anxiety") for higher conversion rates.
* **Relevance:** All keywords selected must genuinely reflect the app’s functionality and content.
