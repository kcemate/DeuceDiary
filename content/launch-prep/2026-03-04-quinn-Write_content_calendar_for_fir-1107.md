Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service. This is a template, so you’ll need to tailor it to *your* specific product, target audience, and launch goals. I’ve included a mix of content types, estimated frequency, and notes on potential themes.

**Assumptions:**

* **You've already done pre-launch buzz:** This calendar assumes some initial excitement and a small audience is already aware.
* **Your product/service is relatively well-defined:** We're not building a brand from scratch here.
* **You’re using multiple channels:**  This calendar assumes you'll be active on social media (Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn – choose what's relevant), your website/blog, and potentially email marketing.

**Overall Month Goal:** Drive awareness, encourage trial/adoption, gather early feedback, and build a small, engaged community.

|---|---|---|---|---|---|

| Day 7 | Social Media (All) | User Generated Content (UGC) - Repost | Share any early positive feedback or images from users (if any). | Social Proof | Tag the original poster. |
