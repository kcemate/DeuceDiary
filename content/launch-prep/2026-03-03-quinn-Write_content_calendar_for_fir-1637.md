Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product/service, broken down by week and day, with suggested content types and rationale. This is a template – you'll need to adapt it heavily based on your specific product, target audience, and launch goals.

**Assumptions:**

* **"Launch" = Successful Release:**  We're assuming the product/service is officially available to the public.
* **Goal:** Drive awareness, initial adoption, and gather early feedback.
* **Platform Focus:** This calendar is geared towards a mix of social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, maybe TikTok) and email marketing, but can be adjusted.


**Overall Theme for Month 1: "Welcome & Explore"** - Focus on introducing the product, highlighting key benefits, and making it easy for people to try it out.

---

**Week 1: Initial Buzz & Education (Focus: Awareness & Excitement)**

* **Monday:** **Launch Announcement (All Platforms)** - Big, celebratory post announcing the launch! Include a high-quality image/video, key benefits, and a link to your website/landing page. (Type: Image/Video, Text)
* **Tuesday:** **Behind-the-Scenes (Instagram/Facebook/TikTok)** - Short video or series of images showcasing the team, the creation process, or a glimpse of the problem your product solves.  Humanize the brand. (Type: Video, Image Carousel)
* **Wednesday:** **Feature Spotlight #1 (Blog Post & Social Media)** - Deep dive into *one* core feature. Explain what it does and why it’s valuable.  Link back to your website. (Type: Blog Post, Infographic, Short Video Demo)
* **Thursday:** **FAQ Post (Facebook/Twitter/X)** - Address common questions people likely have.  "What is [Product Name]? How does it work?" (Type: Text Post, Image with Text)
* **Friday:** **User Testimonial - Early Adopter (Social Media)** - If you have early users, share a quote or short video testimonial.  Social proof is powerful. (Type: Text Post, Video)
* **Saturday/Sunday:** **Reminder & Call to Action (All Platforms)** - Gentle reminder about the launch, with a link to your website.  Consider a simple, engaging question to spark conversation. (Type: Image Post, Poll)



**Week 2: Demonstrations & Value (Focus: Understanding & Interest)**

* **Monday:** **Demo Video - Quick & Easy (YouTube/Website/Social Media)** -  A concise (60-90 second) video showing how to use a key feature. (Type: Video)
* **Tuesday:** **“Problem/Solution” Post (All Platforms)** – Highlight the specific problem your product solves and how it directly addresses it. (Type: Image/Graphic with Text)
* **Wednesday:** **Feature Spotlight #2 (Blog Post & Social Media)** -  Another key feature deep dive.  Focus on a slightly different aspect. (Type: Blog Post, Graphic)
* **Thursday:** **Live Q&A (Instagram/Facebook Live)** - Host a short live session to answer questions in real-time. Promote it in advance. (Type: Live Video)
* **Friday:** **User Tip/Hack (Social Media)** -  Share a clever way to use the product to get more value.  Encourage users to share their own tips. (Type: Image/Video, Text)
* **Saturday/Sunday:** **Customer Case Study (Blog Post & Social Media)** -  If you have a relevant early success story, share it! (Type: Blog Post, Infographic)
