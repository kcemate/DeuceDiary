Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, incorporating different levels and options to make it engaging and effective.

**Program Name:** (Let's call it) "Spread the Word Rewards"

**Overall Goal:** Drive new customer acquisition, increase brand awareness, and foster a sense of community.

**1. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The existing customer who refers a new customer.
* **New Customer:** The person who signs up and makes a purchase (or reaches a specific action goal) through the referrer's unique link/code.
* **Unique Tracking:** Use a reliable referral tracking system (many CRM and marketing automation platforms offer this - e.g., HubSpot, Mailchimp, ReferralCandy, ReferralHero). This is crucial for accurate attribution and reward distribution.
* **Clear Communication:**  Make the program easy to understand with clear terms and conditions.

**2. Incentive Structure (Tiered Rewards - More Incentive = Higher Effort)**

Here's a tiered system – adjust these based on your business model and budget:

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Easy to Achieve)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:**  10% Discount on Next Purchase
    * **Trigger:** New customer makes *any* purchase.
    * **Frequency:**  One-time use per referrer.
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Reward:**  10% Discount on First Purchase
    * **Trigger:** First purchase after signup.


**Tier 2: Mid-Level Referral (Requires More Engagement)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** $10 Store Credit
    * **Trigger:** New customer makes a purchase over $50. (This encourages slightly higher value transactions).
    * **Frequency:** One-time use per referrer.
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Reward:** Free Shipping on First Order (or a small welcome gift – e.g., a sample size product).



**Tier 3:  Top-Tier Referral (For Your Most Active Advocates)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** $25 Store Credit
    * **Trigger:** New customer makes a purchase over $100 *and* remains an active customer for 60 days. (This rewards sustained loyalty).
    * **Frequency:** One-time use per referrer.
* **For the New Customer:**
    * **Reward:**  Exclusive Early Access to New Products/Collections or a Limited Edition Item. (Creates a feeling of VIP status).



**3.  Incentives Beyond Monetary Rewards (Important for Brand Building)**

* **Gamification:**  Consider awarding points for referrals, redeemable for discounts or other rewards.
* **Leaderboard:** (If appropriate for your brand)  Showcase top referrers – this can be motivating.
* **Social Sharing:**  Make it easy for referrers to share their referral links on social media.  Consider small bonuses for sharing.
* **Community Recognition:**  Feature top referrers on your blog or social media – highlight their success.

**4.  Promotional Materials & Channels:**

* **Website:** Prominently display the "Spread the Word Rewards" program on your homepage, product pages, and checkout.
* **Email Marketing:** Send welcome emails to new customers, highlighting the referral program.  Also, send targeted emails to existing customers reminding them to refer.
* **Social Media:**  Create engaging posts showcasing the referral program and its benefits.  Run contests and promotions related to the program.
