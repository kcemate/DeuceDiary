# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, covering different aspects and levels of complexity, with options to tailor it to your specific business.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Catchy Name:**  "Spread the Word," "Refer & Reward," "Get Paid to Share," “[Your Brand Name] Community Boost” – Choose something that aligns with your brand.
* **Visuals:**  Consider a simple logo or graphic for the program to easily identify it.


**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **How it Works:**
    * **Referrer:** The person who refers a new customer.
    * **New Customer:** The person referred by the referrer.
    * **Referral Link/Code:** Each referrer gets a unique link or code to share.
    * **Tracking:**  You *must* have a system to track referrals – ideally through your website, app, or CRM.
* **Eligibility:**  Clearly define who can participate – existing customers, employees, etc.
* **Duration:**  Decide if the program is ongoing or has a specific start and end date. (Starting with a limited-time promotion can create urgency.)


**3. Incentives - Tiered Approach (This is crucial for motivation!)**

Here’s a breakdown of incentive tiers, ranging from lower to higher value:

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Everyone)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  Small discount (e.g., 10% off next purchase, Free Shipping)
    * **New Customer Reward:** Small welcome discount or a small gift (e.g., 5% off their first purchase).
    * **Rationale:** Gets people started and provides immediate value.
* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level Referral (After a certain number of referrals)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  Larger discount (e.g., 20% off), Gift Card to your store (e.g., $25),  Exclusive Product Access.
    * **New Customer Reward:**  Enhanced Welcome Offer (e.g., 15% off + free gift).
    * **Rationale:**  Encourages more frequent referrals and rewards consistent effort.
* **Tier 3:  Top-Tier Referral (For high-performing referrers - Set a threshold, like 5-10 referrals)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** Significant discount (e.g., 30% off), Premium Product, VIP Status, Cash Reward (e.g., $50 - $100),  Experiences related to your brand.
    * **New Customer Reward:**  Highest value welcome offer (e.g., 20% off + premium welcome package).
    * **Rationale:**  Recognizes and rewards the most active and successful referrers, fostering loyalty.

**4.  Payment Structure (Consider these options):**

* **Discount-Based:**  The most common and easiest to implement.
* **Cash Rewards:**  More complex, but can be highly motivating.  Requires a reliable payment system. (Consider PayPal, direct deposit, or gift cards).
* **Points System:**  Referrers earn points for each successful referral, which they can redeem for rewards.

**5. Communication & Promotion:**

* **Dedicated Landing Page:**  Create a page on your website specifically for the referral program, clearly explaining the rules and benefits.
* **Email Marketing:**  Automated emails to:
    * Welcome new customers and invite them to participate.
    * Notify referrers when someone they've referred makes a purchase.
    * Remind people about the program.
* **Social Media:**  Promote the program on all your social media channels.
