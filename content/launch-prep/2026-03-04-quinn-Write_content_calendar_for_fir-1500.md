Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service. This calendar is designed to build excitement, gather feedback, and drive initial adoption. It's a flexible template - tailor it to your specific product, target audience, and marketing goals. 

**Assumptions:**

* **You've just launched your product/service.**
* **You have a basic social media presence (at least Instagram, Facebook, and maybe Twitter/X).**
* **You’re focusing on driving awareness, excitement, and early user acquisition.**


**Key Themes for Month 1:**

* **Celebration & Thank You:** Acknowledge the launch and thank your audience.
* **"How It Works" & Value Proposition:** Clearly communicate what your product does and why it’s valuable.
* **Early User Stories:** Showcase the benefits through real-world examples.
* **Community Building:** Encourage interaction and feedback.
* **Addressing FAQs:**  Proactively answer common questions.
