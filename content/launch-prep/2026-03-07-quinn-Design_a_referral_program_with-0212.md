Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, combining different strategies to maximize engagement and attract new customers:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** “Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love” or “Bring a Friend, Get Rewarded” - Keep it catchy and emphasizes the benefit.
* **Visuals:** Use a consistent logo/graphic for the program that’s easily recognizable.


**2. Program Structure & Tiers:**

We’ll use a tiered system to reward higher levels of participation. This encourages people to refer multiple times.

* **Tier 1: New Referral (Basic Reward)**
    * **Referrer:** The person making the referral.
    * **Referee:** The person being referred.
    * **Reward:**  $10 Store Credit / 10% off their first purchase. (This is a good starting point to incentivize initial sign-ups).
* **Tier 2: Active Referral (Mid-Level Reward)**
    * **Referrer:**  Has successfully referred at least 2 people who have made a purchase.
    * **Reward:** $25 Store Credit / 15% off their next purchase OR a Free [Small Product/Accessory related to your brand - e.g., small brush, extra filter, etc.]
* **Tier 3: Super Referral (Top-Level Reward)**
    * **Referrer:** Has successfully referred at least 5 people who have made a purchase.
    * **Reward:** $50 Store Credit / 20% off their next purchase + Exclusive access to a new product launch or VIP event.


**3. Incentives for Referrers & Referees:**

* **Referrer Rewards:** (As outlined above - tiered)
* **Referee Rewards:** (Crucial for getting initial sign-ups)
    * **10% off their first order:** This is a classic and effective incentive.
    * **Free Shipping:**  Reduces a common barrier to purchase.
    * **Welcome Gift:** (Small, relevant product or discount code) – Great for first-time engagement.
    * **Bonus Points (if you have a loyalty program):**  Stacking rewards is powerful.


**4. Referral Method & Tracking:**

* **Simple Shareable Links:** Generate unique referral links for each user. These should be:
    * **Short & Easy to Type:**  (Bit.ly or similar link shorteners are helpful)
    * **Trackable:** Using a referral program platform is ideal.
* **Social Media Integration:** Make it easy for people to share on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.  Provide pre-written captions.
* **Email:** Send automated emails to both referrers and referees when a referral is made.
* **Dedicated Landing Page:**  Create a landing page specifically for the referral program with clear instructions and visual examples.
* **Referral Program Platform:** Consider using a platform like:
    * **ReferralCandy:** (Popular, integrates with many e-commerce platforms)
    * **Friendbuy:** (Focuses on simplified referral programs)
    * **Upward:** (Powerful features for tracking and optimization)

* **Launch Announcement:**  Announce the program across all channels - website, social media, email, etc.
