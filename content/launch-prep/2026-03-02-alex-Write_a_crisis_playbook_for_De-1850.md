## Deuce Diary Launch: Crisis Playbook

This playbook outlines a structured approach to handling potential crises during the launch of "Deuce Diary," focusing on swift, decisive action and clear communication. It’s divided into scenarios with specific steps, assigning roles and outlining escalation paths.

**Overall Principles:**

* **Transparency is Key:** Be honest with users, acknowledging issues and outlining solutions.
* **Speed of Response:** Time is critical, especially in the initial hours and days.
* **Prioritize User Experience:** Focus on resolving user issues and mitigating negative impacts.
* **Centralized Communication:** Designate a single point of contact for all crisis-related information.
* **Documentation:**  Maintain a detailed log of all actions taken, communications, and issues reported.


**I. Scenario: Server Crash**

* **Likelihood:** High, especially during peak launch periods.
* **Impact:** Lost users, frustration, potential negative PR.
* **Trigger:**  High error rates in the app, inability to access servers, reports of widespread outages.
* **Immediate Actions (0-60 minutes):**
    * **Team Lead (CTO/Lead Engineer):**  Confirm the crash. Initiate immediate troubleshooting – isolate the problem, check resource usage, restart servers.
    * **Communications Manager:** Draft a brief, empathetic message to users on social media and within the app (if possible) acknowledging the issue and stating “We’re working on it! Our team is investigating and we’ll provide updates soon.”
    * **Support Team:**  Prepare a FAQ addressing the issue and directing users to the most recent status updates.
* **Short-Term Actions (60-24 hours):**
    * **Continuous Monitoring:**  Round-the-clock monitoring of servers and app performance.
    * **Root Cause Analysis:**  Identify the cause of the crash.
    * **Scaling Resources:**  Increase server capacity to handle the load.
    * **Public Update:** Release a more detailed update on social media and the app store listing explaining the issue, the steps being taken, and an estimated time to resolution. Offer apologies.
* **Long-Term Actions:** Implement robust monitoring and auto-scaling infrastructure.  Run load tests proactively.



**II. Scenario: 1-Star Reviews & Negative Feedback**

* **Likelihood:** Medium – user feedback is crucial, but negative initial reactions are normal.
* **Impact:** Damage to reputation, potential user churn.
* **Trigger:**  Spike in 1-star reviews, widespread complaints about specific features, bugs, or content.
* **Immediate Actions (0-60 minutes):**
    * **Community Manager:** Monitor review platforms (App Store, Google Play, Reddit, etc.) and social media. Flag particularly critical comments.
    * **Team Lead:** Analyze the recurring themes in the negative feedback.
* **Short-Term Actions (60-24 hours):**
    * **Prioritize Bug Fixes:** Focus on addressing the most frequently reported issues.
    * **Respond to Reviews:** Publicly address common concerns – acknowledge the frustrations, offer solutions (if possible), and thank users for their feedback.
    * **Internal Feedback Loop:** Share the feedback with the development team to prioritize fixes and feature improvements.
* **Long-Term Actions:** Implement a robust feedback system within the app.  Actively engage with user communities.
