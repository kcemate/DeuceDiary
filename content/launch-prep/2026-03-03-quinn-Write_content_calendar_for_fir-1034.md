**Key:**

*   **Social Media (SM):**  Focus on platforms where your audience hangs out (Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, TikTok, LinkedIn, etc.)
*   **Blog:** Longer-form content, SEO focused.
*   **Email:**  Nurturing leads, onboarding new users.

*   **User-Generated Content (UGC):** Encourage customers to share their experiences.
