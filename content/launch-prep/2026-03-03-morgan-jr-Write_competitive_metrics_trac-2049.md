**I. Executive Summary**

* **Purpose:** To consistently monitor and analyze key competitive metrics to identify opportunities for improvement, inform strategic decision-making, and maintain a competitive advantage.
* **Frequency:**  This plan should be reviewed and updated quarterly, with more frequent monitoring (weekly/monthly) of critical metrics.
* **Ownership:**  [Assign responsibility - e.g., Marketing Analyst, Competitive Intelligence Team, Head of Strategy] – Crucially, this needs buy-in from leadership.


**II. Defining Your Competitors**

* **Tiered Approach:** Don’t try to track *everyone*. Segment competitors into tiers based on their significance:
    * **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):**  Companies offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Coca-Cola vs. Pepsi) - Deepest analysis.
    * **Tier 2 (Adjacent Competitors):** Companies offering related products/services or targeting a slightly different segment. (e.g., Spotify vs. Apple Music) – Moderate analysis.
    * **Tier 3 (Potential Future Competitors):** Emerging companies or larger companies expanding into your market. – Light monitoring.
* **Competitor List:**  Create a spreadsheet or database documenting:
    * Company Name
    * Website URL
    * Industry Classification
    * Target Audience
    * Key Products/Services
    * Business Model

This is the heart of the plan.  We'll break it down into categories:

**A. Marketing & Sales Metrics:**

* **Website Traffic:** (Google Analytics) – Sessions, Unique Visitors, Bounce Rate, Pages per Session, Average Session Duration.  *Competitive Comparison:*  Benchmark against your Tier 1 competitors.
* **Lead Generation:** (CRM, Marketing Automation) – Number of Leads, Lead Conversion Rate, Cost Per Lead. *Competitive Comparison:* Compare lead volume and conversion rates.
* **Social Media Engagement:** (Social Media Analytics) – Followers, Likes, Shares, Comments, Reach, Engagement Rate. *Competitive Comparison:* Track brand sentiment and compare engagement levels.
* **Search Engine Rankings:** (SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz) – Keyword Rankings, Domain Authority. *Competitive Comparison:* Identify keywords competitors rank for that you don’t.
* **Advertising Spend & Performance:** (Google Ads, Social Media Ads) – Impressions, Clicks, CTR, Cost Per Click, Conversion Rate, ROI. *Competitive Comparison:* Compare ad spend and performance metrics.
* **Email Marketing:** Open Rates, Click-Through Rates, Conversion Rates.

**B. Product & Service Metrics:**

* **New Product Launches:** Track frequency, features, and market reception.
* **Product Reviews & Ratings:** (Trustpilot, G2, Capterra, Amazon Reviews) - Monitor sentiment and identify areas for product improvement. *Competitive Comparison:*  See how your product stacks up against theirs.
* **Feature Adoption:** (Internal Data) – Track which features are most popular.
* **Pricing:** Regularly monitor competitor pricing structures.
