Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product/service. This is a template – you'll need to tailor it heavily to *your* specific product, target audience, and launch strategy. I've included notes and suggestions for each day to help you flesh it out.

**Assumptions:**

*   **You've already done some initial pre-launch buzz.** This calendar focuses on the *immediate* post-launch momentum.
*   **You have a core set of channels:**  Website, Social Media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn - adjust to where your audience is), Email.
*   **You're tracking analytics.**  This calendar is about driving engagement and gathering data.

**Key Themes for Month 1:**

*   **Celebration & Gratitude:**  Thank your early adopters!
*   **Education:**  Show people *how* to use your product/service and why it's valuable.
*   **Social Proof:**  Highlight positive feedback and testimonials.
*   **Community Building:** Encourage interaction and engagement.
*   **Addressing FAQs:**  Proactively answer common questions.

**Content Calendar - Month 1 Post-Launch**

**Week 1: Launch Week - Excitement & Introduction**

*   **Day 1 (Launch Day):**
    *   **All Channels:** Official Launch Announcement!  High-energy post/video.  Link to purchase/sign-up. Run a launch day promotion (discount, free trial, etc.).  *Key Metric: Website Traffic, Sales, Sign-ups*
    *   **Example:** “🎉 IT’S HERE! [Product Name] is LIVE!  Solve [Problem] and [Benefit] today.  Get 20% off for the first 24 hours! [Link] #NewProduct #LaunchDay”
*   **Day 2:** “Behind the Scenes” – Short video or story showing the development process, the team, or the inspiration behind the product. *Key Metric: Engagement - Likes, Shares, Comments*
*   **Day 3:**  Tutorial 1:  “Getting Started with [Product Name]” –  A quick, visual guide to the core functionality.  (Short video - 60-90 seconds). *Key Metric: Video Views, Click-Through Rate to Product Page*
*   **Day 4:**  User Testimonial (If available) - Share a positive quote from an early adopter. *Key Metric: Social Shares*
*   **Day 5:**  Q&A – Address frequently asked questions in a short video or a dedicated social media post. *Key Metric: Questions Received – Analyze to create more content*
*   **Day 6:**  "Did You Know?" – Highlight a lesser-known feature. *Key Metric: Engagement*
*   **Day 7:**  Reminder of Launch Promotion & Recap of Week 1. *Key Metric: Sales/Sign-ups*



**Week 2: Deep Dive & Value Demonstration**

*   **Day 8:** Tutorial 2:  “Advanced Feature” – Showcasing a more complex feature. *Key Metric: Video Views, Click-Through Rate*
*   **Day 9:**  Blog Post: “5 Ways [Product Name] Can Improve Your [Area of Life/Business]” –  Provide detailed value propositions. *Key Metric: Blog Traffic, Time on Page*
*   **Day 10:**  Social Media Contest/Giveaway –  Increase engagement and brand awareness. (e.g., “Share this post for a chance to win…”). *Key Metric: Contest Entries, Follower Growth*
