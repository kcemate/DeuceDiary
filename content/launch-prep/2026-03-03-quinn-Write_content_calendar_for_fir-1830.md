Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a new product or service, broken down by week and category. This calendar is a template – **you'll need to customize it heavily to fit your specific product, target audience, and launch goals.**  I’ve included estimated frequency suggestions, but adjust based on your resources and analytics.

**Assumptions:**

* **You've already done pre-launch buzz and have an initial audience.**  This calendar focuses on sustaining momentum and driving conversions.
* **You're using a mix of channels:** This calendar considers blog posts, social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn - adjust based on your target audience), email marketing, and potentially short-form video (TikTok, Reels).
* **“Launch” is defined as the official release.**

**Overall Goals for Month 1:**

* **Awareness:** Introduce your product to a wider audience.
* **Engagement:**  Start building a community around your product.
* **Conversion:** Drive initial sales or sign-ups.
* **Gather Feedback:** Understand user reactions and identify areas for improvement.
