Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health & wellness brands, tailored for different types of partnerships and levels of formality. I’ve included variations for different contexts (influencers, complementary brands, media outlets) and provided guidance on customizing them.

**Template 1: Influencer Outreach (Focus: Authentic Connection & Engagement)**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand Name] & [Influencer Name]**

**Body:**

Hi [Influencer Name],

My name is [Your Name] and I'm the [Your Title] at [Your Brand Name], a brand focused on [briefly describe your brand and mission - 1-2 sentences].  I've been a big fan of your content, particularly [mention a specific post/video you genuinely liked – this shows you've done your research!]. We really admire your passion for [mention influencer's niche/values] and how you authentically connect with your audience.

We believe there’s a strong synergy between our brands. [ Briefly explain *why* your brand aligns with the influencer's audience - e.g., “Your audience is clearly interested in mindful living, and we’re creating products designed to support that.”]

We'd love to explore a potential collaboration, such as:

*   **[Option 1: Sponsored Post]:**  A dedicated post/reel featuring [Your Product] and your authentic experience.
*   **[Option 2: Story Series]:**  A series of stories showcasing your routine incorporating [Your Product].
*   **[Option 3:  Giveaway]:**  Collaborating on a giveaway to reach a wider audience.

We're open to your creative ideas too! We're happy to discuss a budget of [Range - e.g., $XXX - $XXX] and offer [mention any incentives – e.g., free product, affiliate commission].

You can learn more about us here: [Link to your website]

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to chat about this further?

Best regards,

[Your Name]

[Your Title]

[Your Brand Name]

[Your Website]

[Your Social Media Link(s)]



**Customization Notes:**

*   **Personalization is KEY:**  Generic emails get ignored.  Research the influencer’s content *deeply*.
*   **Be Specific:**  Don't just say “we’d like to collaborate.”  Outline concrete ideas.
*   **Tiered Approach:**  Consider different levels of collaboration (micro-influencers, mid-tier, macro-influencers) and adjust the budget/scope accordingly.
