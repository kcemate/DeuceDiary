Okay, let's build a revenue optimization plan. This will be a comprehensive framework, and the level of detail needed will depend on the specific business and its goals. Here’s a plan broken down into phases, with actionable steps and considerations:

**Phase 1: Assessment & Diagnosis (Weeks 1-4)**

* **Goal:** Understand the current state of revenue generation – what’s working, what’s not, and why.
* **Key Activities:**
    * **Revenue Audit:** Deep dive into all revenue streams:
        * **Product/Service Revenue:** Analyze sales figures by product/service, pricing tiers, and sales channels.
        * **Subscription Revenue:**  Churn rate, customer lifetime value (CLTV), renewal rates.
        * **Advertising/Affiliate Revenue:** CPM, CPC, conversion rates.
    * **Customer Segmentation:** Define key customer segments based on demographics, behavior, needs, and value. (e.g., High-Value, New Users, At-Risk).
    * **Channel Analysis:** Evaluate the performance of each sales channel (e.g., website, sales team, retail, online marketplaces).
    * **Pricing Analysis:** Review current pricing strategies – are they aligned with value perception, competitor pricing, and cost structure?
    * **Competitive Analysis:** Understand competitor pricing, offerings, and strategies.
    * **Customer Feedback:** Gather feedback through surveys, interviews, and social listening.  Focus on *why* customers buy, and what prevents them from buying.
    * **Identify Key Metrics:** Establish baseline KPIs for: Revenue Growth, CLTV, CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost), Churn Rate, Conversion Rates (across all stages of the funnel), Average Order Value (AOV), and Website Traffic.
* **Deliverables:**
    * Revenue Report (detailed breakdown of revenue streams)
    * Customer Segmentation Profile
    * Channel Performance Report
    * Pricing Recommendation Report
    * Competitive Analysis Document
