**I. January - New Year, New App (Focus: Resolutions & Productivity)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Productivity
* **Dates:** January 1st - January 31st
* **App Store:**
    * **Featured Banner:** Highlight apps that support New Year’s resolutions (fitness, meditation, budgeting, learning).
    * **Updated Screenshots/Video:** Showcase how your app helps users achieve their goals.
    * **Promotional Offers:**  "New Year, New You" - Limited-time discounts or free trial extensions.
* **Platforms:**
    * **Social Media (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok):**  Content focusing on “setting intentions,” productivity tips, inspirational quotes, user-generated content challenge ("Show us your goal tracker!").
    * **Paid Advertising:** Target users interested in self-improvement, productivity, and goal-setting.
* **Metrics:**  Downloads, Trial Sign-ups, In-App Purchase Starts (if applicable).


**II. February - Love & Connection (Focus: Relationship Apps & Sharing)**

* **Dates:** February 1st - February 28th (or leading up to Valentine's Day)
* **Theme:** Romance, Connection, Sharing Experiences
* **App Store:**

    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Keywords like "Valentine's Day gifts," "date ideas," “relationship app.”
* **Platforms:**
    * **Social Media:**  Content around dating tips, gift ideas, romantic inspiration, contests (e.g., "Share your date night idea with #My[AppName]”).
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relationship/lifestyle influencers.
* **Metrics:** Downloads, App Usage (e.g., matching rates for dating apps), In-App Purchases (e.g., premium features for gifting).
