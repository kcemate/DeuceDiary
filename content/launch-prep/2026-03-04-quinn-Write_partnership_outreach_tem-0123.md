**Template 1: Influencer Outreach - Focus on Shared Values & Engagement**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand Name] & [Influencer Name]**

**Body:**

Hi [Influencer Name],

I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand Name], a [brief, compelling description of your brand - e.g., "brand focused on sustainable yoga apparel," "wellness app helping busy professionals prioritize self-care"]. 

I’ve been a huge fan of your content on [mention specific platform - e.g., Instagram, YouTube] and really admire your commitment to [mention something specific you admire - e.g., mindful living, holistic health, realistic fitness].  We both share a passion for [shared value – e.g., empowering others to live healthier lives, promoting positive mental health].

We were really impressed with your recent post about [mention a specific post they did that resonated], and we believe our brand and your audience would be a great fit.

We’d love to explore a potential partnership where you could [suggest specific collaboration ideas - e.g.,  review our product, create a workout routine featuring our apparel, share a discount code with your followers]. 

You can learn more about us here: [Link to your website]

Would you be open to a quick chat to discuss this further?

Best regards,

[Your Name]

[Your Title]

[Your Brand Name]

[Link to your Instagram/Social Media]


**Notes:**

*   **Personalization is key:**  Don’t just copy and paste. Mention specific content.
*   **Start small:** For new influencers, consider offering a product sample instead of a large fee.
*   **Engagement:** Show you’ve actually engaged with their content before reaching out.
