Okay, here are email templates for beta testers, friends, and influencer outreach, tailored to different levels of formality and goals.  I've included variations and notes to help you customize them.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template (Formal & Detailed)**

**Subject: Invitation to Beta Test [Product Name] - Your Feedback is Key!**

**Body:**

Hi [Beta Tester Name],

We're thrilled to invite you to be one of the first to experience [Product Name], our [brief product description - e.g., new productivity app, innovative fitness tracker, upcoming game]. We're in the final stages of beta testing and your valuable feedback will be absolutely crucial in ensuring a smooth and successful launch.

**What You'll Be Doing:**

As a beta tester, you'll be asked to:

*   Use [Product Name] for [duration - e.g., 2 weeks, one month].
*   Complete a series of tasks designed to test key features.
*   Provide detailed feedback on your experience – including what worked well, what was confusing, and any bugs you encountered.
*   Share your screen/audio (if applicable - specify if recording is involved).

**What You’ll Get:**

*   Exclusive access to [Product Name] before its public release.
*   Recognition in our launch announcement (if applicable - e.g., "Thank you to [Beta Tester Name] for their invaluable feedback!").
*   [Optional: Small incentive - e.g., a discount code for the full release, early access to a premium feature].

**To Get Started:**

[Link to onboarding materials, instructions, or a survey]

Please let us know if you’re able to accept this invitation by [date]. We'll then send you login details and a schedule of tasks.

Thank you for your support!

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team

[Website Link]

[Contact Email]


**Notes:**

*   **Personalize:**  Always address the beta tester by name.
*   **Clearly State Expectations:** Be upfront about the time commitment and what’s expected of them.
*   **Highlight Value:**  Emphasize the benefits of being a beta tester.
*   **Track Progress:**  Use a system to manage beta tester feedback (e.g., a spreadsheet, a dedicated feedback platform).



**2. Email Template for Friends (Casual & Enthusiastic)**

**Subject: You HAVE to try this! [Product Name] Beta**

**Body:**

Hey [Friend's Name],

Guess what?! We're working on something really cool – a [brief, exciting description of the product] called [Product Name]!  We’re in beta testing and would *love* for you to give it a whirl.

It's basically [very simple explanation - one sentence].  I think you'd be particularly interested because [mention a connection to their interests/needs].

You can check it out here: [Link to beta version]

Let me know what you think!  I’m super keen to hear your honest feedback – good or bad!  Seriously, any thoughts are welcome.

Cheers,

[Your Name]

[Link to Product/Website - Optional]



**Notes:**

*   **Keep it Light:**  This is for people you already know. Don't over-explain.
*   **Personal Connection:**  Explain *why* you think they’d be interested.
*   **Easy to Try:**  Make it incredibly easy for them to access the beta version.
*   **Encourage Feedback:**  Let them know you genuinely want their opinion.
