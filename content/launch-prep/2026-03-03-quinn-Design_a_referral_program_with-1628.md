# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives! Here's a breakdown, incorporating different tiers, reward types, and tracking mechanisms, designed to be adaptable to various businesses.

**I. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Example:** “Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love” or “Refer & Reward” - Keep it catchy and reflective of your brand.

**II. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The customer who refers new customers.
* **New Customer:** The customer referred by the referrer.
* **Tracking:** Crucial! You’ll need a system to track referrals - ideally integrated with your CRM, website, or using a dedicated referral marketing platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Ambassador, Friendbuy).

**III. Referral Tiers & Rewards (Progression is Key!)**

We'll use a tiered approach to motivate increased referrals.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Level 1 - Immediate Reward)**

* **Referrer Reward:**  [Small Incentive] – e.g., 10% discount on their next purchase, $5 credit, free shipping.
* **New Customer Reward:** Welcome offer (e.g., 5% off their first purchase, a small free gift with their first order).
* **Trigger:**  A new customer makes their first purchase after being referred.
* **Simple Tracking:** Easy to track through email links or a dedicated referral code.


**Tier 2:  Expanded Reward (Level 2 - After 2 Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:**  [Medium Incentive] – e.g., 20% discount on their next purchase, $15 credit, a small exclusive product or service.
* **New Customer Reward:**  [Slightly larger welcome offer] – e.g., 10% off their first purchase, a free sample.
* **Trigger:** The referrer has successfully referred 2 new customers who've made a purchase.
* **Tracking:**  Updated tracking to recognize the referrer's milestone.


**Tier 3:  VIP/Top Tier (Level 3 - After 5+ Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** [Significant Incentive] – e.g.,  30% discount on their next purchase, $30 credit, early access to new products/services, a personalized gift basket, a monthly subscription upgrade.
* **New Customer Reward:** [Premium welcome offer] – e.g., 15% off their first purchase, a more substantial free gift.
* **Trigger:** The referrer has successfully referred 5+ new customers who’ve made a purchase.
* **Tracking:**  Exclusive benefits and recognition for top referrers.

**IV. Incentive Types - Mix it Up!**

* **Discounts:** (Most common and effective) - Percentage off, fixed dollar amount.
* **Credits:**  Store credit for future purchases.
* **Free Products/Services:**  Offer a sample, trial, or a small related product/service.
* **Exclusive Access:**  Early access to new products, sales, or events.
* **Gift Cards:** (To your brand or a popular retailer).
* **Experiences:** (If applicable - VIP treatment, personalized consultation, etc.)
* **Charitable Donation:** (Option to donate a percentage of the new customer’s purchase to a charity - great for socially conscious brands).


**V.  Referral Codes & Tracking**

* **Unique Codes:** Generate unique referral codes for each customer. This allows you to accurately track referrals.
* **Trackable Links:**  Use trackable links that include the referral code in the URL.
* **Dashboard:**  Provide a simple dashboard for referrers to see their progress, rewards earned, and referrals made.
