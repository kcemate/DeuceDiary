**Program Name:** (Choose a catchy name – e.g., “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “[Your Brand] Squad”)

**I. Core Concept: Tiered Rewards**

We’ll use a tiered system to encourage more referrals. This allows for increasing reward value as a referrer gets more involved.

**II. Tiers & Incentives**

* **Tier 1: The Initial Spark (Basic Referral)**
    * **Referral:** New customer refers a friend.
    * **Reward for Referrer:** $5 Store Credit / Discount Code (10% off next purchase)
    * **Reward for Referred Friend:** 10% off their first order
    * **Rationale:**  This is the lowest barrier to entry.  A small reward immediately incentivizes participation. The discount for the new friend is crucial for conversion.

* **Tier 2:  The Active Promoter (Mid-Level Referral)**
    * **Referral:**  Referral leads to a successful purchase (minimum $50 spent).
    * **Reward for Referrer:** $15 Store Credit / Discount Code (15% off next purchase) + Exclusive “Early Access” to new products or features.
    * **Reward for Referred Friend:** 15% off their first order
    * **Rationale:**  This encourages referrals to actually result in sales.  The higher store credit is a bigger motivator, and "Early Access" adds value and exclusivity.

* **Tier 3: The Top Referral (High-Level Referral)**
    * **Referral:**  5 successful referrals within a 3-month period.
    * **Reward for Referrer:** $50 Store Credit + Free Premium Feature Access (if applicable) +  A personalized thank-you gift (branded item, swag)
    * **Reward for Referred Friend:** 15% off their first order
    * **Rationale:** This is for your most loyal and engaged advocates. The significant reward recognizes their commitment.  The premium feature access is a major value add, and the physical gift creates a personal touch.

* **Unique Referral Link/Code:**  Each referrer needs a unique link or code to track referrals.  (Crucial for accurate attribution!)
* **Referral Platform:** Consider using a dedicated referral marketing platform like:
    * **ReferralCandy:** Popular, robust, integrates with many e-commerce platforms.
    * **Friendbuy:** Specifically designed for referral programs.
    * **GrowTraffic:**  Offers a variety of referral tools.
    * **(Or, if you're on a smaller budget, use a simple Google Forms linked to a spreadsheet).**
* **Automated Email Notifications:**  Automate emails to:
    * **Referrers:**  When a referral is made, when a referral makes a purchase, and reminders about the program.
    * **Referred Friends:**  When they sign up, when they receive their discount code, and a welcome message.
