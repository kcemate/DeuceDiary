## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive organic growth through word-of-mouth marketing. It details the mechanics, viral loop design, fraud prevention, tracking, and implementation timeline.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase active users of Deuce Diary by 20% within 6 months.
* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Drive brand awareness and positive sentiment.
    * Reduce customer acquisition cost.
    * Build a community around the app.


**2. Referral Mechanics:**

* **Referrer Rewards:**
    * **Tiered Rewards (Motivates higher referral volume):**
        * **Level 1 (1-5 referrals):** 1 Month Premium Subscription (Devalue slightly to $4.99 to incentivize)
        * **Level 2 (6-10 referrals):** $25 Amazon Gift Card
        * **Level 3 (11+ referrals):** $50 Amazon Gift Card + Featured Profile Spotlight in the App (boost visibility)
* **Referee Rewards:**
    * **Free 1-Month Premium Subscription:** Attract new users and let them experience the full potential of the app.
* **Referral Link Generation:** Users can generate unique referral links via:
    * In-App Button (Most prominent)
    * Sharing via Social Media (Direct links for sharing)
    * Email Invitation 

**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Trigger:** New user signs up through a referral link.
* **Action:**  The referee receives the free 1-month premium subscription.  They're encouraged to explore the app.
* **Reward:** The referrer receives their chosen reward based on the number of referrals they generate.
* **Loop:**  Referrers then actively promote the app to their network, creating a sustainable growth loop.
* **Gamification (Optional - to be tested post-launch):**  Leaderboard showcasing top referrers (with their permission) - adds a competitive element.


**4. Fraud Prevention:**

* **Account Verification:**  Implement measures to prevent fake accounts (e.g., CAPTCHA, email verification, phone verification).
* **Unique Referral Links:**  Ensure each user has a unique referral link to prevent multiple accounts from being created using the same link.
* **Activity Monitoring:**  Monitor referral activity for suspicious patterns (e.g., sudden spikes in referrals from a single IP address).
* **Manual Review:**  Establish a process for manual review of flagged referrals.
* **Thresholds:** Set minimum referral activity thresholds (e.g., minimum referrals needed to qualify for a reward) to deter fraudulent behavior.
* **Amazon Gift Card Tracking:**  Require redemption of the Amazon Gift Card with a unique code to verify actual usage.

* **Platform:**  Firebase Analytics (Cost-effective, robust for mobile apps)
* **Metrics:**
    * **Referral Conversions:** Track the number of users signing up through referral links.

    * **Referee Activation:** Track how many referees actually start using the app after receiving the free subscription.
