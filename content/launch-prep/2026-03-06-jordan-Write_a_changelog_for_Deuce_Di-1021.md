**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is the initial release of the Deuce Diary, a personal journal application built for minimalist reflection and exploration. This version introduces the core features and provides a solid foundation for future development.

**New Features:**

* **Journal Entry Creation:**  You can now create new journal entries with a simple text editor.  Save your thoughts, feelings, and observations!
* **Date and Time Stamping:** Each entry automatically includes a timestamp, making it easy to track your entries over time.
* **Basic Entry Listing:**  Entries are displayed in chronological order, allowing you to browse through your past thoughts.
* **Save & Load Functionality:**  Entries are persistently saved to your device, and can be loaded for editing.
* **Simple Text Editor:**  A basic text editor is included within the app for writing and formatting your entries.  (Currently supports basic text formatting – bolding via click and select).
* **Dark Mode Support:**  A dark mode theme is available, designed to be easier on the eyes in low-light conditions.  (Toggle in Settings)

* **Syncing Not Implemented:**  No cloud syncing or backup functionality is currently implemented.  All data is stored locally.
