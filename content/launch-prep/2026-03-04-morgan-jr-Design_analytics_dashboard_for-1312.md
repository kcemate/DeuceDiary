**II. Dashboard Structure (Sections & Key Metrics):**

We'll organize the dashboard into several key sections for clarity:

**1. High-Level Summary (Top Section - KPI Tiles)**

* **Total Users:**  Overall number of registered users. (Trend line - Daily/Weekly) - *Crucial for understanding initial growth.*
* **New Users (Last 7 Days):** Number of new users signing up. (Trend line) - *Indicates the pace of acquisition.*
* **Active Users (Last 7 Days):**  Number of users who have performed *any* action in the last 7 days (ideally broken down into Daily and Weekly).  (Trend line) - *This is the most important metric -  Shows engagement.*
* **Retention Rate (7 Day, 30 Day):** Percentage of users returning after 7 and 30 days. (Percentage) - *A critical indicator of long-term value.*
* **Conversion Rate (Signup to Active):** Percentage of users who sign up and then become active users. (Percentage) - *Measures the quality of your signup process and initial value proposition.*

**2. Acquisition Channels (Section 2 - Breakdown)**

* **Source/Channel Breakdown:**  Pie chart or stacked bar chart showing the proportion of new users coming from each source.

* **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) (if applicable):** Cost to acquire a new user through each channel.  (Currency) - *Important for optimizing marketing spend.*
* **Channel Conversion Rates:**  Conversion rates specific to each channel (e.g., click-to-signup rate for paid ads). (Percentage)

**3. User Engagement (Section 3 - How Users are Interacting)**

* **Daily/Weekly Diary Entries:** Number of diary entries created per day/week. (Trend line) - *Fundamental metric for a diary app.*
* **Average Diary Entry Length:**  Average words per diary entry. (Number) – *Indicates depth of reflection or content creation.*
* **Features Used:**  Bar chart or heat map showing which features are most frequently used.
    * **Examples:**  Text Entry, Image Upload, Tagging, Mood Tracking, Search, Sharing (if applicable).
* **Time Spent in App (Daily/Weekly):** Average amount of time users spend in the app. (Number) - *Reflects overall engagement.*
* **Most Popular Tags (if applicable):**  Top tags users are applying to their diary entries. (Word Cloud or Table) - *Reveals themes and user interests.*
