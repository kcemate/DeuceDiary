**Month 1 Post-Launch Content Calendar**

**Week 1:  The Excitement & Introduction (High Focus: Awareness & Initial Traction)**

* **Goal:** Generate initial excitement, drive traffic to landing page, collect early adopters.
* **Content Types:**
    * **Monday:** Launch Announcement – (All Channels) –  Big announcement post, video, and blog post.  Highlight key features and benefits. (Effort: High - 8-12 hours)
    * **Tuesday:**  "Behind the Scenes" - (Social Media - Instagram, Facebook, Twitter) – Share a quick video or photo showing the team working on the product/service.  Humanize the brand. (Effort: Low - 2-4 hours)
    * **Wednesday:**  FAQ Post - (Blog, Landing Page) – Address frequently asked questions to clear up any confusion. (Effort: Medium - 4-6 hours)
    * **Thursday:**  Influencer Outreach (if applicable) – Send out early access or review copies to relevant influencers. (Effort: Medium - 6-8 hours - time-sensitive)
    * **Friday:**  Launch Giveaway/Contest - (Social Media) –  Increase engagement and followers. (Effort: Low - 2-4 hours)
* **Estimated Time Investment (Week 1):** 24-40 hours

**Week 2:  Showcase Value & Demonstrate Use (High Focus: User Engagement & Product Understanding)**

* **Goal:**  Show how the product/service solves a problem, drive feature exploration.
* **Content Types:**
    * **Monday:** Tutorial Video - (YouTube, Website, Social Media) –  A short, clear tutorial on how to use a core feature. (Effort: High - 8-12 hours)
    * **Tuesday:**  Customer Testimonial (if available) – Share a quote or short video from an early user. (Effort: Medium - 4-6 hours)
    * **Wednesday:**  "Problem/Solution" Blog Post –  Clearly outline a common problem your product solves and how it does so. (Effort: Medium - 6-8 hours)
    * **Thursday:**  "Tip of the Week" – Short, actionable tip related to your product/service. (Social Media - all platforms) (Effort: Low - 1-2 hours)
    * **Friday:**  Poll/Question on Social Media – Encourage engagement and learn about user preferences. (Effort: Low - 1-2 hours)
* **Estimated Time Investment (Week 2):** 25-44 hours
