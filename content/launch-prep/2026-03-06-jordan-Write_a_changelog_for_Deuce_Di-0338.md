**Deuce Diary - Version 1.0.0 - Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**What's New in Version 1.0.0?**

Deuce Diary is officially released! This marks the beginning of your chronicle as a newly appointed Guardian, tasked with protecting the realm of Aethelgard. This initial release provides the core gameplay experience and sets the stage for future additions.

**Key Features & Changes:**

* **Core Gameplay Established:** You can now:
    * **Explore Aethelgard:** Navigate the initial regions – Blackwood Forest and Silverstream Village – and uncover their secrets.
    * **Manage Your Guardian:**  Adjust your Guardian's stats (Strength, Resilience, Perception) through diligent training.
    * **Complete Introductory Quests:**  Start your journey with the initial quests designed to teach you the basics of Guardian duties and the world's mechanics. (The “Lost Sheep” and “Whispers in the Woods” quests)
    * **Craft Basic Items:** Learn to craft simple remedies and tools from gathered resources.
    * **Interact with NPCs:**  Engage with key characters in Silverstream Village to receive quests and learn about the unfolding story.

* **User Interface (UI):**
    *  A basic, functional UI has been implemented, providing information on your stats, inventory, and quest log.
    *  Initial UI design provides a clear starting point for future improvements.

* **Combat System:**
    *  A basic turn-based combat system is implemented. You'll encounter and battle simple creatures.
    *  Basic attack and defense mechanics are in place.

* **Resource Management:**
    *  Gathering of basic resources (herbs, wood, stone) is introduced.
    *  A simple inventory system allows you to store collected items.

* **Saving & Loading:**
    *  The game now includes basic save and load functionality. This allows you to preserve your progress and return to your adventure whenever you like. (Note: Save data is stored locally on your device.)


**Known Issues & Limitations:**

* **Combat is Basic:** The combat system is simplified and lacks depth. Enemy AI is rudimentary.
* **Limited Exploration:** Only the initial areas of Aethelgard are accessible.
* **UI is Functional, Not Polished:** The UI needs further refinement for a smoother user experience.
* **Occasional Bugs:**  We've identified and addressed some minor bugs, but there may be unforeseen issues.  Please report any bugs you encounter.
* **Inventory Limits:** The inventory has a small capacity.
* **Quest Design:**  Early quests are straightforward and provide a foundation for more complex storylines to come.
