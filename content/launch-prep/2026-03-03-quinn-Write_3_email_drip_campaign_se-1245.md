Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different goal and with varying lengths. I’ve included suggested content, timing, and key metrics to track.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (7 Days - Ideal for New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build a positive first impression, and set expectations for future emails.
* **Audience:** New subscribers who signed up through a website form, landing page, or contest.
* **Tone:** Friendly, welcoming, and informative.

**Sequence:**

* **Day 1: Welcome Email – “We’re So Glad You’re Here!”**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]!
    * **Content:**  Express gratitude for joining, briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values, and offer a small welcome gift (e.g., discount code, free guide download). Include a link to your most popular page.
    * **Timing:** Immediately after signup.
    * **Metrics:** Open Rate, Click-Through Rate (CTR) to welcome gift.
* **Day 3:  Problem/Solution Email – “Are You Facing [Problem]? We Can Help.”**
    * **Subject:** Struggling with [Relevant Problem]?
    * **Content:** Acknowledge a common pain point your audience likely experiences. Briefly explain how your product/service solves it.  Don’t focus solely on selling – provide valuable information.
    * **Timing:** 3 days after Day 1.
    * **Metrics:** Open Rate, CTR to relevant blog post or product page.
* **Day 5:  Brand Story/Values Email – “Our Story & Why We Do What We Do.”**
    * **Subject:**  Meet the Team Behind [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:** Share a brief, authentic story about your brand's origins – why you started, what you believe in. Build trust and connection.
    * **Timing:** 2 days after Day 3.
    * **Metrics:** Open Rate, Time Spent Reading.
* **Day 7:  Resource Recommendation Email – “Here’s What You Need to Know”**
    * **Subject:**  Top [Number] Resources to Help You [Achieve Desired Outcome]
    * **Content:** Curate a list of 3-5 valuable resources (blog posts, videos, templates) related to your industry or product.  Don’t be overly promotional.
    * **Timing:** 7 days after Day 1.
    * **Metrics:** Open Rate, CTR to resources.



**Campaign 2: Lead Nurturing – Product Focused (14 Days - Ideal for Prospects Considering a Purchase)**

* **Goal:**  Educate prospects about specific product features and benefits, address common objections, and move them closer to a sale.
* **Audience:**  Subscribers who have shown interest in a specific product category (e.g., opened emails about a particular product, visited the product page).
* **Tone:**  Helpful, informative, slightly persuasive.

**Sequence:**

* **Day 3: Benefit-Focused Email - “Discover How [Product Name] Can…”**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock [Key Benefit] with [Product Name]
    * **Content:**  Highlight the *primary* benefit of the product. Use imagery and a short, compelling description.
    * **Timing:** 3 days after initial product-related email trigger.
    * **Metrics:** Open Rate, CTR to Product Page.
* **Day 7: Feature Deep Dive – “Behind the Scenes of [Product Name]”**
    * **Subject:**  3 Key Features You Need to Know About [Product Name]
