## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a strategy for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competitors to inform your business decisions and drive growth. It focuses on key metrics, data sources, and reporting to provide actionable insights.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand competitor strategies, identify opportunities for differentiation, and proactively adjust our strategies to maintain or improve our market position.
* **Specific Objectives (Examples):**
    * Identify emerging trends in competitor offerings.
    * Monitor competitor pricing strategies and promotional activities.
    * Assess competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    *  Discover gaps in the market that we can exploit.
    *  Benchmark our performance against key competitors.


**2. Key Competitors & Segmentation:**

* **Identify Core Competitors:**  Start with 3-5 direct competitors. Focus on those with the closest product/service offerings and target audience.
* **Segment Competitors:** Categorize them based on their approach:
    * **Direct Competitors:** Offer similar products/services to the same target audience.
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Offer alternative solutions to the same needs.
    * **Emerging Competitors:** New players entering the market with potentially disruptive strategies.
* **Create Competitor Profiles:**  For each key competitor, document the following:
    * **Company Overview:** Size, History, Mission, Values.
    * **Product/Service Offering:** Key features, pricing, quality, and benefits.
    * **Target Audience:** Demographics, Psychographics, Needs.
    * **Marketing & Sales Strategies:** Website, Social Media, Advertising, Content, Promotions, Distribution Channels.
    * **Strengths & Weaknesses:** Based on market research and analysis.
