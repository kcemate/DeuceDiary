**Purpose:** To provide a framework for responding to potential crises during the launch of “Deuce Diary,” ensuring minimal damage to the brand’s reputation and a swift, effective response.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be honest and upfront with the public whenever possible.
* **Speed:** Rapid response is critical in the initial stages.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge and validate customer concerns.
* **Collaboration:**  Ensure clear communication and coordination between teams.
* **Proactive Monitoring:**  Anticipate potential issues and address them before they escalate.

**III. Response Teams & Roles:**

* **Crisis Command Center:** (Led by: Head of Marketing & Head of Operations)
    * **Responsibilities:** Overall coordination, decision-making, escalation, communication.
* **PR/Communications Team:** (Led by: PR Manager)
    * **Responsibilities:**  Drafting and distributing press releases, managing social media, responding to media inquiries.
* **Technical Support Team:** (Led by: CTO/Lead Developer)
    * **Responsibilities:**  Diagnosing and resolving technical issues, deploying fixes, monitoring system performance.
* **Social Media Team:** (Led by: Social Media Manager)
    * **Responsibilities:**  Monitoring social media channels, responding to comments, escalating concerns, managing sentiment.
* **Legal Counsel:** (Consulted by: Crisis Command Center)
    * **Responsibilities:**  Providing legal guidance, assessing legal risks, drafting legal responses.


**IV. Response Protocol - By Severity Level:**

**A. Low Severity (Technical Glitches, Minor Social Media Complaints):**

* **Response Time:** Immediate (Within 1 hour)
* **Action:**
    * Technical team investigates and resolves.
    * Social media team addresses individual comments politely and directs users to support resources.
    * Monitor sentiment on social media.
* **Communication:** Internal updates only.
