## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**1. Platform Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):**
    * **Purpose:** Real-time discussion, fan art sharing, Q&As, organized events, and building a close-knit community.
    * **Channels:**
        * **#general:**  Announcements, casual chat, general discussion.
        * **#lore:** Deep dives into the world of Deuce Diary, theories, speculation.
        * **#character-discussion:** Focused discussions on specific characters (Deuce, Jack, etc.)
        * **#art-gallery:** Dedicated space for fans to share and appreciate fan art.
        * **#writing-prompts:**  Encourage creative writing inspired by Deuce Diary.

* **Reddit (Secondary Hub - Amplification & Long-Form Discussions):**
    * **Purpose:**  Drive traffic to Discord, promote content, host AMAs (Ask Me Anything), and facilitate broader discussions.
    * **Subreddit:**  r/DeuceDiary
    * **Content Focus:** Links to Discord, image posts, character spotlights, lore discussions, fan art, writing prompts.
