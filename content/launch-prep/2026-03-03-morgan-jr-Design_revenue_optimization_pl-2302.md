* **2. Competitive Analysis:**
    * Identify key competitors – their pricing, marketing strategies, product offerings, and customer reviews.
    * SWOT Analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) – focusing on your competitive landscape.
* **3. Customer Segmentation:**
    *  Divide your customer base into meaningful segments based on demographics, behavior, purchase history, and needs.  (Example: High-Value Customers, Occasional Buyers, New Customers)
* **4.  Internal Stakeholder Interviews:**  Talk to sales, marketing, product, and customer service teams to gather insights.
