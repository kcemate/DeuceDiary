**1. Program Name & Theme:** “Share the Deuce” – Simple, memorable, and reinforces the brand.

**2. Mechanics & Rewards:**

* **Referrer Rewards (Getting Referred):**
    * **Tier 1 (Early Adopters - First 100 Referrals):** 1 Free Premium Subscription (1 month) + 5,000 Deuce Points.
    * **Tier 2 (Consistent Referrers):** 1 Free Premium Subscription (1 month) + 2,500 Deuce Points for every 10 referrals.
    * **Tier 3 (Top Performers - Top 100 Referrers):** 1 Free Premium Subscription (3 months) + 10,000 Deuce Points.
* **Referee Rewards (Getting Referred):**
    * **Welcome Discount:** 20% off their first Premium Subscription.
    * **Bonus Deuce Points:** 1,000 Deuce Points upon signing up through a referral link.
* **Deuce Points:**  Loyalty currency earned through referrals, program activity, and potentially future events. Can be redeemed for discounts, merchandise, or exclusive content.
* **Tier Structure:** Clearly defined tiers based on the number of referrals generated.


**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Social Sharing Buttons:** Prominent share buttons on the website and within the app for easy sharing on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and Pinterest.  Deuce Diary content is visually rich – leverage this for shareable visuals.
* **Direct Sharing Links:**  Unique referral links generated for each user, allowing them to share directly via email, messaging apps (WhatsApp, Messenger), and SMS.
* **Influencer Collaboration (Phase 2):** Partner with relevant wellness/lifestyle influencers to promote the referral program to their audience.
* **Gamified Referral Activity:**  Display leaderboards showcasing top referrers (anonymized, with user consent) to encourage competition and motivation.
* **"Refer a Friend & Get a Treat"  – Limited-time Offers:** Occasional promotions like "Refer a friend and get a free ebook" to incentivize immediate sharing.
