**Key Goals for Month 1 Post-Launch:**

* **Awareness:** Get the word out about your product.
* **Adoption:** Encourage initial users to try the product.

* **Feedback:** Collect early user feedback for product improvements.
* **Education:** Help users understand how to use the product effectively.
