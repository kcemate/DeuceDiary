**A/B Test Setup (Per Variation - Example: App Icon)**

* **Split:** 50% of users will see the A (Control) icon, 50% will see the B (Variation) icon.
* **Traffic Allocation:** Aim for a statistically significant sample size.  Calculate this based on your app’s download volume.  Generally, a minimum of 100-200 installs per variation is recommended, but more is always better.
* **Testing Duration:** 2-3 days *per variation* to allow for sufficient data collection.
* **Data Collection Frequency:** Monitor metrics every 6-12 hours.
