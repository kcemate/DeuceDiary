**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Awareness (Focus: New Subscribers - 7 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, establish trust, and nurture the new subscriber.
* **Trigger:** Subscriber signs up for your email list (via website, landing page, etc.)
* **Timing:** 7 days
* **Sequence:**
    * **Day 1: Welcome Email – “Thanks for Joining!”**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:**  A warm, friendly welcome. Thank them for subscribing. Briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values. Offer a small incentive (e.g., a free guide, discount code) – “As a thank you, here’s [incentive].”  Include a clear call to action (CTA) – “Explore our website!” (Link to your homepage).
    * **Day 3:  Problem/Solution Email – “Are You Struggling With [Relevant Pain Point]?”**
        * **Subject:**  Tired of [Pain Point]? We Can Help.
        * **Content:**  Identify a common problem your target audience faces.  Briefly explain how your product/service solves it.  Don’t just sell; focus on offering value and empathy.  Include a relevant image or short video. CTA: "Learn More About Our Solution" (Link to a specific product page or blog post).
    * **Day 5:  Behind-the-Scenes/Story Email – “Meet the Team/Our Story”**
        * **Subject:**  The Story Behind [Your Brand Name]
        * **Content:** Humanize your brand. Share a bit about your team, the origins of your company, or a recent project.  Focus on authenticity and connection.  CTA: "Read Our Full Story" (Link to your “About Us” page).
    * **Day 7:  Reminder & Value Add Email – “Don't Miss Out!”**
        * **Subject:**  Still Interested in [Your Brand]?  Here's More!
        * **Content:**  Recap the benefits of joining your list.  Offer another piece of valuable content – a link to a popular blog post, case study, or free resource. CTA: "Browse Our Blog" or "Download Our Free [Resource]."
