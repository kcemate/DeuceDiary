Okay, here’s a comprehensive social media influencer outreach template kit specifically designed for micro-influencers (health, humor, lifestyle), including DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief. This kit is adaptable – adjust the tone and specifics to fit your brand’s voice.

**I. DM Template Kit (Segmented by Niche)**

**A. General Introductory DM (All Niches - Send First)**

* **Option 1 (Friendly & Casual):** “Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Just stumbled across your page and I'm seriously impressed with [mention something specific you genuinely liked – e.g., your sense of humor, your healthy recipes, your travel photos]. You’ve got a really engaged audience!  We're [Your Brand Name], and we’re [briefly state what you do & your mission - one sentence]. Would love to chat about potentially working together!”
* **Option 2 (More Formal - Use if your brand is more polished):** "Hi [Influencer Name], I'm [Your Name] from [Your Brand Name]. I've been a big fan of your content and particularly appreciate your focus on [mention a key theme/value]. We're looking to collaborate with creators who share our passion for [relevant area].  Would you be open to a quick chat?"


**B. Health Influencers**

* **Follow-up (after general intro):** "Specifically, we're passionate about [mention a specific area - e.g., mindful eating, fitness for busy moms, sustainable wellness].  Do you have any tips you could share with your audience around this topic?  We'd love to hear them!” 
* **Example - if they've posted about a specific product:** “Saw your recent post on [product] - it looks amazing! We're developing similar products focused on [mention your brand's relevant area]. “


**C. Humor Influencers**

* **Follow-up (after general intro):** “Your content always makes me laugh! We’re a brand that’s all about spreading joy and lightheartedness - something we admire in you.  We’re thinking about creating some fun content and would love to brainstorm ideas with you.”
* **Example (after observing a trend):** “Obsessed with your take on [current trend]!  We were just thinking about how hilarious it would be to create content around that. “


**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **Follow-up (after general intro):** “Your aesthetic is *amazing*!  We're passionate about [mention your brand's core values related to lifestyle - e.g., slow living, travel, conscious consumption]. We'd love to see if there's a way to align our brands.”
* **Example:** "Love how you showcase [specific aspect of their lifestyle - e.g., your cozy home decor, your adventurous travels] – it's so inspiring!"

**Important Notes for ALL DMs:**

* **Personalization is Key:** *Always* mention something specific you like about their content. Generic DMs get ignored.
* **Keep it Short:**  Influencers are busy. Respect their time.
* **Call to Action:** End with a clear question or invitation (e.g., "Would you be open to a quick chat?").
