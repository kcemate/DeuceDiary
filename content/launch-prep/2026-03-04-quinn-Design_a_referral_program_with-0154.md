# Design a referral program with incentives.

Okay, let's design a referral program with a tiered approach and compelling incentives. Here's a breakdown, incorporating best practices and aiming for engagement:

**Program Name:** (Example) "Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love" or "Refer & Reward"

**1. Core Mechanics & Structure**

* **Simple Referral Link:**  Every user should receive a unique referral link or code. This is crucial for tracking.
* **Two-Tiered Structure (Recommended):** This boosts motivation.
    * **Tier 1 (Referrer):** The person who refers a new customer.
    * **Tier 2 (Referee):** The new customer brought in by the referrer.
* **Tracking:** Use a reliable referral tracking platform.  Many CRM systems have built-in referral features, or you can integrate with tools like ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, or ReferralWise.
* **Clear Terms & Conditions:**  Be transparent about rules – what counts as a referral, how long rewards last, and any limitations.


**2. Incentives – Tiered Rewards**

Here’s a suggested tiered incentive structure. Adapt the specifics to your business and budget:

**Tier 1: (Referrer – The person who refers)**

* **Level 1 (First Referral):**  **Small Reward** – 10% Discount Code (e.g., 10% off their next purchase) -  This is an easy, immediate win.
* **Level 2 (Second Referral):** **Mid-Tier Reward** – $25 Gift Card (to your store, or a relevant partner) –  This shows you value their ongoing support.
* **Level 3 (Third Referral & Beyond):** **High-Tier Reward** – Exclusive Early Access to New Products, VIP Status, or a Larger Discount (e.g., 20% off) –  This encourages continued referrals.



**Tier 2: (Referee – The new customer)**

* **Level 1 (First Referral):** **Welcome Discount** – 15% off their first purchase – Incentivizes immediate action.
* **Level 2 (Second Referral):**  **Small Gift/Sample** – (Dependent on your business) –  Could be a small product sample, a branded item, or free shipping.  This creates a positive first experience.



**3. Incentive Values & Considerations**

* **Discount Percentages:** 10%, 15%, 20% are common.  Test what resonates best.
* **Gift Card Values:** $25, $50, $100 - Again, tailor to your audience and the perceived value of your product/service.
* **Product/Sample Value:**  This needs to align with your product margins.  Consider a cost-effective sample.
* **VIP Status:** Define what "VIP" means for your brand (e.g., priority support, exclusive content).

**4. Communication & Promotion**

* **Dedicated Landing Page:**  Create a landing page explaining the program with clear visuals and FAQs.  Link to this page prominently on your website, in emails, and in social media.
* **Email Marketing:**
    * **Welcome Email:**  Introduce the program to new users.
    * **Referral Reminder Emails:**  Send regular reminders to existing users, highlighting the rewards and showing success stories (e.g., "John referred 5 friends and earned a $50 gift card!").
    * **Triggered Emails:** Send an email to a referrer when their referral completes a purchase.
* **Social Media:**  Run contests, share success stories, and use referral-specific hashtags.
* **In-App/On-Site Promotion:**  Make the referral link easily accessible within your app or website.
