## Monitoring and Alerting Setup Guide for a Social App

This guide outlines a comprehensive monitoring and alerting setup for a social app, focusing on key metrics and recommended thresholds. The goal is to proactively identify and address issues impacting user experience and app stability.

**I. Monitoring Components & Tools**

* **Metrics Collection:**
    * **Prometheus:** A popular, open-source time-series database for collecting metrics.  It's highly scalable and integrates well with Grafana.
    * **Telegraf (from InfluxData):**  An agent that can collect various metrics from different sources (servers, databases, applications) and send them to Prometheus.
    * **Application Performance Monitoring (APM) Tools (Optional but recommended):** New Relic, Datadog, Dynatrace – Provide deeper insights into transaction tracing, code-level performance, and dependencies.
* **Visualization & Alerting:**
    * **Grafana:** A powerful dashboarding tool that integrates seamlessly with Prometheus for visualizing metrics.
    * **Alertmanager (Prometheus):** Handles alerts generated by Prometheus based on defined rules.
    * **PagerDuty/Opsgenie/Slack:**  Notification channels for alerts.
