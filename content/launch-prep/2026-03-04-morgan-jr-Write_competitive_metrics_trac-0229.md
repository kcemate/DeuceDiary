## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a structured approach to consistently track and analyze your competitors’ activities, providing actionable insights for strategic decision-making. 

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape, identify opportunities & threats, and inform strategic decisions to improve our performance and market position.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Monitor competitor activities related to marketing, product development, pricing, and customer experience.
    * Identify emerging trends and potential disruptions.
    * Understand competitor strengths and weaknesses.
    *  Develop strategic responses to competitive actions.
    *  Measure the effectiveness of our own competitive strategies.


**II. Key Competitors Identification & Prioritization:**

* **Define “Competitor”:** Broadly, anyone offering a similar product/service to our target audience.
* **Tiered Prioritization (High, Medium, Low):**  Focus efforts based on potential impact.
    * **High:** Direct competitors, largest market share, aggressive marketing.
    * **Medium:**  Similar offerings, significant customer base, emerging trends.
    * **Low:**  Niche players, limited market reach, potential to learn from.
* **Create a Competitor Matrix:**  A spreadsheet listing all identified competitors categorized by tier, with key information captured (see Section IV).
