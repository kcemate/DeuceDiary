**II. CRITICAL SCENARIOS & RESPONSE PROTOCOLS**

**A. Technical Issues (Website Downtime, Payment Failures, Bugs)**

* **Severity:** Critical - IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED
* **Response:**
    * **Tier 1 (IT/Development):**  Diagnose & Resolve - Primary focus. Establish a clear SLA for resolution time (e.g., within 2 hours).
    * **Tier 2 (Marketing/Lead):**  Communicate - Issue immediate public statement across all channels acknowledging the issue, providing an estimated resolution time, and apologizing for the inconvenience.  Be transparent!
    * **Tier 3 (Customer Support):**  Manage Customer Inquiries - Redirect incoming inquiries to a dedicated channel (e.g., a status page with updates).
* **Communication Channels:** Website Status Page, Social Media (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram), Email Newsletter, PR Outreach
* **Metrics:** Website uptime, Error logs, Support ticket volume


**B. Negative Social Media Sentiment (Massive Complaints, Backlash)**

* **Severity:** Critical - IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED
* **Response:**
    * **Tier 1 (Social Media Manager):**  Monitor & Identify - Track mentions, hashtags, and sentiment. Flag high-priority negative posts.
    * **Tier 2 (Marketing/Lead):**  Craft Response - Develop a pre-approved template response addressing common concerns.  Humanize responses – show empathy and a willingness to help.  DO NOT engage in arguments.
    * **Tier 3 (Customer Support):**  Address Individual Concerns - Directly respond to individual users expressing frustration.
* **Communication Channels:**  Social Media (Direct Messaging, Public Comments), PR Outreach (to address broader concerns)
* **Metrics:** Sentiment Score (Social Listening Tools), Number of Negative Mentions


**C. Content Controversy (Offensive Imagery, Misinterpreted Dialogue)**

* **Severity:** High - RAPID RESPONSE REQUIRED
* **Response:**
    * **Tier 1 (Creative Lead/Content Editor):**  Investigate - Immediately assess the issue's validity & potential harm.
    * **Tier 2 (Marketing/Lead):**  Draft Statement - Prepare a clear, concise, and empathetic statement acknowledging the issue and outlining the steps being taken to address it.  Take responsibility if warranted.
    * **Tier 3 (Customer Support):**  Manage Inquiries -  Prepare a response for customer inquiries with a link to the official statement.
* **Communication Channels:** All channels – prioritize transparency and swift action.
* **Metrics:** Media Coverage, Sentiment Score
