**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar - 2024 (Example)**

**I. January - New Year, New Habits (Focus: Motivation, Productivity, Wellness)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, New Year Resolutions
* **Key Dates:** January 1st - January 31st
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Promotional Offers:** Limited-time discounts on premium subscriptions.
    * **Content Marketing:** Blog posts and social media content focusing on goal setting, time management, healthy habits, and mindfulness.
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):**  Update keywords related to "new year," "goal setting," "productivity," "fitness," "meditation."
    * **Social Media Campaigns:**  "Share Your Resolution" contests, user testimonials about how the app helped them achieve their goals.
    * **Email Marketing:**  Welcome series for new users, reminders about premium features.


**II. February - Love & Connection (Focus: Relationship Apps, Social, Entertainment)**

* **Theme:** Romance, Connection, Fun
* **Key Dates:** February 1st - February 29th
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Promotional Offers:** Free trial extensions, bundled offers with other relevant apps.
    * **Content Marketing:**  Articles and videos about dating tips, relationship advice, social connection, and fun activities.
    * **App Store Optimization:**  Focus on keywords like “dating,” “relationships,” “social apps,” "meet people".
    * **Social Media Campaigns:** "Share Your Valentine's Story" campaigns, contests related to the app’s features. Run Valentine's Day themed ads.
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relationship or lifestyle influencers.


**III. March - Spring into Action (Focus: Fitness, Health, Travel, Utilities)**

* **Theme:** Spring Renewal, Getting Active, Travel Planning
* **Key Dates:** March 1st - March 31st
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Promotional Offers:**  Introductory discounts, seasonal offers tied to fitness goals (e.g., “Spring Clean Your Fitness”).
    * **Content Marketing:**  Blog posts and videos about spring workouts, healthy recipes, travel destinations, and utility tips.
    * **App Store Optimization:**  Update keywords around "fitness," "health," "travel," "budgeting," "productivity."
    * **Social Media Campaigns:**  #SpringFitness challenges, travel inspiration campaigns.
    * **Partnerships:** Collaborate with fitness studios or travel companies.



**IV. April - Easter & Earth Month (Focus: Family, Games, Sustainability)**

* **Theme:** Family Fun, Celebrating Nature, Eco-Friendly Living
* **Key Dates:** April 1st - April 30th
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Promotional Offers:**  Family pack discounts, deals on games or educational apps.
    * **Content Marketing:**  Easter-themed content, articles about sustainable living, earth-friendly practices.
    * **App Store Optimization:**  Keywords like “Easter games”, “family apps”, “environmental apps”, “eco-friendly”.
    * **Social Media Campaigns:**  Easter contest,  #EarthDay challenges.
