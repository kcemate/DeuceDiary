Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a detailed breakdown, covering various aspects and offering customizable options:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** "Share the [Your Brand Name] Love" or "[Your Brand Name] Rewards" - Keep it catchy and reflective of the program's purpose.
* **Visuals:** Use consistent branding - logos, colors - in all referral materials.

**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

* **Define Your Ideal Referral:**  Who are the people you *want* to refer your product or service? Knowing your ideal referral helps you tailor the incentives.
* **Key Goals:** What do you want this program to achieve? (e.g., Increase new user acquisition, boost brand awareness, drive sales, expand customer base).

**3. Referral Structure & Mechanics:**

* **Type of Referral:**
    * **Tiered:**  Different levels of rewards based on the number of referrals. (e.g., 1 referral - small reward, 5 referrals - medium reward, 10 referrals - large reward). This encourages ongoing participation.
    * **Simple:** A single reward for each successful referral.  Easier to manage but might not be as motivating.
* **Tracking:** Crucial!
    * **Unique Referral Links:** Generate unique links for each user. This is the foundation for tracking referrals.  Use a referral program platform (see below) to automate this.
    * **Dedicated Referral Code:** (Optional, but good for SMS/email campaigns).  A short, memorable code for each user.
* **Platform:**  Choose a referral program platform. These simplify tracking, communication, and reward distribution.  Some popular options:
    * **ReferralCandy:** (Popular, robust, integrates with many platforms)
    * **Friendbuy:** (Specifically for e-commerce)
    * **Word of Mouth:** (Good for smaller businesses, simple)
    * **Influencer Marketing Platforms:** (If you want to leverage influencers for referrals – often have referral features)

**4. Incentives – The Heart of the Program**

* **For the Referred Customer (New User):**
    * **Discount:**  10-20% off their first purchase. This is a very common and effective incentive.
    * **Free Shipping:**  A popular draw, especially for e-commerce.
    * **Free Trial Extension:** (For SaaS products).  Give them more time to experience the value.
    * **Welcome Gift/Bundle:**  A small, relevant product or service.
    * **Early Access:**  Priority access to new features or products.
* **For the Referring Customer (Existing Customer):**
    * **Discount:**  Similar to the referred customer (10-20%).
    * **Store Credit:**  A versatile option – they can use it on any product.
    * **Points/Rewards System:**  Award points for referrals, which they can redeem for discounts or other rewards.
    * **Entry into a Prize Draw:**  A chance to win a larger prize (e.g., a gift card, a high-value product). – This adds excitement.
    * **Early Access/Exclusive Content:**  Similar to the referred customer.
