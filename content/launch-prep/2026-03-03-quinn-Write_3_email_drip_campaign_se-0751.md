**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscribers - Lead Magnet Focus)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, establish trust, and deliver the promised lead magnet.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who downloaded a free resource (e.g., ebook, checklist, template) on your website.
* **Number of Emails:** 5 Emails
* **Timeline:**  Delivered over 5-7 days.

**Sequence:**

1. **Email 1 (Immediately - Welcome)**
   * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! Here’s Your [Lead Magnet Name]
   * **Content:**  Warm welcome, thank you for downloading, reiterate the value of the lead magnet, encourage them to open the attachment. Short and sweet.
   * **Call to Action (CTA):**  "Open Your [Lead Magnet Name] Now"

2. **Email 2 (2 Days Later -  Dig Deeper into the Lead Magnet)**
   * **Subject:** Unlock the Secrets in Your [Lead Magnet Name]
   * **Content:** Provide a brief overview of the key benefits of the lead magnet and how it solves a specific problem.  Share a short tip or insight related to the lead magnet's topic.
   * **CTA:** “Explore the Full Guide” (link to a blog post or relevant page on your website)

3. **Email 3 (3 Days Later -  Introduce Your Brand Story)**
   * **Subject:**  Meet the Team Behind [Your Brand Name]
   * **Content:**  Tell your brand's story – why you started, your mission, and your values.  Humanize your brand.  Include a photo of your team (if appropriate).
   * **CTA:** “Learn More About Our Story” (link to your “About Us” page)

4. **Email 4 (4 Days Later -  Social Proof & Community)**
   * **Subject:**  Join the [Your Brand Name] Community!
   * **Content:**  Highlight positive customer testimonials, share your social media links (Instagram, Facebook, etc.), and invite them to join your community.
   * **CTA:**  “Follow Us on [Social Media Platform]” & “Join Our Community Forum” (if applicable)

5. **Email 5 (7 Days Later -  Gentle Reminder & Next Step)**
    * **Subject:**  Still Thinking About [Lead Magnet Name]?
    * **Content:**  A friendly reminder of the lead magnet’s value and a subtle nudge towards a relevant product or service. (e.g., "Ready to take the next step towards [Desired Outcome]?")
    * **CTA:** “Browse Our Best-Selling Products” (linking to your shop)
