**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be honest and upfront with the public, acknowledging issues and providing updates.
* **Speed:** Respond quickly to crises, ideally within 30 minutes.
* **Empathy:** Demonstrate understanding and concern for affected individuals.
* **Collaboration:** Utilize a cross-functional team to address issues effectively.
* **Documentation:** Meticulously record all actions taken and communication.
