**1. About Deuce Diary**

Deuce Diary is a revolutionary, user-generated content platform empowering teenagers (13-19) to authentically share their daily lives, thoughts, and experiences through short-form video diaries.  We’re building a vibrant community where genuine voices are celebrated, and young people are given a platform to connect, learn, and be heard. Unlike traditional social media, Deuce Diary focuses on raw, unfiltered content, fostering a sense of vulnerability and relatable storytelling. We believe teenagers are powerful storytellers, and Deuce Diary is the tool to amplify their voices.


**2. Founder Bio:  Leo “Deuce” Ramirez**

Leo “Deuce” Ramirez, 24, is the founder and CEO of Deuce Diary.  Growing up navigating the complexities of adolescence himself, Leo recognized a critical gap in the digital landscape - a space truly designed for authentic teen expression, free from the pressure of perfectly curated feeds.  After years of observation and feedback from his peers, Leo developed the Deuce Diary concept, prioritizing ease of use, a supportive community, and a commitment to celebrating diverse perspectives.  He holds a Bachelor's degree in Communications from State University and has previously worked in social media marketing, lending valuable insights to the platform’s development.  Leo’s passion for empowering young voices drives every aspect of Deuce Diary.
