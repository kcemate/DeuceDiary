## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 Changelog - Launch!

**Date:** October 26, 2023

**Version:** 1.0.0

**Summary:** We're thrilled to announce the launch of Deuce Diary, your personal daily habit tracker designed to help you build a better you! This initial release focuses on core functionality, providing a solid foundation for building positive habits.

---

**I. Social Features (Beta)**

* **Friend Connections:** (Beta) – Users can now connect with friends within the Deuce Diary community.
    * **Connecting:** Send friend requests via username.
    * **Activity Feed:** See what your connected friends are tracking and achieving (limited visibility - focusing on public progress).
    * **Motivation:**  Receive encouraging notifications from friends who are achieving their goals.
    * **Note:** This feature is currently in Beta and may experience occasional performance issues. User feedback is highly encouraged.
* **Community Challenges (Coming Soon):**  Planned for future release -  Participate in group challenges with other users for added motivation and accountability.


**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Creation:**  Easily add new habits with customizable names, descriptions, and icons.
* **Daily Tracking:** Log your progress on each habit with a simple “Complete” or “Missed” toggle.
* **Habit Scheduling:**  Set specific days of the week for habits to be tracked (Monday - Sunday).
* **Customizable Reminders:** Configure daily reminders for each habit to ensure you don't miss a session.
* **Habit Categories:** Organize your habits into categories like “Health,” “Productivity,” “Mindfulness,” and “Fitness”.
* **Streak Tracking:** Automatically track your current streak for each habit.
* **Habit Notes:**  Add short notes to each tracking session to capture your thoughts and feelings.


**III. Gamification Features**

* **Streaks:**  Maintain your daily habit tracking to build and extend your streaks.  Streaks are visually represented to provide a sense of accomplishment.
* **Milestones:** Achieve milestones based on consistent tracking (e.g., 7-day streak, 30-day streak) – unlockable badges are coming in a future update.
* **Completion Percentage:**  Visualize your progress on each habit with a completion percentage bar.
* **Daily Goal Achievement:**  Celebrate the successful completion of your daily goals with a visual confirmation and encouraging message.
