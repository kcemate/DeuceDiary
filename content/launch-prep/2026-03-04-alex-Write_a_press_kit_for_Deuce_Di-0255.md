**About the Book:**

Deuce Hayes isn’t your typical cool kid. She’s a chronic overthinker, a terrible liar, and perpetually stuck in the middle of everyone else’s drama. Her diary is her only refuge – a place where she spills her darkest anxieties, her ridiculous crushes, and her increasingly desperate attempts to fit in at Northwood High. But when a series of embarrassing incidents – a disastrous school dance, a botched attempt at helping her best friend, and a frankly terrifying encounter with a surprisingly intense stranger – threaten to unravel her already fragile world, Deuce realizes she needs to finally confront her fears and, maybe, just maybe, figure out who she actually is. 

*Deuce Diary* tackles themes of self-discovery, friendship, family expectations, and the overwhelming pressure of adolescence with a refreshing lack of polish and a whole lot of heart.
