**II. Scenario 1: Server Crash**

* **Severity:** High - Immediate disruption to user experience.
* **Immediate Actions (0-30 Minutes):**
    * **Alert:** Notify the entire development team immediately.
    * **Diagnostics:** Start troubleshooting - identify the cause (traffic spike, code issue, infrastructure problem).
    * **Status Page:** Immediately update your status page (e.g., Statuspage.io) with “Service Interruption” – “We are aware of an issue impacting our servers and are working to resolve it.” Provide an estimated time to resolution (if possible).
    * **Communicate:** Post to Twitter and other social channels acknowledging the issue and reassuring users that you're on it. (Example: “We're experiencing technical difficulties. Our team is working hard to get Deuce Diary back up and running. Thanks for your patience!”)
* **Short-Term Response (30-120 Minutes):**
    * **Rollback/Hotfix:** Deploy the quickest fix possible – this might involve scaling up servers, rolling back a recent deployment, or applying a hotfix.
    * **Continuous Monitoring:**  Closely monitor server performance and user reports.
    * **Regular Updates:** Keep the status page and social channels updated with progress.
* **Longer-Term Mitigation:**  Invest in robust server infrastructure, automated scaling, and thorough testing procedures.


**III. Scenario 2: 1-Star Reviews & Negative Feedback**

* **Severity:** Medium – Impacts user perception and potential for organic growth.
* **Immediate Actions (0-30 Minutes):**
    * **Monitor Reviews:**  Aggressively monitor App Store reviews, Google Play reviews, and social media mentions.
    * **Centralize Feedback:** Create a triage system to categorize feedback (bugs, feature requests, usability issues, complaints).
* **Short-Term Response (30-120 Minutes):**
    * **Respond Directly:** Respond to individual negative reviews – acknowledge the user’s frustration, apologize, and offer support (link to FAQ, troubleshooting guides).  Don't get into arguments.
    * **Identify Patterns:** Analyze the common themes in negative feedback – are there recurring bugs, confusing UI elements, or unmet expectations?
    * **Prioritize Bug Fixes:**  Immediately prioritize fixing bugs highlighted in reviews.
* **Longer-Term Mitigation:**  Actively solicit user feedback through in-app surveys, beta programs, and community forums.  Use feedback to iterate on the product.
