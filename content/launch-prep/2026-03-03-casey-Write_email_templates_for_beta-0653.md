Okay, here are email templates tailored for Beta Testers, Friends, and Influencer Outreach, with variations to suit different levels of formality and goals.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template**

**Subject: Invitation to Beta Test [Your Product Name] – Help Shape the Future!**

**Option 1: (Formal & Detailed)**

```email
Hi [Beta Tester Name],

We're thrilled to invite you to participate in the beta testing phase of [Your Product Name]! As a valued member of our community, we believe your feedback is crucial in helping us refine and perfect our product before its official launch.

[Your Product Name] is a [brief, clear description of your product - 1-2 sentences]. We’re particularly interested in your feedback on [mention specific areas you want feedback on - e.g., usability, performance, specific features].

**Here's what you'll be doing:**

*   Accessing [Your Product Name] at: [Link to Beta Version]
*   Using the product for approximately [Estimated Time Commitment - e.g., 2-3 hours]
*   Completing a short feedback survey: [Link to Survey]
*   Sharing your thoughts and any issues you encounter through [Preferred Communication Channel - e.g., dedicated Slack channel, email].

**Timeline:**

*   Beta Testing Period: [Start Date] – [End Date]
*   We’ll be checking in with you weekly to gather your insights.

We truly appreciate your time and dedication.  Your input will directly impact the final product!

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team

[Link to your website]
```

**Option 2: (Less Formal & Enthusiastic)**

```email

[Your Name] & The [Your Company Name] Team
```
