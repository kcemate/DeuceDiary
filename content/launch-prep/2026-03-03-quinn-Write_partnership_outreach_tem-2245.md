Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health/wellness brands, tailored for different levels of engagement and with variations to suit different partner types. I've categorized them by approach and included notes on when to use each one.

**1. The Casual & Enthusiastic Intro (Good for Micro-Influencers & Small Businesses)**

* **Subject:** Collaboration Idea - [Your Brand] & [Partner Brand]
* **Body:**
   Hi [Partner Name],
   I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand] - we’re all about [briefly describe your brand & mission - e.g., "making healthy eating easy & delicious," “supporting mindful movement,” "boosting your well-being naturally"]. 
   I’ve been a big fan of [Partner Brand]’s [mention something specific you genuinely admire - e.g., "commitment to sustainable products," "amazing yoga classes," "delicious smoothies"] and think our audiences could really benefit from learning from each other.
   I was wondering if you'd be open to a quick chat about potential collaborations? Maybe a joint giveaway, a cross-promotion on social media, or sharing content?
   Check out our website: [Your Website Link]
   Thanks so much!
   Best,
   [Your Name]

* **Notes:**  This is friendly and approachable.  It’s best for smaller brands or individuals who align with your values.  Personalization is key – *always* mention something specific you like about their brand.


**2. The Value-Focused Proposal (Good for Medium-Sized Influencers & Businesses with Clear Offerings)**

* **Subject:** Partnership Opportunity: Reach [Your Target Audience] Together
* **Body:**
   Hi [Partner Name],
   My name is [Your Name] from [Your Brand], and we specialize in [clearly state your brand's focus - e.g., "plant-based nutrition," “functional fitness coaching,” “stress reduction techniques”].  Our audience is primarily [describe your target audience - e.g., “health-conscious millennials,” “busy professionals seeking wellness,” "parents interested in healthy family meals"].

   We’ve been impressed by [Partner Brand]'s work in [mention specific area of their expertise] and believe a partnership would be mutually beneficial. We’re proposing [Outline 1-2 specific collaboration ideas – be clear & concrete. Examples: "a 4-week healthy eating challenge,” “a joint Instagram giveaway,” “a guest blog post on your website”].

   **Here’s what we offer:** [List 3-4 key benefits for the partner – e.g., “Increased brand exposure to our engaged audience,” “Access to our email list,” "Co-created content opportunities," "Opportunity to run a limited-time promotion"].

   You can learn more about us here: [Your Website Link]
   Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to discuss this further?
   Thanks,
   [Your Name]

* **Notes:** This is more formal and outlines a clear value proposition.  It shows you’ve done your research and have a structured idea in mind. Be prepared to discuss compensation/exchange (e.g., product swaps, affiliate commissions).
