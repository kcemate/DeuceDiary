**Week 1: The "Wow!" & Initial Excitement (Launch Week)**

* **Theme:** Introducing the product & Celebrating the Launch!
* **Monday:**
    * **Content:** Launch Announcement - Blog Post + Social Media Blitz (All Platforms)
    * **Goal:**  Announce the launch, highlight key features, and drive traffic to your website/landing page. (Images/Video)
* **Tuesday:**
    * **Content:** Short Demo Video (1-2 minutes) - Focus on a key benefit.
    * **Platform:** YouTube, TikTok, Instagram Reels, Facebook
    * **Goal:**  Visually demonstrate the product in action.
* **Wednesday:**
    * **Content:** “Behind the Scenes” Post – Share the story of the product’s creation (Team, inspiration, etc.) - Instagram Story, LinkedIn, Facebook
    * **Goal:** Humanize your brand and build trust.
* **Thursday:**
    * **Content:**  FAQ Post - Address common questions. (Blog Post, Instagram Highlight)
    * **Goal:**  Reduce friction for potential users, answer immediate concerns.
* **Friday:**
    * **Content:**  Launch Day Giveaway/Contest - Encourage engagement & sharing. (Social Media)
    * **Goal:**  Increase reach and generate excitement.
* **Weekend:** Monitor social media, respond to comments and questions.


**Week 2: Deep Dive & Feature Focus**

* **Theme:** Exploring the Value – Highlighting Specific Features
* **Monday:**
    * **Content:** Feature Spotlight - Blog Post/Video focusing on *one* key feature. (Examples: “5 Ways [Feature] Can Help You,” “A Closer Look at…” )
    * **Goal:** Drive deeper understanding of specific value propositions.
* **Tuesday:**
    * **Content:**  User Testimonial (if available) - Quote or short video.
    * **Platform:** Website, Social Media
    * **Goal:**  Social proof - Build confidence.
* **Wednesday:**
    * **Content:**  Tutorial/How-To Guide -  Step-by-step instructions on how to use a specific feature. (Blog Post, YouTube Tutorial)
    * **Goal:**  Reduce onboarding friction.
* **Thursday:**
    * **Content:**  Ask Me Anything (AMA) Session - Host a live Q&A session on Instagram or Facebook.
    * **Goal:**  Direct engagement, gather questions, build community.
* **Friday:**
    * **Content:**  Industry Related Article Link - Share an article relevant to your niche and explain how your product fits in. (LinkedIn, Twitter)
    * **Goal:**  Establish thought leadership.
