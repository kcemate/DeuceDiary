**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (7 Days - Ideal for New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and set expectations.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who’ve joined your email list (likely through a website opt-in, lead magnet, or contest).
* **Timeline:** 7 Days
* **Email Sequence:**
    * **Day 1: Welcome Email**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to the [Your Brand Name] Family!
        * **Content:** Thank you for subscribing! Briefly reiterate the value they’ll receive (e.g., “Get exclusive tips on [your industry/topic]”).  Introduce your brand’s mission and a friendly tone. Include a link to your website.
        * **Action:**  Encourage them to visit your website.
    * **Day 3: Highlight a Key Benefit**
        * **Subject:**  Unlock [Specific Benefit] with [Your Product/Service]
        * **Content:**  Focus on ONE key benefit of your offering.  Use a relatable example or story.  Keep it concise and benefit-driven.
        * **Action:** Link to a relevant case study, blog post, or product page.
    * **Day 5:  Introduce Your Team/Values**
        * **Subject:** Meet the Team Behind [Your Brand Name]
        * **Content:**  A short, personal introduction to the people behind the brand.  Highlight your company values – what's important to you? Build connection. (Could be a photo and short bio.)
        * **Action:** Encourage them to connect with you on social media.
    * **Day 7:  Offer a Small Incentive**
        * **Subject:**  A Little Something for Joining Us!
        * **Content:**  Offer a small discount, free resource (e.g., checklist, template), or early access to a new product/service.  This incentivizes engagement.
        * **Action:**  Drive them to redeem the offer.
