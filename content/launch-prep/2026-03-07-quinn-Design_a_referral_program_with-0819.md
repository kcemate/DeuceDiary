Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here’s a breakdown, aiming for a balance of motivation, ease of use, and cost-effectiveness, adaptable to various businesses.

**1. Program Name:** (Choose something memorable & relevant)

*   **Example:** "Share the [Your Brand] Love" or "Bring a Friend & Get Rewarded"


**2. Target Audience & Goals:**

*   **Define:** Who are you trying to reach with referrals? (Existing customers, specific demographics, etc.)
*   **Goals:** What do you want to achieve? (Increase customer acquisition, boost brand awareness, grow your user base, etc.)  This will heavily influence your incentive structure.


**3. Referral Structure & Mechanics:**

*   **Referrer:** The person making the referral.
*   **Referee:** The person being referred.
*   **Tracking:** Crucial!  Implement a system to track referrals – this can be:
    *   **Unique Referral Links:**  Each referrer gets a unique link they can share. This is the most accurate and easiest to implement.
    *   **Coupon Codes:**  Referrers receive a code they can share.
    *   **Dedicated Referral App:**  (For higher-tech businesses)
*   **Simple Flow:**
    1.  Referrer clicks their unique link or uses their code.
    2.  Referee clicks the link/enters the code during signup or purchase.
    3.  The system recognizes the referral.

**4. Incentives (Tiered Structure - Recommended)**

This is where it gets interesting.  A tiered system provides stronger motivation.

*   **Tier 1: Basic Reward (For Both Referrer & Referee)**
    *   **Reward:** Small discount (e.g., 10% off their first purchase) or a small credit to their account.  This is a standard welcome incentive.
    *   **Trigger:**  Referee completes their initial action (e.g., makes a purchase over a minimum amount).
*   **Tier 2: Mid-Level Reward (For Referrer Only)**
    *   **Reward:** A more substantial discount (e.g., 20% off) or a small gift (e.g., a branded item, free shipping) after the referee makes a purchase.
    *   **Trigger:** Referee makes a purchase after a certain period (e.g., 30 days) or reaches a certain spending threshold.
*   **Tier 3: Top-Tier Reward (For Both Referrer & Referee - Limited Time)**
    *   **Reward:**  A significant bonus (e.g., a larger discount, a premium product, a gift card).  This is reserved for generating a high volume of referrals.
    *   **Trigger:**  A set number of referrals are achieved, or a specific referral volume is reached within a defined timeframe.  (e.g., “The first 100 referrals get a $50 gift card!”)

**5. Incentive Values (Examples - Adjust Based on Your Business)**

*   **Small Discounts:** 5%, 10%, 15%
*   **Gift Cards:** $10, $25, $50
*   **Branded Merchandise:** T-shirts, mugs, stickers
*   **Free Shipping:** (Especially effective for e-commerce)
*   **Premium Features:**  A free trial of a premium subscription, access to exclusive content.
