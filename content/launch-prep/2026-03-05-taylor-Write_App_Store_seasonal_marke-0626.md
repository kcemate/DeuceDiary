**I. January – New Year, New Habits (Focus: Motivation & Goal Setting)**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, New Year Resolutions, Habit Building
* **ASO:** Keyword research focusing on "New Year's Resolutions," "Goal Setting," "Productivity," "Habit Tracker."

* **Email:** Welcome new users with a focus on how your app can help them achieve their goals.  Offer a "New Year's Kickstart" guide.
* **Content:** Blog post: “5 Ways [App Name] Can Help You Stick to Your Resolutions.”  Video tutorial – “Getting Started with [App Name].”
* **Paid:**  Targeted social ads to users interested in self-improvement and productivity.

**II. February – Love & Connection (Focus: Relationships & Wellbeing)**

* **ASO:** Keywords: "Valentine's Day gifts," "Relationship apps," "Date ideas," "Self-Care," "Mental Wellbeing”
