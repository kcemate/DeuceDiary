Okay, here’s a comprehensive social media influencer outreach template kit, designed specifically for micro-influencers (with categories for Health, Humor, and Lifestyle), including DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief.

**I. Overall Strategy Notes:**

* **Personalization is Key:** Don’t just copy and paste! Tailor each DM to show you’ve genuinely looked at their content. Mention specific posts, their voice, and why you think they’d be a good fit.
* **Start Small:** Micro-influencers have smaller but often more engaged audiences.  Focus on building relationships first.
* **Be Transparent:** Clearly state your goals and expectations from the start.
* **Offer Value:** What's in it for them?  Free product, paid collaboration, exposure, etc.
* **Follow Up:** Don't be afraid to send a polite follow-up DM if you don't hear back within a week.

**II. DM Templates (Categorized & Customizable)**

**A. Health Influencers:**

* **Initial DM (Warm-up - Day 1):**
   "Hey @[Influencer Name]! 👋 Huge fan of your content – especially that post about [mention a specific post, e.g., mindful eating/yoga]. You have a really genuine and approachable way of sharing wellness tips. We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re passionate about [briefly state your brand’s mission – e.g., supporting healthy habits]. Would love to connect!"
* **Follow-up DM (If No Response - Day 3-5):**
    “Just wanted to check in –  saw your recent post on [topic related to your brand].  Thinking you might find our [product/service] helpful for [specific benefit relevant to their audience].  No pressure, just wanted to see if you’d be open to a chat!”
* **Collaboration Offer DM (After Positive Engagement - Day 7-10):**
    “We'd love to collaborate! We’re offering a [product/service] for you to try & share with your audience. We’re envisioning a [mention content type - e.g., reel showcasing the product in use, honest review, tutorial].  Happy to discuss a [fee range/gift value] and tailor the content to your style.  Would love to hop on a quick 15-minute call to brainstorm.”


**B. Humor Influencers:**

* **Initial DM (Light & Engaging - Day 1):**
   "OMG @[Influencer Name]! Your [mention a specific funny post/video] literally made my day 😂  We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re all about spreading a little joy.  We’re launching something new, and we think your audience would love it."
* **Follow-up DM (Playful - Day 3-5):**
    “Still laughing about that [funny post]!  We're working on [brief, funny description of your product/service] and thought, 'This needs to be in the hands of someone as hilarious as you!’  Serious question: What's the funniest thing that happened to you this week?” (Encourage response)
* **Collaboration Offer DM (Day 7-10):**
    “Let’s make some *hilarious* content together! We'd love to send you our [product/service] to review in a funny way (think TikTok challenge, skit, or relatable meme).  We're open to your creative ideas.  We’re prepared to pay [fee range] or offer a generous gift in exchange for a set of posts/stories.”
