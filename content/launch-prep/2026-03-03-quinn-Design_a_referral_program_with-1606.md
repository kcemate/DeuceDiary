Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives, aiming for something that’s engaging, rewarding, and effective. Here’s a breakdown, covering different elements and options, with considerations for various business types:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Catchy Name:** “Share the [Your Brand] Love,” “Bring a Friend & Get Rewarded,” “Spread the Word & Earn” - keep it simple and positive.
* **Visuals:** Use a consistent visual theme within the referral program itself (logo, colors) to align with your brand.


**2. Core Mechanics & Structure:**

* **Two Key Players:**
    * **Referrer:** The existing customer who refers a new customer.
    * **New Customer (Referred):** The person brought in through the referral.
* **Tracking:**  Crucially, you *must* have a reliable tracking system. Options:
    * **Unique Referral Links:** Each referrer gets a unique link they can share. This is the most common and effective.
    * **Coupon Codes:** Generate unique codes for each referrer.
    * **Dedicated App Feature:** If you have an app, integrate a referral system directly within it.
* **Clear Rules & Terms:**  Outline everything simply:
    * How referrals are tracked.
    * Eligibility requirements (e.g., must be an existing customer for X days).
    * Reward distribution timelines.
    * Any restrictions (e.g., cannot refer yourself, cannot refer your family).


**3. Incentives – Tiered Rewards (Most Effective)**

This is where you really drive participation.  Structure rewards into tiers based on performance:

* **Tier 1:  Initial Referral (Basic)**
    * **Referrer:**  Small discount (e.g., 10% off their next purchase)
    * **New Customer:** Small discount (e.g., 5% off their first purchase)
* **Tier 2:  First Successful Referral (Mid-Level)**
    * **Referrer:**  Higher discount (e.g., 15-20% off) *OR*  Free gift (e.g., small branded item)
    * **New Customer:**  Increased discount (e.g., 10% off) *OR*  Welcome pack (smaller version of what you offer).
* **Tier 3:  Multiple Referrals (High-Value)**
    * **Referrer:**  Significant discount (e.g., 25-30% off), Gift Card (e.g., $50), or Entry into a monthly prize draw.
    * **New Customer:**  Extra discount (e.g., 15% off),  Exclusive product or service access.
