* **New Habit Creation:** Easily add new habits with customizable names, descriptions, target frequency (daily, weekly, monthly), and reminders.
* **Habit Types:** Track habits as "Daily" or "Recurring" (e.g., weekly workouts).
* **Detailed Habit Logging:** Record your progress on each habit entry with a simple "Complete" or "Incomplete" button.
* **Habit Editing & Deletion:** Modify existing habits as your goals evolve or delete them entirely.
* **Habit Categories:** Group your habits into categories (e.g., Health & Fitness, Productivity, Personal Development) for better organization.
* **Streak Tracking:**  Automatically calculate and display your current streak for each habit.
