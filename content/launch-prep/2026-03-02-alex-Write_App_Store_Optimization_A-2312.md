## App Store Optimization (ASO) A/B Test Plan

This plan outlines a structured approach to A/B testing key elements of your app's App Store listing to maximize conversion rates.

**I. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Store Conversion Rate (installs)
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve keyword ranking, increase click-through rate (CTR) from search results, and boost positive reviews/ratings.
* **KPIs to Track:**
    * **Install Conversion Rate:** Percentage of users who view your app page and then install it.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of users who see your app in search or on the App Store page and then click on it.
    * **Keyword Ranking:** Track your app’s position in relevant search results.
    * **App Page Views:** Measures overall interest in your app.
    * **Ratings & Reviews:** Monitor changes in average rating and volume of reviews.


**II. Elements to Test (with rationale):**

We'll focus on elements with the highest potential impact on conversion.

|--------------------|------------------------|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Icon**            | Existing Icon          | Slightly Modified Icon (color, shape, subtle changes) | Icons are the first thing users see. A small refresh can significantly impact initial perception. |

**III. Tools to Use:**

* **App Store Connect:** Apple's platform for managing your app – allows for A/B testing (iOS 14+).
* **Google Play Console:** Google's platform – offers A/B testing for Android apps.
* **Mobile App Marketing Platforms (MAPs):**
    * **AppsFlyer:** Robust analytics and attribution, integration with A/B testing tools.
    * **Adjust:** Similar to AppsFlyer, strong attribution & A/B testing capabilities.
    * **Branch:** Excellent for deep linking and A/B testing.
* **Dedicated A/B Testing Platforms (for advanced experimentation):**
   * **Leanplum:** Powerful for A/B testing, event tracking, and automation.
   * **Apptimize:**  Specifically designed for app A/B testing.
* **Google Analytics for Firebase:**  Track user behavior within your app after install (optional but valuable for deeper analysis).
