**II. Key Metrics & Performance (20 mins)**

**Divide this section into categories for clarity.  For each metric, include:**

* **Metric Name:**
* **Target (Pre-Launch Estimate):** What did we *think* would happen? (Be specific - e.g., 1000 daily active users)
* **Actual Result:** The actual number achieved during Week 1.
* **Variance:** (Actual - Target) – Quantifies the difference.
* **Analysis/Interpretation:** *Most important part!*  Don't just state numbers.  Ask *why* the variance occurred.  Possible questions to guide the discussion:

    * Were there any unexpected trends?
    * Was our target realistic?  Why or why not?
* **Action Items:**  Specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) actions to address the insights. (e.g., "Revise marketing messaging to focus on [specific benefit] by [date]")
