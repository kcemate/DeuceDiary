**Program Name:** (Example: “Spread the Love,” “Refer & Reward,” “[Your Brand] Network”)

**I. Core Structure & Mechanics**

* **Simple Referral Link/Code:** Every user should have a unique referral link or code they can share. This is essential for tracking.
* **Tracking:** Utilize a referral tracking platform (many are available - Mention, ReferralCandy, Viral Loops, Extole) or build your own with analytics.  This is critical to accurately measure success and reward referrals.
* **Clear Communication:** Make the program easy to understand. A clear landing page or welcome message explaining how it works is crucial.

**II. Incentive Tiers & Rewards (Key to Success!)**

We’ll use a tiered system with increasing rewards for higher referral volumes. This encourages more frequent and larger referrals.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (For Both Referrer & Referred)**

* **Referrer Reward:**  [Small Incentive - 10-20% Discount] - E.g., "Get 10% off your next purchase when you refer a friend!"
* **Referred Reward:** [Small Incentive - 10-15% Discount or Free Shipping] - E.g., "Your friend gets 10% off their first order, and you get a welcome discount!"
* **Trigger:** First referral from each user.


**Tier 2:  Mid-Level (For Users Referring 2-5 People)**

* **Referrer Reward:** [Medium Incentive - $10-$25 Credit/Gift Card] - E.g., “Earn a $20 credit to use on our products!”
* **Referred Reward:** [Medium Incentive - 15-20% Discount] - E.g., "Save 20% on your next purchase."
* **Trigger:** 2nd, 3rd, 4th, and 5th referral.

**Tier 3: Premium (For Users Referring 6+ People)**

* **Referrer Reward:** [High Incentive - Higher Credit/Gift Card, Exclusive Product Access, or Early Access] - E.g., "$50 Gift Card," "Exclusive ‘Limited Edition’ Product," "Priority Access to New Product Launches."
* **Referred Reward:** [Significant Incentive - Larger Discount, Free Product Trial Extension] - E.g., “Free 30-day trial extension” or "25% off your entire order."
* **Trigger:** 6th, 7th, 8th+ referral.



**III.  Reward Options - Beyond Discounts**

Don’t *just* rely on discounts. Diversify your rewards:

* **Early Access:**  Give top referrers early access to new products, features, or sales.
* **Exclusive Content:**  Offer access to premium content, tutorials, or webinars.
* **Branded Merchandise:**  Give away branded t-shirts, hats, or other swag.
* **Donations to Charity:**  “For every referral, we’ll donate $X to [Charity Name].” (Great for socially conscious brands)
* **Points System:** Accumulate points for referrals that can be redeemed for rewards.
* **Tiered Loyalty Program Integration:** Connect the referral program with your overall loyalty program.
