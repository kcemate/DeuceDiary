7. **Lack of a Clear Functionality:** Apps that are vague in purpose or don’t provide a clear benefit to the user.  Apple wants apps to “do” something.
8. **Payment Issues:** Problems with in-app purchases, inaccurate pricing, or misleading descriptions.
9. **App Store Guidelines Violations (Specifically Related to Content):**  This covers things like violent content, hate speech, sexually explicit content, and content that promotes illegal activities.
10. **Duplicate Apps:**  Submitting an app that is substantially similar to an existing one without justification.
