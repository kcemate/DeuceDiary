## ASO A/B Test Plan: App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements to improve app visibility, conversion rates, and overall performance.

**I. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase app downloads and conversions (from impression to install).
* **Key Metrics to Track:**
    * **Impressions:** Number of times the app appears in search results or on the App Store page.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of impressions that result in a click on the app's listing.
    * **Install Rate:** Percentage of clicks that result in an install.
    * **Conversion Rate (Impression to Click):**  CTR divided by Impressions.
    * **Conversion Rate (Click to Install):** Install Rate divided by CTR.
    * **Keyword Ranking:** Monitor keyword ranking changes across variations.
    * **App Store Page Views:** Track traffic to the page itself.
* **Test Duration:** 2-4 weeks (depending on traffic and variations)
* **Statistical Significance:** Aim for 80% statistical significance using a 95% confidence level.
