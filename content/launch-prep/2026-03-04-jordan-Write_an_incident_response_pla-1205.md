**1. Purpose**

This Incident Response Plan outlines the procedures for identifying, containing, eradicating, recovering from, and learning from security incidents affecting [Organization Name]'s systems, data, and operations. It aims to minimize disruption, protect assets, and ensure business continuity.

**2. Definitions**

* **Incident:** An event that has the potential to violate security policies, compromise data, disrupt services, or cause harm to the organization.
* **Severity Levels:**  (See Section 4 for detailed descriptions)
    * **P0 - Critical:** Immediate and widespread impact, potentially causing significant business disruption, legal/regulatory violations, or severe data breaches.
    * **P1 - High:**  Significant impact, likely causing disruption to critical systems or services, potential for data compromise, and requires immediate attention.
    * **P2 - Medium:** Moderate impact, likely causing limited disruption, potential for minor data compromise, and requires prompt investigation and resolution.
    * **P3 - Low:** Minor impact, isolated event with minimal disruption, primarily requiring monitoring and documentation.
