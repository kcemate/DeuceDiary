    * **TikTok:** Huge potential for viral snippets. Prioritize creators known for trends, dark humor, and relatable awkwardness.
    * **Instagram:**  Can be used for promotion, but likely less effective than YouTube/TikTok.  Consider focusing on reels that lead back to your content.
* **Audience Demographics:**  Generally, you'll be targeting 18-35 year olds – leaning male but not exclusively.  Consider psychographics – people who enjoy being uncomfortable, appreciate dark humor, and aren't afraid to laugh at themselves.
* **Creator Types:**
    * **Sketch Comedians:**  People who create short, scripted sketches.
    * **Observational Comedians:**  Those who find humor in everyday awkward moments.
    * **Reactors/Commentators:**  Individuals who can react genuinely to your content and add their own commentary.

* **Social Listening:** Use tools like Brandwatch, Mention, or even just advanced Google Alerts to track conversations around bathroom humor and see who's consistently involved.
