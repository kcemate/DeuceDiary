**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar**

**I. New Year (January - February): Fresh Start & Resolutions**

* **Theme:** "Start Fresh," "Reach Your Goals," "New Year, New You"
* **Marketing Focus:**
    * **Promotions:** Offer a limited-time discount, free trial, or bonus content to incentivize new users.
    * **Social Media:** Content focused on goal setting, productivity, and how your app helps users achieve their aspirations.  Run contests related to New Year's resolutions.
    * **App Store Page:** Highlight features that support goal achievement (e.g., tracking, progress, habit building).
    * **Paid Advertising:** Target users interested in self-improvement, productivity, and related apps.
* **Example App Categories:** Productivity apps, fitness apps, language learning apps, budgeting apps.


**II. Winter (February - March): Cozy & Engaging**

* **Theme:** “Warm Up,” “Cozy Nights,” “Stay Connected”
* **Marketing Focus:**
    * **Promotions:** Offer themed discounts or bundles (e.g., discount on a game with winter-themed content).
    * **Social Media:**  Share cozy content, user-generated content of people enjoying your app during winter activities. Run polls and quizzes.
    * **App Store Page:**  Showcase features that are enjoyable during winter (e.g., relaxing games, meditation apps, virtual fireplaces).
    * **Email Marketing:** Send out newsletters with curated content and offers.
* **Example App Categories:** Games (puzzle, simulation, cozy), Meditation apps, Streaming services, Entertainment apps.



**III. Spring (March - May): Renewal & New Beginnings**

* **Theme:** "Spring into Action," “New Adventures," "Bloom"
* **Marketing Focus:**
    * **Promotions:** Limited-time discounts, free content unlocks.
    * **Social Media:** User-generated content, contests related to spring activities (gardening, travel, outdoor adventures). Launch new seasonal content within your app.
    * **App Store Page:**  Highlight features relevant to spring (e.g., travel apps, gardening apps, photography apps).
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with influencers in relevant niches.
* **Example App Categories:** Travel apps, Fitness apps, Photography apps, Gardening apps, Educational apps (nature/science).
