Okay, here’s a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week and focusing on building excitement, demonstrating value, and gathering feedback. This is a template – you’ll need to tailor it specifically to your product/service, target audience, and marketing channels.

**Assumptions:**

* **Launch Date:** We're starting this calendar one week *after* the launch date. (You'll need to adjust the dates based on your actual launch).
* **Channels:** This calendar assumes a mix of social media (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn - adjust based on your audience), email marketing, and potentially a blog.
* **Goal:** Primarily focused on driving initial adoption, collecting feedback, and building a small, engaged community.

---

**Content Calendar: Month 1 Post-Launch**

**Week 1: Celebrate & Educate (Focus: Awareness & Basic Value)**

* **Monday:** **Launch Announcement Recap & Thank You** (All Channels) - A heartfelt post thanking everyone for their support, reiterating the core value proposition. Include a strong call to action - “Try it today!”
* **Tuesday:** **Tutorial Video #1: Quick Start Guide** (YouTube, Website, Social) - A short, engaging video (1-2 mins max) demonstrating the absolute easiest way to get started with the product/service.
* **Wednesday:** **Behind-the-Scenes - The Making Of** (Instagram Story, Blog Post Snippet) – A glimpse into the development process - a team photo, a quick story about a challenge overcome.  Humanizes your brand.
* **Thursday:** **FAQ Post - Top 3 Questions Answered** (Website, Social) – Address common questions people might have.
* **Friday:** **User Spotlight (if possible) / Testimonial Quote** (All Channels) – If you have an early adopter, share their positive experience. Even a quote from beta testers is valuable.
* **Saturday/Sunday:** **Community Engagement – Poll/Question** (Instagram Stories, Facebook) - “What’s the one thing you’re hoping to achieve with [Product/Service]?  Let us know!”

**Week 2: Deep Dive & Use Cases (Focus: Demonstrating Value & Problem Solving)**

* **Monday:** **Tutorial Video #2:  A More Advanced Feature** (YouTube, Website, Social) - Expand on a slightly more complex feature, showing how it solves a specific problem.
* **Tuesday:** **Blog Post: “5 Ways [Product/Service] Can Improve Your [Area of Focus]”** –  Provide practical tips and examples of how the product/service can be used.
* **Wednesday:** **Customer Case Study Snippet** (LinkedIn, Website) – Showcase a particularly impressive early success story (even if brief).
* **Thursday:** **Live Q&A Session (Facebook Live, Instagram Live)** – Host a short Q&A session with your team to address user questions in real-time. Promote this heavily in advance!
* **Friday:** **User Tip/Hack:** (Social Media - particularly Twitter/X) - Share a clever way to get more value from the product.
* **Saturday/Sunday:** **Interactive Content - “This or That”** (Instagram Stories) – Engage users with fun, relevant choices related to the product/service.
