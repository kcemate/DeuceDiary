Okay, here’s a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service. This calendar is designed to build momentum, gather feedback, and drive initial adoption. It’s broken down by week and includes a mix of content types.

**Important Notes Before We Start:**

* **This is a template:** Adapt this to *your* specific product, target audience, and brand voice.
* **Flexibility is Key:** Monitor engagement and adjust this calendar based on what's performing well.
* **Track Everything:**  Use analytics to see what’s working, what's not, and optimize accordingly.
* **Platform Focus:**  This calendar assumes you're focusing on social media (primarily Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter), but tailor it to where your audience spends their time.

**Month 1 Post-Launch Content Calendar**

**Week 1: The Celebration & Introduction (Focus: Excitement & Awareness)**

* **Monday:** (Social Media - All Platforms) - **Launch Announcement RECAP!** – A visually engaging post celebrating the launch with a recap of key features and benefits.  Include a strong call to action (CTA) - "Try it Now!" Link to your website/app. (Image/Video - Short celebratory animation or product hero shot)
* **Tuesday:** (Blog Post) - **"Welcome to [Product Name]!"** - A detailed introduction to the product, addressing common questions and highlighting the core value proposition.  SEO focused!
* **Wednesday:** (Instagram/Facebook - Stories) - **"Behind the Scenes"** - Short, informal video or images showcasing the team involved in the launch, the development process, or a quick product demo. Use polls and questions in stories to engage.
* **Thursday:** (Twitter) – **#LaunchDay Giveaway!** - Run a simple giveaway to generate excitement and followers. (e.g., Retweet to win a free trial/discount code).
* **Friday:** (Social Media - All Platforms) - **Customer Testimonial (if available)** - Share a positive quote or brief video from an early adopter.  Builds social proof.

**Week 2: Deep Dive & Feature Focus (Focus: Education & Value)**

* **Monday:** (Blog Post) – **“[Feature Name] Explained”** – Deep dive into one key feature, outlining its functionality and benefits.
* **Tuesday:** (Instagram/Facebook - Carousel Post) - **"3 Ways to Use [Product Name] to Solve [Problem]"** – Visually demonstrate practical applications.
* **Wednesday:** (Twitter) – **“Quick Tip Tuesday”** – Short, actionable tip related to using the product.
* **Thursday:** (YouTube – Short Demo Video) - **"How To Get Started With [Product Name]"** – A quick, easy-to-follow tutorial.
* **Friday:** (Social Media - All Platforms) - **Q&A Session Announcement** - Promote a live Q&A session for the following week (Instagram Live, Facebook Live, or Twitter Q&A).
