## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Purpose:** To provide a structured approach to managing and mitigating potential crises during the launch of “Deuce Diary,” ensuring minimal damage to reputation, sales, and user trust.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Honest and timely communication is paramount.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge user concerns and frustrations.
* **Speed:** Rapid response is crucial, especially in the initial hours.
* **Collaboration:** Cross-functional teams (PR, Marketing, Dev, Support, Legal) must work together.
* **Documentation:** Maintain a centralized record of all communications and actions taken.


**II. Trigger Categories & Potential Issues:**

| **Category** | **Potential Issues** | **Severity (1-5)** | **Potential Impact** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Technical Issues (Website/App)** | Bugs, Server Overload, Downtime, Payment Processing Errors | 4-5 | Loss of Sales, Negative Reviews, User Frustration, Brand Damage |
| **Content & Narrative** | Controversial Content, Misinterpretations, Perceived Offense | 3-4 | Public Backlash, Social Media Storm, Brand Reputation Damage |
| **User Support Overload** | Inability to handle support requests, Long wait times | 3 | User Dissatisfaction, Negative Reviews, Brand Perception of Unresponsiveness |
| **Social Media Backlash** | Negative Comments, Trolls, Organized Campaigns Against the Book/Author | 4-5 | Amplified Negative Sentiment, Damage to Brand Image, Potential Legal Issues |
| **Security Concerns** | Data Breaches, Phishing Attempts, Account Compromises | 5 | Loss of User Trust, Legal Liability, Significant Damage to Brand |
| **Misinformation/Fake News** | False Claims about the Book, Author, or Related Topics | 3 | Erosion of Trust, Difficulty in Correcting Information |


**III. Response Levels & Team Responsibilities:**

**Level 1: Monitoring & Initial Assessment (0-2 Hours)**

* **Team:** Marketing Lead, PR Lead, Support Lead
* **Actions:**
    * **Real-time Monitoring:**  Constant monitoring of: Social Media (Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, Goodreads), Website Traffic, App Store Reviews, Support Tickets.
    * **Automated Alerts:**  Set up alerts for specific keywords and sentiment analysis.
    * **Initial Assessment:** Determine if a crisis is developing, and its potential severity.

**Level 2: Containment & Communication (2-6 Hours)**

* **Team:** All Teams (with escalating authority to CEO/Founder if necessary)
* **Actions:**
    * **Public Statement (Website/Social Media):** Issue a brief, empathetic statement acknowledging the issue and stating that the team is working on a solution. (Template provided below)
    * **Support Channel Activation:** Prioritize resolving technical issues and escalating support requests.
    * **Internal Communication:** Brief all staff on the situation and their roles.
    * **Identify Spokesperson:** Designate a primary spokesperson for external communication.


**Level 3: Resolution & Damage Control (6-24 Hours)**

* **Team:** All Teams, Legal Counsel
* **Actions:**
    * **Implement Solutions:** Address identified problems – fix bugs, scale servers, improve support processes.
    * **Detailed Communication:**  Provide regular updates to users (blog posts, social media, email).
    * **Engage with Critics:** Respond to criticism constructively and respectfully.  Don’t engage in arguments.
    * **Legal Review:**  Assess potential legal ramifications of any issues and advise on appropriate action.
