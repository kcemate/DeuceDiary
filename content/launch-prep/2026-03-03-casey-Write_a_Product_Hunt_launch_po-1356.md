**What is Deuce Diary?**

Deuce Diary is a minimalist journal app that guides you to consistently practice gratitude.  It’s built around the idea of just 3 things - 2 things you're grateful for, and 1 thing you're looking forward to.  

**Here’s what you get:**

* **Beautifully Designed Templates:**  Choose from clean, calming layouts to inspire your thoughts.
* **Daily Prompts:**  Gentle reminders to focus on the good in your life. (We’ll be adding more prompt options soon!)
* **Simple & Intuitive:**  No complicated features, just a focus on writing.
* **Cloud Sync:**  Access your entries across all your devices.
* **Dark Mode:**  Perfect for nighttime journaling.

**Why we built it:**

We wanted to create a tool that removed the friction from gratitude journaling.  Too many apps are overwhelming – Deuce Diary is designed to be a quick, impactful habit.
