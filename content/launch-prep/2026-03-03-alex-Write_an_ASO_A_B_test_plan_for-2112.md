## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements – primarily title, subtitle, keywords, description, screenshots, and app preview video – to improve install rates and ultimately, user engagement.

**1. Goals & KPIs:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase App Installs & User Engagement (measured through click-through rate (CTR), conversion rate (CVR), and Daily Active Users (DAU)).
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**
    * **Install Rate (IR):** Percentage of users seeing your app in search or browsing and installing it. (Most important)
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of users clicking on your app's listing in search results or browsing.
    * **Conversion Rate (CVR):** Percentage of users who click on your listing and then install the app.
    * **App Store Page Views:** Number of times your app's listing is viewed.
    * **Time Spent on App Store Page:**  Average time users spend viewing your app's listing.
    * **Keyword Ranking:** Track changes in keyword positions over time.


**2. Test Variables & Hypotheses:**

We’ll run separate A/B tests focusing on specific elements, iterating over time. Here's a breakdown of tests we'll conduct, along with example hypotheses:

| **Element**        | **A (Control)**                               | **B (Variant)**                               | **Hypothesis**                                         |
|--------------------|---------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|
| **App Title**       | "Your App Name"                             | "Your App Name - [Key Benefit]"                 | Adding a benefit to the title will increase CTR & IR.    |
| **Subtitle**       | "A brief description of your app."          | "A brief description + a stronger benefit"      | A more compelling subtitle will improve CTR & CVR.       |
| **Keywords (5-10)**| Your current keyword list (e.g., "game", "puzzle") | Slightly modified list incorporating trends & long-tail terms |  Changing keywords to incorporate trending search terms will boost IR. |
| **Description (Short)**| "Concise overview of your app features."     | "Concise overview + problem/solution framing"   | A description focused on the user's problem and solution will boost CVR. |
| **Description (Long)**| "Detailed list of features & benefits."      | "Revised format - problem/solution/features"    |  Re-structuring the long description will improve engagement (page views & time spent). |
| **Screenshot 1**     | Static screenshot of key feature.           | Dynamic screenshot showing feature in action.     |  Dynamic screenshots will have a higher CTR & CVR.       |
| **Screenshot 2-4**  | Standard screenshots with generic icons.        | Screenshots with visually compelling icons & branding. |  Branding on screenshots will improve IR & CVR.         |
| **App Preview Video**| Generic video showcasing app functionality.  | Video highlighting key benefits, user testimonials. |  A compelling video will dramatically increase IR & CVR. |
