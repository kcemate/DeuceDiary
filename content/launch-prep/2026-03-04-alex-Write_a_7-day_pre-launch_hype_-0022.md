**Day 1: The Whisper - Instagram & TikTok Focus**

Run a poll: “What do you think makes this so intriguing?” (Options: Old money, hidden family, dangerous secrets).
* **TikTok (Short Video - 15-30 seconds):**  A quick, looping video with unsettling sound design (rain, ticking clock, whispered voices) that cuts to a blurry shot of a handwritten page. Text overlay: “They thought they’d forgotten. They were wrong. #DeuceDiarySecrets”  Challenge: Encourage users to recreate the blurry page using a filter or drawing. 
* **Reddit (r/Mystery, r/Thriller):** Post a short, intriguing text post. “Just discovered a collection of diaries in the attic. The first entry is... unsettling. Let's discuss: What kind of secrets do you think might be hidden in a forgotten diary? #DeuceDiarySecrets”  Engage actively with comments.
