* **Metric:** **Cost Per Install (CPI)** (Number & Trend) – *Efficiency of Acquisition*
    * **Visualization:** Number with trend line.
    * **Rationale:**  Measures the cost-effectiveness of each acquisition channel.
* **Metric:** **New vs. Returning Users** (Stacked Bar Chart) - *Initial User Pool*
