Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, designed for different goals and with varying lengths. I’ve included estimated timing and content for each email.  You'll need to customize these heavily based on your brand, audience, and specific product/service.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Lead Nurturing (Focus: Building Relationship & Initial Value)**

* **Goal:** Welcome new subscribers, establish trust, and introduce your brand’s value proposition.
* **Target Audience:** Anyone who signs up for your email list (likely a lead magnet download).
* **Timing:**  Sent over 7-10 days.
* **Emails:**

   * **Email 1: (Immediately - Day 0)**
      * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
      * **Content:** Warm welcome, thank you for subscribing, reiterate the value of the lead magnet they downloaded (e.g., “We’re thrilled you grabbed our free guide on [Topic]!”), and a brief introduction to your brand’s mission. Link to your best content piece.
      * **Call to Action (CTA):** “Explore [Link to Top Blog Post]”

   * **Email 2: (3 Days Later - Day 3)**
      * **Subject:**  [Topic] Secrets You Didn’t Know 🤫
      * **Content:**  Share a valuable tip or insight related to the topic from your lead magnet, or from a popular blog post.  Focus on solving a common pain point. Keep it concise and actionable.
      * **CTA:** “Read More Tips Here [Link to Relevant Blog Post]"

   * **Email 3: (5 Days Later - Day 5)**
      * **Subject:** Meet the [Your Brand Name] Team! 😊
      * **Content:** A little bit about your company culture. Humanize your brand – maybe a short story, employee spotlight, or behind-the-scenes peek.
      * **CTA:** “Learn More About Us [Link to About Us Page]"

   * **Email 4: (7 Days Later - Day 7)**
      * **Subject:**  [Specific Offer - 10% Off] - Just for You!
      * **Content:** Introduce a small, low-pressure offer – e.g., 10% off their first purchase, a free resource, or early access to a sale. Highlight urgency.
      * **CTA:** “Claim Your Discount [Link to Product Page]”



**Campaign 2: Product-Focused Drip (Focus: Driving Sales – More Direct)**

* **Goal:** Educate prospects about a specific product and move them closer to a purchase.
* **Target Audience:** People who have shown interest in a particular product (e.g., visited a product page, downloaded a product spec sheet).
* **Timing:** Sent over 5-7 days.
* **Emails:**

   * **Email 1: (Immediately - Day 0)**
      * **Subject:**  Introducing the [Product Name] – Solve [Problem]
      * **Content:** Briefly introduce the product and clearly state the problem it solves.  Focus on benefits, not just features. Include a high-quality image.
      * **CTA:** “Discover [Product Name] [Link to Product Page]"

      * **Content:**  Break down the product’s key benefits with a short example or scenario. Use bullet points for clarity.
