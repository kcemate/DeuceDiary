## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Launch Changelog

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Welcome to Deuce Diary!** We’re thrilled to announce the launch of our first major release, packed with features designed to help you build better habits and track your progress. This release lays the foundation for a robust habit tracking experience and we’re incredibly excited to see you utilize it.

**I. Social Features (Community & Sharing)**

* **Public Diary Profiles:** Users can now create public profiles displaying their tracked habits and overall progress. (Requires Profile Completion)
* **Following & Followers:** You can now follow other users’ diaries to get inspiration and see how others are tackling their goals.
* **Limited Direct Messaging (Beta):**  Introduce a basic, private messaging system allowing users to connect directly with each other.  *Note: This is currently in beta and functionality may be limited.*
* **Community Feed (Public):** A dedicated feed showcasing popular habits, successful diary entries, and community highlights.
* **Shareable Diary Entry Images:**  Users can capture screenshots of their diary entries and share them to other platforms (Twitter, Facebook, etc.).



**II. Tracking Features (Core Habit Management)**

* **Habit Creation & Customization:**
    * Users can create habits with names, descriptions, and icons.
    * Customizable tracking frequencies: Daily, Weekly, or Milestone.
    * Option to define specific goal targets (e.g., "Drink 8 glasses of water").
* **Entry Logging:**
    * Quick and easy logging of habit completion with a simple tap.
    * Ability to add notes and reflections to each diary entry, providing context and motivation.
* **Habit Categorization:**  Organize habits into categories (e.g., Health & Fitness, Productivity, Mindfulness) for better organization.
* **Streak Tracking:** Automatically tracks consecutive days of habit completion, providing a visual representation of your progress.
* **Reminders:** Customizable reminders can be set for each habit to ensure you don't forget to track your progress.



**III. Gamification Features (Motivation & Engagement)**

* **Points System:** Earn points for completing habits, with bonus points awarded for streaks and achieving milestones.
* **Leveling System:** As you accumulate points, you’ll level up, unlocking cosmetic rewards and recognition within the app.
