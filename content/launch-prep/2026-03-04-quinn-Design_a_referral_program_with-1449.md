**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The person who refers a new customer.
* **Referee:** The new customer referred by the referrer.
* **Tracking:**  Use a referral tracking platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, ReferralHero – or build your own if you have technical resources). This is *crucial* for accurate tracking and payouts.

**3. Incentive Structure (Tiered – Suggested)**

This is the heart of the program. Here's a tiered approach to maximize participation and rewards:

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (Simple & Immediate)**

* **For the Referrer:** 
    * **Reward:** 10% Discount on their next purchase. (Low cost, easy to implement)
    * **Trigger:** New customer makes their first purchase *after* being referred.
* **For the Referee:** 10% discount on their first purchase.

**Tier 2: Mid-Level Referral (Increased Value)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** $25 Gift Card to your brand’s store or a related service. (More impactful, but higher cost).
    * **Trigger:** Referee makes a purchase of $50 or more.
* **For the Referee:** 15% discount on their first purchase + Free Shipping.


**Tier 3: Premium Referral (Top-Tier Engagement)**

* **For the Referrer:**
    * **Reward:** $50 Gift Card + Exclusive Brand Merchandise (e.g., t-shirt, hat). (Highest cost, reserved for top referrers)
    * **Trigger:** Referee makes a purchase of $150 or more AND refers at least 2 new customers.
* **For the Referee:** 20% discount on their first purchase + Free Shipping + Exclusive Welcome Gift (a small, relevant item).

**4. Referral Codes & Tracking**

* **Unique Referral Codes:** Each referrer receives a unique code (alphanumeric, easy to share).
* **Tracking Links:**  Provide a unique referral link for each referrer.
* **Automated Tracking:**  The tracking platform automatically detects referrals and triggers the rewards.

**5. Communication & Promotion**

* **Dedicated Landing Page:** Create a clear landing page explaining the program with visuals and FAQs.  Include a shareable graphic.
* **Email Marketing:**
    * **Welcome Email:** Introduce the referral program to new customers.
    * **Referral Reminder Emails:**  Send targeted emails to existing customers encouraging them to refer.  Personalize these based on past purchase behavior.
    * **Reward Announcement:**  Immediately notify the referrer when a new customer signs up.
* **Social Media:** Promote the program actively on all your social media channels. Run contests/giveaways related to referrals.
* **In-App/On-Website Prompts:**  If you have an app or website, display a referral banner or prompt within the user interface.
* **Influencer Marketing:**  Partner with relevant influencers to promote the program to their audience.
