**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscribers - 5 Days)**

* **Goal:** Build initial engagement, introduce your brand, and set expectations.
* **Target Audience:** New email subscribers who signed up through your website, social media, or a lead magnet.
* **Timing:**  Sent over 5 days – perfect for a gentle introduction.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1 (Day 1): Welcome & Thank You**
    * **Subject:** 🎉 Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! Let’s Get Started.
    * **Content:** Warm welcome, reiterate how they signed up (e.g., "Thanks for downloading our guide to [topic]!"), briefly introduce your brand's mission and values, and a friendly call to action: "Take a look at our website to learn more: [Link to Website]".
    * **Image:**  Brand logo or a relevant, inviting image.
* **Email 2 (Day 3): Benefit Highlight #1**
    * **Subject:** Solve [Problem Your Product/Service Solves] with [Your Brand Name]
    * **Content:** Focus on one key benefit of your product/service.  Use a short story or example to illustrate how it helps customers. "Imagine [customer scenario] – [Your product/service] can help you do that!"
    * **Image:** Image related to the benefit.
* **Email 3 (Day 5):  Introduce a Key Product/Service**
    * **Subject:** Meet [Product Name] - The [Key Benefit] You Need
    * **Content:** Introduce a specific popular product or service, highlighting 2-3 key features and benefits. Include a clear call to action: “Learn more & browse here: [Link to Product Page]”.  Consider a limited-time offer.
