**I. Overall Dashboard Goal:**

* **Track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Deuce Diary’s initial launch.**
* **Identify areas of success and areas needing attention.**
* **Provide data-driven insights for ongoing optimization and iteration.**

**II. Dashboard Sections & KPIs:**

**A. Acquisition & Growth (Top of Dashboard - High-Level Overview)**

* **Metric:**  **New Users (7-Day & 30-Day)** – *Count of new users who started using Deuce Diary within the specified timeframe.*
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart (7-day & 30-day trends).  Show daily new user acquisition. Highlight any significant spikes or dips.
* **Metric:** **Total Users (Active)** – *Total number of users currently active in the app.*
    * **Visualization:** Large Number Card.  Clear, prominent display of the current total.
* **Metric:** **Source of Acquisition** – *Distribution of new users by their initial acquisition source (e.g., App Store, Google Play, Referral Program, Paid Ads, Social Media).*
    * **Visualization:** Pie Chart or Donut Chart.  Visually represents the proportion of users from each source.
* **Metric:** **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)** – *The average cost to acquire a new user.*
    * **Visualization:** Number Card –  Show the calculated CPA, preferably segmented by acquisition source. (This will require tracking marketing spend).


**B. Engagement & Usage (Mid-Dashboard - Understanding User Behavior)**

* **Metric:** **Daily Active Users (DAU)** – *Number of unique users who engage with the app on a daily basis.*
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart (7-day & 30-day DAU trend). Shows daily usage trends.
* **Metric:** **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** – *Number of unique users who engage with the app on a monthly basis.*
    * **Visualization:** Line Chart (7-day & 30-day MAU trend).  Longer-term usage trends.
* **Metric:** **DAU/MAU Ratio (Stickiness)** – *Indicates how frequently users return to the app.* (DAU / MAU * 100)
    * **Visualization:** Number Card – Represents the percentage.  A higher ratio signifies a more engaged user base.
* **Metric:** **Session Length** – *Average duration of a single user session.*
    * **Visualization:** Number Card or Bar Chart (Segmented by user cohort – e.g., first-time users, users who’ve been active for 30 days)
* **Metric:** **Key Feature Usage:** (Track usage of core diary functions)
   * **Metric:**  Number of Entries Created – *Total number of diary entries created.*
   * **Visualization:** Line Chart (Trend over time).
   * **Metric:** Average Entries per User – *Average number of entries created per user.*
   * **Visualization:** Number Card
   * **Metric:**  Most Frequently Used Categories/Tags – *Analyze which categories/tags users are most likely to use.*
   * **Visualization:** Bar Chart – Top 5 Categories.


**C. Retention & Churn (Bottom of Dashboard - Long-Term Health)**

* **Metric:** **7-Day Retention Rate** – *Percentage of users who return to the app 7 days after their initial usage.*
    * **Visualization:** Number Card – Shows the retention percentage.
