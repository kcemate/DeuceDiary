**1. Executive Summary:**

This plan outlines the key metrics we’ll monitor and the scaling actions we’ll take as we move from initial launch to sustained growth.  We’ll prioritize scaling based on data, focusing on optimizing user experience, expanding our capacity, and securing additional resources.  The core principle is to scale *responsively* –  we don't want to over-invest before we have validated demand.

**2. Initial Phase (0-30 Days Post-Launch): Stabilization & Validation**

* **Goal:** Confirm core functionality, gather initial user feedback, and establish basic operational efficiency.
* **Metrics & Thresholds:**
    * **Daily Active Users (DAU):** 50-100 (This is a low starting point, and the exact number will depend on the product’s nature) – *Threshold:  DAU < 50 - Review onboarding, identify drop-off points, and address technical issues.  DAU > 100 – Proceed to the next phase.*
    * **Trial Conversion Rate:** 5-10% – *Threshold:  < 5% -  Investigate onboarding flow, simplify the signup process.  > 10% -  Consider small targeted marketing promotions.*
    * **Customer Support Tickets (Average):** < 10 per day – *Threshold: > 10 -  Analyze ticket content for common issues, improve documentation, and potentially add FAQs.*
    * **Server Response Time (Average):** < 200ms – *Threshold: > 200ms - Investigate backend performance issues, optimize database queries, and explore scaling infrastructure.*
* **Scaling Actions:**
    * Bug fixes and minor feature adjustments based on initial feedback.
    * Optimize onboarding flow.
    * Basic server monitoring and maintenance.
    * Implement basic analytics tracking (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)

**3. Growth Phase (30-90 Days Post-Launch):  Early Scaling & Feature Expansion**

* **Goal:**  Drive user acquisition, expand key features, and begin to identify scaling bottlenecks.
* **Metrics & Thresholds:**
    * **DAU:** 200-500 – *Threshold: DAU < 200 – Ramp up marketing efforts (content marketing, social media), explore referral programs.  DAU > 500 – Analyze user segmentation and identify high-value segments.*
    * **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** $500 - $2,000 – *Threshold: MRR < $500 - Focus on conversion optimization. MRR > $2,000 -  Start considering team expansion (sales, marketing).*
    * **Churn Rate:** < 3% monthly – *Threshold: Churn > 3% - Investigate reasons for churn, proactively reach out to at-risk users, and refine onboarding.*
    * **Average Revenue Per User (ARPU):** $10-$20 – *Threshold: ARPU < $10 - Explore upgrade options, feature bundling.*
* **Scaling Actions:**
    * Implement marketing campaigns (paid & organic).
    * Expand core feature set based on user feedback.
    * Begin A/B testing key elements of the product.
    * Monitor server performance closely.
    * Consider upgrading server infrastructure
