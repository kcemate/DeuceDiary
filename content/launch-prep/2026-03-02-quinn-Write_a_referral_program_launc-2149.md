## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive user acquisition, increase engagement, and foster a loyal community. The program focuses on a tiered reward system, a strategically designed viral loop, robust fraud prevention measures, and precise tracking implementation.

**1. Program Name & Branding:** "Double the Diary" -  Plays on the brand and emphasizes the benefit of the referral.

**2. Mechanics & Rewards (Tiered System)**

* **Referrer (Existing User):**
    * **Tier 1 (First 5 Referrals):** 100 Deuce Points (redeemable for premium features, early access to content, or exclusive merch) + $5 Amazon Gift Card.
    * **Tier 2 (After 5 Referrals):** 50 Deuce Points + $3 Amazon Gift Card.
    * **Bonus:** “Referral Rockstar” badge displayed on their profile.
* **Referee (New User):**
    * **Welcome Pack:** 200 Deuce Points (immediately unlocks some premium content) & 1-Month Premium Subscription.
* **Viral Loop:**  "Invite a Friend & Both Get a Boost!" - When a referee completes their onboarding (e.g., sets up their diary, takes a quiz), the referrer instantly receives 50 Deuce Points.  This incentivizes immediate action and accelerates the viral loop.

**3. Viral Loop Design - Key to Success**

* **Immediate Reward:** The 50 Deuce Point reward upon referee completion is crucial. It removes friction and encourages quick referral.
* **Social Sharing Integration:**  Built-in sharing buttons directly to social media (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram) with pre-populated messages: “Join Deuce Diary and get a free premium subscription!  Refer a friend and you'll both get a boost!”
* **Email Marketing:** Automated email sequence for referrers:
    * **Email 1 (Immediately after referral):** Welcome to the Double the Diary program!  Reward details.
    * **Email 2 (7 days after referral):**  Check out what your referee is doing! (Link to their diary).
    * **Email 3 (14 days after referral):**  Limited Time Offer - Double your points! (Special bonus for top referrers)
* **In-App Notifications:**  Notify users when a referral is made, offering a reminder to invite others.

**4. Fraud Prevention**

* **Manual Verification:**  Initially, all referrals will be manually verified to ensure legitimacy.
* **IP Address Monitoring:**  Track referral activity by IP address to identify suspicious patterns.
* **Account Activity Monitoring:**  Flag accounts with unusually high referral rates or referring to a large number of new accounts.
* **Referral Link Tracking:**  Each referrer receives a unique referral link.  Analyze where these links are being used to identify potential bots or malicious activity.
* **Rate Limiting:**  Implement rate limiting on referral submissions to prevent spam.
* **Account Verification:**  Require new users to verify their email address before receiving the welcome pack.

**5. Tracking Implementation**

* **Dedicated Referral Tracking System:** Utilize a dedicated referral marketing platform like ReferralCandy, Viral Loops, or BuildFire.  These platforms offer robust tracking, reporting, and automation features. (Recommended)

    * **Unique Referral Codes:** Generate unique codes for each referrer.
    * **Google Analytics Goals:** Track the number of new users acquired through referral links.
    * **Database Tracking:**  Record referral relationships within the Deuce Diary database.
