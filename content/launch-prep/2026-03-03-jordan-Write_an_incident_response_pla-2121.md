**1. Purpose**

This Incident Response Plan outlines the procedures to be followed in the event of a security incident impacting [Organization Name]. The goal is to minimize damage, restore services, and prevent future occurrences, while maintaining legal and regulatory compliance.

**2. Definitions**

* **Incident:** Any event that violates or threatens to violate security policies, acceptable use policies, or regulatory requirements. This includes, but is not limited to, malware infections, data breaches, unauthorized access, denial-of-service attacks, phishing attempts, and system failures.
* **Severity Levels:**
    * **P0 (Critical):**  Immediate and significant impact.  System downtime, substantial data loss, major legal/regulatory ramifications, or threat to critical infrastructure. Requires immediate escalation and activation of all resources.
    * **P1 (High):**  Significant impact.  Noticeable disruption to business operations, potential data loss, or escalation of a P0 incident. Requires rapid response and dedicated resources.
    * **P2 (Medium):**  Limited impact.  Minor disruption, potential for minor data exposure, or an incident that could escalate. Requires prompt investigation and containment.
