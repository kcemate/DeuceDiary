**Retention Analysis Plan**

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To understand why users are churning and identify opportunities to improve user retention.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    *  Identify key drop-off points in the user journey.
    *  Segment users based on behavior and identify high-risk segments.
    *  Determine the key drivers of retention (what’s working well?).
    *  Establish a baseline for retention performance.
    *  Develop targeted interventions to improve retention rates.
