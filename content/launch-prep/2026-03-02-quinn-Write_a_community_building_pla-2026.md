## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy for building a thriving and engaged community around *Deuce Diary* leveraging Discord and Reddit, with a strong emphasis on fostering a respectful, creative, and supportive environment centered around fan art, discussions, and creative interpretation of the story.

**I. Overall Vision:**

To cultivate a passionate and dedicated community where fans of *Deuce Diary* can connect, share their creative interpretations, discuss the story’s themes, and support each other's artistic endeavors. We want a space that feels like a collaborative creative hub, not just a fan forum.


**II. Platform Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** Discord will be the core of our community, offering real-time interaction and deeper engagement.
    * **Server Structure:**
        * **#general-chat:** All-purpose chat for casual conversation, announcements, and sharing.
        * **#fan-art-showcase:** Dedicated space for showcasing fan art - categorized by medium (digital, traditional, writing) for easier browsing.
        * **#writing-prompts & #writing-feedback:**  Dedicated spaces for writing prompts inspired by *Deuce Diary* and providing constructive feedback on each other's writing.
        * **#character-discussion:** Focused conversations about specific characters, their motivations, and relationships.
        * **#world-building:** Discussions about the lore, magic system, and overall world of *Deuce Diary*.
        * **#creative-challenges:** Regular creative challenges (art, writing, cosplay) to spark engagement and foster collaborative projects.
        * **#moderation-chat:** A private channel for moderators to communicate and resolve issues.
    * **Voice Channels:**  Establish dedicated voice channels for drawing sessions, writing sprints, and group discussions.
* **Reddit (Discovery & Outreach):** Reddit will serve as a discovery channel, promoting Discord and driving traffic.
    * **Subreddit:**  r/DeuceDiary (Ideally, a fully moderated subreddit – see section IV)
    * **Content Strategy:** Regularly post fan art, writing excerpts, discussions, and links back to the Discord server. Encourage users to share their own creations.


**III. Content Strategy:**

* **Initial Content Pillars:**
    * **Character Spotlights:** Deep dives into individual characters, exploring their motivations and backstories.
    * **Lore Deep Dives:** Discussions and analysis of key events, locations, and magical elements within *Deuce Diary*.
    * **Creative Prompts:** Regular prompts to stimulate fan art, writing, and cosplay projects.
    * **“What If…” Scenarios:** Encourage imaginative discussions around alternate storylines and character decisions.
* **Content Cadence:**
    * **Discord:** Constant activity – ongoing conversations, prompt updates, challenge announcements.
    * **Reddit:** 3-5 posts per week – a mix of featured fan art, discussion threads, and promotional content.
* **User-Generated Content (UGC) Focus:** The majority of content should be driven by community members. Encourage sharing, voting, and highlighting exceptional submissions.



**IV. Moderator Guidelines & Team:**

* **Core Values:** Respect, Creativity, Support, Constructive Feedback.
* **Moderator Roles & Responsibilities:**
    * **Enforcement of Rules:** Ensuring adherence to community guidelines.
    * **Conflict Resolution:** Mediating disputes and maintaining a positive environment.
    * **Content Curation:** Identifying and highlighting exceptional fan creations.
    * **Engagement & Outreach:**  Starting conversations, running events, and welcoming new members.
    * **Community Building:** Suggesting new features, fostering collaboration, and shaping the community's direction.
* **Team Size:** Start with 3-5 core moderators, expanding as the community grows.
