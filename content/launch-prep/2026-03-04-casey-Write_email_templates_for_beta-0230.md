**Option 2 (Less Formal):**

Hey [Tester Name],

Super excited to have you join our beta for [App Name]! We’re really working hard to make this awesome, and your feedback is key to getting it just right.

You can download the app here: [Link to App]

We’d love to hear what you think!  Let us know if you run into any bugs, have suggestions for improvements, or just generally how you’re enjoying the experience.  You can share your thoughts here: [Link to Feedback Form/Channel]

Thanks so much for helping us build something great!

Cheers,

[Your Name] & The [Your Company Name] Team


**Key Considerations for Beta Testers:**

*   **Clear Expectations:** State the purpose of the beta, the expected level of involvement, and the timeline.
*   **Specific Feedback Requests:** Don’t just ask for "feedback."  Focus on what's important to you.
*   **Easy Communication:** Provide a clear channel for testers to ask questions and report issues.
*   **Incentives (Optional):** Consider offering small incentives (e.g., early access to future features, a small gift card) to motivate participation.
