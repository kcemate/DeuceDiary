Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, covering different tiers and approaches, with considerations for various business types:

**1. Program Name & Overall Theme:**

* **Option 1 (Simple):** “Spread the Word Rewards”
* **Option 2 (Benefit-focused):** “Grow With Us - Refer & Get”
* **Option 3 (Unique):** (If you have a specific brand personality) - “Level Up Your [Product/Service]”

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **Referrer:** The customer who makes a referral.
* **Referee:** The new customer acquired through the referral.
* **Tracking:**  Crucially, you *must* have a system to track referrals.  This could be:
    * **Unique Referral Links:**  Each referrer gets a unique link (using a tool like ReferralCandy, Gleam.io, or a custom solution). This is the gold standard.
    * **Coupon Codes:**  Referrers get a coupon code to share.
    * **Dedicated Referral Page:** A page on your website specifically for the referral program.
* **Activation:**  Clearly communicate how the referral works on your website, in your app, and in marketing materials.

**3. Incentive Structure (Tiered System - Recommended)**

This allows you to reward different levels of engagement and make the program more enticing.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (For Everyone)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  [Small Incentive] -  Examples:
        * **Discount:** 10-15% off their next purchase.
        * **Free Shipping:**  On their next order.
        * **Small Gift Card:** $5 - $10 to your store.
    * **Referee Reward:** [Small Incentive] - Examples:
        * **Discount:** 10% off their first order.
        * **Welcome Offer:** Free sample or small bonus item.
* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level (For Referrers Who Refer 3+ People)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** [Medium Incentive] – Examples:
        * **Increased Discount:** 20-25% off their next purchase.
        * **Bonus Reward:**  $25 - $50 Gift Card.
        * **Exclusive Access:** Early access to new products, features, or sales.
    * **Referee Reward:** (Same as Tier 1, but potentially slightly increased)
* **Tier 3:  Top-Tier (For Referrers Who Refer 5+ People)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** [Significant Incentive] – Examples:
        * **Premium Subscription:** (If applicable) - Free trial or discounted subscription.
        * **High-Value Gift Card:** $100 - $200.
        * **Personalized Offer:**  A custom product recommendation or consultation.



**4.  Incentive Types - Beyond Discounts:**

* **Gamification:**
    * **Points System:**  Referrers earn points for each successful referral, which they can redeem for rewards.
    * **Leaderboard:** (If appropriate for your brand) – Showcase top referrers to create friendly competition.
* **Experiences:**
    * **VIP Events:** Invite top referrers to exclusive events (online or in-person).
    * **Personalized Experiences:**  A call with your team, a custom product selection, etc.
* **Charitable Donations:**  For every referral, donate a small amount to a charity (especially effective if your brand aligns with a cause).
