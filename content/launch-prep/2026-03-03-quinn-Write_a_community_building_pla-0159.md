## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy to build a thriving and engaged community around *Deuce Diary* on Discord and Reddit. **I. Overall Vision:**

To create a welcoming and passionate community around *Deuce Diary*, where fans can connect over their shared love for the story, explore its themes, and collaboratively contribute to its continued legacy. We aim for a community that feels valued, safe, and creatively stimulated.

**II. Platform Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** This will be the central hub for real-time interaction, discussions, and community events.

        * **#fan-art-and-creative:** Showcase fan art, writing, music, and other creative interpretations.
        * **#roleplay-corner:** (Moderated) Dedicated to collaborative roleplaying based on the story.
        * **#voice-chat:** (Moderated) Regular voice chats for events, Q&As, and spontaneous discussions.
        * **#suggestions-and-feedback:**  For constructive criticism and ideas for the creators (if they’re active).
    * **Frequency:**  Active moderation, regular events (see Engagement Tactics).
* **Reddit (Secondary Hub - Amplification & Long-Form Discussions):** Reddit will be used to reach a broader audience, host longer-form discussions, and leverage community-driven content.
    * **Subreddit:**  /r/DeuceDiary - Central hub for posts, discussions, and content sharing.
    * **Channels:**  Maintain specific channels for:
        *  News & Updates (linked to official sources)
        *  Fan Theories & Discussions
        *  Fan Art & Creative Works
        *  General Discussion


**III. Content Strategy:**

* **Creator-Led Content (Initial Launch & Ongoing):**
