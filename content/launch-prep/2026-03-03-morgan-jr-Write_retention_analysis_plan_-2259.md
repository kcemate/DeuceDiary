**3. Key Metrics & Definitions:**

* **Retention Rate:** Percentage of users who remain active after a specific period.  (Calculated as: (Number of users retained after period / Number of users at start of period) * 100)
* **Cohort Analysis:** Grouping users based on their signup date to track retention trends over time.
* **DAU/MAU (Daily Active Users / Monthly Active Users):**  A measure of user engagement.
* **Churn Rate:**  Percentage of users who stop using the product within a specific period. (Calculated as: 100 - Retention Rate)
* **Time to Value (TTV):**  The time it takes for a new user to experience the core value proposition of the product.
