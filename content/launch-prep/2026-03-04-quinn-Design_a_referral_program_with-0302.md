**Program Name:** “Spread the [Your Brand Name] Love” (Replace "[Your Brand Name]" with your actual brand name)

**I. Core Program Structure:**

* **Goal:** Drive new customer acquisition and increase brand awareness.
* **Duration:** Ongoing (with potential seasonal boosts)
* **Tracking:** Utilize a referral tracking system (most referral marketing platforms offer this – see recommendations below).  This is crucial for accurate reporting and reward distribution.

**II. Incentive Levels – Tiered Rewards**

We’ll use a tiered system to reward increasing levels of participation and success.

**A. Referee (New Customer) Incentives:**

* **Level 1: Initial Sign-Up Bonus (Automatic)** -  10% off their first order (or equivalent – free shipping, a free trial extension, etc.) - *This is the immediate reward for joining.*
* **Level 2: Referral Reward (Earned upon successful referral)** - 15% off their next order. (Could also be a small discount code - $10-$20) - *This is the primary incentive for the new customer.*
* **Level 3: Bonus Reward (After a specific amount spent)** - After the referee spends $100, they receive a $10 gift card to your store/platform. (Encourages continued engagement).


**B. Referrer (Existing Customer) Incentives:**

* **Level 1:  Basic Referral Bonus (Instant)** - $5 credit to their account for every successful referral.  - *Easy to achieve, maintains engagement.*
* **Level 2:  Tiered Bonus (Based on Number of Referrals)**
    * **2-5 Referrals:** $15 credit to their account.
    * **6-10 Referrals:** $30 credit to their account.
    * **11+ Referrals:** $50 credit to their account. - *This creates a “stretch goal” and rewards high-performing customers.*
* **Level 3:  VIP Reward (Limited Time - Quarterly/Annually)** - Top Referrers (those with the most successful referrals within a defined period) receive exclusive gifts, early access to new products, or a higher referral bonus. - *Creates a sense of prestige and exclusivity.*

**III. Referral Methods & Tracking:**

* **Unique Referral Links:**  Each customer receives a unique referral link via email and within their account dashboard.
* **Social Sharing:**  Provide pre-written social media copy and images that customers can easily share.  (Make it *super* easy for them!)
* **Tracking System:**
    * **ReferralCandy:** Popular, easy to integrate with many platforms.
    * **Friendbuy:**  Specifically designed for referral programs, offers features like group rewards.
    * **Refer-It:**  Another robust platform with various integrations.
    * **Your E-commerce Platform’s Built-In Referral Tool:** (Shopify, WooCommerce, etc. often have basic referral features) - *Good starting point, but may have limitations.*
