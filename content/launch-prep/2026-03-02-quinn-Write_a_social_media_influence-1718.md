**B. Health Influencers**

* **Template 3 (Specific Benefit):** "Hey [Influencer Name]! Your content on [mention a specific topic - e.g., mindful eating, workout routines, stress management] is amazing. We’re developing [product/service] to help people [benefit - e.g., boost their energy, improve their sleep], and we think your audience would love it. 💪"
* **Template 4 (Question & Engagement):** "Hi [Influencer Name], we're always looking for ways to support healthy communities.  What are some of your go-to strategies for [relevant topic - e.g., fueling your workouts, maintaining a balanced diet]? We'd love to learn from your expertise! 🍎"

**C. Humor Influencers**

* **Template 5 (Playful & Relatable):** "OMG [Influencer Name]! Your jokes about [mention a specific joke/topic they're known for] are *everything*. 😂 We’re [Your Brand Name], and we’re trying to bring a little laughter into the world – we think you’d be perfect to join us! 🤪"
* **Template 6 (Opportunity Focused):** "Hey [Influencer Name], We're looking for someone who can make people laugh & think. We’d love to collaborate with you on a fun campaign showcasing our [product/service] - it's surprisingly hilarious! 🤣"

**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **Template 7 (Shared Values):** "Hi [Influencer Name], your lifestyle content around [mention specific themes - e.g., travel, home decor, self-care] is so inspiring!  We're passionate about [align with their brand values – e.g., sustainable living, authentic experiences, slow living], and we’d love to explore a collaboration. 🌿"
* **Template 8 (Simple & Genuine):** "Hey [Influencer Name],  We noticed you have a great eye for beautiful things/experiences. We're [Your Brand Name] and we're trying to bring joy to people's everyday lives. Would you be open to chatting about a potential partnership? 😊"
