**January - New Year, New You (Focus: Motivation, Goals, Wellness)**

* **Date Range:** Jan 1 - Jan 31
* **Theme:** Fresh Starts & Intentions
* **App Focus:** Productivity apps, fitness trackers, habit trackers, meditation apps, goal-setting apps. Highlight features related to new year's resolutions.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * Launch "New Year's Resolution" campaign with targeted ads.
    * Offer free trial or discount for new users.
    * Content: Blog posts, social media tips on setting goals, overcoming procrastination. 
    * Influencer outreach to wellness and productivity accounts.
* **Metrics to Track:** App Downloads, Free Trial Sign-ups, User Activation Rate, Retention Rate



**February - Love & Connection (Focus: Relationships, Romance)**

* **Date Range:** Feb 1 - Feb 28
* **Theme:**  Connection & Romance
* **App Focus:** Dating apps, relationship advice apps, communication apps, gift finding apps.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * Run targeted ads during Valentine's Day.
    * Promote features that help users connect with others. 
    * Content: Blog posts/articles on relationship advice, gift ideas, date night suggestions.
    * Consider a Valentine’s Day discount.
* **Metrics to Track:** App Downloads, User Engagement (within the app), Conversion Rate (from free to paid)
