**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 – Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 marks the official launch of our app, providing a revolutionary way to track and reflect on your daily habits and mood. This release introduces a core set of features designed to help you build better routines and understand yourself better. Thank you for your support!

**I. Social Features**

* **Community Forums (Beta):**
    * Introduced a public forum where users can discuss their experiences, share tips, and support each other’s habit-building journeys. (Moderation is ongoing and user feedback is highly encouraged.)
    * **Limitations:** Forum functionality is currently in beta and may experience occasional instability. Content moderation is performed manually.
* **Friend Connections (Limited):**
    * Users can now connect with up to 5 friends.
    * **Functionality:** Friend connections allow you to see a simplified overview of your friends’ progress (only tracking data - no direct messaging or content sharing).  You can encourage each other and celebrate milestones.
    * **Note:** Privacy is paramount.  You control who you connect with.


**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Tracking:**
    * **Core Habit Creation:**  Easily add new habits with customizable names, descriptions, and frequency (daily, weekly, monthly).
    * **Streak Tracking:** Automatic tracking of your consecutive days completing a habit.
    * **Goal Setting:**  Set quantifiable goals for your habits (e.g., "Read for 30 minutes" instead of just "Read").
* **Mood Logging:**
    * **Daily Mood Scale:** Record your mood on a scale of 1-10 (1 = Very Low, 10 = Very High) with an optional free-text field for notes.
    * **Mood Tagging:** Assign keywords to your mood entries to provide more granular analysis (e.g., "Energetic," "Stressed," "Peaceful").
* **Journaling Integration:**
   * Ability to link a daily journal entry to a specific habit or mood.  This helps provide context for your tracking.
