**I. Q1: January - March – New Year, New Habits**

* **Theme:** New Year’s Resolutions, Self-Improvement, Productivity, Goal Setting.
* **January (Resolution Focus):**
    * **Content:** Blog post: “5 Apps to Help You Crush Your New Year’s Resolutions.” Feature your app prominently.  Short video demonstrating quick wins.
    * **App Store:** Highlight features that directly support goal setting/achievement. Updated screenshots showcasing results.
    * **Social Media:** Polls: "What’s your biggest resolution?"  Motivational quotes, tips for sticking to resolutions. #NewYearNewYou #Goals
    * **Paid Advertising:** Target users interested in self-improvement, productivity, and fitness.
* **February (Love & Connection):**
    * **Content:**  If your app relates to relationships (dating, friendship, family), tailor content to Valentine’s Day.  “5 Ways to Connect with Loved Ones” (featuring your app).
    * **App Store:**  Update screenshots to include romantic themes if appropriate.
    * **Social Media:**  Contests & giveaways, user-generated content (encourage sharing about the app's benefits in relationships). #ValentinesDay #Love
* **March (Spring Awakening):**
    * **Content:** Transition to broader self-care & wellness. Blog: “Spring Cleaning for Your Mind & Body” – how your app supports this.
    * **App Store:**  Highlight features related to wellness, stress reduction, or positive habits.
    * **Social Media:**  Introduce seasonal imagery/themes, focus on motivation and a fresh start.  #SpringIntoWellness #NewBeginnings


**II. Q2: April - June – Spring & Back to School**

* **Theme:** Spring, Productivity, Education, Back to School, Mental Wellness.
* **April (Spring & Productivity):**
    * **Content:** Blog: "Boost Your Productivity This Spring” – how your app helps. Tips for Spring cleaning (if relevant).
    * **App Store:** Highlight features that support organization, task management, or time management.
    * **Social Media:** Images/videos of spring activities, user testimonials, productivity tips. #SpringProductivity #GetOrganized
* **May (Mental Wellness & Appreciation):**
    * **Content:** Focus on mental health awareness.  Blog: “5 Ways to Prioritize Your Mental Wellbeing.”  Highlight mindfulness or relaxation features within your app.
    * **App Store:**  Update screenshots to reflect a positive and supportive aesthetic.
    * **Social Media:**  Share mental health resources, run a campaign focused on self-care. #MentalHealthAwareness #SelfCare
* **June (Back to School & Learning):**
    * **Content:** If your app caters to learning, students, or education – “Back to School Survival Guide” – feature your app as a tool for success.
    * **App Store:**  Update screenshots and descriptions to highlight educational features.
    * **Social Media:** Run contests for students, share study tips, promote app's learning capabilities. #BackToSchool #StudentLife
