Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, each designed for a different goal, with estimated timing and content suggestions.  I've aimed for realistic lengths and engagement levels.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Onboarding - (Goal: Establish Relationship & Drive Initial Product Adoption)**

* **Target Audience:** New Subscribers
* **Timeline:** 3-5 Days
* **Goal:**  Welcome the subscriber, introduce your brand, highlight key benefits, and encourage their first purchase/action.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1: (Day 1 - Immediately After Sign-Up)**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand]! We’re Thrilled You’re Here!
    * **Content:**  Warm greeting, express gratitude for joining, brief brand story (1-2 sentences), highlight 1-2 *biggest* benefits of your product/service, and a clear call to action (CTA) - “Explore our [Product/Service]” with a direct link.  Include a friendly, welcoming image.
* **Email 2: (Day 3)**
    * **Subject:**  Unlock the Potential of [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Focus on a specific, common pain point your product solves. Share a short, engaging story or testimonial.  Include a screenshot or GIF demonstrating a key feature.  CTA: “See it in Action” or “Get Started Now”.
* **Email 3: (Day 5)**
    * **Subject:**  Top 3 Ways to Get the Most Out of [Your Product/Service]
    * **Content:**  Quick tips, tutorials, or a link to a helpful resource (blog post, video) demonstrating best practices.  CTA: "Learn More" or "Watch the Tutorial."



**Campaign 2:  Lead Nurturing - (Goal: Move Leads Closer to Purchase)**

* **Target Audience:**  Subscribers who’ve engaged with previous emails (e.g., clicked a link, visited a key page) - or those who’ve shown interest in a specific product category.
* **Timeline:** 7-10 Days
* **Goal:**  Address objections, build trust, and encourage a purchase decision.

**Sequence:**

* **Email 1: (Day 7)**
    * **Subject:**  Still Thinking About [Your Product/Service]? Here’s Why You’ll Love It.
    * **Content:**  Reiterate key benefits, but now address common objections (e.g., "Is it expensive?", "Is it complicated to use?").  Include a social proof element (customer testimonial). CTA: “See Our Pricing”
* **Email 2: (Day 9)**
    * **Subject:**  [Number] Reasons Why [Your Product/Service] is the Right Choice for You
    * **Content:** List of 3-5 compelling reasons to purchase, framed as solutions to the subscriber's needs.  Use bullet points and visuals.  CTA: “Claim Your Exclusive Offer”.
* **Email 3: (Day 11)**
    * **Subject:**  Don’t Miss Out! Last Chance for [Limited-Time Offer]
    * **Content:**  Create a sense of urgency – highlight a limited-time discount, bonus, or free shipping.  Reiterate the core value proposition. CTA: “Shop Now Before It’s Gone!”



**Campaign 3:  Post-Purchase Engagement - (Goal: Increase Customer Loyalty & Encourage Upsells)**

* **Target Audience:** Customers who have recently made a purchase
* **Timeline:** 7-14 Days
* **Goal:**  Thank the customer, provide helpful resources, encourage reviews, and offer relevant upsell/cross-sell opportunities
