Okay, here’s a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service, broken down by week with suggested content types, goals, and estimated effort. This is a template – **you’ll need to customize it heavily based on your specific product, target audience, and launch strategy.**

**Assumptions:**

* **You've already had a launch event/announcement.** This calendar builds on that momentum.
* **You're focused on driving awareness, user engagement, and early adoption.**
* **You'll be using a mix of channels:** Social Media (primarily), Email, Blog, and potentially Paid Advertising.


**Key:**

* **Effort:** (Low, Medium, High) – Based on estimated time to create and schedule.
* **Goal:** What you're trying to achieve with the content.
