## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy for building a thriving and engaged community around *Deuce Diary* utilizing Discord and Reddit as primary platforms, with a strong focus on fostering a respectful, creative, and insightful discussion about the series.

**I. Platform Strategy**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):** Discord will be the central command for real-time discussions, organized activity, and community bonding.
    * **Server Structure:**
        * **#general-chat:** Open discussion, news, memes, general series talk.
        * **#deuce-discussion:** Dedicated to analyzing Deuce's actions, motivations, and the series’ themes.
        * **#fan-art-and-creations:** Showcase of fan art, writing, music, and other creative works.
        * **#roleplay-and-stories:** For longer-form roleplaying and collaborative storytelling. (Requires moderation)
        * **#suggestions-and-feedback:**  A space for community suggestions for the creator.
        * **#off-topic:** A designated area for casual conversation.
    * **Channels for Specific Events:**  Create temporary channels for AMAs (Ask Me Anything) with the creator, watch parties, and special series announcements.
* **Reddit (Content Amplifier & Long-Form Discussion):** Reddit provides a space for more in-depth analysis, meta-discussion, and archiving of conversations.
    * **Subreddit:**  `r/DeuceDiary` -  Focus on fostering detailed discussions, theories, and reactions to the series.
    * **Subreddit Rules:** Strict enforcement of the community guidelines outlined below.


**II. Content Strategy**

* **Creator-Driven Content (Essential):** The creator *must* actively participate in both Discord and Reddit to lead the community.
    * **Regular Q&A Sessions:** Weekly or bi-weekly Q&A sessions on Discord and Reddit.
    * **Behind-the-Scenes Content:**  Teasers, concept art, lore reveals, and insights into the creative process.
    * **Interactive Polls & Discussions:**  Pose thought-provoking questions related to the series to spark debate.
* **Community-Generated Content (Critical for Growth):**
    * **Fan Art & Creations Contests:** Regular contests with prizes (e.g., creator recognition, merchandise) to encourage participation.
    * **Theory Crafting & Analysis Threads:** Encourage detailed analysis of the series’ mechanics, characters, and narrative.
    * **Writing Prompts & Collaborative Stories:**  Prompts to inspire fan-created stories and roleplaying.
    * **"Deuce's Diary" Entries - Fan-Created:** Encourage members to create their own "Diary" entries reflecting their interpretation of the world.



**III. Moderator Guidelines & Roles**

* **Core Values:** Respect, Creativity, Insight, and Collaboration.
* **Moderator Roles & Responsibilities:**
    * **Community Leaders (Lead Moderators):** Experienced members with strong understanding of the series and the community. Responsible for overall moderation, conflict resolution, and planning community initiatives. (2-3 initially)
    * **General Moderators:** Monitor channels, enforce rules, and assist Community Leaders. (5-10 initially)
    * **Channel Moderators:** Focused on a specific channel (e.g., #fan-art) to ensure compliance with channel-specific rules. (As needed)
* **Enforcement:** Consistent and transparent enforcement of community guidelines. Warnings, temporary bans, and permanent bans for violations.
* **Training:** New moderators receive thorough training on community guidelines, moderation tools, and conflict resolution techniques.
