**B. Humor Influencers (Focus: Relatable Content, Memes, Comedy)**

* **Template 1 (Lighthearted - 1-3k Followers):** “OMG @[Influencer Name]! Your [specific meme/video/post] totally made my day! 😂 Seriously, you're hilarious.  We’re [Your Brand Name] and think your humor would be a perfect fit for our brand!”
* **Template 2 (Collaborative Idea - 3-10k Followers):** “Hey @[Influencer Name]! We were brainstorming a fun collab – maybe a series of silly posts/stories around [relevant theme - e.g., relatable work struggles, funny food fails]?  Thoughts? 🤪”
* **Template 3 (Direct Ask - 10-20k Followers):** "Big fan of your content @[Influencer Name]!  We're looking to inject some humor into our [platform - e.g., TikTok, Instagram] feed.  Would you be open to a sponsored post or a funny challenge? 😄”

**C. Lifestyle Influencers (Focus: Fashion, Travel, Home Decor, General Vibes)**

* **Template 1 (Observation - 1-3k Followers):** “Hi @[Influencer Name]!  Your aesthetic is *amazing* – we love how you [specific element - e.g., style your outfits, decorate your space].  We're inspired! ✨”

We’d love to see if you’d be interested in partnering to share our products with your audience! ✈️”
* **Template 3 (Engagement - 10-20k Followers):** “Hey @[Influencer Name]!  What’s your go-to weekend activity?  We’re always looking for inspiration – and your style is always on point! ☀️”
