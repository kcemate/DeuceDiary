**2. Definitions**

* **Incident:** Any event that violates or threatens to violate organizational security policies, standards, or procedures, or could compromise the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of information or systems.
* **Severity Levels:**
    * **P0 (Critical):**  Widespread impact; significant business disruption; data breach impacting sensitive information; potential legal or regulatory implications.  Requires immediate, full-scale response.
    * **P1 (High):**  Localized impact; moderate business disruption; potential compromise of critical systems or data; requires immediate, targeted response.
    * **P2 (Medium):**  Minor impact; limited disruption to business operations; potential vulnerability exploitation; requires prompt, focused response.
