**Retention Analysis Plan**

**1. Goal:** To understand user retention patterns, identify key drivers of churn, and implement strategies to improve overall retention rates.

**2. Timeframes & Benchmarks:**

* **D1 (Day 1):** Initial Activation & Engagement -  Focus: Ensuring the first experience is smooth and sets users up for success.
* **D7 (Day 7):** Early Engagement & Habit Formation - Focus: Identifying if users are returning and developing a core habit.

**3. Metrics to Track:**

* **DAU (Daily Active Users):**  Number of unique users active each day.

* **Churn Rate:** The inverse of retention (1 - Retention Rate).  Calculated for each cohort.
* **Feature Usage:** Track which features users are engaging with most frequently. This can highlight valuable features driving retention or neglected ones.
* **Time Spent in App/Platform:**  A proxy for engagement.
* **Key Actions/Events:** Track completion of critical actions within the product (e.g., completing a tutorial, making a purchase, inviting a friend).  These events often correlate with retention.
* **Net Promoter Score (NPS) / Customer Satisfaction (CSAT):**  Gauge user sentiment - changes here can be indicators of deeper retention issues.
