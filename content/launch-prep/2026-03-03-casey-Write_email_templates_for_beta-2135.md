```email
*   Spend approximately [estimated time – e.g., 1-2 hours] exploring the app/product.
*   Report any bugs you encounter using [Link to Bug Reporting System - e.g., Google Form, Jira].

We'll be actively monitoring the feedback and releasing updates regularly.  We value your honest opinions, even if they’re critical – it's what helps us build the best possible product.

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```
