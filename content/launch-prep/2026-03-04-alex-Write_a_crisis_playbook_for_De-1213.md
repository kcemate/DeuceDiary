**III. Potential Crisis Categories & Response Protocols:**

**A. Technical Issues (Website Downtime, Bugs, Slow Loading):**

* **Severity 1 (Critical - Website Completely Down):**
    * **Immediate Action:** Technical Lead, Communications Lead, Social Media Lead activate.
    * **Communication:** Public statement on website & social media acknowledging the issue, estimated resolution time.  “We’re aware of the problem and our team is working tirelessly to resolve it. We appreciate your patience.”
    * **Resolution:** Prioritize server stability, bug fixes, and load testing.
* **Severity 2 (Major - Significant Bugs/Slow Loading impacting user experience):**
    * **Immediate Action:** Technical Lead, Customer Support Lead escalate.
    * **Communication:**  More detailed statement on social media explaining the bug/issue and steps being taken. Offer alternative solutions (e.g., “If you encounter [specific bug], please contact support.”)
    * **Resolution:**  Implement hotfixes, deploy updates, and closely monitor performance.
* **Severity 3 (Minor - Small bugs/performance issues with minimal impact):**
    * **Immediate Action:** Technical Lead monitors.
    * **Communication:**  Internal notification to Customer Support Lead. 
    * **Resolution:** Address in scheduled maintenance or prioritize based on severity.


**B. Content Concerns (Offensive Content, Misinterpretation, Controversial Topics):**

* **Severity 1 (Critical - Content deemed overtly harmful or illegal):**
    * **Immediate Action:** Crisis Lead, Legal Counsel, Communications Lead.
    * **Communication:** Immediate removal of content, public apology addressing the issue, explanation of content moderation policies.
    * **Resolution:**  Review and update content moderation policies, train team members.
* **Severity 2 (Major - Content raises significant concerns but isn't outright harmful):**
    * **Immediate Action:** Crisis Lead, Communications Lead, Social Media Lead.
    * **Communication:** Acknowledgement of concerns, explanation of the context of the content, and reiteration of values. Offer a space for discussion.
    * **Resolution:**  Review the content, consider adjustments, engage in dialogue with the community.
* **Severity 3 (Minor - Concerns about interpretation/nuance):**
    * **Immediate Action:** Communications Lead monitors and responds.
