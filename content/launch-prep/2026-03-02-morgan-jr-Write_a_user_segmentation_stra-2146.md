* **Habit Tracking Focus:**  Simple metrics – steps, water intake, sleep, mood (single choice selections).  They aren’t meticulously logging everything.
* **Personality:** Relaxed, open to experimentation, potentially a bit skeptical of extreme self-improvement.
* **Behavioral Triggers:**

    * **Push Notifications:** Reminders to log a few key metrics ("Check in on your hydration!"), not nagging.

* **Behavioral Triggers:**

    * **Content:**  Motivational content focused on consistency, productivity hacks, and celebrating streak achievements.
