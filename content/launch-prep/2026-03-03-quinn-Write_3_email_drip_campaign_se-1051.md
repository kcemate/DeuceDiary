Okay, here are three email drip campaign sequences, each with a different focus and length. I've included estimated timing, content suggestions, and key metrics to track.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (3-5 Days - Ideal for New Subscribers)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build excitement, and encourage immediate engagement.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who signed up through your website, social media, or a landing page.
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 1): Welcome & Thank You**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:** Warm welcome, express gratitude for subscribing, briefly reiterate the value they’ll receive (e.g., exclusive tips, discounts, product updates).  Include a friendly image or GIF.
        * **Call to Action:** "Learn more about us" (links to your “About Us” page) or “Explore our latest collection”.
    * **Email 2 (Day 3):  Problem/Solution & Brand Story**
        * **Subject:** Struggling with [Pain Point Your Product Solves]?
        * **Content:** Briefly address a common problem your target audience faces.  Introduce your brand as the solution. Share a *very* short, compelling story about why you started the company – focus on empathy and purpose.
        * **Call to Action:** "Read our story" (links to your "About Us" page) or "See how we can help".
    * **Email 3 (Day 5 - Optional):  Quick Tip/Resource**
        * **Subject:**  [Quick Tip related to your industry]
        * **Content:** Provide a valuable, actionable tip or a link to a free resource (e.g., checklist, infographic, short guide). Position yourself as a helpful resource.
        * **Call to Action:** "Download the guide" or "Read the full tip".

* **Key Metrics:** Open Rate, Click-Through Rate (CTR) on "Learn More" or "Explore" links, Unsubscribe Rate.  Focus on getting people to click on links to your website.


**Campaign 2: Product Focus - Lead Nurturing (7-10 Days -  Ideal for E-commerce or SaaS)**

* **Goal:** Introduce specific products/features, build desire, and drive sales.
* **Target Audience:** Subscribers who have shown some interest in your products (e.g., viewed specific product pages, downloaded a related resource).
* **Sequence:**
    * **Email 1 (Day 7):  Highlight a Key Product Benefit**
        * **Subject:**  Unlock [Desired Outcome] with [Product Name]
        * **Content:** Focus on ONE key benefit of a specific product. Use an image or short video. Explain how it solves a specific pain point.
        * **Call to Action:** “Learn More” (links directly to the product page).
    * **Email 2 (Day 9):  Social Proof & Testimonial**
        * **Subject:**  Don’t Just Take Our Word For It…
        * **Content:** Include a short testimonial from a happy customer or a statistic highlighting the product’s success.
        * **Call to Action:** "Read More Reviews" or "See What Others Are Saying".
    * **Email 3 (Day 11):  Limited-Time Offer/Discount** (Consider including a small discount)
        * **Subject:**  Last Chance: Get [Discount]% Off [Product Name]
        * **Content:** Create a sense of urgency. Remind them of the product's value and the limited-time offer.
        * **Call to Action:** “Shop Now”
