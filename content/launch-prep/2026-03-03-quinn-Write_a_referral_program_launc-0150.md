## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, designed to drive user acquisition, increase engagement, and build a vibrant community. The program leverages a tiered reward structure, viral loop mechanics, robust fraud prevention, and comprehensive tracking.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase active users by 20% within 6 months.
* **Secondary Goals:**
    * Grow the Deuce Diary user base.
    * Boost user engagement (daily/weekly usage).
    *  Generate positive word-of-mouth marketing.
    *  Establish a loyal community around the app.

**II. Referral Program Mechanics:**

* **Referrer (Existing User):**
    * **Tier 1 (Initial Launch):** Refer 1 friend who signs up & completes their first diary entry.  Reward: 500 Deuce Coins.
    * **Tier 2 (After 30 days):** Refer 3 friends who sign up & complete their first diary entry. Reward: 1000 Deuce Coins + Exclusive Deuce Diary Badge.
    * **Tier 3 (After 90 days):** Refer 5 friends who sign up & complete their first diary entry. Reward: 2000 Deuce Coins + Early Access to New Features.
* **Referee (New User):**
    * Upon signup, receives a unique referral link.
    * Upon completing their first diary entry, receives 1000 Deuce Coins.
* **Deuce Coins:** An in-app currency redeemable for exclusive features, premium content, or digital rewards within Deuce Diary.
* **Viral Loop:**  Referrers can share their personalized Deuce Diary with friends via social media, encouraging reciprocal referrals.


**III. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Social Media Sharing:**  Easy sharing buttons integrated within the app for sharing diary entries & referral links on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.  We’ll create pre-designed templates for shareable graphics.
* **"Refer a Friend" Notifications:**  In-app notifications reminding referrers about their referral progress and potential rewards.
* **Community Spotlight:**  Highlight successful referrals and active communities within the Deuce Diary app – boosting excitement and motivating further referrals.
* **Contests & Challenges (Phase 2):** Introduce referral-based contests (e.g., "Most Referrals in a Month") with even bigger prizes to further drive viral growth.


**IV. Fraud Prevention:**

* **Email Verification:**  Require email verification for both referrers and referees to minimize fake accounts.
* **IP Address Monitoring:**  Track IP addresses for suspicious activity – multiple referrals from the same IP address could trigger manual review.
* **Duplicate Account Detection:**  Implement algorithms to identify and flag potential duplicate accounts.
* **Manual Review Queue:**  Establish a dedicated team to manually review flagged accounts and referrals to confirm legitimacy.  This is crucial in early phases.
* **Referral Link Tracking:**  Each referral link will be unique and traceable back to the referrer.
* **Limit Referral Attempts:** Consider a cap on the number of referrals a user can make within a specific timeframe.

* **Dedicated Tracking Pixel:** Implement a tracking pixel on the app landing page and within the app itself.
