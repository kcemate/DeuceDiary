**I. DM Templates (Tailored for Each Niche)**

**A. General Initial DM (All Niches - Send within 24-48 hours of finding their account)**

* **Option 1 (Warm & Casual):** "Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Just came across your account and absolutely love your [mention specific content - e.g., healthy recipes, hilarious memes, travel vlogs]! You have a really engaged audience. [Brand Name] is all about [briefly state your brand’s mission - e.g., making wellness accessible, spreading joy, inspiring authentic living].  Would love to chat about potential collaboration opportunities!"
* **Option 2 (Direct & Value-Focused):** "Hi [Influencer Name], We’re [Brand Name] and we’re always looking for creators who share our passion for [relevant niche - e.g., healthy living, comedy, lifestyle].  We think your style would be a fantastic fit!  Could we schedule a quick call to discuss a collaboration?"

**B. Health Influencers**

* **Follow-up (After initial DM gets a response):** "Great to hear from you! We’re really impressed with your focus on [mention their specific health focus - e.g., mindful eating, yoga, fitness for beginners].  We’re developing [briefly describe your product/service – e.g., a new smoothie recipe book, a workout program] and think it could resonate really well with your followers.  Would you be open to learning more?"
* **Specific Question (if relevant):** “We noticed you share a lot about [a specific topic they focus on].  Would you be interested in discussing how we could create content around that?”

**C. Humor Influencers**

* **Follow-up (After initial DM gets a response):** "Your sense of humor is amazing! 😂  We’re [Brand Name] and we're building a campaign that's all about [describe the campaign’s tone – e.g., lighthearted fun, relatable struggles, silly moments]. We think your style would be perfect!  Tell us, what kind of content do you enjoy creating?"
* **Refer to a Specific Post:** “Loved your recent post about [mention a specific funny post]! It’s hilarious/relatable. We’re working on something similar…"

**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **Follow-up (After initial DM gets a response):** "We admire your curated lifestyle!  [Brand Name] helps people [briefly explain what your brand offers – e.g., create organized spaces, find beautiful home decor, plan amazing travel experiences]. We believe your followers would appreciate our [product/service].  Do you spend a lot of time on [mention a relevant lifestyle area – e.g., home styling, travel planning, wellness routines]?"
* **Ask about their audience:** "What are some of the things your audience is most interested in when it comes to [relevant lifestyle category]?"
