* **Speed of Response:** Time is critical. Acknowledge the issue quickly, even if you don’t have all the answers immediately.
* **Centralized Communication:**  Designate a small, dedicated team to manage all communication channels. (e.g., Lead Developer, Marketing Lead, Community Manager)

* **Prioritize User Wellbeing:**  Your users are your priority. Focus on solutions and mitigating frustration.

    * **Regular Updates:** Provide updates on the progress of the fix – "We’ve identified the bottleneck and are implementing scaling solutions."
    * **Offer Workarounds (If Possible):** Can users temporarily use a limited feature or access data offline?
    * **Extended Support:**  Increase support team staffing to handle increased inquiries.
* **Long-Term Actions:**
    * **Scalability Review:**  Analyze server infrastructure to ensure it can handle peak loads.
    * **Automated Monitoring:** Implement more robust monitoring and alerting systems.
