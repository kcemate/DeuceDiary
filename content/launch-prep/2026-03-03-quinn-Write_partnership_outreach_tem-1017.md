**Template 1: Casual - Email to Micro-Influencers/Smaller Wellness Blogs (Focus: Authenticity & Engagement)**

**Subject: Love Your [Brand Area] - Collaboration Idea!**

Hi [Influencer Name],

Just stumbled across your awesome blog/Instagram feed and really love your focus on [Specific niche - e.g., mindful mornings, healthy snacks, yoga for beginners].  I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand], and we’re passionate about [Your Brand’s Mission - e.g., making healthy eating fun, supporting accessible wellness].

We were thinking, it could be really cool to [Suggest a simple collaboration - e.g., you could try our new smoothie recipe and share it, we could do a giveaway on your page].  No pressure at all, just wanted to see if you'd be open to a chat about potential collaborations!

Check out our page here: [Link to your website/Instagram]

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Brand]

**Why this works:**  It's friendly, acknowledges their content, and suggests a low-commitment first step.

**Here's what we’re proposing:** [Clearly outline the partnership – e.g., Sponsored Post, Product Review, Affiliate Program, Joint Webinar]

*   **Our Offer:** [Detail the value you're offering - e.g., Free product samples, Commission on sales, Featured content on our platform]

*   **Timeline:** [Suggest a realistic timeline - e.g., 4-6 weeks for the initial campaign]

You can find more information about us here: [Link to your website]

We'd love to schedule a brief call to discuss this further.  Are you available for a quick chat next week?

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand]
[Contact Information]
