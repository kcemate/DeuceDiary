**II. Phase 1: Assessment & Foundations (Month 1-3)**

* **Goal:** Understand the current state, identify key opportunities, and establish key metrics.
* **Activities:**
    * **1. Data Audit & Analysis:**
        * **CRM Analysis:** Deep dive into your CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.) - identify lead sources, conversion rates, customer lifetime value (CLTV), and churn rates.
        * **Website Analytics (Google Analytics, etc.):**  Analyze traffic sources, user behavior (bounce rates, time on site, pages visited), and conversion funnels.
        * **Sales Data:**  Analyze sales cycles, average deal size, win rates, and sales team performance.
        * **Marketing Data:** Evaluate campaign performance (email, social media, paid advertising) – ROI, engagement, and lead quality.
    * **2. Customer Segmentation:**
        * Define key customer segments based on demographics, behavior, needs, and value.  Prioritize segments with the highest CLTV and potential for growth.
    * **3. Competitive Analysis:**
        * Understand your competitors’ pricing, products/services, marketing strategies, and customer experience.
    * **4. Key Performance Indicator (KPI) Definition:**
        * **Acquisition:** Cost Per Acquisition (CPA), Lead Quality Score
        * **Activation:** Activation Rate (percentage of new users who complete a key action)
        * **Retention:** Customer Retention Rate, Churn Rate
        * **Revenue:** Average Order Value (AOV), Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV), Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) – (if applicable)
        * **Profitability:** Gross Profit Margin, Contribution Margin
* **Deliverables:**
    * Detailed report outlining current performance against KPIs.
    * Customer segmentation map.
    * Competitive landscape assessment.
    * Defined KPI targets and tracking methodology.

* **Goal:** Implement low-hanging fruit to drive immediate revenue gains.
* **Activities:**

    * **2. Website Optimization:**
        * Improve calls-to-action (CTAs) – make them more prominent and persuasive.
        * Optimize landing pages for specific customer segments and keywords.
        * Streamline the checkout process – reduce friction.
    * **3. Sales Process Enhancement:**
        * Refine sales scripts and training materials.
        * Implement a lead scoring system to prioritize high-potential leads.
        * Shorten the sales cycle.
    * **4. Targeted Marketing Campaigns:**
        * Run retargeting campaigns to re-engage website visitors.
        * Implement email marketing automation to nurture leads and drive conversions.
* **Deliverables:**
    * Documented pricing optimization strategy with results.
    * Updated website copy and CTAs.
