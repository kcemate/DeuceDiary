## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently monitoring and analyzing your competition's activities to inform strategic decision-making. It focuses on identifying key areas of competitive focus, establishing measurable metrics, and building a repeatable process for tracking and reporting.

**I. Goals & Objectives**

* **Strategic Alignment:** Understand how your competitors are impacting your business goals (e.g., market share, customer acquisition, revenue growth).
* **Identify Threats & Opportunities:** Proactively identify potential threats (new entrants, disruptive technologies, competitive pricing) and opportunities (emerging trends, gaps in the market).
* **Inform Tactical Decisions:**  Use competitive insights to adjust your marketing, product development, pricing, and sales strategies.
* **Benchmark Performance:**  Assess your own performance against the competition to identify areas for improvement.


**II. Scope & Focus Areas**

This plan will focus on the following areas, allowing for a targeted approach:

* **Product/Service Offering:** Features, quality, innovation, pricing, and packaging.
* **Marketing & Promotion:** Channels used, messaging, content, campaigns, and promotions.
* **Sales & Distribution:** Sales models, channel partners, customer service, and sales process.
* **Customer Experience:** Customer reviews, support, onboarding, and overall satisfaction.
* **Technology & Innovation:** New products, features, and technologies developed.
