Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a detailed breakdown, considering different budgets and complexity levels, with variations to cater to different business types.

**Program Name:** (Let’s call it) “Share the [Your Brand Name] Love”

**I. Core Components:**

* **Simple Referral Process:**  Make it incredibly easy for both the referrer and the referred to participate.
* **Tracking:** Robust tracking is *essential*. You need to know who sent who.
* **Communication:** Clear and regular communication keeps everyone engaged.

**II. Referral Levels & Incentives (Tiered System)**

We’ll implement a tiered system to reward different levels of activity.  Here’s a framework, adaptable to your business:

**Tier 1: The Basic Referrer (Easy to Achieve)**

* **Trigger:** Referred user makes a purchase over $X (e.g., $50) *or* completes a specific action (e.g., signs up for a free trial, creates an account).
* **Referrer Incentive:** 
    * **Option 1 (Discount):** 10% off their next purchase.
    * **Option 2 (Credit):** $5 credit to their account.
    * **Option 3 (Small Gift):**  Small branded item (sticker, keychain, etc.).  (More budget-friendly)
* **Referred Incentive:** 10% off their first purchase.
* **Ease of Use:**  Simple shareable link, pre-written email template, social media copy.

**Tier 2: The Active Promoter (More Commitment Required)**

* **Trigger:** Referred user makes a purchase over $Y (e.g., $150) *and* at least 3 referrals have been accepted.  (This encourages multiple referrals)
* **Referrer Incentive:** 
    * **Option 1 (Higher Discount):** 15% off their next purchase.
    * **Option 2 (Gift Card):** $25 gift card to your store.
* **Referred Incentive:** 15% off their first purchase.
* **Extra Communication:**  Personalized email update to the referrer on the status of their referrals.

**Tier 3: The Top Referral (Highest Commitment - For Established Brands)**

* **Trigger:** Referred user makes a purchase over $Z (e.g., $500) *and* at least 10 referrals have been accepted.
* **Referrer Incentive:** 
    * **Option 1 (Significant Discount):** 20% off their next purchase.
    * **Option 2 (Exclusive Offer):** Access to a limited-edition product or service.
* **Referred Incentive:** 20% off their first purchase + expedited shipping.
* **Recognition:** Public acknowledgement on social media (with referrer’s permission).



**III.  Incentive Types & Considerations**

* **Discounts:**  Most common and generally effective.
* **Credits/Store Credit:**  Allows customers to choose what they want.
* **Gift Cards:** Versatile, but ensure they can be redeemed.
* **Free Products/Services:** Great for high-value products/services.  Requires careful consideration of margin.
* **Early Access:**  Giving referral participants early access to new products or features.
* **Loyalty Points:**  If you have a loyalty program, integrate the referral bonus into it.
* **Contests/Sweepstakes:** (More complex) – Referral contributes to winning a larger prize.
