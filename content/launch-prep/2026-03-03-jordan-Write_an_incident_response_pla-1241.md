**2. Definitions:**

* **Incident:** An event that has the potential to disrupt business operations, compromise data confidentiality, integrity, or availability, or violate security policies.
* **Severity Levels:**
    * **P0 (Critical):**  Imminent, widespread disruption, significant data loss/compromise, potential legal/regulatory repercussions, or immediate threat to life/safety.

    * **P2 (Medium):**  Minor disruption to non-critical systems, potential for limited data compromise, impacting a small number of users, requiring prompt investigation and resolution.
