**Metrics:**
* **Install Rate (IR):** Percentage of users who install the app after viewing the app store listing. (Primary KPI)
* **Conversion Rate (CR):** Percentage of users who view the app store listing and then install the app. (Important for understanding user journey)
* **App Page Views:**  Number of times the app store listing is viewed. (Indicates interest)
* **Keyword Rankings:** Tracking of keyword positions (more granular tracking can be added with keyword tracking tools).
* **Screenshot Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of users who click on a screenshot within the app store listing.
* **Video View Rate:** Percentage of users who watch the app store video.
* **User Ratings & Reviews:**  Monitoring changes in average rating and sentiment. (Indirectly impacted by ASO)

**Tools:**
* **App Store Connect:** For direct data tracking and manipulation of metadata.
* **Sensor Tower/App Annie/Mobile Action:** For comprehensive keyword tracking, competitive analysis, and advanced analytics.
* **Google Analytics for Firebase:** To track in-app user behavior and correlate with ASO changes. (Optional, but valuable)
