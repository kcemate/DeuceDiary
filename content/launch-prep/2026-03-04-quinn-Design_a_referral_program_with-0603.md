Okay, let's design a referral program with tiered incentives. This design focuses on motivating both the referrer and the referee, creating a win-win situation.

**Program Name:** "Share the [Your Brand Name] Love" (Replace "[Your Brand Name]" with your actual brand)

**Overall Goal:** Increase brand awareness, acquire new customers, and build a strong community.

**I. Program Structure - Tiered Rewards**

We'll use a tiered system to reward different levels of participation and success. This encourages both new and existing users to actively refer.

**Tier 1:  Basic Referral (Everyone)**

* **Referrer Reward:** 10% discount on their next purchase. (Simple and easy to understand)
* **Referee Reward:** 10% discount on their first purchase. (Incentivizes the new customer to try your product/service)
* **Mechanics:**
    * Referrer receives a unique referral link.
    * Referee uses the link to sign up/make a purchase.
    * Both referrer and referee receive the discount code after the referee completes their first purchase.
* **Tracking:**  Use a referral tracking platform (e.g., ReferralCandy, ViralReferral, PartnerStack, or build your own with basic URL parameters) to accurately track referrals.


**Tier 2:  Silver Referral (Reaching a Threshold - e.g., 3 Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** 15% discount on their next purchase + Free [Small Gift - e.g., branded sticker, small accessory relevant to your brand]
* **Referee Reward:** 15% discount on their first purchase.
* **Mechanics:** Same as Tier 1, but the referrer unlocks the enhanced reward after referring 3 new customers.
* **Visibility:**  Make the Silver tier visually distinct in your referral program interface.


**Tier 3: Gold Referral (Reaching a Higher Threshold - e.g., 5 Referrals)**

* **Referrer Reward:** 20% discount on their next purchase + Free [Higher Value Gift - e.g., premium product, exclusive access to something, gift card] + Featured on Your Social Media
* **Referee Reward:** 20% discount on their first purchase.
* **Mechanics:** Same as Tier 1 & 2, but the referrer gets a significant boost.  Highlighting the referrer on social media creates social proof.
* **Exclusivity:**  Consider making Gold tier referrals receive early access to new product launches or special offers.



**II.  Incentive Details & Considerations**

* **Discount Percentages:** Adjust percentages based on your profit margins and the perceived value of your product/service.
* **Gift Value:** The value of the free gifts should be attractive but sustainable for your business.
* **Frequency:**  Discounts are usually applied to the *next* purchase, not immediately.
* **Expiration Dates:**  Set expiration dates for referral codes (e.g., 30 days) to create urgency.
* **Tracking & Attribution:** Absolutely crucial!  Choose a reliable referral tracking platform that accurately attributes sales to the referrer.
* **Communication:**  Clear and consistent communication is vital.
