**Template 1: Short & Sweet - Initial Inquiry (Good for Instagram Influencers & Smaller Brands)**

**Subject: Collaboration Opportunity - [Your Brand] & [Partner Brand]**

Hi [Name],

Love what you’re doing over at [Partner Brand]! We’re [Your Brand Name], a [brief, compelling description of your brand - e.g., "brand focused on mindful movement," "company creating organic skincare," "platform offering guided meditations"].

We think our audiences would really connect – [briefly mention shared values or target audience overlap]. 

Would you be open to a quick chat about potential collaboration ideas?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Website]


**Key Elements:**

*   **Personalized Intro:**  Start with a genuine compliment about their brand.
*   **Concise Description:** Get straight to the point about your brand.
*   **Shared Value/Audience:** Explain *why* you think it’s a good fit.
*   **Call to Action:**  Easy next step (a quick chat).

[Your Name]
[Your Website]
[Link to Social Media]



**Key Elements:**

*   **Demonstrates Research:** Shows you've invested time in understanding their content.
*   **Specific Ideas:**  Offers concrete collaboration suggestions.
*   **Brand Deck (optional but recommended):**  Provides more in-depth information.
*   **Clear Call to Action:**  Suggests a specific meeting time.
