## ASO A/B Test Plan for App Store Elements

This plan outlines an A/B testing strategy for optimizing key App Store elements to improve install rate, retention, and ultimately, revenue.

**I. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase app installs.
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve user engagement (sessions, retention), increase in-app purchase revenue (if applicable).
* **Key Metrics to Track:**
    * **Install Rate (IR):**  Percentage of users who see your app’s listing and then install it. (Most critical)
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) - Title & Icon:** Percentage of users who click on your title and icon in search results.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) - Keyword:** Percentage of users who click on your app listing when searching for specific keywords.
    * **Conversion Rate (CR):** Percentage of users who visit your app’s page and then install it.
    * **Retention Rate (D1, D7, D30):** Measures how many users return to your app after 1, 7, and 30 days.
    * **Average Session Length:** Duration of user sessions within the app.
    * **In-App Purchase Revenue (if applicable):** Measures revenue generated from in-app purchases.

**III. A/B Testing Methodology:**

* **Test Duration:** 4-8 weeks (allowing for sufficient data collection during different times of the day and week).
* **Traffic Allocation:** 50/50 split – Equal number of users exposed to each variant.
* **Segmentation (Optional, for later iterations):** Once initial results are clear, segment based on:
    * **Device Type:** iOS vs. Android
    * **User Demographics:** (If available and ethically sourced – age, location)
    * **Search Terms:** Which keywords users are using to find the app.
* **Tools:**
    * **App Store Connect Analytics:** Primary platform for tracking key metrics.
    * **Sensor Tower, App Annie, Mobile Action:** Advanced analytics and A/B testing tools (consider paid subscriptions for deeper insights).

**IV. Testing Phases & Timeline:**

* **Phase 1: Initial Testing (Week 1-2):**  Focus on testing the most impactful elements – Title, Icon, Short Description.
