Okay, here are 3 email drip campaign sequences, designed for different goals, with estimated timing and content suggestions. These are templates – you’ll need to customize them with your brand voice, specific products/services, and customer data.

**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (Ideal for New Subscribers - 7 Days)**

* **Goal:**  Introduce your brand, build trust, and set expectations.
* **Target:** New subscribers who signed up via a website form, landing page, or contest.
* **Timing:**  Sent automatically 1 day, 3 days, 7 days after signup.
* **Email 1 (Day 1): The Warm Welcome**
    * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
    * **Content:**
        * Thank you for joining the community!
        * Briefly introduce your brand’s mission and values (1-2 sentences).
        * Offer a small incentive – maybe a discount code for 10% off their first purchase, or access to a free resource (e.g., ebook, checklist).
        * Link to your most popular page on your website.
* **Email 2 (Day 3):  A Little More About Us**
    * **Subject:**  What Makes [Your Brand Name] Different?
    * **Content:**
        *  Expand slightly on your brand story – a bit more detail about why you started.
        *  Highlight 2-3 key benefits of your product/service.  Use visuals (images or short video) if possible.
        *  Include a customer testimonial (short and impactful).
* **Email 3 (Day 7):  Popular Content & Community**
    * **Subject:**  You're Not Alone!  Here's What Others Are Loving
    * **Content:**
        * Share a link to a popular blog post, case study, or video tutorial related to your industry/product.
        * Encourage engagement: “Join the conversation!” - Link to your social media pages.
        * Briefly remind them of the incentive offered in Email 1.



**Campaign 2:  Lead Nurturing – Problem/Solution (Ideal for Leads Interested in a Specific Product - 5-10 Days)**

* **Goal:**  Address a pain point your product solves, build desire, and move the lead closer to a purchase.
* **Target:**  Leads who have shown interest in a particular product or service (e.g., downloaded a related ebook, visited a specific product page). *Requires segmentation.*
* **Timing:** Sent every 2-3 days during the 5-10 day period.
* **Email 1 (Day 1): Identifying the Problem**
    * **Subject:**  Struggling with [Specific Pain Point]?
    * **Content:**  Acknowledge the common struggle your product addresses.  Frame it as a relatable experience.  Example: “Are you tired of spending hours on [task]?”
* **Email 2 (Day 3):  Introducing Your Solution**
    * **Subject:**  [Your Brand Name] Can Help You Conquer [Pain Point]
    * **Content:**  Introduce your product as the solution.  Focus on *benefits* – how it alleviates the pain point. Use visuals - show the product in action.
* **Email 3 (Day 5):  Deeper Dive & Social Proof**
    * **Subject:**  See How [Client Name] Solved [Pain Point] With [Your Product]
    * **Content:**  Case study or testimonial highlighting how a customer successfully used your product to overcome the problem.  Quantify the results if possible.
* **Email 4 (Day 8):  Limited-Time
