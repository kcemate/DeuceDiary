**I. SPRING (March - May) - Theme: Renewal, Growth, Fresh Starts**

* **Overall Theme:**  Highlighting new beginnings, motivation, and fresh perspectives. Focus on apps that support personal growth, productivity, health, or creative expression.
* **March - Early April:  “Spring Cleaning” & Productivity**
    * **Theme:**  Declutter your life – both literally and digitally.
    * **Content:** Blog posts about “Spring Cleaning Your Apps,” tips for productivity, “reset your routine” guides.
    * **App Focus:** Productivity apps (task managers, note-taking, calendar apps), cleaning apps, organization apps.
    * **Tactics:**  Run a contest encouraging users to share how they’re using the app to “clean up” their lives.  Limited-time discount on premium features.
* **Mid-April - May: “Get Outside & Grow” & Wellness**
    * **Theme:**  Connecting with nature, healthy habits, and boosting mood.
    * **Content:**  Blog posts about outdoor activities, healthy recipes, mindfulness tips, promoting app’s connection to wellness.
    * **App Focus:** Fitness apps, meditation apps, health & nutrition apps, outdoor activity apps.
    * **Tactics:** Partner with wellness influencers for content. Offer a free trial of a premium feature geared towards outdoor activities.


**II. SUMMER (June - August) - Theme: Fun, Adventure, Connection**

* **Overall Theme:** Embrace summer fun, travel, and connecting with friends and family. Focus on apps that enhance experiences.
* **June: "Summer Kickoff" & Travel**
    * **Theme:** Getting ready for summer, travel planning.
    * **Content:** Guides to summer destinations, packing lists, travel tips, reviews of travel apps.
    * **App Focus:** Travel apps (navigation, booking, currency converters), photo editing apps (for travel photos), map apps.
    * **Tactics:** Run a giveaway of travel accessories.  Promote a travel app tutorial video.
* **July: "Summer Adventures" & Entertainment**
    * **Theme:**  Highlighting user-generated content - Summer adventures!
    * **Content:**  Encourage users to share photos and videos taken with your app using a specific hashtag.  Blog posts about summer activities.
    * **App Focus:** Camera apps, video editing apps, gaming apps, social media apps.
    * **Tactics:**  Feature user-generated content on your social media channels and App Store page.
* **August: "Back to School Prep" & Learning (Light)**
    * **Theme:** Transitioning back to routine, light learning and skill development.
    * **Content:**  Apps for language learning, skill development, and productivity (for students).
    * **App Focus:** Educational apps, language learning apps, productivity apps, skill-sharing apps.
    * **Tactics:** Promote a “back to school” discount.
