## ASO Audit: Health & Social App - “WellConnect” (Example)

**App Name:** WellConnect - Community & Wellbeing

**App Category:** Health & Fitness

**Date:** October 26, 2023

**Executive Summary:** This audit assesses the current ASO strategy for “WellConnect,” a health & social app designed to connect users for motivation, support, and wellness activities. We’ve identified several areas for improvement, focusing on keyword optimization, visual assets, conversion funnel analysis, and competitive positioning to drive downloads and engagement.

**I. Keyword Analysis & Density**

* **Primary Keyword:** Wellbeing, Mental Health, Support Groups
* **Secondary Keywords:** Fitness Community, Wellness Activities, Mindfulness, Meditation, Goal Setting, Social Support, Healthy Lifestyle
* **Long-Tail Keywords:** “Find support groups near me,” “online meditation for anxiety,” “community fitness challenges,” “motivation for weight loss,” “connect with wellness buddies”

**Keyword Density Assessment:**

| Keyword          | Current Usage | Recommended Usage | Rationale                                                                  |
|------------------|---------------|--------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Wellbeing        | High (30%)    | 15-20%              | Over saturation – users are overwhelmed by the term. Needs to be nuanced.      |
| Mental Health     | Medium (20%)   | 10-15%              | Broad, competitive. Prioritize specific user needs.                         |
| Support Groups    | Low (10%)     | 15-20%              | High search volume, opportunity for targeted acquisition.                   |
| Fitness Community | Low (5%)      | 10-15%              | Growing segment; needs better visibility.                                  |

**Recommendations:**

* **Refine Keyword Targeting:** Shift focus to longer-tail keywords with lower competition and higher conversion potential.
* **Keyword Variation:** Utilize variations of keywords throughout the app title, subtitle, keywords field, and app description.
* **Dynamic Keyword Insertion (DKI):**  Explore DKI in paid ads to automatically adjust messaging based on user search queries.
