Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here's a breakdown, combining strategy, different tiers of rewards, and considerations for tracking and execution:

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** “Spread the [Your Brand] Love,” “Refer & Reward,” “Bring a Friend & Get [Benefit]” (Choose something memorable and reflective of your brand).
* **Visuals:**  Use consistent branding – logos, colors, and imagery that reinforce the program.


**2. Core Mechanics & Eligibility:**

* **Who Can Refer?** (Be specific) - Existing customers, employees, loyal subscribers – define the pool.
* **How Does Referral Work?**
    * **Simple Link:** Each referred user gets a unique referral link.
    * **Dedicated Referral Page:** A page on your website where people can easily share the link.
    * **Social Sharing Buttons:** Integrate referral links directly into your social media profiles.
* **Tracking:** Crucial!  You *must* track referrals effectively.  This can be done through:
    * **Unique Referral Links:**  The most reliable method. Track clicks and conversions.
    * **Promo Codes:** Give referrers a unique promo code to use at checkout.
    * **Dedicated Tracking Software:** Consider tools like ReferralCandy, Ambassador, or ReferralStream for more sophisticated tracking, management, and reporting.
* **Terms & Conditions:**  Clear, concise, and easily accessible. Cover:
    *  Eligibility requirements.
    *  How rewards are earned and claimed.
    *  Expiration dates for rewards.
    *  Your right to modify or terminate the program.



**3. Incentive Tiers (Key to Success!)**

This is where you really drive participation. Offer a range of incentives to appeal to different types of referrers.  Here's a tiered approach:

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (Everyone)**
    * **Referrer Reward:** 10% off their next purchase. (Lower cost for you, enticing for the referrer)
    * **Referee Reward:** 5% off their first purchase. (Encourages conversion)
    * **Trigger:**  New customer makes their first purchase *after* being referred.
* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level (Refer 3+ People)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  $25 Gift Card to Your Store / $20 Credit. (Increased incentive)
    * **Referee Reward:** 15% off their first purchase. (Stronger incentive)
    * **Trigger:**  Referee completes their first purchase.
* **Tier 3:  Top Tier (Refer 5+ People - “Brand Advocates”)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  Exclusive VIP Access, Early Access to New Products, or a Limited Edition Item. (High-value, for dedicated fans)
    * **Referee Reward:** 20% off their first purchase + Free Shipping. (Ultimate Incentive)
    * **Trigger:**  Referee completes their first purchase.



**4.  Reward Types & Considerations**

* **Discounts:**  Most common. Easy to administer.
* **Gift Cards:**  Popular and versatile (Amazon, Starbucks, etc.).
* **Free Products/Services:** Excellent for high-value items or recurring subscriptions.
* **Cash Rewards:** (Can be tricky – consider payout thresholds).  Motivates heavily.
* **Experiences:** (If relevant – tickets to events, online courses).  Creates memorable referrals.
* **Tiered Rewards:** Reward larger networks more generously, encouraging continued referrals.
