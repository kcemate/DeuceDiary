## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to track and analyze your competitors' activities, providing valuable insights for strategic decision-making. It focuses on a phased approach, starting with broad monitoring and progressing to deeper, more targeted analysis.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** To understand competitor strategies, identify opportunities and threats, and ultimately improve our own performance.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors (direct & indirect).
    * Track competitor activities in pricing, marketing, product development, and customer experience.
    * Benchmark our performance against key metrics.
    * Identify emerging trends and shifts in the competitive landscape.
    * Inform strategic decisions related to product roadmap, marketing campaigns, and pricing adjustments.


**II. Phase 1: Discovery & Identification (Weeks 1-4)**

* **Identify Competitors:**
    * **Direct Competitors:** Businesses offering similar products/services to the same target audience. (e.g., Nike vs. Adidas)
    * **Indirect Competitors:** Businesses offering alternative solutions that address the same customer needs. (e.g., Spotify vs. Apple Music)
    * **Tools for Identification:** Google Search, Industry Reports, Social Media Search, Customer Reviews, Trade Shows, Competitor Websites.
* **Create a Competitor Matrix:** A spreadsheet to list identified competitors and key information:
    * Company Name
    * Website URL
    * Product/Service Offering
    * Target Audience
    * Market Share (Estimate - based on research)
    * Business Model
    * Strengths & Weaknesses (Initial assessment – will be refined over time)


**III. Phase 2: Metric Tracking & Monitoring (Ongoing - Weekly/Bi-Weekly)**

* **Key Metric Categories & Specific Metrics:** (This is where you’ll tailor metrics based on your industry and business)
    * **Marketing & Sales:**
        * **Website Traffic:**  (Google Analytics, SimilarWeb) - Sessions, Bounce Rate, Time on Site, Pageviews
        * **Social Media Activity:** (Social Media Monitoring Tools - Hootsuite, Brandwatch, Mention) - Followers, Engagement (Likes, Shares, Comments), Brand Mentions, Sentiment Analysis
        * **Advertising Spend:** (Adbeat, SEMrush) - Estimated Spend on Google Ads, Social Media Ads, Display Ads
        * **Lead Generation:** (CRM Data) - Number of Leads, Lead Conversion Rate, Cost Per Lead
        * **Email Marketing Performance:** (Mailchimp, etc.) - Open Rate, Click-Through Rate, Conversion Rate
    * **Product & Innovation:**
        * **New Product Launches:** Track announcements, release dates, features.
        * **Patent Filings:** (Google Patents) -  Understanding competitor innovation.
        * **Software Updates:**  Tracking feature releases and improvements.
    * **Pricing & Promotions:**
        * **Price Changes:** Monitor competitor pricing on products/services.
        * **Promotional Offers:**  Track discounts, bundles, and special offers. (Website monitoring, email subscriptions)
    * **Customer Experience:**
        * **Online Reviews:** (Google Reviews, Yelp, Trustpilot) -  Sentiment Analysis, Key Themes
        * **Social Media Sentiment:**  Track general feelings towards the competitor.
        * **Customer Support Channels:** (Monitor mentions on support forums, social media) -  Response times, resolution rates.
