**1. Core Concept:**

The program's goal is to incentivize existing customers to recommend your product/service to their network.  It's built on a mutually beneficial exchange: the referrer gets something, and the referred person gets something.

* **Tier 2: Gold Referral (For Top Performers)**

* **Tier 3: Platinum Referral (For Brand Advocates - Optional - Best for Established Brands)**
