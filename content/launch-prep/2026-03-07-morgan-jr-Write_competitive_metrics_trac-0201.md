## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how to track and analyze competitor activities to inform strategic decisions and maintain a competitive edge. It focuses on a structured approach, prioritizing key metrics, data sources, and actionable insights.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape, identify opportunities and threats, and inform strategic decisions related to [Your Company's Area - e.g., Marketing, Product Development, Pricing].
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Monitor competitor product releases, feature updates, and pricing changes.
    * Identify competitor marketing campaigns and messaging.
    * Track competitor brand sentiment and customer reviews.
    * Benchmark performance against competitors across key metrics.
    * Proactively anticipate competitor actions.


**II. Key Metrics & Categories:**

We’ll categorize metrics to ensure comprehensive coverage. This list is a starting point and should be tailored to your specific industry and business:

**A. Product & Innovation:**
* **New Product Launches:** Frequency, features, target audience.
* **Feature Releases:** Rate of release, complexity, impact on user experience.
* **Technology Updates:** New technologies adopted, improvements in existing technology.
* **Innovation Pipeline:** (Where possible - often requires deeper intelligence) Patents filed, R&D spending.

**B. Marketing & Sales:**
* **Website Traffic:** (From various sources - Google Analytics, SEMrush) – Overall traffic, traffic by source, bounce rate.
* **Social Media Engagement:** Follower growth, likes, shares, comments, mentions, reach. (Track across all relevant platforms - Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok)
* **Advertising Spend & Performance:** (Using tools like SEMrush, SpyFu) – Keywords targeted, ad spend, click-through rates, conversion rates.
* **Content Marketing:** Blog posts, eBooks, webinars -  Page views, downloads, shares, lead generation.
* **Sales Performance (Where Data is Available):**  Lead conversion rates, average deal size, customer acquisition cost (CAC).

**C. Pricing & Promotions:**
* **Pricing Changes:** Track discounts, promotions, and price adjustments.
* **Bundled Offers:** Analyze competitor bundled offers and their value proposition.
* **Promotional Calendar:**  Identify recurring promotions and seasonal sales.

**D. Customer Perception & Brand Health:**
* **Online Reviews & Ratings:** Monitor reviews on platforms like Google Reviews, Trustpilot, Yelp, and industry-specific review sites.
* **Social Media Sentiment:** Track mentions and conversations around your brand and competitors (using social listening tools).
* **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** (If applicable) Track NPS to gauge customer loyalty and satisfaction.



**III. Data Sources & Tools:**

* **Google Alerts:**  For brand mentions and general industry news.
* **SEMrush/Ahrefs/SimilarWeb:**  Website traffic analysis, keyword research, competitor advertising tracking.
* **Social Media Listening Tools:** Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Brandwatch - For social media monitoring and sentiment analysis.
* **Review Monitoring Tools:**  Reputation.com, Birdeye, Yotpo –  For tracking online reviews.
* **Industry Reports & Analyst Research:** Gartner, Forrester, IDC –  For market trends and competitor strategies.
* **Competitor Websites & Social Media Profiles:**  Direct observation and analysis.
* **Salesforce/CRM Data (If Accessible):** For tracking sales performance and customer interactions.
