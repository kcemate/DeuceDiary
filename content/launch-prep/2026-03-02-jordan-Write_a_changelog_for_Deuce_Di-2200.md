**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Introduction:**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 marks the official launch of our personalized habit tracking and mindful journaling app! This release introduces core features designed to help you build better habits, understand your thoughts, and track your progress in a beautiful and intuitive way.  We’ve poured significant effort into usability and foundational stability. Thank you for joining us on this journey!

**I. Social Features (Beta - Limited Access)**

* **Community Feed (Beta):**  Users can now share their daily entries and celebrate successes with the Deuce Diary community. (Limited to 100 active users initially – invite links will be provided to early adopters).
    * **Sharing:**  Users can choose to share their daily entries (text & media) with the community.
    * **Likes & Comments:**  Users can like and comment on entries shared by others.
    * **Privacy Controls:**  Entries are marked as either 'Public' (visible to the entire community) or 'Private' (only visible to the user).
* **Friend Requests (Coming Soon):**  A feature to connect with friends and share progress directly with them is planned for a future release (Q1 2024).

**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Creation:**
    *  Define habits with custom names, descriptions, and frequencies (Daily, Weekly, Specific Days).
    *  Set reminders for each habit – configurable times and notification types (Push, Email).
    *  Choose habit icons to visually represent each habit.
* **Journaling:**
    *  Rich text editor within entries - Supports bold, italics, lists, and basic formatting.
    *  Date & Time Stamping - Each journal entry is automatically timestamped.
    *  Multimedia Integration - Ability to attach images and audio recordings to journal entries.
* **Habit Tracking Interface:**
    *  Clear visual representation of habit completion across days.
    *  Progress bars to quickly assess daily/weekly/monthly progress.
    *  Ability to mark habits as "completed" or "skipped" for each entry.
* **Streak Tracking:** Accurate tracking of habit streaks to motivate continued progress.

**III. Gamification Features**

* **Points System:** Users earn points for completing habits and writing journal entries.
    *  Points awarded based on frequency and difficulty of habits.
    *  Points awarded for writing long-form entries.
* **Badges:** Unlock badges for achieving milestones (e.g., "7-Day Streak," "100 Entries").
    *  A gallery displays earned badges.
* **Leaderboards (Beta):** (Limited to 100 active users) A community leaderboard showcasing users with the highest point totals.  (Note: This is a beta feature and may be unstable.  Feedback is crucial!)


**IV. UI/UX Improvements**

* **Modern Design:**  A refreshed visual design with a focus on clarity and a clean, minimalist aesthetic.
* **Dark Mode:**  A fully implemented dark mode option for comfortable viewing in low-light conditions.
* **Improved Navigation:** Streamlined navigation for easier access to all features.
* **Onboarding Tutorial:** Interactive tutorial guiding new users through the core functionality of the app.
* **Responsive Design:**  Optimized for a variety of screen sizes (smartphones & tablets).
* **Search Functionality:** Ability to search journal entries for specific keywords.
