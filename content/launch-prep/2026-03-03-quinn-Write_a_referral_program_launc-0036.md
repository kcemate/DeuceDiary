## Deuce Diary Referral Program Launch Plan

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines the launch of a referral program for Deuce Diary, leveraging a tiered system to incentivize both referrers and referees, fostering organic growth and brand loyalty. We'll prioritize a viral loop design, robust fraud prevention, and meticulous tracking for continuous optimization.

**1. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Increase User Base:** Acquire 10% of new users through referrals within the first 3 months.
* **Boost Engagement:** Encourage referred users to actively engage with the Deuce Diary content and features.
* **Build Brand Loyalty:**  Foster a sense of community and ownership among users.
* **Drive Awareness:** Extend reach through existing user networks.

**2. Referral Program Mechanics:**

* **Tiered Rewards:**  We’ll use a tiered structure to create escalating motivation.
    * **Level 1 (Basic):**
        * **Referrer:**  Receives 1 month of premium access to Deuce Diary (typically $19.99).
        * **Referee:**  Free 7-day trial of Deuce Diary Premium.
    * **Level 2 (Silver - After 3 Referrals):**
        * **Referrer:** Receives 6 months of premium access to Deuce Diary.
        * **Referee:** Free 30-day trial of Deuce Diary Premium.
    * **Level 3 (Gold - After 5 Referrals):**
        * **Referrer:**  Exclusive Deuce Diary merchandise (e.g., t-shirt, mug) and 12 months of premium access.
        * **Referee:** Free 60-day trial of Deuce Diary Premium.
* **Referral Link Generation:**  Users will generate unique referral links via a dedicated section in their account settings. This will be easily shareable on social media, email, and messaging apps.
* **Tracking:**  Each referral will be tracked through a unique code assigned to the referrer and referee.
* **Reward Redemption:** A simple and intuitive redemption process within the Deuce Diary app and website.

**3. Viral Loop Design:**

* **Social Sharing:**  Incorporate prominent social sharing buttons directly within the Deuce Diary app and content (e.g., sharing a particularly insightful diary entry with the referral link).
* **Email Marketing:**  Automated email sequence triggered upon referral:
    * **Welcome Email (Referee):**  Thanks them for joining, highlights key features, and includes referral link.
    * **Reminder Email (Referrer):** Reminds them to share their link and offers encouragement.
    * **Reward Claim Email (Referee):**  Reminds them to redeem their free trial.
* **In-App Notifications:**  Notifications to existing users suggesting they share the program with their friends. “Share Deuce Diary and get rewarded!”
* **Content Integration:**  Feature referral program success stories and testimonials within the Deuce Diary blog and content.



**4. Fraud Prevention:**

* **IP Address Monitoring:**  Monitor for suspicious IP address activity – multiple referrals from the same IP.
* **Account Verification:** Implement a basic account verification process (e.g., email verification, social login) to reduce fake accounts.
* **Manual Review:**  Establish a system for manual review of flagged referrals.
* **Limit Referrals per User:**  Implement a reasonable limit on the number of referrals a user can generate within a given period (e.g., 10 per week).
* **Referral Link Tracking:**  Strictly enforce the use of the unique referral link - no bulk referral link generation.

**5. Tracking Implementation:**

* **Dedicated Tracking Pixel:** Implement a tracking pixel on the Deuce Diary website and app to track referral clicks and conversions.
