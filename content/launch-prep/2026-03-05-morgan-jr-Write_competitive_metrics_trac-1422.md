## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines a framework for consistently tracking and analyzing your competitors' activities, ultimately informing your own strategy and driving growth. It focuses on a layered approach, ranging from high-level brand monitoring to deep-dive feature analysis.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Overall Goal:** Understand the competitive landscape to identify opportunities and threats, inform strategic decisions, and maintain a competitive advantage.
* **Specific Objectives:**
    * Identify key competitors and their strengths/weaknesses.
    * Track competitor pricing and promotional activities.
    * Monitor competitor product development and feature releases.
    * Understand competitor marketing strategies and messaging.
    * Identify potential areas for innovation and differentiation.

**II. Target Competitors:**

* **Tier 1 (Direct Competitors):** Companies offering similar products or services to the same target audience. (Example: If you sell SaaS CRM, Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho CRM are Tier 1)
* **Tier 2 (Adjacent Competitors):** Companies offering complementary products or services. (Example: For a CRM, a marketing automation platform would be a Tier 2 competitor)
* **Tier 3 (Potential Disruptors):** Emerging companies or technologies that could significantly impact the market. (Example: A new AI-powered CRM)

**III. Metrics Categories & Specific Metrics:**

This will be broken down by frequency and depth:

**A. High-Frequency (Daily/Weekly - Automated where possible):**

* **Website Traffic:** (Google Analytics, SEMrush, Ahrefs)
    * Overall Traffic
    * Traffic by Source (Organic, Paid, Referral, Direct)
    * Top Pages (Identify popular content and features)
* **Social Media Activity:** (Social Media Monitoring Tools - Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Mention)
    * Follower Growth
    * Engagement Rate (Likes, Shares, Comments)
    * Brand Mentions (Sentiment Analysis - Positive, Negative, Neutral)
    * Content Performance (Which posts are generating the most engagement?)
* **Pricing & Promotions:** (Manual monitoring, Price Comparison Tools – Prisync, CamelCamelCamel)
    * Price Changes
    * New Promotions (Discounts, Bundles, Free Trials)
* **SEO Keyword Rankings:** (SEMrush, Ahrefs, Moz)
    * Track rankings for key search terms

**B. Medium-Frequency (Weekly/Monthly - Manual & Automated):**

* **Product Updates & Feature Releases:** (Competitor Websites, Blogs, Press Releases, Beta Programs)
    * Document new features, enhancements, and API updates.
* **Content Marketing:** (Competitor Blogs, Websites, eBooks, Whitepapers)
    * Track new content types, topics, and publishing frequency.  Analyze content quality & effectiveness.
* **Advertising Campaigns:** (SpyFu, iSpionage, SEMrush)
    * Monitor paid search campaigns – keywords, ad copy, landing pages.
* **Customer Reviews & Ratings:** (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, Google Reviews)
    * Track overall rating and identify common themes in reviews.
* **Sales & Marketing Materials:** (Competitor Websites, Brochures, Case Studies)
    *  Analyze messaging, value propositions, and target audiences.


**C. Low-Frequency (Quarterly/Annually - Deep Dive)**

* **Market Share Analysis:** (Industry Reports - Gartner, Forrester, Statista)
* **Innovation & R&D:** (Patent Filings, Industry News, Tech Conferences) - Identify emerging technologies and potential threats.
* **Strategic Partnerships & Acquisitions:** (Press Releases, News Articles)
* **Customer Satisfaction Scores (CSAT) & Net Promoter Score (NPS):** - Use competitor analysis to understand the *why* behind customer feedback.
