## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 Changelog - Launch!

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Summary:** Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is officially live! This launch marks the beginning of our journey to help you cultivate healthy habits and track your daily progress. This version introduces core features for journaling, habit tracking, and community engagement.

---

**1. Social Features:**

* **Community Feed:** Introduced a basic community feed where users can share their daily entries and encourage each other. (Limited functionality - mainly just viewing entries)
* **Following/Followers:** Users can now follow other users within the Deuce Diary community.
* **Entry Sharing:** Users can choose to publicly share their daily entries on the community feed. (Privacy settings allow users to control visibility)
* **Basic Commenting:** Ability to leave comments on public entries, fostering discussion and support.


**2. Tracking Features:**

* **Daily Journal Entry:** Core journaling feature allowing users to record thoughts, feelings, and experiences.
* **Habit Tracking:** Users can define and track up to 5 key habits per day.
* **Habit Completion Marking:**  Simple checkbox to mark habits as completed for the day.
* **Habit Streak Tracking:**  Automatic tracking of consecutive days a habit has been completed. Displayed prominently.
* **Customizable Habit Reminders:** Set reminders for each habit at specific times. (Initially, only 1 reminder per habit can be configured).
* **Data Visualization (Basic):**  Simple charts displaying habit completion rates over the past 7 days.


**3. Gamification Features:**

* **Daily Completion Reward:** Earn a "Sparkle" badge upon completing all tracked habits for the day. (Sparkle badges are purely cosmetic at this stage)
* **Streak Bonus:** Earn bonus "Sparkle" badges for maintaining longer streaks of habit completion. (Rewards are awarded for streaks of 3, 7, 14, and 30 days)
* **Leaderboard (Limited):** A basic leaderboard showing the users with the most consistent habit completion (Top 10). (Leaderboard data is calculated based on completion percentage over the last 30 days.)
* **Daily Challenge:** A randomly generated daily challenge to encourage users to try new habits (e.g., "Meditate for 5 minutes," "Drink 8 glasses of water").
