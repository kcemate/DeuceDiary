## Deuce Diary v1.0.0 Changelog - Launch!

**Date:** October 26, 2023

**Welcome to Deuce Diary!** This is our first major release and represents a huge step forward in our mission to help you build better habits. We’re incredibly excited to share this version with you and welcome your feedback!

**Summary:** v1.0.0 introduces the core features of Deuce Diary, allowing you to track habits, set goals, and start building a better you.

---

**1. Social Features:**

* **Community Feed (Beta):** Share your daily updates, achievements, and challenges with the Deuce Diary community! (Limited users to allow for testing and moderation).
    *  Post updates with text and images.
    *  Like and comment on other users’ posts.
    *  Basic moderation tools in place – reported posts are reviewed.
* **Follow & Followers:**  Connect with other users and see their progress.
    *  Ability to follow other users' diaries.
    *  Display of followers on user profiles.
* **Privacy Settings:** Control who can see your diary and activity.
    *  Option to set your diary as “Public,” “Followers Only,” or “Private.”


**2. Tracking Features:**

* **Habit Creation:** Easily add new habits with detailed customization.
    *  Name & Description:  Clearly define your habit.
    *  Frequency:  Select frequency (Daily, Specific Days, Weekly) and set reminders.
    *  Goal Setting: Define a quantifiable goal for each habit (e.g., "Drink 8 glasses of water," "Meditate for 15 minutes").
    *  Unit of Measurement: Select from various units (e.g., minutes, reps, number of items).
* **Daily Tracking:** Record your progress for each habit.
    *  Simple checkbox to mark habits as completed for the day.
    *  Ability to add notes to your daily entries to provide context.
* **Habit History:**  View your progress over time with interactive charts.
    *  Line graphs showing habit completion rates.
    *  Calendar view to visualize daily activity.
    *  Ability to filter history by habit and date range.


**3. Gamification Features:**

* **Streaks:** Track your consecutive days of completing habits.
    *  Visual representation of your current streak.
    *  Notifications for maintaining and breaking streaks.
* **Achievements:** Unlock badges for reaching milestones.
    *  Initial set of achievements for completing habits for a certain number of days. (e.g., "First Week," "30-Day Streak")
