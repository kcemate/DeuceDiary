**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar – 2024 (Example)**

**I. January – New Year, New Habits**

* **Theme:** Fresh Starts, Goal Setting, Resolution Apps
* **Focus:**  Promote apps that help users achieve their New Year’s resolutions (fitness, productivity, meditation, learning, etc.).
* **Campaigns:**
    * **Launch Promotions:** Limited-time discounts, free trials, or premium features for new users.
    * **ASO - Keyword Focus:** “New Year’s Resolutions”, “Goal Setting”, “Productivity”
    * **Social Media:**  “Start Strong” campaigns – inspiring quotes, tips for achieving goals, highlighting app features.
    * **Email Marketing:** Welcome email sequence for new users with tips on how to use the app to their advantage.
* **Timeline:**  January 1st – January 31st



**II. February – Love & Connection**

* **Theme:** Romance, Relationships, Social Apps
* **Focus:** Apps related to dating, friendship, connection, and self-care.
* **Campaigns:**
    * **Valentine's Day Promotions:** Discounts, themed content, or partner offers.  Consider offering bundles.
    * **ASO - Keyword Focus:** "Dating Apps," "Relationships," "Friendship," "Romantic Games"
    * **Social Media:** Heart-themed visuals, engagement contests, user-generated content.
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relationship influencers.
* **Timeline:** February 1st – February 29th



**III. March – Spring Awakening (and Women's History Month)**

* **Theme:** Renewal, Wellness,  Empowerment
* **Focus:** Wellness, fitness, healthy eating, self-care apps, and celebrate Women's History Month.
* **Campaigns:**
    * **Spring Launch:** Promote new features or content related to spring.
    * **ASO - Keyword Focus:** “Spring Fitness,” “Healthy Eating,” “Wellness,” “Women’s History Month”
    * **Social Media:** Motivational content, healthy recipes, fitness challenges,  highlighting women in history.
    * **Partnerships:** Collaborate with wellness brands for cross-promotions.
* **Timeline:** March 1st – March 31st

**IV. April – Easter & Earth Month**

* **Theme:** Family, Celebration, Sustainability
* **Focus:** Apps that cater to families,  promote mindful consumption, and support environmental causes.
* **Campaigns:**
    * **Easter Promotions:**  If relevant to your app (e.g., family games, educational apps), offer Easter-themed discounts or content.
