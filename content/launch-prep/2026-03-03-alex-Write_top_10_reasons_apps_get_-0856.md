**Top 10 Reasons Apps Get Rejected from the App Store**

3. **App Store Guidelines Violations - Content:**  This includes anything deemed offensive, misleading, violent, sexually suggestive, or harmful. This is broadly interpreted.
4. **App Store Guidelines Violations - Functionality:** Problems with core functionality – crashes, bugs, incorrect operation, inaccurate features – are frequent rejection reasons.
5. **Misleading or Deceptive Practices:**  Apps that promise something they don’t deliver, use deceptive UI/UX, or engage in fraudulent behavior are rejected.
6. **Intellectual Property Issues:**  Copyright infringement (using unlicensed music, images, fonts, etc.) or trademark violations are big problems.
7. **Technical Issues:**  The app doesn't work as advertised, has poor performance (slow loading times, excessive battery drain), or doesn’t interface properly with iOS.
8. **Lack of Proper Information:** Missing required information (screenshots, descriptions, app name, contact details, etc.) or providing inaccurate information.
9. **App Category Issues:** Submitting an app to the wrong category. Apple is very strict about where apps belong.
10. **Apple's Discretion:**  Sometimes, Apple simply doesn't like an app, even if it technically meets the guidelines. They reserve the right to reject apps for reasons not explicitly listed.
