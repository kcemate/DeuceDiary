**Campaign 1: Welcome & Initial Interest (New Subscribers - Focus: Building Rapport & Brand Awareness)**

* **Goal:** Welcome new subscribers, introduce your brand, and start building trust.
* **Timing:** Day 0, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who have signed up through a website form, lead magnet, or other channel.
* **Tone:** Friendly, welcoming, helpful, and authentic.

**Sequence:**

* **Day 0 (Immediate): Welcome Email**
   * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand]! 🎉
   * **Body:**
     “Hi [Subscriber Name],

     Thanks so much for joining the [Your Brand] community! We’re thrilled to have you.

     We're passionate about [briefly state your brand's mission/what you do].  We create [products/services] designed to [benefit for the customer].

     As a thank you, here’s a [link to your lead magnet - free guide, checklist, etc.] to get you started: [Link]

     Stay tuned for more helpful tips and updates!

     Best regards,
     The [Your Brand] Team”

* **Day 3: Share a Valuable Tip**
   * **Subject:**  [Tip Related to Your Niche] -  Quick Tip for You!
   * **Body:** (Short & actionable tip) - Example: "Struggling with [common problem]? Here's a simple trick..."

* **Day 7: Introduce Your Brand Story (Briefly)**
   * **Subject:**  Meet the Team Behind [Your Brand]
   * **Body:** (Share a brief, humanizing story about your brand’s origins or values - 1-2 short paragraphs).  Include a photo of your team if possible.

* **Day 14:  Engagement Question**
   * **Subject:**  What are your biggest challenges with [relevant topic]?
   * **Body:** “We’re always looking for ways to better serve our community.  We’d love to hear about your biggest challenges related to [topic].  Reply to this email and let us know!” (Consider adding a poll for quick feedback).
