**I. Core Referral Program: “Spread the Word, Get Rewarded”**

* **Name:** Spread the Word, Get Rewarded (or something brand-specific!)
* **Goal:** Drive new customer acquisition, increase brand awareness, and build community.
* **Target Audience:** Existing customers who are enthusiastic about your product/service.
* **Duration:** Ongoing (with seasonal tweaks)
* **Mechanics:**
    * **Referrer:**  Existing customers who refer new customers.
    * **Referee:** New customers who sign up and make a purchase (a minimum purchase threshold is crucial – see below).
    * **Reward Structure (Tiered):**
        * **Level 1 - Basic Referral:**
            * **Referrer:**  Receives a [Discount Code - e.g., 10% off their next order] upon successful referral.
            * **Referee:**  Gets a [Welcome Discount - e.g., 10% off their first order].
        * **Level 2 -  Increased Incentive (After 2 Referrals):**
            * **Referrer:** Receives a [Higher Discount Code - e.g., 15% off their next order] *or* a [Small Gift - e.g., $10 Store Credit]
            * **Referee:**  Gets a [Larger Welcome Discount - e.g., 15% off their first order]
        * **Level 3 - VIP (After 5 Referrals):**
            * **Referrer:**  Receives a [Premium Reward - e.g., Free Month of Service, Exclusive Product, or a Larger Gift Card - e.g., $25]
            * **Referee:** Gets a [Significant Welcome Discount - e.g., 20% off their first order] + a small thank you gift (e.g., branded item).

* **Minimum Purchase Threshold:**  Set a minimum purchase amount for the referee to qualify the referral.  This reduces the cost to you and ensures the referee is genuinely engaged. (e.g., $50 or $100)

* **Tracking:** Implement a referral tracking system (using software like ReferralCandy, ReferralHero, or building your own – depending on your budget and tech expertise). This is *essential* for accurate reporting and reward distribution.

* **Communication:**
    * **Referrer Email:** Automated email sent when a new referral is made.  Includes a personalized link for the referrer to share.
    * **Referee Email:** Welcome email for the new customer, outlining the referral program and how to redeem their discount.
    * **Social Media Promotion:**  Regularly promote the referral program on your social media channels.

* **Gamification:**
    * **Leaderboard:**  Create a leaderboard ranking referrers by the number of successful referrals.  Offer prizes for the top referrers (e.g., featured on your website, additional gift cards).
    * **Referral Challenges:**  Set challenges (e.g., "Refer 3 friends this month for a chance to win...").
