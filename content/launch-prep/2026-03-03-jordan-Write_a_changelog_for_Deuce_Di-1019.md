**What's New?**

Deuce Diary v1.0.0 is here! This is the initial release of the diary, allowing you to record your thoughts and experiences as you play *Deus Ex*.  This version includes the core features you need to start documenting your playthrough.

**Key Features:**

* **Diary Entry Creation:**  You can now create new diary entries, allowing you to write about any event, decision, or observation from your *Deus Ex* adventure.
* **Text Editor:**  A simple, distraction-free text editor is included within the diary to easily record your thoughts.
* **Entry Storage:** All your diary entries are saved locally to your device.
* **Basic Formatting:** Support for basic text formatting (bold, italics) to help emphasize important points.
* **Search Functionality:** Quickly find specific entries by keyword.
* **Import/Export (Future Development):** The base is laid for future import/export of diary data. (Currently only supported through saving to local storage).

**Known Issues & Limitations:**

* **Simple Formatting:** Formatting options are basic. More advanced styling is not currently supported.
* **Limited Search:** The search function is basic and relies on keyword matching.
* **No Synchronization:** Diary data is stored locally and is not synchronized across devices.
