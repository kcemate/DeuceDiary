**Campaign 1: Welcome & Brand Introduction (New Subscribers - 5 Days)**

* **Goal:** Introduce your brand, build trust, and encourage engagement.
* **Target Audience:** New subscribers who have signed up through your website, social media, or lead magnet.
* **Tone:** Friendly, welcoming, informative.
* **Sequence:**

    * **Day 1: Welcome Email (Immediate)**
        * **Subject:** Welcome to [Your Brand Name]! 🎉
        * **Content:** Thank you for subscribing! Briefly introduce your brand - what you do, your mission, and a quick, compelling value proposition.  Include a friendly welcome image.  Link to your most popular blog post or a resource that demonstrates your expertise.
        * **Call to Action (CTA):** “Read Our Top Blog Post” or “Explore Our [Category]”
    * **Day 3:  Story Behind the Brand (Short Video or Image + Text)**
        * **Subject:** Meet the Team Behind [Your Brand Name]
        * **Content:**  Share a short video (30-60 seconds) or a carousel of images introducing the people behind your brand.  Tell a brief story about *why* you started the business – focusing on the problem you solve and your passion. 
        * **CTA:** “Learn More About Our Story” (links to your “About Us” page)
    * **Day 5:  Quick Tip/Resource & Poll (Engagement)**
        * **Subject:**  [Relevant Tip Related to Your Niche] + We Want to Know!
        * **Content:**  Provide a super-short, actionable tip related to your product or service.  Include a simple poll question to get subscribers engaged (e.g., "What's your biggest challenge with [topic]?").
        * **CTA:** “Answer the Poll” or “Read More Tips” (links to a relevant blog post)
