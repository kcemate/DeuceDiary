**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be honest and proactive in communication.
* **Empathy:** Understand and acknowledge concerns.
* **Speed:** Respond quickly and decisively.

* **Data-Driven:** Use metrics and feedback to guide decisions.

**III. Escalation & Communication Protocol**

* **Level 1: Immediate Response (Tech Lead, Social Media Manager)** - Handles minor issues, monitors social sentiment, and provides initial updates.
* **Level 2: Rapid Response Team (PR, Editorial, Community Manager, Legal)** – Activated within 1 hour of Level 1 escalation, coordinates messaging, drafts statements, and addresses complex issues.
* **Level 3: Crisis Management Team (CEO, Marketing Director, Legal Counsel, Head of Operations)** – Activated if the issue is widespread, potentially damaging, or impacting business operations.  This team will oversee strategy, allocate resources, and potentially engage external PR support.

**Communication Channels:**

* **Social Media:** Dedicated monitoring across all platforms (Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, Goodreads) - Use a social listening tool (e.g., Hootsuite Insights, Brandwatch).
* **Email:** Dedicated email address for customer complaints & inquiries (e.g., deuce.diary.support@email.com).
* **Website:** Dedicated FAQ section addressing common concerns.
* **Internal Slack Channel:** For rapid team communication and coordination.
