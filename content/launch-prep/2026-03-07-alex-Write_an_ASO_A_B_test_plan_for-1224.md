## ASO A/B Test Plan: Optimizing App Store Elements

This plan outlines a structured approach to A/B testing key App Store elements, focusing on driving installs and engagement.

**I. Goals & Metrics:**

* **Primary Goal:** Increase app installs and engagement (defined as daily active users - DAU).
* **Secondary Goals:** Improve conversion rate from app page views to installs, increase session duration, and boost positive app ratings.
* **Key Metrics:**
    * **Install Rate (IR):** Percentage of visitors who install the app. (Most Important)
    * **Conversion Rate (CVR):** Percentage of visitors who view the app page and subsequently install.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Screenshots:**  Percentage of visitors who click on a specific screenshot.
    * **Click-Through Rate (CTR) on Videos:** Percentage of visitors who watch a specific video.
    * **Average Session Duration:**  Average time users spend in the app after installing.
    * **App Rating & Reviews:** Changes in average rating and the number of reviews.
    * **Keyword Ranking:** Monitoring keyword ranking changes after the test.
