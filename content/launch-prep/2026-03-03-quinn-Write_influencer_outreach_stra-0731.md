## Influencer Outreach Strategy: Bathroom Humor Creators

This strategy focuses on building relationships with influencers who specialize in bathroom humor – think dark comedy, observational humor, cringe, and absurd situations centered around the bathroom experience. It’s about authenticity and finding creators who genuinely align with your brand’s tone.

**I. Understanding Your Brand & Target Audience:**

* **Define Your Brand Voice:** Is your humor leaning into dark, sarcastic, relatable, or absurdist? Clearly define this. This is crucial for attracting the right influencers.
* **Identify Your Ideal Audience:** Who are you trying to reach? (e.g., young adults, men, women, specific interests like gaming or anime). Knowing this will help you identify relevant influencers.
* **Content Pillars:** What types of content will you be creating? (e.g., short-form videos, memes, illustrated comics, written stories). This dictates the kind of content influencers will be most receptive to.


**II. Identifying & Researching Influencers:**

* **Platforms:** Primarily TikTok, Instagram, YouTube Shorts, and potentially Reddit (r/showerthoughts, r/funny).

* **Tools:**

    * **Instagram Search:** Use hashtags like #darkhumor, #cringecomedy, #bathroomhumor, #relatable, #funny.
    * **YouTube Search:** Similar search terms as above.
    * **Manual Research:**  Look for accounts consistently posting bathroom-related humor and with strong engagement.
* **Key Metrics to Look For:**
    * **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers -  High engagement indicates a passionate audience.
    * **Follower Count:** Don't solely focus on high numbers; micro-influencers (1k-10k followers) with high engagement are often more effective.
    * **Content Style:** Ensure their style aligns with your brand's.
    * **Audience Demographics:** Verify their audience matches your target demographic.
