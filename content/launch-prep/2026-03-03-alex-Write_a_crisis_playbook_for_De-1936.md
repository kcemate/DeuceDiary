## Deuce Diary Launch Crisis Playbook

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** October 26, 2023
**Purpose:** To provide a structured approach to managing potential crises during the launch of "Deuce Diary," ensuring swift, consistent, and effective communication.

**I. Core Principles:**

* **Transparency:** Be upfront and honest with the public.
* **Empathy:** Acknowledge and validate concerns and frustrations.
* **Speed:** Respond quickly and decisively.
* **Consistency:** Maintain a unified message across all channels.
* **Proactive Monitoring:** Anticipate potential issues and be prepared.


**II. Crisis Categories & Response Protocols:**

**Category 1: Technical Issues (Website Downtime, Payment Problems, etc.)**

* **Severity:** High - Immediate impact on user experience and sales.
* **Initial Response (Within 5 Minutes):**
    * **Team Lead (Tech):** Acknowledge the issue on Twitter/X and Discord (if applicable). “We're aware of a technical issue affecting [specific problem] and our team is working diligently to resolve it. We’ll provide updates as soon as possible.”
    * **Community Manager:** Monitor social media for mentions and escalating concerns.
* **Immediate Actions (Within 15-30 Minutes):**
    * **Tech Team:** Identify root cause, implement fix, deploy update.
    * **Update Communication:** Share estimated resolution time.  "We’ve identified the issue as [brief explanation] and are aiming to have it resolved within [timeframe].  We appreciate your patience.”
* **Ongoing Updates:** Regular updates (every 30-60 minutes) – even if there’s no new information, reiterate commitment to resolution.
* **Post-Resolution:** Announce resolution and thank users for their patience.



**Category 2: Content-Related Issues (Controversy, Misinterpretation, Complaints)**

* **Severity:** Medium - Potential for negative PR and user backlash.
* **Initial Response (Within 15 Minutes):**
    * **Community Manager:** Monitor social media for mentions and escalating concerns. Flag serious issues for leadership.
* **Assessment (Within 30 Minutes):**
    * **Lead Creative:** Determine the nature of the controversy - misunderstanding, genuinely offensive content, etc.
    * **Legal Counsel (If Necessary):** Consult on potential legal ramifications.
* **Response Options (Based on Assessment):**
    * **Misunderstanding:** Clear and factual explanation, addressing concerns directly.  "We understand that [specific element] could be interpreted as [incorrect interpretation].  The intention was [intended meaning]."
    * **Genuine Offense:**  A sincere apology, acknowledging harm caused, and explaining steps taken to prevent future occurrences. “We deeply regret that [specific content] caused offense. We take these concerns very seriously and are reviewing our processes to ensure this doesn’t happen again.”
    * **Detailed Response:**  Longer, more detailed explanation if required.
* **Transparency:**  Be open about the process of addressing the issue.


**Category 3: Order Fulfillment Issues (Shipping Delays, Damaged Goods, Missing Orders)**

* **Severity:** Medium - User frustration and potential for negative reviews.
* **Initial Response (Within 15 Minutes):**
    * **Customer Support Lead:** Acknowledge the issue on social media and provide contact information. “We're so sorry to hear about the shipping delay/damaged goods.  Please contact our customer support team at [email address] or [phone number] so we can resolve this for you.”
* **Immediate Actions:**
    * **Customer Support:** Investigate the issue, offer immediate solutions (refund, replacement, expedited shipping).
