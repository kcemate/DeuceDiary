Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. Here’s a breakdown, covering different tiers, incentives, and operational considerations, designed to be adaptable to various businesses.

**1. Program Name & Branding:**

* **Name:** “Spread the Word,” “Refer & Reward,” “[Your Brand] Community,” or something catchy relevant to your brand.
* **Visuals:**  A clear logo or graphic associated with the program.

**2. Core Mechanics:**

* **How it Works:** Customers refer new users to your product/service. Referred users sign up/purchase using a unique referral link or code.
* **Tracking:** Crucial!  Use referral software (e.g., ReferralCandy, Friendbuy, ReferralHero, or build your own with a platform like Zapier) to accurately track referrals, conversions, and rewards.  Manual tracking is prone to errors and can quickly become unmanageable.
* **Terms & Conditions:** Have clear, concise terms outlining the program's rules, eligibility, and any limitations.

**3. Referral Tiers & Incentives (This is the heart of the program!)**

We’ll use a tiered system to incentivize both the referrer and the referee.

**Tier 1: Basic Referral (For Everyone)**

* **Referrer Incentive:**  [Small Reward] – This is the entry-level reward.
    * **Examples:**
        * **Discount:** 10% off their next purchase.
        * **Freebie:** A small digital product (eBook, guide, template), free shipping, or a small accessory.
        * **Points:** 50 points towards future rewards.
* **Referee Incentive:** [Small Welcome Incentive] – This encourages the new user to try your product.
    * **Examples:**
        * **Discount:** 5% off their first purchase.
        * **Free Trial Extension:**  Extend their free trial by 7 days.
        * **Welcome Gift:**  A small, relevant welcome item.



**Tier 2:  Silver Referral (For Top Referrers)**

* **Requirements:** Referrer must have successfully referred at least 3 new users.
* **Referrer Incentive:** [Mid-Level Reward] –  Significantly more valuable.
    * **Examples:**
        * **Discount:** 20% off their next purchase.
        * **Gift Card:** $25 - $50 gift card to your store or a related retailer.
        * **Exclusive Access:**  Early access to new products, features, or content.
* **Referee Incentive:**  [Increased Welcome Incentive] –  More attractive to the new user.
    * **Examples:**
        * **Discount:** 15% off their first purchase.
        * **Bonus Points:** 100 points.



**Tier 3: Gold Referral (For Top Performers - Less Common)**

* **Requirements:** Referrer must have successfully referred at least 10 new users.
* **Referrer Incentive:** [High-Value Reward] –  Reserved for your most engaged customers.
    * **Examples:**
        * **Significant Discount:** 30% off their next purchase.
        * **Premium Product/Service:**  Upgrade to a premium version of your product.
        * **Exclusive Experience:**  A one-on-one consultation, personalized support, or invitation to an exclusive event.
* **Referee Incentive:** [Best-in-Class Welcome Incentive] –  Highest incentive.
    * **Example:**
        * **Large Discount:** 25% off their first purchase.
        * **Bundled Offer:**  A special bundle of your products/services at a discounted rate.
