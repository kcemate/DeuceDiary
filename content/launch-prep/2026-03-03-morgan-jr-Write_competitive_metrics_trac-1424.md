## Competitive Metrics Tracking Plan

This plan outlines how we'll track and analyze our competition, informing our strategy and helping us stay ahead. It’s designed to be adaptable and data-driven.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Strategic Alignment:** Understand competitive strengths and weaknesses to inform our overall business strategy (product development, marketing, sales).
* **Identify Opportunities:** Discover unmet customer needs or underserved market segments our competitors are missing.
* **Benchmark Performance:**  Establish a baseline for our performance and identify areas for improvement.
* **React to Threats:** Quickly detect competitive moves and proactively adjust our strategy.
* **Innovation Inspiration:** Learn from competitor innovations and identify potential new features or services.

**II. Key Metrics Categories & Specific Metrics:**

We’ll track data across these categories, consistently and regularly.

**A. Product & Feature:**

* **New Feature Releases:** Number and timing of new features launched by competitors. (Tracking Tool:  Google Alerts, ProductHunt, Competitor Websites, Industry Blogs)
* **Feature Adoption Rate:**  (If possible – relying on public data or estimates) - How quickly competitors adopt new features.
* **Product Roadmap (Publicly Available):** Monitor competitor roadmaps for future direction. (Tracking Tool:  Competitor Websites, Press Releases, Industry Analysts)
* **Technical Debt (Estimated):**  Infer potential areas of struggle based on product direction & timelines. (Tracking Tool:  Analyst reports, industry forums, estimated development time)


**B. Pricing & Promotions:**

* **Price Changes:** Track any changes to competitor pricing, discounts, and promotions. (Tracking Tool: Competitor Websites, Email Marketing, Social Media, Price Comparison Sites)
* **Promotional Offers:** Monitor frequency, type, and duration of promotions (e.g., discounts, bundles, free trials). (Tracking Tool: Competitor Websites, Social Media, Email Marketing, PR)
* **Subscription Model Variations:** Note any changes to competitor’s subscription tiers or offerings. (Tracking Tool: Competitor Websites,  Industry Publications)

**C. Marketing & Sales:**

* **Website Traffic:** Monitor competitor website traffic (using tools like SimilarWeb, Ahrefs, SEMrush).
* **Keyword Rankings:** Track competitor rankings for relevant keywords (using SEO tools).
* **Social Media Engagement:** Monitor competitor engagement (likes, shares, comments) on relevant platforms. (Tracking Tool: Social Media Monitoring Tools - Hootsuite, Sprout Social, Mention)
* **Advertising Spend (Estimated):**  Infer competitor ad spend based on search volume and industry reports. (Tracking Tool: SEMrush, iSpionage, Keyword Planner)
* **Content Marketing Activity:** Track their blog posts, ebooks, webinars, and other content. (Tracking Tool: Google Alerts, BuzzSumo, Competitor Website)
* **Sales & Lead Generation:** (Difficult to track directly, rely on public data and analyst reports) – Track competitor’s marketing qualified leads (MQLs) and sales qualified leads (SQLs).

**D. Customer Metrics:**

* **Customer Reviews & Ratings:** Monitor competitor reviews on platforms like G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, and app stores. (Tracking Tool: Review Monitoring Tools - Reputology, Birdeye)
* **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** (If publicly available or estimated) - Gauge competitor customer loyalty. (Tracking Tool: Industry Analyst Reports)
* **Customer Support Volume & Sentiment:** (Difficult to track directly, rely on analyst reports) -  Monitor competitor’s support channels and identify common customer issues.
* **Churn Rate:** (If publicly available) -  Indicates customer satisfaction and retention. (Tracking Tool:  Industry Analyst Reports)


**III. Data Collection & Tools:**

* **Google Alerts:**  For tracking mentions
