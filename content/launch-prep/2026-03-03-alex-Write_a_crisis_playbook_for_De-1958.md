* **Speed:** Rapid response is crucial. The faster you acknowledge and address a problem, the better.
* **Empathy:** Show genuine concern for affected users and address their frustrations.
* **Documentation:** Meticulously record all communication, actions taken, and updates.
* **Prioritization:** Focus on the most critical issues first.

**III. Potential Crisis Scenarios & Response Protocols:**

**A. Technical Issues (Buggy Launch, Server Overload)**

* **Severity:** High - Immediately impacts user experience.
* **Initial Response (Within 15 mins):**
    * TL confirms issue, provides estimated time to resolution.
    * SM posts a general statement acknowledging the problem & estimated timeline. (Example: “We're aware that some users are experiencing issues accessing Deuce Diary. Our team is working to resolve this as quickly as possible. We'll provide updates shortly.”)
* **Ongoing Response (Every 30 mins):**
    * TL provides updates on resolution progress.
    * SM maintains consistent updates & addresses user concerns with empathy.  Consider proactively offering a temporary workaround if possible.
    * CS logs all user complaints and passes them to TL & PR.

**B. Negative Social Media Sentiment (Backlash, Criticism)**

* **Severity:** Medium - Can damage brand perception.
* **Initial Response (Within 30 mins):**
    * SM identifies and monitors trending negative conversations.
    * PR confirms awareness & starts gathering information about the issues driving the negativity.
* **Ongoing Response (Every 60 mins):**
    * SM actively engages with frustrated users – acknowledge concerns, offer support, direct to CS.
    * PR drafts a more specific response addressing the underlying criticism (if appropriate) – avoid defensiveness.
    *  Consider a proactive campaign to highlight positive aspects of the diary.


**C. Content Concerns (Offensive Material, Controversy)**

* **Severity:** High - Potential for significant public backlash & legal issues.
* **Initial Response (Within 15 mins):**
    * CL convenes the team.
    * PR & Legal immediately assess the situation and its potential legal ramifications.
    * SM monitors and flags all mentions of the problematic content.
* **Ongoing Response (Every 30 mins):**
    * PR issues a formal statement condemning the content & outlining immediate steps being taken to remove it.
    *  Legal advises on next steps
