**Severity Levels & Response Actions:**

This plan utilizes a tiered severity level system (P0-P3) to prioritize incident response efforts.

**P0 - Critical – Immediate Impact – Requires Immediate Action (Response Time: < 1 Hour)**

* **Definition:** Significant disruption to critical business operations, substantial data breach, imminent threat to security, or widespread system outage.
* **Examples:** Ransomware attack, large-scale data leak, denial-of-service attack impacting key services, successful intrusion into critical systems.
* **Response Actions:**
    1. **Activate IRT:**  Immediately convene the IRT.
    2. **Isolate Affected Systems:**  Disconnect systems from the network to prevent further spread.
    3. **Contain the Breach:** Implement immediate containment measures (e.g., firewall rules, account lockout).
    4. **Notify Key Stakeholders:**  Senior Management, Legal Counsel, PR.
    5. **Preserve Evidence:**  Begin documenting all actions taken and evidence collected.
    6. **Engage External Resources (if needed):** Contact law enforcement, cybersecurity forensics firm, and/or legal counsel.


**P1 – High – Significant Impact – Requires Prompt Action (Response Time: 1-4 Hours)**

* **Definition:**  Significant disruption to business operations, potential data breach, moderate compromise of systems, or confirmed malware infection.
* **Examples:**  Compromised user account with access to sensitive data, targeted phishing campaign, suspicious activity detected on a key server.
* **Response Actions:**
    1. **Activate IRT:** Notify the IRT.
    2. **Assess Scope:** Determine the extent of the incident – affected systems, data impacted, potential users.
    3. **Contain the Breach:** Implement containment measures (e.g., patching vulnerabilities, segmenting networks).
    4. **Eradicate Malware/Threat:** Deploy antivirus, scan for malicious software, remove infected files.
    5. **Restore Systems:**  Restore from backups (if applicable and verified).
    6. **Review Logs:** Analyze logs to identify root cause and potential indicators of compromise.
    7. **Notify Relevant Stakeholders:** HR, Data Protection Officer (DPO), affected users.
