## Beta Testing Strategy for Deuce Diary - TestFlight Rollout Plan

This document outlines a phased beta testing strategy for Deuce Diary, leveraging TestFlight to gather valuable feedback and ensure a smooth launch.

**I. Goals of Beta Testing:**

* **Identify Bugs & Technical Issues:**  Prioritize fixing critical bugs impacting core functionality and user experience.
* **Gather User Feedback on Content & Flow:** Understand how users interact with the diary entries, prompts, and overall flow of the app.
* **Assess Usability & Navigation:** Confirm the app is intuitive and easy to navigate for the target audience.
* **Test Performance & Stability:**  Identify performance bottlenecks and areas for optimization before a wider release.
* **Validate Core Features:** Confirm the primary features (e.g., entry creation, prompt selection, mood tracking) are working as intended.


**II. TestFlight Rollout Plan - Phases & Invites:**

We'll employ a phased rollout to minimize risk and maximize the value of feedback.

**Phase 1: “Seed” Group (7-10 Users) - Week 1-2**
* **Who to Invite:**
    * **Founders/Developers:**  Essential for immediate technical feedback and bug reporting.
    * **Early Adopters:** 3-5 users identified through early interest (e.g., Discord community, email signup list) - people genuinely interested in journaling and digital diaries.  Look for individuals who are articulate and willing to provide detailed feedback.
    * **Tech-Savvy Users:**  1-2 individuals comfortable with reporting bugs, screenshots, and providing technical information.
* **Feedback to Collect:**
    * **Initial Impressions:** Overall satisfaction, first impressions of the UI/UX.
    * **Core Functionality Testing:** Thoroughly test the entry creation, prompt selection, and mood tracking features.
    * **Bug Reporting:**  Prioritize reporting crashes, freezes, and UI glitches.
    * **Navigation Testing:** Confirm ease of movement and finding features.
* **Timeline:** 1-2 weeks.
* **Metrics:** Crash reports, app usage time, feature usage frequency, bug report count.


**Phase 2: “Expanding Circle” (20-30 Users) - Week 3-4**
* **Who to Invite:**
    * **Users from Phase 1 (Invite based on helpfulness):**  Recognize and reward valuable contributors.
    * **Target Audience Members:**  Recruit 10-20 users from our identified target audience based on demographics (age, interests) and journaling habits.  Consider running targeted ads on social media (e.g., Instagram, Facebook) offering early access to the app.
* **Feedback to Collect:**
    * **Expand on Phase 1 feedback:**  Dig deeper into user workflows and preferred features.
    * **Content Feedback:**  Initial thoughts on the prompt library, suggested improvements, and potential new prompt categories.
    * **UI/UX Refinements:**  Specific requests for UI adjustments or navigation improvements.
    * **Long-Term Usage:** Encourage daily/weekly usage to gather data on user habits.
* **Timeline:** 1-2 weeks.
* **Metrics:** Increased app usage time, feature engagement, prompt usage, UI feedback volume, effectiveness of prompts.


**Phase 3: “Community Feedback” (50-75 Users) - Week 5-6**
* **Who to Invite:**
    * **Users from Phase 2 (Invite based on helpfulness and feedback quality):**  Continue to engage and reward active testers.
    * **Larger Scale Recruitment:**  Expand recruitment via app store listings, social media communities, and potentially paid advertising.
* **Feedback to Collect:**
    * **Comprehensive Feedback:**  All
