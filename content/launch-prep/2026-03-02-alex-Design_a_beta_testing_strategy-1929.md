## Beta Testing Strategy: Deuce Diary - TestFlight Rollout

This strategy outlines a phased rollout of Deuce Diary to a select group of beta testers using TestFlight, focusing on gathering critical feedback and ensuring a smooth transition to a full launch.

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

* **Identify & Resolve Critical Bugs:** Prioritize finding and fixing major functionality issues, stability problems, and UX issues impacting core gameplay.
* **Gather User Feedback on Core Mechanics:** Understand player engagement with the diary system, mood tracking, and writing prompts.
* **Assess Performance & Stability:** Monitor device performance, battery drain, and app stability under various usage scenarios.
* **Refine UX & Content:**  Gather feedback on ease of use, clarity of instructions, and the overall experience.
* **Build Community & Gather Early Advocates:**  Identify passionate users who can become advocates for the game.


**II. TestFlight Rollout Plan - Phases & Timelines:**

**Phase 1: Internal Testing (1 Week)** - *Complete before external invites*
* **Participants:** Development Team (Lead Developer, QA Tester, Designers)
* **Focus:** Initial stability testing, basic functionality, critical bug hunting, first impressions.
* **Feedback Collection:**  Daily bug reports, prioritized issue tracking in Jira/Asana, short user interviews.
* **Duration:** 7 Days


**Phase 2: Small Beta (Group 1) - 2 Weeks** - *Limited to 20-30 users*
* **Participants:**
    * **Friends & Family:** Trusted individuals comfortable providing honest feedback. (5-7)
    * **Gaming Communities (Discord/Reddit):** Recruit active members who enjoy journaling, mood tracking, or similar games. (15-23) – Segment by genre interests.
* **Feedback Collection:**
    * **In-App Feedback Form:**  Simple form for quick feedback.
    * **TestFlight Feedback Section:** Monitoring and responding to comments.
    * **Dedicated Discord Channel:**  For direct communication, support requests, and feature suggestions.
    * **Weekly 1:1 Calls:** With 2-3 key users for deeper insights.
* **Content Focus:**  Diary creation, mood logging, prompt engagement, core features.
* **Duration:** 2 Weeks


**Phase 3: Expanded Beta (Group 2) - 3 Weeks** - *Increase to 50-75 users*
* **Participants:**  Users from Group 1 who consistently provided valuable feedback, plus new users recruited through wider channels (e.g., targeted social media ads – small budget).
* **Feedback Collection:**  Maintain all channels from Phase 2, plus:
    * **Short Surveys (Google Forms):**  More structured feedback on specific aspects.
    * **Usability Testing Sessions (Remote):**  Observe users completing specific tasks within the app.
* **Content Focus:**  Expanded features (if applicable - e.g., reminders, sync across devices), deeper customization.
* **Duration:** 3 Weeks


**Phase 4:  Final Stabilization (1 Week Pre-Launch)** - *Reduce to 15-25 users*
* **Participants:**  Most active & helpful users from Phase 3, focusing on users who represent the target audience.
* **Feedback Collection:** Focused on polishing, performance optimization, and any remaining bugs.  High priority on bug verification.
* **Duration:** 7 Days



**III.  Feedback Categories & Metrics:**

* **Bug Reports:** Severity (Critical, Major, Minor), Steps to Reproduce, Device/OS Info
* **Usability:** Task completion rate, Time to complete tasks, User frustration levels.
* **Engagement:** Diary entry frequency, Prompt usage, Mood logging consistency.
