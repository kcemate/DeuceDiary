**Template 1: Short & Sweet - Initial Email (Good for Busy Influencers/Brands)**

I've been a big fan of your work on [mention something specific you admire - e.g., "your recipe videos," "your commitment to sustainable fitness," "your content around gut health"].

[Your Name]
[Your Website]
[Link to your Instagram/Social Profile]


**Notes:**  This is a good starting point for initial outreach to those you admire. It's concise and respectful of their time. Focus on highlighting a shared interest.

**Template 2: More Detailed - Offering Specific Ideas (Best for Established Influencers/Brands)**

**Subject: Partnership Proposal: [Your Brand Name] + [Their Brand Name] – [Focus Area - e.g., "Boosting Energy," “Post-Workout Recovery”]**

Dear [Name],

I’m reaching out from [Your Brand Name], a [detailed description – e.g., “a premium organic tea brand designed to support relaxation and sleep,” “a boutique fitness studio specializing in functional movement”]. We're passionate about [brand values - e.g., “supporting holistic wellness,” “promoting sustainable living”].

We’ve been consistently impressed with your work on [mention specific content, campaigns, or achievements – be detailed here!].  We particularly appreciated [mention *why* you appreciated it - e.g., “your honest approach to navigating common wellness challenges," "your engaging content that makes complex topics accessible"].

**We believe a collaboration could be mutually beneficial, and we're envisioning the following:**

*   **[Idea 1 – e.g., Sponsored Post/Review]:**  You could feature our [product] in a post showcasing [benefit relevant to their audience].
*   **[Idea 2 – e.g., Giveaway]:** We'd love to host a giveaway of our [product] to your audience.
*   **[Idea 3 – e.g., Exclusive Discount Code]:**  You could share a special discount code with your followers.

We’re open to discussing other ideas and tailoring a collaboration to best suit your brand and audience.  We're happy to provide [mention resources - e.g., "product samples," "a detailed brief," "creative assets"].

You can learn more about us here: [Your Website]

Would you be available for a brief call next week to explore this further?

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Brand Name]
[Contact Email]


**Notes:** Use this template when you have a specific idea in mind and you want to demonstrate you've done your research.  It shows you're serious and provides a clear starting point for discussion.  Include visuals (a mock-up of a potential post is a bonus!).
