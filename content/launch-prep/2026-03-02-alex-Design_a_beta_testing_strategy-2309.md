**1. Overall Goals & Philosophy**

* **Goal:** Identify critical bugs, usability issues, and gather user preferences *before* the public launch. Establish a strong initial user base that is invested in the game’s success.
* **Philosophy:**  Move quickly, prioritize actionable feedback, and embrace a “fail fast” mentality. We'll be focused on core mechanics, onboarding, and general enjoyment.

**2. TestFlight Rollout Phases & Invitations**

We'll use a tiered approach, expanding the audience as we gain confidence.

**Phase 1: Inner Circle (Days 1-3)** – **5-10 Users**
* **Who:**  A small group of extremely trusted individuals - ideally, people who have been heavily involved in development, provided early feedback on prototypes, and are deeply familiar with the game's vision. This could include developers, close friends, and family.
* **Invite Criteria:**  Strong understanding of the game’s core concepts, comfortable giving detailed feedback (written & video), and dedicated to consistent testing.
* **Feedback Focus:**
    * **Core Mechanics:** Play through the entire flow (diary creation, entry writing, image uploading, saving). Test all features.
    * **Initial Bug Hunting:**  Anything seems broken, broken!
    * **First Impressions:** "What’s your immediate reaction? What's fun? What’s confusing?"
    * **Usability:** Ease of navigation, clarity of UI elements.
* **Tools:** TestFlight, Discord/Slack for rapid communication, Google Docs for bug reporting.


**Phase 2: Early Access (Days 4-7)** – **25-50 Users**
* **Who:** Users recruited through initial channels (social media, newsletter signup) who expressed interest. Ideally, diverse backgrounds in terms of gaming experience.
* **Invite Criteria:** Willingness to provide regular feedback, active use of the game for a minimum of 30 minutes per day.
* **Feedback Focus:** (Expanding on Phase 1)
    * **Retention:** What keeps them coming back?
    * **UI/UX Refinement:**  Iterate on changes identified in Phase 1.
    * **Content Suggestions:**  “If you could add one feature or element, what would it be?” (To gauge interest in future development)
    * **Performance:**  Report any lag, crashes, or battery drain.
* **Tools:** TestFlight, SurveyMonkey (for structured feedback),  potentially a simple in-game feedback button.



**Phase 3: Wider Release (Days 8-14)** – **100-200 Users**
* **Who:**  Expanded recruitment through paid ads (small budget) and broader social media promotion.
* **Invite Criteria:** Continued commitment to feedback.
* **Feedback Focus:** (Full-scale testing)
    * **All previous feedback categories.**
    * **Long-term Usability:** How does the game feel after extended use?
    * **Social Sharing:**  Ease of sharing entries and diary content.
    * **Competitive Analysis:** How does Deuce Diary compare to similar games?
