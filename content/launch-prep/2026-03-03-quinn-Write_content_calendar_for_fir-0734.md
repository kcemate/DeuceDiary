Okay, here's a content calendar for the first month post-launch of a product or service. This calendar is designed to build excitement, drive initial adoption, and gather feedback. It’s a starting point – adjust it based on your specific product, target audience, and launch strategy.

**Assumptions:**

* **You've already had a launch event or announcement.** This calendar builds on that.
* **You have some initial social media accounts set up.**
* **You're aiming for a mix of content types:**  Video, images, text posts, stories.
* **You're tracking metrics:**  Website traffic, social media engagement, sign-ups, downloads, etc.

**Key Themes for Month 1:**

* **Celebration & Thanks:** Thanking early adopters and celebrating the launch.
* **Explainer Content:**  Clearly demonstrating what your product does and its benefits.
* **Value Demonstration:** Showing *how* users can get value from your product.
* **Community Building:**  Encouraging interaction and feedback.
