**App Store Seasonal Marketing Calendar - 2024 (Example)**

**I. January – New Year, New Goals (Post-Holiday Slowdown)**

* **Theme:**  New Year’s Resolutions, Self-Improvement, Getting Organized.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **App Store Optimization (ASO):** Review keywords, update description based on trending resolutions.
    * **Paid Campaigns:**  Target users searching for terms like “productivity,” “fitness,” “budgeting.” (Lower spend than holiday – focus on awareness)
    * **Social Media:** Content focused on goal setting, tips related to your app's purpose.  Run contests related to resolutions (e.g., “Share your first week using [App Name]!”)
    * **Email Marketing:** Welcome email sequence for new users, offering tips and encouragement.


**II. February – Love & Connection (Valentine's Day & Black History Month)**

* **Theme:** Relationships, Self-Care, Connection, Celebrating Diversity
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **ASO:**  Keyword optimization related to relationships, dating, self-care.
    * **Paid Campaigns:** Target users searching for dating, relationship advice, and gift ideas (Valentine’s Day).
    * **Social Media:** Share content about relationships, self-love, and inclusivity. Run Valentine’s themed promotions (if relevant to your app). Consider supporting Black History Month initiatives.
    * **Influencer Marketing:** Partner with relevant influencers (e.g., relationship experts, wellness bloggers).

* **Theme:**  Productivity, Motivation, Fresh Starts, Empowerment

    * **Social Media:**  Motivational content, tips for starting new habits, promote any seasonal features of your app. Highlight Women’s History Month.
    * **App Updates:** Release updates with Spring-themed features or improvements (if possible).
