Okay, let's design a referral program with incentives. This design focuses on motivating both the referrer and the referee, and it offers scalability for different business types.

**Program Name:** "Share the [Your Brand Name] Love" (Replace "[Your Brand Name]" with your actual brand name)

**I. Goals & Objectives:**

*   **Increase Customer Acquisition:** Drive new users/customers through trusted recommendations.
*   **Boost Brand Awareness:**  Expand reach through personal networks.
*   **Improve Customer Loyalty:** Reward existing customers for their advocacy.

**II. Structure & Mechanics:**

*   **Referrer Rewards:**
    *   **Tiered System (Recommended):**
        *   **Level 1 - First Referral:**  [Discount Percentage]% off their next purchase or [Value Amount] Credit (e.g., $10)
        *   **Level 2 - 2nd & 3rd Referral:** [Higher Discount Percentage]% off their next purchase or [Higher Value Amount] Credit.
        *   **Level 3+ - Every Subsequent Referral:**  [Smaller Discount Percentage]% off or [Small Value Amount] Credit. (This encourages continued referrals)
    *   **Alternative - Simple Reward:**  A fixed reward (e.g., $10 credit) for every successful referral.  This is easier to administer but potentially less motivating.
*   **Referee Rewards:**
    *   **Welcome Offer:**  [Discount Percentage]% off their first purchase or a free [Product/Service] upon signing up/making a purchase. (This is crucial to incentivize the new customer).
    *   **Limited-Time Offer:** A special bonus applicable to the referee’s first order – e.g., "Get 10% off your first order when you use referral code [Code]".
*   **Tracking & Referral Code:**
    *   **Unique Referral Code:**  Every referrer receives a unique code (alphanumeric string) to share. This is the core of tracking.  Consider using a dedicated referral platform if you have high volume.
    *   **Tracking Link:**  Provide a direct link for referrers to share, which automatically attributes the referral. (e.g., `https://yourwebsite.com/refer/share?code=XYZ123`)
    *   **Automated Email Notifications:**  Trigger emails for both the referrer and the referee upon signup/purchase.
