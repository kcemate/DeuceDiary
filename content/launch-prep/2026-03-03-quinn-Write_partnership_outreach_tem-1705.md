Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health/wellness brands, tailored for different levels of engagement and target audiences. I’ve included variations for email and LinkedIn, and categorized them based on the type of partnership you're seeking.

**General Notes Before You Start:**

* **Personalize, Personalize, Personalize:** These are templates – don't just copy and paste.  Research the partner's brand, audience, and content thoroughly. Mention specific things you admire about their work.
* **Clear Value Proposition:**  Quickly explain what's in it for *them*.
* **Call to Action:** Make it easy for them to respond.
* **Follow Up:** Don't be afraid to send a brief follow-up email if you don’t hear back within a week.


**Template 1: Micro-Influencer Collaboration (Instagram/TikTok Focused - Small Following, High Engagement)**

* **Subject (Email):** Partner Opportunity: [Your Brand] & [Partner Brand] – Wellness Collaboration
* **Body:**

Hi [Influencer Name],

I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand], a [brief description of your brand – e.g., small-batch organic tea company, minimalist wellness app].  I’ve been a big fan of your content on [mention specific content you like – e.g., your morning routines, healthy snack ideas, mindfulness tips] – particularly your approach to [mention something specific about their style/message].

We’re looking for authentic voices like yours to help us spread the word about [your product/service] and build a supportive community. We’d love to send you [mention a specific offer – e.g., a free product, a discount code for their followers] in exchange for a genuine review or a few posts/stories showcasing how you incorporate [your product/service] into your wellness routine.

Would you be open to a quick chat to discuss potential collaboration ideas?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Link to your website/Instagram]



* **LinkedIn Connection Request Message (If applicable):**  “Hi [Influencer Name], I really enjoy your work in the wellness space and would love to connect!  I'm [Your Name] from [Your Brand], and we’re focused on [brief brand description].  ”


**Template 2:  Content Collaboration (Blog Post, Guest Post, Social Media Takeover)**

* **Subject (Email):** Collaboration Opportunity: [Your Brand] & [Partner Brand] – Creating Valuable Wellness Content
* **Body:**

Hi [Contact Person - usually Marketing Manager or Content Editor],

I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand], a [brief description of your brand – e.g., meditation app, healthy recipe platform].  We’re impressed with the quality and engagement you generate on [Partner Brand’s platform - e.g., your blog, newsletter, Instagram] with your content focused on [mention their topic area].

We believe a collaboration between our brands would be mutually beneficial. We'd love to create [Suggest a specific collaboration - e.g., a guest blog post on "5 Ways to Incorporate Mindfulness into Your Daily Life," a social media takeover focused on healthy eating, a joint webinar].

We’re confident that this would resonate with your audience and enhance your content while introducing [Your Brand] to a new audience.  We're open to discussing a revenue-share or an exchange of promotion.

Would you be available for a brief call to explore this further?

Thanks so much,
[Your Name]
[Link to your website]
