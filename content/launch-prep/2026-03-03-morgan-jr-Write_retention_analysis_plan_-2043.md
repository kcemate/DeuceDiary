## Retention Analysis Plan

**Goal:** To understand user behavior and identify key areas for improvement to increase user retention across three key time periods: Day 1, Day 7, and Day 30.

**Target Audience:** (Specify your product/service here - e.g., Mobile App, SaaS Platform, Online Game)

**Data Sources:**
* **Product Analytics:** (e.g., Amplitude, Mixpanel, Google Analytics) - Primary source for tracking user behavior.
* **CRM:** (e.g., Salesforce, HubSpot) -  To capture acquisition channel, user demographics, and potentially onboarding interactions.
* **Support Tickets:**  Insights into user frustrations and pain points.
* **User Feedback (Surveys, In-App Feedback):**  Qualitative data to understand reasons for churn.


**Metrics:**

* **New Users:** Number of users who completed the onboarding process.
* **Active Users:**  Users performing key actions within the product/service. (Define “Active” – e.g., Logged in, Completed a key task, Made a purchase)
* **Conversion Rates:** Percentage of users performing desired actions.
* **Churn Rate:** Percentage of users who stopped using the product/service.
* **Daily Active Users (DAU)** - Number of unique users active on a given day.
* **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** - Number of unique users active within a month.
* **Session Duration:**  Average time spent in the product/service per session.
* **Feature Usage:**  Tracking which features are being used most and least.


**Analysis & Benchmarks:**

This plan utilizes D1, D7, and D30 benchmarks to identify critical points in the user journey where retention is typically lost.

**Phase 1: Day 1 Retention (D1)**

* **Goal:** Understand initial user experience and identify roadblocks to immediate engagement.
* **Benchmark:**  **70-80%** - Considered a good initial retention rate.  Anything significantly lower warrants immediate investigation.
* **Analysis Focus:**
    * **Onboarding Completion Rate:**  Are users successfully completing the onboarding process? (Track steps, identify drop-off points)
    * **First-Time User Actions:** What actions did new users take immediately after onboarding? Are they aligned with core value propositions?
    * **Feature Discovery:** Are users discovering the key features they need to get started?
    * **Technical Issues:** Identify any bugs or technical problems reported during the initial onboarding experience.
* **Possible Issues & Mitigation:**
    * **Overly Complex Onboarding:** Simplify onboarding flow, provide more guided tutorials.
    * **Lack of Clear Value Proposition:** Ensure the product's value is immediately apparent.
    * **Technical Glitches:** Prioritize bug fixes.


**Phase 2: Day 7 Retention (D7)**

* **Goal:** Identify points of friction and understanding why users aren't returning after the initial excitement.
* **Benchmark:** **40-50%** –  A typical retention rate for many products. Higher is better.
* **Analysis Focus:**
    * **Feature Adoption:** Are users consistently using key features? Are there features that aren’t being utilized?
    * **Session Frequency & Duration:** Are users logging in frequently and for extended periods?
    * **User Feedback (Surveys/In-App):** Analyze feedback for common complaints, usability issues, or unmet needs.
    * **Cohort Analysis:** Segment users based on acquisition channel and demographics to identify differences in retention.
* **Possible Issues & Mitigation:**
    * **Lack of Ongoing Value:** Introduce new features, content, or rewards to keep users engaged.
    * **Poor UX:** Address usability issues identified through feedback.
