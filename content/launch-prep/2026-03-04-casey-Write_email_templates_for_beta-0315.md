```email
*   Using [App/Product Name] and providing detailed feedback through [Method of feedback - e.g., a dedicated forum, Google Form, Slack channel].
*   Testing specific scenarios we’ll provide (we’ll send these out regularly).
*   Reporting any bugs or issues you encounter.

**To get started:** [Link to access the beta, instructions, or relevant platform].

We’ll be checking in with you regularly and appreciate your honest opinions.  Your input is crucial to making [App/Product Name] the best it can be.

Thanks in advance for your support!

Sincerely,

The [Your Company Name] Team
[Website Link]
```
