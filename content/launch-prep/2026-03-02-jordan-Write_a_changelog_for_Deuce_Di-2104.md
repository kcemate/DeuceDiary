**Deuce Diary v1.0.0 - Launch Changelog**

**Release Date:** October 26, 2023

**Summary:** Deuce Diary v1.0.0 marks the official launch of our habit tracking app, offering a robust foundation for building positive habits and achieving your goals. This release includes core social features, detailed tracking capabilities, gamification elements, a refined user interface, and a stable infrastructure.

**I. Social Features**

* **Community Groups:** Introduced the ability to join and participate in public Community Groups centered around specific habits (e.g., "Morning Routine," “Fitness Challenge,” “Digital Detox”).
    * Users can browse existing groups or create their own (subject to admin approval).
    * Group discussions (text-based) are enabled.
* **Friend Connections:**  You can now connect with friends directly within Deuce Diary.
    * Send friend requests.
    * View friend activity (publicly visible - controlled by friend settings).
* **Activity Sharing (Limited):** Users can choose to share individual habit completion streaks or achievements with their friends. Sharing is opt-in and includes a brief description.
* **Social Feed:** A dynamic feed displaying activities from friends and public Community Groups.  (Focus on core habits and milestones)

**II. Tracking Features**

* **Habit Creation:** Simplified and expanded habit creation process.
    * **Customizable Habit Types:** Choose from predefined categories (e.g., Health, Productivity, Wellness) or create custom ones.
    * **Recurring Habits:** Set habits to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or custom intervals.
    * **Target Setting:** Define daily, weekly, or monthly targets for habit completion.
* **Detailed Logging:**  Record habit completion with optional notes and journal entries.
    * **Time Tracking:** Manually log the time spent working on a habit.
* **Streak Management:**  Automatically tracks and displays your habit completion streaks.
* **Progress Visualization:**  Interactive charts and graphs to visualize your progress over time. (Daily, Weekly, Monthly views)
* **Milestone Tracking:** Ability to define and track milestones related to your habits. (e.g., “Run 5K,” “Read 10 Books”).

**III. Gamification Features**

* **Points System:** Earn points for completing habits and achieving milestones.
* **Levels:** Progress through levels based on accumulated points.
* **Badges:** Unlock badges for achieving specific milestones or streaks (e.g., “7-Day Streak,” “First Habit Completed”).
* **Leaderboards (Community Groups Only):** Participate in friendly competition within Community Groups based on points earned. (Leaderboards are opt-in for each group)
* **Daily Challenges:** Receive daily habit challenges to maintain momentum. (Randomized, based on user interests)

**IV. UI/UX Improvements**

* **Redesigned Dashboard:**  A cleaner, more intuitive dashboard presenting your key habit data at a glance.
* **Improved Navigation:** Streamlined navigation for easier access to all features.
* **Dark Mode:** Added a dark mode option for reduced eye strain.
* **Enhanced Habit Customization:**  More flexible options for customizing habit icons, colors, and descriptions.
* **Mobile-First Design:**  The app is optimized for a seamless experience on mobile devices (iOS and Android).

**V. Infrastructure & Technical Improvements**

* **Server-Side Database:** Migrated to a robust server-side database for improved data storage and retrieval.
* **API Implementation:**  Initial API development for future integrations and expansions.
