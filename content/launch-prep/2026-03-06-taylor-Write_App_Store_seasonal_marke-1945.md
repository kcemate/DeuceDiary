**Seasonal Marketing Calendar – App App Store**

**I. January: Fresh Start & Goal Setting**

* **Theme:** New Year, New You
* **Focus:**  Motivation, self-improvement, habit building.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Social Media:** “#NewYearNewApp” campaign -  Focus on goals your app helps achieve. Run contests/giveaways related to the app’s core purpose. (Instagram, Facebook, TikTok - short video demos)
    * **Email:** Welcome series for new users with tips & tutorials. Promote premium features related to goal setting.
    * **Paid Ads:** Target users interested in fitness, productivity, or self-help.
    * **In-App:**  "New Year Challenge" -  Gamified onboarding experience with daily/weekly challenges. 
* **Metrics:** New user downloads, trial sign-ups, in-app engagement.


**II. February: Love & Connection**

* **Theme:** Connection, Relationships, Self-Care
* **Focus:** Depends on app – romance, friendship, wellness.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Social Media:**  Run campaigns related to app functionality. (e.g., if it’s a dating app, highlight Valentine’s Day themes. If it’s a meditation app, focus on self-love) - User Generated Content (UGC) – Encourage sharing.
    * **Email:**  “Find Your Match” (dating apps) or “Spend Quality Time” (wellness apps) promotions.
    * **Paid Ads:** Targeting demographics related to relationships/love.
    * **In-App:**  Offer special Valentine's Day discounts or features.
* **Metrics:**  App usage frequency, user engagement in core features.



**III. March: Spring & Renewal**

* **Theme:**  Spring Cleaning, Getting Active, New Beginnings
* **Focus:**  Motivation, health, organization, seasonal activities.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Social Media:**  Showcase how the app aligns with spring themes – outdoor activities, healthy eating, cleaning routines. 
    * **Email:**  "Spring Reset" series - Tips for using the app to achieve seasonal goals.
    * **Paid Ads:** Targeting users interested in outdoor activities, gardening, or health.
    * **In-App:** Run seasonal challenges or offer discounts on related products/services.
* **Metrics:**  Increase in app usage related to spring activities.



**IV. April: Earth Month & Productivity**

* **Theme:** Sustainability, Organization, Efficiency
* **Focus:**  Depends on app – eco-friendly activities, productivity hacks, time management.
* **Marketing Activities:**
    * **Social Media:**  Partner with environmental organizations, promote sustainable features within the app.
