**I. Spring (March - May) - Theme: Renewal & New Beginnings**

* **Date Range:** March 1st - May 31st
* **Campaign Focus:** Awareness & Acquisition - Capitalize on fresh starts and motivation.
* **Tactics:**
    * **March: “Spring Cleaning” App Promotion:** Highlight apps related to organization, productivity, decluttering, or fitness. (Focus: Awareness & Conversion)
    * **April (Easter):** Run Easter-themed promotions for apps with family-friendly content. (Focus: Awareness & Conversion) - Consider a gift card giveaway within the app.
    * **May (Mother's Day & Memorial Day):**  Promote apps geared towards gifts, family activities, and relaxation. (Focus: Conversion & Engagement) -  Feature user-generated content (UGC) showcasing gift ideas.
    * **Ongoing:** Run targeted App Store Search Ads (ASSA) using relevant keywords (e.g., “productivity,” “fitness,” “organization”).
* **Metrics:** App Store Search Impressions, Install Volume, App Store Page Views, Conversion Rates.



**II. Summer (June - August) - Theme: Fun & Experiences**

* **Date Range:** June 1st - August 31st
* **Campaign Focus:** Engagement & Retention - Leverage summer trends and vacations.
* **Tactics:**
    * **June (Summer Solstice):**  Promote apps that align with outdoor activities, travel, or entertainment. (Focus: Engagement) – Launch a contest: "Share your Summer Adventure" (UGC).
    * **July (Independence Day - US & other countries):** Run promotions related to fireworks, barbecues, and summer celebrations. (Focus: Awareness & Conversion) -  Offer a limited-time discount.
    * **August (Back to School):** Promote apps for students – learning, study aids, organization, and productivity. (Focus: Conversion) – Consider a bundle offer.
    * **Ongoing:**  Run App Store Featured Collections focusing on summer themes.  Leverage push notifications for in-app promotions and reminders.
* **Metrics:**  Daily/Monthly Active Users (DAU/MAU), Session Length, In-App Purchase Revenue (if applicable), Push Notification Open Rates.
