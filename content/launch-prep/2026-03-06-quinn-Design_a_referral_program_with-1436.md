* **Referred:** The new customer brought in by the referrer.
* **Tracking:**  Crucially, you need a system to track referrals. This can be:
    * **Unique Referral Links:** Each referrer gets a unique URL.
    * **Dedicated Referral Code:**  Each referrer receives a code to share.

**3. Incentive Structure – Layers of Rewards:**

This is where you really make the program attractive. We'll use a tiered system to reward different levels of participation.

* **Tier 1: Basic Referral (For Everyone)**
    * **Referrer Reward:**  Small instant reward upon successful referral.
        * **Example 1 (Cash):** $5 - $10 credit towards their next purchase.
        * **Example 2 (Discount):** 10% off their next purchase.
        * **Example 3 (Small Gift):**  Free small item (branded sticker, small accessory) - good for brand awareness.
    * **Referred Reward:**  Discount or small incentive for the new customer.
        * **Example:** 10% off their first order.  Free shipping on their first order.

* **Tier 2:  Mid-Level – For Top Referrers** (Define criteria – e.g., refer 3+ people)
    * **Referrer Reward:** Increased reward for sustained success.
        * **Example 1 (Higher Cash):** $20 - $50 credit or bonus.
        * **Example 2 (Exclusive Product):** Opportunity to win a premium product or service.
        * **Example 3 (Tiered Discounts):** 15-20% off for a defined period (e.g., 3 months).
    * **Referred Reward:**  Enhanced incentive.
        * **Example:** Free product sample, extended trial period, or premium support.

* **Tier 3:  VIP/Elite – For Top Performers** (Define criteria – e.g., refer 10+ people)
    * **Referrer Reward:** Significant reward for consistent top referrals.
        * **Example 1 (High Cash Bonus):** $100 - $300 bonus.
        * **Example 2 (Exclusive Experiences):**  Early access to new products, invitations to VIP events, personal consultations.
        * **Example 3 (Loyalty Points):**  Large accumulation of loyalty points.
    * **Referred Reward:**  Premium benefit for the new customer.
        * **Example:** Free premium version of the product for a year, personalized onboarding.



**4.  Timeline & Expiration:**

* **Referral Validity:**  Set a reasonable timeframe for referrals to be valid (e.g., 3 months, 6 months). This encourages quicker action.
* **Reward Expiration:**  Specify when rewards expire (e.g., 30 days after the referred customer’s first purchase).
