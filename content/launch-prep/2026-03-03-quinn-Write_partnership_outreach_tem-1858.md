Okay, here are several partnership outreach templates for health & wellness brands, categorized by approach and with variations to suit different levels of formality.  Remember to **personalize** these heavily – generic emails are easily ignored!

**1. The Casual & Enthusiastic Approach (Good for smaller, aligned brands)**

**Subject: Loving Your [Brand Name] & a Collaboration Idea!**

**Body:**

Hi [Contact Name],

Just wanted to say I’m a huge fan of what you’re doing over at [Brand Name]! I especially love [mention a specific product, post, or initiative - *shows you’ve done your research*]. 

I run [Your Brand Name] – we’re all about [briefly describe your brand and mission - 1-2 sentences]. We’re always looking for ways to connect with amazing wellness brands and thought your audience would really appreciate [mention a key benefit your brand offers – e.g., our healthy snacks, mindfulness meditations, etc.].

I had a few initial ideas, like [briefly mention 1-2 specific collaboration ideas – e.g., a joint giveaway, a guest post, a product bundle].  No pressure at all, but I’d love to chat and explore if a partnership could be a good fit.

Check out our brand here: [Link to Your Website]

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Social Media Link (optional)]


**2. The Value-Driven Approach (Good for brands offering tangible benefits)**

**Subject: Partner Opportunity: Reach [Brand Name’s] Audience with [Your Brand’s] [Key Benefit]**

**Body:**

Hi [Contact Name],

I’m reaching out from [Your Brand Name] – we specialize in [briefly describe your brand and mission - 1-2 sentences, focusing on the benefit].

We've been following [Brand Name]'s work in [mention their niche] and we're impressed with your commitment to [mention their values – e.g., healthy living, mindful movement]. 

We believe our [specific product/service] would be a fantastic addition to your offerings because [explain the benefit – e.g., it complements your current products, helps your audience achieve X goal, targets a similar demographic]. For example, [Give a very brief, relevant example of how it works].

We’re open to various partnership opportunities, such as:
*   [List 2-3 specific options – e.g., Sponsored Content, Affiliate Marketing, Co-branded Product]

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to discuss how a collaboration could benefit both our audiences?

You can learn more about us here: [Link to Your Website]

Thanks for your time!
[Your Name]
[Your Title]


**3. The Targeted & Specific Approach (Best when you have a clear idea)**

**Subject: Collaboration Idea: [Specific Campaign/Product] for [Brand Name’s] [Target Audience]**

**Body:**

Hi [Contact Name],

I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand Name]. We’re a [brief brand description - 1-2 sentences] focused on [your specific area of focus].

I’ve been particularly interested in [Brand Name’s] recent work on [mention a specific campaign or product] and how it resonates with [mention their target audience].

I have an idea for a collaboration that I think would be a great fit: [Clearly outline your idea - be specific! – e.g., “A limited-edition smoothie recipe featuring your protein powder alongside our plant-based ingredients”].

This would achieve [mention the key outcome – e.g., increase brand awareness for both brands, drive traffic to our websites, etc.].
