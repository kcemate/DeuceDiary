## Community Building Plan: Deuce Diary

**Executive Summary:** This plan outlines a strategy to build a thriving and passionate community around *Deuce Diary*, leveraging Discord and Reddit as primary platforms. It focuses on fostering a safe, creative, and collaborative environment centered around fan-made content, theories, and discussions.

**I. Core Values & Community Identity:**

* **Respect & Kindness:** Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, or disrespectful behavior.
* **Creative Encouragement:** Celebrate and support fan-made content – art, writing, music, cosplay, etc.
* **Collaborative Theory Building:**  Promote thoughtful, evidence-based discussions around the series' mysteries and lore.
* **Authenticity & Fan Voice:** Prioritize community input and decisions, fostering a genuine connection with the source material.


**II. Platform Strategy:**

* **Discord (Primary Hub):**
    * **Purpose:** Real-time interaction, quick discussions, event coordination, and a dedicated space for content sharing.
    * **Channels:**
        * **#general:**  Announcements, casual chatter.
        * **#deuce-diary-lore:**  Deep dives into the series' lore, asking questions, and debating interpretations.
        * **#fan-content:**  Showcase of fan-made creations (art, writing, music, etc.) – with clear tagging and sharing guidelines.
        * **#theory-central:**  Dedicated to dissecting theories, debating evidence, and proposing new ones.
        * **#cosplay-corner:**  Showcase of cosplay creations and advice.
        * **#music-moods:**  Sharing music that matches the series’ mood/themes.
        * **#off-topic:**  For casual conversations outside of *Deuce Diary*.
    * **Role System:**  Moderators, Active Contributors, New Members (with introductory permissions).
* **Reddit (Secondary Hub - Subreddit r/DeuceDiary):**
    * **Purpose:**  Longer-form discussions, archiving content, sharing external articles, and broadening reach.
    * **Content Focus:**  Links to Discord, curated discussions, longer form analysis, meme creation.
    * **Moderation:** Collaborative with Discord moderation, focusing on content quality and maintaining subreddit rules.


**III. Content Strategy:**

* **Initial Content Seed (Weeks 1-4):**
    * **Official Launch Announcements:** Both platforms.
    * **"Welcome to the Diary" Thread:**  Introduction to the community and key rules.
    * **“What are your first impressions?” Thread:** Get initial community feedback.
    * **Creator Spotlight:** Interview or feature any available creator insights.
* **Ongoing Content Pillars:**
    * **Weekly “Lore Dive”:**  A moderator or active member leads a discussion on a specific aspect of the series’ lore.
    * **“Theory of the Week”:**  A community member presents a theory, sparking debate.
    * **“Fan Creation Feature”:**  Highlighting outstanding fan-made content – awarding recognition, promoting visibility.
    * **Regular Polls & Quizzes:**  Engage members with simple interactive content.
    * **Event-Based Content:**  Announce and cover relevant events (e.g., new episodes, conventions).


**IV. Moderator Guidelines:**

* **Selection:**  Chosen based on enthusiasm, understanding of the series, strong communication skills, and commitment to community values.
* **Responsibilities:**
    * **Enforcement of Rules:**  Consistent and fair application of community guidelines.
    * **Channel Management:**  Maintain organized channels, encourage participation.
    * **Conflict Resolution:**  Mediate disputes calmly and constructively.
    * **Content Moderation:**  Removing inappropriate content promptly.
