Okay, here’s a comprehensive social media influencer outreach template kit designed for micro-influencers (Health, Humor, Lifestyle niches), including DM templates, a collaboration offer, and a content brief.  This is designed to be adaptable – read the notes at the end for customization.

**I. DM Templates (Tailored by Niche)**

**A. General Opening (All Niches – Send Immediately)**

* **Option 1 (Warm & Casual):** “Hey [Influencer Name]! 👋 Just stumbled across your page and *loved* your [mention something specific you genuinely liked - e.g., hilarious take on morning coffee, inspiring fitness tip, amazing travel photo]. You've got a great vibe!”
* **Option 2 (More Formal - Use if you’ve researched them deeply):** “Hi [Influencer Name], I’m [Your Name] from [Your Brand].  I’ve been a long-time follower of your work and really appreciate your perspective on [mention a specific area they focus on].”

**B. Health Influencers**

* **After General Opening:** “We’re [Your Brand] – we’re all about [briefly describe your brand and mission – e.g., healthy snack ideas, mindful movement, wellness tips].  We think your audience would genuinely benefit from [mention a specific product/service relevant to their content].”
* **Call to Action:** "Would you be open to a quick chat about a potential collaboration? ✨"

**C. Humor Influencers**

* **After General Opening:** “Your content always makes me laugh so hard! 😂 We’re [Your Brand], and we’re trying to bring a little more joy into the world with [describe your brand – e.g., funny memes, relatable life hacks, silly challenges].  We think our brand would fit right in with your audience's sense of humor!”
* **Call to Action:** “Seriously, we’d love to brainstorm some funny ideas together. 🤪”

**D. Lifestyle Influencers**

* **After General Opening:** "I'm loving your style & approach to [mention a specific area - e.g., home decor, weekend adventures, self-care]. We're [Your Brand], and we're passionate about [describe your brand – e.g., sustainable living, creating cozy spaces, mindful experiences]. “
* **Call to Action:** "Would you be interested in seeing how our brand could integrate with your lifestyle content?"
