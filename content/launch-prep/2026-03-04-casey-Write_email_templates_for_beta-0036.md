Okay, here are email templates tailored for beta testers, friends, and influencer outreach, with variations to suit different levels of formality and goals.

**1. Beta Tester Email Template (Focus: Feedback & Relationship Building)**

**Subject: Invitation to Test [Product Name] - Help Shape the Future!**

**Option 1 (More Formal):**

> Dear [Beta Tester Name],
>
> We’re thrilled to invite you to join our beta testing program for [Product Name], our new [Product Category - e.g., productivity app, mobile game, SaaS platform]! We’re incredibly excited about this project and believe your insights as a [mention their area of expertise/interest - e.g., tech enthusiast, user of similar products] would be invaluable.
>
> We're looking for testers to provide feedback on [mention specific areas you want feedback on - e.g., usability, features, bugs, overall experience].  Your honest and detailed feedback will directly contribute to shaping the final product.
>
> **Here’s what you’ll get:**
> *  Early access to [Product Name]
> *  The opportunity to influence the development process
> *  A chance to be recognized as a key contributor
>
> To learn more and sign up, please visit: [Link to Beta Tester Signup/Details]
>
> We appreciate your time and enthusiasm!
>
> Sincerely,
> The [Your Company Name] Team

**Option 2 (More Casual):**

> Hey [Beta Tester Name],
>
> We're so excited to be launching [Product Name] and we'd love your help! We’re looking for beta testers to give us the lowdown on the app/product and help us make it the best it can be.
>
> We’re especially keen to hear your thoughts on [mention 1-2 key things you want feedback on].  Basically, we want to know what you think!
>
> You’ll get early access and a chance to really make a difference.  Check it out here: [Link to Beta Tester Signup/Details]
>
> Thanks a bunch!
> [Your Name] / The [Your Company Name] Team



**Key Elements for Beta Testers:**

*   **Personalization:**  Mention their interest/expertise if you know it.
*   **Clear Expectations:**  Specifically state what kind of feedback you need.
*   **Incentives:** Highlight the benefits of participating.
*   **Easy Sign-Up:**  Provide a direct link.
