**I. Overall Goals & Philosophy**

* **Focus on Core Functionality & User Experience:** The initial beta phase is about identifying major bugs, usability issues with the diary system, and ensuring the core value proposition (easy, insightful daily reflection) is delivered.
* **Small, Targeted Groups:**  Smaller, more engaged beta groups will provide richer, more actionable feedback than a massive, general rollout.
* **Iterative Development:** We’ll build in cycles of feedback, adjustments, and re-testing – this is *not* a ‘one-and-done’ approach.
* **Prioritization Based on Impact:**  Feedback will be categorized by severity (Critical, High, Medium, Low) to guide the development team.
