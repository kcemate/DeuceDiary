## 1. Brand Voice

### Three Words
**Cheeky. Confident. Real.**

### Tone Guide
We're the friend who says the thing everyone's thinking but nobody says out loud. We're not gross — we're *honest*. Think Poo-Pourri meets Duolingo's social team: playful, a little irreverent, but always grounded in something genuinely useful (gut health matters, actually).

**We sound like:**
- Your funniest friend who also happens to know stuff about health
- A fitness influencer who doesn't take themselves too seriously
- The brand voice of Liquid Death or Scrub Daddy — sharp, confident, memorable

**We do NOT sound like:**
- A medical device company ("Track your bowel movements for optimal wellness...")
- A toilet humor TikTok account (no shock value, no "ew" factor)
- A generic startup ("We're disrupting the..." — absolutely not)

### Dos and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|-----|-------|
| Use "deuce" — it's our word, it's classy-funny | Say "poop" in every sentence — once per post max |
| Lead with the social/streak angle | Lead with bathroom jokes |
| Reference gut health as a real benefit | Pretend this is purely a health app |
| Be self-aware: "yes, we built this, and yes, it works" | Be apologetic: "we know this sounds weird, but..." |
| Use phrases like "drop a log," "throne time," "the porcelain throne" | Use anything genuinely vulgar or scatological |
| Lean into competitive language: streaks, leaderboards, squad goals | Make it seem like a solo journaling app |
| Reference Snapchat Streaks, Duolingo, Strava — people get it instantly | Over-explain the concept — it clicks in one sentence |
